# High Watcher Alena

A priestess of Torm who became a respected community organizer and spiritual guide for the Elturian refugees in the wake of the Fall and the Avernus crisis. Now leader of the [Hellriders of New Elturel](../summary.md) and the Elturians' chief political voice in Baldur's Gate.

## Manifesto

> They call us refugees. They call us a crisis. We are the children of Elturgard. We are the survivors of Hell. We built this community from the mud and our memories. We police our own streets, we tend to our own sick, and we keep our own faith. We do not ask for pity. We demand our rights. We are the Hellriders, and our watch has not ended.

## Role

Alena runs both the **militia** and the **informal government** of New Elturel from the **High Rider's Stand**. Her senior officer **Lyra Sunstone** handles operational command of patrols and brigade formation. Alena's authority rests on three things: her position in Torm's clergy, her record in the early refugee crisis, and her steady refusal to either capitulate or escalate.

## Current pressures

- **The leaked petition.** Her formal citizenship petition to the High Hall was leaked, alongside a falsified cost-analysis report, to *Baldur's Mouth*. The article stoked public fear of "the refugee burden" and pushed citizenship further out of reach.
- **The Refugee Question hearing.** Alena gave fiery, well-prepared testimony at the Council's public hearing. The hearing devolved into shouting between nativists and merchants seeking cheap labor, and her testimony was ignored.
- **Wyrm's Crossing protest.** Following the brutalization of an Outer City merchant by a [Flaming Fist](../../flaming-fist/summary.md) patrol, Alena organized a mass peaceful protest at Wyrm's Crossing — joined by the [Followers of the Phoenix](../../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md). The protest forced negotiations open with the [Council of Four](../../council-of-four/summary.md).
- **Collapsed negotiations.** Alena flatly refused the Council's insulting offer, unwilling to betray the [Free Traders of the Outskirts](../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md). Negotiations collapsed; both sides have hardened.
- **The Call to Arms.** With mercenaries at the gate and negotiations dead, Alena has issued a formal call to arms, organizing refugees into militia brigades and beginning public drills.
- **Plague-of-Remembrance infiltration.** Lady Emmeline Vhage is currently trying to turn a disillusioned Hellrider officer into an informant, exploiting his bitterness over the failed citizenship deal.
