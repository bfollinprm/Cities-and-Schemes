# Lyra Sunstone

A young paladin of Torm who serves as senior officer under [High Watcher Alena](high-watcher-alena.md) at the [High Rider's Stand](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md). Operationally she runs the Hellriders' patrol command — duty rosters, brigade formation, scout reports — leaving Alena free for politics.

## Role with the party

Lyra is the party's first named Hellrider contact after Alena. She delivered Alena's invitation to the Weary Plowman in [Session 2](../../../50-Sessions/session_2/player_summary.md) and escorted them through New Elturel to the audience in [Session 3](../../../50-Sessions/session_3/player_summary.md). The party reads her as competent, formal, and quietly impressed by the medicine corridor.

## Captured at the Circus and recovered (week 3)

In **week 3**, Lyra is one of the patrons [Zara the Sorrow-Eater](../../carnival-of-whispers/people/zara.md) leads backstage at the [Circus of the Last Days](../../carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_3/sowing-sweet-sorrows.md). She trades her *Memories of Elturel's Fall* for *The Strength to Prevent Another* with [Lucretius](../../carnival-of-whispers/people/lucretius.md), losing her Hellrider identity entirely; the party finds her doing chores in *The Ephemera Collection* with superhuman strength but devoid of purpose. They [win her memory back](../../party/activity/week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md) by answering Lucretius's three riddles. She is restored — purpose intact — and now owes the party a significant personal debt. Her experience makes her a hard-won expert on fey bargains within the Hellrider command.

## Quartermaster of the alliance (week 5)

Restored, Lyra resumes the Hellrider Quartermaster role and immediately puts it to use under [Alena's call to arms](../activity/week_4/the-call-to-arms.md). In [Session 12](../../../50-Sessions/session_12/player_summary.md) she catches the party on the doorstep of the Pilgrim's Parchment and presents Hellrider surplus as thanks for [restoring her memory](../../party/activity/week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md): full plate to Kaelan, leather and a perfectly-balanced greatsword to Spark. She tells the party the Hellriders have moved to the [Basilisk Gate](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) to throw in with [Mad Meggan](../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md). She then ushers them into the [war council that seals the Hellrider–Free Trader alliance](../activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) in [Session 16](../../../50-Sessions/session_16/player_summary.md), and is the camp's day-to-day handler for the party for the rest of the week.
