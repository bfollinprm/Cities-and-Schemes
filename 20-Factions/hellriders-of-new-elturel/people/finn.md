# Finn

An orphaned tiefling girl from [New Elturel](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md), hardened by life on the camp's streets and accompanied by a pet rust-weasel named **Scrap**. She wields minor magical training — at least Thaumaturgy, used to boom-voice "Back off!" the first time Elia reached for her.

## With the party

Finn met the party in [Session 5](../../../50-Sessions/session_5/player_summary.md) when she crossed paths with them in the sewer cisterns chasing Scrap, who had stolen her biscuit. She joined the party's push deeper, recognized the cultists' "whispering stone," went down to a cult fanatic in the Plague-Father's lab, **rolled a natural 20 on her death save**, and personally turned the fight by stabbing the wounded Plague-Father out of his retreat. After the lab fell, the party walked Finn and the surrendering cultist to [High Watcher Alena](high-watcher-alena.md), who agreed to take Finn into Hellrider care.
