# Jaredith Heartsong

A senior Hellrider lieutenant and paladin of Torm. Once one of [High Watcher Alena's](high-watcher-alena.md) most trusted officers; now drifting toward open militancy through the **Phoenix Pits**, the radical fighting wing of the [Followers of the Phoenix](../../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md), in particular through his association with the young tiefling [Mikkel](../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/mikkel.md).

## Pressures

Jaredith's faith and his patience have come unmoored from the rest of his command. He now openly argues for striking the [Flaming Fist](../../flaming-fist/summary.md) — exactly the pretext the Council needs to crush the refugees. Alena cannot afford to discipline him publicly without splitting the militia, and cannot afford to let him drift further without losing him to the Phoenix entirely.

## Role with the party

In [Session 3](../../../50-Sessions/session_3/player_summary.md), Alena tasks the party (see [accept-the-jaredith-mission-from-alena](../../party/activity/week_1/accept-the-jaredith-mission-from-alena.md)) with discreetly investigating Jaredith — find the influences pulling him under, and persuade him off the violent path. The mission is open at week's end.
