# High Watcher Alena

High Watcher of the [Hellriders of New Elturel](../summary.md). Priestess of Torm. Community organizer, spiritual guide, and increasingly a war leader.

## Manifesto

> They call us refugees. They call us a crisis. We are the children of Elturgard. We are the survivors of Hell. We built this community from the mud and our memories. We police our own streets, we tend to our own sick, and we keep our own faith. We do not ask for pity. We demand our rights. We are the Hellriders, and our watch has not ended.

## Role

Alena rebuilt the refugee community of New Elturel from camps into a borough-in-fact, modeling its militia on the original Hellriders. She has spent the campaign threading a narrow needle: keeping the [Followers of the Phoenix](../../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) from starting a riot that would destroy the refugee cause, while pressing the city for aid and citizenship.

By week 5 the strategy shifted. The Archduke's Sembian mercenary deployment to the Whisperwood pushed Alena into formal military alliance with [Mad Meggan](../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) at the Basilisk Gate, with landmines and terror tactics authorized. By week 8, the Followers of the Phoenix have visibly upstaged her — The Ember's song-and-faith movement ended the protest more decisively than her organized political pressure could.

She has used the party as deniable agents repeatedly: the medicine heist (week 1), Jaredith de-radicalization (week 6), Plague of Remembrance prisoner debrief.
