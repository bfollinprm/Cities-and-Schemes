# The Call to Arms

[High Watcher Alena](../../people/high-watcher-alena.md), with [last week's citizenship negotiations dead on the floor](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_3/the-negotiations-price.md) and [the Archduke's mercenaries fortifying inside the Basilisk Gate](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md), issues a formal **call to arms** at the [High Rider's Stand](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) parade ground. By evening she has organized the able-bodied refugees of New Elturel into militia brigades — tiefling and human ranks side by side, drilled by [Jaredith Heartsong](../../people/jaredith-heartsong.md) and the surviving Hellrider lieutenants. Public drills run dawn and dusk in the Outer City squares; refugees who attended [the Ember's recent sermon at the Phoenix Gate](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_4/a-martyrs-sermon.md) report for muster. Alena does not call this an army. She calls it the city's last legitimate watch in the Outer City. Recovered Quartermaster [Lyra Sunstone](../../people/lyra-sunstone.md), her purpose restored after the Feywild [Ephemera Collection](../../../party/activity/week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md) episode, is back at her side and signing requisitions for steel.
