# The Interrogation

[High Watcher Alena](../../people/high-watcher-alena.md) breaks the captured [Plague of Remembrance](../../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) cultist before handing him to the party at the [High Rider's Stand](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md). His intelligence: a "sister in disguise" — a human mage masquerading as a tiefling — is embedded in the Followers of the Phoenix; the cult intends to martyr [Jaredith Heartsong](../../people/jaredith-heartsong.md) to ignite a refugee riot the Fist will be forced to crush. Leveraging the political goodwill from [the peaceful protest resolution](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_2/the-call-for-martyrs.md) and her reputation as the only authority anyone trusts in the Outer City, Alena uses the same week to open formal negotiations with the [Council of Four](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_2/contain-the-crises.md) and **[Lyra Sunstone](../../people/lyra-sunstone.md)** on a path to citizenship for the Elturian refugees.
