# The Shield Wall

[High Watcher Alena](../../people/high-watcher-alena.md) integrates her Hellriders directly into the [Free Trader militia](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) at the Basilisk Gate barricade. Drilling day and night, Hellrider veterans teach the ragtag traders the tight-formation discipline they learned in Avernus — interlocked shields, braced spears, calm steps backward when a charge breaks the line. Within three days the chaotic barricade is a disciplined defensive position capable of withstanding a mercenary cavalry charge.

The transformation is visible from the [Outer City roads](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_6/the-starvation-siege.md) where Sembian patrols ride past — and they ride past warily now. When [the Council's warning volley](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_6/the-warning-shot.md) lands, the line does not even ripple; the shield wall absorbs it and the wounded are pulled back through tested lanes. Alena's drilling is the load-bearing answer to the Archduke's threats: the blockade is no longer a *barricade* but a *front*, and crossing it would now cost real Sembian lives.
