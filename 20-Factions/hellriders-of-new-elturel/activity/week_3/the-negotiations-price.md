# The Negotiation's Price

[High Watcher Alena](../../people/high-watcher-alena.md) takes the [Council's citizenship offer](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_2/contain-the-crises.md) into formal session at [High Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md) — and walks out flatly refusing it. The terms required Hellrider abandonment of the [Free Trader siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_3/press-the-advantage.md) and a public condemnation of ['Mad' Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md), neither of which Alena will deliver while the city's corrupt machinery is what the Free Traders are blockading. Negotiations collapse. The refugees still have no official path to citizenship; the Council's stance against them hardens; and Alena now publicly stands with Meggan against the Council, foreshadowing the formal Hellrider–Free Trader military alliance that becomes inevitable once the [Archduke's private mercenaries arrive](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_3/the-private-option.md).
