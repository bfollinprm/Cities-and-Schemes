# The Witch Hunt

After [last week's confirmation that the captured Reaper was poisoned by his own prison rations](../../../takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_6/the-prison-silence.md), [High Watcher Alena](../../people/high-watcher-alena.md) now knows what the party only suspected: **a Bhaalist or Zhentarim mole sits in the Hellrider supply line**. She places the High Rider's Stand on internal lockdown and convenes a quiet inquisition of every quartermaster, cook, water-bearer, and stable-boy who handled food in the previous tenday.

The work is brutal and slow. Three quartermasters are stood down without charges; one cook is held for questioning; one stable-boy disappears overnight before he can be brought in for a second interview. Alena suspends the Hellriders' joint patrols with [the Free Traders](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) until the hunt resolves — *the New Elturel piece of the buffer state is shrinking inward.* She sends a quiet request to [the Nightingale](../../../harpers/people/the-nightingale.md) for a Harper interrogation specialist; the cells find no traitor by week's end, but morale at the Stand collapses to the lowest point in the camp's history.
