# Organize a Protest at Wyrm's Crossing

Following the public beating of an Outer City merchant by [Manip Jerrol's](../../../flaming-fist/people/manip-jerrol.md) Fist patrol and the Council hearing's dead end, [High Watcher Alena](../../people/high-watcher-alena.md) organizes a mass, deliberately peaceful protest at the southern end of [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md). The demand is concrete: end the Fist tolls, open citizenship talks. The [Followers of the Phoenix](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) join under [the Ember's](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) preaching, swelling the crowd. The protest plus the Free Trader [Basilisk Gate blockade](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_1/blockade-the-basilisk-gate.md) seal the bridge and Lower City supply for several days.

The protest is also where the [party](../../../party/summary.md) first meets Alena: they negotiate a corridor through the protest line for the medicine cart they had just liberated for the Open Hand Temple. Alena registers them as a useful, deniable instrument and books a follow-up meeting at the High Rider's Stand.
