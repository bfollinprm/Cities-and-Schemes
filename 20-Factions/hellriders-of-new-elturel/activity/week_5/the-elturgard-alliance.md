# The Elturgard Alliance

[High Watcher Alena](../../people/high-watcher-alena.md), reading [the Archduke's ultimatum](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md) as the inevitable attack [her week-4 call to arms](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_4/the-call-to-arms.md) had already prepared for, formally reaches across the gate. From the parade ground at the [High Rider's Stand](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) she dispatches a written alliance offer to ['Mad' Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md): disciplined Hellrider regulars, drilled twice daily by [Jaredith Heartsong](../../people/jaredith-heartsong.md), to be deployed in support of [the fortified Free Trader blockade](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_5/heed-the-ultimatum.md), in exchange for a unified front against the city. Meggan signs on the same evening. The Hellriders march a full century across Wyrm's Crossing the next morning to take up positions behind the inner barricade. **The militia of New Elturel and the Free Traders of the Outskirts are now one army.** Alena's pulpit is now a command tent; her congregation is now a brigade. Concealed risk: [Lady Vhage's turned officer](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_4/the-rot-from-within.md) is in the room when the alliance terms are drafted, and [a second cult agent moves on Alena this week](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_5/the-serpents-kiss.md).
