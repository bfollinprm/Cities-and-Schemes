# "Mad" Meggan

A tough-as-nails caravan master who has survived a dozen bandit attacks on the Coast Way and isn't afraid of anyone from inside the walls of Baldur's Gate. The de facto leader of the [Free Traders of the Outskirts](../summary.md) and current organizer of the largest civic-disobedience action in the [Outer City](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md)'s living memory.

## Manifesto

> The walls aren't the edge of the world, and your tariffs aren't the price of breathing. Out here we move our own goods, we guard our own gates, we feed our own families. We pay what we owe, and not a copper more. They call me mad. I notice they pay me.

## Role

Meggan operates out of the **[Wyrm's Bend Market](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-bend-market.md)** and the inn-rooms above it. Her authority within the Free Traders is informal but absolute — earned by results, not bestowed. She negotiates directly with city officials when she has to, and stops the gates with wagons when she doesn't.

## Current pressures

- **The mutual-protection pact.** Meggan organized the founding pact among [Outer City](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md) caravan masters, agreeing to share intelligence on Flaming Fist patrols at [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) and to travel in combined convoys.
- **The [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) brawl.** A confrontation with a Fist patrol erupted into a public brawl on the bridge. The Free Traders refused to pay; the resulting blockade was a multi-day disaster for the Fist.
- **Vessel impoundment.** The [Chionthar Consortium](../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md), backed by the Fist, has impounded one of Meggan's trading vessels under the new Harbor Dredging Levy as an example.
- **Brutalization at [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md).** A merchant in her cooperative was beaten by a Fist patrol led by Manip Jerrol; the public outrage is fueling the [Outer City](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md)'s anger.
- **Basilisk Gate blockade.** The blockade was violently broken by [Blaze Liara Portyr](../../flaming-fist/summary.md). Several caravan masters were arrested and wagons impounded, but the political damage has accelerated the larger crisis.
- **The captive cultist.** Meggan extracted a public confession from a captured [Plague of Remembrance](../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) cultist who named several prominent patriar families as backers. The Council and patriar press are now suppressing the story; *Baldur's Mouth* has refused to print her demands under pressure from the implicated families. Meggan's pamphlets are being dismissed as radical propaganda.
- **The Sembian rejection.** Meggan rebuffed an overture from [Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../sembian-kontor/summary.md), refusing to come to the negotiating table on Sembian terms. The Basilisk Gate remains closed.
- **Refused negotiation.** When the Council offered a side-deal that would have given the Hellriders citizenship in exchange for breaking the blockade, [High Watcher Alena](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) refused — a refusal Meggan publicly thanked her for.
