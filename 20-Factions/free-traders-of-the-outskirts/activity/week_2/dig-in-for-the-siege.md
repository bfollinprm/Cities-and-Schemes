# Dig In for the Siege

['Mad' Meggan's](../../people/mad-meggan.md) caravan masters dig in at the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) and turn the week-1 picket into a sustained siege. Meggan extracts a public confession from the cultist prisoner the party hands her in exchange for **6,000 gp** (see [the party's deal](../../../party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md)); the cultist names several prominent patriar families as the Plague of Remembrance's secret backers. Meggan publishes the confession to the assembled crowd, igniting a class-warfare political firestorm regardless of whether any individual name is true. The [Council of Four](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_2/contain-the-crises.md) cannot now negotiate the gate open without conceding to her on patriar accountability.

Meggan does not yet know that during the negotiation Fen lifted the [**Medallion of Thoughts**](../../../party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md) from her neck — a serious blow to her own intelligence-gathering, and the leverage the party holds back.
