# Digging In

['Mad' Meggan](../../people/mad-meggan.md), reading [the Archduke's new mercenary camp](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md) as a direct provocation, orders the Free Trader blockade fortified — ditches, sharpened stakes, an overhead lookout. The order goes out in the morning. By midday a disciplined company of [the Archduke's mercenaries](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md) marches out of their new Eastway barracks in shield-wall formation, halts in front of the half-dug ditches, and locks shields. Meggan's caravan crews are forced to drop their tools. The shovels stand upright in the half-finished ditch all afternoon. Patriar passers-by laugh; refugees do not. The fortification is a public humiliation: the mercenaries have demonstrated, without firing a crossbow, that **they own the ground in front of the Basilisk Gate**. Meggan walks the line that night and tells her crews to put the shovels back to work after dark — and, separately, sends a runner to [High Watcher Alena](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md).
