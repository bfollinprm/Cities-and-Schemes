# Blockade the Basilisk Gate

In retaliation for the impounding of ['Mad' Meggan's](../../people/mad-meggan.md) vessel and the Shipwrights' guild-only repair edict, the Free Traders ring their wagons across the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) and refuse to move. The blockade holds for several days, throttling overland supply into the Lower City. **Blaze Liara Portyr** of the [Flaming Fist](../../../flaming-fist/summary.md) ends it personally: a heavy Fist contingent storms the line, arrests several caravan masters, and impounds their wagons. The blockade is broken; Outer City fury at the Fist scales another notch and folds directly into the [Hellrider protest at Wyrm's Crossing](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_1/organize-a-protest-at-wyrms-crossing.md).
