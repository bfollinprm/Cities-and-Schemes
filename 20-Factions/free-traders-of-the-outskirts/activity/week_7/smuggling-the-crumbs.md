# Smuggling the Crumbs

Facing [the Archduke's starvation siege](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_6/the-starvation-siege.md) and [the Press Blackout's confiscation of personal food stores](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_7/the-press-blackout.md), ['Mad' Meggan](../../people/mad-meggan.md) issues a strict rationing edict inside the barricade — *every wagon, every tent, every refugee on the same gruel ration* — and turns the Free Traders' enforcement arm into a **food-recovery network**.

A halfling raiding cell threads forgotten storm-aqueducts under [Eastway](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md), surfacing inside Lower City pantries at dawn. They take small loads — a sack of millet, a wheel of cheese, a jar of pickled eggs — and slip back through the drains before the patrolling Sembian companies can react. The yield is small but the **morale shock** is huge: the Outer City is feeding itself off the Lower City's groceries despite the gates being closed. By midweek the Sembian commanders are quietly assigning sappers to map the storm-drains. Meanwhile, [the Vine Barricades](../../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_7/vine-barricades.md) keep the front gate closed enough that the gruel ration is the only thing keeping the camp from starving.
