# Heed the Ultimatum

['Mad' Meggan](../../people/mad-meggan.md), reading [the Archduke's three-day ultimatum](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md) as confirmation of what [her week-4 fortification order](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_4/digging-in.md) had already named, organizes her caravan crews into a defensive militia. Wagons are dragged into a *second* inner barricade behind the original blockade — staggered, overlapping, anchored on water-butts and the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) curtain wall. Smiths beat trade-iron into pike-heads. Stall-keepers learn to load a crossbow. Meggan does not call this an army either — she calls it the same thing every Free Trader has called Outer City self-defense for two decades — but every wagon-master in the Outskirts now knows where to stand if the mercenary shield-wall comes back. By dusk, [High Watcher Alena's runner arrives](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) with a formal alliance offer; Meggan signs it on a barrel-head. **The Free Trader blockade is now a fortified position with disciplined infantry behind it.**
