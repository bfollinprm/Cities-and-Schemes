# Rationing the Hope

With the [Western Gate Trading Company choking the supply lines](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_6/the-starvation-siege.md), ['Mad' Meggan](../../people/mad-meggan.md) institutes strict rationing inside the Basilisk Gate barricade. Bread is halved, salt-meat goes out by ladle, and a single tin cup of broth becomes a ward-elder's full evening meal. Meggan's lieutenants — including the older Free Trader caravan masters who first followed her into the blockade — are tasked with rooting out hoarders, breaking up the small bribery rackets that have sprung up around the food wagons, and enforcing equal share-out across both Free Trader and Phoenix-refugee families inside the line. It is a fragile balancing act: keep morale high while the gnawing hunger sharpens.

The rationing holds because it is *visibly fair*. Meggan eats the same ladle of broth as the families she's protecting; her lieutenants do too. The [free feast under the gate last week](../../../party/activity/week_5/errinthals-relief-caravan-pr-coup.md) is now memory — the political coup that ended Errinthal's caravan stunt also marked the last full meal anyone behind the barricade has had. Hellrider quartermasters discreetly slip flour-sacks across from New Elturel under cover of the [shield-wall drills](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_6/the-shield-wall.md). The line holds. For now.
