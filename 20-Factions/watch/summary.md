# The Watch *(aka Upper City Watch)*

A patriar-funded watch force responsible for policing the **Upper City** — distinct from the broader, charter-bound [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md), and answerable in practice to the [Council of Four](../council-of-four/summary.md). Led on paper by a rotating Watch Captain, in practice by whichever patriar is currently footing the bill.

## Ideology

Maintain order and decorum where the city's rich actually live. The Watch's beat is the Upper City — Manorborn, Bloomridge, the Temples District, the Bazaar, the Old Wall — and its priorities reflect patriar comfort: visible patrols at the gates, peace-tied weapons in the markets, lanterns flaring at curfew.

## Membership

Smaller than the Fist; staffed by Upper City professionals, often retired Fist veterans or third sons of patriar houses. Funding flows from a mix of city tax and direct patriar subsidy, which makes the Watch effectively patriar-aligned even when its remit is officially civic.

## Methods

- Visible patrols at the Upper City Bazaar, the Old Wall arches, and the Temples District.
- Curfew enforcement on the Old Wall arches and Citadel approaches.
- Recently authorized by Archduke [Lyra Silvershield](../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) to extend patrols onto **Western Gate logging routes** outside the city — a controversial overreach onto [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md) jurisdiction that drew angry objections from [Lord Corin Durinbold](../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md).
- Recently failed to crack down on "unsanctioned art" in the Upper City; the resulting mocking caricatures of Watch officers near the Citadel were a minor humiliation.
- With the Marshal recalling Fist loyalists to Wyrm's Rock, the Watch is now the *de facto* Upper City police, stretched thin across Manorborn and Temples District.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Patrons.** Patriar families and the sitting Archduke.

**Friction.** Constant jurisdictional tension with the [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md) (over Upper City and now hinterland patrols), and with **Guild Wardens** wherever the Watch's writ overlaps a Worshipful Company's chartered turf.
