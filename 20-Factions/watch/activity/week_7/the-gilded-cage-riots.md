# The Gilded Cage Riots

Trapped behind their own [interior gate lockdown](../../activity/week_6/the-gate-lockdown.md) for the second straight tenday, panicked **minor nobles and wealthy merchants** in the Upper City begin rioting against the Watch itself, demanding to be let out *before* the Lower City mobs (which have not yet materialized) break in. The first scuffle erupts at the Manorborn Gate on the third morning; by the fifth night a Watch captain has lost teeth to a thrown candelabra at the Wide.

The Watch does not have the bodies to suppress an Upper City riot — the lockdown was sized for *keeping people out*, not *keeping people in*. Within four days [the Knights of the Shield](../../../knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_7/the-gilded-extortion.md) are *paid to provide riot suppression* by the Manorborn houses themselves. The Watch's last monopoly inside the gates collapses. Watch Captain reports request emergency Council authorization to lift the lockdown; the Council does not respond. **The Upper City has riots inside its own walls and an infernal-pact private army patrolling them.** [Lord Hhune's alchemical sigil-burnings](../../../elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_7/the-alchemical-arson.md) are the kindling.
