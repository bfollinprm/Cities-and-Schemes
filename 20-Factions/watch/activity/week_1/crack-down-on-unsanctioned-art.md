# Crack Down on 'Unsanctioned Art'

The Watch's heavy-footed sweep against "unsanctioned art" in the Upper City produces no arrests; the artists, faster than the patrols, simply move ahead of them. The campaign is quietly abandoned within days, and mocking caricatures of Watch officers begin appearing on walls near the Citadel and around [Manorborn](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/manorborn.md). The episode hands the Patriar press a small reputational dent to the Watch precisely as it stretches itself across the new Whisperwood deployment.
