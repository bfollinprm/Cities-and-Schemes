# The Plea for Reinforcements

A senior Watch Captain — name redacted from the official register — files a formal written plea with the [Flaming Fist command at Wyrm's Rock](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_5/the-integration-drills.md) requesting Fist reinforcement for [the Manorborn burglary spree](../../../watch/activity/week_4/the-manorborn-burglaries.md) and the [Bhaalist ritual-dagger evidence sitting unworked in the Watch's evidence room](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_4/an-offering-in-the-dark.md). The plea is delivered by hand. It is denied at the gate. [Marshal Flint](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md), already running a parade-ground integration drill with [K'laar's githyanki phalanx](../../../unchained/people/k-laar.md), reads the plea aloud to the assembled barracks before refusing it — and goes further: he openly mocks the Upper City for **"calling for the Fist when the patriars cower under their own beds"**. The mockery is repeated in every guardhouse of the Upper City by nightfall. The Watch's captains pull patrols out of the outer Upper City wards rather than be seen begging again. Several patriar estates lose their evening watch. **The crime spree the Watch warned about now has no obstacle.** [The Knights of the Shield walk through the gap inside three days](../../../knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_5/a-desperate-bargain.md).
