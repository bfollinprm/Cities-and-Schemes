# Maintain Appearances

With [Marshal Flint recalling Fist loyalists to Wyrm's Rock](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_3/circle-the-wagons.md), Upper City Watch coverage thins out fast. The Watch Captain on duty pulls every available patrol to [the Temples District](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/temples-district.md) and [Manorborn](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/manorborn.md) — the addresses that pay the Watch's salaries — and writes off the rest of the Upper City as undermanned. The optics hold for the patriar bloc; [Lord Durinbold's](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) [Heritage Society gala](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_3/defend-the-marshal.md) gets full ceremonial cover. The cost is felt elsewhere: the [Wide](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/the-wide.md), the back lanes off the [Citadel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md), and the merchant approaches from the Lower City wall are all dangerously underpoliced for the rest of the week. Burglary reports go up. The Watch's official line is that "with the situation at the Basilisk Gate, all available manpower is committed to essential routes."
