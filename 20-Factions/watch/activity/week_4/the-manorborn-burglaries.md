# The Manorborn Burglaries

With the [Flaming Fist consolidated at Wyrm's Rock](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_4/an-alliance-of-steel-and-silver.md) and political crises eating Watch staffing — the High Hall brawl required [Watch presence on the chamber floor](../../../parliament-of-peers/activity/week_4/a-debate-of-the-deaf.md) — the Watch is unable to prevent a string of high-profile burglaries in [Manorborn](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/manorborn.md). The thief targets Peerage-of-Coin patriars specifically: stolen valuables, but more notably *sensitive correspondence* — Coin-bloc whip lists, [Emergency Levy](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_4/the-emergency-levy.md) ledger drafts, private debt schedules. Within days an unsigned pamphlet quoting some of the lifted documents circulates the Wide. The new-money elite is now paranoid that the burglaries are not random but coordinated political work. They are. The Watch can do nothing about it; the same week [a Bhaalist ritual dagger is recovered in their district](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_4/an-offering-in-the-dark.md) and goes uninvestigated.
