# Vine Barricades

[Elder Elion](../../people/elder-elion.md) commits the Inner Grove to active defense of the [Free Trader blockade at Basilisk Gate](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_6/rationing-the-hope.md). His druids spend three nights singing the wagon-barricade into a living wall: thorny river-cane and razor-vine grow up through wagon-spokes, snarling wheel-rims into the cobbles and weaving across the gaps between carts faster than the Sembian sappers can clear them.

The first probing assault by [the Archduke's mercenaries](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) loses two scouts to barbed strangler-vines and falls back without testing the line in force. The Sembian commanders rapidly revise their estimate of the barricade upward; the [Archduke's escalation orders](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_7/the-press-blackout.md) shift from breaking the line to **starving the Outer City out**. The Druids have made the wall so expensive to assault directly that the Council is forced to make the war a war on civilians instead — exactly what [Elion warned would happen](../../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_6/the-source-of-the-rot.md), and exactly what the Druids want on the Council's ledger.
