# Purify the Blight

[Elder Elion's](../../people/elder-elion.md) druids quietly use druidic magic to *reinforce* the [Free Traders'](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) [Basilisk Gate siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md) — not in solidarity with the caravan masters, but because the halt to industrial traffic gives the [Chionthar River](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) and the Rivington sewers a few days of relief from runoff and refuse. The Circle reads the blockade as an unintended ecological mercy and feeds it. No one outside the Grove links the river's improved clarity to druidic intervention.
