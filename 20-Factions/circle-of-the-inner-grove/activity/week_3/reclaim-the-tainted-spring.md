# Reclaim the Tainted Spring

[Elder Elion](../../people/elder-elion.md) leads a small druidic circle to a spring near the Chionthar tributary that the [Worshipful Company of Alchemists & Apothecaries](../../../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/summary.md) and Lower City tanneries have been using as a runoff drain for months. The purification ritual backfires catastrophically. Concentrated industrial filth and the residue of biological reagents — including, the Circle suspects, runoff from [the Plague-Father's destroyed laboratory](../../../party/activity/week_1/destroy-the-plague-fathers-laboratory.md) — animate as a foul water weird that attacks the druids mid-rite. The Circle disperses it after several minutes of brutal close combat, with injuries. The spring is now actively hostile to the living; the corruption has spread further down the tributary, killing fish and sickening wildlife miles downstream. Elion's response is not to retreat but to escalate: the Circle will not see the wilderness become a city's sewer, and [the captured Sembian scouts](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_3/the-alternate-route.md) returned with that warning the same week were not chosen at random.
