# Binding the Blight

[Elder Elion](../../people/elder-elion.md) leads a day-long binding ritual at a spring on a Chionthar tributary, intending to chain the angry water weird the [purification ritual backfired into last week](../../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_3/reclaim-the-tainted-spring.md). The circle's working is technically correct, but the elemental's wrath has grown faster than Elion's preparation. The concentrated magical energy *supercharges* the water weird instead of binding it. The creature breaks the druids' control entirely, escaping the tributary and surging into the main flow of the Chionthar — now a more powerful and *more directly threatening* hazard to river traffic, including [the Consortium's barge convoys](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md). The circle suffers two casualties; Elder Elion is wounded. The Grove begins to discuss, for the first time, calling on outside help — possibly Hellrider, possibly Harper, possibly the wandering druid the Hinterlands dossier names.
