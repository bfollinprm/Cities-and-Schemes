# Hunt the River Horror

[Elder Elion](../../people/elder-elion.md) and his Circle, having to clean up [their own week-4 binding ritual that supercharged the water weird instead of containing it](../../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_4/binding-the-blight.md), now actively hunt the elemental they accidentally birthed. The creature has grown larger and more aggressive in the open Chionthar — fishermen out of [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) report a skiff overturned downstream of [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md), two boatmen drowned, a stretch of bank "alive with the water." Elion's druids and rangers quietly take a flat-bottomed barge downriver in the morning and spend the day stalking a stretch of polluted shallows where the weird has reportedly fed. They do not catch it. The creature is faster, larger, and *angrier* than the binding suggested it would be. The Circle takes one casualty — a young druid swept under by a column of water that would not let her surface — and breaks off the hunt at dusk. The river-traffic threat is now public. The Circle's responsibility for it is *not* — yet. **The water-weird remains loose, hostile to river traffic, and growing**, and the city's downstream dock economy is starting to notice.
