# Ruin a Log Shipment

[Elder Elion](../../people/elder-elion.md) leads a night raid on the Western Gate logging camp at the [Whisperwood Copse](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/hinterlands/wood-of-sharp-teeth/README.md), intending to rot or warp the felled timber before it can leave the hinterlands. The Sembian mercenaries — anticipating reprisal after first blood — have set magical traps and alarms. The Circle is ambushed approaching the cut, takes wounds, and retreats without reaching the timber. Two druids are wounded; none captured.
