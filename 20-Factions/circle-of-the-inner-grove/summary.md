# Circle of the Inner Grove

A small, secretive druidic sect that has watched the Chionthar for generations. The aggressive logging and quarrying of the Decade of Reconstruction pushed them from passive guardians into active sabotage; in 1502 DR they are at open war with the [Western Gate Trading Company](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) and the city's most polluting guilds. Led by the wizened half-elf druid [Elder Elion](people/elion.md).

## Ideology

To protect the Chionthar River and the surrounding lands from the city's pollution and expansion. They believe any action — including ones that endanger human lives — is justified if it serves the balance of nature. The city is a parasite; their job is the fever that fights the infection.

## Membership

A small circle of druids and rangers who meet in a hidden grove upriver from the city. Membership is closely vetted and the grove's location is a guarded secret. Elder Elion leads with quiet authority.

## Methods

- Eco-sabotage. Guiding dangerous wildlife toward logging camps, infesting tanneries with flesh-eating molds, magically spoiling industrial water supplies.
- Ritual warding. Week 0: Elion's Ritual of Warding was corrupted by the region's lingering infernal energy and produced fiendishly twisted animals that attacked loggers and druids alike — a costly own-goal that blighted the wood it was meant to protect.
- Active resistance against [Western Gate](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) logging. Week 1: skirmishes with Sembian mercenaries hired by Matron Silvershield in the Whisperwood Copse — a wounded druid, two wounded mercenaries.
- Inflicting industrial cost on the [Worshipful Company of Butchers & Tanners](../wc-butchers-tanners/summary.md) for tannery runoff into Chionthar tributaries.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** The [Western Gate Trading Company](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) — primary adversary, with mercenary force now deployed against them. The [Worshipful Company of Masons & Sculptors](../wc-masons-sculptors/summary.md) for quarrying. The [Worshipful Company of Butchers & Tanners](../wc-butchers-tanners/summary.md) for runoff. The [Chionthar Consortium](../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) for river-trade pollution.

**Allies of opportunity.** The [Free Traders of the Outskirts](../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md), who share an interest in restraining Western Gate aggression on Outer City roads.
