# Elder Elion

Wizened half-elf druid, leader of the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../summary.md). Elder Elion has watched the Chionthar for centuries and has reluctantly authorized the shift from passive guardianship to active eco-sabotage.

## Manifesto

> The city is a parasite. It drinks the river, carves the earth, and exhales a poison breath. It forgets that the stone it builds with and the water it drinks are not its property. They are the blood and bone of the world. We are the fever the world has created to fight this infection.

## Role

Elion personally leads the Circle's Rituals of Warding — including the corrupted week-0 ritual that produced fiendish animals. He has authorized direct action against the [Western Gate's](../../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) logging crews and is currently coordinating skirmish defense in the Whisperwood Copse against Sembian mercenaries hired by Matron Silvershield.
