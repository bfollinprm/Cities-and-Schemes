# Lady Emmeline Vhage

Founder and leader of [The Plague of Remembrance](../summary.md). A patriar noblewoman whose son — a Flaming Fist soldier killed during the Absolute Crisis — became the foundation of a xenophobic crusade.

## Manifesto

> They call this a city reborn. They are wrong. It is a city infected. We offered shelter to the survivors of a fallen city, and they brought its rot with them. Their despair is a plague, their grief a shadow that chokes our streets. Pity is a poison that weakens the blood of Baldur's Gate. We will be the surgeons of our own fate.

## Backstory

Vhage's son died in the chaos of the Absolute Crisis, a death she irrationally blames on the disorder caused by the refugee influx. Consumed by grief and hatred, she gathered other nativist patriars and downtrodden citizens whose fears she could shape into holy crusade. She maintains social respectability in Upper City salons; the cult's operations are run through trusted agents like the now-exposed **Zora**.
