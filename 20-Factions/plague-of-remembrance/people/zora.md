# Zora

Senior field operative of the [Plague of Remembrance](../summary.md) and one of [Lady Emmeline Vhage's](emmeline-vhage.md) most capable agents. Posed in [New Elturel](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) as a charismatic tiefling preacher inside the radical wing of the [Followers of the Phoenix](../../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md), grooming the paladin [Jaredith Heartsong](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) into a martyr whose death would force the Flaming Fist to massacre the refugee crowd and ignite the civil war Vhage wants.

## Operational profile

- Human mage with high-tier prepared casting: *Cone of Cold*, *Bless* (concentration), *Absorb Elements*, *Shield*, teleport extraction.
- Skilled at narrative reframing under pressure — turned [Bror's public accusation at the Phoenix Pits](../../party/activity/week_2/expose-zora-at-the-phoenix-pits.md) into an "ember cast" duel that protected her cover.
- Carries cult tokens shaped as a **cracked vial** — the same sigil [the Plague-Father's lab](../../party/activity/week_1/destroy-the-plague-fathers-laboratory.md) used.

## Recent state

- Phoenix Pits cover blown to the party; not exposed publicly.
- [Watchtower ambush](../../party/activity/week_2/survive-the-watchtower-ambush.md) on the cliffs near New Elturel failed; *Dissonant Whispers* compelled her to teleport away.
- [Extracted from the city by Vhage](../activity/week_2/silence-and-slander.md) through New Elturel and Wyrm's Crossing after the failure.
- Now an active case file at the [Sorcerous Sodality](../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_2/trace-the-arcane-signature.md), unlicensed-magic charge.
