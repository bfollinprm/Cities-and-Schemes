# The Plague of Remembrance

A xenophobic death cult of Baldurian purists, founded and led by [Lady Emmeline Vhage](people/emmeline-vhage.md), a grieving patriar noblewoman who blames the influx of Elturian refugees for the death of her son in the Absolute Crisis. The cult's strategic objective: trigger a civil war by framing the refugees of [New Elturel](../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) for atrocities, justifying their mass expulsion or worse.

## Ideology

The "soul" of Baldur's Gate is being poisoned by the "foreign humors" of the Elturian survivors. Refugees are not grateful survivors but a "plague of grief" whose despair is a tangible corrupting force. The city must perform a "civic exorcism" — cut out the diseased tissue and cauterize the wound.

## Membership

A mix of bitter old-money patriars from the [Peerage of Blood](../peerage-of-blood/summary.md), and angry working-class Baldurians convinced their livelihoods have been stolen. Pet mages who can conjure minor plagues. Field operatives — including the agent **Zora**, exposed at the Phoenix Pits in week 6.

## Methods

- Cultists disguised as Hellriders or refugees committing heinous murders. Active threat through the campaign — the party delivered a captured Plague cultist to High Watcher Alena in week 5 specifically as an intelligence asset against the cult.
- Lady Vhage's social-circle whisper campaigns. Week 0: a sustained rumor campaign blaming refugees for rising crime and disease in Lower City taverns and Upper City salons.
- Pet-mage minor plague conjuration emanating from the refugee camps.
- Bribed press coverage. The [Baldur's Mouth](../baldurs-mouth/summary.md) hit piece on the [Worshipful Company of Alchemists & Apothecaries](../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/summary.md) for "antitoxin hoarding" is suspected to be Plague-funded — psychological warfare to inflame class tensions.
- Embedded agents in refugee subcultures. Zora preached at the Phoenix Pits and used magical deception to escalate Jaredith's radicalism — the party exposed her in week 6, but she escaped to attempt the Watchtower Ambush in week 7. Ambush failed; Zora's status uncertain.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** The [Hellriders of New Elturel](../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) and the entire refugee population — the targets. The [Followers of the Phoenix](../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md), whom the Plague specifically tried to weaponize via Zora and failed. The [Guild](../guild/summary.md) and [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md) by virtue of preferring stability to civil war. The party, post-Phoenix-Pits.

**Allies of opportunity.** Sympathetic patriars from the [Patriar Heritage Society](../patriar-heritage-society/summary.md). The [Society of Baldurian Integrity](../society-of-baldurian-integrity/summary.md), with which membership occasionally overlaps.
