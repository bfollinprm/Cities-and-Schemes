# Plant 'Evidence' of a Refugee Murder Plot

[Lady Emmeline Vhage's](../../people/emmeline-vhage.md) cell murders a Baldurian merchant and tries to plant a forged letter implicating the [Hellriders](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) in the killing. A wandering Watch patrol stumbles onto the scene before the letter goes onto the body; the cultists flee. The death is filed as a robbery and [Ettvard Needle](../../../baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md), already paid for the matching front-page splash, has no "evidence" to print and shelves the story — pocketing Vhage's gold and earning her quiet, durable fury.
