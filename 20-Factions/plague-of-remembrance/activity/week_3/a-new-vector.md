# A New Vector

Down a [destroyed laboratory](../../../party/activity/week_1/destroy-the-plague-fathers-laboratory.md), a [dead frame-up of the Free Traders](../week_2/silence-and-slander.md), and a mage who teleported out of the Outer City under [Sodality scrutiny](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_3/follow-the-resonance.md), [Lady Emmeline Vhage](../../people/emmeline-vhage.md) pivots the cell to a quieter approach: contaminate a grain shipment bound for the Dawnmarket in [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) and seed a fresh outbreak that will discredit [the Hellriders'](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) refugee feeding effort while echoing the original Sewer Plague brief. The plan dies at the gate. A Hellrider grain inspector trained to look for Plague-Father reagent traces after the [Sewer Plague at the Worm Spit](../../../party/activity/week_1/destroy-the-plague-fathers-laboratory.md) flags the bags as contaminated; the shipment is seized and burned at the Dawnmarket, publicly. [High Watcher Alena](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) pockets the win. Vhage has now lost a vector, exposed a Plague link to anyone watching, and burned significant capital — and [Zora](../../people/zora.md) is still missing.
