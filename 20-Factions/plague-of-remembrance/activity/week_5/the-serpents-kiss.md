# The Serpent's Kiss

[Lady Emmeline Vhage](../../people/emmeline-vhage.md), running [her turned Hellrider officer inside Alena's command tent](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_4/the-rot-from-within.md), attempts a second, sharper move: a Plague agent trained as a courtesan is tasked with seducing a senior Hellrider officer at [the High Rider's Stand](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md), drugging him with a Plague-of-Remembrance "sleeping draught" laced with a memory-erasing tincture, and walking out with the alliance dispositions [signed with Mad Meggan's barrel-head signature this week](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md). The officer is a New Elturel veteran. He notices the wax seal on the vial does not match a Calimite apothecary's standard — a tiny, particular thing — and *bashes the vial against the wall* before the agent can pour. The agent runs. Hellrider patrols give chase across two New Elturel rooftops; she escapes the district but loses her cover and a length of the Plague's encoded pass-phrase. **High Watcher Alena now knows the cult has agents inside her command tent.** Vhage's pre-existing turned officer is, for the moment, undetected. Both sides have just tightened.
