# The Refugee Riot

[Lady Emmeline Vhage](../../people/emmeline-vhage.md) builds on [last week's false-flag](../../activity/week_6/the-false-flag.md) at the Sow & Piglet by upgrading the operation to a **full breadline massacre**. Her cultists, dressed in counterfeit Flaming Fist armor lifted from [the Officers' Mess bribery network](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_6/the-officers-mess.md), attack a refugee bread-line at the Sow & Piglet at sundown of the fifth day. Eleven refugees are killed; thirty more are wounded in the panic-crush.

The hungry, terrorized Elturian refugees riot exactly as Vhage planned: the morning *Baldur's Mouth* (now firmly Consortium-aligned via [the Puppet Press](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_7/the-puppet-press.md)) calls it the "**Sow & Piglet Refugee Riot**" with no mention of the Fist-uniform attackers. [The Council's Break the Buffer raid](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_7/break-the-buffer.md) has primed Eastway tempers; Vhage's rifle-shot lands in the dry tinder. The Hellriders confirm the cultists were not Fist by sundown of the next day, but *the riot has already happened*: the Outer City has lost eleven of its own to a uniform that wasn't real, and **civil war is one provocation closer**.
