# The False Flag

Exposed in New Elturel after [last week's seduction-ploy on a Hellrider officer collapsed publicly](../../activity/week_5/the-serpents-kiss.md), [Lady Emmeline Vhage](../../people/emmeline-vhage.md) shifts tactics. She pays a half-dozen Eastway thugs — out-of-work dock-toughs, two of them recent Plague-cult recruits — to **dress as Elturian refugees** (homespun, the crimson Phoenix sash, soot on the cheekbones) and stage a violent attack on the Sow & Piglet, a Baldurian working-class tavern in the Outer City. The thugs smash three patrons unconscious, set a table on fire, scream invented Phoenix-cult slogans, and disappear into the night before the Watch arrives.

The bait works. By morning the Baldur's Mouth has the story (sketched, not staged this time, since [Ettvard Needle is busy with the bigger Siege Edition](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_6/the-siege-edition.md), but his junior journalists run with it on page three); the Outer City Baldurian wards demand the Flaming Fist crack down on the "violent" Phoenix refugees. Marshal Flint is already politically [tied up by the Officer's Mess](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_6/the-officers-dilemma.md) and cannot easily refuse. **The Plague has manufactured the riot it wanted.** [The Phoenix temple-construction crowd](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_6/the-foundation-stone.md), already radicalized, will spend the next week trying not to be lynched for a thing they did not do. Lady Vhage is, for the first time in three weeks, actually winning.
