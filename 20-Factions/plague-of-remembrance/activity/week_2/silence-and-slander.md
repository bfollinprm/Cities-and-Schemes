# Silence and Slander

After the [Phoenix Pits exposure](../../../party/activity/week_2/expose-zora-at-the-phoenix-pits.md), the [watchtower ambush failure](../../../party/activity/week_2/survive-the-watchtower-ambush.md), and [Jaredith's deconversion](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_2/the-call-for-martyrs.md), [Lady Emmeline Vhage](../../people/emmeline-vhage.md) extracts senior agent **Zora** from the city by teleport-and-courier through [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) and [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md), preserving operational security on her cover. The cult also amplifies [the Bhaalist murder-frame on the Free Traders](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_2/a-murder-in-the-crowd.md) through whisper networks across [Heapside](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) and [Tumbledown](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md). The Plague's New Elturel cell is rolled up; their Lower City whisper-network survives.
