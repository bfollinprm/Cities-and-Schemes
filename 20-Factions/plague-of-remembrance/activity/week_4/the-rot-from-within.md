# The Rot from Within

[Lady Emmeline Vhage](../../people/emmeline-vhage.md), unable to break the Hellriders with the [Sewer-Plague-flagged contaminated grain](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_3/a-new-vector.md) or [last week's external slander](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_3/a-new-vector.md), changes tactics. She instructs her agents to identify a *disillusioned* Hellrider officer — one bitter over [the Council's collapsed citizenship negotiations](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_3/the-negotiations-price.md) — and approach him with promises of power, revenge, and a quiet way to retire to the Outer City. The approach is made at [the High Rider's Stand](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) the same week [Alena calls the muster](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_4/the-call-to-arms.md). The officer accepts coin in earnest. The cell now has a turned ear inside the Hellrider command tent — exactly when it can do the most damage.
