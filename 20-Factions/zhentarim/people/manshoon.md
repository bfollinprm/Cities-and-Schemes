# Manshoon

A clone of the immortal Zhentarim founder. Arrived in Waterdeep c. 1491 DR; the **Zhentarim Schism** dates from his arrival. Runs a brutal purist faction split from the modern Black Network — opposed to the profit-first **Doom Raiders** under Davil Starsong, openly at war with the **Xanathar Guild** in the Dock and Trades Wards.

## The play

Manshoon does not want to take Waterdeep by storm. He wants the chair. The route is corruption of the [Masked Lords](../../masked-lords/summary.md), not the destruction of them.

- **Two votes already.** [Corylus Thann](../../masked-lords/people/corylus-thann.md) and [Jelenn Urmbrusk](../../masked-lords/people/jelenn-urmbrusk.md) are blackmailed; they vote as required.
- **One alchemist being turned.** Skeemo Weirdbottle of the Doom Raiders is being walked across the line.
- **The Shadow War as cover.** Public street violence with the Xanathar Guild keeps the Watch and the Magisters busy, the council distracted, and the city's appetite for "stability" growing — which Manshoon intends to satisfy from the Open Lord's chair.

## Methods

Bribery, blackmail, brutal proportionate reprisals. Where the Doom Raiders run discreet smuggling and money-lending, Manshoon's faction trades on fear, executes informants in public, and incites street violence. His clones are persistent: killing him is theater, not victory.
