# Roah Moonglow

Local overseer of the [Zhentarim](../summary.md) Black Network in Baldur's Gate. A long-time Zhent trader with the discipline to compartmentalize her cells and the appetite to push them hard against [The Guild's](../../guild/summary.md) territory.

## Manifesto

> Honor? Justice? Tradition? These are the fairy tales the powerful tell you to keep you poor. There is only one truth: everything has a price. Loyalty can be bought. Fear can be sold. Power is a commodity, and we are its most aggressive traders. While The Guild plays at being the people's champion, we are honest about what we are.

## Role

Moonglow runs the local Zhent operation through compartmentalized cells of assassins and enforcers. Her aggressive Brampton expansion — burning a Guild-protected tavern, establishing toll roadblocks outside the city, undermining Nine-Fingers Keene's claim to be the only protection that matters — provoked the inevitable Guild counter-strike. The Velvet Hand safehouse was destroyed in The Guild's week-6 smokepowder operation; her current status is unconfirmed but operations have not visibly stopped.
