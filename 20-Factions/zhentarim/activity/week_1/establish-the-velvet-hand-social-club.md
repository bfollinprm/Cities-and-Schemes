# Establish the 'Velvet Hand' Social Club

[Roah Moonglow's](../../people/roah-moonglow.md) agents seat a new gambling-and-loan-sharking parlor — the **Velvet Hand** — directly across from the ashes of the Guild-protected tavern they burned in week 0. The placement is the message: the [Zhentarim](../../summary.md) is not visiting [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md), it is moving in. The gambit dares [The Guild](../../../guild/summary.md) to escalate — a dare Tusgront's door-to-door tour later in the week answers without a single thrown punch.
