# The Smokepowder Plot

[Roah Moonglow](../../people/roah-moonglow.md), faced with [Vex's bloody raid on the Velvet Hand](../../../guild/activity/week_4/retaliation.md), green-lights a much harder counter-strike: smuggle a payload of Lantaner smokepowder through the Coast Way pipeline and use it on the Guild's Brampton headquarters. The plot collapses before it begins. An informant inside the Black Network's smuggling chain — most plausibly recruited during [the Brampton recruitment ambush](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_3/an-offer-you-cant-refuse.md) — tips [Vex's enforcers](../../../guild/people/vex.md), who set their own ambush at the handoff point and seize the entire shipment intact. Roah loses the smokepowder, loses the operatives carrying it, and — worst of all — has just disclosed her intent. The Guild now possesses both an unambiguous casus belli **and** a stockpile of explosives. Roah retreats to the Velvet Hand to count what is still standing.
