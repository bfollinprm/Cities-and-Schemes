# The Ledger Logic

Capitalizing on [last week's Counting House infiltration](../../activity/week_5/the-financial-front.md), [Roah Moonglow's](../../people/roah-moonglow.md) Zhentarim ledger-clerks subtly **alter the tax records of several key Guild-aligned businesses** — small adjustments to declared income, missed quarterly filings, recategorized warehouse stock. Within forty-eight hours the falsified data triggers automated audits and asset freezes by the city's tax-and-bond authority, exactly as Roah designed. Three Guild-front laundries are frozen, two Guild-aligned warehouses are sealed pending audit, and a popular Brampton tavern owned-of-record by [a Guild lieutenant](../../../guild/people/farlin.md) loses its liquor license overnight.

The strike is bloodless and devastating. Roah hits [Nine-Fingers Keene's](../../../guild/people/astele-keene.md) finances exactly where they hurt without firing a crossbow bolt or losing a cipher to the city's prison. The Guild loses an estimated 8,000 gp in working capital before the Velvet Hand smoke even clears, and Nine-Fingers' [own internal forensic audit](../../../guild/activity/week_6/the-financial-audit.md) burns time and good will inside her own organization, hunting for the slicer that did it. **Roah is winning the Lower City paper war while Wharf Rats torch one of her warehouses.** [The Wharf Rats counter-punch](../../../wharf-rats/activity/week_6/the-counter-punch.md) costs her four thousand in product. The Guild's frozen ledgers cost Nine-Fingers eight. The Zhentarim ends the week ahead.
