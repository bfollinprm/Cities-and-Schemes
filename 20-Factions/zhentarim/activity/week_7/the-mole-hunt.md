# The Mole Hunt

[Roah Moonglow](../../people/roah-moonglow.md) — losing six ledger-clerks to [the Guild's Digital Purge](../../../guild/activity/week_7/the-digital-purge.md) and three more Counting House assets to [the Wharf Rats' sewer skirmish](../../../wharf-rats/activity/week_7/the-sewer-skirmish.md) — runs **two parallel operations in the same tenday**: a feint and a real hunt. The feint: a fresh round of altered Guild tax records to keep Nine-Fingers Keene's auditors busy and her assassins distracted. The hunt: dispatch Roah's two best interrogators to **the High Rider's Stand** in New Elturel to find and *extract* the compromised Bhaalist mole the party's [poisoned Reaper](../../../takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_6/the-prison-silence.md) revealed last tenday — *before the party finds the same mole.*

The interrogators reach the Stand in time to witness [Alena's witch-hunt](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_7/the-witch-hunt.md) in progress. They identify the cook Alena has cleared as the actual mole within an hour (the cook's eye-contact pattern is a Bhaalist tell), and abduct him from a side-street alley after his shift. By week's end the cook is dead in a Heapside cellar after a night of Zhent interrogation; his body is left in a Hellrider supply wagon as a *demonstration to Alena that the Zhentarim got there first*. **Roah has out-played both the Guild and the Hellriders in the same tenday.** Whether the Hellriders interpret the gift as friendly or hostile is going to be a question for next tenday.
