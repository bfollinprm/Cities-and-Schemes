# Consolidate the East End

With the [Flaming Fist warrant on the party](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_2/plan-the-unthinkable.md) freshly logged, [Roah Moonglow](../../people/roah-moonglow.md) dispatches an agent into [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) with a pitch: erase the Fist warrant in exchange for the party's services to the Black Network. The pitch lands as a parallel offer to [Vex's open Guild break with the party](../../../guild/activity/week_2/retaliation-on-the-river.md). Concurrently, the [Velvet Hand](../../activity/week_1/establish-the-velvet-hand-social-club.md) seats a second floor of high-stakes loan-sharking; the Zhentarim's east-end footprint expands materially. Finn, working alone, has lifted **a waterproof map of the smuggling tunnels under the Velvet Hand** and the party is now sitting on it without telling anyone.
