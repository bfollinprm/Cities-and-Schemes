# An Offer You Can't Refuse

[Roah Moonglow](../../people/roah-moonglow.md) sends a Zhentarim recruiter into [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) to peel the Brampton Boys — Guild-affiliated street muscle — into the Black Network's pay, leveraging the [Velvet Hand expansion of week 2](../activity/week_2/consolidate-the-east-end.md) as proof that the Zhentarim are the rising power in the east end. The Brampton Boys prove more loyal — or more frightened of [the Guild](../../../guild/activity/week_3/recover-the-asset.md) — than Roah anticipated. They take the meet, smile through the pitch, refuse outright, and immediately walk the contact straight to their Guild Ward Boss. [Vex Corvin](../../../guild/people/vex.md) coordinates an ambush on the Zhentarim agent the next night; the agent is eliminated and a small intelligence haul on Roah's east-end recruitment list comes back with him. The Black Network's east-end recruitment program is set back significantly. The same week, Roah is also [sending paid Zhent muscle to Factor Errinthal at the docks](../../../wharf-rats/activity/week_3/the-solidarity-tax.md), opening a quietly profitable second front the Guild has not yet noticed.
