# The Financial Front

[Roah Moonglow](../../people/roah-moonglow.md), with [the smokepowder cache lost to the Guild](../../../guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md) and [the Velvet Hand sitting on top of an unknown amount of buried sabotage](../../../guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md), shifts the front. Steel has not worked. Coin will. Black Network agents fan out into the [Counting House](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/counting-house.md) — not the vaults, the *clerks' rooms*. Forged endorsements appear on Guild-fronted ledger entries; a respectable Brampton wine-importer's books are quietly altered to show a trade with a non-existent Zhent supplier; two of the [Goldsmiths' chasing-ghosts decoy ledgers](../../../wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_5/chasing-ghosts.md) are augmented by Zhent hands so that a Guild-aligned moneylender appears to have laundered the party's 6,000 gp. By week's end the Counting House is opening Guild commercial files and finding *bookkeeping inconsistencies* it cannot quickly explain. The Guild's front-businesses — taverns, fences, lamp-oil monopolies — are quietly under audit. Roah has not killed Nine-Fingers. **She has put a tax-collector in her pocket.**
