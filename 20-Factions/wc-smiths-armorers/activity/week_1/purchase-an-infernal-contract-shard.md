# Purchase an 'Infernal Contract' Shard

[Dammon](../../people/dammon.md) follows up a [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) rumor of a tiefling carrying a physical shard of an Avernus-era devil's contract — the script half-burned into the matter when Elturel fell. He buys it; the seller is grateful for the coin. Dammon believes the inscription can be folded into his forge-work, and the purchase puts him in private contact with cultic and pact buyers who would have wanted the same shard.
