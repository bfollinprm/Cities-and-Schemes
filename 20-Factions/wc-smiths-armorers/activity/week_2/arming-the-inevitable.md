# Arming the Inevitable

[Master Tor-keg Silverhand](../../people/tor-keg-silverhand.md) signs a formal multi-guild petition — co-signed by the Masons, Cartographers, Goldsmiths, and Wharf Rats — to the [Council of Four](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_2/contain-the-crises.md) demanding immediate action on the [Basilisk Gate siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md), citing collapsing supply of raw iron and stone into the city. The Smiths' petition lands the same day the Council censures Marshal Flint. The Forge of the Nine begins quietly stockpiling finished arms in the [Seatower](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/seatower.md) cellars on the assumption that the Council will eventually break and authorize a Fist storming raid.
