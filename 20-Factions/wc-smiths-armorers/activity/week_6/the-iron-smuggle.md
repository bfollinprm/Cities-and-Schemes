# The Iron Smuggle

Using the blockade-bypass exemption [the Smiths petitioned for last week](../../activity/week_5/an-appeal-to-the-old-guard.md) — or perhaps a forged Council seal that looks like the exemption — [Master Tor-keg Silverhand](../../people/tor-keg-silverhand.md) runs a massive shipment of raw iron through the [Basilisk Gate checkpoint](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_6/rationing-the-hope.md) under the polite fiction of "civic defense materials." Six wagons rolling at dawn under guild colors, paperwork stamped by a Council clerk who used to drink at the Smiths' guildhall before being posted to the gate.

In reality the iron is for the Smiths' own forges. With the siege threatening to stretch into a long blockade, Silverhand is **stockpiling material to keep guild forges hot through whatever comes next**. Some of the stockpile will go to the Council and the Sembian mercenaries; some, quietly, to the [Hellrider militia at Eastway](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_6/the-shield-wall.md); some, more quietly, to a Lantaner contract paid in [Cogwork Quay scrip](../../../lantaner-concession/activity/week_6/the-prototype-assembly.md). Silverhand will sell to whichever side is winning come month's end, and is determined to be the only smith with iron left to sell. The Free Traders catch wind by week-end and quietly approach the guild for blockade-side rates.
