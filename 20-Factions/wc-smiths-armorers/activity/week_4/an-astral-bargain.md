# An Astral Bargain

[Master Tor-keg Silverhand](../../people/tor-keg-silverhand.md), reading [the public welcoming of the K'liir contingent into Wyrm's Rock](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_4/an-alliance-of-steel-and-silver.md) as a forge-supply opportunity, requests a private audience with [Commander K'laar](../../../unchained/people/k-laar.md) at the fortress. He brings a respectful proposal: Worshipful Company smithing services — repairs, custom fittings, replacement strappings — for the githyanki contingent, at a smith-to-soldier discount. K'laar listens. K'laar takes offense. The githyanki view their astral steel as *fundamentally superior* to anything mortal forges produce; she reads the offer as the same crude condescension as a stable hand offering to shoe a unicorn. Tor-keg is curtly dismissed from the audience hall. The Worshipful Company is now formally on K'laar's *contempt* list, and the alliance Flint just made with the Unchained will not buy the Smiths a single contract.
