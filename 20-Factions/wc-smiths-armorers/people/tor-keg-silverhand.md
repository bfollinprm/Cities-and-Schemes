# Master Tor-keg Silverhand

Master of the [Worshipful Company of Smiths & Armorers](../summary.md). A respected dwarven master smith, conservative in temperament and reliably ruthless in trade negotiation.

## Manifesto

> Peace is a lie told between wars. Strength is not found in laws or coin, but in the shield that turns a blade and the sword that holds the line. We are the heart of this city's strength. We arm the Fist that guards the walls and the adventurer that clears the sewers.

## Role

Silverhand presides over a Court of Assistants that includes Dammon of the Forge of the Nine — the city's leading expert on infernal-iron metallurgy. Together they pursue Lantaner smokepowder secrets through every channel short of open warfare. Silverhand also brokers the major arms-supply contracts with the [Flaming Fist](../../flaming-fist/summary.md) and the city's Kontor Guards.
