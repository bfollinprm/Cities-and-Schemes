# The Great Retraction

[Factor Errinthal](../../people/factor-errinthal.md) — humiliated by the [Siege Edition](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_6/the-siege-edition.md) and bleeding river volume to [the Amnian river-blockade](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_7/consolidate-river-control.md) — uses Ettvard Needle's absence to push a sweeping editorial **retraction** through the [puppeted *Baldur's Mouth*](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_7/the-puppet-press.md). The retraction column claims Kaelan's *Insurance Scam* was "based on falsified ledgers planted by the Zhentarim," names no Consortium principals, and apologizes for the "regrettable distress" caused to Lower City families.

The gambit is a gaslight: the Consortium's actual records are still in [Coster Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) where the party copied them. But the public copy of the *original* exposé is being [bought up by Nansi Gretta](../../../bibliophile/activity/week_7/the-missing-ink.md), the new Mouth will not reprint it, and the only voice still naming the Consortium for what it did is an oral one. From [Kostner Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md), Errinthal moves to recover the Consortium's standing with the patriar bench by week's end.
