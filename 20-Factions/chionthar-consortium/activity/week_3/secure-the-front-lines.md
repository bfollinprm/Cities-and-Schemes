# Secure the Front Lines

Reeling from [last week's assassination of a Consortium factor](../../../gilded-vein/activity/week_2/accelerate-the-crash.md) and the demonstrable unreliability of the conscripted adventurers (the party, who [walked the medicine cart in week 1](../../../party/activity/week_1/heist-the-rivington-medicine-cart.md) and [vanished after week 2](../../../party/activity/week_2/take-vex-circus-assignment.md)), [Factor Errinthal](../../people/factor-errinthal.md) hires a squad of seasoned, [Guild](../../../guild/summary.md)-adjacent enforcers to harden the [Chionthar warehouses at Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md). The same week, he authorizes [Roah Moonglow's Zhent muscle](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_3/an-offer-you-cant-refuse.md) to break [the Wharf Rats' solidarity tax](../../../wharf-rats/activity/week_3/the-solidarity-tax.md) on his piers. He opens a paranoid internal investigation into the factor's murder; his investigators read the planted seals exactly as [the Amnian Kontor](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_3/identify-the-assassins-patron.md) does and conclude **Amn** is responsible. Errinthal is now arming himself to fight the wrong rival, with security paid for by a Black Network the Guild thinks is his enemy.
