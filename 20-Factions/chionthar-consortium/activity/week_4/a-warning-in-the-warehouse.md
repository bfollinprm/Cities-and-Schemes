# A Warning in the Warehouse

[Factor Errinthal's](../../people/factor-errinthal.md) attempt at a "subtle warning" to [the Amnian Kontor](../../../amnian-kontor/summary.md) — Consortium enforcers staging an accident with a consignment of Amnian-flagged crates in [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) — goes disastrously wrong. The crew is sloppy. A vigilant [Watch](../../../watch/summary.md) patrol turning a corner at the wrong moment witnesses the *deliberate* drop and arrests the perpetrators on destruction-of-property charges. The footage of Consortium toughs being marched off in irons feeds [the Baldur's Mouth's](../../../baldurs-mouth/summary.md) Lower City correspondents within hours. What was meant to be a private signal escalates into a *criminal matter*: the Amnian Kontor has its first significant legal advantage in the trade war it cannot afford to fight openly, and Errinthal has just confirmed for [the Watch](../../../watch/summary.md) that the Consortium's enforcer arm is willing to stage incidents on the dock. [The week's separate Shipwrights' "crane malfunction" sabotage](../../../wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_4/a-crane-malfunction.md) is now read in light of this misstep.
