# The Public Patrons

[Factor Errinthal](../../people/factor-errinthal.md) hands the [adventurers his Consortium publicly conscripted in week 1](../../../party/activity/week_1/be-co-opted-by-the-chionthar-consortium.md) their first formal assignment — and the party [walks into the Mad Meggan deal at the Basilisk Gate](../../../party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md) instead, defying both the [Flaming Fist's order](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_2/plan-the-unthinkable.md) and the implicit Consortium expectation that the party stay in Errinthal's column. The party is now publicly marked as unreliable on the Consortium ledger; Errinthal's distrust hardens. Concurrently, [Lord Perrine Hhune's](../../../elturel-remembrance-society/people/perrine-hhune.md) [warehouse-break attempt](../../../elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_2/infernal-residue.md) gives the Consortium a free win in the press it badly needed.
