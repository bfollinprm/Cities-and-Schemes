# Impound an Independent Vessel

To enforce the new Harbor Dredging Levy, the Consortium leans on the [Flaming Fist](../../../flaming-fist/summary.md) to impound a [Free Trader](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) vessel belonging to ['Mad' Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) for non-payment. The seizure happens in daylight at [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md), making an example for every independent captain on the river — and lighting the fuse for the Free Trader blockade later in the week.
