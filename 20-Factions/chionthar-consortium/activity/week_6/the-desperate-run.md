# The Desperate Run

With [the Consortium's barges seized](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_5/retaliatory-asset-seizure.md), [the Undercity gold cache stolen](../../activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md), and [the Amnian Kontor now openly buying out his struggling subsidiaries](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_6/the-hostile-takeover.md), [Factor Errinthal](../../people/factor-errinthal.md) takes a gamble he would have refused six months ago. He hires a crew of notorious independent smugglers — not [River-Rats](../../../river-rats/summary.md), not [Wharf Rats](../../../wharf-rats/summary.md), but a cell of free-agents working out of Wyrm's Crossing — to run a shipment of illicit goods (cut Sembian wine, untaxed Calishite spice, two crates of pre-licensure spellgreen) past the Amnian river blockade after dark, hoping a fast injection of black-market coin can save his payroll for the week.

The run goes wrong inside ninety minutes. The Amnian patrols are *waiting* — Athar's intelligence cell has had eyes on the Wyrm's Crossing dockmaster for weeks — and the smuggler skiffs are intercepted between Wyrm's Crossing and the Lower City quays. The cargo is impounded, the smugglers scatter into the night, and one boatswain drowns trying to swim shore in his armor. **The shipment is lost. Errinthal has spent his last clandestine reserve on nothing.** By dawn the Counting House gossip mill has the bones of the story and is laughing about them. Errinthal's only remaining play is the [partnership with Mad Meggan he has been quietly funding all along](../../../party/activity/week_5/errinthals-relief-caravan-pr-coup.md) — and the party already knows.
