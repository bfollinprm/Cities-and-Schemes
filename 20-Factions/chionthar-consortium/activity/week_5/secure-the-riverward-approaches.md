# Secure the Riverward Approaches

[Factor Errinthal](../../people/factor-errinthal.md), still bleeding goodwill after [last week's Watch-witnessed warehouse "warning" mess](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_4/a-warning-in-the-warehouse.md) and [the Shipwrights' rigged crane "malfunction"](../../../wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_4/a-crane-malfunction.md), tries to fortify the Consortium's riverward flank by hiring [Silas the Eel's River-Rats](../../../river-rats/people/silas-the-eel.md) to relocate the reserve gold cache from a Brampton sub-cellar to a deeper [Undercity](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/undercity/README.md) hide. The crew is unreliable. Two of Silas's men, drunk in the Undercellar before the run, are heard naming the route by an Amnian spy planted at the bar two months ago. By the time Silas's lanterns reach the cache, [Amnian Coin-Swords have walked the cargo out the back-door of a different sluice](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_5/retaliatory-asset-seizure.md). The Consortium loses *the entire reserve gold position* — and discovers in the same morning that [their finest two river-barges have been seized at Gray Harbour under Amnian writ](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_5/retaliatory-asset-seizure.md). The Chionthar warehouses on Gray Harbour are now critically exposed. **The Consortium has been hit underground and on the docks in the same twelve hours.**
