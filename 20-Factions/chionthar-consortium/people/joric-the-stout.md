# Master Joric the Stout

A former river captain who rose to command the [Chionthar Consortium](../summary.md). The gruff, pragmatic, plainly-dressed face of native Baldurian merchant power.

## Manifesto

> The Chionthar is the artery of this city, and we are its heart. For generations, our families have worked these waters. We know the currents, the eddies, the dangers. Now, these foreign Kontors arrive with their foreign coin and foreign laws, thinking they can buy our river. They are wrong. The river is not for sale. We will not be supplanted in our own home. From the Gray Harbour to the Reaching Woods, this water belongs to Baldur's Gate.

## Role

In the hyper-competitive environment created by the establishment of the Foreign Kontors, the old, loosely-affiliated Baldurian merchant guilds found they could not compete. They were forced to consolidate, merging into larger, more aggressive Merchant Consortiums. The guilds controlling river trade, warehousing, and shipping became the **Chionthar Consortium** under Joric's leadership — a deliberately united front against the newcomers.

He sits in the [Peerage of Coin](../../peerage-of-coin/summary.md) and is widely regarded as the most reliable opposition voice to Amnian and Sembian agendas, even when his own bloc benefits from cutting deals with them.
