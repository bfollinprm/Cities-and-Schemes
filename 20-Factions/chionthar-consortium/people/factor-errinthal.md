# Factor Errinthal

A mid-rank factor of the [Chionthar Consortium](../summary.md). Errinthal moves with the bureaucratic confidence of a man who knows his Counting House line numbers cold. He travels with a personal security detail in river-barge livery — the same emblem the party clocked at the [Little Calimshan](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/little-calimshan.md) warehouse during the medicine heist.

## Role with the party

Errinthal intercepts the party leaving the High Rider's Stand in [Session 3](../../../50-Sessions/session_3/player_summary.md). He claims the stolen medicine had been earmarked for a Consortium PR play (true enough), prices the resulting "debt" in the thousands of gold, and threatens collection. Elia talks him into an elegant alternative: rebrand the rescue as Consortium-directed and recover the narrative. Errinthal, intrigued, accepts — the party is now "in Consortium employ" with debt to be worked off in service tasks.

He does not mention the **second cart**. The propaganda materials Elia stole are, as far as Errinthal knows, still inside the warehouse. They are not. (See [be-co-opted-by-the-chionthar-consortium](../../party/activity/week_1/be-co-opted-by-the-chionthar-consortium.md).)
