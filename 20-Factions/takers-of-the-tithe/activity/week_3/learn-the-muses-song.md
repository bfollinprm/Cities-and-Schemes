# Learn the Muse's Song

[The Collector](../../people/the-collector.md) puts a Spotter — silent, unremarkable, professionally patient — on [the Ember (Alfira)](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) for the full week. The brief is reconnaissance, not strike: learn the route between [her new Phoenix Gate temple site](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_3/the-first-sermon-at-the-phoenix-gate.md) and [Dammon's forge](../../../wc-smiths-armorers/people/dammon.md), her sleep schedule (she works late, alone, after the new converts disperse), her allies ([Jaredith Heartsong](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md), Dammon, the inner Followers ring), and the moments she is least covered. The Spotter logs every gap. The kill is not this week. The Collector wants to know precisely when in her routine the Ember can be removed — taken, not assassinated, the cult prefers — without producing a martyr. The reconnaissance is clean. By week's end the Takers know the Ember's habits better than the Followers do.
