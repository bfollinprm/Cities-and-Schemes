# Silence the Loose End

[The Collector](../../people/the-collector.md), faced with [a captured Reaper still alive in the New Elturel stockade after last week's failed sermon-stage assassination](../../../takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_4/the-reapers-gambit.md) and [the Ember's open mockery of the attempt](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_5/the-survivors-sermon.md), dispatches a second assassin to silence the prisoner. The infiltrator slips the [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) stockade walls in the small hours, kills two sentries quietly, and is *not* quiet enough for the Hellrider duty mastiff. The dog smells the cell-block before the assassin reaches it. A bloody melee in the lamplit corridor — short, brutal, half a dozen wounds traded — ends with the assassin escaping over the stockade roof with a torn-out throat to show for the night. The captured Reaper is alive, awake, and aware that **his own cult just tried to murder him in his cell**. By breakfast he is asking the Hellrider chaplain for parchment. The Tithe's most jealously guarded cult secrets are now twelve hours from being traded for a lighter sentence.
