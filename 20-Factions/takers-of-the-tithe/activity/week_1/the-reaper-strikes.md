# The Reaper Strikes

The Takers' "Reaper" attempts the cell's tithe-killing on master goldsmith **Elara Varrin**, a senior artisan loosely affiliated with the [Worshipful Company of Goldsmiths & Jewelers](../../../wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/summary.md). Varrin had — unusually — engaged a recently hired bodyguard. The ambush fails, the Reaper is wounded and forced to retreat. Varrin survives, sleeps in [Manorborn](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/manorborn.md) under guard, and now knows the cult exists. The cell owes [the Collector](../../people/the-collector.md) a tithe it cannot pay this week.
