# The Prison Silence

[The Collector](../../people/the-collector.md) cannot risk [the captured Reaper at the New Elturel stockade](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) talking — and after [last week's failed direct-assassination attempt was bloodied off by a Hellrider guard dog](../../activity/week_5/silence-the-loose-end.md), the cell pivots to method. A corrupt cook on the Hellrider supply-line is paid forty platinum and the promise of a clean death later to slip a packet of **necrotic poison** into the prisoner's bowl of evening rations. By the time the party arrives at the High Rider's Stand to interrogate him further, the Reaper is dead in his cell, foaming, the soul drawn out and **sealed in a polished obsidian gem the cook smuggles out in his apron pocket.**

The murder is invisible — the cook is hours back at his post by the time the Hellriders find the body — but the *fact of the murder* is not. Singa's cell has demonstrated that **the Hellrider stockade is not safe** and that the cult has logistical infiltration inside Alena's camp. The party catches the surviving "Spotter" cultist breaking under the demonstration before the same poison reaches the Spotter's bowl. The Spotter offers to lead the party to Singa's hideout on Dusthawk Hill in exchange for protection and a new identity. The Reaper's silence is purchased; the price is the cell's exposure to the party that very afternoon.
