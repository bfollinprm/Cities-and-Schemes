# The Grieving Genius

[The Collector](../../people/the-collector.md) personally surveils [the Ember](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) at the **Forge of the Nine** in the Lower City after her amplification of [Jaredith's protest-ending ballad](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_2/the-call-for-martyrs.md) at Wyrm's Crossing flags her as "exceptional." The Takers mark her as their next high-value tithe target. **Dammon** of the [Smiths' Company](../../../wc-smiths-armorers/summary.md), the Forge's master smith and the Ember's gate-keeper, is logged as the obstacle to handle first. No move yet; the surveillance continues.
