# The Reaper's Gambit

[The Collector's](../../people/the-collector.md) Reaper, dispatched after [the Spotter's full week of routine work](../../../takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_3/learn-the-muses-song.md), strikes at [the Ember](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) outside the [Phoenix Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md). The attack fails spectacularly. The Reaper underestimates the density and devotion of Alfira's followers — within seconds the assassin is mobbed, beaten down with prayer-staves and tankard-handles, and dragged unconscious into a refugee militia tent. The Hellriders take custody before the Watch arrives; **the cult now has a live captured Reaper sitting in a refugee militia hold**, with [Alena's interrogators](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) on the way. The Takers' existence — masked-cult artisan-killers — is no longer a rumor in the Watch's open files but a confirmation in the Hellriders' closed one. [The Ember turns the failed attack into the most-attended sermon of her ministry](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_4/a-martyrs-sermon.md) by the next morning.
