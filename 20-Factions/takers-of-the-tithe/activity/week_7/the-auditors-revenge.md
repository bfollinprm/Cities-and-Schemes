# The Auditor's Revenge

Furious over [the death of Singa](../../people/singa.md) and the [loss of the Tithe Ledger to the Skittering Beetle](../../people/the-skittering-beetle.md), [The Collector](../../people/the-collector.md) dispatches a **Hunter-Killer cell** of three Bhaalist assassins — a Reaper, a Spotter, and a Keeper of unusually high rank — into [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) with explicit orders to find and kill the party. The cell carries no surviving copy of the Ledger: the Beetle has it; they are operating on word-of-mouth descriptions from the [Spotter the party left alive](../../../../50-Sessions/session_25/player_summary.md), now a debriefing source under the Collector's knife.

The cell sets up a forward base at an abandoned tannery shed on the outer Rivington road and begins **shadowing the Plowman** from a distance. They identify Alfira convalescing in the back room within two days. They do not move on her — Singa's failure was *engaging the party near the Ember*; this Reaper has explicit orders to wait until the party splits again. By week's end they have logged Bror, Elia, and Finn departing for Tumbledown and are watching Kaelan's movements between the Plowman and Sow's Foot. **The party is being hunted and does not know it.**
