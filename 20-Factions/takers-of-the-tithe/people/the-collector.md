# The Collector

Founder and leader of [The Takers of the Tithe](../summary.md). Identity unknown — rumored to be a minor patriar with a family history steeped in necromancy, or a surviving priest from Myrkul's ancient clergy. Communicates with followers only through written messages delivered by skeletal messengers.

## Manifesto

> The city teems with life. A chaotic, squirming mass of flesh. But life is not a right; it is a debt owed to death. Most souls are a pittance, a copper coin tossed into a void. But some... some shine. The soul of a hero, a master artisan, a peerless musician. These are the platinum pieces. We are the Takers. We collect the tithe for a king long forgotten.

## Role

The Collector designs targets, dispatches three-cultist hunting cells, and maintains absolute compartmentalization between cells. The week-0 Goldsmiths' surveillance failure cost the cult a target but not, apparently, a cell — the Spotter's identity is uncovered but the Reaper and Keeper remain unknown.
