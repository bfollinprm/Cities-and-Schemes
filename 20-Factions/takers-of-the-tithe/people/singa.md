# Singa

The Bishop-tier cell leader of the Takers of the Tithe operating out of a hidden alcove on the north face of [Dusthawk Hill](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md). Tall, gaunt, sexless in presentation, dressed in the auditor's grey robe with a heavy ledger clasped at the belt and a scythe-quill in hand. Singa received target names directly through Bhaalist revelation and ran her cell as a methodical political-assassination program rather than a freelance murder-cult.

## Method

Singa styled herself an **Auditor of Death**. The cell's hideout was set up like a counting house — a long desk, stacks of bloody vellum ledgers, an iron-handled abacus etched with Bhaalist sigils — not a temple. Her working frame: every life is a debt; murder is *foreclosure*. The cell ran the standard Takers triad (Spotter, Reaper, Keeper), but Singa added the **Tithe Ledger** working — a literal physical book whose entries bound the named soul to Bhaal upon death. Forging the ledger was treated as theological crime. The cell drew its operational pattern from [The Collector's](the-collector.md) Bhaalist doctrine but ran with a degree of strategic autonomy unusual for a Bishop-level cell.

## Strategic intent

- **Civil war by martyrdom.** Singa intended to kill [Alfira (The Ember)](../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) in a way that implicated the Baldur's Gate government or the Flaming Fist, sparking a refugee riot that would burn the city from within.
- **Methodical target list.** The cell's [week-1 attempt on Master Elara Varrin](../activity/week_1/the-reaper-strikes.md), the [Phoenix Gate Reaper attempt of week 4](../activity/week_4/the-reapers-gambit.md), and [the silencing of the captured Reaper at the New Elturel stockade in week 6](../activity/week_6/the-prison-silence.md) are all Singa's work.
- **Compromised supply chain.** Her cell maintained a paid mole at the Hellrider supply line — a low-visibility cook in the High Rider's Stand kitchen, used to poison the captured Reaper before he could talk.

## Death

Singa was confronted at the Counting House sanctum atop Dusthawk Hill in [Session 26](../../../50-Sessions/session_26/player_summary.md). She used a soul-link to mirror damage onto the suspended Alfira and teleported around the room with her scythe-quill, but Bror grappled her into the obsidian pit with a satchel of smokepowder; Kaelan and Jaredith finished her with a *Fireball* and a *Chromatic Orb*. The Tithe Ledger was surrendered to the Skittering Beetle in trade for the party's lives.
