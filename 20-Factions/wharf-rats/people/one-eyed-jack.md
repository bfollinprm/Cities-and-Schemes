# One-Eyed Jack

Leader of the [Wharf Rats](../summary.md). Career longshoreman turned crew boss, double-affiliated with [The Guild](../../guild/summary.md) (operational) and the [Worshipful Company of Fishmongers](../../wc-fishmongers/summary.md) (union). Increasingly bitter about both after the failed week-0 strike and Matron Saltwind's subsequent levy-exemption deal that traded the Wharf Rats' leverage away.

## Role

Jack runs cargo skimming out of Gray Harbour and the small-craft transfer trade between the harbor and the Chionthar mouth. The week-0 Lantaner clockwork-parts skim was characteristic — small enough to hide in a damage report, valuable enough to clear weeks of crew pay. He has not yet decided whether the next strike action will go through Saltwind or around her.
