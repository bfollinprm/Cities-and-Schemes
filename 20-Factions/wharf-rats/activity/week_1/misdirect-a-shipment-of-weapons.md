# 'Misdirect' a Shipment of Weapons

[One-Eyed Jack](../../people/one-eyed-jack.md) and his Guild dockside contact arrange to divert a [Flaming Fist](../../../flaming-fist/summary.md) crossbow shipment moving through [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md). Security on the transport is unexpectedly tight; the dockside contact fails to create the opening. The shipment lands intact at the Fist barracks. The failure costs the Wharf Rats a useful inside man and signals that the [Guild's](../../../guild/summary.md) informant network on the docks may be compromised — or pre-empted.
