# Licking Their Wounds

[One-Eyed Jack](../../people/one-eyed-jack.md), what is left of him after [last week's Zhent gibbet on the Consortium pier](../../../wharf-rats/activity/week_4/the-pier-side-war.md), pulls his surviving crew off the docks and into a [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) safehouse over a brine-vat. The plan is simple — go *quiet*, lick the wounds, rebuild the crew slow. The plan does not survive the week. A Wharf Rat picked up by Zhent muscle in the Bloomridge gambling den six days back has talked under cordial pressure; he gives up the safehouse address for a guaranteed berth out of the city. By dawn third-day a Zhent hit squad is at the alley-mouth. The Rats fight a desperate defensive action — kitchen-table flipped against the door, crossbows from the loft, a fire-pot — and *survive*, but barely. Two more dead. The traitor is in the wind on a barge to Calimport. The Rats flee the Gray Harbour safehouse in daylight. Jack now has fewer crew than he did last week, no usable safehouse on the docks, and **no working Wharf Rat intelligence pipeline against the Zhentarim**. The Wharf Rats have been driven below the waterline.
