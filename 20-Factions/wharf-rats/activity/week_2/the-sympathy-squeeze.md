# The Sympathy Squeeze

['One-Eyed' Jack](../../people/one-eyed-jack.md) runs a labor slowdown at the [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) docks at [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) — half-tempo unloads, "accidentally" misrouted manifests, dockmen quietly walking off when patriar inspectors arrive. With overland trade still strangled by the [Free Trader siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md), the Consortium concedes higher dock wages within four days. The Wharf Rats lock in formal control of harbor labor; [Vex's reach into Jack's crew](../../../guild/activity/week_2/retaliation-on-the-river.md) becomes the Guild's primary lever on Gray Harbour.
