# The Solidarity Tax

[One-Eyed Jack](../../people/one-eyed-jack.md) tries to convert [last week's labor-slowdown win](../activity/week_2/the-sympathy-squeeze.md) into an open extortion: a daily "solidarity tax" levied on every load worked at the [Chionthar Consortium docks](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md), framed publicly as protest dues, paid actually to the [Guild](../../../guild/activity/week_3/recover-the-asset.md). The Consortium, already reeling from [the assassinated factor and the sabotaged shipment](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_3/secure-the-front-lines.md), refuses to tolerate it on its own piers. [Factor Errinthal](../../../chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) hires a small, brutal squad of [Zhentarim](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_3/an-offer-you-cant-refuse.md) sellswords through Roah Moonglow's network to "clear the air." The pier-side brawl is loud, ugly, and short. Three of Jack's enforcers are hospitalized; the solidarity tax breaks; [the Guild's](../../../guild/summary.md) hold over the Consortium docks — held since [the strike of week 0](../../../wc-fishmongers/activity/week_0/organize-a-dockworker-strike.md) — is shattered for the first time. Zhentarim sellswords are now visibly working Consortium pay. The implications are not lost on either side.
