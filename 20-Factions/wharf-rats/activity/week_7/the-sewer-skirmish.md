# The Sewer Skirmish

[One-Eyed Jack](../../people/one-eyed-jack.md) — anticipating Zhentarim retaliation for [last tenday's Velvet Hand warehouse arson](../../activity/week_6/the-counter-punch.md) — preempts it with a **counter-raid in the sewers beneath Gray Harbour**. Jack's crew picks off a Zhent retaliation squad at the storm-drain confluence under the Counting House and turns the engagement into a *running, hours-long battle through the harbor sewers*. Two Wharf Rats die at the second sluice gate; four Zhent enforcers go down; the rest scatter west toward Brampton.

The fight is *loud* by sewer standards. Lower City harborwives report the muffled crashes through their floor-drains all night. By dawn Jack's crew controls four key drainage junctions under Gray Harbour they did not control the night before — including a cistern overflow that opens directly into the Counting House subbasement. Jack is one tenday closer to **a sewer-launched strike on the Counting House strongroom itself**. Roah Moonglow accelerates [the Mole Hunt](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_7/the-mole-hunt.md); the ledger-clerk purge in [the Guild's Digital Purge](../../../guild/activity/week_7/the-digital-purge.md) closes the rest of her Counting House blind spot.
