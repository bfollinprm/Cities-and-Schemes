# The Pier-Side War

[One-Eyed Jack](../../people/one-eyed-jack.md), embittered after [the Zhent muscle squad broke his solidarity tax](../../../wharf-rats/activity/week_3/the-solidarity-tax.md), launches a guerilla war against the Zhentarim sellswords now openly working Consortium pay on the [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) docks. The first ambushes go well — two Zhent enforcers down a back alley, a third pulled from a tavern booth. Then the Black Network responds with disciplined, professional brutality. Several of Jack's crew are captured, tortured, and made a *gruesome public example* on the pier with rope and a fishing-gaff. The bodies are left up overnight. The Wharf Rats are cowed into submission by morning. The rebellion is crushed before it could become anything; Jack's labor coalition fractures, and the Zhentarim cement themselves on the harbor docks.
