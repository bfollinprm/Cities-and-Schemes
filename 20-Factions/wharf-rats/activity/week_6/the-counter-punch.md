# The Counter-Punch

Backs against the wall after [last week's traitor-led Zhentarim hit on the safehouse](../../activity/week_5/licking-their-wounds.md) and watching [Master Tallowshand's drydock lockout](../../../wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_6/the-drydock-lockout.md) cripple their independent-vessel revenue, [One-Eyed Jack](../../people/one-eyed-jack.md) organizes a counter-raid on a Zhentarim warehouse fronting [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md). Using their unmatched knowledge of the dock-side sewers and tide-tunnels, the Rats slip a six-man crew under the building, pop the floor at midnight, smash the warding glyphs, and torch a stockpile of [Zhentarim](../../../zhentarim/summary.md) illicit cargo — cut narcotics, forged Heritage-printed bonds, and a small smokepowder cache (a quiet thank-you to Jack from the [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md)).

The fire eats the warehouse from the inside out. By the time the Fist arrives — late, [for unrelated and unsurprising reasons](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_6/the-officers-dilemma.md) — the building is a smoking pit and the Wharf Rats have already vanished into the harbor mud. **A clean, vicious raid that levels the ledger after two months of Zhent humiliation.** [Roah Moonglow](../../../zhentarim/people/roah-moonglow.md) loses an estimated 4,000 gp in product and a face-saving location her Counting House operation was using to launder. Jack drinks at a Wharf Rats safehouse for the first time in three weeks; his crew calls him "Cap'n" again.
