# The Nightingale

Codename of the local [Harpers](../summary.md) cell leader in Baldur's Gate. Her real identity is one of the better-kept secrets in the city; informed parties suspect a senior figure embedded in patriar society or the magistracy.

## Manifesto

> The tyrant's throne is built on secrets and lies. The plutocrat's power is forged in deals made in darkness. We are the listeners in the shadows. We are the agents of balance. We do not seek to rule, only to ensure that no single power — be it a council, a guild, or a cult — can chain this city.

## Role

The Nightingale runs a loosely-organized cell of spies and agents across every level of Baldurian society. Her preferred technique is the surgical leak: damaging information, applied through [Baldur's Mouth](../../baldurs-mouth/summary.md) or quiet conversation, at the moment of maximum effect. Her week-0 attempt to plant an agent in the [Worshipful Company of Masons & Sculptors](../../wc-masons-sculptors/summary.md) is currently a strategic embarrassment — the cover broke on a small detail and the agent is being fed misinformation under the impression of being trusted.
