# Lord Vescaras Ammakyl

Spymaster of the [Harper](../summary.md) cell in [Waterdeep](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/README.md). One of the cell's two senior officers, called the **"Wise Owls"** — the other is [Remallia Haventree](remallia-haventree.md). Patriar by birth, Harper by conviction; the Ammakyl name buys him the salons, the Harpers buy the rest.

## Role

Vescaras runs the cell's intelligence apparatus: planted bards, dockhand watchers, magistrates with quiet sympathies. The Waterdeep cell's stated primary target is the [Zhentarim](../../zhentarim/summary.md); Vescaras is the man who decides which Zhent operative gets a watcher and which one gets a knife. Day-to-day operational handling is delegated to lieutenants like Randal Evenwood; the Nightingale's strategic pattern in Baldur's Gate has its closest peer in Vescaras's standing in Waterdeep.
