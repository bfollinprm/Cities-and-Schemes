# Lady Remallia Haventree

Cells coordinator of the [Harper](../summary.md) presence in [Waterdeep](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/README.md). One of the two **"Wise Owls"** — the other is the spymaster [Vescaras Ammakyl](vescaras-ammakyl.md). Where Vescaras runs the eyes and ears, Remallia runs the structure: which agent reports to which handler, where dead drops live, who is burned and who is still safe.

## Role

The Waterdeep cell is comparatively large and compartmentalized; Remallia is the connective tissue. She is the standing answer to *"how do we move people without anyone seeing the whole map?"* — a discipline learned from watching the Zhentarim do it in Skullport and resolving to do it better.
