# Randal Evenwood

Senior field operative of [the Harpers'](../summary.md) Baldur's Gate cell, working under [the Nightingale](the-nightingale.md). Evenwood handles inside jobs, infiltration, and the kind of long-cultivation source work the cell prefers over open confrontation. Older than the bards in the noble salons, harder than the dock contacts at the gate; the lieutenants the Nightingale actually trusts to bring back paper.

## Recent operations

- **The burned Archduke clerk.** Evenwood attempted to flip a clerk in [Lyra Silvershield's](../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) household. The clerk was a loyalist running a sting; Evenwood is now burned to the Archduke's faction.
- **The Singing Lute back-channel (week 4).** Evenwood and the Nightingale [brokered the moderate-faction compromise meeting](../activity/week_4/the-back-channel-bargain.md) at the Singing Lute in Eastway. An informant inside the cell leaked attendees and notes to a rival pamphlet; the compromise died in print.
- **Sembian Kontor investigation (week 5).** Tasked by the Nightingale to [find proof that Bursar Korgan Ironhand is fanning the war for profit](../activity/week_5/expose-the-war-profiteer.md), working the Iron Ledger from the outside.
