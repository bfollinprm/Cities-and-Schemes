# Tilly Falkenrath

A field operative of [the Harpers](../summary.md) in Baldur's Gate, currently working the [Outer City](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md) and undercity routes. Pragmatic, low-profile, and unsentimental about which favors are owed to whom.

## Current operation

Falkenrath is one of three principals in a three-way standoff at the [Tumbledown](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md) gates: a cart of smuggled grain is bound through the crypts toward the besieged [Basilisk Gate](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) blockade. Her opposite numbers are [Silas the Eel](../../river-rats/people/silas-the-eel.md) ([Guild](../../guild/summary.md)) and [Old Man Hemlock](../../gravekeepers/people/hemlock.md). The Harpers' interest is the same as ever: keep any one player from owning the route, and quietly steer the grain to wherever it least entrenches a corrupt power.
