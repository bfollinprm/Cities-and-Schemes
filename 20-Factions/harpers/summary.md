# The Harpers

A semi-secret organization with a long history of thwarting major threats to the Sword Coast. In post-crisis Baldur's Gate their focus has shifted from overt threats to the insidious rot of plutocracy and corruption — making sure no single power, council, guild, or cult, can chain the city. Led locally by [The Nightingale](people/the-nightingale.md), inspired by the legacy of legendary members like Jaheira.

## Ideology

To gather information, identify abuses of power, and intervene in the most subtle ways possible to maintain stability and protect the common folk from the ambitions of the powerful. Their working belief: balance is preserved by truth applied at the right pressure point, not by overt force.

## Membership

A loosely organized cell led by The Nightingale. Spies and agents are placed across society — bards in patriar salons, magistrates in the legal system, merchants on the docks, fronts at **Danthelon's Dancing Axe**, an unofficial node at the [Waterdhavian Kontor](../waterdhavian-kontor/summary.md).

## Methods

- Espionage, information brokerage, surgical intervention.
- Leak-and-launder relationship with [Baldur's Mouth](../baldurs-mouth/summary.md) — when The Nightingale wants public sentiment shifted, the leak goes to Needle (and after his kidnapping, the relationship is in question).
- Agent placement. Week 0: tried to plant an agent in the [Worshipful Company of Masons & Sculptors](../wc-masons-sculptors/summary.md); the cover had a flaw and the agent is now under surveillance, fed misinformation as a compromised double agent.
- Subtle financial intervention against the worst exploits of the Council and the Kontors.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** Sworn enemies of the [Zhentarim](../zhentarim/summary.md) and the various dark cults — particularly the [Cult of the Returning Lord](../cult-of-the-returning-lord/summary.md), [Silent Shroud](../silent-shroud/summary.md), [Hands of the Absolute](../hands-of-the-absolute/summary.md), and [Carnival of Whispers](../carnival-of-whispers/summary.md). Wary of the [Flaming Fist's](../flaming-fist/summary.md) corruption and the [Council's](../council-of-four/summary.md) overreach.

**Targets of conspiracy theories.** The [Unveiled](../unveiled/summary.md) consider the Harpers prime architects of the doppelganger conspiracy. The [Society of Baldurian Integrity](../society-of-baldurian-integrity/summary.md) sees them as Waterdhavian memetic-warfare agents.

**Allies of convenience.** The [Waterdhavian Kontor](../waterdhavian-kontor/summary.md) (informal Lords' Alliance partnership). The [Sorcerous Sodality](../wc-sorcerous-sodality/summary.md) when uncontrolled magic is the threat.
