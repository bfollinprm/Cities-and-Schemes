# Find a New Inlet

[Randal Evenwood](../../summary.md), with the [Nightingale's](../../people/the-nightingale.md) blessing, attempts to turn a junior clerk inside [Archduke Silvershield's](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) Bloomridge Park Manor household, hoping to seat a paper-trail asset close enough to read [the private mercenary contract](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_3/the-private-option.md) before it reaches the city. The trap is the wrong way around. The clerk is a loyal company hand feigning discontent on the Archduke's standing instructions; security is in place when Evenwood arrives at the Manor. Evenwood aborts and flees, barely escaping a Western Gate security detail. The Harper agent is now burned in the Manor's records, and the Archduke knows someone — and the Nightingale will assume it is the Sembian Kontor or a Coin rival — is trying to read her household's correspondence. Counter-intelligence inside the Western Gate goes up; the Harpers' Upper City work has just become harder.
