# Expose the War Profiteer

[The Nightingale](../../people/the-nightingale.md), accepting that [last week's Singing Lute back-channel](../../../harpers/activity/week_4/the-back-channel-bargain.md) closed the peaceful path, refocuses the Baldur's Gate cell. The new target is [Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../../sembian-kontor/people/korgan-ironhand.md): the Harpers now believe he is fanning the conflict to maximize the Sembian Kontor's mercenary-supply margins — [a daily wagon train flowing out of the Iron Ledger to the Eastway camp this week](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_5/arming-the-aggressors.md) is the visible part of the operation. [Randal Evenwood](../../people/randal-evenwood.md), still burned to the Archduke's faction, is tasked with finding written proof: a price-list, a side-letter, a clerk's notebook, anything that connects the contract numbers to a *promise of escalation*. The Iron Ledger, Lower City, is the holding-yard. Evenwood spends the week working a sympathetic Sembian apprentice over wine in a side-tavern; the apprentice is afraid but not yet willing to talk. By week's end the Harpers have a cultivation arc started, a set of likely document-storage locations, and a working hypothesis. They do not yet have proof. **The investigation is live; the cell is patient.**
