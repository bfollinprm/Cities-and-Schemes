# Find the Quill

[The Nightingale](../../people/the-nightingale.md) reads the [ransacked Baldur's Mouth print shop and the editorial pivot](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_7/the-puppet-press.md) for what they are: not a kidnapping but a **silencing operation by the Western Gate Trading Company**. Recovering [Ettvard Needle](../../../baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) alive, with his pen, would re-open the city's only credible press while the [Great Retraction](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_7/the-great-retraction.md) is still drying.

She dispatches three small Harper cells — a chimney-walker, a gutter-listener, and a forged-papers clerk — to scour the **Western Gate Trading Company's warehouses** along the Outer City roads and the Tumbledown rim. The teams find Western Gate manifests heavy with shipments labeled *literary materials* moving in volumes no honest paper-house would buy. *Someone is buying Ettvard's distinctive paper stock in bulk and warehousing it.* No body is recovered, no Ettvard is sighted, but the Nightingale has her first thread: the buyer is operating through a Western Gate front. The party — searching the same Tumbledown crypts from the other end — does not yet know they have allies on the surface.
