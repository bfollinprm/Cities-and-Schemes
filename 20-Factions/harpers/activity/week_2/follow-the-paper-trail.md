# Follow the Paper Trail

[The Nightingale](../../people/the-nightingale.md) and field-handler **Randal Evenwood** try to plant a leak in [the Baldur's Mouth](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_2/fan-the-flames.md): paperwork pulled from a [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) warehouse on the Lower City docks suggesting [Archduke Lyra Silvershield's](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) Western Gate is double-billing infernal-shard cargo through the [Smiths' Company](../../../wc-smiths-armorers/summary.md). [Ettvard Needle](../../../baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) reads the dossier carefully, recognizes the bait, and refuses to print without independently verifiable proof. The leak dies on the editor's desk; the Harpers walk away unburned.
