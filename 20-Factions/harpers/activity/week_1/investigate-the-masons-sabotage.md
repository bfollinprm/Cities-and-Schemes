# Investigate the Masons' 'Sabotage'

The [Nightingale](../../people/the-nightingale.md) sends a Harper agent to the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) collapse site to gather evidence countering Master [Borin Stonehand's](../../../wc-masons-sculptors/people/borin-stonehand.md) "Zhentarim sabotage" frame. Stonehand has the site under heavy Guild Warden guard; the agent is unable to gain access. Without forensic evidence the Harpers cannot publicly counter the *[Baldur's Mouth](../../../baldurs-mouth/summary.md)* coverage, leaving the truth of the collapse contested — and the Mason-controlled wall repair queue undisturbed.
