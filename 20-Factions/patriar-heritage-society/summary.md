# Patriar Heritage Society

Not a chartered organization but the **organizing club of the [Peerage of Blood](../peerage-of-blood/summary.md)** — an informal alliance of "old money" patriar families who act as a bloc against the rising "new money" of the [Peerage of Coin](../peerage-of-coin/summary.md). De facto led by [Lord Corin "the Elder" Durinbold](../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md); meets in private salons of [Upper City](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md) manors, never in a public hall.

## Ideology

Roll back the reforms of the **Great Bargain** and restore the primacy of birthright over naked commerce. The foreign Kontors are a cancer on the city; the Merchant Consortiums are a symptom of decay; the [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md), corrupt and diminished as it is, remains a symbol of the *old Baldurian order* worth defending. "Baldurian Purity" is the slogan and the cover for the politics underneath.

## Membership

The oldest and wealthiest patriar families: **Durinbold, [Jannath](../peerage-of-blood/associations/house-jannath.md), [Hlath](../peerage-of-blood/associations/house-hlath.md), Oberon**, and others. Influence runs through ancient names, dwindling fortunes, and deep institutional connections — not subscription rolls. Reach is widest in the [Peerage of Blood](../peerage-of-blood/summary.md), the Manorborn salons, and among patrons of the Watch and the Fist.

## Methods

- **Bloc voting** in the Parliament of Peers, organized over dinner the night before. Recently used to **defend Marshal Flint** and block a Coin motion to replace the Fist with foreign mercenaries.
- **"Baldurian Purity" Gala.** Durinbold hosted an exclusive gala for the Peerage of Blood — a political rally against the Kontors and consortiums, celebrating the [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md)'s "defense of tradition" and isolating Archduke Silvershield.
- **The "Silver Fist" loyalty fund.** A massive purse collected from Heritage Society families and quietly distributed as silver "bonuses" to Fist sergeants and manips at the [Elfsong](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/elfsong.md), keeping rank-and-file loyalty with the old guard rather than Flint's Githyanki allies. Has already produced *the Officer's Dilemma* — bribed officers slow-rolling Flint's smuggling crackdown.
- **Failed Hlath sponsorship.** Durinbold's public attempt to take in the dispossessed Hlaths was outmaneuvered when [Amnian Kontor](../amnian-kontor/summary.md) agents intercepted the family first.
- **Pamphlet and slander campaigns** framing the [Free Traders](../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) as anarchists responsible for the food shortages.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Leadership.** [Lord Corin Durinbold](../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) — de facto leader; his procedural mastery and his Silver Fist coin are the Society's two main weapons. No new chair has been named in the source.

**Allies.** The [Peerage of Blood](../peerage-of-blood/summary.md) (organizing target); the [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md) rank-and-file (bought, for now); the conservative Worshipful Companies — notably the [Worshipful Company of Goldsmiths & Jewelers](../wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/summary.md), which crafted Durinbold's gold-and-silver canary commission to cement the patriar–guild axis.

**Enemies.** The [Amnian Kontor](../amnian-kontor/summary.md) — outmaneuvered Durinbold on the Hlath sponsorship; primary nativist target. The [Peerage of Coin](../peerage-of-coin/summary.md) and the [Council of Four](../council-of-four/summary.md) under Archduke Silvershield.
