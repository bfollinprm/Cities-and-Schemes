# Sponsor the Dispossessed Hlath Family

[Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) tries to publicly sponsor the dispossessed [Hlaths](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/lord-hlath.md) — a propaganda coup against the [Amnian Kontor's](../../../amnian-kontor/summary.md) foreclosure. [Consul Vorlamin Athar's](../../../amnian-kontor/people/vorlamin-athar.md) agents reach the family first, intimidating or buying off [Lord Hlath](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/lord-hlath.md) into a public refusal of Heritage Society aid. Durinbold absorbs the loss in silence; the snub becomes the week's quiet humiliation among the patriars.
