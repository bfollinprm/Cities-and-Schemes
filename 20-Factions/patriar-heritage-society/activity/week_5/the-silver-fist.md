# The Silver Fist

[Lord Corin Durinbold's](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) [Loyalty Fund](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_4/the-loyalty-fund.md) starts paying out. Trusted Heritage agents — third-cousin patriars, retired Fist quartermasters, Bloomridge club-staff with the right family connections — meet Flaming Fist sergeants and *manips* at booths in [the Elfsong Tavern](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/elfsong-tavern.md). The transactions are small enough not to register and large enough to matter: a heavy purse of silver in the hand, a promise of more to come, a quiet word about which loyalties will be remembered when the chambers come back to life. The pitch lands harder for [the talk of the new githyanki phalanx at Wyrm's Rock](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_5/the-integration-drills.md): every Fist sergeant offered the purse has been on the parade ground next to *astral steel*, and every one of them is being told, with money, that **the old Fist still has patrons who remember whose coat they wear**. By week's end Durinbold has bought the loyalty of enough Fist enlisted to make any future order from [Marshal Flint](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) — about a Free Trader assault, about a githyanki deployment, about anything — a question of which paymaster a sergeant fears more.
