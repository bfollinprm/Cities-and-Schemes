# Defend the Marshal

[Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) reframes [Marshal Flint's](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) public defiance of [the Archduke](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) as the defense of old Baldurian institutions against "new-money tyranny." The Heritage Society throws the social weight of the Manorborn old families behind the Fist publicly — gala, pamphlet, and pulpit — and quietly behind blocking [the Peerage of Coin's mercenary motion](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_3/whip-the-votes.md) at [High Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md). The motion fails, [the no-confidence vote dies](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_3/the-vote-of-no-confidence.md) with it, and the patriar bloc emerges as the visible savior of the Marshal. Durinbold gains a debt the Marshal will be reluctant to pay but will be forced to acknowledge.
