# The Loyalty Fund

To deepen his tactical alliance with [Marshal Flint](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md), [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) opens a side ledger from his Manorborn estate: the **Loyalty Fund**. He passes the hat — discreetly, by cordial dinner invitation — to the old patriar families of the Heritage Society and collects a substantial purse to be paid as a "morale bonus" directly to Fist rank-and-file at [Wyrm's Rock](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md). The bonus is not channeled through the Fist's command structure or the city treasury. It lands in soldiers' purses with a [Mercer-tailored](../../../wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_3/the-color-of-authority.md) Heritage-red ribbon attached. Where [the Emergency Levy](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_4/the-emergency-levy.md) buys the Archduke's mercenaries, this purse buys the Fist's enlisted ranks. The patriar bloc is now openly purchasing the loyalty of armed men.
