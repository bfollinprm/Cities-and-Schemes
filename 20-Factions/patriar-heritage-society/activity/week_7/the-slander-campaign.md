# The Slander Campaign

[Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) opens his manor's purse for a flood of pamphlets blanketing the Lower City and Sembian camps. The pamphlets paint [the Free Traders](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) as **dangerous anarchists** responsible for the bread shortages, the printer-strike at Baldur's Mouth, and the riot at the Sow & Piglet that has not yet happened. The pamphlets are timed exactly to land before [the Plague of Remembrance's manufactured riot](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_7/the-refugee-riot.md), so the Fist will already be primed to read the violence as anarchist provocation.

The slander campaign serves a second purpose: it gives the Fist sergeants Durinbold has been bribing [since the Officers' Mess](../../activity/week_6/the-officers-mess.md) **plausible cover** to refuse Marshal Flint's orders. The pamphlets are, in effect, a written excuse for the [paralyzed patrols](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_7/the-paralyzed-patrols.md). By sundown half the [Eastway](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) barracks-wall is lined with copies pinned up by the noncoms themselves.
