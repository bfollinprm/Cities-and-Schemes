# Empower the Old Guard

[Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) hosts a widely-publicized gala at [High Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md) for the city's old-money elite, framed as a celebration of the [Flaming Fist's "defense of tradition"](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_2/plan-the-unthinkable.md). Translation: a public Peerage-of-Blood endorsement of [Marshal Flint's defiance of Archduke Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_2/archdukes-mandate.md). The gala further isolates the Archduke and lifts Durinbold's profile inside the Peerage of Blood — useful cover for [the Sodality scrying ritual](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_2/trace-the-arcane-signature.md) quietly running on him at the same time.
