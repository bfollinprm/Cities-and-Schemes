# Black Lilly

The signature narcotic of [the Xanathar Guild](../summary.md): a powerful, addictive recreational drug distributed across [Waterdeep](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/README.md), particularly through [Dock Ward](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/dock-ward/character.md) and [Trades Ward](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/trades-ward/character.md) channels. The drug is the consumer-facing pillar of the Guild's economy, paired with slave-trading and protection rackets.

## Strategic role

Where the [Zhentarim](../../zhentarim/summary.md) wants the underworld through bribery and blackmail, the Xanathar wants it through *addiction*. Black Lilly is the operational expression of that doctrine: every regular user is a recurring revenue line and a leverage point. The drug is named in the dossier alongside [Nihiloor's](../people/nihiloor.md) intellect-devourer plants as one of the two pillars of the Guild's grip on the city — chemical and psionic, working in parallel.

## Distribution

- Cut and packaged below the city; surface distribution runs through Dock Ward fences and Trades Ward "alchemists' shops" that exist mostly as fronts.
- The Watch has caught individual dealers and shut down individual rooms; the supply chain has not been broken.
- High-status users in [North Ward](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/north-ward/character.md) salons are an open secret — leverage the Guild collects but rarely cashes in publicly.
