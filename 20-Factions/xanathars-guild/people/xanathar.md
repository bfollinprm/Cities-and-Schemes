# The Xanathar

A beholder. Hereditary title; the current holder runs an empire of slavers, addicts, and intellect-devourer puppets out of the Undercity and **Skullport**. The Guild bears his name; he does not need to leave his lair to make the city tremble.

## Conflict

Open war with **Manshoon's Zhentarim** since 1491 DR (the Shadow War). Where Manshoon's play is corrupting the [Masked Lords](../../masked-lords/summary.md) toward the Open Lord's chair, the Xanathar's play is the underworld itself: addiction, debt, slavery, and the puppet network [Nihiloor](nihiloor.md) plants inside the city's institutions.
