# Nihiloor

Mind flayer. Senior lieutenant in the [Xanathar Guild](../summary.md). Operates from the Dock Ward and Skullport, with intellect-devourer agents seeded throughout the city.

## The puppet program

Nihiloor's signature operation. Intellect devourers replace the host mind and walk the body to its old desk, its old shift, its old marriage. Known case: **Meloon Wardragon**. The dossier treats the program as the most insidious of the Xanathar's tools — the Guild does not need to control a noble house if it controls the noble's body.

The Watch and the Magisters have not publicly admitted the program exists. Recovering even one of the puppets intact would be a major intelligence prize.
