# Whip the Votes

Representatives of the major Consortiums and Kontors run a frantic backroom whip operation at [High Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md) and [the Gold-Gable Hall](../../../amnian-kontor/summary.md), trading bribes and veiled threats to lock the new-money bloc into a unified vote against [Marshal Flint](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md). [Consul Vorlamin Athar](../../../amnian-kontor/people/vorlamin-athar.md) drives the Amnian Kontor's count personally; [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) leans on the Western Gate's allies. The discipline holds — but the [Peerage of Blood's filibuster](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_3/filibuster-the-mercenary-act.md) keeps the bloc from voting at all, and [the no-confidence motion fails](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_3/the-vote-of-no-confidence.md) on the floor. The Peerage of Coin has counted its votes and lost anyway.
