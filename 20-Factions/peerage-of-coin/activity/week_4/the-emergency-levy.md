# The Emergency Levy

To give [the Archduke's private mercenary company](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md) a veneer of public legitimacy, [the Peerage of Coin](../../summary.md) — under [Consul Vorlamin Athar's](../../../amnian-kontor/people/vorlamin-athar.md) whip — leverages its majority to push through the **Emergency Commerce Protection Act**. The Act levies a new fee on all goods passing through the city to pay for "additional security." The wording does not name the Archduke's mercenaries. The wording does not need to. Every coin collected at the Basilisk and Black Dragon gates from this day forward funds the Eastway barracks. The city is now paying — quietly, automatically, by the cart-load — for the private army aimed at its own Outer City. The vote is contested but it carries; [the Peerage of Blood's protest](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_4/the-letter-of-censure.md) does not have the votes to stop it.
