# Legislate Aggression

[Consul Vorlamin Athar](../../../amnian-kontor/people/vorlamin-athar.md) of the [Amnian Kontor](../../../amnian-kontor/summary.md) drives a Peerage-of-Coin motion authorizing the Council to hire foreign mercenaries to break the Free Traders' siege at the Basilisk Gate. The motion fails by two votes — [the Peerage of Blood](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_2/a-charity-of-spite.md) frames it as a sovereignty issue, the Knights of the Shield's quiet whip operation breaks down, and the [Western Gate](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_2/archdukes-mandate.md) abstains rather than endorse a Coin-led adventure. The result is legislative gridlock at [High Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md) for the second week running.
