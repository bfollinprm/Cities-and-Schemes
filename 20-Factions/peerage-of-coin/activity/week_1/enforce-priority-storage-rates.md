# Enforce 'Priority Storage' Rates

Riding the Harbor Dredging Levy victory, the Peerage of Coin lets [Master Joric the Stout's](../../../chionthar-consortium/people/joric-the-stout.md) [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) triple "priority storage" rates for cargo originating from the [Sembian Kontor](../../../sembian-kontor/summary.md), citing "additional security." It is open economic warfare: the Sembian books absorb the cost while [Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../../sembian-kontor/people/korgan-ironhand.md) tasks his intelligence network with retaliation later in the week.
