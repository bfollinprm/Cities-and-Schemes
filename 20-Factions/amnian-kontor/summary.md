# Amnian Kontor *(aka The Gold-Gable Hall)*

The largest and most opulent of the foreign enclaves established under the Great Bargain — a slice of a mighty mercantile empire that seeks to dominate Baldur's Gate through control of banking, debt, and the flow of southern luxuries. Led by [Consul Vorlamin Athar](people/vorlamin-athar.md) from the Gold-Gable Hall.

## Ideology

Total economic dominance over the Sword Coast, using Baldur's Gate as the primary base. The Kontor wields debt as its principal weapon, pursuing controlling influence over the [Council of Four](../council-of-four/summary.md).

## Membership

Staffed entirely by Amnian nationals and led by Consul Vorlamin Athar. The enclave is protected by its own professional force, the **Coin-Swords**, who answer to Amnian rather than Baldurian law within the Hall's walls.

## Methods

- Dominates the city's financial sector: large-scale loans, letters of credit, daily currency-exchange rates.
- Primary importer of southern luxuries — spices, rare woods, fine glassware.
- Operates under its own laws within the walled enclave; Baldurian writ stops at the gate.
- Holds a seat in the [Peerage of Coin](../peerage-of-coin/summary.md), recalculated annually based on tax receipts.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** Quiet financial warfare with the rival Kontors — [Waterdhavian](../waterdhavian-kontor/summary.md), [Sembian](../sembian-kontor/summary.md), [Lantaner](../lantaner-concession/summary.md) — and with the local [Chionthar Consortium](../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) and [Western Gate Trading Company](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md), who resent their reach.

## At Sword Coast scale

The Kontor is **Amn's beachhead in Baldur's Gate**, not the empire itself. The home polity — capital **Athkatla**, ruled by the Council of Six — is treated as setting matter in [10-Setting/polities/calimshan-amn.md](../../10-Setting/polities/calimshan-amn.md); the Sword-Coast-wide picture is in [00-Campaign-Frame/multipolar-coast.md](../../00-Campaign-Frame/multipolar-coast.md). Amn also occupies **Snowdown** in the Moonshaes (garrison; viceroy; tariff-rigorous quayside) and quietly issues letters of marque to Nelanther captains against Waterdhavian shipping — see [30-Places/sword-coast/moonshae-isles.md](../../30-Places/sword-coast/moonshae-isles.md) and [30-Places/sword-coast/nelanther-isles.md](../../30-Places/sword-coast/nelanther-isles.md).
