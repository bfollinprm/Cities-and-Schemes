# Consolidate River Control

Following [last week's hostile takeover of Brindlestone & Sons](../../activity/week_6/the-hostile-takeover.md), [Consul Vorlamin Athar](../../people/vorlamin-athar.md) moves to crew the captured fleet wholly with **Coin-Sword retainers** and run the Chionthar like a tollroad. Within four days, Coin-Sword crews fly Amnian colors at every dock the Brindlestone hulls used to call on, and Athar publishes a new "river-traffic schedule" out of [the Gold-Gable Hall](../../summary.md): all non-Amnian shipping above [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) must register and pay a transit fee.

The result is a **physical river blockade** mirroring the [Free Trader land siege at Basilisk Gate](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_5/heed-the-ultimatum.md) — except the river blockade is run by a *foreign kontor* with no Baldurian charter, openly collecting tariffs the Council never voted. The [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) cannot answer it: their last clandestine reserve was burned [in the Desperate Run](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_6/the-desperate-run.md). Athar now controls Lower City riverfront tonnage in everything but name.
