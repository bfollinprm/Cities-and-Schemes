# Identify the Assassin's Patron

[Consul Vorlamin Athar](../../people/vorlamin-athar.md) puts the Kontor's investigators on [the Gilded Vein hit on the Chionthar factor](../../../gilded-vein/activity/week_2/accelerate-the-crash.md) and the carefully planted forged seals it left in its wake. The Kontor reads the trail exactly as the [Gilded Vein](../../../gilded-vein/activity/week_3/twist-the-knife.md) intended: the assassination is concluded to be Sembian work. The misattribution sends ripples through Lower City finance. Athar quietly authorizes a trade-war response against [the Sembian Kontor](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_3/the-alternate-route.md): increased shipping fees on Sembian-flagged hulls, a freeze on Iron Ledger letters of credit, a discreet word to [the Goldsmiths](../../../wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_3/follow-the-money.md) about scrutinizing Sembian deposits. None of it is loud, none of it is public, and the [Sodality](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_3/follow-the-resonance.md) does not yet know to read it as connected to either Vein or Stone work. By the time the misattribution surfaces, both Kontors will be financially bleeding into each other.
