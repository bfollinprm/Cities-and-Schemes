# Sell the Hlath Estate to an Ally

[Consul Vorlamin Athar](../../people/vorlamin-athar.md) tries to flip the foreclosed Hlath estate to a friendly buyer at speed. [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) finds an obscure pre-Crisis property statute and files an injunction tying the sale up in the city magistrates' bureaucracy. The estate stays empty — a rare, embarrassing legal defeat for the Amnian books.
