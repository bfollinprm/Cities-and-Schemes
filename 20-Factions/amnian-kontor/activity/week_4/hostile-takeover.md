# Hostile Takeover

[Consul Vorlamin Athar's](../../people/vorlamin-athar.md) attempt to financially strong-arm a Sembian-aligned supplier — squeezing Iron Ledger debt to seize the man's contracts — backfires. The Sembian Kontor is alerted to the move (the Amnians' own [trade-war pressure on the Iron Ledger from last week](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_3/identify-the-assassins-patron.md) gave the Sembians the early warning) and steps in with a generous counter-offer, paying off the supplier's debt in full and securing his loyalty for the Iron Ledger. The Amnian Kontor not only fails to capture the asset but burns significant capital and political face on a public board. [The Gold-Gable Hall](../../summary.md) loses ground in the Lower City finance war it inadvertently started — and worse, [Korgan Ironhand's lawyers](../../../sembian-kontor/people/korgan-ironhand.md) are now using their newfound goodwill to grease [the Archduke's mercenary contract](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_4/legitimizing-the-contract.md) through the bureaucracy.
