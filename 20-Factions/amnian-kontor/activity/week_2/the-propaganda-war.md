# The Propaganda War

[Consul Vorlamin Athar's](../../people/vorlamin-athar.md) internal sweeps to find the source of the anti-[Amnian](../../summary.md) slander still circulating in the [Lower City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/README.md) come up empty. The cart of slanderous broadsheets [stolen out of the Chionthar warehouse](../../../party/activity/week_1/heist-the-rivington-medicine-cart.md) the week before is the vector and the Kontor cannot see it; paranoia inside [Gold-Gable Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/README.md) ratchets up. [Ettvard Needle](../../../baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) keeps printing both sides for whoever pays.
