# Retaliatory Asset Seizure

[Consul Vorlamin Athar](../../people/vorlamin-athar.md), retaliating for [the Chionthar Consortium's sloppy "warning" warehouse raid last week](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_4/a-warning-in-the-warehouse.md), uses the financial leverage the Amnian Kontor was built for. Athar's clerks identify a Consortium-loyal shipping company carrying an old, papered-over Amnian debt — a margin call the Consortium had been politely allowing to slide for years — and **call it in**. The papers are presented at the company office at dawn. The company cannot pay. The same morning, Amnian Coin-Swords board the company's two finest river-barges at the [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) docks, post Amnian seal-flags on the rigging, and walk longshoremen ashore at sword-point. The seizure is *legal*; it is also *theatrical*, watched from the wharf by every dockhand and every Consortium clerk in earshot. [Factor Errinthal's reserve gold quietly walked out of an Undercity cache the night before](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md); the river-barge seizure is the public humiliation that goes with the private theft. **The Amnians have hit the Chionthar Consortium twice this week, and the docks know which house won.**
