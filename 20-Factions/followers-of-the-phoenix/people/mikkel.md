# Mikkel

A young tiefling devotee of [Karlach](../summary.md) and a regular at the **Phoenix Pits** — the more militant, fight-circle wing of the [Followers of the Phoenix](../summary.md). Hot-tempered, articulate when he wants to be, and increasingly visible to the rest of the city's factions as the public face of Phoenix radicalism.

## Pressures

Mikkel is the closest known influence pulling the Hellrider lieutenant [Jaredith Heartsong](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) toward direct action against the [Flaming Fist](../../flaming-fist/summary.md). [High Watcher Alena](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) considers him the proximal trigger for what could split the Hellriders militia. From Mikkel's side, Jaredith's swing is a major recruiting win.

## Role with the party

The party first saw Mikkel in [Session 2](../../../50-Sessions/session_2/player_summary.md), arguing with [Gareth](../../wc-masons-sculptors/people/gareth.md) at the Weary Plowman over a *Baldur's Mouth* article. Alena named him by name in [Session 3](../../../50-Sessions/session_3/player_summary.md) as Jaredith's likely radicalizer.
