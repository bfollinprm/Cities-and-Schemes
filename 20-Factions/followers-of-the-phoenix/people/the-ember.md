# The Ember (Alfira)

Founder and leader of [Followers of the Phoenix](../summary.md). Tiefling bard turned cult preacher, the most rapidly rising spiritual figure in [New Elturel](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md).

## Role

Alfira built the Followers through public sermons in the Phoenix Pits, growing the movement from Karlach-veneration into a coherent faith of righteous anger and refugee dignity. Her instinct for narrative warfare is the cult's most dangerous asset: she does not confront radicals, she co-opts and reshapes them. Her handling of Jaredith after the Phoenix Pits incident — turning his grief into folk-hero material rather than dissipating it — is a textbook example.

By Session 9 her song-and-faith movement ended the Outer City protests more decisively than [High Watcher Alena's](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/alena.md) disciplined political pressure could. The Followers are now an officially recognized power bloc, with land granted for a temple. The internal tension with Alena's moderates is real and growing.
