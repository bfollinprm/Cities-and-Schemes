# The First Sermon at the Phoenix Gate

[The Ember (Alfira)](../../people/the-ember.md), riding the political momentum of [Jaredith Heartsong's](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) protest-ending ballad in week 2, breaks ground on a permanent temple at the **Phoenix Gate** in [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) and delivers her first formal sermon there. Jaredith stands beside her in his Hellrider colors — convalescent, not preaching, but present — and the visual is unmissable: the city's rising faith movement and its disciplined refugee militia are openly aligned. Hundreds attend. New converts pour in from the [Phoenix Pits](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/phoenix-pits.md) crowd Spark won during [the duel](../../../party/activity/week_2/expose-zora-at-the-phoenix-pits.md). The political consequence is that the Outer City now has an unambiguous spiritual leader the Council cannot pre-empt and [the Takers of the Tithe](../../../takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_3/learn-the-muses-song.md) can identify by name.
