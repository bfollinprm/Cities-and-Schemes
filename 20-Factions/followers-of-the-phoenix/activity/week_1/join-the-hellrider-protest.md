# Join the Hellrider Protest

[The Ember](../../people/the-ember.md) marches the Followers' growing tiefling-and-outcast congregation onto the [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) protest organized by [High Watcher Alena](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md). The Ember preaches; their fiery rhetoric turns the gathering from a citizenship petition into a rights-rally for everyone the city has cast off. **Spark** — a member of the [party](../../../party/summary.md) on direct mission from the Followers — speaks alongside, and is later credited with brokering a corridor through the protest line for the Open Hand Temple medicine cart.
