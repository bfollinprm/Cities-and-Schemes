# A Martyr's Sermon

[The Ember (Alfira)](../../people/the-ember.md), having survived an assassination attempt on her by [the Takers of the Tithe's Reaper](../../../takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_4/the-reapers-gambit.md), holds a sermon at the newly-consecrated [Phoenix Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) temple ground. She does not call for vengeance against the cult that sent the assassin. She uses the attack as proof that *the city's rot fears their light*. [Jaredith Heartsong](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) sings the descant. The crowd doubles, then triples; converts pour in from across the Outer City and from refugees still drifting in [from the militia drill grounds](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_4/the-call-to-arms.md). The Phoenix faith is now the load-bearing spiritual fact of New Elturel, and the Phoenix Gate is now its load-bearing place. The Hellrider militia and the Phoenix congregation are visibly the same crowd by sundown.
