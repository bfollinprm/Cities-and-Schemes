# The Call for Martyrs

Following the [granted council seat and temple land won in week 1](../../../party/activity/week_1/accept-the-jaredith-mission-from-alena.md), [Mikkel](../../people/mikkel.md) breaks ground on the new Phoenix temple in [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md). [Jaredith Heartsong's](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) heroic ballad at [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) — staged by [the Ember](../../people/the-ember.md), unintended by Jaredith himself — pulls a wave of new converts into the cult, including young tieflings and sympathetic Baldurians. The Phoenix's standing eclipses [High Watcher Alena's](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) more disciplined Hellrider organization in New Elturel for the first time.
