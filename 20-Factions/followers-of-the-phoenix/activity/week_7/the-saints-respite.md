# The Saint's Respite

While [The Ember (Alfira)](../../people/the-ember.md) recovers at [the Weary Plowman](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) after [her near-sacrifice on Dusthawk Hill](../../../../50-Sessions/session_26/player_summary.md), the [Phoenix Temple Foundation Stone construction](../../activity/week_6/the-foundation-stone.md) at the Phoenix Gate accelerates rather than slows. [Jaredith Heartsong](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) — who [stayed in New Elturel](../../../../50-Sessions/session_27/README.md) to oversee construction in Alfira's absence — presides at the worksite each dawn with a lute on his back instead of a sword on his hip.

The faithful interpret Alfira's *survival* as **divine confirmation** that the Phoenix walks with the temple. Volunteer enrollment doubles in three days; donations of stone, timber, and salvaged metal arrive from refugees who have never set foot in the camp. The temple's stone footprint is fully laid by the seventh day. The faction is consolidating from a street-level cult into a **physical institution with permanent roots in Baldur's Gate**.
