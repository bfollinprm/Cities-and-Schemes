# The System Reboot

With [the scrying network crashed last week](../../activity/week_5/analyze-the-arcane-traffic.md), the Sodality mages attempt a high-risk ritual to reboot the city's [scrying censers](../../activity/week_4/the-scrying-censors.md). Six analysts and Lorroakan-trained ritualists assemble around the central crystal matrix at [Sorcerous Sundries](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/sorcerous-sundries.md), channeling raw arcane energy across the burnt sensor array to flush out the corruption from [the Githyanki interference](../../../unchained/activity/week_5/establish-the-perimeter.md) and [the unshielded refugee artifacts](../../activity/week_5/analyze-the-arcane-traffic.md).

The reboot fails halfway through and *backlashes catastrophically*. The Githyanki astral-traffic resonance is too strong; the matrix kicks back; **windows blow out for a block in every direction around Sorcerous Sundries**, three apprentices are knocked unconscious, and the central crystal cracks down its long axis. The censers are partially restored but the database remains corrupted, and the analysts are now nauseously aware that the city is being scryed *back* by the K'liir presence. **The Sodality has lost its information edge for at least another fortnight.** Worse: at the exact moment of the failed reboot, [the Hands of the Absolute's psionic ritual was firing in Heapside](../../../hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_6/the-silent-scream.md) — the simultaneous spikes are now in the censers' broken logs, and the Sodality cannot distinguish them.
