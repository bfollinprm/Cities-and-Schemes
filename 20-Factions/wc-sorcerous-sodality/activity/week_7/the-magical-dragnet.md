# The Magical Dragnet

With the scrying censers rebuilt after [last week's catastrophic reboot](../../activity/week_6/the-system-reboot.md), the Sodality's analysts run an aggressive **detection sweep** across [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), targeting unshielded magical signatures of the adventuring party that attacked [the Plague-Father's watchtower in week 2](../../../plague-of-remembrance/people/zora.md). The censers light up the Outer City like a feast-night.

The dragnet catches the wrong people: three hedge-witches, a blink-dog handler, a Phoenix-Temple acolyte holding [the Foundation Stone](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_6/the-foundation-stone.md), and the warding amulet on Lena Oakes's gate at [the Weary Plowman](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). The party — currently in Tumbledown beneath cover — does not register because Tumbledown's natural necromantic energies wash out their signatures. The Sodality nevertheless sends a "**magical disturbance audit**" demand to every Outer City merchant flagged by the censers; six are summoned to [Sorcerous Sundries](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/sorcerous-sundries.md) for fines and licensing reviews. The dragnet does not find the party; it does, however, **alienate every honest small caster in Rivington** in a single tenday.
