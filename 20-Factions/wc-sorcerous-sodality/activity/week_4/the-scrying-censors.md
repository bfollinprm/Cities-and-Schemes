# The Scrying Censors

In response to the powerful unregistered teleport magic [Zora used to escape the watchtower ambush in week 2](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_2/silence-and-slander.md), to [the Hands of the Absolute's failed Sundries break-in this week](../../../hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_4/the-sundered-vault.md), and to the increasingly chaotic ambient arcane field around the Outer City, [Lorroakan's](../../people/lorroakan.md) investigators sign off on a permanent installation: **scrying censers** at [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) and the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md), wired to the Sodality's central registry. Any significant arcane signature passing through either checkpoint now wakes a Sodality watch officer. The censers replace [the simpler trace-arcane sensors of week 2](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_2/trace-the-arcane-signature.md) with always-on scrying. The party — currently camped in [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) without an active spell signature — is not flagged. Anyone trying to walk Zora-grade magic through the gates from this week forward is.
