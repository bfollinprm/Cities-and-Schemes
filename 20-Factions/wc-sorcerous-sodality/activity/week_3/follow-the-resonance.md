# Follow the Resonance

Sodality investigators, working from [the case file opened on Zora last week](../activity/week_2/trace-the-arcane-signature.md) and the censers seeded at [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) and the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md), follow [Zora's](../../people/zora.md) teleportation signature out of the [Phoenix Pits](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/phoenix-pits.md) into the chaotic ambient magic of [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) — and lose it. Her teleport is too powerful, the Outer City's residual emanations are too noisy, and the trail dissipates before they can pinpoint her destination. Officially, the Sodality now logs an unregistered, hostile combat-tier mage at large in the city; they cannot place her. Unofficially, [Lorroakan](../../people/lorroakan.md) shifts the case from "investigation" to "standing alert" and asks [the Cartographers](../../../wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_3/chart-the-chaos.md) for any siege-line residual-magic notes their surveyors are picking up. The censers stay live.
