# Analyze the Arcane Traffic

[The Sodality's permanent scrying censers, installed at Wyrm's Crossing and the Basilisk Gate last week](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_4/the-scrying-censors.md) under [Master Lorroakan's](../../people/lorroakan.md) authorization, are immediately overwhelmed. The arrival of [Commander K'laar's githyanki phalanx into Wyrm's Rock](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_4/an-alliance-of-steel-and-silver.md) generates a *constant* psionic resonance the censers were not calibrated for. The refugees streaming through New Elturel carry unshielded Avernal artifacts in coat pockets and refuse-piles. [The Hands of the Absolute's failed Heapside ritual lashes psionic feedback across the city](../../../hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_5/the-psychic-miasma.md) on Tendays-eve. The Sodality analysts at [Sorcerous Sundries](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/sorcerous-sundries.md) try to tune the censers up, then down, then off-and-on. The scrying sensors *crash* under the strain on the third day; the database itself is corrupted; ten days of arcane-signature logs degrade to noise. In the same window, **the fugitive mage Zora slips through the Basilisk Gate completely undetected** — her resonance signature, the entire reason the censers were built, lost in an unreadable hash. The Sodality's surveillance regime has been stood up, broken, and walked through in nine days.
