# Trace the Arcane Signature

A Sodality investigator dispatched by [Master Lorroakan](../../people/lorroakan.md) sweeps the ruined watchtower outside [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) where the [party survived an ambush](../../../party/activity/week_2/survive-the-watchtower-ambush.md), and reads the residue: high-tier *Cone of Cold*, a teleport hop, and *Absorb Elements* — none of it licensed. The Sodality opens an active case file on the fugitive mage **Zora** and installs **permanent scrying censers** at [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) and the Basilisk Gate to catch significant arcane crossings. Lorroakan also formalizes the [Sodality scrying ritual on Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_2/empower-the-old-guard.md) into an active operation at [Sorcerous Sundries](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/sorcerous-sundries.md).
