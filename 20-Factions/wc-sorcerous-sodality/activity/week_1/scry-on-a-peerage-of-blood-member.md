# Scry on a Peerage of Blood Member

The Master of the Sodality, [Lorroakan](../../people/lorroakan.md), has one of his licensed wizards run a scrying ritual on [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) — looking for personal secrets or political plans usable against the traditionalist bloc. The ritual lands cleanly; what it returns is now a held card, not a spent one. The Sodality keeps the contents to itself for the moment, banking it against future Parliament fights.
