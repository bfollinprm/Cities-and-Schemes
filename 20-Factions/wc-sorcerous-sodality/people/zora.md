# Zora

A fugitive mage. Subject of an open case file with the [Worshipful Company of Sorcerous Sodality](../summary.md) (Sorcerous Sundries) — Sodality Investigators have been chasing her arcane trail for some time.

## The trail

- **Powerful unlicensed magic** detected at a ruined watchtower; the Sodality's Investigators picked up the trace there.
- **Trail dissipated near the Phoenix Pits in New Elturel.** Lorroakan's investigators lost her there. Active.
- **Status: open.** The Sodality has neither caught her nor closed the file. The bigger the case stays open, the louder the political pressure on the Sodality to produce *somebody* in irons.
