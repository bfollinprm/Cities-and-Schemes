# Master Lorroakan

Master of the [Sorcerous Sodality](../summary.md) (or his successor — the position has been disputed at moments since the Absolute Crisis). The single most powerful licensor of magic in the city.

## Manifesto

> Magic is not a toy. It is a fire that can warm a city or burn it to ash. The unworthy and the untrained will not be allowed to play with it. We are the arbiters of the arcane. We determine who is fit to wield this power. We license, we regulate, we control. For a world without magical order is a world of pure chaos, and we are the wall that holds that chaos back.

## Role

Lorroakan operates from the tower of Sorcerous Sundries and presides over both the licensing office and the Sorcerous Vault. His cooperation with the [Council of Four](../../council-of-four/summary.md) on magical threats is contingent on the Sodality retaining its enforcement monopoly. The [Unveiled](../../unveiled/summary.md) consider him an architect of the conspiracy they're hunting; the warlock-pact cults consider him an enemy by definition.
