# Vlaakith

Lich-queen of the **Githyanki Empire**. The standing antagonist for [the Unchained](../summary.md) — the Outer City exile cell loyal to **Prince Orpheus**.

The Unchained's reason for being on the Material Plane is the war against Vlaakith. They are not refugees; they are a *training cadre in exile*. Vlaakith's reach is long enough that the Unchained's presence in Baldur's Gate is itself an operational risk — but the Outer City is far enough from her notice, for now.
