# The Astral Quarantine

[Commander K'laar, the Unshackled](../../people/k-laar.md) — having read [last tenday's psionic shockwave from the Hands of the Absolute as enemy intrusion](../../activity/week_6/the-psionic-sweep.md) — orders an **absolute astral quarantine** around [Wyrm's Rock](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md). Unchained psionicists ring the rock with a permanent psychic ward; anyone attempting to cross [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) without explicit Unchained authorization is **physically repelled** by a wave of nausea, vertigo, and *somebody-else's* memory.

The quarantine cuts foot-traffic across Wyrm's Crossing by two-thirds within three days. Refugees who once used the bridge as a shortcut now circuit a full mile downstream. Word reaches the [Council of Four](../../../council-of-four/summary.md) by midweek that an entire bridge of the city has been unilaterally closed by a foreign military faction with no charter to do so. K'laar declines to respond to the Council's first written inquiry. **The Unchained have asserted sovereignty over a piece of Baldurian infrastructure** in front of every refugee in the Outer City. The Council has not yet decided whether to escalate.
