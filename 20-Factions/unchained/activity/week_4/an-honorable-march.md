# An Honorable March

[Commander K'laar](../../people/k-laar.md) honors [last week's contract with Marshal Flint](../../../unchained/activity/week_3/an-honorable-contract.md). At dawn she leads a disciplined phalanx of githyanki warriors out of [the K'liir camp](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/the-kliir.md) in full astral battle gear: silver-leaf scale, drawn silver greatswords held high, no banners. They march in formation across [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md), through the Outer City crowds, up to the gates of [Wyrm's Rock](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md), where [Flint formally welcomes them in](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_4/an-alliance-of-steel-and-silver.md). The route is deliberate: every Hellrider, every Free Trader, every Watch tower along the bridge sees the contingent pass. The march itself is the message — the contract is real, the city's leadership has signed it, and these are the soldiers who will guard the Fortress that hosts the Marshal. K'laar's price for honoring the contract is silence on the diplomatic terms. Flint pays it.
