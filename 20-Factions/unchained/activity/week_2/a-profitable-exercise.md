# A Profitable Exercise

[K'laar, the Unshackled](../../people/k-laar.md) runs the [K'liir](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/the-kliir.md) through a week of internal training exercises and tactical simulations — formation drills, psionic-resistance practice, mock raids on a mocked-up Sembian warehouse layout. K'laar himself slips out for a side-job: brokering [the Sembian Kontor's quiet approach to Mad Meggan](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_2/test-the-barricade.md) for hard coin while the camp stays disciplined and visible. Combat readiness held; one psi-sensitive recruit on the camp periphery is now flagged by the [Hands of the Absolute](../../../hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_2/harvest-the-echo.md) as a future kidnapping target.
