# Begin Investigating Psychic Echoes

A Githyanki squad runs sweeps through the tenements adjacent to the [K'liir](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/the-kliir.md) camp on [K'laar's](../../people/k-laar.md) order, hunting the source of the psychic echoes their meditators keep flagging. The [Hands of the Absolute](../../../hands-of-the-absolute/summary.md) cell leaves no trace they can read; after a thorough but fruitless sweep, the squad reports up that the source could not be located and may have been transient. The investigation is shelved — exactly the result the Hands counted on.
