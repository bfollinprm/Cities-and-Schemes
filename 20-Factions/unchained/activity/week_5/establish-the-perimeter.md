# Establish the Perimeter

[Commander K'laar](../../people/k-laar.md), reading [the Archduke's ultimatum](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md) and [Marshal Flint's joint drills](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_5/the-integration-drills.md) as the conditions of the agreement, takes operational responsibility for the fortress perimeter. Githyanki warriors begin standing their own watch around [Wyrm's Rock and Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md) — silent, unmoving, *psionically aware* in the way the Fist patrols never were. A pickpocket attempting the bridge crowd is identified before he raises a hand. A Sodality acolyte trying to map the curtain wall is identified, [stripped, mind-wiped, and deposited naked at the bridge end](../../../wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_5/mapping-the-battlefield.md). Nothing crosses the bridge that K'laar's warriors have not noticed. **The fortress's security regime is now absolute.** It is also unsettling for the Outer City crowd that has to walk past it twice a day; the merchants of Wyrm's Bend report a noticeable chill on traffic by midweek. K'laar does not care. The perimeter is a perimeter. The Free Traders' inner barricade is a different problem on a different gate.
