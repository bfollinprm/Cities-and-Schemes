# An Honorable Contract

A discreet agent of [Marshal Flint](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) approaches [Commander K'laar](../../people/k-laar.md) at [the K'liir](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/the-kliir.md) with a serious offer: a lucrative neutral peacekeeping contract at [Wyrm's Rock](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md), explicitly disclaiming political allegiance. K'laar takes it under serious consideration. The terms are clean — payment in finished steel, billeting under [the K'laar's](../../people/k-laar.md) own command, no oath of fealty to the Council or to the Marshal personally — and the implication, that the Fist's own discipline cannot be relied on once the Archduke's mercenaries enter the city, is a frank acknowledgement K'laar respects. No agreement is signed this week, but the door is open, and [Flint's Wyrm's Rock consolidation](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_3/circle-the-wagons.md) starts to look less like fortification and more like preparation for a guard rotation that includes githyanki.
