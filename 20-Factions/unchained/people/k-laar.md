# Commander K'laar, the Unshackled

Commander of [The Unchained](../summary.md), founder of the K'liir camp in the Outer City. A pragmatic Githyanki strategist loyal to Prince Orpheus, who chose Baldur's Gate over the Astral Sea as the place to gather strength for the war on Vlaakith.

## Manifesto

> *Tsk'va*. You see this world. We see a stepping stone. Your city is a place of refuge, a place to hone our blades and minds for the True War. Prince Orpheus liberated this plane; we will not see it fall. We do not ask for your friendship, only your tolerance. Do not mistake our patience for peace.

## Role

K'laar runs K'liir as a strict military encampment. She brokers selective mercenary contracts through the [Sembian Kontor](../../sembian-kontor/summary.md) — only honorable work — and refuses any contract that would put her warriors in the role of private muscle for banking disputes (notably refusing an Amnian Kontor offer in week 0). She treats the [Hands of the Absolute](../../hands-of-the-absolute/summary.md) as a standing extermination target.
