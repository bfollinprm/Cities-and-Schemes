# Commander K'laar, the Unshackled

Pragmatic Githyanki commander of the [Unchained](../summary.md) and founder of the **K'liir** in Baldur's Gate. A loyalist to Prince Orpheus who chose, after the Netherbrain's defeat, to gather strength here rather than rejoin the fragmented rebellion above the [Astral Sea](../../../30-Places/astral-sea.md).

## Manifesto

> *Tsk'va*. You see this world. We see a stepping stone. Your city is a place of refuge, a place to hone our blades and minds for the True War. Prince Orpheus liberated this plane; we will not see it fall. We do not ask for your friendship, only your tolerance. Do not mistake our patience for peace. Our war is in the [Astral Sea](../../../30-Places/astral-sea.md), but we will finish any fight started here.

## Role

K'laar runs the K'liir as a permanent military encampment. She approves every mercenary contract personally and is famous for refusing more than she accepts. She holds her warriors to a strict martial code, and the camp's discipline is its most visible feature to Baldurians — disturbingly visible, as far as the [Unveiled](../../unveiled/summary.md) are concerned.

## Current pressures

- **Declined Amnian contract.** K'laar refused a lucrative [Amnian Kontor](../../amnian-kontor/summary.md) offer to act as private muscle, telling the agent the work was without honor and that her warriors are not for rent to settle banking disputes.
- **Psychic-echo investigation.** Patrols dispatched around the K'liir to investigate **psychic echoes** found nothing. The [Hands of the Absolute](../../hands-of-the-absolute/summary.md) cell operating nearby leaves no traces, and the squad concluded — incorrectly — that the source could not be located.
- **[Wyrm's Rock](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md) garrison.** Approached by [Marshal Flint](../../flaming-fist/summary.md) with a contract to garrison Wyrm's Rock as a neutral peacekeeping force — a contract she seriously considered for its honorable framing and lack of political allegiance, and ultimately accepted. A disciplined phalanx of Githyanki marched in full battle gear from the K'liir, across [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md), and into Wyrm's Rock — solidifying the alliance and shocking the city's leadership.
- **The Sembian failed-scouts.** A Sembian scouting team sent toward the Whisperwood was captured by the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md); the survivor was sent back with a warning. The Sembians have approached K'laar separately about brokering more reliable contracts.
- **Internal training.** Despite political pressure from outside, K'laar's first instinct is always to drill: a recent successful series of internal exercises and tactical simulations maintained the camp's combat readiness through the [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) crisis.
