# Western Gate Trading Company

An influential and politically powerful consortium, backed by patriar families and led by the sitting Archduke, focused on controlling the overland caravan routes and the exploitation of the city's hinterland resources. Led by [Matron Lyra Silvershield](people/lyra-silvershield.md), the current Archduke.

## Ideology

Secure and control the land trade routes into Baldur's Gate and the resources — timber, ore, stone — of the surrounding territories. The Company represents the interests of patriar families looking to expand their wealth through control of raw materials.

## Membership

A consortium of powerful patriar families and the guilds they control, all with interests in land-based resources and trade. Led by the politically formidable Matron Lyra Silvershield, who leveraged the Company's wealth into the Archducal seat.

## Methods

- Uses immense political power — especially with their leader as Archduke — to secure favorable charters, land grants, and tariffs.
- Aggressive expansion of logging and quarrying operations in the lands surrounding the city, including a recent clear-cut of the **[Whisperwood Copse](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/hinterlands/whisperwood-copse.md)**.
- Hires mercenaries (brokered through the [Sembian Kontor](../sembian-kontor/summary.md)) to "secure" logging routes against the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md).
- Recently authorized [Upper City Watch](../watch/summary.md) patrols on the new logging routes — a controversial overreach onto [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md) jurisdiction.
- **Private mercenary army inside the city (week 3).** After [the Council and Parliament both fail to remove Marshal Flint](activity/week_3/the-private-option.md), Lyra Silvershield contracted a small elite mercenary company through [Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../sembian-kontor/people/korgan-ironhand.md) on personal coin — soldiers who answer to her, not to the Fist or to any chamber.
- **Eastway barracks deployment (week 4).** Silvershield [orders the company into a fortified Eastway camp inside the Basilisk Gate](../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md), [requisitions a WGTC warehouse for it](activity/week_4/the-barracks-requisition.md), and gets [a "City-Sanctioned Peacekeepers" contract filed by Korgan Ironhand](../sembian-kontor/activity/week_4/legitimizing-the-contract.md) to give the deployment legal cover. [The Peerage of Coin's Emergency Levy](../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_4/the-emergency-levy.md) now uses the city's own commerce to pay the company's upkeep. The mercenaries are no longer a private retainer — they are, on paper, an arm of municipal authority.
- **Rural-road interdiction (week 5).** Following [the Archduke's three-day ultimatum](../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md), WGTC caravan-masters and Sembian-supplied detachments [stand up new checkpoints on the rural roads ringing the Outer City](activity/week_5/choke-the-supply-lines.md), seizing food and water shipments bound for the Free Trader blockade. **Supply-line interdiction on rural-road chokepoints is now established WGTC operational doctrine** for the duration of the political war.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** Direct competition with the Foreign Kontors over bulk goods, especially the [Sembian Kontor](../sembian-kontor/summary.md). Their aggressive logging and quarrying puts them in open conflict with the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md) and other wilderness protectors.
