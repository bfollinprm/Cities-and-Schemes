# Western Gate Trading Company

An influential and politically powerful consortium, backed by patriar families and led by the sitting Archduke, focused on controlling the overland caravan routes and the exploitation of the city's hinterland resources. Led by [Matron Lyra Silvershield](people/lyra-silvershield.md), the current Archduke.

## Ideology

Secure and control the land trade routes into Baldur's Gate and the resources — timber, ore, stone — of the surrounding territories. The Company represents the interests of patriar families looking to expand their wealth through control of raw materials.

## Membership

A consortium of powerful patriar families and the guilds they control, all with interests in land-based resources and trade. Led by the politically formidable Matron Lyra Silvershield, who leveraged the Company's wealth into the Archducal seat.

## Methods

- Uses immense political power — especially with their leader as Archduke — to secure favorable charters, land grants, and tariffs.
- Aggressive expansion of logging and quarrying operations in the lands surrounding the city, including a recent clear-cut of the **Whisperwood Copse**.
- Hires mercenaries (brokered through the [Sembian Kontor](../sembian-kontor/summary.md)) to "secure" logging routes against the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md).
- Recently authorized [Upper City Watch](../watch/summary.md) patrols on the new logging routes — a controversial overreach onto [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md) jurisdiction.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** Direct competition with the Foreign Kontors over bulk goods, especially the [Sembian Kontor](../sembian-kontor/summary.md). Their aggressive logging and quarrying puts them in open conflict with the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md) and other wilderness protectors.
