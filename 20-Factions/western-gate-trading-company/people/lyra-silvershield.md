# Matron Lyra Silvershield

Matron of the [Western Gate Trading Company](../summary.md) and the current Archduke of Baldur's Gate. The single most politically consolidated figure in the city — chief of a patriar-funded trading consortium and head of its executive government in the same breath.

## Manifesto

> A city does not end at its walls. It lives or dies by the roads that feed it and the land that sustains it. The forests provide its timber, the mountains its stone, the caravans its lifeblood. We are the guardians of that lifeblood. The Western Gate is not merely an entrance to the city; it is our claim on the lands beyond. The land is our birthright, and we will not allow foreign interests to feast on our inheritance.

## Role

Like the [Chionthar Consortium](../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md), the Western Gate Trading Company was forged from the consolidation of older merchant guilds and powerful patriar families to compete with the Foreign Kontors. By focusing on overland trade and raw materials, they carved out a powerful niche, and Silvershield leveraged that wealth to become Archduke — fusing the consortium's commercial agenda with the city's executive seat.

She holds the power to ratify legislation passed by the [Parliament of Peers](../../parliament-of-peers/summary.md) and directs the (chronically underfunded) [Flaming Fist](../../flaming-fist/summary.md). Her recent authorization of [Watch](../../watch/summary.md) patrols on private logging routes has angered [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../peerage-of-blood/summary.md) and the rest of the [Peerage of Blood](../../peerage-of-blood/summary.md), who see it as using city resources for private gain.
