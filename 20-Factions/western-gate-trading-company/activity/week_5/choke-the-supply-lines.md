# Choke the Supply Lines

Within hours of [Archduke Silvershield's ultimatum](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md), [WGTC](../../summary.md) caravan-masters and [Sembian-supplied mercenary detachments](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_5/arming-the-aggressors.md) fan out onto the rural roads ringing the [Outer City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). New checkpoints rise at every cart-track approach to the Basilisk Gate — Rivington side, Coast Way side, the long Trade Way bend. Carts carrying grain, salt fish, fresh water, anything edible bound for [Meggan's blockade](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_5/heed-the-ultimatum.md) are stopped, inventoried, and *seized* under the legal cover of the City-Sanctioned Peacekeepers contract. Drivers are turned back; cargo is requisitioned to the Eastway barracks mess. The intent is plain and openly stated by the company captain at the Rivington junction: *starve the blockade out before the three-day clock runs*. The Hellrider–Free Trader alliance now has a logistics problem the inner barricade cannot solve. **Supply-line interdiction on the rural roads is, by week's end, an established WGTC operational doctrine.**
