# The Starvation Siege

[Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../people/lyra-silvershield.md) tightens the noose around the Outer City. Beyond [last week's interception of commercial shipments on the rural roads](../../activity/week_5/choke-the-supply-lines.md), her Sembian mercenaries now confiscate **personal food stores** — flour bags, dried meat, cheese rounds — off any traveller crossing into the city, regardless of paperwork. The seizures are loud, public, and aimed at one outcome: starve [the Basilisk Gate blockade](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_5/heed-the-ultimatum.md) into surrender rather than storm it.

The cruelty is working. Outer City families that were one bad week from rationing are now there. By midweek the soup-line at the [Open Hand Temple](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/open-hand-temple.md) is twice last week's length and a third the volume. Every family the patrols rob becomes a recruitment poster for the Free Traders. The Archduke does not care. The political cost is owned by the Council; the food is owned by her.
