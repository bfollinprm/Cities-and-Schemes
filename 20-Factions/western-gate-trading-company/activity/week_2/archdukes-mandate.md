# Archduke's Mandate

[Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../people/lyra-silvershield.md) issues a direct order to the Flaming Fist to forcibly disperse the [Free Traders' siege at the Basilisk Gate](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md). [Marshal Valerius Flint](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) refuses on the public ground that storming the line would trigger a refugee uprising — and on the private ground that the [Plague of Remembrance](../../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) is hunting for exactly that pretext. The refusal is the first open public crack between the city's political and military leadership. The Western Gate's caravan revenues continue to bleed; Silvershield orders Watch patrols extended into the [hinterlands](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/hinterlands/README.md) and quietly buys ['alternate route' maps from the Cartographers](../../../wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_2/monetize-the-maze.md) to keep her own caravans moving.
