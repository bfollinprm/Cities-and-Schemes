# Deploy Watch to 'Secure' Logging Routes

Citing "banditry" — a euphemism for the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md) — Archduke [Lyra Silvershield](../../people/lyra-silvershield.md) authorizes [Upper City Watch](../../../watch/summary.md) patrols along the Western Gate's new logging routes outside the walls. Watch sergeants oblige despite obvious overreach onto [Flaming Fist](../../../flaming-fist/summary.md) ground. The deployment angers [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md), who frames it publicly as Watch resources subsidizing one patriar's private enterprise.
