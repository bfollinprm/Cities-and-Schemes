# First Blood in the Whisperwood

The Sembian mercenary band Archduke [Lyra Silvershield](../../people/lyra-silvershield.md) hired through the [Sembian Kontor](../../../sembian-kontor/summary.md) draws first blood against the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md) near the [Whisperwood Copse](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/hinterlands/wood-of-sharp-teeth/README.md). A druid is wounded; two mercenaries are injured before the Circle melts back into the trees. The skirmish converts a logging dispute into an open frontier fight and gives the [Watch](../../../watch/summary.md) deployment a body count to point at.

> Source-faction note: rolled into Western Gate per the issue-27 directive folding "Sembian Mercenaries" into the Western Gate Trading Company for week 1.
