# The Private Option

Furious at [Marshal Flint's](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) public insubordination and stripped of any Council route to break the siege after [her no-confidence motion fails](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_3/the-vote-of-no-confidence.md) and [the Peerage's mercenary act is filibustered](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_3/whip-the-votes.md), [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../people/lyra-silvershield.md) opens her personal coffers and contracts a small, elite mercenary company through the [Sembian Kontor](../../../sembian-kontor/summary.md). [Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../../sembian-kontor/people/korgan-ironhand.md) signs the paper at the Iron Ledger; the contract is private, the soldiers are not Fist, and they answer only to her. The plan is simple: when the political route closes, the Free Trader blockade gets broken at the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) by men whose payroll she controls. The implications — a privately-funded army inside the city, accountable to no chamber and no Marshal — are still concealed, but they will not stay so.
