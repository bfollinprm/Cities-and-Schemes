# The Barracks Requisition

To house her newly-deployed private army, [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../people/lyra-silvershield.md) uses Council authority to requisition a large Western Gate Trading Company warehouse in [Eastway](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md), inside the Basilisk Gate. The warehouse is converted in days into barracks, mess, and command post for the mercenary commander. The requisition is technically legal, technically temporary, and unmistakably permanent in feel: the WGTC's own logistics arm is now the bunkhouse for the Archduke's [Council-ordered shield-wall in Eastway](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md). The patriar caravan masters who stored their goods here are paid out and asked, politely, to find storage elsewhere.
