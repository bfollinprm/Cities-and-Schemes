# Sigil of Repulsion

[Calliope, the Silent Muse](../../people/calliope.md) stages a "counter-memetic" ballad outside a [Chionthar Consortium warehouse on the Lower City docks](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md), targeting the Wyrm's Crossing crowd with a public exorcism of "foreign cultural contamination." It is met with widespread ridicule. The performance becomes the joke of the week in [Baldur's Mouth](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_2/fan-the-flames.md) sketch coverage, and the Society slips from threat to laughingstock in the same news cycle that takes [the Elturel Remembrance Society](../../../elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_2/infernal-residue.md) down with it.
