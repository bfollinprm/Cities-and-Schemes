# Deface a Kontor-Funded Mural

[Calliope's](../../people/calliope.md) followers plan a midnight defacement of the new Amnian-funded mural in [Heapside](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md). A paid informant tips the [Amnian Kontor](../../../amnian-kontor/summary.md), whose Coin-Sword detail is already in position. The Society is ambushed and chased off without a single thrown bucket of paint. The Kontor frames the foiled vandalism in *[Baldur's Mouth](../../../baldurs-mouth/summary.md)* as proof of nativist criminality, costing the Society popular sympathy.
