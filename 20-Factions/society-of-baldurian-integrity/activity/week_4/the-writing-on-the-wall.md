# The Writing on the Wall

[Calliope, the Silent Muse](../../people/calliope.md), reading [the Archduke's Eastway barracks](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md) as a *cultural* incursion — foreign mercenaries garrisoned on Baldurian stone — sends a midnight raid of nationalist followers to paint slogans on the new barracks walls. The mercenary barracks is well-guarded. Disciplined sentries spot the followers from the rooftop overwatch before a single brush touches plaster. A short, ugly chase through Eastway alleys ends with several of Calliope's followers caught and beaten down by mercenary patrols, then handed over to [the Watch](../../../watch/summary.md) for charging. The Archduke's command tent now has *prisoners to interrogate* about the nativist movement — and a Society activist's name on a sworn statement for the first time. Calliope, who was a vague rumor a week ago, is now in a Watch file.
