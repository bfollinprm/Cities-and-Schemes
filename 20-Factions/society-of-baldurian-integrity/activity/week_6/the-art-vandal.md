# The Art Vandal

[Calliope](../../people/calliope.md) targets the **new Waterdhavian Kontor statues** erected in the Upper City plazas during last month's Splendid Yard cultural mission — three life-size Lords' Alliance allegorical figures in pale marble, paid for and installed by [Factor Mornwood's](../../../waterdhavian-kontor/people/elara-mornwood.md) cultural office. The Society's nightlings move through the gate-locked Upper City after dark (using the Heapside-tunnel route, since [the Watch's gate lockdown](../../../watch/activity/week_6/the-gate-lockdown.md) closes the surface routes) and **deface the foreign idols with paint bombs filled with distinctively Baldurian sluiceway mud** — black, faintly fishy, and recognizably Lower-City.

By dawn the statues are coated and a small printing run of pamphlets is scattered around them: *"Erasure of Local Culture — A Reply in Mud."* The pamphlets are unsigned but written in Calliope's recognizable cadence. The Watch is too humiliated to call attention to the breach (the lockdown was supposed to make this impossible); the Splendid Yard cultural office is publicly furious and privately uncertain whether to demand prosecution or restore the statues quietly. Mornwood, [already navigating the Harper-immunity scandal](../../../waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_6/the-diplomatic-immunity.md), files no formal complaint. **The Society scores a clean cultural-vandalism propaganda hit on a Sunday morning, and the Upper City patriars wake up to find their gilded cage is also a public latrine.**
