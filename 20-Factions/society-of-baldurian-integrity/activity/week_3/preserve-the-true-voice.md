# Preserve the True Voice

[Calliope, the Silent Muse](../../people/calliope.md) marches her followers to [High Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md) during the [parliamentary debate](../../../parliament-of-peers/activity/week_3/vote-of-no-confidence.md). They do not protest. They form a continuous, somber chant of ancient Baldurian folk ballads outside the Hall's doors, framed as a "psychic reinforcement" of the [Peerage of Blood](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_3/filibuster-the-mercenary-act.md)'s resolve. The actual effect is more subtle: the chant gives the Baldurian-tradition narrative a public soundtrack while [Durinbold filibusters inside](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md), and patriar voters leaving the chamber walk past their own city's hymns. The Society earns the warmest patriar reception it has had in months, and a tacit invitation to the next [Heritage Society gala](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_3/defend-the-marshal.md).
