# The Sonic Cleansing

[Calliope, the Silent Muse](../../people/calliope.md) — convinced after a tenday of failed exorcisms that the Elfsong Tavern's resident ghost is *singing in a foreign mode* — leads four cantors into the [Elfsong](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/elfsong-tavern.md) at midnight with **enchanted tuning forks** tuned to a "True Baldurian Major" interval. The plan is to drown the foreign lament under a booming traditional Baldurian sea shanty until the ghost capitulates or evaporates.

The first chord wakes every drunk in the Elfsong. The shanty starts strong. Three minutes in, the ghost adopts the shanty's melody and begins singing it in counterpoint, raising the dust off three centuries of beam-tops. The room *pauses* — *the song is genuinely beautiful*. A barmaid weeps. A dwarven merchant proposes marriage to a rival merchant. Calliope's cleansing has produced not exorcism but **cultural fusion**. She is humiliated; the city has a new Elfsong duet; and the Society's Baldurian-purity doctrine has just been refuted *publicly* by its own founder. Membership inquiries quietly stop the next morning.
