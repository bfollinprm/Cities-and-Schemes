# Society of Baldurian Integrity

A cultural-purity faction convinced the unique "spirit" of Baldur's Gate is being killed by memetic warfare from foreign powers — Waterdeep, Sembia, and the [Harpers](../harpers/summary.md) above all. Founded and led by [Calliope, the Silent Muse](people/calliope.md), a former playwright whose magnum opus was eclipsed by a Waterdhavian troupe and who now leads a crusade against "pretty poisons." One of the four Conspiracist cells.

## Ideology

There is a magic no one talks about: memetic warfare. The "spirit" of Baldur's Gate is a kind of psychic entity, and foreign powers (led by Waterdeep and the Harpers) are working to kill it. Clashing architectural styles are sigils of chaos disrupting the city's harmony. The ghostly lament in the Elfsong Tavern is a sonic plague instilling subconscious sadness. The Circus of the Last Days is an extraplanar beachhead for thought-forms invading dreams.

The Society's mission: purge the city of all "memetically impure" influences before the Baldurian soul is replaced.

## Membership

Disgruntled local artists, fiercely nativist patriars from the [Patriar Heritage Society](../patriar-heritage-society/summary.md), tavern owners who only serve local ale, and young idealists. The Society maintains lists: banned songs, architectural features to be vandalized, artists to be "re-educated."

## Methods

- "Cultural counter-terrorism." Heckling bards singing Waterdhavian ballads (week 0: heckled a Waterdhavian bard at the Singing Lute, attempted DC 5, failed). Splashing paint on Sembian-funded buildings (week 1: red paint on Sembian-style statues at the Jannath Estate, dismissed as "youthful hijinks" by the [Watch](../watch/summary.md)).
- Attempted exorcism of the "foreign ghost" from the Elfsong Tavern.
- Calliope's paladin-of-conspiracy abilities: making people perceive "hidden psychic barbs" in foreign art.
- Quiet coordination with the [Patriar Heritage Society](../patriar-heritage-society/summary.md), with whom membership overlaps.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** The [Waterdhavian Kontor](../waterdhavian-kontor/summary.md) (the conspiracy's heart). The [Harpers](../harpers/summary.md) (the conspiracy's secret agents). Any non-Baldurian artist, especially the [Carnival of Whispers](../carnival-of-whispers/summary.md) / Circus of the Last Days. The [Sembian Kontor](../sembian-kontor/summary.md) for its architectural patronage.

**Allies of opportunity.** The [Patriar Heritage Society](../patriar-heritage-society/summary.md), with shared nativist agenda.
