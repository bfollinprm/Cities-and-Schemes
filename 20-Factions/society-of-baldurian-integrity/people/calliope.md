# Calliope, the Silent Muse

Founder and leader of the [Society of Baldurian Integrity](../summary.md). A former playwright and failed artist now leading a crusade against the "cultural contamination" of Baldur's Gate. Oath of Conspiracy paladin sworn to preserve the city's soul.

## Conspiracy theory

There is a magic no one talks about: memetic warfare. The unique, stubborn, resilient "spirit" of Baldur's Gate is a kind of psychic entity, and foreign powers — led by Waterdeep, with the [Harpers](../../harpers/summary.md) as their agents — are trying to kill it. Architectural styles are "sigils of chaos." The Elfsong Tavern's ghost is a "sonic plague" instilling nostalgia for a weaker time. The extraplanar Circus of the Last Days is a beachhead for invading thought-forms. The goal is to make Baldurians forget who they are, to soften them into placid consumers.

## Backstory

Calliope's magnum opus, *The Ballad of the Blushing Mermaid*, a play celebrating the city's roguish, independent spirit, was a colossal flop — overshadowed by a Waterdhavian troupe performing elegant, sterile dramas. In her bitterness, she had a revelation: her play hadn't failed. It had been *suppressed*. Every cultural shift since became evidence of the same memetic war. She swore an oath to the city's soul and now wages war against the "pretty poisons" of foreign art.
