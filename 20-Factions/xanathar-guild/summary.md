# The Xanathar Guild

Waterdeep's underworld empire. Beholder-led. Operates out of the Undercity tunnels and the subterranean port of **Skullport**, with surface fronts throughout the Dock and Trades Wards.

## Ideology

Less an ideology than an appetite. The Guild wants the city's underworld whole and obedient. Not territory by territory: *all of it*. Where Manshoon plays for the Open Lord's chair, the Xanathar plays for the city's basement and below.

## Methods

- **Black Lilly.** A new addictive narcotic distributed at industrial scale. Hooks the Field Ward and the Dock; debt to the Guild follows.
- **Slavery.** Through Skullport. Long-running and openly acknowledged in the right rooms.
- **Protection rackets.** Standard but disciplined.
- **Intellect-devourer puppetry.** [Nihiloor](people/nihiloor.md), a mind-flayer lieutenant, plants intellect devourers inside citizens of consequence — known case: **Meloon Wardragon**. The puppets sit in offices, in Watch barracks, in noble households.

## The Shadow War

Open hostility with **Manshoon's** Zhentarim faction since the 1491 DR Schism. Public street violence in the Dock and Trades Wards is the war's surface; the deeper layer is competing infiltration of the Watch, the Magisters, and the noble houses. The **Doom Raiders** (the modern profit-first Zhentarim under Davil Starsong) keep their distance and wait the war out.

## Geography

- **Skullport** — subterranean port; the Guild's major access route.
- **Waterdeep Undercity** — the tunnel network connecting Skullport to surface Dock Ward cellars.
- **Dock Ward fronts** — taverns, warehouses, brothels.
