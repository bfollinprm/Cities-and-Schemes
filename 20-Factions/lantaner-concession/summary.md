# Lantaner Concession *(aka The Cogwork Quay)*

A secretive and technologically advanced gnomish enclave, known as the **Cogwork Quay**, holding a state-sanctioned monopoly on the creation and sale of the volatile but effective smokepowder. Led by [Chief Artificer Glim-Nimblewhiz](people/glim-nimblewhiz.md).

## Ideology

Technological supremacy. The Concession's primary goal is to protect Lantan's technological secrets at all costs while engaging in industrial espionage to acquire new knowledge for itself.

## Membership

Led by Chief Artificer Glim-Nimblewhiz. Staffed entirely by gnomes from Lantan, all bound by an obsessive code of secrecy. Outsiders are not permitted past the Quay's outer halls.

## Methods

- Operates with extreme secrecy behind the walls of the Cogwork Quay.
- Sole legal source of smokepowder; sales are restricted, heavily monitored, and largely contracted to the [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md) and patriar private guards.
- Stages dazzling public demonstrations of trivial technology to mask the true work happening behind closed doors.
- Targets industrial spies aggressively; recently lost a partial smokepowder schematic to an apprentice tied to the [Worshipful Company of Smiths & Armorers](../wc-smiths-armorers/summary.md).

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** Every faction with an interest in technology — including the local consortiums, rival Kontors, and the [Worshipful Company of Smiths & Armorers](../wc-smiths-armorers/summary.md) — would happily steal their secrets. They are also the obsession of [The Purified](../purified/summary.md), whose paranoid leader believes the Concession is dosing the water supply with mind-controlling clockwork.
