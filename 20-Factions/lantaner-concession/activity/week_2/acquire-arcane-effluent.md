# Acquire Arcane Effluent

[Chief Artificer Glim-Nimblewhiz](../../people/glim-nimblewhiz.md) field-tests a new clockwork reconnaissance device — a small mechanical sparrow seeded into the Lower City logistic flow — and uses it to map [Sembian Kontor](../../../sembian-kontor/summary.md) warehouse rotations and security protocols at the [Iron Ledger](../../../sembian-kontor/summary.md). The intelligence is significant; the device returns intact. Cogwork Quay quietly logs the test as a successful prototype graduation. The Sembians do not know the audit happened.
