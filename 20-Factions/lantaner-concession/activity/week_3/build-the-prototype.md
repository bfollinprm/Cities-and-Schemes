# Build the Prototype

[Chief Artificer Glim-Nimblewhiz](../../people/glim-nimblewhiz.md) pushes the Cogwork Courier prototype through its first major systems test at [the Cogwork Quay](../../summary.md) in the Lower City. A flaw in the [arcane effluent](../activity/week_2/acquire-arcane-effluent.md)-fed power core overloads the prototype mid-test and triggers a small but potent smokepowder explosion inside the workshop. The accident destroys the prototype, injures two artificers (one badly), blackens a wall of the Quay, and sets the project back weeks. The smoke is visible from [Bloomridge](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/bloomridge.md). The [Worshipful Company of Smiths & Armorers](../../../wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_3/an-appeal-to-the-marshal.md) will use the explosion as evidence of Lantaner recklessness in their next petition; [the Purified](../../../purified/activity/week_3/analyze-the-cure.md) will use it as evidence of mind-control nano-cog technology gone unstable. Glim-Nimblewhiz's reaction to either is unprintable.
