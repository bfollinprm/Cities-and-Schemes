# The Foundry Scavenge

[Chief Artificer Glim-Nimblewhiz](../../people/glim-nimblewhiz.md), needing rare alloys to rebuild the prototype lost in last cycle's Cogwork Quay explosion, mounts a clandestine, high-risk expedition into the defunct **Steel Watch Foundry** in [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md). The Foundry has been shuttered since the close of the Absolute Crisis and the inactive constructs inside have been sealed off by city writ — but the alloys plated into their joint-rings are exactly what Glim-Nimblewhiz needs. Three rope-teams cross the Foundry roof under cover of dock-side noise. Saws come out. By dawn they have stripped two constructs of their forearm plating, a condenser core, and most of a steam regulator. They depart leaving the Foundry's main floor convincingly undisturbed. Glim-Nimblewhiz's workshop is back at full operational tempo by mid-week. The Foundry's seal is technically intact; the dwarven seal-master who walks the building once a tenday is the next problem. **The Lantaners now hold a haul of post-Crisis Steel Watch alloy nobody else in the city has touched.**
