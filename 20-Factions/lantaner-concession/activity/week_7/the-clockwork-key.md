# The Clockwork Key

[Chief Artificer Glim-Nimblewhiz](../../people/glim-nimblewhiz.md) finalizes [the prototype assembly from last week](../../activity/week_6/the-prototype-assembly.md): a palm-sized geared cylinder calibrated to **instantly seal or open any mechanical gate in the city** — Watch portcullis, Counting House vault, Hellrider stockade, Lantaner workshop door, the Steel Watch Foundry's master sluice. The device exploits standardized Cogwork mechanisms the Concession itself supplied across two decades of city contracts.

Covert field-tests across [the Cogwork Quay](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) confirm the key works on every Lantaner-built fixture in three districts. Glim-Nimblewhiz keeps the prototype sealed inside a ledger-vault with two Cogwork sentries on rotation. The Concession now possesses a **silent override on the city's locked architecture**. They have not used it in earnest yet. The decision of *when* and *for whom* will define the next month of Cogwork Quay politics.
