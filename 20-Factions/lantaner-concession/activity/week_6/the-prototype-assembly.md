# The Prototype Assembly

Using the alloys [Glim-Nimblewhiz's team scavenged from the Steel Watch Foundry last week](../../activity/week_5/the-foundry-scavenge.md), the [Cogwork Quay's](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/events.md) artificers begin the delicate assembly of their new defensive prototype. The Quay echoes day and night with the sound of grinding gears, arcing electricity, and the throat-clearing of small gnomish argument; **the prototype is intended as a one-shot weapon big enough to break a mercenary advance and small enough to be paid for from the Concession's monthly profit**. The schematic is closed-cell — only [Chief Artificer Glim-Nimblewhiz](../../people/glim-nimblewhiz.md), two senior gnomes, and a single Sembian-paid bodyguard see the full design.

Assembly is mostly working. There are setbacks: a coil burns out twice on the third day; the steam reservoir fails to seal against pressure on the fifth; an obviously-Sembian *thief* is caught at the perimeter on the sixth day and the Concession's security team quietly drowns him in the dock. The party-rumors carried in [the Baldur's Mouth this week](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_6/the-siege-edition.md) — that the Concession is "building a massive construct using scavenged Foundry parts" — are correct in shape and wrong in scale. **The prototype is real, not yet finished, and very, very small for the size of the threat the Concession is preparing for.** [The Purified's water-watch panic](../../../purified/activity/week_6/the-water-watch.md) will be louder this week; that, ironically, gives the Concession cover.
