# Post an Anonymous Bounty

To recover the mechanical parts the [Wharf Rats](../../../wharf-rats/summary.md) "lost" off a Cogwork Quay crate in week 0, Chief Artificer [Glim-Nimblewhiz](../../people/glim-nimblewhiz.md) routes a generous, anonymous bounty for "lost clockwork mechanisms" through a third-party fence at the Elfsong Tavern. Admitting the breach publicly would be worse than paying for the parts back; the Concession would rather buy from a thief than concede a security gap.
