# Finance the Smugglers

The [Oberon family](../../people/oberon-family.md) approaches the city's newest celebrity adventuring company at the [Blushing Mermaid](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md) with a financing offer for an expedition. The party — newly **6,000 gp** richer from [the Mad Meggan deal](../../../party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md) and now operating without obvious financial pressure — declines the Oberons' purse outright. The offer was insultingly small for what the Coster reads as the party's apparent means; the Oberons will need to redirect their adventurer-bankrolling pivot elsewhere.
