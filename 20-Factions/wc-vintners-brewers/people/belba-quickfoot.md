# Master Belba Quickfoot

Master of the [Worshipful Company of Vintners & Brewers](../summary.md). A halfling whose unassuming manner conceals a sharp grasp of distribution networks, parliamentary horse-trading, and the precise vintage that will buy a Peerage vote.

## Manifesto

> A deal is not struck, a friendship is not forged, a sorrow is not drowned without our say-so. We are the masters of fellowship and the brewers of courage. The nobles in their halls and the sailors in their cups all drink from our barrels. We do not sell wine and ale; we sell laughter, solace, and the lubrication for every transaction of power in this city.

## Role

Quickfoot maintains the guild's monopoly through licensing fees, raids on unlicensed distilleries, and the Vintners' seat in the [Peerage of Coin](../../peerage-of-coin/summary.md). She is a regular at private tastings in patriar manors and works The Guild's Ward Bosses for protection of her distribution lanes through the Lower City. In week 1 she successfully bribed a Peerage of Coin member with priceless Amnian wine to defeat a luxury-tax proposal.
