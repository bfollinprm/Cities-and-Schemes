# Stem the Illicit Tide

[Master Belba Quickfoot's](../../people/belba-quickfoot.md) inspectors raid an unlicensed distillery in [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) and shut it down hard — barrels staved, mash dumped, owner publicly fined at the Weary Plowman door. The crackdown sends a clear message to Outer-City tavern keepers about defying the Company's monopoly. The Vintners walk back from the [week-1 wine bribe of a Peerage of Coin member](../../../wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_1/bribe-a-peerage-of-coin-member.md) and back into a regulator role, restoring some lost reputational ground. The Rivington still-runner the Wharf Rats had been backing loses his market overnight.
