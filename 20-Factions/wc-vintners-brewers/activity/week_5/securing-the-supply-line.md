# Securing the Supply Line

[Master Belba Quickfoot](../../people/belba-quickfoot.md), having moved [the Vintners into a public role as informal house-brewer of the Eastway barracks last week](../../../wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_4/a-new-patron.md), entrusts a major shipment of fine ales to [Silas the Eel's River-Rats](../../../river-rats/people/silas-the-eel.md) — a long-standing courier relationship she trusts more than she should this week, given [Silas's standing has just collapsed](../../../river-rats/activity/week_5/the-smokepowder-run.md). The casks never reach the [mercenary barracks in Eastway](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md). [Zhentarim agents](../../../zhentarim/people/roah-moonglow.md), running parallel operations through the same tunnels in the same week, *intercept and poison* the casks at a sluice rendezvous. The shipment arrives nominally on schedule. The mercenaries drink at the evening mess. Several fall ill within hours; two are gravely sick by morning. The Archduke's company commander — already short-fused from [the Bibliophile's bad-faith dossier going around camp](../../../bibliophile/activity/week_5/the-warlords-dossier.md) — *furiously cancels the contract* before noon. The Vintners are barred from the camp. The brand-recovery campaign Quickfoot built last week has, in seven days, become the brand-disaster of a poisoning scandal. **The guild has lost a fortune in product and the only real customer it had.**
