# Bribe a Peerage of Coin Member

Hearing whispers of a luxury-goods tax queued for the next session, Master [Belba Quickfoot](../../people/belba-quickfoot.md) walks several casks of priceless Amnian wine — sourced through the Undercellar — to a key [Peerage of Coin](../../../peerage-of-coin/summary.md) seat. The "gift" is openly delivered; the vote it secures is not. The tax never makes it onto the floor.
