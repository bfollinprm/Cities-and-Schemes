# A New Patron

[Master Belba Quickfoot](../../people/belba-quickfoot.md), publicly humiliated by [Lord Durinbold's return of her brown-ale gift in week 3](../../../wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_3/a-taste-of-gratitude.md), pivots her guild's political allegiance the moment [the Archduke's mercenaries set up barracks in Eastway](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md). She personally delivers a lavish "hospitality gift" of rare Calishite spirits and Waterdhavian wines to the mercenary commander at the Eastway barracks — accompanied by an open offer of supply at favored-house prices. The mercenary commander accepts. The Vintners are now the *informal house brewer* of the Archduke's private army. Belba has bought herself a seat at the Eastway barracks officers' mess; Lord Durinbold's traditionalist faction has lost a valuable mid-tier supplier in a single afternoon.
