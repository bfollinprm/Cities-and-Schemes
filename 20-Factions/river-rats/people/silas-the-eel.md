# Silas the Eel

Leader of the [River-Rats](../summary.md). A long-serving Guild smuggler who has skimmed his runs for years under the protection of *Farlin*, the Eastway Ward Boss — a relationship that became kompromat when the Guild operative Vex documented it.

## Role

Silas runs sewer-canal smuggling out of Brampton. His failed week-0 cache attempt and accumulated failed runs marked the crew for termination. The Brampton smokepowder run was framed to him as a high-value contract; in fact it was [Nine-Fingers Keene's](../../guild/people/nine-fingers-keene.md) cleanup — unstable powder, vague orders, no extraction plan. The party intercepted him in the sewers, secured the explosives, and exposed the suicide-mission nature of the plot.
