# The Blockade Boom

With the [Flaming Fist tied up in the Archduke standoff](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_2/plan-the-unthinkable.md) and overland trade choked by the [Free Trader siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md), [Silas the Eel](../../people/silas-the-eel.md) runs a major contraband shipment up the Chionthar through the [Undercity](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/undercity/README.md) tunnels and into [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md). The Rats clear the largest single-week haul on record, paid out to [The Guild](../../../guild/summary.md). Lower-City patriar customers are furious about the blockade, and now even more dependent on Guild river runs to keep luxury goods flowing.
