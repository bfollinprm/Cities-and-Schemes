# The River Retrieval

[Silas the Eel](../../people/silas-the-eel.md), desperate to regain face after [the smokepowder shipment was intercepted by the party in the sewers under Brampton](../../../party/activity/week_5/mine-eastway-and-detonate-the-velvet-hand.md), accepts a high-risk contract from the [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) to dive the wreck of a barge sunk in last week's running fight at Gray Harbour and retrieve a Consortium lockbox of cut gems before the Amnian Kontor can salvage it. Silas himself takes the dive, leading a three-man crew of his best swimmers off a borrowed Wharf Rats skiff under a moonless cloud cover.

The wreck is deeper and more entangled than the maps showed; an Amnian patrol skiff is overhead inside an hour; the lockbox is *missing* — already pulled by an [Amnian Kontor](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_6/the-hostile-takeover.md) recovery crew the previous night. Silas surfaces empty-handed under crossbow fire, loses one swimmer to the river, and barely escapes through a sewer outflow into Brampton. **The contract is unfulfilled, the fee is forfeit, and Silas has now lost crew and reputation to two consecutive humiliating jobs.** Within his own organization, the question of replacing him is being asked aloud for the first time.
