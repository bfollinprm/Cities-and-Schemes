# A Favor for a Friend

[Silas the Eel](../../people/silas-the-eel.md), still bleeding crew from [the Undercellar ghoul disaster two weeks back](../../../river-rats/activity/week_3/the-undercellar-run.md), takes a small favor-job for a [Harper](../../../harpers/summary.md) contact: walk a packet of sensitive documents through the Undercity tunnels and out the Brampton end. The route is one of his oldest. It is also where [the Flaming Fist's increased patrols](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_4/an-alliance-of-steel-and-silver.md) — pivoted toward sewer surveillance after [the Gravekeepers' temple report](../../../gravekeepers/activity/week_4/the-source-of-the-sickness.md) — collide with him at the worst possible angle. Silas is forced to choose between capture with the cargo and escape without it. He throws the packet into a drainage sluice running with sewer water, dives, and barely makes it out. The packet is gone. The Harpers' sensitive intelligence — Bloomridge cipher key and several agent names — is lost into the storm-drain system. Silas's reputation as the city's reliable sewer courier is *finished*.
