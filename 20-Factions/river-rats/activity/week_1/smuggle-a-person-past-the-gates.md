# Smuggle a Person Past the Gates

[Silas the Eel's](../../people/silas-the-eel.md) crew accepts a high-paying job: get a disgraced patriar's son out of the city through the [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) sewer cache they seated last week. A [Flaming Fist](../../../flaming-fist/summary.md) patrol — acting on an anonymous tip — ambushes the smugglers in the tunnels. Silas's crew breaks contact and exfiltrates clean, but the patriar's son is captured and jailed. The patriar family scandal lights up the gossip-papers; the source of the tip is suspected, not confirmed, to be a Guild informant defending sewer territory.
