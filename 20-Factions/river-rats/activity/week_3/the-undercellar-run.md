# The Undercellar Run

Riding [last week's record contraband haul](../activity/week_2/the-blockade-boom.md), [Silas the Eel](../../people/silas-the-eel.md) overplays the route. He pushes a crew through the [Undercity](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/undercity/README.md) corridor under [the Undercellar](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/undercity/the-undercellar.md) carrying a high-margin wine consignment for a powerful patriar client. The route runs too close to a nexus of undead activity that [the Gravekeepers have just started mapping](../../../gravekeepers/activity/week_3/a-pattern-in-the-rot.md). Ghouls ambush the crew in a flooded chamber. Half the crew is killed; the entire shipment is lost; the survivors barely reach the [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) end of the run. Silas has now failed a powerful patriar contract, owes the client compensation he cannot pay, and the operation's reputation is bleeding. The Wharf Rats and the Guild are watching; [Roah Moonglow's Zhentarim](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_3/an-offer-you-cant-refuse.md) are already pricing his collapse.
