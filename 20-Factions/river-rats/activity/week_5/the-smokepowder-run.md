# The Smokepowder Run

[Silas the Eel](../../people/silas-the-eel.md), trying to recover his standing inside the Guild after [the Harper packet went into a sewer drain last week](../../../river-rats/activity/week_4/a-favor-for-a-friend.md), accepts what he assumes is a routine high-priority contraband walk: move a Zhent smokepowder shipment through the tunnels between [the Guildhall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/undercity/README.md) and [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) for an unnamed buyer. The buyer is the Guild itself; the shipment is [the seized Zhentarim cache](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_4/the-smokepowder-plot.md); the Guild's *own* enforcer team — including [Vex Corvin](../../../guild/people/vex.md), and the party in tow — is moving counter to him in the same tunnel system to [rig the Velvet Hand from below](../../../guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md). The two crews collide in lamplight. The firefight is short, ugly, and decisive. *Vex is killed.* The party intercepts the cargo. Silas escapes by dropping into a flooded sluice he has used twice before in his life. He surfaces in the Chionthar with no smokepowder, no crew worth the name, and a Guildmaster who has lost an enforcer on his run. **Silas's standing inside the Guild collapses.** The River-Rats are no longer a trusted Guild asset. Silas survives the night; nothing else does.
