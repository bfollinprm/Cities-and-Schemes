# The Desperate Dive

[Silas the Eel](../../people/silas-the-eel.md) — out of credibility after [the failed wreck-dive](../../activity/week_6/the-river-retrieval.md) and [last tenday's busted people-smuggle](../../activity/week_1/smuggle-a-person-past-the-gates.md), and out of cash after losing two crews to the Amnians — gambles the cell on a **direct armed raid** against a **Zhentarim safehouse** in [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md). The plan is suicidal: hit the safehouse vault, steal enough coin to kick a tribute up to Nine-Fingers Keene, buy the Eel one more month of Guild patience.

Silas leads the raid in person. He gets through the cellar door; he does not get through the safehouse strongroom. **Two Zhentarim journeyman casters** were stationed for exactly this contingency. Silas takes a shocking-grasp through his shoulder, drops his lead torch, and has to be dragged back into the sewers by his last surviving crew-chief. Three River-Rats die in the run. The cell ends the week with no coin, no leverage, and a **Zhentarim contract on Silas's head** — and Silas himself unable to lift his right arm.
