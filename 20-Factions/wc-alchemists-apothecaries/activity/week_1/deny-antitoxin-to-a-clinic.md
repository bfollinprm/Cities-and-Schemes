# Deny Antitoxin to a Clinic

The Alchemists' Company refuses to release antitoxin to the Open Hand Temple in [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), trying to set a price-gouge precedent on emergency supply during the Sewer Plague. The blockade is broken almost immediately: the [party](../../../party/summary.md) — already on Hellrider business at [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) — heists the medicine off a [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) warehouse cart in [Little Calimshan](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/little-calimshan.md) and delivers it to the temple. The guild loses the precedent and the public credit; [Phineas Droog](../../people/phineas-droog.md) shoulders the embarrassment privately while [Factor Errinthal](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) tries to retroactively claim the rescue as Consortium PR.
