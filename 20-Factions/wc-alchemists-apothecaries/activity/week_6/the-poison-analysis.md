# The Poison Analysis

Approached by [Master Belba Quickfoot](../../../wc-vintners-brewers/people/belba-quickfoot.md) regarding [last week's poisoned mercenary-camp ale](../../../wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_5/securing-the-supply-line.md), [Master Phineas Droog](../../people/phineas-droog.md) accepts a formal Alchemists' analysis contract. Three days of laboratory work at his Crimson Draughts shop in the Lower City confirm the toxin is a **Zhentarim signature blend** — a cut-rate adulterant of ergot mold and powdered nightcap fungus that would not kill a healthy adult but would put a barracks of mercenaries on their knees for thirty-six hours, exactly as observed at Eastway.

Droog hands Quickfoot a sealed Alchemists' guild attestation she can use to defend the Vintners' reputation publicly — the price of which is that the Alchemists also retain the right to **sell expensive antidotes to the mercenary camps directly**. Within hours, Crimson Draughts is selling 50-gp bottles of the matched antidote to Sembian quartermasters at the Eastway barracks gate. The Alchemists win three ways: a cleared Vintners ledger trade ([Quickfoot's embargo backed by guild attestation](../../../wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_6/the-ale-embargo.md)), a fat antidote contract from the same Sembians the Vintners have just embargoed, and a documented confirmation of [Roah Moonglow's](../../../zhentarim/people/roah-moonglow.md) Zhentarim role in the poisoning. **Three profitable ledgers from one analysis.**
