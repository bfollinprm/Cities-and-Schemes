# The Vault Lockdown

[Master Phineas Droog](../../people/phineas-droog.md), aware that [the Guild walked out of last week with three of the Alchemists' most valuable potion formulas — including the Sewer Plague antitoxin](../../../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_4/a-desperate-bargain.md), initiates a full security overhaul of the Lower City vault under the guild's hall. He hires a team of dwarven masons out of [the Steeps](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/the-steeps.md) and a complement of [Sembian mercenaries](../../../sembian-kontor/people/korgan-ironhand.md) — Sembians being, this week, the only spare disciplined armed force not already deployed to the Eastway camp — to reinforce the vault wall, seat new mage-locks against the door, and stand guard while the locks are *changed*. The work is loud, expensive, and visible from the street; an apprentice walks pamphlets to every Worshipful Company hall by Tendays-eve naming the security overhaul as proof of Alchemist resolve. The locks are new before the week ends. **The remaining formulas are, for the moment, secure.** The market price on the lost three formulas, meanwhile, is already being quoted under the Guildhall's table for whichever consortium can afford to bid first.
