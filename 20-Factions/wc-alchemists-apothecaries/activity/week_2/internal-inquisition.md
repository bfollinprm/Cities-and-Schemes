# Internal Inquisition

[Master Phineas Droog](../../people/phineas-droog.md) altered the Crimson Draughts storeroom ledgers to conceal the theft of sensitive alchemical reagents — the same Nightmare-Root-and-friends inventory the [Plague-Father's lab](../../../party/activity/week_1/destroy-the-plague-fathers-laboratory.md) had been drawing from before the party burned it down. The forged ledgers hold up under the Company's internal audit. The Alchemists' compromised inventory is officially clean and the [Plague of Remembrance](../../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) supply chain's Lower City endpoint stays buried.
