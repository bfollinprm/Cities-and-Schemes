# A Desperate Bargain

The apprentice [Master Phineas Droog](../../people/phineas-droog.md) framed for the reagent thefts in [the week-3 scapegoat play](../../../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_3/find-a-scapegoat.md) makes contact with a [Guild](../../../guild/summary.md) lieutenant operating out of a Lower City safehouse. He offers three of the Worshipful Company's most valuable potion formulas — including the antitoxin that became politically charged during [the Sewer Plague](../../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) — in exchange for protection and safe passage out of Baldur's Gate. The Guild takes the deal. The formulas are now in [Nine-Fingers Keene's](../../../guild/people/astele-keene.md) hands. The apprentice walks. **The Guild possesses powerful alchemical secrets the Worshipful Company has spent a decade keeping in-house** — and Master Droog does not yet know it. [The Purified, hunting the formulas independently](../../../purified/activity/week_4/the-scapegoats-secret.md), arrive at the safehouse hours too late.
