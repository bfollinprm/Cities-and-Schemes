# Master Phineas Droog

Master of the [Worshipful Company of Alchemists & Apothecaries](../summary.md). An eccentric, paranoid alchemist who keeps three tasters and runs the city's potion market with the cold logic of a man who genuinely believes everyone is trying to poison him.

## Manifesto

> Flesh is a chemical equation. Sickness, strength, life, death — they are all merely formulas waiting to be written. The gods offer prayers; we offer cures. The assassins offer blades; we offer poisons that leave no trace. We hold the secrets to the body's machinery, and for the right price, we will happily provide the key to wind it up or shut it down.

## Role

Droog runs the Court of Assistants from a warded private laboratory beneath the guildhall. He authorized the Sewer Plague price-tripling and the antitoxin denial to the Rivington clinic — both popular within the guild, both politically expensive when the party found a workaround. The [Plague of Remembrance](../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) is suspected to be running a slow-burn smear campaign against him through [Baldur's Mouth](../../baldurs-mouth/summary.md), painting the Alchemists as antitoxin-hoarders to inflame class tension.
