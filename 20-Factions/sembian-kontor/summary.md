# Sembian Kontor *(aka The Iron Ledger)*

An utterly ruthless and efficient trading house from the merchant kingdom of Sembia, known as the **Iron Ledger**. Focused on dominating bulk goods (grain, ore, timber) and acting as the city's primary broker for mercenary contracts. Led by [Bursar Korgan Ironhand](people/korgan-ironhand.md).

## Ideology

Pure, unadulterated profit. The Iron Ledger is famously apolitical and will work with any faction that can pay the price. It pursues monopolies on key supply chains and profits from the region's conflicts by brokering mercenaries.

## Membership

Led by the dwarven Bursar Korgan Ironhand. Staffed by pragmatic Sembians and protected by the professional soldiers of the **Iron Guard**.

## Methods

- Mastery of logistics and supply-chain management to control bulk goods.
- The great hall of the Iron Ledger is the primary hiring hub for mercenary companies and adventuring parties in the region.
- Mercenary contracts are infamous for their unforgiving clauses.
- Sembian merchants brokered the contract that put Sembian mercenaries in the Whisperwood for the [Western Gate Trading Company](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md).

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** In competition with all other major economic players, but without personal animus. Chief rivals are whoever currently stands between them and a margin — most often the local [Chionthar Consortium](../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) and [Western Gate Trading Company](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md).
