# The Alternate Route

[Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../people/korgan-ironhand.md) dispatches scout teams east of the Coast Way to map an overland bypass around the [Basilisk Gate siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_3/press-the-advantage.md), so caravans can move under the [Iron Ledger](../../summary.md)'s mark without paying Free Trader tolls. The scouting mission is a failure. One team is wiped out by an owlbear pack in the wilderness east of the Coast Way; another is captured by the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_3/reclaim-the-tainted-spring.md), who view the company-armed mercenaries as predators of the land. The Circle returns the sole survivor with a grim warning: the wilderness does not suffer the city's greed. The Sembian Kontor's planned end-run around the siege is closed for now, even as Ironhand simultaneously [signs the Archduke's private mercenary contract](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_3/the-private-option.md) at the same hall.
