# The Starvation Premium

[Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../people/korgan-ironhand.md) triggers a second siege-conditions clause in the Sembian master contract: a **300% markup** on hoarded grain, sold *only* to bidding patriar households. Within four days the Iron Ledger's strongroom prices read like a parody — a sack of millet costs what a side of beef cost six tendays ago — but the patriars who can pay, pay. By midweek Korgan has unloaded almost the entire warehouse onto the Manorborn supply chain at a profit margin no Sembian operation in the city has ever seen.

The cost is moral and political. The [Free Traders](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_7/smuggling-the-crumbs.md) are stealing from the Lower City because no honest market is open to them. [The Vintners' embargo and the Alchemists' antidote monopoly](../../../wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_7/the-dry-riot.md) follow Korgan's pricing model directly. [The Council's Refugee Riot blame-game](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_7/the-refugee-riot.md) lands on a population already starved by the *price* of grain, not its absence. Korgan's premium has converted the siege from a tactical operation into a **structural crisis** the Council cannot end without forcing him to refund.
