# Legitimizing the Contract

[Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../people/korgan-ironhand.md) walks the [Iron Ledger's](../../summary.md) lawyers through the city bureaucracy and emerges with a contract giving [the Archduke's mercenary company](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md) official, if temporary, standing as **City-Sanctioned Peacekeepers**. The instrument is signed and sealed at the Iron Ledger, filed at High Hall, and gives the Eastway barracks legal cover for arrests, searches, and use of force inside the city walls. The mercenaries are no longer just hired retainers — they are, on paper, an arm of municipal authority. [The Peerage of Blood](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_4/the-letter-of-censure.md) is incandescent. The Sembians, who only had to draft language and sit through one tedious hearing, are now the legal underwriters of [the Archduke's entire private war](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_3/the-private-option.md).
