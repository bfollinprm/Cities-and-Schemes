# Recruit a New Information Asset

[Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../people/korgan-ironhand.md) — bleeding margin from the new Chionthar "priority storage" gouge — orders his intelligence network to seat a recruited asset inside the [Flaming Fist](../../../flaming-fist/summary.md) barracks at Wyrm's Rock. The asset's brief is narrow: advance warning of any "disruptions to trade" — read, of any decision to break the Free Trader blockade or move on the Hellrider protest. Recruitment lands cleanly; the placement is now Sembia's pair of eyes inside the bridge.
