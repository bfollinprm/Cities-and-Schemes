# Arming the Aggressors

[Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../people/korgan-ironhand.md), having [legitimized the mercenary contract last week through a "City-Sanctioned Peacekeepers" filing](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_4/legitimizing-the-contract.md), pivots the Iron Ledger from law-firm to logistics-house. A daily wagon train begins rolling out of the Lower City warehouse the kontor uses as the Iron Ledger's holding-yard, bound for [the Eastway mercenary camp](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) just inside the Basilisk Gate: short-swords by the dozen-crate, scale by the bale, crossbow quarrels by the keg, hardtack and salt-pork by the wagon. The drivers are paid Sembian, the manifests are stamped Sembian, the cut is taken Sembian. By midweek the Iron Ledger is the *sole* outfitter of [the Archduke's company](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md). The Sembian Kontor is no longer a trading house with a side-line in mercenary brokerage. It is the supply train of a private army. [The Vintners' shipment, intercepted and poisoned by Zhent agents](../../../wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_5/securing-the-supply-line.md), is the only break in an otherwise steady stream.
