# Test the Barricade

[Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../people/korgan-ironhand.md) sends a quiet emissary — and **K'laar** of the [Unchained](../../../unchained/summary.md) along as the Sembian-side broker — to ['Mad' Meggan's](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) command wagon at the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md), proposing a private deal: open the gate to Sembian-tagged caravans and the Iron Ledger will discreetly bankroll the siege. Meggan refuses outright. The barricade holds; Sembian iron and bulk grain begin spoiling on the road.
