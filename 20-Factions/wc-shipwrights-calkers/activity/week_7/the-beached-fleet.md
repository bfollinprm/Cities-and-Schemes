# The Beached Fleet

[Master Helma Tallowshand's](../../people/helma-tallowshand.md) [drydock lockout from last tenday](../../activity/week_6/the-drydock-lockout.md) compounds into structural crisis: the backlog of damaged independent vessels barred from drydock access has now **fully beached** every non-guild hull on the Gray Harbour mudflats below the breakwater. Independent captains are sleeping under their own keel-strakes; their crews are scattered in Lower City taverns or signed onto Amnian ships at Athar's docks.

The result is a **functional crippling of Baldur's Gate's non-guild maritime transport capacity**, exactly when [the Amnians are consolidating river control](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_7/consolidate-river-control.md) and [the Fishmongers are extorting toll on every skiff](../../../wc-fishmongers/activity/week_7/the-harbor-toll.md). The Guild controls every working hull above the bilge-line in three districts. The Shipwrights are richer in monopoly power than they have been in a generation. They are also **publicly visible as the choke-point**, and the Lower City harborwives are starting to notice.
