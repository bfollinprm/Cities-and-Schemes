# A Port in the Storm

[Master Helma Tallowshand](../../people/helma-tallowshand.md), riding [last week's dock-repair project](../activity/week_2/exploit-the-idle.md), tries to convert the Company's monopoly on Gray Harbour repair into a "secure mooring" service for nervous out-of-city captains, pricing premium berths for whatever the [Free Trader siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_3/press-the-advantage.md) market will bear. The play overreaches. The [Sembian](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_3/the-alternate-route.md) and [Chionthar](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_3/secure-the-front-lines.md) consortiums, furious at the open undercutting of their own moorings, collude to drastically lower their docking fees for a single sharp window. Most captains take the cheaper option. Tallowshand's "secure" Gray Harbour moorings sit empty for most of the week. The Company eats a significant loss for no gain — the second straight week of profitless drama — and Helma's standing inside the Worshipful chamber softens. The [Smiths' multi-guild emergency petition](../../../wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_3/an-appeal-to-the-marshal.md) gets one more co-signer than it would have.
