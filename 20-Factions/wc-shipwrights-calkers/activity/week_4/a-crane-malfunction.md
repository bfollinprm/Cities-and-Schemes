# A Crane "Malfunction"

[Master Helma Tallowshand](../../people/helma-tallowshand.md), wounded by [last week's premium-mooring price-cut](../../../wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_3/a-port-in-the-storm.md) coordinated by the Sembian and Chionthar consortia, retaliates in the language she knows: dock sabotage. Her enforcers loosen the load-pin on a key Chionthar Consortium loading crane in [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) on a Tendays-eve night-shift. The "malfunction" goes off the next morning under a full pallet of ironware: pallet through the deck, two longshoremen injured, one warehouse-front wall cracked, repairs and lost shipments cost the Consortium a small fortune. Vancroft's stevedores tell the Watch the rigging looked tampered. [Errinthal](../../../chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md), already paranoid after [the warehouse arrest fiasco](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_4/a-warning-in-the-warehouse.md), correctly traces the sabotage to the Shipwrights — but cannot prove it without giving up his own enforcers. The trade war on the docks gains a third dimension.
