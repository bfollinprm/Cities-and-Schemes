# Enforce Guild-Only Repairs

Master [Helma Tallowshand](../../people/helma-tallowshand.md) leverages the Harbor Dredging Levy to announce that her drydocks will no longer service vessels lacking a Shipwrights' Company seal. Independent captains face a binary: join the guild or watch their hulls rot. The announcement squeezes the [Free Traders of the Outskirts](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) and the smaller [Chionthar](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) holdouts simultaneously, and feeds directly into the Free Trader blockade decision later in the week.
