# The Captive Market

The [Jhasso family](../../people/jhasso-family.md) sends a rare-silk expedition south to Calimshan, gambling that the closed [Basilisk Gate](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md) means Lower-City demand for luxury cloth will spike enough to refloat their books. The expedition is lost at sea. The Coster's already-thin reserves crater further; [Manorborn](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/manorborn.md) creditors begin circling.
