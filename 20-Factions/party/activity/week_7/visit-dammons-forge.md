# Visit Dammon's Forge

The party reads [the morning *Baldur's Mouth*](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_7/the-puppet-press.md) at [the Weary Plowman](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) — the paper has *visibly* shifted under the new Interim Editor, blaming the [Plague of Remembrance refugee massacre](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_7/the-refugee-riot.md) on the cult while elevating the Archduke's siege as "restoring order." Recap: Bror still owes [Mother Nocturne](../../../silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md) the *sentient source* of the secret; [Ettvard Needle's note](../../../../50-Sessions/session_27/player_summary.md) names the Tumbledown crypts as his hiding place.

Before riding for Tumbledown the party visits **[Dammon's forge](../../../wc-smiths-armorers/people/dammon.md)** in [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md). Dammon has been working day and night to arm the Hellrider–Free Trader joint detail at the Basilisk Gate ([as the Druids' vine reinforcements thicken](../../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_7/vine-barricades.md)). He fits Bror with **a new pair of dwarven gauntlets** that grant *+1 to hit on unarmed strikes*, then tests Spark's recently attuned cloak by pressing a **glowing forge prong to their chest** — the fire reads as *welcoming and comforting*, not painful. Dammon reveals the cloak's clasp and metal tendrils were forged from the discarded infernal iron of **Karlach's original heart**, salvaged by the Foundry Smiths after she returned from Avernus. He suggests Spark talk to Alfira — currently sleeping in the Plowman's back room — about Karlach when she wakes.
