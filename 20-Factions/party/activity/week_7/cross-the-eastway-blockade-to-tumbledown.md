# Cross the Eastway Blockade to Tumbledown

The party visits [Master Phineas Droog](../../../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/people/phineas-droog.md) at [Crimson Draughts in Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) for a route into [Tumbledown](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md). Droog is in the middle of his [Antidote Monopoly](../../../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_7/the-antidote-monopoly.md) and refuses Kaelan's appeal to relax the restrictions for Outer City clinics, but he hands the party a **Guild coin** and the name of one [Garrett "Grit" Fallow](../../../guild/people/garrett-grit-fallow.md), a Tumbledown gravedigger on Nine-Fingers Keene's quiet retainer. Show the coin; pass without questions.

The party debates the route. The sewers under Heapside would have been faster but Bror reports the [Hands of the Absolute's resonance lattice is leaking through the storm-drains](../../../hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_7/the-resonance-chamber.md) and his Whispering Stone hums when he steps near a sewer-grate. They commit to **the surface route through the Eastway blockade** instead, attempting disguises to dodge their personal bounties. The disguises are *unconvincing*: Spark's horns barely fit under a wool merchant's hood; Kaelan's clean linen reads as Lower City no matter how he dirties it; Bror as "Stoney" passes only because Stoney's whole point is being unmemorable.

The Sembian gate detail at Eastway is preoccupied with [the deserter mercenaries from the Vintner Dry Riot](../../../wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_7/the-dry-riot.md) and waves the party through with a perfunctory cargo check. They reach Tumbledown by midafternoon. The district is crypt-still and necromantic-cold; the **Gravekeepers' new perimeter patrols** are visibly heavier than any of the party expected (Hemlock's [Cliffside Consecration](../../../gravekeepers/activity/week_7/the-cliffside-consecration.md) is operationally on). After a perception check the party clocks **a five-strong gravedigger patrol** standing in the next yard — this is where session 28 ends.
