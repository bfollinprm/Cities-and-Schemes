# Destroy the Plague-Father's Laboratory

In a converted cistern past a heavy iron door the party finds the source of the Sewer Plague: three copper vats of glowing green liquid, two cult fanatics, and the masked **Plague-Father**. After a brutal fight — Plague-Father teleporting, dropping a nausea cloud that benches half the party, and starting to drain the vats into a pump — Bror, Finn, Spark, and Elia bring him down. A surrendering cultist is captured.

Loot: two wooden coins carved with a **cracked-vial** sigil ([Plague of Remembrance](../../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) cell-token by Bror's read), the Plague-Father's iron neck-key, and sacks of reagents including two doses of Nightmare Root used in **psionic-concentration potions** — pointing the cult toward [Hands of the Absolute](../../../hands-of-the-absolute/summary.md) supply chains as well. The party hand prisoner and Finn off to High Watcher Alena and long-rest. **Level 3.**

The Plague-Father lab is the durable canon shift this week: this is the first concrete Plague of Remembrance facility the party has seen, and the cracked-vial token is now a recognizable insignia.
