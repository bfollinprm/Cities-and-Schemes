# Accept the Jaredith Mission from Alena

At the High Rider's Stand, [High Watcher Alena](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) tasks the party to discreetly investigate her radicalized lieutenant [Jaredith Heartsong](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md), who has fallen in with the Phoenix Pits' violent wing — particularly the young tiefling [Mikkel](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/mikkel.md) — and now advocates striking the [Flaming Fist](../../../flaming-fist/summary.md). The party accepts; Alena pays a 100 gp/PC advance. Spark, in the same conversation, secures **official Followers-of-Phoenix representation in New Elturel's council and land for a temple** — a real political concession won on the back of the medicine corridor. The mission is open at week's end.
