# Track the Sewer Plague to the Worm Spit

Acting on Brother Billiam's 50 gp Open Hand bounty, the party traces the unnatural Sewer Plague — paranoia, hallucinations, "worms in their minds" — to its source. Kaelan rules out any natural sewer-borne illness; Elia reads a poison-aura on the afflicted. Earliest cases all originate from the **Worm Spit**, a low shantytown on Rivington's edge where a wide drainage canal feeds the Chionthar.

The party descend into the canal. They find two murdered [Guild](../../../guild/summary.md) operatives in the cistern — throats cut from a side passage, with a note from "J." referencing Nine-Fingers, the bioluminescent moss, and a "weeping skull" mark. They survive an Otyugh ambush in the junction chamber when Elia drops the killing Eldritch Blast from cover.
