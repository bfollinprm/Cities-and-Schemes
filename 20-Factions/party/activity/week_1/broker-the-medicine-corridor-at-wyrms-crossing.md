# Broker the Medicine Corridor at Wyrm's Crossing

Spark approaches [High Watcher Alena](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) at the [Wyrm's Crossing protest](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_1/organize-a-protest-at-wyrms-crossing.md) and persuades her to allow the medicine cart safe passage through the protest line. A nervous Fist crossbow grazes Spark on the way through; Finneas and Spark talk the Fist sergeant down to a deal: a single medicine wagon for the temple, both directions. The cart reaches the Open Hand. The cordon holds. Alena registers the party as useful, deniable, and books a meeting at the High Rider's Stand.
