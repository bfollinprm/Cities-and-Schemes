# Heist the Rivington Medicine Cart

While the [Worshipful Company of Alchemists & Apothecaries](../../../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_1/deny-antitoxin-to-a-clinic.md) is denying antitoxin to the Open Hand Temple, the party walks into a [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) warehouse in [Little Calimshan](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/little-calimshan.md) under Flaming Fist escort and walks back out with the medicine cart. Kaelan bribes the warehouse crew off-shift with drinks; the rest climb in a window, find four carts, identify the medical cart, and roll it. **Elia, on impulse, also takes a second cart** — later identified as carrying anti-Amnian propaganda the Consortium had been about to deploy publicly. The medicine reaches the Open Hand; the second cart becomes leverage banked against the Consortium.
