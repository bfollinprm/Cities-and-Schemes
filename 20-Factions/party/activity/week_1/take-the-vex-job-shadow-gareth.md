# Take the Vex Job — Shadow Gareth

[Vex](../../../guild/people/vex.md), a [Guild](../../../guild/summary.md) operative the party meets at the Weary Plowman, offers a "trust-building" job: silence the loud-mouthed mason [Gareth](../../../wc-masons-sculptors/people/gareth.md), no killing, quietly. Bror tails Gareth home through Rivington's twisting streets, gets noticed by a neighbor with a quarterstaff, plays vagrant, and slips the contact. He now knows where Gareth lives. The job is taken; the job is not yet done.
