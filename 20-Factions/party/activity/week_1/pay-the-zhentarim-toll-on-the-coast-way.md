# Pay the Zhentarim Toll on the Coast Way

The party converges for the first time at a [Zhentarim](../../../zhentarim/summary.md) "traveler's tithe" four miles south of [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). Finneas leads with mercy-pleas; Spark backs him with an unsubtle headcount of how many travelers are queued behind. Bror scouts the dunes and clocks several [Guild](../../../guild/summary.md) operatives watching the encounter. The party defuses the standoff for 18 gp pooled, and grateful caravan-folk later reimburse Finneas 38 gp.
