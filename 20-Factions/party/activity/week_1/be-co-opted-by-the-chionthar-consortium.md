# Be Co-opted by the Chionthar Consortium

Outside the High Rider's Stand, [Factor Errinthal](../../../chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) of the [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) intercepts the party with two river-barge-emblem guards. He claims the Open Hand medicine "belonged" to a planned Consortium PR play and prices the debt in the thousands. Elia pivots: she persuades Errinthal that the Consortium can rebrand the rescue as Consortium-directed and recover the narrative. He agrees. The party is now publicly the Consortium's hired hand, with debt to be worked off in service tasks.

Crucially, **Errinthal never mentions the second cart**. The anti-Amnian propaganda materials Elia stole are now hidden leverage — the Consortium does not know the party has them.
