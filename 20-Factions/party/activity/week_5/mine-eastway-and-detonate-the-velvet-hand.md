# Mine Eastway and Detonate the Velvet Hand

The party intercepts [Silas's River Rats](../../../river-rats/activity/week_5/the-smokepowder-run.md) in the [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) sewers as the Guild's most dangerous payload — the smokepowder bound for [the Velvet Hand](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_5/the-financial-front.md) — moves toward Roah Moonglow's safehouse. Investigation reveals the load is *far* in excess of what the demolition needs: enough to level a city block. Realization: [Nine-Fingers](../../../guild/people/astele-keene.md) intended the explosion to take out **both** the Zhentarim base *and* Silas's incompetent crew in one stroke, tying up two loose ends at once. The party convinces the terrified teenagers to leave the powder behind.

They split the cargo three ways. **Personal stash** — backpacks each, with the rest dumped to inert. **Eastway mining** — Elia summons spectral oxen and hauls one third back through the sewers to [Eastway](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md), to be planted as defensive mines under [Alena](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md)'s plan. **Cover mission** — the final third stays beneath the Velvet Hand to execute the original Guild contract. **Finn lights the fuse and cartwheels away.** [The Velvet Hand collapses](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_5/the-financial-front.md). The Zhentarim's smokepowder plot dies with its target.

The party then locates [Farlin](../../../guild/people/farlin.md), the Eastway Ward Boss, and coerces him with the [kompromat from Vex's office](kill-vex-and-loot-her-office.md). Bror intimidates; Kaelan reasons; the Saffron evidence finishes it. Farlin agrees to plant the smokepowder mines on the routes the Sembian mercenaries would use to march on the Basilisk Gate. Within a day, **Eastway is a defensive minefield** under the Sembian advance corridor — the first physical fortification of the Hellrider–Free Trader alliance, and the [Guild's smokepowder plot turned inside out](../../../guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md) on every front.
