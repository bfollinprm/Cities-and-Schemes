# Install Finneas as Guild Boss of Rivington

The party formalizes its survival plan: rather than flee [Nine-Fingers Keene](../../../guild/people/astele-keene.md)'s wrath for [killing Vex](kill-vex-and-loot-her-office.md), they will install **Finneas Kaller** as the new Guild boss of [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), with [Simbar](../../../guild/people/simbar.md) as nominal puppet. The pitch to Nine-Fingers writes itself: a Rivington that is more profitable, more stable, and more loyal than Vex's was — a Guild presence that delivers real security and real economic uplift to its locals while still cutting the percentages upstream. **A public-service crime franchise.** ['Mad' Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) backs the move and offers to double the Free Traders' "protection" payments to seed the operation.

The complication: Vex's former inner-circle fixers — [Glass Malakor](../../../guild/people/glass-malakor.md) (intelligence), [Runa "The Hook"](../../../guild/people/runa-the-hook.md) (enforcement), and [Belvin "Soot" Hamhock](../../../guild/people/belvin-soot-hamhock.md) (logistics) — each have their own conditions before they will follow an outsider's lead. Finneas adds them to his ledger.

Finneas opens "office hours" at the [Weary Plowman](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), the Plowman's back room turning over to a soft-campaign listening post — anyone with a grievance can walk in and leave with the new boss's word that he will look into it. Lena Oakes pours tea. Kaelan, working a parallel front, secures an investigative-journalism position at the *Baldur's Mouth* from [Ettvard Needle](../../../baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) and walks out with the **Credential of Balduran** — a heavy signet ring reputed to open restricted doors across the city. Information dropboxes are established at the print shop and at the Plowman.

Week 5 ends with the party halfway-installed in Rivington, Kaelan press-credentialed, the Eastway minefield silently waiting under foot, and Nine-Fingers's verdict on Vex still pending. The Rivington Gambit is in motion.
