# The Rivington War Council

After [Vex's killing](kill-vex-and-loot-her-office.md), the party brings the captured Guild scout [Simbar](../../../guild/people/simbar.md) and Vex's intel cache through the sewers to the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) refugee/trader camp. [Lyra Sunstone](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) ushers them into a command wagon to address [High Watcher Alena](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) and ['Mad' Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) directly.

The party makes full disclosure — the killing of Vex, the unsent betrayal note, **Vex's appointment book for Rivington**, the stolen Guild intel on [Errinthal's anti-refugee print campaign](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md), the [Velvet Hand smokepowder map](../../../guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md), and the [Farlin kompromat](../../../guild/people/farlin.md). Meggan reads the print-campaign file and starts laughing — political leverage to crack the patriar coalition. Alena reads the smokepowder map and goes still: *"We can take the gate down."*

Lyra Sunstone names the Guild problem out loud: kompromat does not save the party from Nine-Fingers. Meggan offers the way through — **install Simbar as the puppet "Guildmaster of Rivington"** to keep the ward operationally Guild-aligned while pivoted refugee-side. Simbar, given the choice between dying in the warehouse and *thriving*, agrees on the spot. The [Hellrider–Free Trader alliance](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) now has its first joint covert operation.

Sanctuary is granted in the camp. The party takes a short rest. **Level 5.** They walk out of the wagon as the alliance's de facto field instrument — political-operations on the Errinthal side, smokepowder-mining on the Alena side, Rivington takeover on the Meggan side, all three running concurrently from week 5 onward.
