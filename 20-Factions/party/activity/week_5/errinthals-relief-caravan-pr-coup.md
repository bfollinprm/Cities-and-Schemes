# Errinthal's Relief Caravan PR Coup

The party walks into [Coster Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) intending to deliver an ultimatum to [Factor Errinthal](../../../chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md): withdraw funding from the Archduke or the print-block evidence goes to the Amnian Kontor and the *Baldur's Mouth*. Errinthal has prepared. He is hosting **[Ettvard Needle](../../../baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md)** in his office when they arrive, and before the party can produce a single document, Errinthal stands and "thanks" them on the record for volunteering to escort a [Consortium relief caravan to the Basilisk Gate](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md). Refusing makes them villains in tomorrow's front page. Kaelan slips a note re: sensitive information; Errinthal answers under his breath: *"a story is nothing without proof."* The trap is sealed.

The party rolls out as the Consortium's smiling escort. Five magic-driven luxury carts, all branded *Heroes of Rivington*. Bror and Kaelan run distractions to inventory the cargo (Bror gets briefly hog-tied for it); Elia rituals *Flock of Familiars* and confirms the cargo is genuine. As the caravan reaches [Eastway](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md), the party signals Mad Meggan's rooftop scouts that they are operating under duress.

At the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) checkpoint, a Free Trader lieutenant and a Hellrider squad halt the caravan. Consortium guards insist on delivering through the gate — into the upper district, where the photographs would feed only soldiers. **Finneas turns it.** Successful Persuasion to both the guards and the gathered crowd: *the starving masses are right here.* A guard named Jimmy stands aside. Children and families pour out of [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) and Eastway. The luxury food is distributed under open sky for the rest of the day.

Errinthal banks the *Baldur's Mouth* PR — *Chionthar Consortium feeds Eastway.* The party banks goodwill where it matters, with the people they intend to govern next week. **Both sides record a win.**
