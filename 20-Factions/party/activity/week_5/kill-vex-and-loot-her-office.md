# Kill Vex and Loot Her Office

[Vex](../../../guild/people/vex.md) leads the party from the [Blushing Mermaid](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md) into a non-descript [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) warehouse, then drops the act: she demands the Whispering Stone, signals four hidden archers, and orders her two door-guards to hold the only exit. The party fights blind in Bror's *Darkness*. **Finn lands the killing blow on Vex** with the same chair-and-whip combo that opened the fight; Elia and Kaelan finish the guards and one archer; Bror downs another. Two archers flee, one of them mortally wounded. The other escapes into the warehouse stacks. Vex is dead.

The party loots methodically. From Vex: an iron desk-key, 24 gp, a mechanical-bird tube containing **an unsent betrayal note to [Nine-Fingers Keene](../../../guild/people/astele-keene.md)** confirming she had always intended to take the artifact and turn the party in, and a ciphered ledger which Finn cracks via Thieves' Cant into Vex's [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) appointment book. From the desk (open): a sewer-map of [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) marked *"Roah's new rat-nest. Silas's crew has the present"* — a written confession of [the Velvet Hand smokepowder plot](../../../guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md). From the desk (locked): stolen Guild intel linking **[Factor Errinthal](../../../chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md)** to the [Chionthar Consortium's anti-refugee print campaign](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md), needing only the print blocks the party stole weeks ago to publish; and **kompromat on [Farlin](../../../guild/people/farlin.md)**, the Eastway Ward Boss — Saffron skim from the Amnian Kontor, cover-fire for Silas. Bror confirms the Saffron in the warehouse stock and stages Vex and the dead guards into the crates to delay discovery.

The party walks out of the warehouse with **a Guild Lieutenant dead, one witness loose, and four explosive secrets in their packs**. The Rivington underworld will know inside a week.
