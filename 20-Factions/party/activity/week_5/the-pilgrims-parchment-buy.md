# The Pilgrim's Parchment Buy

Returning to [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) after a fey-induced absence and a [near-arrest at the Plowman](../../../../50-Sessions/session_12/player_summary.md), Elia, Spark, and Finneas walk into **The Pilgrim's Parchment**, a chaotic used-book shop tucked into the ward. The elderly halfling proprietor reveals a curtained "Shelf of Ascension" of Candlekeep-grade tomes priced at 600 gp apiece. After lengthy haggling, Bror and Elia broker **three books for 900 gp**; Spark, party treasurer, pays.

Choices: Elia takes the **untitled scaly-leather book**, a dense Underdark scientific text linking illithid psionics to unknown star charts not visible from Faerûn — clearly missing a foundational "key." Bror takes **The Stonemason's Secret**, which the party then decodes into two ciphers — a letter-to-number message *"THE FORGE THINKS STILL"* and a chapter-title acrostic *"FOLLOW SOLSTICE SUN FROM KARAK-DUR"* — relics of the lost dwarven Clan Iron Eye and a path to a forge that tempers will and forges sentient constructs. Finneas takes **The Wyrmway Wardens Weekly Ledger**, which the party decodes into the Guild's **Rosetta Stone** — a coded directory of contacts, safehouses, and assets across the wards. Cross-referenced with the cat-doodle margins of *Meditations on the Weave* (which they did not buy but rummaged through), the ledger spits out concrete handles for a "map scribe" and a "code breaker."

Before the buy, [Lyra Sunstone](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) catches the party on the doorstep and presents Hellrider surplus as thanks for [restoring her memory in the Feywild](../week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md): full plate for Kaelan, leather and a perfectly-balanced greatsword for Spark. She is in a hurry — the Hellriders have moved to the Basilisk Gate to throw in with [Mad Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md).

The party leaves the shop with three books that will reshape week 5: a Guild ward map, a forge-of-Karak-Dur lead, and a psionic star-chart no one can yet read.
