# Attune to the Whispering Stone

Bror's first attempt to attune to the **Whispering Stone** during downtime at the [Weary Plowman](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) backfires — every voice in the city floods his head and he is left **deafened**. After the [watchtower ambush](survive-the-watchtower-ambush.md), he travels to the **Penumbra Playhouse** in [Twin Songs](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/README.md) and gives [Mother Nocturne](../../../silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md) a full debrief — sewer cult, Phoenix Pits, Zora, the political entanglements — in exchange for ritual guidance. The second attunement attempt also fails (Wisdom save, psychic damage, brief madness), and Mother Nocturne instructs him to seek his companions' aid for the next try. Bror has now durably plugged the [Silent Shroud](../../../silent-shroud/activity/week_2/amplify-the-despair.md) into the party's intelligence stream.
