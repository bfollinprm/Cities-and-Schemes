# Survive the Watchtower Ambush

The party — joined by [High Watcher Alena](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md), persuaded to come — meets **Zora** at midnight at a ruined watchtower on the cliffs near [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md). Zora drops her tiefling disguise, reveals her [Plague of Remembrance](../../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) cell, and opens with *Cone of Cold* — Finneas downed; two thugs cut off escape downstairs. Bror floods the upper floor with **magical Darkness**; Elia's *Devil's Sight* lets her *Eldritch Blast* through it; Spark goes off the cliff in a rage tackle and survives; Finn drops one thug with a sneak-arrow. Zora downs Elia and Bror, *Darkness* ends, Zora drops Bror, and Elia finally lands *Dissonant Whispers* — Zora is forced to flee, teleporting away. **Loot:** clay tokens shaped as cracked vials (cult cell-tokens) and a few silvers. Jaredith is delivered to the [Weary Plowman](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) deconverted and shocked; party takes **five days of downtime**.
