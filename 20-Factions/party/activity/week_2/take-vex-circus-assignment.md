# Take the Vex Circus Assignment

In an alley near the [Weary Plowman](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) the morning after [the Mad Meggan deal](sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md), [Vex](../../../guild/people/vex.md) confronts Kaelan and Bror. Two Guild agents went missing investigating the same sewer site as [the Plague-Father's lab](../week_1/destroy-the-plague-fathers-laboratory.md) and the *Baldur's Mouth* coverage was insufficient. The party reveals — but refuses to surrender — the **Whispering Stone** recovered in week 1; Vex pivots and assigns them a new higher-priority job: investigate the [Circus of the Last Days](../../../carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_2/a-feast-of-frustration.md) and its ringmaster, who "deals in whispers." This supersedes the open Gareth-silencing job. The party does not begin the investigation this week. While the alley meeting runs, Elia's obelisk reacts to a stone hawk figurine at a nearby trinket stall and she purchases it for 25 gp; identification reveals an alien magical "rat catcher" construct.
