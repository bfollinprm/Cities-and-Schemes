# Burglarize the Arcane Emporium

The orphan tiefling [Finn](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/finn.md), working alone during the party's pre-watchtower downtime in [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), breaks into the closed-up "Wondrous Trinkets and Arcane Emporium" — a [Sorcerous Sundries](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/sorcerous-sundries.md) branch shop. She climbs to the roof, repels down the chimney, bypasses a magical trap on a lockbox, and pulls a **scroll of *Pass Without Trace***. Hidden inside the scroll's handle is a **waterproof map of the smuggling tunnels under [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md)**, marking a security weak point in the [Zhentarim's Velvet Hand](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_2/consolidate-the-east-end.md) gambling den. Either [Vex](../../../guild/people/vex.md) or [Roah Moonglow](../../../zhentarim/people/roah-moonglow.md) would pay heavily for it. The party tells neither.
