# Witness Jaredith End the Protest

After five days of downtime in [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), the party intercepts a composed [Jaredith Heartsong](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) descending the Weary Plowman stairs with his lute. Kaelan offers him a Lathanderian blessing for the road. Jaredith walks to [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md), climbs an overturned cart, and plays a ballad of his own life — Elturel, the Hells, prejudice in Baldur's Gate, Hellrider service, and Zora's betrayal. [The Ember](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) appears and amplifies the song across the entire protest. The Hellrider standoff dissolves into communal empathy: a young tiefling drops a brick; merchants open their carts to share food with refugees. The party watches, supports Jaredith afterward, and **levels up to 4**. The Outer City's working majority shifts visibly toward [the Followers of the Phoenix](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_2/the-call-for-martyrs.md) and away from [Alena's disciplined Hellriders](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_2/the-interrogation.md).
