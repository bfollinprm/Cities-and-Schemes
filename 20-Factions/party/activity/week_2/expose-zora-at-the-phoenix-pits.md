# Expose Zora at the Phoenix Pits

Following [Alena's intelligence on the "sister in disguise"](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_2/the-interrogation.md), the party walks into the [Phoenix Pits](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/phoenix-pits.md) in [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) during a sermon by a tiefling preacher named **Zora**, accompanied by [Jaredith Heartsong](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) and [Mikkel](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/mikkel.md). Bror and Kaelan clock that her fervor is performance — eyes cold, cataloguing the crowd. Spark grabs burning embers from the bonfire and offers them to her; Zora silently casts *Absorb Elements* (Kaelan's *Detect Magic* confirms it). Bror accuses her of cheating, Zora reframes the accusation as an insult to the faith and goads Jaredith into an "ember cast" duel. **Spark fights in Bror's place**, takes Jaredith down by armbar, and — instead of finishing him — yields gracefully to leave Jaredith honored, injured, and unable to lead a violent charge for several days. Zora's identity is exposed to the party but not to the wider crowd; her [planned riot](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_2/silence-and-slander.md) is derailed.
