# Sell the Cultist to Mad Meggan

Ordered by [Manip Jerrol](../../../flaming-fist/people/manip-jerrol.md) — who deputizes Kaelan to sidestep a Council-of-Four faceoff at [the High Rider's Stand](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) — to deliver the captured [Plague of Remembrance](../../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) cultist to the [Sea Tower](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/seatower.md), the party instead hires town criers and parades the prisoner publicly across [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) to ['Mad' Meggan's](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) [siege blockade at the Basilisk Gate](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md). Meggan offers a chest of **6,000 gp** (1,000 gp per PC) for custody, recognizing the cultist as a political weapon she can use to delegitimize the patriar elite. The party accepts. Kaelan formally deputizes one of Meggan's traders to receive the prisoner. While Kaelan and Bror negotiate inside the wagon, **Fen** (Finn) lifts a [**Medallion of Thoughts**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/4679-medallion-of-thoughts) from Meggan's neck — a serious blow to her own intelligence-gathering that Meggan does not yet know she has taken.

The party walks away wealthier, in open defiance of [the Flaming Fist](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_2/plan-the-unthinkable.md) (warrant issued by week's end), publicly broken with [the Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_2/the-public-patrons.md), and now sitting on Mad Meggan's amulet.
