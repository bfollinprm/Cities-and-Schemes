# Infiltrate the Circus of the Last Days

Pursuing [Vex's investigation assignment](../week_2/take-vex-circus-assignment.md), the party walks into the Circus encampment east of [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). Bror confirms the dwarf lookout is [Guild](../../../guild/activity/week_3/recover-the-asset.md) by lifting a token from a wall crevice. Kaelan's *Detect Magic* and *See Invisibility* read the entire grounds as suffused with planar bleed: ethereal ghosts in the bleachers, unseen servants in the rigging, the barrier between planes thin throughout. Elia's obsidian shard hums in resonance with Bror's Whispering Stone for the first time.

The party is drawn to a violet silk tent — "A glimpse of what you deserve" — operated by a grey-skinned elven proprietor surrounded by hundreds of floating mirrors. **Elia** picks up an ornate copper hand mirror and sees a perfected version of herself, master broker of the Undercellar; **Bror** picks up a square of polished obsidian and sees himself alone in a silent dwarven hold commanding shadows. The proprietor names Bror "Shadow Stone" without being told. Both PCs accept fey bargains: a true secret each, owed at a later date, in exchange for the visions and the path to them. They then watch [Zara the Sorrow-Eater](../../../carnival-of-whispers/people/mister-smiles.md) harvest grief from the Big Top crowd — including Hellrider Quartermaster [Lyra Sunstone](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md), led backstage afterwards visibly diminished. The party chooses to follow.
