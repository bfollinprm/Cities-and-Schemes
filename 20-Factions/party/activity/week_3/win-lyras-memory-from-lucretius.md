# Win Lyra's Memory from Lucretius

In a perpetually-twilit Feywild glade the party is split by extreme time dilation: Finn waits over a week before Bror arrives, who waits hours for Elia/Spark/Kaelan. Once reassembled, they enter Lucretius's wagon — *The Ephemera Collection* — and confront the ringmaster directly. The wagon's shelves hold jars of harvested memories. A ledger shows [Lyra Sunstone](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) traded "Memories of Elturel's Fall" for "The Strength to Prevent Another"; she is visible nearby, performing chores with superhuman strength but stripped of her former self.

Lucretius offers a fey bargain: answer three riddles, win Lyra's memory back. Win or lose, he will activate Elia's shard — but if she fails, he gets to listen in. The party agrees.

- **Riddle 1.** "A mirror." Correct.
- **Riddle 2.** "A promise." Finn. Correct.
- **Riddle 3.** "An answer." Finn. Correct.

Victorious, the party receives Lyra's memories. Restored, she collapses in anguish at the trauma of Elturel's fall — then rises with her purpose intact and thanks the party. Lucretius honors the bargain and activates Elia's shard, watching but not listening. The party has saved a Hellrider Quartermaster, made a future ally, and unlocked the [Shard of the First Scribe](unlock-the-shard-of-the-first-scribe.md). The mirror-tent debts to the proprietor remain unpaid.
