# Evade the Fist Warrant

The morning after [Jaredith ended the Hellrider protest in song](../week_2/witness-jaredith-end-the-protest.md), the party lingers outside the [Weary Plowman](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) and clocks a four-Fist patrol marching toward the door — almost certainly carrying [the Marshal's warrant](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_3/circle-the-wagons.md) for [the public sale of the Plague cultist to Mad Meggan](../week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md). The party speaks to [Lena](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/people/lena.md), the Plowman's proprietor, who points them at a discreet back-alley exit. They slip out as the Fist enters; Lena misdirects the soldiers convincingly enough that the warrant goes unserved. The party is now publicly fugitive — the warrant is on the books, [the Bibliophile's network is selling officer profiles](../../../bibliophile/activity/week_3/the-price-of-treason.md), [the Goldsmiths are tracing their gold](../../../wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_3/follow-the-money.md), and [the Waterdhavian Kontor's offer of patronage](../../../waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_3/an-offer-of-patronage.md) is sitting unreceived at the Splendid Yard. Lena's loyalty is now an asset they cannot afford to spend twice.
