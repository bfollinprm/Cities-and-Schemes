# Force the Feywild Portal

Backstage at [the Circus of the Last Days](../../../carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_3/sowing-sweet-sorrows.md), Finn slips ahead in disguise; the rest of the party follows under stealth. They find Zara leading [Lyra Sunstone](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) and the other harvested patrons through a shimmering portal of bone-white wood — a confirmed Feywild gateway, guarded by a hulking silent clown ("**Grinmaw**") and two displacer beasts. Lucretius, the ringmaster, appears, names Bror and Elia as the holders of his bargains, and steps through the portal himself, daring them to follow.

Combat begins. **Bror** dashes through the portal first, vanishing. Kaelan spends a point of Edge to act first, *Silences* the area, and shuts down Grinmaw's voiced commands. Elia summons illusory "displacer kittens" to distract the beasts (it amuses them); a real displacer mauls Kaelan with claws and tentacles. Spark rage-charges and shoves Grinmaw to the ground, opening the lane. Finneas slips through the portal under the disengage. Grinmaw rises and wounds Spark; Kaelan caps the round with *Burning Hands* on Grinmaw and a beast. Elia, Spark, and Kaelan flee through the portal together. The Feywild closes behind them.
