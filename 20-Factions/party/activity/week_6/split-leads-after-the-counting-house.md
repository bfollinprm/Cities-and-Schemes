# Split Leads After the Counting House

After [resurrecting Alfira and walking down Dusthawk Hill](storm-dusthawk-hill.md), the party escorts the Ember back to [the outskirts of New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) and convinces her to rest at [the Weary Plowman](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) instead of returning to the High Rider's Stand. **Jaredith stays in New Elturel to oversee the temple construction in Alfira's absence**, formally parting ways with the adventuring group.

At the Plowman the party splits to chase parallel leads:

- **Bror, Elia, and Finn** ride for [Twin Songs](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) and the Penumbra Playhouse to deliver the secret Bror owes [Mother Nocturne](../../../silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md) under the Whispering Stone bargain. Mother Nocturne wants the **physical manifestation** of the secret, not just the word — *the sentient source*. Until Bror can deliver, the debt is unpaid.
- **Kaelan** crosses to the [Lower City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) to bring [Ettvard Needle](../../../baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) the Singa-cult exposé and propose a follow-up Mouth piece. He arrives to find the Baldur's Mouth print shop **ransacked** and Ettvard nowhere in sight. A hidden note in the wreckage hints Needle has fled to the dark crypts under [Tumbledown](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md), on the western bank of Dusthawk Hill — *just where the party left a smoking crater and a pit of obsidian shards.*

The week ends with two open threads: **Bror owes the Night Mother a sentient secret-source**, and **Ettvard Needle is missing**, with [the Consortium-aligned editorial line](../../../../50-Sessions/session_27/gm_summary.md) waiting in the wings. The Reverse Siege strategy is on hold until the party can verify their press contact is alive and willing.
