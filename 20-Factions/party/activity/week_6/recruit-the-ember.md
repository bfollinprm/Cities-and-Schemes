# Recruit the Ember

The party rides to [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) and finds [The Ember (Alfira)](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) presiding over [the Foundation Stone ceremony for the new Phoenix Temple](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_6/the-foundation-stone.md). Spark approaches her with [the Reverse Siege pitch](the-reverse-siege-pitch.md). The Ember is reluctant: her followers are a coalition of faith and refugees, not an army equipped for a prolonged military siege. Spark counters that the party only asks them to bolster a line that is already being held — not to start a war, but to deny the Archduke the food she is stealing from them.

While Spark and the Ember talk, the party walks the construction site and reads the crowd. The mood is **incendiary**. Volunteers work with frantic, almost manic energy; the rhetoric has shifted from survival to holy war; multiple congregants tell the party they are *"ready to start fires"* and burn the city if that's what it takes to survive. The Phoenix faction is no longer passive. Trigger them and they riot.

Over a private lunch in the Ember's tent, Alfira reveals her true reluctance:

- **The Takers of the Tithe** have placed a target on her. She fears any aggressive move by her flock will be the signal for her assassination.
- **The Reaper.** The Hellriders captured a Reaper ([silenced shortly after by necrotic prison-poison](../../../takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_6/the-prison-silence.md)); Alfira is terrified of what that capture means is coming.

The party agrees to help neutralize the Tithe threat before pressing the Reverse Siege pitch further. Spark, Bror, Elia, and Kaelan ride for the High Rider's Stand at first light to interrogate the captured Reaper.
