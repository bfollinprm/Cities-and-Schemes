# Storm Dusthawk Hill

The party — joined by [Jaredith Heartsong](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md), now reformed-pacifist but willing to fight for Alfira — is ambushed in a fog-heavy alley between the High Rider's Stand and the captured Reaper's cell by a Takers of the Tithe collection unit (Spotter, Reaper, Keeper, Thralls). The party kills the Reaper outright; the Keeper escapes after smashing his necromantic lantern; the Spotter is captured. The party recovers a **soul-trapping polearm** and the **Tithe Ledger**. Elia identifies the ledger and confirms that **Alfira, High Watcher Alena, and the entire party are now listed as fresh targets.**

At the High Rider's Stand the party interrogates the captured Reaper through an elaborate "unkillable" ruse — Elia forges the Reaper's name into the Tithe Ledger, Kaelan stages a *Revivify*-after-execution to break his theological resolve, and the Reaper confesses: cell leader is named **[Singa](../../../takers-of-the-tithe/people/singa.md)**, the cell's hideout is on the north face of [Dusthawk Hill](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md), and the cell is targeting high-profile political figures to incite civil war. The Reaper is then poisoned mid-confession by [a corrupt Hellrider cook on the Takers' payroll](../../../takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_6/the-prison-silence.md). The captured Spotter, terrified, agrees to lead the party to Singa's hideout in exchange for protection.

[The Ember disappears from the High Rider's Stand](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) during the interrogation — Elia checks the ledger and Alfira's name is *glowing*. She has been taken for a ritual. The party rides for Dusthawk Hill at dusk.

The ascent is gated by **Four Cairns**, each demanding payment of a "theological debt" by a named party member. [The Skittering Beetle](../../../takers-of-the-tithe/people/the-skittering-beetle.md) offers brokered settlements at each cairn; Kaelan and Elia refuse and take damage and curses (party-wide *Malaise*; Elia's *Fractured ID*); Spark accepts ("blasphemy of logic"; one level of exhaustion); Bror accepts the Beetle's deal and **sells his voice**. Jaredith touches a stone but is not on the ledger and the cairns ignore him.

At the summit the party finds **the Counting House** — Singa's sanctum, styled as an auditor's office — and Alfira chained over an obsidian pit. Singa teleports, mirrors damage onto Alfira via a soul-link, and is grappled by Bror into the pit with a satchel of smokepowder. The Beetle offers Elia the Tithe Ledger trade at the brink of a TPK; Elia surrenders the ledger; debts and curses lift. Kaelan finishes Singa with *Fireball*; Jaredith with a *Chromatic Orb* of fire. Singa burns. The cell on Dusthawk Hill is broken. **Kaelan revives Alfira with a 300-gp diamond Bror finds in the pit.** The party walks her back down the Hill at dawn.
