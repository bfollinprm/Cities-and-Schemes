# The Reverse Siege Pitch

After [reading the Baldur's Mouth Siege Edition at the Plowman](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_6/the-siege-edition.md) — Kaelan's own *Insurance Scam* exposé folded inside it on the investigative-journalism page — the party debates tactical options at the Rivington safehouse. Spark's pitch wins the table: rather than fighting *into* the city against [Sembian patrols](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_6/the-price-of-steel.md) and [the Council's warning shot](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_6/the-warning-shot.md), the party should *reinforce* the Basilisk Gate blockade and **starve the Upper City** of resources from the outside. The Upper City is a parasite on Outer City food, water, and labor; cut the supply lines completely and the patriars must come to the table.

The party maps the three supply routes:

- **Overland.** Already contested — [Mad Meggan's Free Traders](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_6/rationing-the-hope.md) and [Alena's shield-walled Hellriders](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_6/the-shield-wall.md) hold the Basilisk Gate barricade.
- **River.** Weakened — [Errinthal's Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_6/the-desperate-run.md) is bleeding from [the Amnian hostile takeover](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_6/the-hostile-takeover.md); the river is half-shut already.
- **Sea.** Hardest — controlled by the Amnian and Waterdhavian Kontors at Gray Harbour.

The plan: **consolidate allies first.** Recruit The Ember to commit the Phoenix-radicalized refugee crowd as auxiliary blockade-reinforcement, then approach Ettvard Needle and Glim-Glim to sustain the propaganda and water-supply pressure on the Archduke. The party rolls out for New Elturel before the morning is over.
