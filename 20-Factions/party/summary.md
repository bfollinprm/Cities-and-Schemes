# The Party

The four player characters at the table. They began week 0 as strangers thrown together by the city's pull rather than any prior compact: a Sharran monk on a long observation, a fiery Karlach-cultist barbarian, a Great-Old-One warlock chasing a buried secret, and a cynical Lathanderian cleric raising coin for a starving home town. They have no shared faction, no employer of record, and no public name. That deniability is the closest thing the party has to an asset.

## Ideology

There is no shared creed. Each member's reasons for adventuring point in a different direction — knowledge, money, observation, fury — and the party's working compact is pragmatic: protect each other, take work that pays, do not get cornered by any single faction. The shared instinct is **independence**: refuse to be owned by Kontor, guild, cult, or government.

## Membership

Four PCs. Each gets a page under [`people/`](people/) with the source-specified stats, backstory, and faction ties:

- [Bror "The Shadow Stone"](people/bror.md) — Mountain Dwarf monk, Sharran observer.
- [Spark](people/spark.md) — Zariel Tiefling barbarian, Followers of the Phoenix.
- [Elia Thorne](people/elia-thorne.md) — Half-Elf warlock, Great Old One pact.
- [Kaelan of Beregost](people/kaelan.md) — Human Life Cleric of Lathander, raising coin for his home town.

NPC allies who travel with the party (e.g. Finn of New Elturel, Jaredith Heartsong) are recorded under their faction or location pages, not under `party/people/`.

## Methods

Mixed and improvised: Bror's stealth and observation, Spark's overwhelming violence, Elia's eldritch utility, Kaelan's healing-for-fee. The party leans into work that other factions need done deniably — exactly the kind of work the [Guild](../guild/summary.md), [Harpers](../harpers/summary.md), and [Hellriders](../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) cannot do under their own banners.

## Hooks and Relationships

Inbound ties:

- [Followers of the Phoenix](../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) — Spark's parent cult; in week 1 Spark won the Phoenix official representation in New Elturel's council and land for a temple.
- [Hellriders of New Elturel](../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) — Alena reads the party as a useful, deniable instrument after the medicine corridor; in week 1 she paid 100 gp/PC and tasked them with discreetly turning her radicalized lieutenant [Jaredith Heartsong](../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) back from the Phoenix Pits.
- [Chionthar Consortium](../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) — [Factor Errinthal](../chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) co-opted the party in week 1 over the heisted Open Hand medicine cart; the party now holds the **second cart** of anti-[Amnian Kontor](../amnian-kontor/summary.md) propaganda the Consortium does not know it lost.
- [The Guild](../guild/summary.md) — [Vex](../guild/people/vex.md) is testing the party as a freelancer; an open "silence [Gareth](../wc-masons-sculptors/people/gareth.md)" job is on the books.
- [Plague of Remembrance](../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) — the party destroyed the Plague-Father's Rivington-sewer cell and now holds the cell-token's **cracked-vial** sigil and a captured cultist (handed off to Alena).
- [Sembian Kontor](../sembian-kontor/summary.md), [Western Gate Trading Company](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) — both visibly active in the hinterlands the party will work in.

In **week 2** the party [sold the captured cultist to 'Mad' Meggan for 6,000 gp](activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md) at the Basilisk Gate siege, openly defying a [Fist warrant](../flaming-fist/activity/week_2/plan-the-unthinkable.md) and breaking with the [Chionthar Consortium](../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_2/the-public-patrons.md). The [Guild has formally broken with them](../guild/activity/week_2/retaliation-on-the-river.md) over their refusal to surrender the **Whispering Stone**, and [Roah Moonglow's Zhentarim](../zhentarim/activity/week_2/consolidate-the-east-end.md) has offered to erase the warrant in exchange for Black Network service. [Bror's secret-trade with Mother Nocturne](activity/week_2/attune-to-the-whispering-stone.md) at the Penumbra Playhouse has [embedded the Silent Shroud as an intelligence asset inside the party](../silent-shroud/activity/week_2/amplify-the-despair.md). The party reaches **Level 4** at the end of session 9. Current leverage: see [leverage.md](leverage.md).

In **week 3** the party [evades the Fist warrant](activity/week_3/evade-the-fist-warrant.md) at the Weary Plowman with [Lena's](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/people/lena.md) help and walks the [Circus of the Last Days investigation](activity/week_3/infiltrate-the-circus-of-the-last-days.md) for [the Guild](../guild/activity/week_3/recover-the-asset.md). Inside, **Elia and Bror each accept fey bargains with [the mirror-tent proprietor](../carnival-of-whispers/people/mirror-tent-proprietor.md) — one true secret each, owed at a later date.** They [force the Feywild portal](activity/week_3/force-the-feywild-portal.md), confront [Lucretius](../carnival-of-whispers/people/lucretius.md) inside *The Ephemera Collection*, and [win Lyra Sunstone's memory back](activity/week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md) by answering three riddles. Lucretius honors the bargain and [activates Elia's shard](activity/week_3/unlock-the-shard-of-the-first-scribe.md) — Elia and Bror share a cosmic vision of a forgotten **God of Naming** that severed the very piece of itself Elia now carries. The shard reshapes into the **Shard of the First Scribe**. The party returns to find **two weeks have passed** on the Material Plane; the Fist warrant has cooled. Current leverage: see [leverage.md](leverage.md).

In **week 7** the party opens the tenday at [the Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) reading the new (post-Needle) [*Baldur's Mouth*](../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_7/the-puppet-press.md) — visibly Consortium-aligned, blaming the [Sow & Piglet massacre](../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_7/the-refugee-riot.md) on the Plague-of-Remembrance disguising as the Fist, and reporting [Master Phineas Droog's antidote restriction](../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_7/the-antidote-monopoly.md) and [K'laar's astral quarantine](../unchained/activity/week_7/the-astral-quarantine.md) as wartime necessities. They [visit Dammon's forge](activity/week_7/visit-dammons-forge.md): Dammon outfits Bror with **dwarven unarmed-strike gauntlets (+1 to hit)**, tests Spark's cloak with a glowing forge prong (the fire reads as *welcoming*, not painful), and reveals the cloak's clasp and tendrils are forged from **Karlach's discarded original-heart infernal iron**. They [cross the Eastway blockade to Tumbledown](activity/week_7/cross-the-eastway-blockade-to-tumbledown.md) carrying Phineas Droog's **Guild coin** and the name of [Garrett "Grit" Fallow](../guild/people/garrett-grit-fallow.md), a gravedigger on Nine-Fingers Keene's quiet retainer. The Sembian gate detail is preoccupied with deserter-mercenaries from [the Vintner Dry Riot](../wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_7/the-dry-riot.md); the party's poor disguises pass unnoticed. They reach Tumbledown by midafternoon and clock a **five-strong Gravekeeper patrol** in the next yard ([Hemlock's Cliffside Consecration](../gravekeepers/activity/week_7/the-cliffside-consecration.md) is operationally on). What the party does not know yet: [the Takers' Hunter-Killer cell from the Collector is shadowing the Plowman](../takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_7/the-auditors-revenge.md) and has logged Bror, Elia, and Finn's departure for Tumbledown. Current leverage: see [leverage.md](leverage.md). Open threads: the gravedigger patrol decision; the descent into the Tumbledown crypts to find Ettvard Needle alive; the still-unpaid sentient-source debt to Mother Nocturne; the Reverse Siege.

In **week 6** the party reads [the *Baldur's Mouth* Siege Edition with Kaelan's *Insurance Scam* exposé folded inside](../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_6/the-siege-edition.md), Spark proposes [the Reverse Siege strategy](activity/week_6/the-reverse-siege-pitch.md) — reinforce the Basilisk Gate barricade and starve the Upper City — and [the party rides for New Elturel to recruit The Ember](activity/week_6/recruit-the-ember.md). The Ember will not commit until the Takers' target on her back is removed. The party's investigation of the captured Reaper at the High Rider's Stand turns up [Singa's cell on Dusthawk Hill](../takers-of-the-tithe/people/singa.md) and a **mole in the Hellrider supply line**; [Alfira is abducted mid-interrogation](../../50-Sessions/session_25/player_summary.md), and [the party storms the Hill](activity/week_6/storm-dusthawk-hill.md). Four Cairns levy theological-debt audits along the way: Kaelan and Elia refuse and absorb damage and curses, Spark accepts an Exhaustion, **Bror sells his voice to [the Skittering Beetle](../takers-of-the-tithe/people/the-skittering-beetle.md)**. At the Counting House summit Singa's soul-link mirrors damage onto Alfira; Bror grapples Singa into a smokepowder-detonating obsidian pit; the Beetle accepts **the physical Tithe Ledger** in trade for the party's lives and lifts every curse; Kaelan and Jaredith finish Singa with *Fireball* and *Chromatic Orb*. **Singa is dead. Alfira is revived. Jaredith stays in New Elturel to oversee the Phoenix Temple construction.** The party walks Alfira to the Plowman to rest. Kaelan crosses to Heapside to find the [Baldur's Mouth print shop ransacked, Ettvard Needle missing](activity/week_6/split-leads-after-the-counting-house.md), with a hidden note hinting Ettvard has fled to the Tumbledown crypts — *just where the party left a smoking crater the day before*. Bror, Elia, and Finn deliver Bror's secret to [Mother Nocturne](../silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md); the Night Mother demands the **physical sentient source**, not the words; the debt remains unpaid. Current leverage: see [leverage.md](leverage.md). Open threads: rescue Ettvard Needle from Tumbledown; produce a sentient secret-source for Mother Nocturne; figure out who the Hellrider mole is; press on with the Reverse Siege.

In **week 5** the party [buys three Candlekeep-key books at the Pilgrim's Parchment](activity/week_5/the-pilgrims-parchment-buy.md), [kills Vex in a warehouse ambush in Gray Harbour](activity/week_5/kill-vex-and-loot-her-office.md), and walks out with the intel cache that breaks the city's hidden plots open. They [bring it to Alena and Mad Meggan at the Basilisk Gate camp](activity/week_5/the-rivington-war-council.md), reach **Level 5**, and become the alliance's field instrument. Concurrent operations: [mining Eastway with the captured smokepowder while detonating the Velvet Hand](activity/week_5/mine-eastway-and-detonate-the-velvet-hand.md); [walking out of Errinthal's Coster Hall ambush as the smiling escort of a relief caravan they then turn into a free feast](activity/week_5/errinthals-relief-caravan-pr-coup.md); and [installing Finneas as the new Guild Boss of Rivington](activity/week_5/install-finneas-as-guild-boss-of-rivington.md), with [Simbar](../guild/people/simbar.md) as nominal puppet, behind which Kaelan also lands a [Baldur's Mouth investigative-journalism position from Ettvard Needle](../baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) and the **Credential of Balduran**. Current leverage: see [leverage.md](leverage.md).
