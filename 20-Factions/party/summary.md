# The Party

A four-person freelance crew working as deniable agents across post-crisis Baldur's Gate. Diverse — competing faiths, ambitions, and patrons — but operationally cohesive enough to have become a real factor in faction politics across the campaign's first eight weeks.

## Membership

- **[Bror "The Shadow Stone"](people/bror.md)** — Shadowfell-tied; carries an obsidian stone resonating with Sharran and Circus-of-the-Last-Days energies. In transactional pact with [Mother Nocturne](../silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md) since Session 9.
- **[Spark](people/spark.md)** — Follower of the Phoenix, Karlach-worshipper, the party's battering ram. Negotiated formal recognition for the [Followers of the Phoenix](../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) (Session 3); subject of "immortality" rumors with saint-figure consequences.
- **[Elia Thorne](people/elia-thorne.md)** — Great Old One warlock; carries the *Whispering Stone* that triggered The Guild's betrayal.
- **[Kaelan of Beregost](people/kaelan.md)** — Cleric of Lathander, freelance healer, sending earnings back to a starving home town.

## Ideology

No shared ideology. Each member runs on a distinct personal motive: Bror's pact, Spark's faith, Elia's research, Kaelan's mission home. The crew holds together by professional respect and a pattern of taking contracts more reputable factions cannot publicly take.

## Methods

- Deniable agency. [High Watcher Alena](../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/alena.md) has used the party repeatedly for internal political crises she cannot address publicly — the medicine heist, Jaredith de-radicalization, Plague of Remembrance prisoner debrief.
- Negotiation and de-escalation as preference. Sessions 1–3 saw the party resolve crises through diplomacy and creative solutions rather than violence; Spark's natural-20 persuasion shaped the New Elturel political balance.
- Direct intervention when needed. Bypassed the [Worshipful Company of Alchemists & Apothecaries](../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/summary.md) by recovering Chionthar Consortium medical shipments for the Open Hand Temple. Intercepted Silas in the sewers and exposed the Guild's smokepowder cleanup.
- Freelance intelligence brokerage. Selling captured prisoners to factions who can use them ([Mad Meggan](../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md), week 6, for 6,000 gp) while looting the buyer at the same time.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Active threats.** [The Guild](../guild/summary.md) — Vex authorized to retrieve the *Whispering Stone* "by any means necessary." [Plague of Remembrance](../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) — Zora's Watchtower Ambush failed but she may still be in the field. [Worshipful Company of Alchemists & Apothecaries](../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/summary.md) — privately furious about the Open Hand Temple workaround. [Cult of the Returning Lord](../cult-of-the-returning-lord/summary.md) — engaged at Dusthawk Hill, ongoing tension over the captured Reaper at the Phoenix temple site.

**Allies / patrons.** [Hellriders of New Elturel](../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) and especially [High Watcher Alena](../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/alena.md). [Followers of the Phoenix](../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) through Spark and the Ember. [Silent Shroud](../silent-shroud/summary.md) through Bror's transactional pact with Mother Nocturne.

**Compromised relationships.** [Free Traders of the Outskirts](../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) — Mad Meggan does not yet know the party stole from her wagon. [The Guild](../guild/summary.md) — formerly an asset, now actively hostile.

**Acquired companions.** **Finn of New Elturel** (orphaned tiefling, translator/runner), **Jaredith Heartsong** (de-radicalized Hellrider paladin), **Lyra Sunstone** (Hellrider quartermaster, gift-bearer and messenger).
