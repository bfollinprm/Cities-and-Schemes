# Spark

Acolyte of the [Followers of the Phoenix](../../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) and the party's ranged-and-melee force-of-presence. Orphaned in the [Outer City](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md), raised by the Followers, devoted to Karlach as saint of righteous anger. One of the four members of [the party](../summary.md).

## Role within the party

The party's battering ram in combat, its emotional center in negotiation. Spark's natural-20 persuasion check in Session 3 won formal recognition for the Followers and a temple site in New Elturel — fundamentally reshaping the New Elturel political balance.

## Faction relationships

- **[Followers of the Phoenix](../../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md):** Member, increasingly mythologized after Bror planted "immortality" rumors. The Followers may begin to view Spark as a literal Phoenix vessel — a development Spark has not asked for and the party may not welcome.
- **[Hellriders of New Elturel](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md):** Trusted by [High Watcher Alena](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) as the bridge between her moderates and the Followers' radicals — a role she leveraged for the Jaredith de-radicalization. Received leather armor and a perfectly-balanced greatsword from [Lyra Sunstone](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) on the Plowman doorstep ([Session 12](../../../50-Sessions/session_12/player_summary.md)).
- **[Plague of Remembrance](../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md):** Defeated **Zora** in non-lethal duel at the Phoenix Pits (Session 7), exposing her cult ties.

## Treasurer

Acts as the party's coin-keeper through week 5. Pays the **125 gp Symbian-mercenary bribe** at the Plowman ([Session 12](../../../50-Sessions/session_12/player_summary.md)) and the **900 gp** for [three Candlekeep-key books](../activity/week_5/the-pilgrims-parchment-buy.md) at the Pilgrim's Parchment ([Session 12](../../../50-Sessions/session_12/player_summary.md)).
