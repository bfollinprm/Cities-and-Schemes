# Bror "The Shadow Stone"

A Shadowfell-tied operative who carries an obsidian stone resonating with both Sharran sanctum-magic and the entropic emanations of the [Carnival of Whispers'](../../carnival-of-whispers/summary.md) Circus of the Last Days. One of the four members of [the party](../summary.md).

## Faction relationships

- **[Silent Shroud](../../silent-shroud/summary.md):** Transactional pact with [Mother Nocturne](../../silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md) since Session 9. The Sharrans now have an intelligence channel into the party's discoveries via the obsidian stone.
- **[Cult of the Returning Lord](../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/summary.md):** Accepted a deal from the "Beetle" Soul Broker entity at Dusthawk Hill (Session 12) — terms and consequences uncertain.
- **[Carnival of Whispers](../../carnival-of-whispers/summary.md):** Stone resonance to the Circus's planar tampering creates an unwelcome sensitivity to its emotional aura.
