# Finn

Tiefling girl from [New Elturel](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md), street-hardened and magically trained at least to Thaumaturgy. Travels with a pet rust-weasel named **Scrap**. The fifth and most recent member of [the party](../summary.md), having joined in week 1.

## Role within the party

The party's burglar and small-spaces operator. Finn runs solo side-jobs the heavier PCs cannot reach — the [Wondrous Trinkets and Arcane Emporium burglary](../activity/week_2/burglarize-the-arcane-emporium.md) is the canonical example: roof, chimney, magical lockbox, scroll-of-*Pass-Without-Trace* hiding the Velvet Hand smuggling map. Inside larger fights she punches well above her weight, having stabbed the wounded Plague-Father out of his retreat after rolling a natural 20 on her death save in [Session 5](../../../50-Sessions/session_5/player_summary.md).

## Faction relationships

- **[Hellriders of New Elturel](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md):** Ward of [High Watcher Alena](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) since [Session 5](../../../50-Sessions/session_5/player_summary.md). Alena formally takes responsibility for her after the Plague-Father's lab is destroyed; the practical arrangement has Finn back out the door with the party most weeks.
- **[Plague of Remembrance](../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md):** Survived the Plague-Father's lab as one of his intended victims; recognized the cultists' "whispering stone" before the party did.
