# Elia Thorne

Great Old One warlock who treats her pact as a research partnership with a cosmic intelligence rather than worship. Carries the *Whispering Stone* psionic artifact that has put the party in The Guild's crosshairs. One of the four members of [the party](../summary.md).

## Pact and personality

Elia's pact patron is a Great Old One she scoffs at calling a god — to her, it is a co-investigator. Her exterior is intellectually arrogant and pedantic; her interior is afraid of what whispers in her ear. She frames every expedition as research and every acquisition as evidence.

## Faction relationships

- **[The Guild](../../guild/summary.md):** Vex authorized to retrieve the *Whispering Stone* from her "by any means necessary" after the party's three-week absence. Active threat.
- **[Hands of the Absolute](../../hands-of-the-absolute/summary.md):** The Stone's psionic resonance is exactly what The Voice in the Choir's resonance chambers are hunting for. Elia is not yet known to them, but if she is, she becomes a target.
- **[Sorcerous Sodality](../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/summary.md):** Unlicensed warlock-pact magic; would be classified as a hedge-warlock if discovered.
