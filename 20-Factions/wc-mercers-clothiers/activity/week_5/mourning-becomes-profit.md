# Mourning Becomes Profit

[Mistress Elethra Vance](../../people/elethra-vance.md), reading the city's grim mood — [an ultimatum at the gates](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md), [a Bhaalist butchering rumor under Tumbledown](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_5/relocate-the-sanctum.md), [murdered Wharf Rats on a Gray Harbour pier](../../../wharf-rats/activity/week_5/licking-their-wounds.md) — converts atmosphere into product. The Mercers' atelier in [Bloomridge](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/bloomridge.md) unveils a new exclusive line: **somber couture**, mourning-clothes in the latest cuts, lined in restrained black silk imported via Sembian channels and trimmed with Waterdhavian jet beadwork. The line is announced at a private salon at Vance's atelier above the Facemaker's Boutique. By the third afternoon, the line is the *only* thing the Manorborn patriar elite will be seen in. Two Coin-bloc peers send notes ordering matching family suites. A Hhune cousin commissions a cape *with a hood*. The Mercers' coffers fill faster than the looms can keep up. **In a city without anything pleasant left to wear, Vance has sold the patriar elite the next year's wardrobe in three days.**
