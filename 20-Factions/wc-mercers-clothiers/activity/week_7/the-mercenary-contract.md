# The Mercenary Contract

Riding the success of [last tenday's "siege chic" line](../../activity/week_6/the-winter-collection.md), [Mistress Elethra Vance](../../people/elethra-vance.md) closes a far larger contract: the **uniform contract for Archduke Silvershield's Sembian private army**. The Mercer line at the Facemaker's Boutique pivots from patriar fur-coats to mass-production of Sembian field tunics, livery sashes, distinct Coin-Sword unit-pips, and three weights of officer overcoats — *all cut, dyed, and embroidered in Bloomridge to the Archduke's specifications*.

The work is enormous, the margin is massive, and the political signal is unmistakable: **the Mercers' Guild is now openly the Archduke's outfitter.** Bloomridge's manufacturing district begins running double shifts. Apprentices are pulled off bridal commissions and put on twin-stitch piecework. The Mercers are *visibly* and *publicly* picking a side in the city's internal cold war, and their pick is the Sembian Archduke — exactly the moment the Peerage of Blood is filing its [Ultimatum to the Duke](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_7/the-ultimatum-to-the-duke.md). Vance bets on Silvershield surviving. Time will tell.
