# The Color of Authority

[Mistress Elethra Vance](../../people/elethra-vance.md) follows up [last week's officer-uniform contract](../activity/week_2/weave-a-web-of-whispers.md) with the actual designs — and presents them at [Wyrm's Rock Fortress](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md). The new dress uniform is a quiet political statement. The piping is in the deep red of the [Peerage of Blood](../../../peerage-of-blood/summary.md); the collar device echoes [the Patriar Heritage Society's](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_3/defend-the-marshal.md) standard. The Fist's senior officers — already swayed by [Durinbold's defense of the Marshal](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_3/filibuster-the-mercenary-act.md) at Parliament — accept the designs without comment. By the time the first orders are cut, the Fist's parade-ground silhouette has shifted toward old-money colors and away from the Council's. Vance has made the political alignment of the Fist visible on its own uniforms before the Fist's officers will admit it themselves.
