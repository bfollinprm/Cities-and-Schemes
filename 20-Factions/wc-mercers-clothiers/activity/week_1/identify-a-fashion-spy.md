# Identify a Fashion Spy

Mistress [Elethra Vance's](../../people/elethra-vance.md) internal security identifies the journeyman tailor leaking patriar winter-gown designs to a rival [Bloomridge](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/bloomridge.md) boutique. The man is fired and quietly blacklisted across the trade. The Mercers neither prosecute nor publicize: the message is meant for other journeymen, not for the magistrates.
