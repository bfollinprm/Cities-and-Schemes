# The Winter Collection

Anticipating a long siege and another freezing month, [Mistress Elethra Vance](../../people/elethra-vance.md) pivots [the wildly successful "somber couture" line of last week](../../activity/week_5/mourning-becomes-profit.md) into a new theme: **"siege chic"** — warm, durable, yet fashionable patriar coats, fur-lined and storm-cuffed, in the Bloomridge Boutique's signature dark plum and forest green. The collection is staged in the boutique window with a single tableau showing the new coats over candlelit braziers, alongside imported smokeglass spectacles and steel-tipped walking-canes for the icy roads.

The line sells out within forty-eight hours. Every sold coat is at four times the previous quarter's average per-unit margin, and the Mercers' Bloomridge office runs three additional cutting shifts to keep up with re-orders. Vance is *delighted*. The siege will not end soon, the patriars are trapped in the Upper City by [the Watch's gate lockdown](../../../watch/activity/week_6/the-gate-lockdown.md), and they would rather freeze fashionably than freeze inelegantly. **Vanity survives the siege.** It is also a quietly meaningful reading of the city's political mood: the patriars who can pay 800 gp for a coat are not seriously expecting the siege to end.
