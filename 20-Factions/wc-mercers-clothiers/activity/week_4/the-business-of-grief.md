# The Business of Grief

With tensions at all-time-high after [the High Hall brawl](../../../parliament-of-peers/activity/week_4/a-debate-of-the-deaf.md), the published [Letter of Censure](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_4/the-letter-of-censure.md), and several high-profile patriar deaths still in the broadsheet (the Reaper attempt, the Bhaalist patrol skirmish, the Heapside escapes), the demand for fine mourning clothes among the city's elite skyrockets. [Mistress Elethra Vance](../../people/elethra-vance.md) reads the curve and triples the price of all black silk and velvet from her [Bloomridge](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/bloomridge.md) atelier overnight. The patriars pay. Vance pockets the largest single-week margin of her career and quietly redirects part of the windfall to widen the [Mercer-officer-uniform pipeline she runs through Wyrm's Rock](../../../wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_3/the-color-of-authority.md). The Mercers are now the financial winners of the political crisis they helped dress.
