# Sell the Solution to Fear

Capitalizing on the [Bhaalist murder spree](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_2/a-murder-in-the-crowd.md) and the wider Outer-City unrest, [Mistress Anya Brightwood](../../people/anya-brightwood.md) closes a profitable contract with [High Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md) to expand lamplighter coverage in the **Norchapel** district of the Upper City. The Chandlers add a tithe of "lighting fees" to nearby patriar households as the bonus. Lamplighter crews now have a sanctioned reason to be on Norchapel streets all night — useful surveillance reach for whoever buys their books later.
