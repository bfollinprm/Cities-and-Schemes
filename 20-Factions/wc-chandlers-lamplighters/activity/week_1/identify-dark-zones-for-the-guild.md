# Identify 'Dark Zones' for The Guild

In exchange for [Guild](../../../guild/summary.md) protection on her lamplighter crews working the rougher Lower City beats, Mistress [Anya Brightwood](../../people/anya-brightwood.md) hands a Ward Boss a curated list of streets where lamps will "malfunction." The list creates predictable dark zones perfect for ambushes and smuggling runs. The Guild gets a route map; the Chandlers get to send crews home alive.
