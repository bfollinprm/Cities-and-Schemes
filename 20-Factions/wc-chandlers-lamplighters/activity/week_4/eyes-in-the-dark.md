# Eyes in the Dark

A sharp-eyed [Zhentarim](../../../zhentarim/summary.md) agent in [Eastway](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) notices the same pair of [Chandler lamplighters](../../people/anya-brightwood.md) walking the same beat past the same Black Network safehouse three nights running. He follows one home, intercepts him in a back alley, and interrogates the man until he confesses his role as a [Guild](../../../guild/summary.md) informant — part of [the lamplighter intelligence subscription Anya Brightwood opened in week 3](../../../wc-chandlers-lamplighters/activity/week_3/a-light-in-the-storm.md). The lamplighter is released bruised but alive, on the condition that he keeps reporting up the chain — so the Zhentarim can begin feeding [Vex](../../../guild/people/vex.md) deliberately false intelligence through him. The Chandlers' surveillance network in Eastway is now compromised in the worst possible way: still functional, no longer trustworthy.
