# The Guided Darkness

[Mistress Anya Brightwood](../../people/anya-brightwood.md) — already coerced by the Guild into [last tenday's maintenance blackout](../../activity/week_6/the-dark-streets.md) — is forced to **escalate**: a planned, total streetlamp blackout across **every street within five blocks of the Velvet Hand**, Brampton's flagship Zhentarim social club. The blackout is ordered as a "lamp-oil supply audit" routed through the regular Chandler maintenance schedule.

The blackout night is the night [the Guild's assassins finish their digital purge](../../../guild/activity/week_7/the-digital-purge.md). Six Zhentarim ledger-clerks, two enforcers, and one journeyman caster are killed inside the four-hour window between sundown and the lamps' "scheduled" relighting. Brightwood is herself in the Chandlers' guildhall the entire night with witnesses; her fingerprints are not on any of the deaths. **The Chandlers' Guild is now functionally a Guild auxiliary unit operating as a public utility.** The Zhentarim notice. [Roah Moonglow](../../../zhentarim/people/roah-moonglow.md) adds Brightwood to a personal target list she has not previously kept.
