# A Light in the Storm

[Mistress Anya Brightwood](../../people/anya-brightwood.md) fulfills [the Norchapel expansion contract she sold the patriars on at the height of the Bhaalist scare](../week_2/sell-the-solution-to-fear.md). Crews push lamplighter coverage out across [Norchapel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/norchapel.md) on the [Watch's recommended](../../../watch/activity/week_3/maintain-appearances.md) routes. The hidden value is unchanged from week 2: the same crews, lighting lamps, watch the same streets every night and discreetly report unusual activity back through the Company. Anya now has a low-level standing intelligence network across half the Outer City's residential streets — people who write down which Hellrider patrols pass which corner at which time, which doors stay lit late, and which warehouse takes deliveries after midnight. The product is sold neutral, mostly to the [Watch](../../../watch/activity/week_3/maintain-appearances.md) and [the Goldsmiths](../../../wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_3/follow-the-money.md), at modest rates that cover the lamp oil with margin to spare.
