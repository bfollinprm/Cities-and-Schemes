# Mapping the Battlefield

[Master Elias Vancroft](../../people/elias-vancroft.md), having [made coin selling tactical maps to both sides of the blockade last week](../../../wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_4/playing-both-sides.md), ups the ambition: surveyors in the field, mapping [Commander K'laar's githyanki positions in and around Wyrm's Rock](../../../unchained/activity/week_5/establish-the-perimeter.md). The product — a precise plat of githyanki billet quarters and patrol arcs — would sell for staggering money to the Free Traders, the Knights of the Shield, the Iron Throne dossier-mill, and possibly the Council itself. The surveyors do not get the chance. K'laar's psionic perimeter detects them before they reach the third milestone. Three Cartographers are taken alive at the bridge mid-survey. They are stripped of their equipment, their measuring rods broken across the bridge stone, their memories *wiped* of the day's reading, and deposited *naked* at the Wyrm's Crossing terminus with a note pinned to the lead surveyor's belt: a single githyanki rune that translates as "stay away." The Cartographers' Guild has lost three sets of expensive instruments, no maps, and several very specific memories. **The warning is unmistakable.** Vancroft pulls all surveyors out of Outer City fieldwork by sundown.
