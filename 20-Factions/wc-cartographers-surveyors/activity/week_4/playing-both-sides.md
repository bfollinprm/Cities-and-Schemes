# Playing Both Sides

[Master Elias Vancroft](../../people/elias-vancroft.md), [already double-selling assault and defense maps last week](../../../wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_3/chart-the-chaos.md), commissions duplicate sets of his Basilisk Gate blockade tactical surveys. One set goes to [the Archduke's mercenary command](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md). The other goes — through a stall on the Eastway side — to ['Mad' Meggan's](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) second-in-command. Master Vancroft prices each set at a hostile-deployment premium. The Cartographers' Guild profits from both sides of the impending conflict and from neither knowing the other has the same map. (Independently this week, Vancroft also lets [the Bibliophile](../../../bibliophile/activity/week_4/the-price-of-strategy.md) acquire copies of Wyrm's Rock interior plans through a sympathetic clerk.)
