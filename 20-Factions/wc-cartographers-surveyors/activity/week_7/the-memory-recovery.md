# The Memory Recovery

After [last week's failed Greater Restoration on the K'liir-wiped surveyor](../../activity/week_6/the-memory-loss.md) — the Cartographers learning the wipe was *psionic, not magical* — [Master Elias Vancroft](../../people/elias-vancroft.md) hires a *psion* through a Sodality intermediary. The psion (a Sodality apprentice paid in untraceable gemstones) sits with the survivor for three days running an interlinked dream-walk and successfully **recovers the Githyanki map data** the K'liir psionic sweep had wiped clean.

The recovered data is gold: a partial map of the Astral Plane bridges the Unchained have been quietly threading underneath Wyrm's Rock, and the location of one that opens onto an old Sembian smuggling route. Vancroft sells the smuggling-route fragment to [Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../../sembian-kontor/people/korgan-ironhand.md) within hours for a handsome fee. The Cartographers are **back in the espionage game**. Vancroft does not yet know — and the Sodality apprentice has not yet noticed — that the dream-walk also picked up *the Hands of the Absolute's psionic lattice signature*, sitting buried in the survivor's wiped memory like a thumbprint left in fresh wax.
