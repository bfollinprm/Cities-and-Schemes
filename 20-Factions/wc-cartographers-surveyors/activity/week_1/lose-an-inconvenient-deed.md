# 'Lose' an Inconvenient Deed

The Cartographers' attempt to silence a minor patriar and "lose" a land deed inconvenient to the [Western Gate Trading Company](../../../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) fails outright. The patriar dodges the planned "accident" and successfully files the ancient deed with the city magistrates, putting Western Gate's claim to a key piece of property into open public dispute. [Elias Vancroft](../../people/elias-vancroft.md)'s guild absorbs the legal exposure and the bill, while [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) finds a fresh fire to put out.
