# Monetize the Maze

[Master Elias Vancroft](../../people/elias-vancroft.md) sells proprietary "alternate trade route" maps to the [Western Gate Trading Company](../../../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) through the [Bibliophile](../../../bibliophile/activity/week_2/intelligence-for-sale.md) — routes that bypass the [Free Trader siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md) at the Basilisk Gate but quietly omit the dangerous fauna along several stretches. The Western Gate pays full price; their first caravan-guard contract along the new route is the same one [the Iron Throne lost the bid on](../../../iron-throne/activity/week_2/the-high-risk-wager.md). [Roah Moonglow](../../../zhentarim/people/roah-moonglow.md) is also in Vancroft's ledger this week, buying a private copy.
