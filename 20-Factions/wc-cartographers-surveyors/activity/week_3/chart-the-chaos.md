# Chart the Chaos

[Master Elias Vancroft](../../people/elias-vancroft.md) has his surveyors making detailed tactical maps of both fortifications: the [Free Trader siege line at the Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) (sightlines, ditches, the new sharpened-stake reinforcements going up by the day), and [Wyrm's Rock Fortress itself](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md) (gate timings, sentry rotations, the [Manip Jerrol unit's](../../../flaming-fist/people/manip-jerrol.md) recall pattern as the Fist consolidates). The intelligence package is for sale to all comers — [the Archduke's mercenaries](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_3/the-private-option.md) for the assault map, ['Mad' Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) for the defense map, [the Bibliophile](../../../bibliophile/activity/week_3/the-price-of-treason.md) for the consolidated package, and [the Sodality](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_3/follow-the-resonance.md) and [Cartographers' fellow Worshipful Companies](../../../wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_3/an-appeal-to-the-marshal.md) for whatever they're willing to pay. War is the most profitable cartography in a generation.
