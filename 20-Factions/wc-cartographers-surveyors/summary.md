# Worshipful Company of Cartographers & Surveyors

A once-quiet guild made indispensable by the post-crisis redrawing of property lines and the explosion of new trade routes. Their seal is a legal requirement on every land deed in the city, and they hold the most detailed extant maps of the sewer and undercity systems — closely guarded as both intelligence asset and revenue stream. Led by the precise [Master Elias Vancroft](people/elias-vancroft.md).

## Ideology

To be the sole, trusted source of geographic and property information in the region. They treat accuracy as sacred duty — and the right to define what is accurate as the foundation of their political power.

## Membership

The city's most meticulous cartographers, surveyors, and architects. The Court of Assistants includes the surveyors who certified post-crisis property reconstruction. Junior journeymen do the field work; master cartographers control the archive.

## Methods

- Mandatory seal on land deeds — a fee on every property transaction.
- Trade-route maps sold at premium to merchant consortiums and Kontors. Week 0: Vancroft sold an "exclusive, safe passage" map through the Trollclaws to two competing caravan masters at the same time. The route is notoriously dangerous.
- Closely-guarded sewer maps — the single most valuable trade secret in the guild's archive, repeatedly targeted by [The Guild](../guild/summary.md) for theft.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** [The Guild](../guild/summary.md), which wants the sewer maps. Rival merchant consortiums seeking advantage in opening new trade routes. Caravan masters who have been sold the same "exclusive" route at full price.

**Allies of convenience.** Whichever consortium is willing to pay for premium intelligence in any given week.
