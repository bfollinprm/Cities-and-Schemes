# Lucretius

Tall, pale, dressed in shadow-black velvet — the figure the party meet face-to-face at the [Circus of the Last Days](../summary.md) in week 3. He gives the headline speech in the Big Top about the circus as "a beautiful lie" that helps people forget their sorrows, and personally appears at [the Feywild portal](../../party/activity/week_3/force-the-feywild-portal.md) backstage to challenge the party. Inside *The Ephemera Collection* wagon he honors a fey-bargain riddle game with the party, returns [Lyra Sunstone's](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) memory, and activates Elia's [Shard of the First Scribe](../../party/activity/week_3/unlock-the-shard-of-the-first-scribe.md).

## Read

A connoisseur of *ephemera* — memories, secrets, powerful souls — and a patient collector. He is not a thug. He uses curated access (Grinmaw and the displacers as a filter, the riddle game as an entrance exam) to test the people he is interested in, and he honors fair-game bargains even when he loses. His real interest is Elia's shard: a cosmic memory fragment of a forgotten "God of Naming." He loses the bargain to win the long con — by activating the shard while Elia is still bound to it, he ensures her obsession with the artifact will only deepen.

He recognized **Bror** as "Shadow Stone" without being introduced and showed [genuine curiosity](../../party/activity/week_3/infiltrate-the-circus-of-the-last-days.md) when their eyes met during his speech.

## Identity (DM choice)

The relationship between Lucretius and [Mister Smiles](mister-smiles.md) is not committed in the source. Possibilities:

- Lucretius is **the Feywild patron** Mister Smiles serves, surfacing in person to handle a memory of cosmic value.
- Lucretius is **a persona Smiles wears** when dealing with high-tier targets, as opposed to the carnival-barker public face.
- Lucretius and Smiles are **separate fey nobles** sharing the Circus as a venue.

The source supports any of the three; the dm-notes consistently treat Lucretius as the operator the party meets in week 3.
