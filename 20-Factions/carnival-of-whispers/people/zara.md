# Zara the Sorrow-Eater

The Big Top's mass-harvest performer at the [Circus of the Last Days](../summary.md). Her closing dance is a ritualized sorrow-siphon: a beautiful, mournful performance that draws faint gray wisps of grief out of the audience and into her, leaving the patrons "cathartically light" and her visibly more vibrant.

## Read

Zara is the **renewable-resource** half of the Circus's harvest. Where [the mirror-tent proprietor](mirror-tent-proprietor.md) takes secrets one-on-one and [Lucretius](lucretius.md) collects cosmic memory at the high end, Zara generates a steady, repeatable feed of generic grief from the working-class crowd that returns again and again for the relief.

Her targets escalate. After the main show she selects emotionally drained patrons — including Hellrider Quartermaster [Lyra Sunstone](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) in week 3 — and leads them backstage through a bone-white wood Feywild portal into [Lucretius's *Ephemera Collection*](../../party/activity/week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md), where the bespoke memory trades happen. The patrons walk out hollowed: Lyra emerged with superhuman strength but stripped of her Hellrider identity until [the party recovered her memories](../../party/activity/week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md).

The party witnesses the harvest in week 3. Kaelan, watching the post-show procession, observes that Lyra appears **diminished** while Zara appears **more vibrant and powerful**.
