# The Debt Comes Due

[Mister Smiles](../../people/mister-smiles.md), trying to send a Memory Mite courier across Baldur's Gate to call in a long-deferred fey debt, watches the message vanish before it reaches its recipient. The Mite is intercepted and *consumed* by another, unfamiliar fey creature drawn to the city by the chaotic magical residue swelling around it — likely a downstream effect of [Lucretius's Feywild dilation](../../../party/activity/week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md), [the Sundries vault arcane alarms](../../../hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_4/the-sundered-vault.md), and [the Sodality's new permanent scrying censers](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_4/the-scrying-censors.md) lighting up the planar weather. The message is lost. Worse, Mister Smiles now knows that an *unknown entity of his order* is in the city, hunting his couriers. The Carnival shifts to high alert. The two PCs holding [unpaid fey bargains](../../../carnival-of-whispers/people/mirror-tent-proprietor.md) are filed under "do not lose track of."
