# Sowing Sweet Sorrows

[Mister Smiles](../../people/mister-smiles.md) dispatches his performers — silent clowns, juggling skeletons, hawking barkers — out of the Circus encampment to mingle with the crowds along the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) blockade line. They are not picking pockets. They are using subtle fey magic to amplify the despair already thick in the protest crowd: a quiet sob made louder, a tired sigh made bottomless, a spike of hopelessness when the [Sembian envoys](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_3/the-alternate-route.md) walk past. The harvested grief flows back to the Circus and feeds his patron. Outwardly the crowd seems unchanged; medically, the [Open Hand Temple](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/open-hand-temple.md) records a sharp uptick in despondency cases by the end of the week. Smiles is also positioning to receive the grief that will follow when the Archduke's mercenaries arrive.
