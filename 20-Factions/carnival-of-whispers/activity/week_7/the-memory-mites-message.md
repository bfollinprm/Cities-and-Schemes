# The Memory Mite's Message

[Mister Smiles](../../people/mister-smiles.md) — running [the alley circus behind the Blushing Mermaid](../../activity/week_6/the-whispering-alley.md) — escalates from passive harvesting to *active manipulation*. Using a **Memory Mite** seeded into the dreams of three Lower City refugees, he tracks the missing **Bror** and **Elia** through the dream-spaces of everyone who has shared a tavern with them in the last tenday. The Mite returns each night with fragments: a Twin Songs candle-shadow, the sound of a writing-slate scraping, the distant smell of crypts-mud and silver burn.

Smiles uses the same dream-net to **feed his patron** the city's ambient anxiety: every refugee dreaming of the closed Upper City gates, every patriar dreaming of the Lower City mob breaching their door, every Sembian mercenary dreaming of his unpaid pay-week. The Carnival's patron is *fattening*. By week's end Smiles knows roughly where Bror has been (Twin Songs, Tumbledown's edge) without ever being seen on the same street. He files the location with [Lucretius](../../people/lucretius.md) for next tenday's collection.
