# Host a 'Dream-Inducing' Performance

[Mister Smiles](../../people/mister-smiles.md) puts on a public night-show at the Circus of the Last Days designed to bait the audience into a shared dream-state his fey patron can harvest. The illusion-weaving is unstable; the show comes off as confusing and tonally flat rather than ecstatic. No usable emotional residue is harvested. The fey patron is left unsatisfied — and the Carnival's working capital with the patron contracts again.
