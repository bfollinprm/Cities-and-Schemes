# A Feast of Frustration

[Mister Smiles'](../../people/mister-smiles.md) circus, encamped at the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md), abducts a wealthy merchant trapped on the wrong side of ['Mad' Meggan's](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) [siege blockade](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md). The merchant's intense relief and joy at being "rescued" by carnival performers is harvested. He is now another mysterious disappearance officially logged near the circus grounds — and another pressure node Vex has flagged as Guild business when she [taps the party to investigate the carnival](../../../guild/activity/week_2/retaliation-on-the-river.md).
