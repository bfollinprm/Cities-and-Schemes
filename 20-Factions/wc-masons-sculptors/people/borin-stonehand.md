# Master Borin Stonehand

Master of the [Worshipful Company of Masons & Sculptors](../summary.md). The single most powerful figure in the city's construction trade, and one of the wealthiest individuals in the [Peerage of Coin](../../peerage-of-coin/summary.md).

## Manifesto

> When this city fell, who answered the call? Not the politicians, not the bankers. It was the Masons. We raised these walls from the rubble. Every stone in the new Heapside, every pillar in the High Hall, every cobblestone beneath your feet is a testament to our strength. This city does not stand on the whims of Dukes; it stands on the foundations we lay.

## Role

Rose during the Decade of Reconstruction by bidding aggressively, delivering quickly, and never letting a competitor in past the Wardens. The cracked section of new wall near the Basilisk Gate is his — covered up cosmetically rather than rebuilt. His attempt to deflect blame to the Zhentarim has hurt his reputation, and the [Harpers](../../harpers/summary.md) are now actively running a (compromised) agent inside his lodges.
