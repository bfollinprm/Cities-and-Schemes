# Gareth

An out-of-work Baldurian mason currently haunting Rivington taverns. By his own account he was forced to sell his father's prized chisels to make ends meet after the [Chionthar Consortium](../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) hired non-guild labor at half the standard rate on a warehouse-foundation job. His bitterness is real and economic; his voice for it has slid into open nativism, blaming refugees ("beggars") for the squeeze.

## Pressures

Gareth is the kind of laborer the Mason guild and the [Society of Baldurian Integrity](../../society-of-baldurian-integrity/summary.md) both profit from when his anger gets loud. He owns a modest two-story house in a Rivington residential alley, watched over by a quarterstaff-armed neighbor who clocked Bror tailing him in [Session 2](../../../50-Sessions/session_2/player_summary.md).

## Role with the party

Currently the [Guild's](../../guild/summary.md) chosen "trust-building" target for the party — see [take-the-vex-job-shadow-gareth](../../party/activity/week_1/take-the-vex-job-shadow-gareth.md). [Vex](../../guild/people/vex.md) has been clear: silence him, do not kill him, and do not make a scene.
