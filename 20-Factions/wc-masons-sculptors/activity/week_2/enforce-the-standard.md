# Enforce the Standard

[Master Borin Stonehand](../../people/borin-stonehand.md) calls an internal Mason Company meeting over the [Chionthar Consortium's](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) ongoing use of scab labor on Outer City rebuilds. The meeting splits the Company down the middle: senior Masons want to enforce the guild standard and threaten a strike; the rebuild-faction is taking Consortium piecework and refusing. No resolution. The internal divide deepens — and forecloses a Mason reaction to the [week-1 Basilisk Gate sabotage frame collapse](../../../wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_1/publicly-blame-sabotage-for-flaw.md) for at least another week.
