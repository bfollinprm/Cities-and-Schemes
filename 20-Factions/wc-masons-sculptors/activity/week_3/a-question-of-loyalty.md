# A Question of Loyalty

A small ring of dissenting Masons — the same internal faction that [split with Master Stonehand over the Consortium scab labor in week 2](../activity/week_2/enforce-the-standard.md) — plot to leak the Worshipful Company's restricted plans for [Wyrm's Rock](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md) repair-cycle access points to a sympathetic [Harper](../../../harpers/activity/week_3/find-a-new-inlet.md) contact at a Lower City tavern. An informant loyal to [Master Borin Stonehand](../../people/borin-stonehand.md) catches wind of the meeting before it happens; the plans are recovered, the dissenters are identified, and the entire ring is brutally expelled from the guild — two with broken hands, the message clear. The leak fails. The plot only consolidates Stonehand's grip on the Company further and makes him noticeably more paranoid; the Wyrm's Rock plans are now held by fewer hands; and the Harpers, [already burned at Bloomridge Park Manor](../../../harpers/activity/week_3/find-a-new-inlet.md) the same week, lose a second potential entry point.
