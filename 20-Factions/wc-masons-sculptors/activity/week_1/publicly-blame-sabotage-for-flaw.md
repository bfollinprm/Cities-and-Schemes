# Publicly Blame Sabotage for Flaw

After a section of the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) wall collapses on a recent Mason repair, Master [Borin Stonehand](../../people/borin-stonehand.md) tries to launder the failure as [Zhentarim](../../../zhentarim/summary.md) sabotage. [Ettvard Needle](../../../baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) prints the claim in *[Baldur's Mouth](../../../baldurs-mouth/summary.md)* — but frames it as dubious. Without evidence the story dies in a day, and Stonehand's reputation for honesty takes a hit instead of the public funds he was angling for. The collapse becomes a hook the [Unveiled](../../../unveiled/summary.md) and the [Harpers](../../../harpers/summary.md) both reach for.
