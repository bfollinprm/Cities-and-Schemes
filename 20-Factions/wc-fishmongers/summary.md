# Worshipful Company of Fishmongers

One of the city's oldest guilds, predating the patriars' parliaments by centuries. The Fishmongers' power is not glamorous — it is the herring on every working-class table. By controlling the fishing fleets and dockside processing houses of Gray Harbour, they control the primary protein source for tens of thousands of laborers. Led by the no-nonsense [Matron Marga Saltwind](people/marga-saltwind.md).

## Ideology

To preserve their hold on the fishing industry and the loyalty of the dockworkers and sailors who depend on it. They cast themselves as the blood of "old Baldur's Gate" — the guild that was here before the gates and will be here after them.

## Membership

Captains of the city's fishing fleets, owners of the dockside salting and curing houses, and the unionized dockworkers and netmenders who form the largest organized labor force in the city. The Court of Assistants meets at the Saltcellar guildhall on the Gray Harbour quay.

## Methods

- Price-fixing on fish across the city's markets.
- Control over the best berths at Gray Harbour — granted to allied captains, denied to rivals.
- Cooperation with [The Guild](../guild/summary.md) on harbor traffic; their dockworkers are the muscle behind any "labor action."
- Strike and counter-strike. Week 0: a Wharf Rats walkout was preempted when the [Chionthar Consortium](../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) brought in Elturian refugee strikebreakers, collapsing the strike and creating bitter resentment between native dockworkers and refugees.
- Backroom dealmaking. Week 1: Saltwind cut a private deal with the Chionthar Consortium — exemption from the Harbor Dredging Levy in exchange for guaranteed lower fish prices for Consortium mess halls.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** The fanatical **Umberleen Unveiling** sea-cult, whom the guild sees as an existential threat to commercial fishing. Conflict with the [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md) over harbor taxes and customs jurisdiction.

**Allies of convenience.** [The Guild](../guild/summary.md) for muscle on the docks; the [Chionthar Consortium](../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) for the levy-exemption deal.
