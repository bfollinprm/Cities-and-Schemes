# The Herring Gouge

[Matron Marga Saltwind](../../people/marga-saltwind.md) hosts a popular waterfront festival at [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) celebrating "the reopening of the Chionthar" — pegged to the Hellrider protest's collapse rather than the [Free Trader siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md), which is still very much closed. The festival clears the Company's surplus stock at full retail and fixes the Fishmongers in the public eye as the workaday guild "feeding the city" while the larger consortia argue politics. The [Wharf Rats' simultaneous slowdown at the Consortium docks](../../../wharf-rats/activity/week_2/the-sympathy-squeeze.md) keeps the headline clean.
