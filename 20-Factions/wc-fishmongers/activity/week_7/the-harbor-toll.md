# The Harbor Toll

[Matron Marga Saltwind's](../../people/marga-saltwind.md) [armed harbor militia](../../activity/week_6/the-harbor-patrol.md) escalates from petty-thief drowning to **organized extortion**. Every independent fishing skiff arriving at Gray Harbour is now stopped at the breakwater and assessed a "**protection toll**" — paid in fish, in coin, or in indentured fishing labor at the next tide. Skiffs that refuse the toll are *rammed and sunk* on the spot; their crews are pulled half-drowned to the dock and told to find new work.

By the fourth day five small craft are sitting on the harbor floor and the Fishmongers' **independent-fleet count has dropped to zero** in three districts. Every fish reaching the Lower City retail market is now Fishmonger-Guild fish, priced for a starving city. The Wharf Rats — already battling Zhentarim in [the sewer skirmish](../../../wharf-rats/activity/week_7/the-sewer-skirmish.md) — cannot defend their own dockworkers from Saltwind's net-knife crews. **Marga Saltwind is the sole legal authority on Gray Harbour.** The Council has not noticed yet because nobody at the Council eats from a fishmonger's stall.
