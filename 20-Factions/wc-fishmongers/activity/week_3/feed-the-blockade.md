# Feed the Blockade

[Matron Marga Saltwind](../../people/marga-saltwind.md) arranges for daily salted-fish shipments out of [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) to be sold at cost to ['Mad' Meggan's](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) [Free Traders at the Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md). The optics are pure goodwill — bread for the blockade — and Saltwind makes a public point of the Worshipful Company "feeding Baldurians regardless of which side of the gate they sleep on." The economics are excellent. The Company opens a new captive market that pays in coin while the [Sembian Kontor's outreach is refused](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_3/press-the-advantage.md), positions itself as the Outer City's preferred supplier when the siege resolves either way, and makes Marga an unlikely hero to the blockaders who used to scab on her piers. Quiet bonus: the same crews unloading the daily shipments quietly report back on the Free Trader barricade reinforcements going up by the day.
