# The Solidarity Stew

[Matron Marga Saltwind](../../people/marga-saltwind.md), expanding [her cost-price salted-fish runs from week 3](../../../wc-fishmongers/activity/week_3/feed-the-blockade.md), authorizes a public **solidarity stew** kitchen at the Basilisk Gate to feed [Mad Meggan's blockaders](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_4/digging-in.md). The cauldron is up by mid-morning, the line stretches around two stalls by midday, and the goodwill is real. Then a single rival faction agent — most plausibly [Plague-of-Remembrance hands](../../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) testing a softer vector after [the Vhage rot-from-within play](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_4/the-rot-from-within.md) — sabotages a single large cauldron. By dusk dozens of Free Traders, refugees, and curious Eastway gossipers are vomiting in the gutters. The kitchen is shut down within the hour amid public accusations of either incompetence or deliberate harm. Marga's budding alliance with the Free Traders is shattered before it could be formalized; her reputation as the Lower City's reliable fishmonger takes its first real dent.
