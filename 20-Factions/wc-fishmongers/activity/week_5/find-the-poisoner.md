# Find the Poisoner

[Matron Marga Saltwind](../../people/marga-saltwind.md), having seen [her solidarity-stew kitchen for the Free Traders sabotaged into a public food-poisoning scandal last week](../../../wc-fishmongers/activity/week_4/the-solidarity-stew.md), posts a substantial reward at [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) for any *credible* information naming the poisoner. The reward is heard from one end of the docks to the other. Within a day Saltwind's enforcers have a queue of "informants" — every dock-rat and idle apprentice in the harbour — telling stories that contradict each other on every detail. A Fishmongers crew brawls with three innocent Tethyrian sailors over a contradicting tip. A second crew threatens an alewife on a tip that was a deliberate fabrication by a rival fishhouse. By week's end the Fishmongers have spent a great deal of coin and their reputation, again, on chasing nothing. **The actual poisoner — almost certainly [Plague-of-Remembrance work](../../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) — remains untouched.** The guild's solidarity with the Free Traders is now, for working purposes, dead.
