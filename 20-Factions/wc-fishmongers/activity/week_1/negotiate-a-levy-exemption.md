# Negotiate a 'Levy Exemption'

After the previous week's collapsed strike, Matron [Marga Saltwind](../../people/marga-saltwind.md) cuts a backroom deal with the [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md): Fishmonger fleets are exempted from the Harbor Dredging Levy in exchange for guaranteed below-market fish for Consortium mess halls. The deal saves the guild's quarter and undercuts every independent fisher on Gray Harbour. Solidarity with the [Free Traders of the Outskirts](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) burns down to ash.
