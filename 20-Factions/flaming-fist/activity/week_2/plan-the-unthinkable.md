# Plan the Unthinkable

After the party hands the cultist prisoner to ['Mad' Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) at the [Basilisk Gate siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md) instead of delivering him to the [Sea Tower](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/seatower.md) as ordered, [Marshal Valerius Flint](../../people/valerius-flint.md) issues an official warrant for the arrest of [Kaelan of Beregost](../../../party/people/kaelan.md) and his associates on charges of insubordination and aiding sedition. The warrant is real but unenforced — Flint cannot afford to spend Fist on a public manhunt while [refusing the Archduke's order to break the Free Trader siege](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_2/archdukes-mandate.md). The warrant is leverage held over the party rather than a manhunt.
