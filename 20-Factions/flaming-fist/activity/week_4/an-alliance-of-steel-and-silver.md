# An Alliance of Steel and Silver

[Marshal Valerius Flint](../../people/valerius-flint.md), having [taken the Unchained's discreet peacekeeping offer seriously last week](../../../unchained/activity/week_3/an-honorable-contract.md), formally welcomes a contingent of githyanki warriors into [Wyrm's Rock](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md). The handover is staged as a public ceremony at the fortress gate — Flint in dress regalia, [Commander K'laar](../../../unchained/people/k-laar.md) at the head of [a disciplined phalanx that has just marched the length of the city](../../../unchained/activity/week_4/an-honorable-march.md). Astral steel manning the merlons next to red Fist tabards. The crowd that gathers does not cheer. They stare. The sight sends a shockwave of fear and awe across Baldur's Gate: the Marshal has rented in the only soldiers in the city to whom [the Archduke's new mercenaries](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md) cannot give orders. **Wyrm's Rock has changed meaning.** It is no longer the Fist's fortress. It is the *fear-symbol* of the city's new political war.
