# Circle the Wagons

Facing political annihilation after [his refusal to break the siege last week](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_2/contain-the-crises.md), [Marshal Valerius Flint](../../people/valerius-flint.md) recalls his most loyal officers from neighborhood patrol back to [Wyrm's Rock Fortress](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md), effectively ceding parts of the city to non-Fist authorities — primarily the [Watch](../../../watch/activity/week_3/maintain-appearances.md), the [Hellriders](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md), and the criminal landlords of Brampton and Heapside. Blaze [Liara Portyr](../../people/manip-jerrol.md) coordinates the consolidation; [Manip Jerrol](../../people/manip-jerrol.md) is pulled off the [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) beat. Quietly, Flint opens a back-channel to [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) — not to bargain, but to tell him the political siege is now real and the patriar bloc holds the votes that keep him in command. The Fist is preparing for a fight the city's chambers, not its streets, will start.
