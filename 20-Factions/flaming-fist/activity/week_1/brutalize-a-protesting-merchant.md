# Brutalize a Protesting Merchant

A [Free Trader](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) merchant who refuses the [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) "toll" is beaten near-senseless by a Fist patrol led by [Manip Jerrol](../../people/manip-jerrol.md). Dozens of caravan crews and refugees witness the assault. Within hours [High Watcher Alena](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) is using it as the spark for the citizenship protest later in the week, and the [Followers of the Phoenix](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) are folding it into their sermons.
