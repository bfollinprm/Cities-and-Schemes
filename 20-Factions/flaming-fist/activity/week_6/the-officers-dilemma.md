# The Officer's Dilemma

The "Silver Fist" bribes [planted in the Elfsong last week](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_5/the-silver-fist.md) and [reinforced over wine at Durinbold Manor this week](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_6/the-officers-mess.md) take root in the Lower City Fist barracks. When [Marshal Valerius Flint](../../people/valerius-flint.md) orders a coordinated crackdown on smuggling activity in [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) and [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) — meant to disrupt [Wharf Rat](../../../wharf-rats/summary.md) and [River-Rat](../../../river-rats/summary.md) operations and reassert Fist control — several key officers (those paid by [the Patriar Heritage Society](../../../patriar-heritage-society/summary.md)) deliberately delay their response, "misinterpret" timing on the orders, and patrol the wrong streets.

The Fist's smuggling crackdown collapses before it begins. Flint receives a barracks-room report that reads as competent but produces no arrests, no contraband, and three sergeant-level signed timesheets that all happen to align with Durinbold's silver. The Fist's ability to police the Lower City — already shredded by [the Watch–Fist tension](../../../watch/activity/week_6/the-gate-lockdown.md) and [the integration drills with K'liir](../../activity/week_5/the-integration-drills.md) — is now visibly *paralyzed* by its own internal corruption. Flint suspects the rot but cannot prove it, and the patriars have given him a Marshal's headache that will not go away with one purge.
