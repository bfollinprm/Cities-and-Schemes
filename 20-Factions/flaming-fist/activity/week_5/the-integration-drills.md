# The Integration Drills

[Marshal Valerius Flint](../../people/valerius-flint.md), having [formally welcomed Commander K'laar's phalanx into Wyrm's Rock last week](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_4/an-alliance-of-steel-and-silver.md), orders joint drills on the parade ground inside [Wyrm's Rock Fortress](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md) — Fist regulars on one side, [K'laar's githyanki](../../../unchained/people/k-laar.md) on the other, working through line drill, shield-wall transition, gate defense, sewer counter-incursion. The drills are intended to build cohesion. They build the opposite. The githyanki run a perfect synchronized formation in seven languages of psionic battle-cant; the Fist regulars run a tolerable Baldurian one and notice. **The skill gap is humiliating.** Sergeants who have served twenty years find themselves outmatched by a phalanx that does not need to talk to coordinate. Resentment ferments fast in the barracks ale-tents — exactly the soil [Lord Durinbold's silver](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_5/the-silver-fist.md) is being paid into the same week. By Tendays-end the rank-and-file of the Flaming Fist are simultaneously feeling outclassed by their new allies and being paid by the old guard to remember it.
