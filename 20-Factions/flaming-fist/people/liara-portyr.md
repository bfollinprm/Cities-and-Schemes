# Blaze Liara Portyr

A senior officer of the [Flaming Fist](../summary.md), holding the rank of **Blaze** — the tier directly below [Marshal Valerius Flint](valerius-flint.md). Younger than the Marshal, sharper at politics, and openly running for his job.

## Role

Portyr is the named example in the dossier of an "ambitious officer" working the Fist's internal politics during Flint's managed retreat to [Wyrm's Rock](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md). The recall of loyalists to the fortress thinned other [Upper City](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md) beats and forced [the Watch](../../watch/summary.md) to cover gaps; Portyr's faction sees that thinning as an opening, not a problem.

She is positioned to either **succeed** the Marshal cleanly through retirement and council favor, or **supplant** him if the Wyrm's Crossing crisis crests into a public-relations failure he cannot survive — the [Manip Jerrol](manip-jerrol.md) blockade fallout being a candidate trigger.
