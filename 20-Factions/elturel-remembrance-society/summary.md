# Elturel Remembrance Society

A nominal support group for Elturian survivors, in fact an echo chamber for Lord Perrine Hhune's conviction that the Fall of Elturel was a *successful* mass-sacrificial ritual orchestrated by Baldurian patriars to transmute divine energy into infernal power. Founded and led by [Lord Perrine Hhune, the Damnation-Seeker](people/perrine-hhune.md). One of the four Conspiracist cells in the city.

## Ideology

On the surface: cultural preservation and trauma support for Elturian refugees. Underneath: every Baldurian merchant who profited from the post-crisis boom is suspect; every contract may be a devil's contract; every piece of "infernal geometry" in the rebuilt districts is evidence of Asmodean conversion.

Perrine's specific theory: the [Knights of the Shield](../knights-of-the-shield/summary.md) inner circle, in league with devil-worshipping patriar families, orchestrated the Fall of Elturel as a transmutation ritual. The reconstruction of Baldur's Gate is funded by soul-forged hell-gold; the city is being converted brick by brick into a new infernal bastion.

## Membership

Almost entirely Elturian refugees, particularly New Elturel residents who harbor deep hatred for the Baldurian establishment. Bound by shared trauma and a thirst for righteous vengeance. The Society's salons function as both grief support and conspiracy reinforcement.

## Methods

- "Purity tests" on goods and merchants, seeking traces of infernalism.
- Sabotage of businesses believed to be funded by hell-gold.
- "Liberation" of artifacts thought to be part of the grand infernal bargain.
- Public denunciation. Week 0: a protest against a "demonstrably infernal" Baldurian merchant collapsed when the targeted (popular) merchant was defended by his neighbors and the local Guild Ward Boss dispersed the protesters as an annoyance — branding the Society as ineffective zealots.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** The patriar families of the [Peerage of Blood](../peerage-of-blood/summary.md), particularly any in or near the [Knights of the Shield](../knights-of-the-shield/summary.md) — the conspiracy's supposed beneficiaries. The [Western Gate](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md), [Chionthar](../chionthar-consortium/summary.md), and Foreign Kontors as the new infernal-laundering merchant class. Suspicious even of the [Hellriders of New Elturel](../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) leadership for compromising with the "devils" of the Council of Four.
