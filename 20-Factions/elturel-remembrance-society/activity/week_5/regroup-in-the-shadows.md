# Regroup in the Shadows

[Lord Perrine Hhune](../../people/perrine-hhune.md), a fugitive after [the Jannath Estate "infernal heist" was wiped out by the estate's own wards last week](../../../elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_4/the-infernal-heist.md), establishes a new clandestine base of operations in a forgotten cellar beneath [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) — a sub-basement under the floor-stones of an abandoned Elturian survivor's almshouse, accessible only through a coal-chute. From it Hhune sends out coded messages, runner by runner, to the scattered Society remnants: a stitched cloth left at a particular Phoenix Gate corner; a chalk-mark on a Wyrm's Crossing pillar. The messages call the survivors to him. Within four nights nine of the original Society are crouched around a single table in the cellar, naming what they intend to do next. Hhune's eyes are bright in lamplight. *His failure has only sharpened him.* He no longer believes the Knights of the Shield architected the Fall alone — he believes the [Council of Four](../../../council-of-four/summary.md) and the [new mercenary deployment](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md) are the Material-Plane operatives of the same project, and his next strike will not be a heist — it will be martyrdom on a Council member.
