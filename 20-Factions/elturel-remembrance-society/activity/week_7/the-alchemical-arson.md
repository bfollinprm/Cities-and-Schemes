# The Alchemical Arson

[Lord Perrine Hhune's](../../people/perrine-hhune.md) agents act on [last week's marked-door target list](../../activity/week_6/the-marked-doors.md). On the third night of the new tenday they slip three teams into the Upper City Commercial District through Heapside service-tunnels and **ignite the alchemical sigil-ink** on six storefronts they believe were funded with Knights-of-the-Shield infernal gold.

Two storefronts go up — a Mercer milliner and a Goldsmith subsidiary's loan office. The other four sigils fail to ignite (the Watch had quietly washed three of them under [the new gate-lockdown patrols](../../../watch/activity/week_6/the-gate-lockdown.md); the fourth was a forged sigil planted by [the Bibliophile's correction-counterfeits](../../../bibliophile/activity/week_6/the-correction.md)). The fires burn out before dawn but the message lands: the Remembrance Society can reach into the Upper City *during* the lockdown. Patriars in [Manorborn](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/manorborn.md) hire their own torch-watchers — an **eighth private army** in a city already overflowing with them.
