# The Infernal Heist

[Lord Perrine Hhune](../../people/perrine-hhune.md), still smarting from [the diabolist patriar's public dismissal of his patronage bid last week](../../../elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_3/seek-a-new-patron.md), authorizes his amateur followers to break into the Jannath Estate in [Manorborn](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/manorborn.md) and recover what he believes are infernal artifacts hidden in the family vaults. The heist is a complete failure. The Jannath wards detect the breach inside thirty seconds; the estate's professional guards corner the followers in the wine cellar. Perrine, watching from a brougham across the lane, is forced to abandon his people and flee. The followers are bound and turned over to [the Watch](../../../watch/summary.md). Perrine's plot — and his name — are now in a sworn statement on the Watch desk in [the Wide](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/the-wide.md). The Society's Manorborn footprint is *gone* and the diabolist patriar Perrine wanted to impress is publicly amused.
