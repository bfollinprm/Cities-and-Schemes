# Seek a New Patron

After [last week's public arrest at the warehouse](../week_2/infernal-residue.md) and the Society's collapsed credibility, [Lord Perrine Hhune](../../people/perrine-hhune.md) attempts to rebuild upward. He arranges a private meeting with a discreetly diabolist patriar at a Manorborn estate in the [Upper City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/manorborn.md), pitching the Society as a serious infernal partner ready to escalate beyond pamphlets. The meeting is a humiliation. The patriar — uninterested in fanatics he cannot control, quietly courted by the [Knights of the Shield](../../../knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_3/a-friend-in-the-coin.md) anyway — dismisses Perrine as a delusional amateur. Perrine is shown out without ceremony. He returns to [the Society](../../summary.md) more isolated than ever, with no access to the infernal power he wants and a fresh anecdote to give the [Bibliophile](../../../bibliophile/activity/week_3/the-price-of-treason.md) the next time she comes asking what he knows.
