# The Marked Doors

[Lord Perrine Hhune's](../../people/perrine-hhune.md) agents — Elturian-survivor radicals, Hellrider deserters, two Heritage-Society-trained nightlings — move through the [Upper City commercial district](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/the-wide.md) at night, painting **the symbol of Zariel** in *invisible alchemical ink* on the doors of businesses they believe are funded by "Hell-gold" — Lower-City-Kontor capital, Sembian wartime profit, and (incorrectly) one Mercer storefront whose owner once dated an Elturian. The marks are visible only to anyone using *detect magic* or under specific aurora-tinted lamp angles.

The marking exercise is a **target-list publication**. The Society's members and aligned saboteurs can now read which Upper City businesses to attack later — fire, vandalism, theft, or Sharran-style smear-campaigning, depending on what the Society can afford that month. The marks are physically there for hours; the public never knows. Lord Hhune is alive and unmolested in the [hidden New Elturel cellar](../../activity/week_5/regroup-in-the-shadows.md), drafting the next round of targeting. The Society has finished pivoting from a grief-circle into a coordinated nightlings-and-marks shadow operation. **This is the longest planning horizon any cult in the city is currently working on.**
