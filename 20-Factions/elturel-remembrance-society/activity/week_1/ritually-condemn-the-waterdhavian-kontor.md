# Ritually Condemn the Waterdhavian Kontor

[Lord Perrine Hhune](../../people/perrine-hhune.md) stages a public ritual condemnation of the [Waterdhavian Kontor](../../../waterdhavian-kontor/summary.md) for translating the *[Tome of the Companion](../../../waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_1/translate-the-tome-of-the-companion.md)* — a ceremony meant to read as righteous and read instead as pathetic. The Kontor's Griffin Guard disperses the small protest cleanly; the city press does not bother to cover it. The Society loses members afterward, several to the more open arms of the [Followers of the Phoenix](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md).
