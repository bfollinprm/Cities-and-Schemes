# Infernal Residue

[Lord Perrine Hhune's](../../people/perrine-hhune.md) Society agents try to break into [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) warehouses on the Lower City docks looking for evidence of "infernal residue" — proof that the Consortium's wealth is laundered hellfire gold. They are caught and very publicly arrested. The Consortium denounces them as paranoid fanatics; the *[Baldur's Mouth](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_2/fan-the-flames.md)* runs the arrest splash. The Society's reputation craters. Hhune slips back to the New Elturel cellar he is using as a safehouse and his focus shifts toward the alchemical-ink "Zariel marks" and the abandoned cult lab beneath the [Weeping Wound](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) instead.
