# Lord Perrine Hhune, the Damnation-Seeker

Founder and leader of the [Elturel Remembrance Society](../summary.md). A zealous Elturian refugee and the last scion of a disgraced noble house. Oath of Conspiracy paladin sworn to expose the "devils in silks" who he believes sacrificed Elturel for Baldurian profit.

## Conspiracy theory

Perrine believes the Archdevil Zariel was tricked. The true culprits were a secret inner circle of the [Knights of the Shield](../../knights-of-the-shield/summary.md), in league with devil-worshipping patriar families, who orchestrated the Fall of Elturel to "transmute" divine energy into infernal power. The reconstruction of Baldur's Gate is funded by soul-forged gold from the Nine Hells; the new Consortiums and Kontors are merely fronts laundering damned wealth. Karlach and Wyll's descent into Avernus wasn't heroic — they were collected as part of the deal.

## Backstory

Perrine was a child in Elturel when it was dragged into Hell. He remembers the burning sky and the brimstone — and figures on a distant hill performing a ritual *as the chains fell*. Brought to the refugee camps, he saw Baldurian merchants preying on the survivors and connected the ritual he had seen to the city's miraculous boom. His oath was sworn to the "Truth Beneath the Brimstone," to expose the conspiracy and recover his home.
