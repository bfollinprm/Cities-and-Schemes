# The Hidden Lord

The unnamed apex of the [Knights of the Shield](../summary.md) inside Baldur's Gate — recognized by other Knights only by an octagonal silver-and-onyx ring and a forged Watch contract on his House Defense Subscriptions. His true identity is held by no more than three patriars, one of whom is dead and one of whom is presumed missing. *Nobody at the Council knows who he is*; nobody at Parliament has ever heard his voice.

The Hidden Lord operates exclusively through cut-outs: [The Recruiter](the-recruiter.md) handles fresh patriar pacts at the Manorborn end of the trade; mid-tier solicitors notarize the contracts at [the Helm and Cloak](../../silent-shroud/activity/week_7/the-helm-and-cloak-infiltration.md); the Counting House quarterly billing is settled in **soul-coins**, an off-ledger infernal currency the Knights mint themselves and reconcile with their patron once per moon. He has not been seen in person at the Manorborn end of any contract in three years, and may not be physically present in the city at all.

## Activity

Twelve patriar bloodlines in lien by week's end.
