# A Bargain in the Barracks

[The Hidden Lord](../../people/the-recruiter.md) personally approaches [Marshal Valerius Flint](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) at the [Basilisk Gate Barracks](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/seatower.md) with a clandestine offer of Knight backing for Flint's [public defiance of the Archduke](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_2/archdukes-mandate.md). Flint, no closer to dealing with diabolists than he was last week, refuses the offer flat. The Knights pivot, looking instead to corruptible mid-tier officers and consul aides for their next purchase.
