# Swear in Lord Hlath

The Knights' [Recruiter](../../people/the-recruiter.md), already turned away by [Lord Hlath](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/lord-hlath.md) once during week 0, brings him to a clandestine meeting in the [Upper City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md) for the actual swearing. Hlath balks at the literal infernal phrasing of the oath, panics, and flees the meeting. He is now too compromised to deny the contact happened and too cowardly to follow through on it — leverage in both directions for whoever finds out next.
