# The Gilded Extortion

With [the Watch's Upper City gate lockdown](../../../watch/activity/week_6/the-gate-lockdown.md) entering its second week and Lower City mob rumors reaching every Manorborn parlor, **the Hidden Lord** of the Knights of the Shield works the panic. His agents call on trapped patriar households one at a time, offering "guaranteed" infernal protection — a Knight-of-the-Shield warding pact that *demonstrably* keeps the Lower City out — for an exorbitant fee in **soul-coins**, settled in the Knights' own off-ledger currency.

Three minor patriar houses sign in the first three days. The price is hidden inside a "House Defense Subscription" the Knights bill quarterly to the Counting House under a forged Watch contract. *The Knights are converting Upper City fear into infernal credit.* Inside seven days the Hidden Lord holds liens on a dozen patriar bloodlines — fresh fuel for the [Infernal Ledger he opened last tenday](../../activity/week_6/the-infernal-ledger.md). [The Hellscream Bathhouse Brawl](../../../parliament-of-peers/activity/week_7/the-bathhouse-brawl.md) is going to make this market still richer.
