# A Desperate Bargain

[The Hidden Lord](../../people/the-recruiter.md), needing a recovery target after [the Brampton Ward Boss reported him to Vex last week](../../../knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_4/a-deal-in-the-gutters.md), shifts the angle from the Outer City back to the Upper. He identifies a Peerage-of-Coin patriar in [Manorborn](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/manorborn.md) — one of the [Watch-burglary victims of last week](../../../watch/activity/week_4/the-manorborn-burglaries.md) whose stolen private debt schedules ended up in [an unsigned pamphlet](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_5/the-propaganda-campaign.md), and whose estate is undefended under [the Watch's collapsed patrol coverage](../../../watch/activity/week_5/the-plea-for-reinforcements.md). The Hidden Lord arrives by carriage, alone, after dusk. The pitch is extreme: *signed infernal protection of the estate, a hellish ward against further burglary and slander, in exchange for the patriar's permanent loyalty and the Coin-bloc's whip-list*. The patriar is terrified, drunk, and recently widowed by [a Bhaalist-rumor murder in his own bedchamber](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_4/an-offering-in-the-dark.md). He signs in red wax. **The Knights of the Shield have just bought their first Manorborn patron of the new political war.** The pact is private; the patriar's loyalty is not.
