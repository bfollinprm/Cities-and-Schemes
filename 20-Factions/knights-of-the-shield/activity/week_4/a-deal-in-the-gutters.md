# A Deal in the Gutters

[The Hidden Lord](../../people/the-recruiter.md), pivoting after [last week's failure to recruit a junior Chionthar member](../../../knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_3/a-friend-in-the-coin.md), tries the Outer City instead: an after-dark meeting at the Salt-Cellar in [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) with the local Guild Ward Boss. The pitch is the usual — infernal patronage, soft money, useful curses — in exchange for letting Knights operatives work the district. The Ward Boss is more cautious than corrupt. The infernal nature of the offer spooks him. He smiles, drinks the Hidden Lord's wine, walks out, and reports the meeting up to [Vex](../../../guild/people/vex.md) before sunrise. The Guild is now on high alert for diabolist incursion in the Outer City — and the Knights' anonymity, which has so far been their only real defense, has cracked open.
