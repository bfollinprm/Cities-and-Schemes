# A Friend in the Coin

The Hidden Lord, working through the Knights' patrician contacts, attempts to recruit a junior member of [the Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) over a private dinner at [The Blushing Mermaid](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md). The pitch — discreet infernal patronage in exchange for board influence at the Counting House — backfires hard. The junior consortium member is terrified by the diabolist overture and reports the contact in full to his superiors at [Factor Errinthal](../../../chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md). The Consortium is now on high alert for infernal influence within its own ranks; internal security goes up, prospective new members are vetted for occult ties, and the Knights' decade-long project of seating a sympathetic Consortium voice fails publicly inside the Consortium even if not yet on the street. The order's nearest path back into Lower City finance has just narrowed sharply.
