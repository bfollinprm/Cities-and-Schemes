# Reinforce Brampton's Loyalty

After the [Zhentarim's](../../../zhentarim/summary.md) week-0 arson and the new "[Velvet Hand](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_1/establish-the-velvet-hand-social-club.md)" gambling den going up across the ashes, [Nine-Fingers Keene](../../people/astele-keene.md) sends her enforcer **Tusgront** to walk every shop in [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) door to door. He starts no fights. He stands quietly, asks after the family, reminds the proprietor of how generous Guild protection has been, and suggests it would be "unwise" to associate with newcomers. Brampton's shopkeepers do the math.
