# Retaliation on the River

[Nine-Fingers Keene](../../people/astele-keene.md) reads the party as more liability than asset after the [Mad Meggan deal](../../../party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md): they kept the [Whispering Stone](../../../party/people/bror.md), they lied about the Circus assignment, and they walked away with 6,000 gp the Guild did not bank. Keene authorizes [Vex](../../people/vex.md) to retrieve the **Whispering Stone** by any means necessary — formally ending the temporary alliance — and pivots her to [One-Eyed Jack](../../../wharf-rats/people/one-eyed-jack.md) at [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) to re-coordinate dock leverage. Vex's revenue stream from the party (the offered Zora-illusion-breaking spell scroll for **6,000 gp**) is left on the table as a dominance test the party cannot afford.
