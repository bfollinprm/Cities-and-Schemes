# Retaliation

[Nine-Fingers Keene](../../people/astele-keene.md), under pressure from [the Brampton Boys ambush going public last week](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_3/an-offer-you-cant-refuse.md) and the visible appearance of Zhent muscle on Consortium docks, authorizes a public reprisal. [Vex](../../people/vex.md) leads a brutal raid on **the Velvet Hand**, [Roah Moonglow's](../../../zhentarim/people/roah-moonglow.md) Brampton gambling den. The crew goes in at first hour after sunset, breaks the door, breaks the bouncers, breaks the high-stakes table, drags the night's takings into the street, and walks out without setting the building on fire. The point is not destruction. The point is the spectacle: Brampton sees the Guild reassert its claim with steel in broad streetlight. Two Zhent dealers are killed; the den's ledger walks out with Vex. The Black Network's east-end footprint takes a public, bloody step backward. Roah's reply is already in motion — [the Smokepowder Plot](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_4/the-smokepowder-plot.md), which fails for separate reasons before it can be deployed.
