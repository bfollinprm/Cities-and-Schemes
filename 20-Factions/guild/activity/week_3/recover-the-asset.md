# Recover the Asset

After the party's [late-week-2 break with Vex](../week_2/retaliation-on-the-river.md), Nine-Fingers Keene authorizes [Vex Corvin](../../people/vex.md) to retrieve the Whispering Stone from them by any means necessary, and Vex puts a retrieval team on [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) — eyes at the Weary Plowman, eyes at the [Circus of the Last Days encampment](../../../carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_3/sowing-sweet-sorrows.md), eyes on Bror specifically. The trail goes completely cold. The party disappears into the Circus mid-week and does not come out. For two weeks (in-city time) there is no sign of them: not at the Weary Plowman, not at the Open Hand Temple, not at any of the Lower City fences the Guild watches. Vex, conservative with crew, recalls her agents and concludes the party has either fled the city or met a mysterious end. The Whispering Stone is now logged as **lost** in the Guild's books. The same retrieval team also intercepts [a Bhaalist murder attempt](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_3/consecrate-the-blockade-with-blood.md) on Eastway and bloodies a [Zhentarim recruiter in Brampton](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_3/an-offer-you-cant-refuse.md) — a profitable week despite the failure on the artifact.
