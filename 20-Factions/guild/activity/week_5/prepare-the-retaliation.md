# Prepare the Retaliation

[Nine-Fingers Keene](../../people/astele-keene.md), sitting on [the smokepowder seized from Roah Moonglow's foiled plot last week](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_4/the-smokepowder-plot.md), decides to use the Black Network's own weapon back on it. [Vex Corvin](../../people/vex.md) is tasked with rigging the main sewer artery beneath the **Velvet Hand** in [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) — directly under Roah's gambling-den base — with the captured charges. Place the casks at the load-bearing junction; collapse the floor under the parlour from below; bury Roah's east-end footprint in its own cellar. The party — having returned from the Feywild and walked back into a Guild contract — handles the actual sewer descent. *They run into [Silas the Eel and the River-Rats moving the smokepowder shipment in the same tunnel](../../../river-rats/activity/week_5/the-smokepowder-run.md)* and the encounter goes badly for the Rats: [Vex Corvin is killed in the firefight](../../../river-rats/activity/week_5/the-smokepowder-run.md), Silas escapes, and the party intercepts the shipment for the Guild before it can reach the safe house. Charges are placed. The detonation order has not yet been given. **The Guild now controls a working smokepowder counter-attack on the Velvet Hand** — and Keene has lost her own enforcer Vex in the same operation.
