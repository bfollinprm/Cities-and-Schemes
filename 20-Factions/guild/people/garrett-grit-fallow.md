# Garrett "Grit" Fallow

A Tumbledown gravedigger on the Gravekeepers' rolls, *also* on the Guild's: Grit holds a discreet retainer from [Nine-Fingers Keene](astele-keene.md) to keep the [Guild's interests in the Tumbledown crypts](../../gravekeepers/activity/week_7/the-cliffside-consecration.md) walked, listened to, and quietly reported back. He has not been compromised by the Gravekeepers' senior leadership; [Old Man Hemlock](../../gravekeepers/people/hemlock.md) does not know.

Grit appears mid-fifties, broad in the shoulder from a lifetime of digging, missing the upper joint of a right ring-finger from a careless headstone. He recognizes the **Guild coin** (a stamped half-disc, blunted on the edge) on sight and accepts it as a free pass into Tumbledown's interior crypt-yards without asking who is calling on his employer's hospitality. Off-duty he drinks at a small Sow's Foot taproom with a single mug and a single clay pipe, listens more than he talks, and remembers everything.

## Encountered

- **Session 28** — Tumbledown. The party arrives with a Guild coin from [Master Phineas Droog](../../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/people/phineas-droog.md) and a name. They are about to make contact with Grit when they spot a five-strong patrol of Gravekeepers nearby.
