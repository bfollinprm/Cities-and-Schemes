# Belvin "Soot" Hamhock

Logistics chief of [Vex](vex.md)'s former inner circle in [Rivington](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). Runs the ward's supply, smuggling routes, and warehouse traffic — including, until recently, the [Saffron pipeline that Farlin was skimming](farlin.md).

## With the party

Identified in [Session 20](../../../50-Sessions/session_20/player_summary.md) as one of three "fixers" who must be brought to heel before [Finneas's installation as Guild Boss of Rivington](../../party/activity/week_5/install-finneas-as-guild-boss-of-rivington.md) holds. Belvin is the operationally critical one — the others can be replaced; the warehouse logbooks, dock contacts, and sewer-route knowledge live in his head. Doubling the [Free Trader protection rates](../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_5/heed-the-ultimatum.md) under Mad Meggan's offer is the lever Finneas will pull first.
