# Runa "The Hook"

Enforcement chief of [Vex](vex.md)'s former inner circle in [Rivington](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). Holds the ward's muscle — door-shakedown crews, dock retributions, the lessons taught with a tin-handled hook.

## With the party

Identified in [Session 20](../../../50-Sessions/session_20/player_summary.md) as one of three "fixers" who must be brought to heel before [Finneas's installation as Guild Boss of Rivington](../../party/activity/week_5/install-finneas-as-guild-boss-of-rivington.md) holds. Of the three, Runa is the easiest to win on a transactional basis — Finneas's "active mercy" Plowman office hours generate work for her crew at a rate Vex never matched — and the hardest to keep from sliding into independence the moment her income exceeds her loyalty.
