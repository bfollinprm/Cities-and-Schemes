# Glass Malakor

Intelligence chief of [Vex](vex.md)'s former inner circle in [Rivington](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). Runs the ward's information apparatus — informants in the markets, dock-side payouts, the kept ears at the Plowman tap.

## With the party

Identified in [Session 20](../../../50-Sessions/session_20/player_summary.md) as one of three "fixers" who must be brought to heel before [Finneas's installation as Guild Boss of Rivington](../../party/activity/week_5/install-finneas-as-guild-boss-of-rivington.md) holds. Glass has a price; Finneas does not yet know what it is. Until that conversation, Glass is the most dangerous of the three because he can route the news of Vex's death up the chain whenever he chooses.
