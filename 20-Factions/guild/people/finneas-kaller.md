# Finneas Kaller

Long-time travelling companion of [the party](../../party/summary.md), now installed as the de-facto Guild Boss of [Rivington](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), with [Simbar](simbar.md) as the public puppet "Guildmaster." Soft-spoken, mercy-first, the kind of negotiator who pays for a round before he asks for anything.

## With the party

Finneas crossed paths with the four founders at the [Coast Way Zhentarim toll](../../party/activity/week_1/pay-the-zhentarim-toll-on-the-coast-way.md) in [Session 1](../../../50-Sessions/session_1/player_summary.md), opening with mercy-pleas while Spark counted the travelers. Settled at 18 gp; the grateful caravans reimbursed him 38 gp on the far side. Same week he and Spark talked the Fist sergeant down at [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) into the [single-medicine-wagon corridor](../../party/activity/week_1/broker-the-medicine-corridor-at-wyrms-crossing.md) that broke the [protest cordon](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_1/organize-a-protest-at-wyrms-crossing.md). He has been a fixture of the party's working group since.

## Guild Boss of Rivington

[Installed by the party in week 5](../../party/activity/week_5/install-finneas-as-guild-boss-of-rivington.md) (Sessions 20–21) after [the killing of Vex](../../party/activity/week_5/kill-vex-and-loot-her-office.md) — the survival pitch they took to [Nine-Fingers Keene](astele-keene.md) over Vex's body. **Simbar** wears the title; Finneas runs the ward. Pitch to the locals: *active mercy, not extortion.* He opens "office hours" in the back room of the [Weary Plowman](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md); [Lena](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/people/lena.md) pours tea; anyone with a grievance walks out with his word that he will look into it. [Mad Meggan](../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) doubled the Free Traders' "protection" payments to seed the operation.

He absorbed Vex's former inner circle — [Glass Malakor](glass-malakor.md), [Runa "The Hook"](runa-the-hook.md), and [Belvin "Soot" Hamhock](belvin-soot-hamhock.md) — onto his ledger, each on their own conditional terms. By [week 6's brutal internal audit](../activity/week_6/the-financial-audit.md), his are the only ward-boss books in the Guild that come back clean.

## Standing with the party

Listed in [`leverage.md`](../../party/leverage.md) as an **ally on call**: he runs Rivington Guild ops the party can draw on; the standing cost is staying useful to him. [Nine-Fingers Keene's verdict on the Vex killing](../../../60-Threads/nine-fingers-vex-verdict.md) is still pending, and Finneas's installation is the load-bearing argument keeping that verdict from coming due.
