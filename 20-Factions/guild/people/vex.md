# Vex

A composed, steely-eyed Lieutenant of [The Guild](../summary.md) with a visible Guild tattoo. Vex worked the Rivington taverns — the [Weary Plowman](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/people/lena.md) among them — as a mid-tier intake officer for new freelancers. She did not introduce herself to walk-ins; people who mattered noticed her, and she nodded.

## Role with the party

In [Session 2](../../../50-Sessions/session_2/player_summary.md) Vex acknowledges the Coast Way handling, watches Bror and Kaelan flirt around the topic, and offers a "trust-building" job: silence [Gareth](../../wc-masons-sculptors/people/gareth.md), the loud-mouth nativist mason — intimidation or persuasion, not killing, and quiet.

## Death

In [Session 15](../../../50-Sessions/session_15/player_summary.md), Vex lures the party from the Blushing Mermaid into a [Gray Harbour](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) warehouse and springs a [pre-planned ambush](../../party/activity/week_5/kill-vex-and-loot-her-office.md) — two door-guards, four hidden archers, and a demand for the Whispering Stone. She had been planning to take the artifact and report the party as "unreliable" to Nine-Fingers (an unsent betrayal note in a mechanical bird tube confirms the intent). The fight ends with Vex dead — Finn lands the killing blow — and her office stripped: her Rivington appointment book, the [Velvet Hand smokepowder map](../activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md), stolen Consortium intel on [Errinthal's print campaign](../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md), and **kompromat on [Farlin](farlin.md)**. Her body is hidden in saffron crates in the warehouse; one archer, [Simbar](simbar.md), escapes to be captured later that night.

Her death leaves Rivington's Guild operations open. The party's [Rivington Gambit](../../party/activity/week_5/install-finneas-as-guild-boss-of-rivington.md) plays into that vacuum.
