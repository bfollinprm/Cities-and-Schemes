# Vex

A composed, steely-eyed operative of [The Guild](../summary.md) with a visible Guild tattoo. Vex works the Rivington taverns — the [Weary Plowman](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/people/lena.md) among them — as a mid-tier intake officer for new freelancers. She does not introduce herself to walk-ins; people who matter notice her, and she nods.

## Role with the party

In [Session 2](../../../50-Sessions/session_2/player_summary.md) Vex acknowledges the Coast Way handling, watches Bror and Kaelan flirt around the topic, and offers a "trust-building" job: silence [Gareth](../../wc-masons-sculptors/people/gareth.md), the loud-mouth nativist mason — intimidation or persuasion, not killing, and quiet. Success opens a real conversation about real Guild work. The job is taken (see [take-the-vex-job-shadow-gareth](../../party/activity/week_1/take-the-vex-job-shadow-gareth.md)) and not yet completed.
