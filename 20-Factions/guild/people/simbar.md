# Simbar

A junior [Guild](../summary.md) archer attached to [Vex](vex.md)'s warehouse-trap detail in [Gray Harbour](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md). The only one of her hidden archers to escape the [warehouse fight](../../party/activity/week_5/kill-vex-and-loot-her-office.md) alive.

## Capture

Simbar fled the warehouse through the sewers in [Session 15](../../../50-Sessions/session_15/player_summary.md), making for **[Farlin](farlin.md)** rather than [Nine-Fingers Keene](astele-keene.md) — Farlin and Vex were rivals, and Simbar wanted protection from a Guild lieutenant who would not blame him for Vex's death. Kaelan ran him down in a flooded service tunnel and dragged him back to the warehouse.

## The puppet

In [Session 16](../../../50-Sessions/session_16/player_summary.md), the party brings Simbar with their full intel cache to [Mad Meggan](../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) and [High Watcher Alena](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) at the [Basilisk Gate](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) camp. Offered the choice between dying in the warehouse and *thriving* as a face-saving placeholder, Simbar agrees on the spot to be installed as the puppet **"Guildmaster of Rivington"** while Finneas runs the actual ward. He is rope-thin, frightened, and quick-eyed — the right choice of figurehead, because no one inside the Guild expects him to do anything but nod.
