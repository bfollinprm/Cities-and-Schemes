# Farlin

[Guild](../summary.md) Ward Boss for the [Eastway](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) district. Vex's rival in life; the party's coerced asset after her death.

## Profile

Farlin runs Eastway the way most middle-managers run a difficult ward — quietly, with hands deeper in the till than his bosses know. **Skim from the [Amnian Kontor](../../amnian-kontor/summary.md) Saffron shipments** has been ongoing for some time, with the loss covered up by misrouted manifests and a bribed clerk. He has also been **covering for [Silas the Eel](../../river-rats/people/silas-the-eel.md)** — Silas lost a major package for "N.F." (Nine-Fingers) at the Basilisk Gate, and Farlin's protection kept Silas off the Guild's books for the failure. Both facts were sitting in [kompromat Vex was assembling against him](../../party/activity/week_5/kill-vex-and-loot-her-office.md) when she died.

## With the party

The party finds the kompromat in Vex's office in [Session 15](../../../50-Sessions/session_15/player_summary.md). In [Session 17](../../../50-Sessions/session_17/player_summary.md) they confront him in Eastway — Bror intimidates, Kaelan reasons, the Saffron evidence finishes it. **Farlin agrees to plant the [smokepowder mines](../activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md) under the Sembian advance routes through Eastway.** The mines go in under the [Hellrider–Free Trader](../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) operational plan; Farlin executes them as the party's forced asset.

He is not yet broken — he runs the ward, takes the cuts, and reports up — but his loyalty is now to whichever party holds the Saffron evidence longest.
