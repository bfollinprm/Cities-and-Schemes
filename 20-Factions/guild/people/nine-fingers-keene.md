# Guildmaster Astele "Nine-Fingers" Keene

Guildmaster of [The Guild](../summary.md). Pragmatist, populist, and one of the three or four genuinely indispensable figures in post-crisis Baldur's Gate.

## Manifesto

> The Lords and Dukes in the High Hall see a city of gold. They see ledgers and profits. They don't see you. They don't see the rent, the sickness, the hunger. We see you. The Guild is the government you actually have. We keep the peace. We find you work. We put bread on your table when the state offers only laws and walls.

## Role

After the Absolute Crisis, with the official government paralyzed and the Flaming Fist gutted, Keene transformed her organization from a thieves' guild into a parallel state — providing protection, social services, and informal justice in the Lower and Outer Cities. The Ward Boss System is her invention.

She runs the Guild as a pyramid: herself, Sachems by district, Ward Bosses by neighborhood, and an army of informants. Operatives like Vex execute specialized contracts. She has demonstrated repeated willingness to burn her own crews when they become liabilities — the Brampton smokepowder plot was a Keene cleanup, intended to remove the Zhentarim threat *and* the failing [River-Rats](../../river-rats/summary.md) crew at once.

She authorized Vex to take the *Whispering Stone* from the party "by any means necessary" after their three-week absence.
