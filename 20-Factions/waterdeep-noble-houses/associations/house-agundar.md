# House Agundar

**Tier:** Court (Great Game).
**Business:** Mercenary contracting.

A Court mercenary house. House Agundar leases swords to whoever pays — caravan escorts, private wars in the hinterlands, the kind of work the City Guard will not touch. The house keeps a private army close to the legal cap of seventy soldiers (the 1248 DR settlement) and trades on a reputation for following the contract to the letter.

Their Court standing is steady rather than rising; the house wins or loses prestige campaign by campaign.
