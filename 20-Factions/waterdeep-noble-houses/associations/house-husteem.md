# House Husteem

**Tier:** Dregs (Great Game).
**Business:** Caravan trade.

A Dregs house: publicly scorned, off the Sea Ward guest list, kept at the edge of the social season. The scandal that pushed Husteem off the social ladder is older than living memory; what remains is the caravan business and the bitter remembrance of having once been Court.

Functionally, Husteem caravans still run. The family keeps a private army at or near the legal cap. They are the kind of house adventurers can hire when nobody else will sponsor the work.
