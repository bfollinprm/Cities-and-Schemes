# House Maerklos

**Tier:** Dregs (Great Game).
**Business:** Mercenary contracting.

A mercenary house publicly scorned for a miscalculation no one in the family will name. Maerklos still fields private soldiery and still takes contracts, but invitations to the social season are not forthcoming and Sea Ward villas pretend not to know the name.

The dossier groups Maerklos with **Husteem** and **Zun** as the city's three Dregs houses — the rejected and the ridiculed.
