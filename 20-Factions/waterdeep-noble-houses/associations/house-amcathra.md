# House Amcathra

**Tier:** Throne (Great Game).
**Business:** Politics, fine wine, sword-smithing.
**Ward:** Sea Ward.

One of the city's three current Throne houses. Amcathra plays the long political game: ennobling alliances, bench-warming Masked Lord candidates, and quietly endorsing legislation that protects old money. The vineyards and the smithy are real businesses — the wine is famous, the swords are heirloom — but both also serve as conduits for political favor.

Quiet confidence, walled gardens, no public scandal. The Sea Ward archetype.
