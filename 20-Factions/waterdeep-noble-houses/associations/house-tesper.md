# House Tesper

**Tier:** Throne (Great Game).
**Business:** Martial spectacle, public duels.
**Ward:** Sea Ward.

The third of the city's three Throne houses, alongside Cassalanter and Amcathra. Tesper plays loud where Amcathra plays quiet: tournaments, sponsored duels in the Field of Triumph, public displays of arms. Their training salles are a finishing school for the Sea Ward's noble sons; their challenge slips travel by morning post and ruin reputations by evening.

A house defined by visibility. The cost of staying on the Throne is bigger spectacle every year.
