# House Urmbrusk

**Tier:** Court (Great Game).
**Business:** Money-lending.
**Compromised:** **Jelenn Urmbrusk** is one of the two Masked Lords currently blackmailed by Manshoon's Zhentarim — the lever is personal rather than financial.

A Court house whose profits rotate through the city's noble debt and whose seat on the Masked Lords council is no longer her own. Jelenn votes the way Manshoon needs her to vote. Together with Corylus Thann, that is two votes inside the anonymous council that no one else on the council can identify, much less remove.
