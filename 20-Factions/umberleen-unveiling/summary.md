# The Umberleen Unveiling

An apocalyptic Umberlee cult working the [Baldur's Gate](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/README.md) waterfront. The Unveiling reads the harbor's recent storms — and the Sword Coast's general weather instability since the Far Realm pressures of 1501 DR — as the **Bitch Queen's veil lifting**: a coming reckoning in which the sea takes back what mortals built on her shores.

## Why they matter

The Unveiling is named in the dossier as the standing antagonist of [the Worshipful Company of Fishmongers](../wc-fishmongers/summary.md). [Matron Marga Saltwind](../wc-fishmongers/people/marga-saltwind.md) treats them as dangerous fanatics — not because their theology is wrong (Umberlee's anger is on the books) but because their *operational* answer to it is to disrupt the city's most reliable fish supply at exactly the moment the harbor needs it most. Their priests preach that fishmonger fleets are heresy: the sea's bounty is not for sale, and every netted catch is a debt that storms will come for.

## Methods

- Quayside sermons and bait-fire on Fishmonger jetties.
- Forecast-rumours that drive crews to keep boats in harbor — a slow strangulation of the catch.
- Ritual drownings on the city's worst storm nights, billed publicly as "the Queen's choosing"; in practice, abductions of crew chiefs the cult has marked.
