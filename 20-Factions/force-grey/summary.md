# Force Grey

The city of [Waterdeep](../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/README.md)'s elite reserve — adventurers, war-mages, and high-magic specialists held in standing for threats beyond the [City Watch](../city-watch/summary.md) and [City Guard](../city-guard/summary.md). Sworn to defend the city. Commanded by the [Blackstaff](../masked-lords/people/vajra-safahr.md).

## Status

On paper a sanctioned reserve under the [Open Lord's](../masked-lords/people/laeral-silverhand.md) supreme command. In practice, detached from the Open Lord's chain of command since [Vajra Safahr](../masked-lords/people/vajra-safahr.md) and [Laeral Silverhand](../masked-lords/people/laeral-silverhand.md) fell out: Vajra runs Force Grey on missions she sanctions personally — sometimes with the Open Lord's tacit consent, often without. The unit is one of Waterdeep's standing political wounds: the city's most dangerous formation answers to the Lord Mage, not the executive.

## Composition

Adventurers of name and arcanists with a reputation. Membership rotates; the unit is famously hard to track because it is not a barracks-and-roster outfit but a first-call list the Blackstaff keeps in her head.

## Hooks

- Unsanctioned operations against threats the Watch cannot handle and the Open Lord may not have authorized — a constitutional grey area as old as the unit's name.
- Officially excluded from gang-war duties (a Watch-and-Guard problem); in practice, deployed by Vajra against [Manshoon's](../zhentarim/people/manshoon.md) clone faction whenever the lines blur into the arcane.
