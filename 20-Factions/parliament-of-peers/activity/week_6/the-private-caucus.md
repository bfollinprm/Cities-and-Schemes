# The Private Caucus

Frustrated by [the previous week's Parliament collapse for lack of quorum](../../activity/week_5/the-failed-session.md) and the visible reality that neither chamber will act with [the Council's mercenaries firing at Baldurians](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_6/the-warning-shot.md), the moderate peers of both [the Coin](../../../peerage-of-coin/summary.md) and [Blood](../../../peerage-of-blood/summary.md) factions meet in secret at the Grom Hellscream Bathhouse in [the Lower City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/events.md). Eight peers attend — three from Coin, four from Blood, and one independent — looking to draft an across-the-aisle compromise that could de-escalate the blockade and restore Parliament's quorum.

Trust is so low that the meeting collapses inside two hours. A Coin peer accuses Durinbold's wing of cutting [the Sembian private-security contract](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_6/the-private-security.md) only to consolidate Blood's leverage; a Blood peer accuses Coin of taking Sembian gold under the table. Tempers escalate; one of Durinbold's allies pulls a knife. The bathhouse staff defuses the brawl before any blood spills, but **the secret caucus is over by midnight, and word will spread among the senior peers within a day**. The moderates who were the city's last legislative escape valve have just discovered they cannot even drink together without coming to blows.
