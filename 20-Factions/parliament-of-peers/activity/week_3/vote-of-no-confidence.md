# Vote of No Confidence

The emergency joint session called to vote out [Marshal Valerius Flint](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) descends into chaos at [High Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md). The [Peerage of Blood's filibuster](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_3/filibuster-the-mercenary-act.md) — speeches on tradition, sovereignty, and the dignity of native arms — runs the [Peerage of Coin's whip count](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_3/whip-the-votes.md) into the floor. The motion fails. The chamber adjourns gridlocked, and the public sees what the new-money houses cannot pass even with [the Council of Four](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_3/the-vote-of-no-confidence.md) at their back: the gulf between the city's old guard and its plutocrats is now load-bearing.
