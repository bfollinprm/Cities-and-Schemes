# The Bathhouse Brawl

A discreet meeting between moderate peers of Coin and Blood at the **Grom Hellscream Bathhouse** — convened to find any face-saving language for an emergency *Commerce Protection Act* that would re-open the Upper City gates without endorsing the Sembian mercenaries — collapses into a **physical brawl**. [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) calls a Coin moderate a "ledger-clerk in patriar collars"; the Coin man swings a brass towel-rail; two Knights-of-the-Shield-aligned junior peers wade in on Durinbold's side; the Watch detail extracts a half-dressed Coin lord through the steam-room.

The morning *Baldur's Mouth* runs the brawl as a **Coin-versus-Blood culture war**, exactly as [the Knights of the Shield](../../../knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_7/the-gilded-extortion.md) wanted. After [last week's Hellscream collapse](../../activity/week_6/the-private-caucus.md) and now this, **Parliament has no functioning back-channel between the houses left.** Any vote on the gate lockdown will be along bloc lines. The Knights' soul-coin extortion of trapped patriars goes unchecked for at least another tenday.
