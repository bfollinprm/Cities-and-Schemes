# Cleanse the Sanctum Entrance

A solo Sharran agent tries the cleansing ritual on the long-buried sanctum entrance — alone, because [Mother Nocturne's](../../people/mother-nocturne.md) cell is still rebuilding from week 0. The pre-Crisis wards are too strong for one operative; the ritual fizzles harmlessly and the door stays sealed. The Silent Shroud is now visibly behind schedule on its core long-arc objective, with no second attempt staffed.
