# Monitor the Tithe

[Mother Nocturne](../../people/mother-nocturne.md) sends two of her surveillance agents — one veteran shadow-walker, one younger cell-member working a tradesman's cover — to confirm whether **Bror** has taken receipt of [the first Sharran tithe instruction left for him at the Plowman last week](../../../silent-shroud/activity/week_4/the-first-tithe.md). Bror is back in the Material Plane and walking the city again. The agents pick him up off the Wide and trail him along Upper City streets. They lose him almost immediately. A sudden surge of Helmite Watchful Shield patrols — drawn out of the [Watchful Shield Shrine](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/watchful-shield-shrine.md) by [a Bhaalist alarm filed by the Watch](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_5/relocate-the-sanctum.md) — floods the Wide and the Manorborn approaches. The shadow-walker takes a Helmite *detect divine* in the shoulder and breaks off. Bror walks out of Upper City unobserved. The Whispering Stone's data cannot be cross-checked against the agent's eye-witness; the cell has no way to verify whether Bror has read the tithe instruction or simply put it in his pocket. **Mother Nocturne's cell is in the dark on its own new asset.** Paranoia inside the Shroud blooms by week's end about who, exactly, has been tasked.
