# The Helm and Cloak Infiltration

Acting on Bror's reconnaissance from week 4 and the additional intelligence Bror delivered to the Penumbra Playhouse [last session](../../../../50-Sessions/session_27/player_summary.md), [Mother Nocturne](../../people/mother-nocturne.md) **finishes the Purge of the Blind** in her own cell — three cultists quietly bled out in the Twin Songs catacombs after psionic interrogation cleared none of them — and deploys her elite Sharran spies into **the Helm and Cloak**, the patriar-only Upper City club where the Knights of the Shield's contracts are notarized over brandy.

The infiltration is a long-game blackmail operation: Sharran scribes copy patriar conversation logs in shorthand and route them to safe-houses in Heapside before sundown each night. Within seven days the Silent Shroud has compromising material on five mid-tier patriars, two of whom are signatories on [the Knights' Gilded Extortion contracts](../../../knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_7/the-gilded-extortion.md). Mother Nocturne is now positioned to *blackmail the blackmailers* — the Knights' soul-coin business cannot survive a Sharran leak of its contract list. She is patient. She is waiting for [Bror to deliver the sentient source](../../../../50-Sessions/session_27/player_summary.md) before she plays the cards.
