# The First Tithe

Having [established her leverage over Bror through the Whispering Stone in week 2](../../../silent-shroud/activity/week_2/amplify-the-despair.md) and [reached for the Stone unsuccessfully in week 3](../../../silent-shroud/activity/week_3/whispers-from-the-stone.md), [Mother Nocturne](../../people/mother-nocturne.md) gives Bror his first instructed mission. She delivers it through a junior Sharran cell at the [Penumbra Playhouse](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/sorcerous-sundries.md) intermediary network: take the Whispering Stone to the **Helm and Cloak**, the patriar club in the Upper City, and *listen* across an evening's drinks for political weakness — names, debts, infidelities, anything Mother Nocturne can use as leverage on a peer. The instruction is left at the Plowman with the rooming charge. The party is in the Feywild. Bror does not know the instruction is waiting. The Sharran cell waits.
