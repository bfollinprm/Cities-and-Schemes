# Amplify the Despair

[Mother Nocturne's](../../people/mother-nocturne.md) cell opens a magical-surveillance campaign on the dwarf monk [Bror](../../../party/people/bror.md) after he visits the **Penumbra Playhouse** in [Twin Songs](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/README.md) and reveals he is in possession of the **Whispering Stone** and a fresh dossier on the Outer-City sewer cult, the [Plague of Remembrance](../../../plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) Phoenix Pits agent Zora, and the party's political entanglements. Mother Nocturne pays for his attempted attunement ritual; the price is the first valuable secret recovered through the stone. The Sharrans have now embedded a long-term intelligence asset inside the party.
