# Whispers from the Stone

[Mother Nocturne's](../../people/mother-nocturne.md) Sharran agents attempt to influence the Whispering Stone directly through [Bror](../../../party/people/bror.md) — to use the recruitment hook seated last week as a remote channel into the artifact's voice. The stone resists. Its chaotic, alien nature throws off the Sharran ritual; the operators take a psychic backlash that severs the connection mid-rite, and a nondescript psychic flicker — strong enough to be noticed by [a powerful patron of Bror's of an unnamed entity](../../../party/people/bror.md) — registers somewhere outside the Penumbra Playhouse, somewhere not Shar. Mother Nocturne now has a worse problem than a failed ritual: another agent of significant power is interested in her new acolyte and his strange artifact. The intelligence brokerage on Bror is no longer single-buyer, and Nocturne adjusts her standing instructions to him accordingly — no further public attunements without her direct supervision.
