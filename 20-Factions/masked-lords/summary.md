# The Masked Lords *(of Waterdeep)*

Waterdeep's ruling council. At least twelve members, identities concealed by magical helms whenever they sit. Founded in 1032 DR by the mage **Ahghairon** after he killed the would-be emperor Raurlor; revived in 1273 DR by Lords **Baeron and Shilarn Silmaeril** after the Guildmasters' Folly; in continuous operation since.

## Doctrine

**Ahghaironism** — anonymity as the guarantor of incorruptibility. The argument is that a Lord who cannot be lobbied, threatened, or romanced is a Lord who can rule honestly. The masks are not deceptions; they are the technology of the office.

The reformist counter-argument (the *Open Rulers*) is that secrecy itself is tyranny: anonymity also shields treason, and the **Masked Lord Murders of 1491 DR** prove the doctrine is no longer paying for itself.

## Structure

- **Open Lord.** The public face — currently [Laeral Silverhand](people/laeral-silverhand.md). Convenes the council, speaks for the city, never appears un-helmed at a session.
- **Masked Lords.** ≥12 members, anonymous to the public and to each other where possible. Sit in helms; vote in helms.
- **Magisters.** Black-robed judiciary, appointed by the council.
- **Force Grey.** Elite reserve company, traditionally led by the **Blackstaff** ([Vajra Safahr](people/vajra-safahr.md)) — currently *detached* from the Open Lord's chain of command.

## Current crisis

- **The 1491 DR coup.** A faction inside the council murdered nearly half the others. The plot was foiled, but the council has not recovered. Paranoia about any new concentration of power — including [Laeral's](people/laeral-silverhand.md) — is institutional now.
- **Two compromised seats.** [Corylus Thann](people/corylus-thann.md) and [Jelenn Urmbrusk](people/jelenn-urmbrusk.md) are blackmailed by **Manshoon's** Zhentarim. Manshoon already votes through them; no one in the council can act against him without exposing whose helms are compromised.
- **The Open Lord's secret.** [Laeral Silverhand](people/laeral-silverhand.md) lost the bulk of her Chosen-of-Mystra power to the Second Sundering and has concealed it for two decades.
