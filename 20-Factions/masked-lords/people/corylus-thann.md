# Corylus Thann

Head of [House Thann](../../waterdeep-noble-houses/associations/house-thann.md) — Court-tier, vintners — and one of the Masked Lords currently sitting under a magical helm at council sessions.

## Compromised

Manshoon's Zhentarim hold financial leverage over Corylus that would, if produced, end House Thann as a Court family. Corylus votes the way Manshoon's people require him to vote. He is one of *two* compromised Masked Lords, alongside [Jelenn Urmbrusk](jelenn-urmbrusk.md).

The other Masked Lords do not know which seats are compromised. Neither does [Laeral Silverhand](laeral-silverhand.md) for certain. The compromise is the operational basis for Manshoon's bid to install himself as Open Lord — he already votes inside the council; he is patient enough to wait for an opportunity to seat himself publicly.
