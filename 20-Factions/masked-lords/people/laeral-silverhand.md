# Laeral Silverhand

Open Lord of Waterdeep. The public face of the [Masked Lords](../summary.md). Sister of the Seven Sisters, archmage of long standing, and — as of c. 1489 DR — the city's reluctant chief executive.

## The wound she does not name

The Second Sundering (c. 1482–1487 DR) cost Laeral the bulk of her power as a Chosen of Mystra. She has concealed this for twenty years. The performance is total: she still projects unbroken archmage authority at the Palace, at Sea Ward galas, and in the council chamber. Only a small handful of people know how thin the projection has worn.

The dossier reads this as the city's central act of *splendor as theater*: the Open Lord's office runs on the assumption that Laeral is still what she was, and the city's enemies make the same assumption.

## How she came to office

The Masked Lords forced **Dagult Neverember** out in c. 1489 DR for corruption — he had been selling noble titles to wealthy merchants, fueling the *new money / old money* schism — and reluctantly elevated Laeral. *Reluctantly* matters. Several Masked Lords would have preferred a less luminous successor; the 1491 DR coup attempt drew on that resentment.

## Current standing

- **Estranged** from [Vajra Safahr](vajra-safahr.md), the Blackstaff. Force Grey runs unsanctioned missions. Their break has not been made public.
- Holds the council together through stagecraft and personal authority. There is no obvious successor.
- Quietly aware that two seats on her council are compromised; cannot act on the knowledge without exposing the compromise.
