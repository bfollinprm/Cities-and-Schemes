# Khelben "Blackstaff" Arunsun

The original **Blackstaff**: archmage of Waterdeep, founder of the office, bearer of the [Blackstaff](vajra-safahr.md) artifact whose pact-spirit still rides the staff today. Long dead by 1502 DR; named in the corpus as a historical anchor for the modern Lord Mage role.

## Why he matters

The Lord Mage of Waterdeep is *Khelben's* office. [Vajra Safahr](vajra-safahr.md) inherited the title and the staff; Khelben's spirit, bound to the artifact at the founding, is the reason the Blackstaff is institutional rather than personal. The current strain between Vajra and [Laeral Silverhand](laeral-silverhand.md) — Vajra running [Force Grey](../../force-grey/summary.md) on unsanctioned missions while detached from the Open Lord's chain of command — runs along Khelben's old fault line: should the city's mage power answer to its public lord, or to its own oath?
