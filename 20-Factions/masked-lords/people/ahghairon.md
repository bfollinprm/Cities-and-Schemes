# Ahghairon

Mage. Slayer of the warlord **Raurlor**, who had declared himself emperor in 1032 DR. Founder of the [Masked Lords](../summary.md) council and of *Ahghaironism* — the doctrine that anonymity is the technology of just government.

Historical figure. Long dead by the present (1502 DR); died c. 1256 DR, after which the **Guildmasters' Folly** briefly seized the city before Lords **Baeron and Shilarn Silmaeril** restored the council in 1273 DR.

## Why he is still cited

Every argument about the legitimacy of the Masked Lords passes through Ahghairon. Traditionalists invoke him to defend the masks; reformists invoke the Guildmasters' Folly to argue that the system never recovered from his death. Either way, his name is the founding myth no one in Waterdhavian politics can do without.
