# Jelenn Urmbrusk

Head of [House Urmbrusk](../../waterdeep-noble-houses/associations/house-urmbrusk.md) — Court-tier, money-lending — and a sitting Masked Lord. Compromised.

## Compromised

The lever Manshoon holds over Jelenn is personal rather than financial. Specifics are deliberately not in the dossier; what matters operationally is that her vote belongs to the Zhentarim. Together with [Corylus Thann](corylus-thann.md), she is half of Manshoon's two-vote presence inside the anonymous council.

The arrangement is the spine of Manshoon's Waterdeep play: he does not need to assassinate his way to the Open Lord's chair if he can corrupt his way there.
