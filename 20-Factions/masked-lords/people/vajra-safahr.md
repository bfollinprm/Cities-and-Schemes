# Vajra Safahr

The **Blackstaff**. Lord Mage of Waterdeep, holder of the staff-artifact (containing the spirits of every Blackstaff before her), commander of **Force Grey** — the city's elite reserve of adventurers and high-magic specialists.

## The break

Vajra is currently **estranged** from [Laeral Silverhand](laeral-silverhand.md). Force Grey is detached from the Open Lord's chain of command in everything but ceremony: Vajra runs sanctioned missions on her own authority, picks her own targets, and reports the results after the fact when she reports them at all. The break has not been made public; the city's enemies do not know the Blackstaff and the Open Lord do not speak.

## Standing

- Sworn to defend the city. The oath has not bent; it is the oath that the dossier reads as the most likely to *hold* if Waterdeep enters open crisis.
- Functionally outside the [Masked Lords'](../summary.md) ordinary politics, though still appointed by them and still treated as the senior officer of state magic.
- The Blackstaff title carries forward continuity that no living archmage can match — the staff itself is the office.
