# Dagult Neverember

Open Lord of Waterdeep until c. 1489 DR. Forced out by his own [Masked Lords](../summary.md) for corruption: he had been selling noble titles to wealthy merchants, monetizing the rolls of the eighty-five houses.

## Why it mattered

The sales were not merely graft. Each new title fed the *new money / old money* schism, dragged the **Great Game** toward the *pro-plutocracy* axis, and — long after Neverember's removal — gave Sea Ward villas a permanent grievance against the Court. The fault line he opened still runs under House Cassalanter's banking floor, House Husteem's caravan accounts, and most of the city's ennoblement scandals.

He was replaced by [Laeral Silverhand](laeral-silverhand.md), a choice the council made without enthusiasm.
