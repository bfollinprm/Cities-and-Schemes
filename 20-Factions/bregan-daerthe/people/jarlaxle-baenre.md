# Jarlaxle Baenre

Founder and unchallenged head of [Bregan D'aerthe](../summary.md). Born of [House Baenre](../../../10-Setting/cosmology/realms/) in Menzoberranzan; long since renounced — though he keeps the name for leverage. Rumored to be one of the most resourceful drow alive, and almost certainly the wealthiest who is willing to spend coin on the surface.

## Profile

Slight, dark-skinned, eye-patched (the patch is magical, ornamental, or theatrical depending on the day), absurdly well-dressed in a hat that should not work and somehow does. Charismatic, fluent in a half-dozen surface tongues, ostentatiously friendly. A pragmatist — never a fanatic.

## Operating profile in 1502 DR

- **Front of choice:** the [Sea Maidens Faire](../associations/sea-maidens-faire.md), a traveling sailing carnival that lets him appear in port without a single drow visible on deck.
- **Base:** [Luskan](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/luskan/README.md) — patient power-broker behind the High Captains.
- **Long game:** legitimize Luskan with a [Lords' Alliance](../../../10-Setting/polities/lords-alliance.md) seat, paid for with [Vault of Dragons](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/vault-of-dragons.md) gold. A surface-recognized Luskan gives Bregan D'aerthe a permanent home not subject to Menzoberranzan recall.

## Hooks

- Will sell to anyone whose contract pays and whose ask does not require burning the brand.
- Personal vendetta against any Menzoberranzan house that tries to expose his surface operation.
- Open to information trades with the Harpers, Lords' Alliance, or the [Open Lord](../../masked-lords/people/laeral-silverhand.md) — provided the price is right and the conversation never officially happened.
