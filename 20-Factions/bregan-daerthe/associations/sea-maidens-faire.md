# The Sea Maidens Faire

The public face of [Bregan D'aerthe](../summary.md) on the Sword Coast. A traveling carnival aboard a fleet of converted galleons — costumed performers, themed shows, a sea-themed festival every time the ship makes port. Ostensibly run by a charismatic surface-elf impresario; actually a [Jarlaxle Baenre](../people/jarlaxle-baenre.md) operation, captained and crewed largely by drow mercenaries with magical disguises.

## What it does

- **Docks in the [Waterdeep Dock Ward](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/dock-ward/character.md)** on a published schedule and elsewhere along the coast on demand. Standard berth, standard dues, no special privileges asked or granted.
- **Runs the show.** Performances, illusion-driven spectacle, paid revelry. The Faire makes real money on its own — it would survive as a business even without the cover function.
- **Moves Bregan D'aerthe assets.** Agents, drow operators, contraband, and intelligence ride the Faire from port to port. Jarlaxle himself appears occasionally, masked.
- **Provides Luskan a face.** When [Luskan](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/luskan/README.md) needs to send something to Waterdeep that should not arrive on a Luskan ship, it arrives on the Faire instead.

## Open secret

Waterdeep's Open Lord and Blackstaff both know the Faire is a Bregan D'aerthe front. So far they treat it as a manageable hazard rather than a threat to neutralize. The Faire pays its dues, keeps its troupe in line, and does not provoke; in exchange it docks unmolested.
