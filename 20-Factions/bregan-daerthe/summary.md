# Bregan D'aerthe

A drow mercenary company out of Menzoberranzan, founded and led by **[Jarlaxle Baenre](people/jarlaxle-baenre.md)**. Pragmatic, capable, and openly for hire to surface powers — a profile no other drow house can offer. In the [Sword Coast](../../30-Places/sword-coast/README.md) of 1502 DR they operate from [Luskan](../../30-Places/sword-coast/luskan/README.md) and [Waterdeep](../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/README.md), fronting as the **[Sea Maidens Faire](associations/sea-maidens-faire.md)**.

## Mission

Recover the gold of the **[Vault of Dragons](../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/vault-of-dragons.md)** to bankroll a [Lords' Alliance](../../10-Setting/polities/lords-alliance.md) seat for [Luskan](../../30-Places/sword-coast/luskan/README.md), legitimizing the High Captains' regime and giving Jarlaxle a permanent surface foothold. Long-term goal: continued surface operations on Jarlaxle's terms, not Menzoberranzan's.

## Methods

- **Surface front.** Operate openly through the [Sea Maidens Faire](associations/sea-maidens-faire.md) — a traveling carnival/sailing-show that docks at the [Waterdeep Dock Ward](../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/dock-ward/character.md) periodically. Provides cover for ferrying agents, contraband, and information.
- **Mercenary contracts.** Available to anyone whose interests align — patriar houses, kontors, even the [Lords' Alliance](../../10-Setting/polities/lords-alliance.md) itself in a pinch.
- **Pragmatist alliances.** No permanent enmities by design. Bregan D'aerthe will sit at any table that pays.

## Current state

- **Luskan play active.** The High Captains tolerate Bregan D'aerthe because Jarlaxle's coin keeps the city solvent. The Lords' Alliance bid is the long arc.
- **Waterdeep presence covert.** Sea Maidens Faire docks freely; the Open Lord and Blackstaff both know the front for what it is. So far, mutual non-interference holds.
- **Vault of Dragons hunt ongoing.** The hoard's location remains the city's most valuable open secret.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Allies of convenience.** Anyone whose contract pays. The Bregan D'aerthe pitch is "we will deliver, we will not flip on the contract, we will not sell the file" — and so far, the brand holds.

**Friction.** [Xanathar Guild](../xanathars-guild/summary.md) (rival underworld presence in [Skullport](../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/skullport.md) and the Undercity); house factions in Menzoberranzan that view Jarlaxle's surface operations as an existential embarrassment.
