# Salvage the Loss

After [the spoiled livestock shipment of week 2](../activity/week_2/secure-the-herd.md), [Goodman Borlu](../../people/borlu.md) tries to recover by selling the questionable remainder as cheap rations to refugees in **Sow's Foot** out of [Hamhock's Slaughterhouse](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/undercity/hamhocks-vault.md). A Hellrider patrol working the refugee district — already on heightened spoilage watch since [the seized contaminated grain at the Dawnmarket](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_3/a-new-vector.md) earlier the same week — notices the smell and the poor cut quality. The patrol seizes the entire shipment and burns it on the spot, publicly, in front of the queue waiting to buy it. The Butchers' Company faces a massive public-relations disaster, an open Hellrider report on contaminated meat sold to refugees, and the threat of formal action. Borlu's Sow's Foot operation is poisoned for months; he will spend most of the next week pricing whose silence he has to buy.
