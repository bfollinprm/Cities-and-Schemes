# Killing the Story

Facing the public-relations disaster of [last week's spoiled-meat seizure at Hamhock's Slaughterhouse](../../../wc-butchers-tanners/activity/week_3/salvage-the-loss.md), [Goodman Borlu](../../people/borlu.md) calls in a favor at the Baldur's Mouth Print Shop. He meets [Editor Ettvard Needle](../../../baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) personally with a bag of coin and a counter-pitch: kill the spoiled-meat story, run a piece praising the Worshipful Company's "generous food donations" to Sow's Foot refugees instead. Needle, who is having [a catastrophic week of his own](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-menace.md) and badly needs friendly money, takes the bag and the angle. The pivot piece runs in the next edition. It is read by no one outside the patriar households Needle still has — the Outer City already saw the burning meat in week 3 and does not believe the new story for a heartbeat. Borlu has paid full price for a half-dead retraction.
