# The Donation Drive

[Goodman Borlu, "the Cleaver,"](../../people/borlu.md) attempting to repair the Butchers' public reputation after [the spoiled-meat scandal that ate last cycle's headlines](../../../wc-butchers-tanners/activity/week_4/killing-the-story.md), spends a notable sum on high-quality grain and fresh produce purchased through Outer City brokers and stages a *public* donation drive in the [Sow's Foot](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md) market square. Stalls go up on Tendays-morning. Loaves and apples are handed out by aproned journeymen with the Butchers' guild-sigil on every basket. A paid-off Baldur's Mouth journalist — a Ettvard Needle hire — stands at Borlu's elbow and writes the puff-piece in real time. The same Sow's Foot ward is, on the same week, *already stirred up* by [the Bhaalist ritual-blood spill that started yesterday's butcher mob](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_5/relocate-the-sanctum.md), and a working butcher receiving a basket of Borlu's grain from Borlu's hand is a man who saw a real cult run from his cleaver hours before. The pitch lands. **The Butchers' reputation in Sow's Foot is materially repaired.** The same crowd that drove the Bhaalists out is now publicly grateful to the Cleaver.
