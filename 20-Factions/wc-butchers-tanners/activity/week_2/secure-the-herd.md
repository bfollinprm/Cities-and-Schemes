# Secure the Herd

[Goodman Borlu, "the Cleaver,"](../../people/borlu.md) tries to slip a critical livestock shipment through the [Free Trader siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md) by bribing a Flaming Fist customs officer at the Basilisk Gate. The bribe is refused — Fist officers are now twitchy after [the Marshal's censure](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_2/contain-the-crises.md) and unwilling to be caught taking purse money on a contraband case. The shipment rots on the road; a midweek cattle die-off becomes a [Baldur's Mouth](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_2/fan-the-flames.md) sidebar.
