# Intimidate a Health Inspector

Goodman [Borlu](../../people/borlu.md) sends Guild Wardens to lean on a city health inspector poking at the tanneries' pollution. The inspector — either unusually brave or quietly backed by a rival faction — refuses to flinch. He files a damning report; Borlu eats a hefty fine and unwanted official scrutiny of his operations. The line of "just write down what we tell you" no longer reliably works on the city's inspectors.
