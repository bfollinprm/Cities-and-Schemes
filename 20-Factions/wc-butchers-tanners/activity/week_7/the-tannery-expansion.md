# The Tannery Expansion

Emboldened by [the skirmish victory over the Druids' choking-vines last tenday](../../activity/week_6/the-tannery-defense.md) and rebuilding the broken vat-block at speed, [Goodman Borlu, "the Cleaver"](../../people/borlu.md) arms the entire Butchers' Guild with **cleavers and short axes** and orders the Tannery's daily waste **dumped directly into the Chionthar** in defiance of every standing environmental ordinance. The river along the [Lower City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) bank turns the color of old blood by the fourth day.

The expansion is openly retaliatory: Borlu wants [the Inner Grove Druids](../../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md) to attempt another assault so he can break their cell on his own ground this time. He posts a standing ten-gold bounty on any druid identified at the Tannery perimeter and increases armed guard rotations to three shifts. The fishmongers downstream complain to the Council; the Council ignores them — they are too consumed by [the Refugee Riot](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_7/the-refugee-riot.md) and [the Bathhouse Brawl](../../../parliament-of-peers/activity/week_7/the-bathhouse-brawl.md) to enforce river law. **The Tannery is now openly armed and openly poisoning the river.**
