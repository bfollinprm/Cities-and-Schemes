# The Correction

[Nansi Gretta](../../people/nansi-gretta.md) realizes [the "Warlord's Dossier" she compiled and sold the previous week](../../activity/week_5/the-warlords-dossier.md) is filled with active disinformation — Commander Valerius's lieutenants fed her agents specific, plausible *false* numbers, and three of her highest-paying buyers have already noticed the discrepancies. Her reputation is on the line. Scrambling to recover before her client list collapses, she does the thing she has spent twenty years not doing: **she goes into the field herself.** A long-prepared cover identity, a forged Sembian quartermaster's seal, and a black-cloak commission walk her into the mercenary camp's officers' tent in the middle of the night.

The infiltration goes badly inside the first hour. The forged seal is good but not perfect; a junior officer notices the wrong wax-color, raises the alarm, and Gretta has to flee through the camp under a thrown lantern's light. She makes it out alive — barely — by abandoning her notebook and a brooch worth 200 gp under a tent flap. **The actual supply manifests are not recovered.** The Bibliophile is now exposed by name to the Sembian command (which puts her on the same enemy list as the Harpers' [Trade Way saboteurs](../../../harpers/activity/week_6/the-supply-sabotage.md)) and her clients are quietly ending their subscriptions by the end of the week. The most-successful information broker in the city has just had **the worst week of her professional life.**
