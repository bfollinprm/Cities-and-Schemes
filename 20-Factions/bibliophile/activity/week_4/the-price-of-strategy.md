# The Price of Strategy

[Nansi Gretta](../../people/nansi-gretta.md), having already sold [intelligence on Marshal Flint's officers in week 3](../../../bibliophile/activity/week_3/the-price-of-treason.md), now sells *Wyrm's Rock itself*. She acquires detailed tactical maps of the fortress — interior galleries, sally-port timing, sewer egress — from a sympathetic cell inside the [Cartographers' Guild](../../../wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_4/playing-both-sides.md), repackages them at a steep markup, and discreetly sells them to the commander of [the Archduke's mercenary force at the Eastway barracks](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md). The sale is invisible. The information is not — by the end of the week the mercenaries' duty officer is annotating a Wyrm's Rock floor plan in their command tent. Nansi books her largest single payment of the campaign.
