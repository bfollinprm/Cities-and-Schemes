# The Warlord's Dossier

[Nansi Gretta](../../people/nansi-gretta.md), wanting an exclusive product on [the new mercenary commander](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md) to undercut [the Iron Throne's parallel rumor-mill operation](../../../iron-throne/activity/week_5/the-rumor-mill.md), sends a small infiltration team into the [Eastway](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) mercenary camp. The team is good. The lieutenants are better. The infiltrators are recognized at the perimeter — a cap worn the wrong way, a manifest forged in a hand the camp's clerk has seen before — and *brutally interrogated* in the command tent's back chamber. They give Nansi nothing useful. The lieutenants give *them* something. Specifically: false orders of battle, fabricated supply-line schedules, names of fictional sub-companies, and a deliberately-misrouted approach map. The team is then released, bruised, alive, and gratefully reporting back to the Bibliophile that they survived. Nansi binds the misinformation into her dossier in the same week. By the time the product goes to market — to a Free Trader buyer, to a patriar buyer, to a Sembian buyer who is the camp commander's own relative — **Nansi is selling a working enemy disinformation campaign with her own seal on it.**
