# The Price of Treason

[Nansi Gretta's](../../people/nansi-gretta.md) network, working out of the back rooms of [the Bibliophile](../../summary.md) shop in [Bloomridge](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/bloomridge.md), assembles a detailed dossier on [Marshal Flint's](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) most loyal officers — their families, their debts, the names of their lovers, the routes their children walk to school. Nothing on the Marshal himself; the package is leverage on the people whose loyalty Flint will need [as the political siege closes around Wyrm's Rock](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_3/circle-the-wagons.md). Gretta plans to auction the dossier into the escalating crisis: [the Patriar Heritage Society](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_3/defend-the-marshal.md) wants to buy the officers' loyalty quietly; [the Peerage of Coin](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_3/whip-the-votes.md) wants to break it; [the Baldur's Mouth](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_3/the-price-of-insubordination.md) is the safety net. The dossier is complete by week's end; the bidding starts next.
