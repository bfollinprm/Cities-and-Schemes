# The Missing Ink

[Nansi Gretta](../../people/nansi-gretta.md) — humiliated by [last week's near-exposure at the Sembian camp](../../activity/week_6/the-correction.md) and watching her client list cancel — pivots to a **commodity play** the Consortium will need badly inside seven days. She quietly buys every remaining copy of [the Siege Edition exposing the insurance fraud](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_6/the-siege-edition.md) from corner sellers, broadside-readers, and back-room collectors across the Lower City.

By midweek there are perhaps a hundred copies of the original exposé left in circulation, and [the Consortium's Great Retraction](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_7/the-great-retraction.md) is running daily. Nansi prices the surviving copies at fifty gold apiece for collectors and **ten thousand gold for the entire surviving stock**, sealed bid, payable to the buyer with the right reasons to want them gone. [Factor Errinthal](../../../chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) receives the price at his Brampton office on the fifth day. *Every copy still in the city is now a single bookkeeper's asset.* Nansi is back in the game.
