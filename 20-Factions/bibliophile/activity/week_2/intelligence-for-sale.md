# Intelligence for Sale

[Nansi Gretta](../../people/nansi-gretta.md) compiles two distinct intelligence packets on the party — the freshly-public adventuring company that has now entangled itself with the [Hellriders](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_2/the-interrogation.md), [the Guild](../../../guild/activity/week_2/retaliation-on-the-river.md), the [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_2/the-public-patrons.md), and now [Mad Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md) — and sells one to the [Waterdhavian Kontor](../../../waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_2/profile-the-agitator.md) (clinical), one to the [Zhentarim](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_2/consolidate-the-east-end.md) (with the Fist warrant flagged). The same shop also sells the [Cartographers' new "alternate" hinterland route maps](../../../wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_2/monetize-the-maze.md) to the Western Gate. Tailored, profitable, and neutral on its face.
