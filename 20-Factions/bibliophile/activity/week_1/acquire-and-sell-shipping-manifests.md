# Acquire and Sell Shipping Manifests

[Nansi Gretta](../../people/nansi-gretta.md) accepts pre-orders from both the [Sembian Kontor](../../../sembian-kontor/summary.md) and the [River-Rats](../../../river-rats/summary.md) for the [Chionthar Consortium's](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) upcoming shipping manifests. The Consortium — already burned by industrial espionage this season — has just upgraded internal security. The Bibliophile's agent cannot reach the documents. Both buyers go without; Nansi's reliability takes a measurable, if private, hit.
