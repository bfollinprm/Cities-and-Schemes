# Nansi Gretta

Proprietor of [The Bibliophile](../summary.md). Older, unassuming, surrounded by books. Sells the most expensive intelligence in the Lower City and reliably forgets every face she has ever seen at the asking.

## Role

Gretta runs her bookshop as a front for a high-margin information brokerage. Her stable of stringers includes household staff in patriar manors, retired Guild informants, and dockside watchers — none of whom know the others exist. Her reputation for accuracy is what keeps clients returning; her reputation for discretion is what keeps her alive.

She has standing arrangements with [The Guild](../../guild/summary.md), the [Harpers](../../harpers/summary.md), several Kontors, and at least two cults that she would not name aloud.
