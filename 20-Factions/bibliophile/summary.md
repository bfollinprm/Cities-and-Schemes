# The Bibliophile

A seemingly legitimate bookstore in **Bloomridge** that smells of old leather and peppermints — and one of Baldur's Gate's most sophisticated spy networks. Run by [Nansi Gretta](people/nansi-gretta.md) from the back table, where information politely changes hands.

## Ideology

**Information is a commodity, and neutrality is the product.** The Bibliophile profits by selling tailored intelligence to whichever buyer can afford it; she will sell on both sides of the same conflict so long as no client believes themselves uniquely served. Her one rule: never burn a paying client. Every other loyalty is for sale.

## Membership

A small, secretive cell whose members are known only to one another and to Gretta:

- **Nansi Gretta** herself, the bookshop's proprietress and proprietor of the network.
- A handful of researchers, "readers," and pickpocket-couriers who run packets between the shop and her clients.
- A wider, paid network of one-off informants — disgruntled clerks, off-shift Watch, talkative servants — managed through dead drops in books.

## Methods

- **Tailored intelligence packets.** Gretta sells dossiers customized for the buyer's known interests and rivals.
- **Acquisition by espionage.** When the network can't buy what it needs, it steals — agents try to lift shipping manifests, internal documents, tactical maps.
- **Multiple buyers, single product.** A particularly valuable scoop is sold to two or three rival clients with carefully different framings, so each thinks they got the better deal.
- **Bloomridge front.** The bookshop is impeccably legitimate; the back room is a meeting place for clients who could not be seen meeting each other anywhere else.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Clients.** The [Council of Four](../council-of-four/summary.md), [The Guild](../guild/summary.md), the [Harpers](../harpers/summary.md), the [Sembian Kontor](../sembian-kontor/summary.md), and the [River-Rats](../river-rats/summary.md) have all been clients in the recent past. The party themselves have been the *subject* of intelligence packets sold simultaneously to two rival factions.

**Frictions.** Recent operational failures: an attempt to acquire the [Chionthar Consortium](../chionthar-consortium/summary.md)'s upcoming shipping manifests was blocked by upgraded internal security, leaving the Sembian Kontor and the River-Rats without intelligence they had paid for.

**Hooks.** Gretta is currently compiling a dossier on **Marshal Flint's most loyal officers** — their families and weaknesses — to sell into the escalating Wyrm's Crossing crisis. She has also acquired tactical maps of **Wyrm's Rock** from the [Worshipful Company of Cartographers & Surveyors](../wc-cartographers-surveyors/summary.md) and sold them to the commander of the Archduke's mercenary force.
