# The City Guard *(of Waterdeep)*

Waterdeep's standing military, garrisoned at [Castle Waterdeep](../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/castle-ward/castle-waterdeep/README.md) under the supreme command of the [Open Lord](../masked-lords/people/laeral-silverhand.md). Distinct from the [City Watch](../city-watch/summary.md) (the civilian police) and from Baldur's Gate's [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md).

## Remit

Walls, gates, and the harbor wall. The Guard mans the perimeter, projects force outside the walls when ordered, and stays out of internal policing by tradition. They will intervene in the [Field Ward](../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/field-ward/README.md) only to keep traffic moving.

## Structure

Career officers in the Castle Waterdeep chain of command. Garrison administration runs through the Castle's senior staff; the Open Lord's actual day-to-day involvement is light, by design — the Watch and Guard split is meant to keep civilian and martial authority separate.

## Current state

- **Open Lord's diminished hand.** [Laeral Silverhand](../masked-lords/people/laeral-silverhand.md) is supreme commander on paper. In practice, garrison routine continues without her, and her [Sundering-diminished](../../10-Setting/cosmology/sundering.md) magic limits how forcefully she can lead from the front.
- **[Force Grey](../force-grey/summary.md) is not the Guard.** The city's elite reserve answers to the [Blackstaff](../masked-lords/people/vajra-safahr.md), not the Castle. In a major crisis, the Guard holds the walls; whether Force Grey holds the line beside it depends on Vajra's mood that day.
- **Wartime stance is theoretical.** The Guard has not fought a serious siege in living memory; its institutional muscle is gate-watching and ceremonial display, not field warfare.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Patrons.** The [Open Lord](../masked-lords/people/laeral-silverhand.md); the [Masked Lords](../masked-lords/summary.md) collectively.

**Friction.** The [City Watch](../city-watch/summary.md) (jurisdictional overlaps at the gates and in the Field Ward); [Force Grey](../force-grey/summary.md) (chain-of-command broken at the top).
