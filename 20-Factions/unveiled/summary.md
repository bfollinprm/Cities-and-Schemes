# The Unveiled

A clandestine cell of "truth-seekers" who believe the entire Absolute Crisis was a false-flag operation orchestrated by a cabal of shapeshifting elites. Founded and led by [Xylia, the Seeker of the Hidden Sky](people/xylia.md).

## Ideology

The Unveiled exist to "expose the stagecraft" of the ruling powers. Every collapse, every popular new official, every piece of public misfortune is read as evidence of doppelganger infiltration and ongoing psychic operations meant to keep the populace scared and compliant. They communicate through coded messages and hidden symbols scrawled on alley walls, and meticulously document inconsistencies in the official narrative of the Absolute Crisis.

## Membership

A coalition of Baldur's Gate's most paranoid:

- Fringe scholars.
- Disgraced journalists.
- Low-level members of [The Guild](../guild/summary.md) who have seen too much.
- Anyone who feels the official story is a lie.

## Methods

The Unveiled do not fight openly. They engage in counter-intelligence:

- Breaking into the offices of the *Baldur's Mouth* to alter printing plates.
- Replacing official proclamations with their own manifestos.
- Acquiring items that can reveal shapeshifters.

They see themselves as righteous information warriors.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** They are obsessed with the [Sorcerous Sodality](../wc-sorcerous-sodality/summary.md) and the [Harpers](../harpers/summary.md), believing these organizations possess the magical and espionage tools necessary to pull off such a grand illusion and are therefore the conspiracy's architects. They view the [Unchained / K'liir](../unchained/summary.md) Githyanki with terror, seeing them as the cabal's secret police.
