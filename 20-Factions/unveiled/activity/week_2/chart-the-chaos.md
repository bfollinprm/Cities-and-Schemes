# Chart the Chaos

[Xylia](../../people/xylia.md) and her pact-paladin cell run an investigation through the [Wyrm's Crossing protest](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_2/the-call-for-martyrs.md) hunting for evidence of Harper mind-control magic — a doppelganger psyop, by their reading. They get nothing. ['Mad' Meggan's](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) [ringside cultist confession](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md) and [the Ember's](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) protest-ending song ought to be the cleanest evidence the conspiracy exists; the Unveiled instead conclude their adversary's methods must be more subtle than they had assumed and pivot the cell toward studying [the Sodality's new scrying censers](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_2/trace-the-arcane-signature.md).
