# The Great Escape

[Xylia](../../people/xylia.md), with [her last surviving operatives lost in the cellar-grate ambush during last week's prison break](../../../unveiled/activity/week_4/the-prison-break.md), refuses to accept that the captured Unveiled in [Heapside Prison](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) are forfeit. She organizes a second attempt with the very last of her people — a four-strong cell of pre-Crisis warlock-pact magicians who know the [Lower City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/README.md) drainage system better than the Watch ever has. The plan exploits a service tunnel from a Heapside coal-chute through to the prison's eastern wall. Patches of the route are already worked. The plan is not yet executed at week's end — the cell is still surveying floodgate timing — but **The Unveiled have committed their last operational reserve to the rescue**. The cult cannot survive another loss; if the second break fails, Xylia is alone with a bound book and a memory of a better century. Any successful operation will return three of the Unveiled's last spell-trained members to the city — a cult that, this week, looks closer to extinction than it has in a decade.
