# The Safehouse Shuffle

Fresh from [last week's Heapside prison break](../../activity/week_5/the-great-escape.md), [Xylia](../../people/xylia.md) and her cell work frantically to keep the rescued operatives one step ahead of the Fist dragnet. The four "truth-seekers" — would-be paladins recruited on the doppelganger-conspiracy belief, wanted now for both the original arrests and the violent jailbreak — are moved between three Undercity safehouses on a rotating schedule using the city's pre-Crisis smuggling tunnels. Xylia personally walks each shift change. The cell's three remaining external operatives run distraction patterns above ground.

The shuffle works. By Tendays-end the Fist's dragnet has lost the trail entirely, **the rescued comrades are alive and underground**, and Xylia has mapped a working network of three reliable Undercity-tunnel hops between safehouses she trusts. The price is exhaustion. The Unveiled have spent every favor and every hidden cache on keeping their people alive for one more week, and they have produced no propaganda, no investigation, no new public exposure of the doppelganger conspiracy they exist to expose. **They have survived without growing.** [The Plague's false-flag attack at the Sow & Piglet](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_6/the-false-flag.md), accidentally, becomes the cover that lets the Unveiled blend back into the noise. *And the new editorial line at the Baldur's Mouth, when [Ettvard Needle goes missing at week's end](../../../../50-Sessions/session_27/player_summary.md), will start naming the Unveiled by name.*
