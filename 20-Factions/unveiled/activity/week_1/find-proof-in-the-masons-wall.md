# Find 'Proof' in the Masons' Wall

[Xylia's](../../people/xylia.md) conspiracists pick over the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) wall collapse and declare — to their own satisfaction — that the way the stones fell proves a "psionic resonance weapon" being tested by the imagined "Shapeshifter cabal" that supposedly engineered the Absolute Crisis. Pamphlets go out in [Heapside](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) and [Tumbledown](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md). The "evidence" is nonsense, but it muddies the [Harpers'](../../../harpers/summary.md) and [Masons'](../../../wc-masons-sculptors/summary.md) competing narratives further.
