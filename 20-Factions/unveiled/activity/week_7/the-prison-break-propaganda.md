# The Prison Break Propaganda

Following [last week's safehouse-shuffle](../../activity/week_6/the-safehouse-shuffle.md) that kept the cell intact, [Xylia](../../people/xylia.md) commits the Unveiled to a *visible* propaganda push. Her runners distribute pamphlets through every Lower City alley and Sow's Foot doss-house claiming the recent **Bhaalist murders are a false flag** orchestrated by [Archduke Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) to justify martial law and the Sembian mercenary deployment.

The pamphlets cite the *Tragus Ballus* killing the Unveiled has long held to be a doppelganger demonstration; in this telling, the Bhaalist cell on Dusthawk Hill never existed and **the party were Council assets staged as adventurers** to deliver a fake victory to a fake threat. The narrative is internally consistent and almost entirely wrong, but in a Lower City that has watched the [puppet *Baldur's Mouth* go silent](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_7/the-puppet-press.md) and [the Council fire on its own civilians](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_7/break-the-buffer.md), it lands. By week's end the Unveiled has tripled its quiet membership in [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) — and it has irretrievably misframed the party's actual victory at [Dusthawk Hill](../../../../50-Sessions/session_26/player_summary.md).
