# The Prison Break

[Xylia](../../people/xylia.md), trying to recover the two Unveiled members caught in [last week's Wyrm's Rock infiltration fiasco](../../../unveiled/activity/week_3/the-puppet-marshal.md), authorizes a desperate prison break at Heapside Prison in [the Lower City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md). Her crew uses an old sewer tunnel into the prison cellar — a tunnel the Watch has flagged as a known vulnerability since the Crisis. Watch and Fist guards are *waiting in ambush* at the cellar grate. The entire rescue team is captured without a fight. The number of Unveiled members held in Heapside has now doubled. Xylia's leadership is crippled; she has lost her best operatives twice in two weeks. The cell collapses to a kitchen-table cabal of theorizers without serious field arm.
