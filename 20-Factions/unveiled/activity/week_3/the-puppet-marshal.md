# The Puppet Marshal

[Xylia](../../people/xylia.md) sends a small crew of Unveiled into [Wyrm's Rock Fortress](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md) hunting "proof" that [Marshal Flint](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) is a Harper or patriar puppet — the cult's preferred frame for the Marshal's defiance of [the Archduke](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md). The infiltration is a fiasco. The fortified, paranoid Fist garrison spots the team almost immediately; two are captured and locked in the cells, the rest barely escape. The Unveiled finds nothing — no hidden Harper sigil, no Sembian payroll, no evidence of mind-control — but they have drawn the direct, angry attention of [the Flaming Fist](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_3/circle-the-wagons.md) onto their organization at the worst possible moment, while the Fist is consolidating. The captured cultists are now leverage Flint can spend at [the Council](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_3/the-vote-of-no-confidence.md) on his own terms.
