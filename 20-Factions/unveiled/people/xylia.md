# Xylia, the Seeker of the Hidden Sky

A former refugee who survived the chaos of the nautiloid attack by hiding in the sewers. She is now an Oath of Conspiracy paladin and the founder of [The Unveiled](../summary.md).

## Conspiracy theory

Xylia believes mind flayers and the Netherbrain were merely props — illusions or bio-engineered weapons. The real masterminds, in her telling, are a secret society of doppelgangers and elder warlocks who have replaced key city leaders. Their goal: manufacture a crisis so terrifying that the people would *beg* for salvation, willingly accepting the "Great Bargain" and the erosion of their freedoms.

In her reading:

- The heroes who "saved" the city were either unknowing pawns or high-level members of the conspiracy.
- The Githyanki are not here to hunt mind flayers; they are here to silence anyone who gets too close to the truth about the "hoax."
- The lingering magical disturbances and psychic phenomena aren't after-effects — they are ongoing psy-ops to keep the populace scared and compliant.

## Backstory

Xylia was a common merchant from the city's outskirts. Her family was killed in the initial panic of the Absolute Crisis. Hiding for weeks in the sewers, she witnessed things that didn't add up:

- "Citizens" who moved with unsettling coordination.
- "Cultists" whose bodies dissolved into grey goo when unobserved.
- Flaming Fist officers who seemed to deliberately guide people *toward* danger.

She emerged from hiding with the conviction that what the *Baldur's Mouth* reported and what she had seen were two different realities, and swore an oath to the "Truth Unseen" to expose the architects of the lie.
