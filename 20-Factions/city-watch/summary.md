# The City Watch *(of Waterdeep)*

Waterdeep's civilian police force. Walking patrols cover every ward, in green-and-black livery, under the nominal command of the [Open Lord](../masked-lords/people/laeral-silverhand.md). Distinct from the [City Guard](../city-guard/summary.md) (the standing military) and from Baldur's Gate's [Watch](../watch/summary.md).

## Remit

Public order, day-to-day policing, response to street crime. Walking patrols everywhere except the [Field Ward](../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/field-ward/README.md) (which they will not enter) and the [Dock Ward](../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/dock-ward/character.md) (where they patrol in heavily armed groups, never alone). Generally respected; the city likes them better than any other force.

## Structure

Career officers and rank-and-file watchmen drawn from middle-class Waterdhavian families. Sergeants and captains rotate through ward postings; senior commanders report up through the Castle Ward chain to Castle Waterdeep, where Watch and Guard share an administrative spine.

## Current state

- **Outmatched in the Shadow War.** The dossier is explicit: the Watch is ill-equipped to contain the [Zhentarim](../zhentarim/summary.md) / [Xanathar Guild](../xanathars-guild/summary.md) street war, and everyone on the force knows it.
- **Compromised in the Dock Ward.** [Nihiloor's](../xanathars-guild/people/nihiloor.md) intellect-devourer puppet network is deployed across the ward. A common Watch sergeant in this part of the city may not actually be himself.
- **Magister overlap.** The [Magisters](../masked-lords/summary.md) interpret and enforce city law; the Watch executes their warrants. The line between policing and judicial enforcement is thin and getting thinner under crisis.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Patrons.** The [Open Lord](../masked-lords/people/laeral-silverhand.md) on paper; the [Masked Lords](../masked-lords/summary.md) and Magisters in practice.

**Friction.** The [City Guard](../city-guard/summary.md) (jurisdictional — Watch polices inside the walls, Guard mans them); [Force Grey](../force-grey/summary.md) (the Blackstaff's elite reserve, increasingly running missions the Watch is not briefed on).
