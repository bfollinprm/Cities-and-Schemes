# Lobby for 'Heritage Preservation' Laws

[Lord Corin Durinbold](../../people/corin-durinbold.md) brings his "Heritage Preservation Act" — a bundle of patriar-favorable property and inheritance protections — to the floor of [Parliament](../../../parliament-of-peers/summary.md). The unified [Peerage of Coin](../../../peerage-of-coin/summary.md) bloc, leveraged by every Kontor in the city, kills it on the day. The defeat is large enough to read publicly as a generational shift; the patriars' political ceiling now sits well below where it was a year ago.
