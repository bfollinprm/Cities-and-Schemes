# The Ultimatum to the Duke

Emboldened by [the Officers' Mess bribery network already paralyzing the Fist](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_7/the-paralyzed-patrols.md), the Peerage of Blood files a **formal ultimatum** at the High Hall demanding [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) **step down from the Council of Four**. The ultimatum names her Sembian mercenary deployment an *illegal foreign occupation force* under Article XII of the Charter and demands her replacement by a regent of patriar blood within thirty days.

The text — drafted in Durinbold's parlour and signed by twenty-three Blood peers — is read aloud at the open session to a packed gallery of Manorborn observers. It is the **first formal succession demand** since the post-Crisis charter was sealed. Silvershield does not attend; her seat sits empty as the speaker reads. The vote to forward the ultimatum to the Council fails by four — but it would have passed had [the Bathhouse Brawl](../../../parliament-of-peers/activity/week_7/the-bathhouse-brawl.md) not poisoned the cross-aisle Coin moderates the night before.
