# A Charity of Spite

[Lord Corin Durinbold](../../people/corin-durinbold.md) marshals the Peerage of Blood vote against [the Peerage of Coin's motion to hire foreign mercenaries](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_2/legislate-aggression.md) to break the Free Trader siege — and frames the killing of the bill not as legislative obstruction but as a defense of Baldurian sovereignty against "rented foreign steel." The motion fails. Patriar voters at his [Heritage Society gala](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_2/empower-the-old-guard.md) the same week swallow it as principled. Parliament is now in formal gridlock and the Free Traders' siege holds for a second week.
