# The Letter of Censure

Frustrated by their inability to stop [the Archduke's unilateral deployment](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md), and outvoted on [the Emergency Commerce Protection Act](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_4/the-emergency-levy.md), [the Peerage of Blood](../../summary.md) under [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../people/corin-durinbold.md) drafts and passes a formal **Letter of Censure** against Lyra Silvershield. It carries no legal force. It carries every other kind. It is read aloud on the High Hall floor and reprinted in full on the front page of [the Baldur's Mouth](../../../baldurs-mouth/summary.md) the following morning. The Letter accuses the Archduke of standing up a private army on city soil, billing the city for its upkeep, and granting it [Sembian-laundered legal cover](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_4/legitimizing-the-contract.md) — and demands her resignation. She does not resign. The patriar bloc has put its grievance on the historical record while the streets fill with mercenary boots.
