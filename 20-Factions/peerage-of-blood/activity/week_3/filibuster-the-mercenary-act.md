# Filibuster the Mercenary Act

[Lord Corin Durinbold](../../people/corin-durinbold.md) burns the floor at [High Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md) with a sustained filibuster against the proposal to publicly fund foreign mercenaries to break the Free Trader siege. He frames the bill as "rented foreign steel" against Baldurian sovereignty, and walks the [Peerage of Coin's whip count](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_3/whip-the-votes.md) onto the rocks. The bill dies, [the no-confidence motion against Flint dies with it](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_3/the-vote-of-no-confidence.md), and the public victory is real — but [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) now circumvents Parliament entirely and [hires the mercenaries on her own coin](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_3/the-private-option.md). Durinbold has won the chamber and lost the city: the Archduke now fields a private army with no accountability to the High Hall.
