# The Propaganda Campaign

[Lord Corin Durinbold](../../people/corin-durinbold.md), having [opened the Loyalty Fund last week to buy Fist enlisted ranks one ribbon at a time](../../../patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_4/the-loyalty-fund.md), takes the next step. From [Bloomridge Park Manor](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/bloomridge.md) — leased through a cousin so the press-runs cannot be traced to Durinbold money — he commissions thousands of pamphlets slandering [the Archduke's company](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md) as **"Silvershield's Foreign Thugs"**. The text is calibrated: nativist rhetoric for the Outer City, atrocity-rumor for the Lower, and a warning to patriars that *foreign mercenaries answer to no Baldurian assembly*. Pamphlet-runners hit the Wide, the Wyrm's Bend Market, the Phoenix Gate, the gates of every Worshipful Company hall before dawn. By midweek the Coin-bloc collectors trying to enforce [the Emergency Tax](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_5/the-emergency-tax.md) are being read their own neighbours' verdict on the Archduke's army out of a folded sheet from the gutter. The Peerage of Blood has not yet brought the Archduke down. They have ensured that **every street they govern hates her army before it ever has to fight**.
