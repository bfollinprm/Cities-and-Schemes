# Lord Corin Durinbold

Patriar of House Durinbold and the leading public voice of the [Peerage of Blood](../summary.md). Operates through procedural mastery and patriar solidarity rather than personal charisma.

## Role

Durinbold's signature move is the loophole. He found the obscure procedural fault in the city charter that let him table the **Hinterland Logging Expansion Charter** for "further review," delaying it indefinitely and dealing a public blow to Archduke [Lyra Silvershield](../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md). He has used the same tactic to:

- Block the Amnian Kontor's **New Kontor Wayleave** proposal in the Peerage.
- File an injunction to freeze the [Amnian Kontor](../../amnian-kontor/summary.md)'s sale of the foreclosed Hlath Estate to one of Athar's allies, citing an obscure ancient property law.

His attempt to publicly sponsor the dispossessed Hlath family failed — Amnian agents intercepted them first and bought (or intimidated) them into refusing his aid, making him look outmaneuvered.

He commissions ostentatious gifts to cement alliances: the **gold-and-silver canary automaton** crafted for him by the [Worshipful Company of Goldsmiths & Jewelers](../../wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/summary.md) was meant to solidify the conservative-guild–patriar axis.

## Knights of the Shield

The [Knights of the Shield](../../knights-of-the-shield/summary.md) tried to recruit a *dispossessed* patriar (Lord Hlath, not Durinbold) and failed when he balked at the infernal oaths. Durinbold himself is a known traditionalist who finds the Knights' diabolism repugnant, and was anonymously tipped off about their resurgence.
