# House Jannath

One of the oldest and wealthiest Baldurian patriar families. Listed alongside **Durinbold**, **Hlath**, and **Oberon** as the inner ring of the [Patriar Heritage Society](../../patriar-heritage-society/summary.md).

## What the campaign references

- **Heritage Society core.** Jannath money documented as flowing into the **Silver Fist Fund** (the loyalty purse for Flaming Fist sergeants) alongside Durinbold and Oberon contributions, before the Hlath foreclosure complicated the donor list.
- **Baldurian Purity Gala.** Jannath are routine attendees and underwriters.
- **The Marshal Flint endorsement.** When the full social weight of the Heritage Society — Durinbold, Jannath, Oberon, others — visibly backed Marshal Flint as the *symbol of the old Baldurian order*, Jannath was one of the names making it stick.

Influence runs through ancient names, dwindling fortunes, and deep institutional connections — not subscription rolls.
