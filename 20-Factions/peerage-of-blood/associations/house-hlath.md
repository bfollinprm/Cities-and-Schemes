# House Hlath

One of the inner-ring Baldurian patriar families — listed with **Durinbold**, **Jannath**, and **Oberon** as the historical core of the [Patriar Heritage Society](../../patriar-heritage-society/summary.md).

## Recent collapse

- **Foreclosure.** Hlath has lost its Silver Fist Fund standing as a documented donor — the foreclosure cut the contributions off. The Heritage Society's donor pages now list Hlath as past-tense.
- **The sponsorship loss.** Outmaneuvered Durinbold on the Hlath sponsorship — the Heritage Society's primary nativist target was, briefly, Hlath itself. The story behind that defeat is a working scandal in the Peerage of Blood and elsewhere.

The family still appears in the Heritage Society's social architecture as a name; the money is gone.
