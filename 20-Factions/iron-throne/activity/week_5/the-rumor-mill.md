# The Rumor Mill

With [the Rivington Recovery Group front company outed in the Baldur's Mouth last week](../../../iron-throne/activity/week_4/the-rivington-recovery-group.md) and the underworld watching to see whether the old coster sinks, [the Anonymous Throne's](../../people/the-anonymous-throne.md) agents pivot away from front-companies entirely. The new product is *information*. Throne brokers working the [Blushing Mermaid](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md) tap the network: retired Fist quartermasters, dock-side measure-takers, a pamphlet-runner with a hangover, a Sembian clerk with a debt. By midweek the Throne has assembled a saleable dossier on [the Archduke's mercenary company](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md) — numbers, leadership, [supply-line routing through the Iron Ledger](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_5/arming-the-aggressors.md), barricade plans, the names of three lieutenants and what they drink. The dossier is sold *twice*. Once to a [Mad Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) lieutenant in Wyrm's Bend; once to a [Guild](../../../guild/people/astele-keene.md) cut-out in Brampton. The two purchasers do not know about each other yet. The Throne's coin position has not been this strong in two years. **The pivot from front-company to information-brokerage is now the durable Iron Throne strategy.**
