# The Double Cross

Playing both sides — a logical extension of [last week's pivot from front-company to information broker](../../activity/week_5/the-rumor-mill.md) — Iron Throne agents working out of [the Blushing Mermaid](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md) sell **detailed maps of the Sembian mercenary patrol routes** to the [Free Traders](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) at the Basilisk Gate camp. At the same time, through a different intermediary at a different table on a different night, the same agents sell **the Free Traders' weakest barricade points** to the Sembian commanders at the Eastway barracks.

The double-handed sale is profitable on three ledgers: the Free Traders pay 1,200 gp; the Sembians pay 2,000 gp; and the Iron Throne keeps the trust of the Blushing Mermaid as a "neutral" information venue both sides will keep visiting. The intent — Anonymous Throne is unsentimental about it — is *maximum casualties and chaos when the lines next collide*, which sells more information at higher rates afterward. **The remnant coster is now actively engineering the violence it profits from.** The party have heard the table-rumors at the Plowman; whether they pull on the thread depends on what kind of week it has already been.
