# The High-Risk Wager

[The Iron Throne's anonymous leadership](../../people/the-anonymous-throne.md) bids for a high-risk caravan-guard contract for the [Western Gate Trading Company](../../../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) running the new logging routes through the wilderness outside Baldur's Gate. They are underbid by a new, independent adventuring company — the same celebrity adventurers the city is now talking about (the party). The Coster's bid for relevance fails again; their margins thin further with the [Free Trader siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md) closing the gate they normally route through.
