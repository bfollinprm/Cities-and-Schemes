# Broker Risky Undercity Expedition

The remnant Iron Throne fronts an adventuring crew for a paying client — a desperate merchant chasing a family heirloom in the [Undercity](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/undercity/README.md). The expedition is wiped out by a [Guild](../../../guild/summary.md) patrol that was clearly tipped off. The heirloom is lost, the merchant is ruined, and the Throne loses both its investment and a measurable chunk of underworld credibility.
