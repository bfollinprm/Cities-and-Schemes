# The Rivington Recovery Group

To launder [last week's distressed-debt acquisitions](../../../iron-throne/activity/week_3/scavenge-the-scraps.md) into a respectable operating company, [the Anonymous Throne](../../people/the-anonymous-throne.md) directs an agent to incorporate the **Rivington Recovery Group** out of an office on the [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) high street. The paperwork is a rush job. An inquisitive clerk at the Seatower of Balduran cross-references the listed officers against the city's old Iron Throne files and recognizes three names. He leaks the lot to [the Baldur's Mouth](../../../baldurs-mouth/summary.md) — which, [busy fending off counter-evidence on its own mercenary story](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-menace.md), runs the leak in the Lower City pages within a week. The Recovery Group is publicly outed as an Iron Throne front; [the Flaming Fist](../../../flaming-fist/summary.md) and [the Guild](../../../guild/summary.md) both file the company's address for surveillance. The Throne's quiet land grab is now unquietly on every faction's books.
