# The Sabotage Brokerage

Following [last week's double-cross at the Blushing Mermaid](../../activity/week_6/the-double-cross.md), [the Anonymous Throne's](../../people/the-anonymous-throne.md) agents move from selling *patrol routes* to selling **architectural plans** — the detailed schematics of [the Free Trader Basilisk Gate barricades](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_5/heed-the-ultimatum.md), including the [vine-knit reinforcements the Druids added this week](../../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_7/vine-barricades.md), and crucially the storm-aqueduct map the [Free Trader smuggling network](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_7/smuggling-the-crumbs.md) is using to feed itself.

The Sembian commanders pay an enormous sum — three thousand gold in mixed coin, settled in a back room at [the Blushing Mermaid](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md) — for the full set. The Throne is now *literally on both sides of the siege*, providing the assault party with the breach map and the defenders with the patrol intelligence to mount a counter-ambush. The coster ends the week solvent for the first time since [Brindlestone fell](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_6/the-hostile-takeover.md) and as institutionally amoral as it has ever been.
