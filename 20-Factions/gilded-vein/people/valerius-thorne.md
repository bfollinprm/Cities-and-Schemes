# Valerius Thorne, "the Auditor"

Founder and leader of [The Gilded Vein](../summary.md). A rising star in the Gold-Gable Hall before his proposal to intentionally bankrupt several Baldurian guilds — to consolidate Amnian power — was deemed too aggressive, and he was exiled.

## Manifesto

> Observe this city. It mistakes frantic motion for health. It celebrates the chaotic pulse of supply and demand, of greed and fear. This is not strength; it is a fever. Commerce is a machine, and it has been left in the hands of poets and gamblers. We are the auditors of this failed system. We will strip the gears of their sentiment.

## Backstory

Humiliated by his exile, Thorne came to believe his colleagues' hesitation was the failure — not his proposal. He founded the Gilded Vein to pursue, by other means, the perfect predictable market he had been denied. His worship of Axiom is genuine; the assassinations are auditable.
