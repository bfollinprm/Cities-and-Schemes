# Poisoning the Well

[Valerius Thorne's](../../people/valerius-thorne.md) attempt to deepen the [Chionthar–Amnian trade war](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_3/identify-the-assassins-patron.md) by sabotaging a Consortium grain shipment at the Chionthar warehouses in [Gray Harbour](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) backfires. The Vein agent is clumsy and leaves behind a vial of the unique alchemical compound used to spoil the grain. The Consortium's own [investigation team](../../../chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md), already on alert after [last week's Vein framing letter](../../../gilded-vein/activity/week_3/twist-the-knife.md), traces the substance not to Amn but to a formula fingerprint known to be used by underworld alchemists associated with Thorne himself. The Vein's involvement is exposed for the first time. The Amnian Kontor learns it was being framed; the Consortium learns the same. Both factions are *infuriated* — and now, separately, looking for Thorne. The Lower City finance war has gained a third visible combatant.
