# Manipulate Grain Futures

[Valerius Thorne's](../../people/valerius-thorne.md) cell forges shipping manifests to spike grain futures in the Counting House — a textbook Axiom-doctrine "purifying chaos" play. An alert clerk spots inconsistencies in the forgeries and raises an alarm. The [Sembian Kontor](../../../sembian-kontor/summary.md), already in hostile reactive posture from the Chionthar storage gouge, jumps to release stabilizing statements. The plot collapses, costing the Gilded Vein significant capital and exposing the forgery method to both the Sembian and Counting House intelligence apparatuses.
