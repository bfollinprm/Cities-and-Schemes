# The Liquidated Assets

With [their safehouses raided last week](../../activity/week_5/go-to-ground.md) and the cell scattered, [Valerius Thorne](../../people/valerius-thorne.md) orders the **emergency liquidation of all remaining cult assets**. Agents race across the Lower City to pull funds from the Gilded Vein's seventeen dummy accounts — names, ledgers, blind trustees, all designed to look like legitimate small-trader businesses — before [the Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) and [Roah Moonglow's Counting House inserts](../../../zhentarim/activity/week_6/the-ledger-logic.md) can freeze them.

It is a chase through the financial district. Five accounts are pulled successfully (a total of 9,500 gp); four are frozen mid-withdrawal when a Counting House clerk recognizes a Gilded Vein cipher and locks the till; eight are sealed before the agents even arrive. The chase ends in three near-arrests, two outright street fights with Consortium house-thugs, and one Gilded Vein agent killed in an alley after losing the runner with a Consortium tail. **The cult salvages a third of its emergency reserve.** Two-thirds is gone. Thorne goes quietly insane in his shuttered Bloomridge office that evening — *the system that was supposed to be the Gilded Vein's god is now eating it from inside, and the chaos he engineered is now his own bankruptcy.*
