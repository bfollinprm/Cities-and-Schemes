# Accelerate the Crash

[Valerius Thorne's](../../people/valerius-thorne.md) Vein assassinates a [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) factor — found dead in a Lower City counting-room with the books open. The Vein simultaneously plants rumors at the [Iron Ledger](../../../sembian-kontor/summary.md) and a few key Lower-City coffeehouses naming the [Amnian Kontor](../../../amnian-kontor/summary.md) as the paymaster, exploiting the still-unsourced [anti-Amnian propaganda](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_2/the-propaganda-war.md) already in circulation. River-trade insurance premiums rise sharply; both Kontors lose a week of margin to the Vein's frame.
