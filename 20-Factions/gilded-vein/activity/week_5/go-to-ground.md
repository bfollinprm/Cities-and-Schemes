# Go to Ground

[Valerius Thorne](../../people/valerius-thorne.md), having ordered the Vein to dissolve into the populace after [last week's grain-warehouse alchemical fingerprint exposed him to both the Amnians and the Consortium](../../../gilded-vein/activity/week_4/poisoning-the-well.md), discovers the order has already failed before it can be executed. A disgruntled former member — humiliated by Thorne months earlier and waiting for the moment to bite back — sells the locations of three primary safehouses to the [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) in the same week the Consortium [is being financially bled by the Amnian Kontor](../../../amnian-kontor/activity/week_5/retaliatory-asset-seizure.md). Errinthal needs a public win and takes it. Consortium thugs raid all three safehouses inside ten hours. The Vein's accountant-assassins are scattered into the [Undercity](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/undercity/README.md) without supplies or coordinated communication; **the cult's entire accumulated treasury is taken or dispersed**. Two senior cell-leaders are dead in their beds. Thorne escapes through a sluice he had drilled his lieutenants on. By week's end, *the Gilded Vein has no safehouses, no funds, and no leadership in proximity to one another* — just Thorne, alone, in a forgotten Undercity vault, planning revenge with a blackened ledger.
