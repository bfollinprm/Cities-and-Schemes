# Twist the Knife

[Valerius Thorne's](../../people/valerius-thorne.md) operatives, riding [last week's framed assassination](../activity/week_2/accelerate-the-crash.md), draft a second package of forged Amnian-Kontor letters of credit and target a senior Chionthar factor at the [Counting House](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/counting-house.md) for a personal sting designed to break the Consortium's internal trust in any Amn-issued paper. The factor — older, more experienced, hardened by [last week's murder](../../../chionthar-consortium/activity/week_3/secure-the-front-lines.md) — notices subtle inconsistencies in the wax seals and pattern of approvals. He does not arrest. He alerts [Factor Errinthal](../../../chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md), who quietly tightens internal security and adds a third party — neither [Sembian](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_3/the-alternate-route.md) nor Amnian — to the suspicion list. The Vein has not made the Consortium more aggressive against Amn; it has made the Consortium **harder to deceive**, the opposite of the desired outcome. Thorne adjusts the playbook. The next forgery will need to read like an internal Consortium product.
