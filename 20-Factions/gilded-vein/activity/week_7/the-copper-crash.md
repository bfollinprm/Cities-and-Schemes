# The Copper Crash

[Valerius Thorne](../../people/valerius-thorne.md) — bleeding cult capital after [the emergency liquidation](../../activity/week_6/the-liquidated-assets.md) — orders the surviving funds **converted into high-quality forged copper pieces** and pumped into Lower City markets to artificially trigger a localized economic panic. His agents push the forgeries into pay-windows, day-laborer queues, refugee-bread lines, and Sow & Piglet round-buys for a full tenday, scaling up each evening.

By the fifth day the Lower City retail price of copper has dropped 18% against silver; by the seventh, three Counting House clerks refuse copper entirely from the Lower City pay window, which causes a small riot at the wage queue. *Axiom is purified through the localized chaos.* The cult ends the week ahead of where it began the tenday in spiritual currency, even though the Vein's monetary reserves are nearly gone. The economic "wound" they wanted to inflict on a single Counting House market has spread to the entire Lower City retail economy. Thorne considers this Axiom-blessed.
