# The Gilded Vein

A cult of economic assassins that believes the city's chaotic post-crisis market is a fever to be cured by violence. Founded and led by [Valerius Thorne, "the Auditor"](people/valerius-thorne.md), a disgraced former banker from the [Amnian Kontor](../amnian-kontor/summary.md), exiled for proposing a plan too aggressive even for his superiors. They worship "Axiom," a personified god of pure logical commerce.

## Ideology

The city's prosperity is a chaotic, sentimental mess. Competition, booms, and busts are impure variables. By surgically removing the most unpredictable and powerful market players, the Vein can induce a market crash so severe that survivors will beg for the cold logical stability only the Vein's system can provide. Every assassinated consortium head is a "removed variable."

## Membership

Disenfranchised financial agents, disgraced patriar accountants, and alchemists obsessed with the symbolic power of gold. Recruitment is selective; new members must demonstrate both financial sophistication and the willingness to murder a colleague.

## Methods

- Highly trained assassins targeting consortium heads, Kontor directors, and Guild moneylenders.
- Alchemist-forged "cursed currency" that spreads misfortune.
- Blackmail and extortion to gain leverage before the kill.
- Pigeon-loft poisoning. Week 0: a cultist assigned to poison [Western Gate Trading Company](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) carrier pigeons was caught by a stable hand and identified, costing the Vein the element of surprise on that target.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** Every other financial power is ultimately a target. Primary marks: the [Amnian Kontor](../amnian-kontor/summary.md) (Thorne's exile), the [Chionthar Consortium](../chionthar-consortium/summary.md) (powerful local bloc), the [Western Gate Trading Company](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) (also a Vein target as of week 0).
