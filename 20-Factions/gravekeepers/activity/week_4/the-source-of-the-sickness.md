# The Source of the Sickness

Following [the necromantic-seepage map they completed last week](../../../gravekeepers/activity/week_3/a-pattern-in-the-rot.md), [Old Man Hemlock's](../../people/hemlock.md) keepers descend the sewers beneath [Tumbledown](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md) and into the [Undercity tunnels](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/undercity/README.md). At the bottom of a half-flooded shaft they find what they came for: a **hidden Bhaalist temple**, walls slick with congealed offerings, four sewer altars in side chapels, the unmistakable sigil of [the Cult of the Returning Lord](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/summary.md) carved into the keystone. The unholy pressure of the rituals above is what is making the dead in Tumbledown stir. Hemlock orders no engagement — the temple is too well-built and the cell almost certainly active — but the location is logged, sketched, and reported up: to [the Watch](../../../watch/summary.md), to [the Hellriders](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md), and to a Harper contact. The intelligence pairs uncomfortably well with [the ritual dagger recovered above ground](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_4/an-offering-in-the-dark.md).
