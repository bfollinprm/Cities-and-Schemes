# Plan the Cleansing

[Old Man Hemlock](../../people/hemlock.md), proceeding from [the Tumbledown sewer temple discovery last week](../../../gravekeepers/activity/week_4/the-source-of-the-sickness.md), assembles the materials for a cleansing strike on the Bhaalist sanctum. Vial after vial of consecrated water is cured beneath [Candulhallow's Tombstones](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md); silver pellet shot is cast in moulds; the protective wards are blessed at the chapel altar. The work takes weeks of patient ritual. Two things happen this week to ruin it. First: the holy-water supply is found, on the morning of the planned descent, contaminated with **necrotic residue** — the necromantic seepage from the very temple they intended to cleanse has fouled their cellar's well. Weeks of consecration are unusable. Second: their scout reports the cult's hidden temple has *already been moved* — empty altars, scrubbed sigils, [the Bhaalists routed back to the sewers by the Sow's Foot butcher mob](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_5/relocate-the-sanctum.md). **The Gravekeepers' intelligence is obsolete and their consecrated water is poison.** Hemlock spends the rest of the week in the chapel, calmly, beginning the cleansing ritual cycle from the start.
