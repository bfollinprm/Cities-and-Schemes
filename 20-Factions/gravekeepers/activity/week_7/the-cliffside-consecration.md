# The Cliffside Consecration

Having [moved the order's stockpile of consecrated corpses to Cliffside Cemetery in silence](../../activity/week_6/the-corpse-caravan.md), [Old Man Hemlock](../../people/hemlock.md) opens a **mass consecration ritual** on the cliff face: a holy perimeter of bound undead guardians, faceless and silent, ringing the Cliffside burial terraces and aimed outward at the [Bhaalist remnants in Sow's Foot](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_7/blood-in-the-slaughterhouse.md).

The ritual takes four nights. By the fifth dawn Cliffside is a **walled crypt** in the literal sense: every legitimate funeral procession passes through a row of saluting bone-honor-guards before reaching the family vault. Hemlock's gambit is theological: by *publicly* opposing the Bhaalists with publicly-blessed undead, he is staking the Gravekeepers' claim to be the city's official cult-defense order before the [Hellriders](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) or the [Lathanderites](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) can occupy the niche. *The Gravekeepers are choosing visibility.* They will pay for it later.

The party — having [navigated Tumbledown's gravedigger patrols this session](../../../../50-Sessions/session_28/player_summary.md) under cover of a Guild coin — has direct firsthand experience of how heavily Hemlock's order now patrols the burial districts.
