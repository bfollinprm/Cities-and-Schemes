# Secure the Sepulchers

[Old Man Hemlock](../../people/hemlock.md) catches an undead manifestation in the [Tumbledown](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md) cemetery early — likely a knock-on effect of the [Plague of Remembrance laboratory destroyed under Rivington in week 1](../../../party/activity/week_1/destroy-the-plague-fathers-laboratory.md), still venting psychic residue through the connected sewer network. He puts it down quietly, repairs the wards in the Rivington sewer entrances, and lets nothing reach the public ear. The graveyard holds.
