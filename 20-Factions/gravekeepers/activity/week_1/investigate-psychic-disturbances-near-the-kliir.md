# Investigate Psychic Disturbances near the K'liir

[Hemlock](../../people/hemlock.md) puts a Gravekeeper team on the psychic ripples coming out of the tenements neighboring the [K'liir](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/the-kliir.md) Githyanki camp. The [Hands of the Absolute](../../../hands-of-the-absolute/summary.md) cell is well-hidden and their rituals leave no physical trace; after several nights of fruitless search the team writes the disturbance off as a passing echo from the [Unchained's](../../../unchained/summary.md) own meditations. The Hands keep working, unobserved.
