# A Pattern in the Rot

After [last week's Tumbledown manifestation](../week_2/secure-the-sepulchers.md), [Old Man Hemlock](../../people/hemlock.md) puts every Gravekeeper on a single quiet task: map the recent undead manifestations against the Lower City sewer entrances and map both onto known [Plague-Father](../../../party/activity/week_1/destroy-the-plague-fathers-laboratory.md) lab sites. The pattern is unmistakable. The clusters hug sewer accesses near older burial-ground catchments, particularly under Tumbledown and the old Worm Spit corridor. Hemlock's interpretation is that something is stirring the city's dead from the sewers below — not a single source, but a current of necromantic seepage following the old underground waterways. He drafts a cautious request for [Sodality](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_3/follow-the-resonance.md) coordination and makes a quiet note to keep [Bhaalist activity at the slaughterhouses](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_3/consecrate-the-blockade-with-blood.md) on the same map. The Gravekeepers now have a working hypothesis the Watch does not.
