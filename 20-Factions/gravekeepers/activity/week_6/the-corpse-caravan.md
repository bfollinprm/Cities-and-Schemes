# The Corpse Caravan

With [the Tumbledown Bhaalist temple compromised](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_5/relocate-the-sanctum.md) and [the Bhaalists themselves now miscalling rituals in the Undercity](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_6/the-gutter-prayer.md), [Old Man Hemlock](../../people/hemlock.md) cannot leave his order's stockpile of consecrated corpses where it stood. He organizes a **macabre caravan** to move the cult's relic-bodies — three dozen ritually-prepared Gravekeeper-blessed corpses, used in their work to ward and detect Bhaalist necromantic emanations — from Tumbledown to a deeper, harder-to-find vault in the [Cliffside Cemetery](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md).

The move is done in **total silence**. Nine Gravekeepers in oilcloth aprons, two muffled handcarts, no torches, no lanterns. They cross seven blocks of the Lower City sleeping streets between midnight and the false dawn, choosing routes that avoid both the Fist's [now-paralyzed patrol grid](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_6/the-officers-dilemma.md) and the Guild wards. The caravan reaches Cliffside without incident. The corpses are re-consecrated and sealed in a vault behind a double-locked iron grate. **The order's working stockpile is preserved. Hemlock has bought another month.** What he does not know is that one of his apprentices has been seen by [a Sharran scryer](../../../silent-shroud/activity/week_6/the-blind-eye.md) and the Silent Shroud is now logging Cliffside as a place of interest.
