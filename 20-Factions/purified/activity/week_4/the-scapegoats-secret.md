# The Scapegoat's Secret

[The Purified](../../summary.md) catch wind of [the Alchemists' Company's framed apprentice](../../../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_3/find-a-scapegoat.md) and his attempt to barter dangerous formulas for safe passage out of the city. To the Purified, "dangerous alchemical formula" sounds like *another Lantaner pacification compound*. They dispatch a cleansing team to intercept the apprentice and "purify" him before the formulas can spread. They arrive at the Lower City warehouse hours too late: [the apprentice has already made contact with a Guild lieutenant](../../../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_4/a-desperate-bargain.md) and been moved to a secure Guild safehouse. The Purified team retreats with no kill and no formulas, but with an updated list of Guild safehouse hand-signs they did not have last week. [Glim-Glim's](../../people/glim-glim.md) cell is now openly hunting Guild assets in addition to Lantaner ones.
