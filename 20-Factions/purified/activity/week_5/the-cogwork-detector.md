# The Cogwork Detector

[Glim-Glim, "the Clear-Eyed,"](../../people/glim-glim.md) and his Purified followers complete what Glim-Glim has been promising for half a year: the **Cogwork Detector**. It is a fragile device of spinning crystal lenses, alchemically treated copper wires, and three brass tuning forks tied to a small bellows, set up on a workbench in a hidden Whitkeep workshop on the [Outer City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md) edge. The build is sincere; the diagnostics are nonsense. Glim-Glim begins testing the device on samples of city water — barrel-water, well-water, Chionthar river-water, even a flask drawn from a [Sow's Foot](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md) butcher's basin — and watches the lenses spin a pattern he is convinced is the signature of [the Lantaner mind-control plot he has been preaching against for a year](../../../lantaner-concession/summary.md). His followers are radicalized further by every reading. Word travels in Whitkeep that the Purified now have *proof*. The proof is a brass apparatus that responds to a draft and a thumb on the bellows, but it tests every barrel of water that comes through the workshop and produces conviction at the rate of one barrel per Whitkeep household. **The Purified are no longer a fringe — they are a movement with an instrument.**
