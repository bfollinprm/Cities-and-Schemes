# Analyze the 'Cure'

[Glim-Glim, 'The Clear-Eyed'](../../people/glim-glim.md) acquires a sample of the antitoxin distributed at [the Open Hand Temple](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/open-hand-temple.md) during the [Sewer Plague resolution](../../../party/activity/week_1/heist-the-rivington-medicine-cart.md) — the same antitoxin the [Worshipful Company of Alchemists & Apothecaries](../../../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_3/find-a-scapegoat.md) is currently writing a forged paper trail around. He runs a series of bizarre alchemical tests on it from a hidden Whitkeep workshop. The Purified are convinced the "cure" is a more potent vehicle for [Lantaner](../../../lantaner-concession/summary.md) mind-control nano-cogs, and Glim-Glim's tests obediently confirm what they want to find — the antitoxin contains "alien mechanical residue" by his methodology. He has no actual evidence; he has a sealed bottle, a compelling narrative, and a self-confirming test rig. The next move, already drafted, is to send the "evidence" to a sympathetic [Baldur's Mouth](../../../baldurs-mouth/summary.md) freelancer.
