# Purge the Pump Station

**Glim-Glim, "The Clear-Eyed"** — a cult convert from the [Lantaner Concession](../../../lantaner-concession/summary.md) — disables the Rivington water pump station as a "purification" strike: drain the pipes, deny the gnomes their delivery vector, force residents back onto the cult's vials. The act causes a localized water shortage in [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) and [Heapside](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) and is publicly dismissed as random vandalism by the [Watch](../../../watch/summary.md). Within the cult it is treated as a major operational success.
