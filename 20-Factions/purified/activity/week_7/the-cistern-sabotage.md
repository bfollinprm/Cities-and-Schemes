# The Cistern Sabotage

Armed with [the Cogwork Detectors from the water-watch](../../activity/week_6/the-water-watch.md), [Glim-Glim, "the Clear-Eyed"](../../people/glim-glim.md) decides the only purification that will work is **direct alchemical purge**. Three Purified cells move on the **Rivington cisterns** at the third hour of the morning, carrying barrels of caustic-detection acid intended to "burn out the Lantaner plague" they believe is dissolved in the city's drinking water.

Two cells are intercepted by Free Trader water-warden patrols (the [Druids' Vine Barricades](../../../circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_7/vine-barricades.md) released auxiliaries to the cistern detail this week); one cell reaches the secondary cistern at the [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) drinking-station and dumps two of three barrels before they are taken. The cistern is poisoned for **the entire fresh-water draw of southern Rivington for at least a tenday**. Free Trader water-rationing tightens by half. The Purified cell-leaders captured at the cistern's edge swear under interrogation that the Lantaner contamination is *visible to the right eye*; their own cleric reads them as sincere. Glim-Glim escapes through the storm-drains, untouched.
