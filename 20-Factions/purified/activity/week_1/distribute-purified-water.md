# Distribute 'Purified' Water

[Glim-Glim](../../people/glim-glim.md) and his followers move through [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) and [Heapside](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) handing out small vials of "purified" rainwater while warning anyone who'll listen that the city's water is a gnomish mind-control agent seeded by the [Lantaner Concession](../../../lantaner-concession/summary.md). Most of the recipients drink the water, throw the warning away, and tell the story at supper as comedy. The Purified count distribution as a win regardless.
