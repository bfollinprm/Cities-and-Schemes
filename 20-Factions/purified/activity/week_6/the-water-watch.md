# The Water Watch

Armed with [the Cogwork Detector completed last week](../../activity/week_5/the-cogwork-detector.md), [Glim-Glim, "The Clear-Eyed"](../../people/glim-glim.md) and a half-dozen Purified followers set up a permanent vigil at the Basilisk Gate public pumps in the [Outer City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/events.md). The detector — a tin-and-glass contraption that hums and clicks — is jammed against the brass spout of every bucket and barrel filled at the pump. When the device buzzes, Glim-Glim "scans" the citizen filling it and demands they tip the bucket out for the safety of the ward.

The detector buzzes constantly. It has been calibrated, in Glim-Glim's words, *"with the holy paranoia of the displaced."* The first day's "scan" alone causes three fistfights, two bucket-spills, and one woman in tears in the rain because she could not get water for her sick child. Outer City families that have been [rationed by Mad Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_6/rationing-the-hope.md) and [starved by the Archduke](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_6/the-starvation-siege.md) now have to fight Glim-Glim's water militia for the right to drink at the public pump. The panic spreads. **The Purified have moved from a paranoid tract-cell to a public nuisance with a buzzing alchemical device** — and ironically, that visibility provides convenient cover for [the actual Lantaner prototype assembly happening in the Cogwork Quay this week](../../../lantaner-concession/activity/week_6/the-prototype-assembly.md).
