# Glim-Glim, "the Clear-Eyed"

Founder and leader of [The Purified](../summary.md). A gnome tinker and former apprentice from the [Lantaner Concession](../../lantaner-concession/summary.md), exiled for "dangerous and unsubstantiated theories." Now an Oath of Conspiracy paladin sworn to Gond's "Hidden Cog of Truth."

## Conspiracy theory

Smokepowder is a cover story. The Lantaner Concession's true purpose is social engineering. The whirring and clanking from the Cogwork Quay are not invention but mass-grinding of magical metals into nano-cogs — microscopic clockwork machines that regulate emotion and produce compliance when ingested via the water supply. Recent prosperity and social peace are not recovery; they are the conspiracy's success.

## Backstory

As an apprentice, Glim-Glim was tasked with water purification for the Concession. He discovered "anomalous metallic particulates" in the outflow that were not present in the inflow. His master told him it was harmless runoff. Glim-Glim built a special lens and saw, in his telling, impossibly tiny gears. When he presented his findings, he was branded a lunatic, stripped of his tools, and cast out. He swore his oath the same night.
