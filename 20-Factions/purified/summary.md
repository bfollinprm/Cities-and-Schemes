# The Purified

A small, deeply paranoid Conspiracist cell convinced that the [Lantaner Concession](../lantaner-concession/summary.md) is pacifying the city through microscopic clockwork organisms — "nano-cogs" — distributed through the water supply. Founded and led by [Glim-Glim, "the Clear-Eyed"](people/glim-glim.md), a gnome tinker exiled from the Concession for his theories. One of the four Conspiracist cells.

## Ideology

The smokepowder monopoly is a cover story. The Lantaner Concession's real project is mass social engineering: massive grinders breaking magical metals into nano-cogs that, when ingested, regulate emotions and make the populace docile and suggestible. The post-crisis prosperity and relative social peace are not signs of recovery — they are evidence of the conspiracy's success. The Lantaners aren't pursuing technological supremacy; they are creating the perfect compliant workforce for an eventual takeover.

The "Purified" are those who have purged the nano-cogs from their systems and can therefore "see clearly."

## Membership

A handful of exiled gnomes, a few crazed alchemists, and hypochondriac citizens convinced they are being controlled. Membership is small, paranoid, and fractured — anyone who *seems* too at peace is suspected of contamination.

## Methods

- Subsistence on collected rainwater and specially prepared rations.
- Construction of "cog-detectors" from scrap metal and stolen alchemical reagents.
- Sabotage of city water pumps and cisterns. Week 0: a clumsy pump-sabotage attempt was foiled when a [Worshipful Company of Masons & Sculptors](../wc-masons-sculptors/summary.md) Guild Warden investigating the broken pump found strange non-functional gadgets — bringing a hostile guild down on the cell's activities.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** The [Lantaner Concession](../lantaner-concession/summary.md) — absolute. The [Worshipful Company of Alchemists & Apothecaries](../wc-alchemists-apothecaries/summary.md), believed to be in league with the gnomes by providing chemical binders for the nano-cogs. The High House of Wonders (Gond's Temple) as the conspiracy's spiritual center.
