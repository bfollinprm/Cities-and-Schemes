# The Diplomatic Immunity

[Factor Elara Mornwood](../../people/elara-mornwood.md) intervenes when a Harper agent is cornered by a Flaming Fist patrol on the [Upper City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/events.md) streets near the Wide. The agent — almost certainly running orders connected to [Randal Evenwood's Trade Way sabotage](../../../harpers/activity/week_6/the-supply-sabotage.md) — is about to be hauled to a Watchful Shield holding cell. Mornwood arrives in formal Splendid Yard livery with two clerks and **invokes diplomatic immunity**, claiming the agent is a "cultural attaché" of Waterdeep's Lords' Alliance mission to the city.

The Fist sergeants do not have the standing to override a Lords'-Alliance-credentialed Factor in front of a midmorning Upper City crowd. The agent is released into Mornwood's escort and walked, openly, back into the [Splendid Yard](../../summary.md) compound. The intervention works — the Harper escapes — but the **strain on Council relations is immediate**. By afternoon, the High Hall has lodged a formal complaint with Waterdeep's mission asking why an Alliance "cultural attaché" was running in disguise outside Bursar Ironhand's compound. Mornwood signs the diplomatic reply herself, with care. The Lords' Alliance is now publicly engaged on the Harpers' side of the supply war — and Waterdeep has chosen.
