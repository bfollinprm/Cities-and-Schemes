# Acquire 'Lost' Elturian Tomes

[Factor Elara Mornwood](../../people/elara-mornwood.md) uses her agents to "acquire" a set of rare historical texts, supposedly lost during the fall of Elturel, from a struggling refugee scholar in [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) for a pittance. The books are added to the [Splendid Yard](../../summary.md)'s library, a source of bitterness for Elturian traditionalists.
