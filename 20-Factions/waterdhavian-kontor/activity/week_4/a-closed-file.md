# A Closed File

[Factor Elara Mornwood](../../people/elara-mornwood.md), having dispatched [a recruitment agent in week 3](../../../waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_3/an-offer-of-patronage.md) who could not find the party, watches another two material-plane weeks pass with no contact and no sighting. The party went into the Circus of the Last Days and did not come out. Mornwood does what a careful Lords' Alliance asset-handler does: she formally **closes the file** on the party as potential agents, marks the dossier as inactive, and instructs her Splendid Yard staff to identify a new, more reliable group to cultivate. Two clerks immediately suggest a Hellrider veteran's circle. Mornwood does not yet know — none of the Splendid Yard does — that the party is in the Feywild and will be back this week with a forgotten god-fragment in the bag of one of its members.
