# An Offer of Patronage

Building on the [dossier the Kontor bought from the Bibliophile last week](../week_2/profile-the-agitator.md), [Factor Elara Mornwood](../../people/elara-mornwood.md) profiles the party as effective but chaotic free agents and decides to bid for them rather than report on them. She dispatches a discreet senior agent to approach the party (who are at this point hiding in the [Circus of the Last Days](../../../carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_3/sowing-sweet-sorrows.md) and inaccessible) with a clean offer: the [Lords' Alliance](../../summary.md) will use its political influence to clear the [Flaming Fist warrants](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_3/circle-the-wagons.md) on Kaelan and the others in exchange for a long-term retainer of loyalty to Waterdeep. The agent never finds them. By week's end Mornwood is concerned but not yet writing them off — the offer is on the table, the front door at [the Splendid Yard](../../summary.md) is open, and her standing instruction to find new chaotic free agents is, for the moment, on hold.
