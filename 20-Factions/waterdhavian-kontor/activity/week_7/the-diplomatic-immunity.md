# The Diplomatic Immunity

Building on [last tenday's intervention against the Fist's arrest of a Harper agent](../../activity/week_6/the-diplomatic-immunity.md), [Factor Elara Mornwood](../../people/elara-mornwood.md) opens **formal Lords' Alliance negotiations** with [High Watcher Alena](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) at the Splendid Yard's Upper City annexe. Mornwood offers Lords' Alliance logistical support — grain shipments out of Waterdeep, magical antitoxins through the Sodality's northern channels, a Waterdhavian weapons-import exemption — in exchange for a Hellrider commitment to **publicly oppose Sembian influence** inside Baldur's Gate.

The conversations are opaque to outsiders but the body-language at the front gate is unmistakable: Alena's quartermasters and Mornwood's clerks are walking shipping ledgers together for two days running. The Sembian Kontor's intelligence picks the meeting up by the third morning; [Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../../sembian-kontor/people/korgan-ironhand.md) sends a formal Sembian protest to the Council that goes unanswered. **Waterdeep has formally chosen the Hellriders over the Sembian Archduke** in front of every patriar with a window onto the Wide. The Lords' Alliance is making a bid for soft-power dominance of the New Elturel district.
