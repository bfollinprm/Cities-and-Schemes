# Cultivate a New Asset

[Factor Elara Mornwood](../../people/elara-mornwood.md), having [formally closed the file on the party last week](../../../waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_4/a-closed-file.md), instructs her Splendid Yard staff to identify a replacement asset group. Within days her watchers have flagged a small adventuring crew working out of [the Elfsong Tavern](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/elfsong-tavern.md) — a half-elf scout with a Lantaner work-permit, two human hires of demonstrable nerve, a tiefling of unspecified arcane training. The crew is *not* the party Mornwood was watching for two months. It is something newer: discreet, low-profile, willing to take small Lords' Alliance jobs through cut-outs. Mornwood's agents begin the same patient cultivation she had tried with the party — a tip, a small job at decent coin, a shared safe-courier route that costs nothing to the Yard and adds up to a working relationship. Within the week the new crew has run two minor errands without asking why. The party — should they walk back into the Yard's orbit — will discover their replacement is *already operational* in the same city blocks, and that **Mornwood is now actively cultivating a new asset group out of the Elfsong**.
