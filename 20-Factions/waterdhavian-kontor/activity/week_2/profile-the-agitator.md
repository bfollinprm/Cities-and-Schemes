# Profile the Agitator

[Factor Elara Mornwood](../../people/elara-mornwood.md) compiles a clinical preliminary dossier on the emerging adventuring party from her observation post at the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md): effectiveness, mysterious funding (the Gold-Gable Hall has not yet placed the **6,000 gp** in their hands), and disparate allegiances ([Hellriders](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md), [Followers of the Phoenix](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md), [Chionthar Consortium](../../../chionthar-consortium/summary.md), [the Guild](../../../guild/summary.md), and now ['Mad' Meggan](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md)). The dossier — supplemented by [the Bibliophile's intelligence packet](../../../bibliophile/activity/week_2/intelligence-for-sale.md) — goes back to Waterdeep through the Splendid Yard's diplomatic pouch. The Kontor is not yet acting on the party, only noting them.
