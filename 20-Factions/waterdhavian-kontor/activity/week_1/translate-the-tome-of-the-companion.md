# Translate the 'Tome of the Companion'

[Factor Elara Mornwood's](../../people/elara-mornwood.md) scholars begin the slow translation of the *Tome of the Companion*, the binding-text on Elturel's lost celestial guardian — one of the "lost" Elturian volumes the Kontor acquired during week 0. They are not chasing the Companion itself; they are chasing the binding magics. The work is open enough to draw the [Elturel Remembrance Society's](../../../elturel-remembrance-society/summary.md) ire later in the week.
