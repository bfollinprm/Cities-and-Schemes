# Council of Four

The executive authority of Baldur's Gate — a boardroom of ruthless capitalists whose primary concern is the market's health, not the people's welfare. Currently chaired by Archduke [Lyra Silvershield](../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) of the [Western Gate Trading Company](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md).

## Ideology

A city is a business; the citizens are its assets, the guards its security, and the laws its operating procedures. Public works are debated on return on investment, treaties on trade advantage. Compassion is a poor commodity. Tradition is a liability if it does not yield a return.

## Membership

Four Dukes drawn from the most powerful players in the [Parliament of Peers](../parliament-of-peers/summary.md). The title of Grand Duke has been replaced by the more imperious **Archduke**, automatically granted to the Duke whose affiliated economic bloc contributed the most to the city's coffers in the preceding fiscal year. Patriar names still carry weight, but a ducal seat is effectively for sale; the heads of the most powerful Merchant Consortiums and (in theory) the foreign Kontors can secure positions through immense financial leverage.

## Methods

- Ratifies legislation passed by both chambers of the Parliament.
- Directs the city's bureaucracy and gives orders to the (chronically underfunded) [Flaming Fist](../flaming-fist/summary.md).
- Chief tool is financial leverage; chief restraint is the fractious balance between the [Peerage of Blood](../peerage-of-blood/summary.md) and the [Peerage of Coin](../peerage-of-coin/summary.md).

## Hooks and Relationships

**Enemies.** Constant power struggle with [The Guild](../guild/summary.md), which it sees as a challenge to its authority. The Council is also beholden to the very Konsortiums and Kontors its members represent — making the line between regulator and regulated almost meaningless.
