# The Vote of No Confidence

[Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) brings the formal motion of no confidence in [Marshal Valerius Flint](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) to a Council vote at [High Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md), backed by the [Peerage of Coin's whip operation](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_3/whip-the-votes.md). [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) and the [Peerage of Blood](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_3/filibuster-the-mercenary-act.md) successfully filibuster. The motion fails. Flint stays in command of the Fist; the Archduke's authority is publicly broken on her own chamber floor. With Council channels closed to her she pivots immediately to [the private mercenary option](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_3/the-private-option.md), and the city's military and political leaderships are now in open schism.
