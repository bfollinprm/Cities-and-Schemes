# Issue the Ultimatum

With [the Eastway barracks operational](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md) and [Korgan Ironhand's "City-Sanctioned Peacekeepers" contract filed at High Hall](../../../sembian-kontor/activity/week_4/legitimizing-the-contract.md), [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) issues a formal, public ultimatum from the High Hall steps. Three days. The Free Trader blockade at the Basilisk Gate dismantles itself, or the mercenaries dismantle it. The text is short, the seal is large, and the language is unambiguous: *force is on the table*. By midday criers are reading it at every gate; copies are nailed to the Wide, the Counting House, the Phoenix Gate, the High Rider's Stand, and the Wyrm's Bend Market post. **It is the first time an Archduke has formally declared force against a Baldurian assembly in living memory.** [Parliament cannot reach quorum to respond](../../../parliament-of-peers/activity/week_5/the-failed-session.md). [Mad Meggan turns the ultimatum into a fortification order](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_5/heed-the-ultimatum.md) the same afternoon. [Alena and Meggan sign their alliance](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) by sundown. The political war is now formally a martial one, and the Archduke owns the timeline.
