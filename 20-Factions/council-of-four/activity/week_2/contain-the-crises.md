# Contain the Crises

With the [Free Trader siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md), the [Mad Meggan confession](../../../party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md), [an open Hellrider citizenship negotiation](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_2/the-interrogation.md), and [the Peerage of Coin's failed mercenary motion](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_2/legislate-aggression.md) all hitting at once, [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) is unable to contain any one fire without lighting another. The Council formally censures [Marshal Valerius Flint](../../../flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) for insubordination over [his refusal to break the siege](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_2/archdukes-mandate.md), deepening the rift between the city's political and military leadership. The censure makes the rift load-bearing for the rest of the season.
