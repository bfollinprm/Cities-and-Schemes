# The Mercenary Deployment

With Parliament deadlocked after [last week's no-confidence collapse](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_3/filibuster-the-mercenary-act.md) and the [Mercenary Act dead on the floor](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_3/whip-the-votes.md), [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) acts unilaterally. She orders the small elite mercenary company [she contracted privately through the Sembian Kontor](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_3/the-private-option.md) to establish a fortified camp in [Eastway](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) just outside the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) — barracks, drill yard, command tent, all on requisitioned [Western Gate Trading Company warehouse ground](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_4/the-barracks-requisition.md). The deployment is public, immediate, and impossible to walk back: a privately-funded army inside the city wall, in shield-wall distance of [the Free Trader blockade](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_4/digging-in.md). The Archduke has converted last week's contract into this week's troops and dared the Council, the Parliament, and the Marshal to do anything about it.
