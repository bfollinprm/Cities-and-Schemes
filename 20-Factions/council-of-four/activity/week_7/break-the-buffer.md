# Break the Buffer

[Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) orders a Sembian mercenary night-raid on the Rivington-Eastway corridor — the **buffer state** the party has been quietly building between the [Free Trader blockade](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) and the [Hellrider patrols](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md). Two patrol companies burn three Free-Trader food caches and three "Guild ward" warehouses in a single night, then withdraw before the Hellrider standing watch can mass.

The political target is sharper than the physical one: the Council wants to **discredit the buffer's legitimacy** before it can be recognized as a self-governing district. The morning bulletin from the [puppet *Baldur's Mouth*](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_7/the-puppet-press.md) frames the burned warehouses as Guild contraband, not civilian stores. Refugee-camp tempers in [Eastway](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) move from hungry to incandescent overnight — exactly what [the Plague of Remembrance needs](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_7/the-refugee-riot.md) for their false-flag operation later in the week.
