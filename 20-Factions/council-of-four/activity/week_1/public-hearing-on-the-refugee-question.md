# Public Hearing on the 'Refugee Question'

Pressed by the previous week's leaked petition and the death of the Refugee Relief Act, the Council convenes a televised public hearing on the "Refugee Question." It collapses inside an hour into a shouting match between [Patriar Heritage Society](../../../patriar-heritage-society/summary.md) nativists, merchant lobbyists hungry for cheap labor, and a thin moralist contingent led by Ilmatari clergy. [High Watcher Alena](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) of the [Hellriders](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) gives prepared, fiery testimony that everyone applauds and no one acts on. The Council adjourns without a vote — a procedural success that hardens both flanks.
