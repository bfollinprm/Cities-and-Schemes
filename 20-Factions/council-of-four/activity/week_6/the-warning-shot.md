# The Warning Shot

To test the resolve of the [Free Traders](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) behind their barricade, [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) orders her [Sembian mercenaries](../../../sembian-kontor/summary.md) to fire a volley of warning shots — crossbow bolts, rocks from a small trebuchet, and a single fire-arrow that lands short — over the [Basilisk Gate barricade](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_5/heed-the-ultimatum.md). It is a public show of force meant to terrify the civilians into surrendering before a full assault. The Council moves jointly with the Western Gate writ behind it; this is the sitting Archduke speaking with mercenary teeth.

The shots fall on a [Hellrider-trained shield wall](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_6/the-shield-wall.md) and a [Phoenix-radicalized refugee crowd](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_6/the-foundation-stone.md) that does not break. By sundown a [Petition of Grievances](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_6/the-petition-of-grievances.md) is at the High Hall steps and Baldur's Mouth has [its lead story for the week](../../../baldurs-mouth/activity/week_6/the-siege-edition.md). The Council's "warning shot" instead becomes the moment public opinion in the Lower City turned. **It is the first time in living memory mercenaries hired by the Archduke have fired on Baldurian civilians.**
