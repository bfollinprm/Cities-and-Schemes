# Kaelan

Stringer (freelance reporter) for [Baldur's Mouth](../summary.md). The canonical holder of a **Credential of Balduran** — Ettvard Needle's signet ring that compels high-level NPCs to interact with the bearer.

## What he was working on

- **The Consortium insurance-fraud investigation.** Issued his Credential during *The Siege Edition* arc to deliver this story.
- Per Needle's own framing, Credential-holders are "a suicide squad of journalists" sent into dangerous rooms. Kaelan went in.
- A morgue of stringer notes survives — insurance-fraud working files, source statements for the Consortium hit piece, and an unprinted manuscript of Mad' Meggan's cultist-captive bombshell that Needle wouldn't run "without more sources." Kaelan's case-file is the campaign-relevant artifact.
