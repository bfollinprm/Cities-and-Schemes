# Baldur's Mouth

The city's primary newssheet, run out of a single chaotic print shop in the Lower City. Tagline: ***"We Hear All, And Print Most of It!"*** Edited (until week 7) by [Ettvard Needle](people/ettvard-needle.md), the *Mouth* is a transactional institution — its goal is profit, achieved by selling papers and, more importantly, by selling its **influence** to whichever faction has paid for the next front page.

## Ideology

The *Mouth* has no ideology of its own. Its real product is **slant for hire**: the [Council](../council-of-four/summary.md), the [Guild](../guild/summary.md), the Kontors, the [Harpers](../harpers/summary.md), and the [Patriar Heritage Society](../patriar-heritage-society/summary.md) all use it to plant stories and steer public discourse. Under Needle, the dominant tone is populist, lurid, and cynical — sympathetic to the underdog when it sells papers, scornful of patriar pomp when it doesn't.

After Needle's disappearance, an **Interim Management Board** seizes the presses, denounces him as a libelous fugitive, and converts the paper into an unapologetic mouthpiece for the [Chionthar Consortium](../chionthar-consortium/summary.md), the [Peerage of Coin](../peerage-of-coin/summary.md), and the Council.

## Membership

A small print shop in the Lower City with a stable of stringers, sketch-artists, and woodcut engravers. Editorial control sits with whoever holds the editor's chair. Needle, then a vacuum, then the Interim Management Board after the Western Gate "press blackout."

## Methods

- **Paid front pages.** Lyra Silvershield's *"Pro-Consortium Puff Piece"* praising the [Western Gate Trading Company](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) for "bravely taming the wilderness." Goodman Borlu's bribe to kill the spoiled-meat story and run a Butchers' Company "donation drive" instead.
- **Sensational woodcuts.** *"The Githyanki Garrison"* (full-page woodcut atop Wyrm's Rock, record-breaking sales). *"The Siege Edition"* — staged sketches of starving Outer City children to whip up anti-Council sentiment, plus an investigative piece on Consortium **insurance fraud**.
- **Refusal to print.** Will sit on a story when evidence is missing — Mad' Meggan's bombshell about cultist captives went unprinted "without verifiable proof," and patriar-family pressure killed a similar Free Traders piece.
- **Leak destination.** Default drop point for the [Harpers](../harpers/summary.md) when the Nightingale wants public sentiment shifted.
- **Disappearance.** By week 7 Needle is missing. The Harpers send agents to scour [Western Gate](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) warehouses; the [Bibliophile](../bibliophile/summary.md) quietly buys up surviving copies of the insurance-fraud edition. The Interim Board issues "The Great Retraction" and shifts permanently pro-Consortium.

## Hooks and Relationships

**Patrons (paying).** [Western Gate Trading Company](../western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) (puff piece, then the **press blackout** when the Archduke locks down the presses). [Worshipful Company of Butchers & Tanners](../wc-butchers-tanners/summary.md) (donation-drive cover-up). The [Patriar Heritage Society](../patriar-heritage-society/summary.md) (anti–Free Trader pamphlet wave).

**Targets of coverage.** [Hellriders of New Elturel](../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) — refugee-burden coverage from a leaked, falsified cost-analysis. [The Unveiled](../unveiled/summary.md) — target of plate-altering attacks (printing-plate sabotage, per `factions.docx`).

**Allies of convenience.** [Harpers](../harpers/summary.md) — leak-and-launder relationship.
