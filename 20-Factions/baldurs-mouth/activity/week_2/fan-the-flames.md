# Fan the Flames

[Ettvard Needle](../../people/ettvard-needle.md) prints a new edition built around two paid pieces sitting next to each other on the same front page: a blistering editorial condemning the [Flaming Fist's "inaction" against the Free Trader siege](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_2/plan-the-unthinkable.md) — bought by the [Western Gate Trading Company](../../../western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_2/archdukes-mandate.md) — and a sympathetic refugee feature humanizing [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md), bought by the [Hellriders](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_2/the-interrogation.md). The same edition splashes the [Elturel Remembrance Society arrest](../../../elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_2/infernal-residue.md), buries the [Society of Baldurian Integrity ballad fiasco](../../../society-of-baldurian-integrity/activity/week_2/sigil-of-repulsion.md) in the back page, and refuses [the Harpers' Archduke leak](../../../harpers/activity/week_2/follow-the-paper-trail.md) for lack of proof. Print sales spike.
