# Publish a Story Blaming Refugees for Merchant's Murder

[Ettvard Needle](../../people/ettvard-needle.md) takes [Lady Vhage's](../../../plague-of-remembrance/people/emmeline-vhage.md) gold up front for a planned splash blaming the [Hellriders](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) for the murder of a Baldurian merchant. The supporting "evidence" — a forged letter Vhage's cell was supposed to plant on the body — never makes it onto the corpse. With nothing to cite, Needle quietly shelves the story and pockets the retainer. The *Mouth* prints a different anti-refugee piece that week, but the murder-frame Vhage paid for never sees print.
