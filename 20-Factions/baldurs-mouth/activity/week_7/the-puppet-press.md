# The Puppet Press

With [Ettvard Needle missing in the Tumbledown crypts](../../../../50-Sessions/session_27/player_summary.md), [an "Interim" Editor](../../people/the-interim-management-board.md) installed by the paper's silent owners takes over the Lower City print shop. The first edition under the new byline halts all investigative coverage of the [insurance-fraud exposé](../../activity/week_6/the-siege-edition.md), runs an apologetic "context-and-corrections" column, and lays out three full pages of [Archduke Silvershield's](../../../western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) "Restoring Order" series above the fold.

Inside seven days the *Baldur's Mouth* has gone from *the* dissident voice in the city — the paper that printed Kaelan's [Insurance Scam](../../../party/people/kaelan.md) under its own banner — to a Consortium-aligned mouthpiece. Lower City newsboys see their daily print runs cut by half; [Nansi Gretta of the Bibliophile](../../../bibliophile/people/nansi-gretta.md) starts [quietly buying every remaining copy of the Siege Edition](../../../bibliophile/activity/week_7/the-missing-ink.md) from corner sellers — once those issues are off the streets, *the public has no record the original story was ever printed.*
