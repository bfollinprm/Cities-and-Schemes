# The Price of Insubordination

Smelling blood after [Marshal Flint's public defiance of the Archduke](../../../flaming-fist/activity/week_3/circle-the-wagons.md), Editor [Ettvard Needle](../../people/ettvard-needle.md) sets the *Baldur's Mouth* room on Marshal Flint himself. Reporters work [Wyrm's Rock Fortress](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md) gates and the [Baldur's Mouth Print Shop](../../summary.md) night desk for any usable dirt — supply contracts, deputizations, drinking habits — intending to sell the package to the highest bidder at [High Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/README.md). The story Needle is really pricing is not the Marshal's: it is what [Lord Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) will pay to bury it and what the [Peerage of Coin](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_3/whip-the-votes.md) will pay to publish it. Either way, the *Mouth* gets paid.
