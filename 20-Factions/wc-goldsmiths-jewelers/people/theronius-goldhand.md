# Master Theronius Goldhand

Master of the [Worshipful Company of Goldsmiths & Jewelers](../summary.md). A stern, meticulous dwarven master jeweler whose personal vouch is the closest thing to a guarantee of value in the city.

## Manifesto

> Coin is a promise. A gem is a memory. Gold is permanence. In a city of liars and thieves, we are the keepers of true value. Every transaction, from the smallest copper to the largest patriar loan, is built on the trust our seal provides. We do not just weigh metal; we weigh the city's faith in itself.

## Role

Goldhand chairs the Court of Assistants and runs the city's flagship assay office at the guildhall. He cultivates working relationships with both the [Amnian Kontor](../../amnian-kontor/summary.md) (for currency exchange) and the [Peerage of Blood](../../peerage-of-blood/summary.md) (through patriar commissions and the recently-presented canary automaton for Lord Durinbold). He treats counterfeiting as personal insult and works pragmatically with [The Guild](../../guild/summary.md) when their interests align.
