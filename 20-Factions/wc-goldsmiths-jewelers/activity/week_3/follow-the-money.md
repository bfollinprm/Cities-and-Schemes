# Follow the Money

[Master Theronius Goldhand](../../people/theronius-goldhand.md), continuing [last week's investigation into a large unbanked sum circulating in the Outer City](../activity/week_2/corner-the-silver.md), follows the audit trail back to ['Mad' Meggan's](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) [6,000 gp payout to the party](../../../party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md). The trail dies at the [Counting House](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/counting-house.md). Meggan, anticipating exactly this kind of scrutiny, funneled the payment through a chain of shell accounts and third-party Outer City brokers before the gold ever touched a Lower City house. Goldhand's agents can confirm a single large withdrawal but cannot link it to any named recipient. He notes the size and timing for the file, opens [a discreet underworld bounty for any name attached to the coin](../activity/week_2/corner-the-silver.md), and accepts a [Chandlers' lamplighter intelligence subscription](../../../wc-chandlers-lamplighters/activity/week_3/a-light-in-the-storm.md) to cover the Outer City pubs the missing party is most likely to surface in. The party is, of course, currently inside a Feywild pocket dimension.
