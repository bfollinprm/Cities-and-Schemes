# Create a Masterwork for a Patriar

Master [Theronius Goldhand](../../people/theronius-goldhand.md) personally oversees the crafting of a gold-and-silver canary automaton commissioned for [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md). The piece is meant as a quiet alliance gift binding the traditionalist Goldsmiths' Company to the [Peerage of Blood](../../../peerage-of-blood/summary.md) bloc — and is delivered in a private dinner the same week the [Heritage Preservation Act](../../../peerage-of-blood/activity/week_1/lobby-for-heritage-preservation-laws.md) dies on the floor.
