# Corner the Silver

[Master Theronius Goldhand](../../people/theronius-goldhand.md) opens a discreet investigation out of the [Gold-Gable Hall](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/README.md) into a large unbanked sum of gold circulating in the [Outer City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md). The Goldsmiths suspect a major heist or smuggling operation; the audit-trail in fact starts at ['Mad' Meggan's](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) chest of [6,000 gp paid to the party](../../../party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md), now visibly recirculating through Rivington innkeepers, [the Forge of the Nine](../../../wc-smiths-armorers/summary.md), and the Weary Plowman. The Goldsmiths do not yet know the answer, but they are looking in the right ledgers.
