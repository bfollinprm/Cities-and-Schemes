# The Public Assay

Following [last week's open-vault audit](../../activity/week_6/the-gold-standard.md) and watching [the Gilded Vein's copper crash](../../../gilded-vein/activity/week_7/the-copper-crash.md) shake retail confidence in the Lower City, [Master Theronius Goldhand](../../people/theronius-goldhand.md) stages a **magical assay of the city's emergency gold reserves** in the public reading room of [the Counting House](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/counting-house.md). The Parliament of Peers is invited; reporters from the (now Consortium-aligned) *Baldur's Mouth* are seated in the front row.

Goldhand walks the Peerage of Coin and the Peerage of Blood through every reserve crate, runs *Detect Forgery* and a Guild-witnessed alloy assay on each, and demonstrates **the city's reserves are pure**. The performance is the most compelling piece of governance theatre the Counting House has hosted since the Crisis. The morning *Mouth* runs the assay above the fold; the bankruptcy rumors die. The Goldsmiths' Guild is — by week's end — *the most institutionally trusted Worshipful Company in the city*, even though the Counting House itself is now [a private armed fortress](../../../peerage-of-coin/activity/week_7/private-armies.md) the Fist cannot enter.
