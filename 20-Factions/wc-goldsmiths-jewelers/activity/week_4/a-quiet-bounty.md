# A Quiet Bounty

[Master Theronius Goldhand](../../people/theronius-goldhand.md), with [last week's audit trail dead at the Counting House](../../../wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_3/follow-the-money.md), shifts to underworld channels. He places a discreet, high-paying bounty through a fence at the Blushing Mermaid in the Lower City: any verifiable information regarding the source or the recipients of the **6,000 gp** that walked out of [Mad Meggan's siege wallet](../../../party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md). The bounty does not name the party. It does describe their composition. The reward is generous enough that within a day every fence in [Brampton](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) and the Undercellar is awake to it. The party is in the Feywild and unreachable, but their face will land on a fence's wall the moment they walk back into the Lower City.
