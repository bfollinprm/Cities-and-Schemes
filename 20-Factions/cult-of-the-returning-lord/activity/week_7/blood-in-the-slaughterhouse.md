# Blood in the Slaughterhouse

The Bhaalist remnant — survivors of [the Dusthawk Hill purge](../../../takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_6/the-prison-silence.md) who fled the pit before the party arrived — re-coalesces in the cellar beneath **Hamhock's Slaughterhouse** in [Sow's Foot](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). The location is no accident: it is owned-of-record by [Belvin "Soot" Hamhock](../../../guild/people/belvin-soot-hamhock.md), the cellar drains directly into the [Undercity](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/undercity/hamhocks-vault.md), and the daily slaughter of livestock provides perfect cover for the actual rite.

The cell begins **mass ritual murder of Sow's Foot residents** — refugees, drunks, day-laborers — folded into the meat-floor's evening kill-stream so the blood-trace reads as cattle. The goal is a power-stockpile sufficient to attempt an **avatar manifestation of Bhaal**. The [Gravekeepers' Cliffside consecration](../../../gravekeepers/activity/week_7/the-cliffside-consecration.md) is intended to wall off the Hill; *the Bhaalists have already moved to a different cellar.* They are working under the city's protectors' noses.
