# Consecrate the Blockade with Blood

The Bhaalist cell working the [Eastway](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) corridor near the [Basilisk Gate](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) sets a ritualized ambush meant to murder a Flaming Fist sergeant in plain sight of the [Free Trader siege line](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_3/press-the-advantage.md), so the killing reads as Free Trader vengeance and tips the political crisis into an arrest sweep. They are interrupted mid-strike by a [Guild patrol](../../../guild/activity/week_3/recover-the-asset.md) on its rounds. In the brawl the assassins are forced to flee; the sergeant survives wounded; and a ritual dagger — Bhaal-marked, unmistakable — is recovered on the cobblestones. The Fist now has hard evidence of a Bhaalist cell operating along the siege line. The cult's frame collapses, their bloodlust is unsated, and Marshal Flint gains a new, terrifying public threat to point at when the Council demands he break the blockade.
