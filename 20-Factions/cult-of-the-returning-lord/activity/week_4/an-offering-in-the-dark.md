# An Offering in the Dark

Bhaalist assassins set an ambush for a Watch patrol in [Manorborn](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/manorborn.md), aiming to claim a sacrifice and consecrate the killing on Upper City stone. The patrol that turns the corner includes a hardened veteran who reacts faster than the cell expects. The skirmish is bloody, brief, and the wrong way around: the assassins lose two of their own and are forced to retreat through the back lanes empty-handed. They leave behind a distinctive **ritual dagger** with the cult's sigil — the second such dagger recovered in two weeks ([the first was dropped at the Basilisk Gate](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_3/consecrate-the-blockade-with-blood.md)). [The Watch](../../../watch/summary.md) now has concrete, in-hand evidence of an organized Bhaalist cell active in the Upper City — at exactly the moment [the Watch is stretched too thin to investigate it](../../../watch/activity/week_4/the-manorborn-burglaries.md). Underneath the same district, [the Gravekeepers find the cell's hidden sewer temple](../../../gravekeepers/activity/week_4/the-source-of-the-sickness.md).
