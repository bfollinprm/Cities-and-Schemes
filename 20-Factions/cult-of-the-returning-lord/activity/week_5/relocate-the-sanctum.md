# Relocate the Sanctum

With [the Gravekeepers logging their hidden temple under Tumbledown last week](../../../gravekeepers/activity/week_4/the-source-of-the-sickness.md) and [the Watch holding their dropped ritual dagger as evidence](../../../cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_4/an-offering-in-the-dark.md), the Bhaalist cell tries to relocate. The chosen new sanctum is **Hamhock's Slaughterhouse** in [Sow's Foot](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md), Outer City — already a place of consecrated death, easy to camouflage. The Bhaalist assassins move the idols and the consecrated knives by night. They almost get there. A careless acolyte at the relocation's tail end — exhausted, sleepless, drunk on consecrated wine — tips a flask of *ritual blood* in a Sow's Foot side-alley. The smell is unmistakable, even at half-strength, even at first light, even in a slaughterhouse district. The local butchers — hard-bitten, superstitious, [already on edge after last week's Sow's Foot Gravekeeper sweeps](../../../gravekeepers/activity/week_4/the-source-of-the-sickness.md) — form a *mob*. Cleavers come out. The cultists are driven back into the [Tumbledown](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md) sewer mouths empty-handed. The unholy idols are lost in the chase. **The Cult of the Returning Lord has no secure base in Baldur's Gate this week.**
