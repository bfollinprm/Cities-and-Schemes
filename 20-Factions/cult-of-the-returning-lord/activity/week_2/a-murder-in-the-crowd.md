# A Murder in the Crowd

A Bhaalist initiate cell carries out a series of brutal, ritualized killings in the [Outer City](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md) under the cover of the political chaos around the [Basilisk Gate siege](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md). The crowd-press of refugees, Free Trader pickets, and Fist patrols screens the work, and the cult successfully shifts initial blame onto the [Free Traders](../../../free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) themselves — a frame the [Plague of Remembrance](../../../plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_2/silence-and-slander.md) is happy to amplify. The Cult's standing in [the Bhaal Celebrant's](../../people/the-bhaal-celebrant.md) underground rises sharply on a successful initiation streak.
