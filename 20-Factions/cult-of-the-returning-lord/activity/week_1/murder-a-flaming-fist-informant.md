# Murder a Flaming Fist Informant

A [Bhaal-Celebrant](../../people/the-bhaal-celebrant.md)–blessed cell tries a sanctified murder against a [Flaming Fist](../../../flaming-fist/summary.md) informant — both as offering and as initiation test. The informant fights, draws blood, and runs. The cultists scatter before completing the kill, leaving behind a terrified, living witness who reports the attack to the Fist within the hour. The cell flunks its test; Bhaal accepts no half-tithe.
