# The Netherbrain

The illithid mastermind of the **Absolute Crisis** — defeated, dead in the conventional sense, and yet the central object of the [Hands of the Absolute](../summary.md) cult's entire theology.

## Its present role in the campaign

- **The Echo.** The Hands of the Absolute claim to hear the Netherbrain's psychic *Echo* in the city's substrate. The cult's job is to listen, recognize, and propagate the Echo. This is splinter-cult business; nobody outside the cell agrees the Echo is real.
- **The Unveiled's heresy.** The conspiracy-paladin cell [The Unveiled](../../unveiled/summary.md) believes — through their member **Xylia** — that mind flayers and the Netherbrain were *merely props* in a larger doppelganger false-flag. In their telling, the real masterminds were never illithid at all.
- **K'liir's frame.** [K'liir](../../unchained/people/k-laar.md) treats the Netherbrain's defeat as the moment that *freed* loyalist Githyanki to gather strength on the Material Plane rather than continue the war.

## Status

Defeated. Dead. Whichever cult is right about the Echo, the central illithid mind is gone in the form it had during the Crisis. What persists is *interpretation*.
