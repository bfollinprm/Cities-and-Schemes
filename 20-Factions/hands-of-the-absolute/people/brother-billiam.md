# Brother Billiam

Quartermaster of the [Open Hand Temple](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/open-hand-temple.md) in [Rivington](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). The party first met him in week 1 when he hired them to find the source of the Sewer Plague.

## Role with the Hands

Billiam is a low-tier asset — not a cell member, but a "friendly" the [Hands of the Absolute](../summary.md) cultivate to reach Open Hand patients with unusual psychic resonance. Cult contacts trade him alms-coin and discreet healings for advance lists of plague victims and refugees showing "nightmares of the deep." In week 2 the cult used Billiam's intake records to [hunt for a potential "Voice"](../activity/week_2/harvest-the-echo.md) among the temple's psi-sensitive patients before the trail went cold.
