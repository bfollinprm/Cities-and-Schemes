# The Voice in the Choir

Founder and leader of the [Hands of the Absolute](../summary.md). A high-level administrator in the original Absolute cult — never implanted with a tadpole because her organizational skills were considered too valuable to risk. The result: a free-willed survivor utterly devoted to the cause.

## Manifesto

> You cling to your individuality as if it were a treasure. But what is it? A cage of bone. A cacophony of fear, desire, and doubt. The Absolute offered the ultimate gift: peace. Unity. An end to the terrible silence of being alone in your own mind. It was not destroyed. It was scattered, a divine song shattered into a million lonely notes. We will bring the song together again, and all of you will finally be free.

## Role

The Voice runs Hands of the Absolute operations from a network of resonance chambers in Lower City basements. Her week-0 attunement ritual succeeded too well — contact made with a feral psychic entity that killed and broke her cultists. She is now operating with a diminished, terrified flock and the knowledge that *something* in the city's psychic substrate is responding to her calls.
