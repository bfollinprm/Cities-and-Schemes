# Scour the Slums for a Spark

The Hands cell working under [The Voice in the Choir](../../people/the-voice.md) sweeps **Sow's Foot** in the Outer City and the [New Elturel](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) refugee margins for an individual whose psychic resonance can substitute for the candidate they lost at [the Open Hand Temple](../activity/week_2/harvest-the-echo.md). They probe and question the mentally fragile — addicts, refugees with breakdowns, the half-mad regulars at [Glim-Glim's clinic-front](../../../purified/people/glim-glim.md). They find no one with the resonance the cult requires. Their visible probing draws the attention of a local Guild Ward Boss, who [is already suspicious of cult activity in his territory](../../../guild/activity/week_3/recover-the-asset.md), and quietly has the cell driven out of Sow's Foot before they can finish their sweep. The cult has now lost two key recruiting grounds in two weeks and is running out of slum.
