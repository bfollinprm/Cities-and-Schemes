# Harvest the Echo

[The Voice in the Choir](../../people/the-voice.md) sends a cell into the [Open Hand Temple](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/open-hand-temple.md) in [Rivington](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) hunting for a potential "Voice" candidate — a psi-sensitive whose trauma during the sewer plague the cult logged in week 1. **Brother Billiam**, the temple's quartermaster, is the contact-of-record but not the target; the candidate slips out of Rivington during the chaos around the [Wyrm's Crossing protest's collapse](../../../followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_2/the-call-for-martyrs.md), and the psychic trail goes cold inside two days. The cell logs the failure and rotates back to the [K'liir periphery](../../../unchained/activity/week_2/a-profitable-exercise.md) for the next sweep.
