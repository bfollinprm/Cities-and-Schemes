# The Psychic Miasma

[The Voice in the Choir](../../people/the-voice.md), still smarting from [the failed break-in at Sorcerous Sundries last week](../../../hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_4/the-sundered-vault.md), pivots from *taking* psionic prizes to *harvesting* the city's free fear. A new ritual is staged in a hidden Heapside attic above a Lower City corn-merchant's loft. The cell is meant to siphon the collective panic of the populace — three days of [the Archduke's ultimatum](../../../council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md) and [militia drills in New Elturel](../../../hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) and a Sow's Foot butcher-mob — into the resonance chamber, refine it into Echo-signal, find the Absolute's voice through the noise. The chamber overloads. The fear of a city of two hundred thousand is more than the cell calibrated for. The psychic feedback loop *lashes back*. The Voice is knocked unconscious; three of the cell are bleeding from the ears; the resonance chamber itself burns through, brass to slag. **The Hands of the Absolute are psychically blinded** for the rest of the week — unable to hear the Echo, unable to coordinate cells across districts, unable to feel for [Sodality scrying censers](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_4/the-scrying-censors.md) that would normally show them where to walk. They wake up deaf to the city.
