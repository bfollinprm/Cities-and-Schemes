# The Sundered Vault

[The Voice in the Choir](../../people/the-voice.md) directs a small infiltration team against [Sorcerous Sundries](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/sorcerous-sundries.md) to seize a psionic artifact rumored to be cataloged in a "low-security" vault. The intelligence is wrong. The vault is protected by unforeseen magical alarms and glyphs of warding that activate the moment the cell crosses the threshold. Arcane constructs animate; magical sirens howl across Lower City rooftops; the cult's infiltrators flee empty-handed across the Sundries' rooftop walks. [Lorroakan's](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/people/lorroakan.md) Sodality investigators secure the vault by morning and inventory exactly what was *attempted*: a piece of psionic-resonant crystal already on [the Sodality's Zora-related case file](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_3/follow-the-resonance.md). The Sodality's response — [permanent scrying censers across the gate checkpoints](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_4/the-scrying-censors.md) — is approved that afternoon. The Hands have just hardened the magical surveillance regime against themselves.
