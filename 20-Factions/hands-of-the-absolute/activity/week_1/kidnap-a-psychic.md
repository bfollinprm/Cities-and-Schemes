# Kidnap a Psychic

The Hand cell tries to abduct a fortune-teller from [the Wide](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/the-wide.md) for use as a psychic focus in their Echo-listening rituals. The target is unexpectedly resilient and makes a public scene; a Watch patrol drifts toward the noise. The cultists abandon the snatch and exfiltrate clean, leaving [the Voice](../../people/the-voice.md) without the psychic component the cell needs for the next ritual cycle.
