# The Silent Scream

Desperate to wake [The Voice in the Choir](../../people/the-voice.md) from [the psychic coma their week-5 fear-harvest backfire put her in](../../activity/week_5/the-psychic-miasma.md), the Hands of the Absolute kidnap a low-level psi-sensitive — a young half-elf clerk who works the periphery of [the K'liir camp](../../../unchained/summary.md) at Wyrm's Crossing. They drag him through the sewers to a hidden cellar in [Heapside](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) and attempt a dangerous **psionic-energy transfusion** ritual: the kidnapped man strapped to a slab, copper wire grounding him to the Voice's pallet, the cell's remaining mind-flayer-touched acolytes channeling raw psionic force across the gap in hope the shock will jumpstart their leader's mind.

The ritual goes catastrophically. The clerk's mind shatters within the first minute — he goes brain-dead, soils the slab, and stops breathing. The Voice does not wake. Worse, the *attempt itself* throws a psionic shockwave detectable across the Lower City — picked up by [Commander K'laar's psionic-sweep patrols at Wyrm's Crossing](../../../unchained/activity/week_6/the-psionic-sweep.md), by [the Sodality's reset-rebooted scrying censers](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_6/the-system-reboot.md), and by [Bror's Whispering Stone in the party's pocket](../../../party/activity/week_5/install-finneas-as-guild-boss-of-rivington.md). The Hands have made themselves *louder* in the city's psychic substrate at the exact moment the Voice was supposed to be listening for the Echo. **The cult is now psychically blind, psychically deaf, and psychically tagged.**
