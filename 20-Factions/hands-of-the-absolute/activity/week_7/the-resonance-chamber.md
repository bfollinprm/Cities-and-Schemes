# The Resonance Chamber

After [last week's botched transfusion killed the captive psi-sensitive on the slab](../../activity/week_6/the-silent-scream.md), [The Voice in the Choir](../../people/the-voice.md) commits to a **larger** chamber and a *living* battery. Brother Billiam's snatch-teams pull three new sensitives off the streets above [Heapside](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md): a child of a Bhaalist refugee family, a Sodality apprentice walking home from late shift, an Unchained recruit on leave from Wyrm's Rock. The captives are wired into the chamber's psionic lattice over three days of secret ritual.

The lattice **leaks** before it stabilizes. Everyone within a three-block radius of the Heapside cellar starts experiencing **waking nightmares** — children screaming at empty corners, drunks weeping over unseen wounds, two cases of catatonia at the [Penumbra Playhouse](../../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) reported as "stage panic." [Mother Nocturne](../../../silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md) reads the bleed as a Sharran sign and starts her own [purge of the blind](../../../silent-shroud/activity/week_7/the-helm-and-cloak-infiltration.md). [The Sorcerous Sodality's magical dragnet](../../../wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_7/the-magical-dragnet.md) picks up the lattice's harmonics but cannot triangulate them through the surrounding interference.
