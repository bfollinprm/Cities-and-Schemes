# The Feywild

A parallel plane — the *twilight reflection* of Toril, alike in geography but lit differently, scaled differently, ruled by entirely different powers. Time runs at its own pace; a night spent at a fey ball can be a year on the Material, or vice versa.

## Why it matters now

The **Spellplague** a century past pulled the Feywild close to Toril, and crossings between worlds quietly multiplied. With the **Weave healed** and Mystra's return, those crossings have *stabilized rather than closed* — meaning fey traffic into the Sword Coast is now a routine fact, not an exception. By 1502 DR, the [fey courts](../fey-courts/README.md) are stakeholders in Faerûn's future.

## Notable inner geography

- **Senaliesse** — Titania's living palace; golden boughs, the **Tree of Stars**, dragonfly-wing lanterns. Site of the [Court of Stars](../fey-courts/court-of-stars.md).
- **The Mushroom Fields of Myrtil** — recently reported corruption of resident fey ([Far Realm](../far-realm.md) intrusion).
- **The Crystal Fens of Murmurond** — same.

## Crossings to the Sword Coast

Points where the Feywild touches Toril are called **fey crossings**. Active ones in 1502 DR:

- **Neverwinter Wood** — opens onto the eladrin city of New Sharandar; eladrin guards lately report unfamiliar constellations through the gap.
- **Misty Forest** — a glade that once drifted with golden fireflies now exudes a viscous purple fog at midnight; a hunter who stepped into its mushroom ring lost a day and his hair turned white.
- **Ardeep Forest** — the weirwood circle, attended by the **Lady of Twilight** (a Wild Archfey).
- **Laughing Hollow** (near Daggerford) — pixies, centaurs, satyrs alongside wild elves, under an ancient fey prince. Pact-brokered envoys to Waterdeep via the [Green Masquerade](../fey-courts/green-masquerade.md).
- **Moonshae Isles** — **Sarifal** on Gwynneth, where a fey queen openly rules in mortal stead. The Moonwell of Sarifal blackened for one night during the recent Far Realm portents.

The crossings are **shuddering** under foreign pressure — the [Far Realm](../far-realm.md) is using the Feywild's naturally thin veils to push toward the Material.

## What it costs to enter

- **Time slips** — minutes there can be days here, or the inverse. No way to know in advance.
- **Names heard** — the Feywild *remembers* names spoken aloud; a true name uttered there is leverage forever after.
- **Bargains owed** — every drink accepted, every meal eaten, every favor received accrues a [debt](../../../00-Campaign-Frame/fey-bargains.md).
