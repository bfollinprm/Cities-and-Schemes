# Toril

The Material Plane. The world. Where the campaign happens and where its institutions live.

The dossier treats Toril cosmologically only as the *anchor* for the Feywild: the [Feywild](feywild.md) is described as Toril's **twilight reflection** — alike in geography but lit, scaled, and ruled differently. The **Spellplague** a century past pulled the two close together; the **Second Sundering** wounded and healed the Weave but left the crossings *stabilized rather than closed*.

For most surface campaign purposes, "Toril" is shorthand for "the Material" — the side of the boundary your character is currently standing on.
