# Avernus

First layer of the Nine Hells. Asmodean territory at its most basic: blood-iron landscape, infernal warlords, the war-engines of devils.

## Why the campaign cares

- **The Fall of Elturel.** The city of Elturel was dragged into Avernus and survived; the survivors became the **Elturian refugees** and the volunteer militia that calls itself the [Hellriders of New Elturel](../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md). The Hellriders' core conviction — *we will not be saved a second time* — is the **Avernus crisis** as a permanent political stance.
- **Infernal-iron salvage.** Hell-damaged armor and weapons recovered from the Fall trade at premium in Baldur's Gate. **Dammon at the Forge of the Nine** is the campaign's named smith for this work.
- **High Watcher Alena.** Priestess of [Torm](../deities/torm.md), respected community organizer, leader of the Hellriders. Her standing is grounded in surviving Avernus.
