# Torm

God of duty, courage, and self-sacrifice. The paladin's god.

## In the campaign

- **High Watcher Alena.** Priestess of Torm and respected Elturian community organizer; leads the [Hellriders of New Elturel](../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) volunteer militia.
- **Faith-based community organizing.** Torm's clergy are the spine of the Hellriders' civic operation — mass protests at the High Hall, refugee mutual-aid networks, public petitions. Where the Hellriders organize politically, Torm's chapels are usually the venue.

The Hellriders read the Fall of Elturel and the survival of Avernus as a Torm story: oath kept, suffering shared, the survivors guarded.
