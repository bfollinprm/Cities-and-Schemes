# Bhaal

Lord of Murder. Long-dead by the standards of Forgotten Realms theology; his cult never gets the message.

## In the campaign

- **The Cult of the Returning Lord.** Bhaal-worshipping death cult consecrating sewer altars with the blood of "nobodies" — the dispossessed, the unhoused, the disappearable. See [20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/summary.md](../../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/summary.md).
- **The Bhaalspawn crisis.** A century-and-some past, but still cited: the catastrophe shaped the modern shape of Baldur's Gate's economic order. Old factions like the [Iron Throne](../../../20-Factions/iron-throne/summary.md) still talk about themselves as ghosts of the pre-Bhaalspawn city.
- **Harpers' standing target.** [The Harpers](../../../20-Factions/harpers/summary.md) treat Bhaal cells as primary cleanup targets in Baldur's Gate.
