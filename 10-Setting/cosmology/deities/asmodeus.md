# Asmodeus

Lord of the Nine Hells. Archdevil. The campaign's archetype of *the bargain you do not admit*.

## In the campaign

- **House Cassalanter's secret pact.** [House Cassalanter](../../../20-Factions/waterdeep-noble-houses/associations/house-cassalanter.md) projects philanthropy and Sea Ward respectability while *secretly worshipping Asmodeus*. The bargain costs them children unless they keep paying in.
- **The campaign's central image of veiled threat.** Cassalanter as a public face is the dossier's textbook example of *splendor as theater, paranoia as weather*; Asmodeus is the bill behind the splendor.
- **Worshippers (Asmodean) in the city.** Adjective form. Sea Ward salons in 1502 DR contain at least one quiet Asmodean for every loud Lathandrine.
