# Mystra

Goddess of magic. Patron of the Weave. Source of the Chosen-of-Mystra power that — among others — once ran through [Laeral Silverhand](../../../20-Factions/masked-lords/people/laeral-silverhand.md).

## Why she matters in this campaign

- **The Second Sundering (c. 1482–1487 DR).** Mystra withdrew. The Weave was wounded. Laeral's Chosen power was permanently diminished and has been concealed since.
- **The return.** Mystra is back, the Weave is healed, but the cosmic damage to the planar borders is still settling. The crossings into the Feywild *stabilized rather than closed*.
- **Spellcasting in the Realms still functions because she chose to come back.** Casters take her return as the un-spoken precondition of every modern operation.
