# Lathander

The **Morninglord**. God of dawn, renewal, beginnings.

## Where he appears in the campaign

- Iconography. The stained-glass window an eladrin knight came through at the Autumnal Ball at Dweomerheart Hall is a Lathander window.
- Clergy of Lathander run a number of dawn-rite shrines in Waterdeep's Sea Ward; the temple connection between old money and orthodox Faerûnian gods is mostly Lathandrine.

A god of *renewal* sits oddly in a city built on concealment and old wounds. The temples preach it anyway.
