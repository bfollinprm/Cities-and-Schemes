# Vlaakith

Lich-queen of the **Githyanki Empire**. A planar power, ruling the githyanki from the Astral Plane through ritual, terror, and the consumption of her own subjects' soul-strength.

## Why she matters in this campaign

- **The standing antagonist of [the Unchained](../../20-Factions/unchained/summary.md).** The Outer City exile cell exists to oppose her; their loyalty is to **Prince Orpheus**, the rebel her empire wants dead.
- **A long reach.** Vlaakith's notice extends across the Astral Plane and through it into the Material. The Unchained's presence in Baldur's Gate is itself an operational risk — the Outer City is far enough from her gaze, for now. *For now* is the operative phrase.
- **A figure invoked, not encountered.** The campaign treats Vlaakith as cosmic-scale — a power named in summaries and curses, the way the dossier names Asmodeus or Mystra, not as someone the city will ever see in person.
