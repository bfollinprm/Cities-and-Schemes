# Prince Orpheus

Rebel **Githyanki** prince and the standing antithesis of [Vlaakith](vlaakith.md). The [Unchained](../../20-Factions/unchained/summary.md) — encamped at [the K'liir](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/the-kliir.md) in the [Outer City](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md) — are loyal to him, not to the lich-queen.

## Why he matters in this campaign

- **The cause that put the Unchained on Toril.** Orpheus's role in the [Absolute Crisis](../history/absolute-crisis.md) — liberating the Material from the [Netherbrain](../history/absolute-crisis.md) — is the moral debt the Unchained believe Baldur's Gate owes them. Their patience with the city is rooted in that debt, not in friendship.
- **A planar power, off-screen.** Like Vlaakith, Orpheus is treated as cosmic-scale: invoked in [K'laar's](../../20-Factions/unchained/people/k-laar.md) manifesto, never expected to walk into a scene. He liberated this plane; he will not see it fall, and his loyalists will finish any fight started here on his behalf.
- **The "True War."** Orpheus's war is in the [Astral Sea](../../30-Places/astral-sea.md) — the rebellion against Vlaakith's rule. Baldur's Gate is a stepping stone, a refuge in which Orpheus's loyalists hone their blades and minds. *Tsk'va.*
