# The Far Realm

A plane *outside* the known cosmology — neither Material, Feywild, Shadowfell, nor any of the inner or outer planes. Its physics defy law and its inhabitants defy form. It does not invade so much as *seep*: through thinned veils, through dreams, through the warps the Spellplague left in the Weave.

## What it does

- **Twists living things.** Creatures touched by the Far Realm are warped into aberrations — extra mouths, kelp-like tendrils where heads should be, stone where flesh should be. The change is bodily, mental, and permanent.
- **Bleeds through reflections.** Mirrors and still water at the moment of intrusion show *another place*: leafless trees under bruised skies, an unblinking eye where a sun should be.
- **Plants false memory.** Sensitive mortals recall events that never happened to them — most often an *illithid invasion of the Feywild* with no record. Sages call this **memory bleed**.
- **Distorts arcane magic.** Illusion and enchantment spells produce off-key results near a breach — a glamer makes the caster hear distant music, a *sending* returns hissing chorus voices in Deep Speech. The effects spike at new moon.

## The 1502 incursion

After the [Court of Stars](fey-courts/court-of-stars.md) convened in 1501 DR over reports of *corrupted fey*, signs spread to the Sword Coast itself:

- A tentacled dryad in the Ardeep Forest, screaming in Sylvan and Deep Speech.
- A pixie in the Laughing Hollow with tentacles where its wings should be.
- A petrified unicorn in the High Forest, branded with circular aberrant marks.
- The Moonwell of Sarifal blackened for one night.
- A **Star Spawn** infiltrating a Green Masquerade ball in Waterdeep — see the [Night of the Howling Masks](../../40-Timeline/history.md).

## What works against it

- **True sunlight** — magnified daylight (a chandelier turned brilliant) repels Far Realm shapes.
- **Cold iron and true-sight** at thresholds catches infiltrators wearing mortal guise.
- **Sylvan warding-songs** (especially Titania's hymn) steady the mind against the telepathic *"Hungry…"* assault.
- **Eladrin knights of the Summer Court** — they came through stained glass at Dweomerheart Hall on a gale of blossoms and sealed the breach.

## What does not

- Negotiation. The Far Realm has no court, no envoy, and no terms. It only hungers.
