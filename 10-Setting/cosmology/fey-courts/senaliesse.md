# Senaliesse

[Titania's](titania.md) **living palace**. Seat of the [Seelie Court](seelie.md). A grove-as-citadel: golden boughs, the **Tree of Stars** at its heart, dragonfly-wing lanterns lighting the paths.

Senaliesse is also where the rare cross-court convocations are held — most recently the [Court of Stars](court-of-stars.md) of 1501 DR, called to address the Far Realm breach.
