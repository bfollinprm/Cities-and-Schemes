# The Unseelie Court

The dark half of fey politics, ruled by the **Queen of Air and Darkness** from a midnight realm she never leaves. She speaks through emissaries — most often a **cold-eyed eladrin duke** who serves as her voice abroad. Hags, redcaps, gloom sprites, quicklings, and sylvan things best left unnamed answer to her court.

## Influence on the Sword Coast

- **Temptations.** Where the Seelie deal in boons, the Unseelie deal in *temptations*. A despairing noble finds a beautiful stranger with an otherworldly solution at exactly the moment he is willing to pay any price.
- **Dark dealings in Waterdeep.** Masked balls and moonlit revels in Waterdeep that end with mysterious disappearances are usually Unseelie work.
- **Fey-touched wilds.** Hags, redcaps, and worse have grown bolder in the [Misty Forest](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/misty-forest.md) and [Neverwinter Wood](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/neverwinter-wood.md). Will-o'-wisps and Unseelie sprites lead travelers astray in the Laughing Hollow near Daggerford.
- **Dream incursion.** The dreams of influential mortals can be touched by Unseelie glamour, nudging them toward fear or folly.

## How they bargain

The Unseelie keep the same rules as any fey (see [00-Campaign-Frame/fey-bargains.md](../../../00-Campaign-Frame/fey-bargains.md)) but their bargains tend toward the *cold favor*: a small, exquisite kindness given in exchange for a debt the recipient cannot calculate the size of. They are not wantonly cruel — many Unseelie believe mortal power must be checked and call their work *balance*. The results, however, often slide from mischief into madness.

## Notable powers

- **The Queen of Air and Darkness.** Veiled, distant, never personally present on the Material. Her pacts are mediated through her duke.
- **The Prince of Frost.** Cruel-beautiful Unseelie noble; attended the [Court of Stars](court-of-stars.md) and exchanged a somber nod with Titania's niece. Sent the Emerald Enclave a coded warning (an unseasonable Midsummer frost in the High Forest) about the Far Realm.

## Mortal contacts

The [Green Masquerade](green-masquerade.md) routes its Winter-leaning circle to the Unseelie. **Lady Duskrose** is the public face — a half-elf enchantress whose twilight-rose mask marks her as the Queen's preferred contact in Waterdeep. She quietly integrates lesser Unseelie (gloom sprites, a friendly quickling) into the city's shadowed corners. She walks a careful line: keeping the Queen's favor without letting the Masquerade slide into outright malice.

## Common-folk lore

Leave cream at the doorstep for the pixies. Avoid rings of toadstools. Do not eat what is offered without naming a price. Common-folk wisdom is *almost always right* about the Unseelie.
