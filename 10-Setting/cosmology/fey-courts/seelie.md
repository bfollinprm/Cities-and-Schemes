# The Seelie Court

The bright half of fey politics, ruled by [**Titania the Summer Queen**](titania.md) from her living palace at [**Senaliesse**](senaliesse.md), where golden boughs blossom around the **Tree of Stars**. Pixies, dryads, eladrin, and sylvan knights serve her. On the Sword Coast they patronize life, art, music, and growth — quietly.

## Influence on the Sword Coast

- **Boons.** A Summer-aligned fey will bless a village's crops, soften a winter, ripen an orchard out of season. The recipient may not know who paid for the boon.
- **Truces.** The Emerald Enclave maintains an uneasy truce with Seelie agents in the High Forest. Some druids of the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md) likely deal with them.
- **Enforcement.** Broken oaths and willful harm to sylvan glades draw Seelie ire — delivered with the velvet glove of curse and accident, not open war.

## How they bargain

Seelie fey are *capricious benefactors*, not safe ones. A Summer knight may bless a village one week and chase a whimsical quest the next, leaving promises half-kept. They follow the same rules as any fey — see [00-Campaign-Frame/fey-bargains.md](../../../00-Campaign-Frame/fey-bargains.md). What distinguishes them is that the *form* of their dealings tends toward beauty, music, and ceremony. A Seelie pact is sealed with a song or a danced figure, not a written word.

## Notable powers

- **Titania, the Summer Queen.** Her smile ripens crops; her frown sets forests ablaze. If the Feywild itself is harmed, she will dispatch her own avatar — a sobering threshold of escalation.
- **Lady Shandria of Astrazalian.** Titania's niece; seen at the [Court of Stars](court-of-stars.md), exchanged a knowing nod with the Unseelie Prince of Frost.
- **Lord Larethian.** Seelie knight, attended the Court of Stars; spoke at length with a dark fey witch on the same night, suggesting he is one of the brokers across the divide.

## Mortal contacts

The [Green Masquerade](green-masquerade.md) routes its Spring- and Summer-leaning circles to the Seelie. Lord Fox of Secomber and his Laughing Hollow contacts are the public-facing example: a country lordling who brokered the pact allowing the Hollow's pixies to send envoys to Waterdeep.
