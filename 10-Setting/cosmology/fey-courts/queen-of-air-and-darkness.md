# The Queen of Air and Darkness

Sovereign of the [Unseelie Court](unseelie.md), ruling from a midnight realm in the Feywild she is said never to leave. Counterpart and rival of [Titania](titania.md). Her court is the dark half of fey politics: hags, redcaps, gloom sprites, quicklings, and sylvan things best left unnamed.

## How she acts on the Sword Coast

She does not. Not in person. Her presence is mediated entirely through emissaries — most often a **cold-eyed eladrin duke** who serves as her voice abroad. The duke attended the [Court of Stars](court-of-stars.md) in 1501 DR speaking on her behalf; she did not. This is consistent: the Queen's bargains, when they reach mortals, have already passed through three layers of intermediary, and the Queen herself is unreachable to anyone short of an Archfey.

## Method

Her tools are *temptations* and the *cold favor* — a small, exquisite kindness given for a debt the recipient cannot calculate the size of. Many Unseelie call this *balance*; the recipient often calls it the worst decision of their life.

## Mortal-side contact

The [Green Masquerade](green-masquerade.md) routes its Winter-leaning circle through her. [Lady Duskrose](lady-duskrose.md) is the public face of that line in [Waterdeep](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/README.md).
