# Fey Courts

The political structure of the Feywild as it touches the Sword Coast. Three court traditions — Seelie, Unseelie, and Wild — operate independently in normal times, and convened jointly at the recent **Court of Stars** in response to the [Far Realm](../far-realm.md). One mortal society, the **Green Masquerade**, brokers between all three on Waterdeep's behalf.
