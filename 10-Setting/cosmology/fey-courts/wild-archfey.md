# The Wild Archfey

The independent powers of the Feywild — primal spirits of forest, stone, and storm — who owe loyalty to neither [Titania](titania.md)'s light nor the Queen's darkness. Some sages say they *are the land made manifest*. They are slow to stir, but unstoppable in defense of their domains.

## Influence on the Sword Coast

- **Untouched groves.** Their presence is felt in places like the **[Ardeep Forest](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/ardeep-forest.md)** weirwood circle — an ancient ring tended by unseen faerie guardians, said to be the meeting place of the **Lady of Twilight** and mortal druids.
- **The wild hunt.** A Northern ranger's tale almost always includes an ageless huntsman in the winter woods who issues a challenge or a boon before vanishing like morning frost — likely the **Huntsman of the Wild Hunt** himself.
- **Unpredictable rescue and unpredictable wrath.** A Wild Archfey may answer a desperate village facing magical blight, or unleash a hunt through the halls of a corrupt lord. They do not treat with kings.

## How they bargain

Wild Archfey are the most dangerous to bargain with: they have no court structure to appeal to, no etiquette to fall back on, no Lord above them to intercede. Their pacts are *personal* and *direct*. Standard fey-bargain rules apply (see [fey-bargains.md](../../../00-Campaign-Frame/fey-bargains.md)) but enforcement is the Archfey's mood.

## Notable powers

- **Oberon, the Green Lord.** Ancient being of the primal wood. Largely ignores mortal politics — *but acts when great swathes of the natural world are threatened by catastrophe*. When the High Forest shakes or the Evermoors fester unnaturally, a towering antlered figure has been seen striding through the trees. Some whisper that the [Green Masquerade's Emerald Regent](green-masquerade.md) is Oberon in disguise, testing mortals' mettle in Waterdeep — almost certainly false, but the rumor's persistence is itself a fact about Oberon's reputation.
- **The Huntsman of the Wild Hunt.** A presence more than a person; the embodiment of the Hunt itself.
- **The Lady of Twilight.** Tied to the [Ardeep Forest](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/ardeep-forest.md) weirwood circle; possibly a contact for druidic orders, including locally the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md).
- **The unnamed Wild Archfey at the Court of Stars.** Ragged, clad in moss and feathers; spoke the warning *"It has no name that tongues can safely speak. Name it not, lest it hear. But know… it hungers."* That warning is now the most-quoted line of the [Court of Stars](court-of-stars.md).

## The Carnival's patron

Mister Smiles' patron — the unnamed *Lord of the Feywild* who feeds on emotion through the [Carnival of Whispers](../../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/summary.md) — is most likely a Wild Archfey or an Unseelie, judging by appetite and method. The dossier does not confirm.
