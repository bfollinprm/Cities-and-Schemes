# Lady Duskrose

Mask-name of a half-elf enchantress in [the Green Masquerade](green-masquerade.md). Her twilight-rose porcelain mask marks her as the [Queen of Air and Darkness](unseelie.md)'s preferred contact in [Waterdeep](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/waterdeep/README.md) — the public face of the Masquerade's [Winter-leaning circle](unseelie.md).

## Role

Duskrose quietly integrates lesser Unseelie — gloom sprites, a friendly quickling, the occasional darker thing — into the city's shadowed corners. The work is delicate by design: the Queen wants standing in mortal politics, but the Masquerade's premise is that no single court owns the regent. Duskrose walks the line by keeping the Queen's favor without letting Waterdhavian fey-business slide into outright malice.

## Eyewitness to the Howling Masks

Duskrose was on the line with [Lord Fox of Secomber](seelie.md), [Aurelia "Northwind" Thorne](aurelia-thorne.md), and a half-elf bard from Evereska when the [Far Realm incursion](../far-realm.md) broke through the Autumnal Ball. The chandelier-magnified noon-light revealed two infiltrators; Duskrose was among the four who held until the ritual closed.
