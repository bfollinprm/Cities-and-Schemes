# Titania

**The Summer Queen.** Sovereign of the [Seelie Court](seelie.md). Rules from her living palace at [Senaliesse](senaliesse.md), where golden boughs blossom around the Tree of Stars.

A power high enough that her own avatar is the *threshold of escalation* — Titania does not normally take the field, and when she does, the consequences are felt across both planes. *Her smile ripens crops; her frown sets forests ablaze.*

## Recent acts

- **Convened the 1501 DR Court of Stars.** Titania named the threat *"an alien breach in the farthest glades"* — the Far Realm intrusion now driving the cross-court coalition.
- **Sylvan warding-songs.** Especially **Titania's hymn** — sung against the telepathic *"Hungry…"* assault from the breach.
