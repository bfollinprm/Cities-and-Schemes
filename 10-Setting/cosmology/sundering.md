# The Sundering

**The Second Sundering**, c. 1482–1487 DR. The cosmic event that defines the present campaign's metaphysical state.

## What it did

- **Cosmic rebalancing.** [Mystra](deities/mystra.md) withdrew. The Weave was wounded. Other deities re-sorted across the pantheon.
- **The personal cost.** [Laeral Silverhand's](../../20-Factions/masked-lords/people/laeral-silverhand.md) Chosen-of-Mystra power was permanently diminished. She has concealed it for two decades. The dossier reads this concealment as the city's central act of *splendor as theater*.
- **The planar consequence.** The Spellplague had pulled the [Feywild](realms/feywild.md) close to [Toril](realms/toril.md); the Sundering healed the Weave but the crossings *stabilized rather than closed*. The fey are still the closest neighbors any era has had.

## Why characters keep saying "post-Sundering"

It is the boundary between the world that was and the world that is. The Hellriders speak of pre-Sundering Elturel. The Open Lord's office runs on the assumption of post-Sundering concealment. The Green Masquerade was founded *because* of post-Sundering fey traffic. Every modern Waterdhavian institution dates from one side of the line or the other.
