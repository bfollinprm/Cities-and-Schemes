# Cloaks And Conspiracies

Normalized source files from the campaign bundle, kept in Linux-safe names for later ingestion.

## Index

- `baldurs-mouth.docx`: Newspaper-style source material tied to Baldur's Gate events.
- `dm-notes.docx`: GM-facing notes for the campaign arc.
- `dossier-baldurs-gate-1502-dr-a-city-reborn.docx`: City dossier on Baldur's Gate after the Netherbrain crisis.
- `dossier-sea-of-swords.docx`: Regional dossier on the Sea of Swords and nearby powers.
- `dossier-waterdeep-1502-dr-a-city-of-veiled-threats.docx`: City dossier on Waterdeep's political and social climate.
- `faction-quests.xlsx`: Spreadsheet of faction quest hooks and structure.
- `factions.docx`: Overview of campaign factions and relationships.
- `insurance-fraud-tips.docx`: In-world or campaign reference document on fraud tactics.
- `meditations-on-the-weave.docx`: Setting notes centered on the Weave.
- `party.docx`: Main party reference document.
- `player-companion.docx`: Player-facing companion guide for the campaign.
- `player-faction-details.docx`: Player-facing faction details and expectations.
- `the-fey-courts-and-the-green-masquerade-1502-dr.docx`: Dossier on the Fey Courts and Green Masquerade.
- `the-hinterlands-of-baldurs-gate-a-dossier.docx`: Dossier on the lands surrounding Baldur's Gate.
