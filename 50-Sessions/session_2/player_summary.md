# Session 2 — Player Summary

## The Weary Plowman

The party retreated to **The Weary Plowman**, a Rivington tavern run by the formidable [Lena](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/people/lena.md). They walked in on a tavern fight: a Baldurian mason named [Gareth](../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/people/gareth.md) was waving a copy of *Baldur's Mouth* and shouting at a tiefling youth named [Mikkel](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/mikkel.md) about refugees. Lena defused it cold.

Finneas paid for a round. Kaelan bought, on a whim, a 2 gp unlabeled bottle that smelled like clean air after a lightning strike — and got an immediate, intense stretch of sensory clarity from a single sip.

Bror noticed a steely woman with a [Guild](../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) tattoo winning at dice in the corner. She caught his eye and nodded.

## Lyra's invitation

A young paladin of Torm named [Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) arrived as messenger from [High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md): a formal invitation to the High Rider's Stand the next morning at 10:30. The party accepted.

## Vex's offer

Bror approached the Guild dicer with one of Kaelan's mystery glasses. She introduced herself as [Vex](../../20-Factions/guild/people/vex.md) and acknowledged the Coast Way handling. Kaelan and Elia cut Bror's cryptic intro short and asked plainly for paying work.

Vex offered a "trust-building" job: silence Gareth, the loud-mouthed mason. Intimidation or persuasion, not killing; quiet, not loud. Success would lead to a real conversation about real work.

Bror gathered rumors from Lena before turning in:

- The Hellriders' protest is still blocking [Wyrm's Crossing](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md).
- The Zhentarim are still running shakedowns on the Coast Way.
- The Flaming Fist seized 'Mad' Meggan's boat over the new tax. The [Free Traders](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_1/blockade-the-basilisk-gate.md) have blockaded the Basilisk Gate in retaliation, choking overland supply.

Most of the party turned in. Bror stayed in the common room to study Gareth, eventually following him home through Rivington's twisting streets and getting clocked by a neighbor; he played vagrant and slipped away. He now knows where Gareth lives. He slept on a street corner.
