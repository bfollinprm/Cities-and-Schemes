# Session 19

- **In-game week:** 5
- **Real-world date:** 2025-11-17
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - **The relief caravan.** Five magically self-propelled carts of high-grade food and sundries from Coster Hall, branded *Heroes of Rivington* in [Chionthar Consortium](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) sigil. The party is the official escort.
  - **In transit.** Bror and Kaelan distract the "crack troop" guards to inventory the cargo; Bror gets briefly hog-tied and stacked on a cart. Elia uses *Flock of Familiars* to scout — confirms genuine luxury goods, no traps. As the caravan reaches [Eastway](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md), the party signals to Mad Meggan's scouts that they are operating under duress.
  - **The standoff.** At the [Basilisk Gate](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) checkpoint, the caravan is halted by a Free Trader lieutenant and a Hellrider squad. The Consortium guards insist on delivering the goods *through* the gate. Bror tries to wave off the guards; they refuse to move.
  - **The pivot.** Finneas steps in. Successful Persuasion to both the guards and the gathering crowd: the starving masses are *here*, on this side of the gate; the Consortium's stated goals are best served by feeding them now.
  - **The free feast.** A guard named Jimmy relents. Children and families pour out of Eastway and [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). The luxury food is distributed under the open sky for the rest of the day. Consortium guards depart, mission "accomplished." The crowd spends the afternoon thanking the [Chionthar Consortium](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md) for the bounty. **Errinthal banks the PR.** The party banks the goodwill among the Free Traders and the people they actually intend to govern next week.
