# Session 19 — Player Summary

You leave Coster Hall as the Chionthar Consortium's official "relief escort." Five magically self-propelled carts of high-grade food and sundries roll out of the courtyard, branded with the *Heroes of Rivington* slogan and the Consortium sigil. You are the smiling face of [Errinthal's PR ambush](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md).

## In transit

Bror and Kaelan try to distract the "crack troop" guards long enough to inventory the cargo. Bror gets briefly hog-tied and stacked on top of a cart for his trouble. Elia rituals *Flock of Familiars* and sends a flock of scout-birds across the load — it's genuine: cheese, crackers, smoked fish, soap, mended boots. No traps. As the caravan rolls into [Eastway](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md), you signal Mad Meggan's scouts on rooftop overwatch that you are operating under duress.

## The Basilisk Gate standoff

At the gate, a Free Trader lieutenant and a squad of Hellriders halt the caravan. The Consortium guards insist on delivering the goods *through* the gate, into the upper district, where they will photograph well and feed only soldiers. Bror tries to wave the Consortium guards off; they refuse.

## Finn turns it

Finneas steps forward and addresses both the guards and the crowd that has gathered. Successful Persuasion. *"The starving masses are right here. The Consortium's stated mission is best served by feeding them now."* A guard named Jimmy relents, then nods, then stands aside. Children and families pour out of [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) and Eastway. The luxury food is distributed under the open sky for the rest of the day.

The Consortium guards leave with the carts emptied, photogenic, and on script. Errinthal banks the *Baldur's Mouth* front-page PR — *Chionthar Consortium feeds Eastway.* But the people who eat the food spend the afternoon thanking *the people who handed it out.* You bank the goodwill where it actually matters: the Free Trader rank-and-file and the Rivington locals you intend to govern next week.

The caravan rolls back to Coster Hall empty. Mission accomplished.
