# Session 8 — Player Summary

## A tense debrief

Late evening, the party reported back to [High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) at her [warehouse in Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). They explained that while [Jaredith](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) was injured enough to prevent him from leading a charge, [Zora](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/people/zora.md) had invited them to a private midnight meeting at a ruined watchtower. Bror revealed the **Whispering Stone**; Alena flew into a panic — it was an active magical listening device and had to be muffled at once. After Bror argued the impending ambush was her problem too, she agreed to come.

## Unproductive business

At the [Weary Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), Kaelan tried to report progress on the [Circus assignment to Vex](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/take-vex-circus-assignment.md); she immediately saw through the bluff. They asked if [the Guild](../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) could provide a way to break Zora's illusion. Vex's price: **6,000 gp** — exactly what she knew the party had. They left to prepare for the meeting. ([Guild — Retaliation on the River](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_2/retaliation-on-the-river.md).)

## Downtime and diversions

A few hours before midnight:

- **Spark** sought a masseuse to tend the duel injuries.
- **Bror** [tried to attune to the Whispering Stone](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/attune-to-the-whispering-stone.md) and was flooded with every voice in the city, leaving him **deafened**.
- **Finn** [broke into the Wondrous Trinkets and Arcane Emporium](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/burglarize-the-arcane-emporium.md), bypassed a magical trap on a lockbox, and lifted a **scroll of *Pass Without Trace*** with a hidden waterproof map of the [Brampton](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) smuggling tunnels marking a security weak point in the [Zhentarim Velvet Hand](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_2/consolidate-the-east-end.md).
- **Kaelan** sourced sack and cloth scraps to muffle the Stone better.
- **Elia** meditated.

## Ambush at the watchtower

At midnight on the cliffs above New Elturel, Bror's tremorsense pinged a pacing figure on the upper floor. Spark, Kaelan, and Elia went up the stairs openly; Bror and Finn held back to flank. Upstairs: Zora and a dazed Jaredith. Zora dropped her tiefling disguise — a severe-looking human woman with fanatical eyes — and two thugs emerged from the shadows below to cut off escape.

- **Spark** rage-tackled Zora, missed, went over the **60-foot cliff** and survived (rage soaked the fall).
- **Zora's *Cone of Cold*** caught Elia, Kaelan, Finneas, and Alena at the top of the stairs; Finneas dropped.
- **Bror** cast **magical Darkness** at the base of the stairs.
- **Finn** dropped one thug with a sneak-arrow; the other engaged her.
- **Elia**, *Devil's Sight* through the Darkness, blasted Zora; Bror followed up with a flurry.
- **Alena** *Bless*; Kaelan *Healing Word* on Finneas.
- Spark and Finn took down the second thug.
- Zora downed Elia and Bror; *Darkness* dropped; Zora downed Bror again.
- Final move: **Elia's *Dissonant Whispers*** compelled Zora to flee — she **teleported away**.

## Aftermath

Loot from the thugs: a few silvers and small clay tokens crudely shaped as a **cracked vial** — the [Plague of Remembrance](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) cell sigil. Spark tried to sow discord through the un-muffled Whispering Stone with a "performance" conversation implying Zora was a traitor; the bluff was unconvincing. The party escorted the deeply shaken Jaredith and the wounded Alena back to the Weary Plowman and committed to **five days of downtime**. Spark paid for the lodgings.
