# Session 8

- **In-game week:** 2
- **Real-world date:** 2025-09-01
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - Alena debrief in Rivington: she panics over the Whispering Stone, persuaded to come to the watchtower.
  - Vex debrief at the Weary Plowman: she sees through the Circus-progress lie, demands **6,000 gp** for an illusion-breaking spell scroll.
  - Bror's first attunement attempt to the Whispering Stone backfires — deafened.
  - Finn breaks into the Wondrous Trinkets and Arcane Emporium and recovers a **scroll of *Pass Without Trace*** with a hidden waterproof map of the Brampton smuggling tunnels under the [Velvet Hand](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_2/consolidate-the-east-end.md).
  - Watchtower ambush: Zora drops *Cone of Cold*; two thugs cut off escape; Bror's *Darkness*; Spark goes off the cliff in a rage tackle and survives. Elia's final *Dissonant Whispers* compels Zora to teleport away.
  - Loot: clay tokens shaped as cracked vials (Plague of Remembrance cell-tokens). Jaredith deeply shaken; party takes **five days of downtime**.
