# Session 14 — GM Summary

Lifted from `.context/converted/dm-notes.md` — Week 5, Session 14.

## Incident 1 — Planning the infiltration

- **Location.** Rivington (party safe house).
- **DM intent.** Present a clean choice between high-guard infiltration (Upper City Fixer) and high-chaos infiltration ([Brampton](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) sewers leading to the Velvet Hand). The party chose the latter — walking willingly into the middle of the Guild–Zhentarim gang war. Saving the *Pass without Trace* scroll was a smart, resource-conscious move.

## Incident 2 — The smokepowder plot

- **Location.** Sewers beneath Brampton, near the Velvet Hand.
- **Primary factions.** [The Guild](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md) (specifically [the River-Rats](../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_5/the-smokepowder-run.md)); Zhentarim (target).
- **Strategy.** Not a random encounter — the party stumbled into a critical faction operation. Nine-Fingers has tasked the River-Rats with smuggling captured explosives to rig the artery beneath the Velvet Hand and collapse the Zhentarim base from below. The Zhent markings on the crates are a "return-to-sender" taunt. The "idiot teenagers" *are* the River-Rats. The party witnessed the operation in motion and walked away with their primary intel (Vex's location) without burning their hand on it. Elia's read was tactically brilliant.

## Incident 3 — The automaton guardian

- **Location.** Flooded sewer cistern (unmapped).
- **Primary factions.** Unknown — ancient construct, possibly gnomish.
- **Construct objective.** Guard the pump and ensure it remains *broken*, keeping the cistern flooded. **It is a saboteur, not a guardian.** Its masters want this area submerged — possibly hiding an underwater entrance or protecting something in the cistern.
- **The "nanobots" exclamation.** Major strange clue. Possible links: the [Lantaner Concession](../../20-Factions/lantaner-concession/) gnomes, or the [Purified](../../20-Factions/purified/) "nano-cog" conspiracy theory. Park the thread.
- **Consequence.** Kaelan *fixed* the pump, undoing the construct's mission. Its masters will almost certainly notice the cistern draining; future quest hook seeded — what was the water hiding?

## Incident 4 — Debriefing Vex

- **Location.** [Gray Harbour](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md), [Blushing Mermaid](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md).
- **Vex's objective.** Lure the party to a secure private location to retrieve **the Whispering Stone** from Bror — by force if necessary. **This is the trap.** The party's three-week absence shifted faction standing: Nine-Fingers Keene authorized Vex to retrieve the psionic artifact "by any means necessary." Vex's surprise at seeing them is genuine; she likely thought them dead.
- **Strategy.** Friendly demeanor, free ale, willingness to share intel even after a Nat 20 — all ruse to build false trust. Her sarcastic *"I trust your vacation in the feylands was enjoyable"* shows she does not care about their alibi; she wants the artifact. The invitation to the "office" is the spring on the trap.
- **Party state.** Walking overconfident from recent successes, directly into a prepared ambush by a major faction lieutenant.
