# Session 14 — Player Summary

## Planning the route

Hunting Vex, the party weighs the Upper City Fixer (well-guarded, two gates) against the *Pass without Trace* sewer map, which threads under the Basilisk Gate into [Brampton](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md). They pick the sewers, take a short rest while Bror heals, and study the map.

## Sewers under Brampton

The journey is **tense and foul** — old blood, slaughterhouse runoff, contradictory graffiti (a skeletal hand, a three-pointed crown). They duck a mercenary patrol thumping along the street overhead and find nothing of value.

## The River-Rats and the smokepowder

Deeper, near the Velvet Hand, they cross a crew of six nervous teenagers hauling crates. Bror's keen eye reads them as **idiot kids, not killers** — Guild-affiliated. Kaelan whispers the Guild dock passphrase *"Hay-Weight,"* delivered in such a Shakespearean register that it echoes from every wall, and approaches with a *Light* spell.

The smugglers are Silas's crew — the **River-Rats** — running a load of **smokepowder** marked with Zhentarim seals along the path the map shows ends at the **Velvet Hand**. *"Taking it back where it came from."* The party puts the pieces together: this is [the Guild's retaliation against the Zhent](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md), and [the Rats are the delivery mechanism](../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_5/the-smokepowder-run.md). One youth confirms **Vex is hiding in the Dock Ward.** Elia argues hard against meddling in a gang war, and the party stands aside.

## The nanobot automaton

A flooded cistern blocks the way; a rusted iron pump squats in the middle. Elia sends an *Unseen Servant* ahead. The link snaps the moment it touches the pump, and an **eight-foot bronze-and-copper construct** rears out, hosing acid. Kaelan eats 23 acid damage and goes to 4 HP; Elia takes 11.

Elia covers their retreat with *Phantasmal Force* — a unicorn galloping into the water. The construct shrieks *"No, not the nanobots!"* and rounds on the illusion. Kaelan, on a **Nat 24 Insight**, realizes the thing isn't a guardian — it is **sabotaging the pump to keep the cistern flooded**. He sprints to the broken component and casts *Mending*. The pump roars back to life. Bror's whip-grapple fails; he sprints. The party clears the chamber as the water begins to drain.

## Arrival at Gray Harbour

They emerge into the [Gray Harbour](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) — a forest of masts, cranes, Kontor liveries, and Flaming Fist patrols. Elia identifies the [Blushing Mermaid](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md) and the [Counting House](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/counting-house.md).

Then they spot **Vex.** She is on a pier, flanked by two big finger-tattooed Guild dockworkers, **calmly ignoring an apoplectic Thark Consortium merchant** while her crew slow-unloads his barge. Bror strides up. Vex looks genuinely surprised to see them alive.

## At the Blushing Mermaid

Vex sweetly excuses herself, buys a round of cheap ale, and listens. The party reports Silas's crew and *"the weird automaton talking about nanobots."* Vex confirms Silas runs the **River-Rats** — *"idiot teenagers."* On Elia's Nat 20 Persuasion, she lets slip that Silas's crew recently fumbled a major job — losing documents being smuggled for *"some important folk"* — a failure **Nine-Fingers** would not forgive easily. Kaelan summarizes the circus and the Feywild dilation as their alibi.

Vex motions for them to save the rest for a quieter place: her **office** in a nearby warehouse. The session ends as the party rises to follow.
