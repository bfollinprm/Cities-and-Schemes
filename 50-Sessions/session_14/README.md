# Session 14

- **In-game week:** 5
- **Real-world date:** 2025-10-13
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - Planning. Vex's Upper City Fixer is too well-guarded; the party uses the *Pass without Trace* sewer map instead, routing under the Basilisk Gate to [Brampton](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md).
  - Sewers. Tense, foul, contradictory graffiti, no recent clues. They narrowly avoid a mercenary patrol overhead.
  - **Silas's crew, the [River-Rats](../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_5/the-smokepowder-run.md).** Six nervous teenagers smuggling Zhentarim-marked **smokepowder** toward the Velvet Hand. Bror's Nat 20 Insight clears them as non-hostile; Kaelan's Nat 20 Performance (with the Guild passphrase "Hay-Weight") puts them at ease. They confirm Vex is hiding in the Dock Ward.
  - Elia talks the party into letting the Rats walk — interfering in [the Guild's retaliation](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md) against the Zhentarim is suicide.
  - **The automaton.** Off-map cistern, broken pump. Elia's *Unseen Servant* trips an 8-foot bronze-and-copper construct armed with an acid nozzle. It nearly kills Kaelan. Elia's *Phantasmal Force* unicorn distracts it; **Kaelan's Nat 24 Insight** reveals the construct is *preventing* the pump's repair, not guarding it. He mends the pump while it screams about *"the nanobots!"* The party flees as the cistern drains.
  - Gray Harbour. The party emerges into the [Gray Harbour](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) docks. Elia identifies the [Blushing Mermaid](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md) and the [Counting House](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/counting-house.md).
  - **Vex.** On a pier, calmly ignoring an apoplectic Thark Consortium merchant, flanked by two finger-tattooed Guild dockworkers. Bror walks up; she sweetly excuses herself and leads the party into the Blushing Mermaid for cheap ale.
  - Debrief. Elia's Nat 20 Persuasion pries loose that Silas lost documents for "important folk" — a failure Nine-Fingers will not forgive. Kaelan reports the circus and the three-week Feywild dilation. Vex saves "the juicy stuff" for her **office** in a nearby warehouse.
  - Session ends as the party prepares to follow her in.
