# Session 27

- **In-game week:** 6
- **Real-world date:** 2026-01-12
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark, Jaredith Heartsong (NPC ally; departing this session)
- **Notable beats:**
  - **The road back.** The party walks Alfira down from Dusthawk Hill at dawn. Theological discussion of the Cult of the Dead Three; Bror notes Spark's apparent age and asks (writing on a slate) whether they might be an incarnation of Karlach. Alfira appears nonplussed — and quietly considers the possibility.
  - **The Weary Plowman as respite.** Alfira agrees to rest at [the Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) instead of immediately returning to the High Rider's Stand. She owes the party a great debt.
  - **Jaredith departs.** [Jaredith Heartsong](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) chooses this moment to leave the adventuring party, staying in New Elturel to oversee the continued construction of the [Phoenix Temple](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_6/the-foundation-stone.md) in Alfira's absence. The party's legacy in New Elturel is now "builders" rather than just "mercenaries."
  - **Splitting the web.** The group splits at the Plowman:
    - **Bror, Elia, and Finn** ride for [Twin Songs](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) and the Penumbra Playhouse to deliver Bror's Whispering Stone secret to [Mother Nocturne](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md). The Night Mother wants the **physical manifestation** of the secret — *the sentient source* — not just the word. The debt is unpaid.
    - **Kaelan** crosses to the Lower City to bring Ettvard Needle the Singa exposé. He finds the [Baldur's Mouth](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/summary.md) print shop **ransacked**; [Ettvard Needle](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) is missing. A hidden note hints he has fled to the dark crypts under [Tumbledown](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md), on the western bank of Dusthawk Hill.
  - **Two open threads.** Bror owes a sentient source to the Night Mother. Ettvard Needle is missing.
