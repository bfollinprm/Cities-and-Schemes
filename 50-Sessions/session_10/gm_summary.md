# Session 10 — GM Summary

Lifted from `.context/converted/dm-notes.md` — Week 3, Session 10.

## Incident 1 — Evading the Fist

Lena's misdirection is genuine. She is a neutral but loyal innkeeper whose reputation depends on protecting paying tenants from extracurricular trouble. Her cover for the party is sincere — but it is a one-time favor she now considers spent. The Fist warrant is real and active; the party is now publicly fugitive in the Outer City and cannot use the Plowman as a public safehouse without putting Lena at material risk.

## Incident 2 — Investigation at the Circus of the Last Days

- **Location.** An extraplanar or semi-planar circus encamped on the outskirts of [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). Barriers between Material and Ethereal are notably thin here.
- **Primary factions.** [The Guild](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_3/recover-the-asset.md) is using the party as deniable assets for intelligence-gathering. The lookout's minimal interaction ("Go in") confirms the brief is *explore and report,* not directed strike. The [Circus / Carnival of Whispers](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_3/sowing-sweet-sorrows.md) is harvesting emotional energy on two scales: mass siphoning by the main show, and personalized transactions in side tents.

### Strategy

The Circus's overall play is **emotional vampirism**:

1. **Attraction.** Lure the downtrodden with the promise of forgetting their pain.
2. **Harvesting.** Use mesmerizing performances to pull powerful emotion from the crowd. Renewable resource — patrons return for more.
3. **Targeted transactions.** For "discerning" customers (powerful souls / destinies), bespoke services in side tents. Higher cost, longer hooks.

### NPC objectives

- **[The mirror-tent proprietor](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/mirror-tent-proprietor.md).** Collect potent secrets and memories (his "true currency"). Targets individuals with deep regret and ambition. Both bargains accepted; collection deferred.
- **[Lucretius](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/lucretius.md).** Charismatic ringmaster, magically-amplified voice. Sets the stage for the harvest. His genuine curiosity at Bror suggests recognition of unusual destiny.
- **[Zara the Sorrow-Eater](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/zara.md).** Mass harvest. Empowered directly by the grief she pulls.

### Other considerations

- **Planar bleed.** Solid-moonlight effects, ethereal ghosts in the audience. The Circus is not entirely Material — likely a Feywild traveling pocket or Shadowfell-touched venue.
- **Resonating stones.** Both Elia's and Bror's stones reacted strongly. Elia's is tied to the Shadowfell-adjacent forgotten god; Bror's to Shar via [Mother Nocturne](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md). The Circus taps similar entropic / negative-emotion currents.
- **Lyra Sunstone.** A senior Hellrider quartermaster is now under Carnival influence — a potential vector to extend Circus reach into Outer City politics, or simply evidence of how deep the despair runs.

## Incident 3 — The Feywild Portal (combat)

- **Location.** Backstage at the Circus.
- **Primary faction.** Carnival of Whispers.
- **NPC objectives.**
  - **Grinmaw and the displacer beasts:** non-verbal security, a living wall to filter unauthorized entry.
  - **Lucretius:** the encounter is an *entrance exam*, not an assassination. He recognizes Bror and Elia as individuals of interest because of their bargains and powerful artifacts. If they cannot push past simple guards they are not worth his time.
- **Strategy.** Curated access. Lucretius is a collector of rare ephemera and does not want common thugs in his collection. Kaelan's *Silence* disrupted the intended Grinmaw–displacer voice synergy and forced a chaotic physical fight the party could exploit.

## Secrets owed

Both bargains accepted at the mirror tent are **owed in-fiction** (player-companion records the actual secret each PC told). The proprietor will eventually surface to collect.

- **Elia.** Her trip to Durlag's Tower; what she found there and what she promised to gain it.
- **Bror.** The truth about the sealed wall in his clan's hold and what spoke to him from the dark.
