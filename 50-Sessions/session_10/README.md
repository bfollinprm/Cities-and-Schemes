# Session 10

- **In-game week:** 3
- **Real-world date:** 2025-09-15
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - Fist patrol arrives at the [Weary Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) with a warrant for the [public sale of the Plague cultist to Mad Meggan](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md). Lena misdirects the soldiers; the party escapes via the back alley.
  - Party walks the Circus of the Last Days on [Vex's investigation assignment](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/take-vex-circus-assignment.md). Bror confirms the Guild lookout (token in a wall crevice). Sensory overload inside; *Detect Magic* and *See Invisibility* read the entire grounds as planar-bleed. Elia's obsidian shard hums in resonance with Bror's Whispering Stone for the first time.
  - **Mirror-tent bargains.** Elia and Bror each accept fey bargains with [the grey-skinned elven proprietor](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/mirror-tent-proprietor.md): a true secret each, owed at a later date, in exchange for visions and the path to them. Elia sees herself as a master broker of the Undercellar; Bror is named "Shadow Stone" without being told.
  - Big-Top main act: ringmaster [Lucretius](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/lucretius.md) gives a "beautiful lie" speech and shows genuine curiosity at Bror; [Zara the Sorrow-Eater](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/zara.md) closes with a grief-siphon dance. Hellrider Quartermaster [Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) is among the patrons led backstage afterward, visibly diminished.
  - Party follows backstage. Finn slips ahead in disguise; the rest go stealth. They find a Feywild gateway of bone-white wood guarded by **Grinmaw** (a hulking silent clown) and two displacer beasts. Lucretius names Bror and Elia by their bargains and steps through, daring them to follow.
  - **Combat at the portal.** Kaelan *Silences* Grinmaw's commands; Spark shoves him; Bror dashes through; Finn slips through. The party flees through the gateway under fire.
