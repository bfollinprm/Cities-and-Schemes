# Session 10 — Player Summary

## The Fist comes for the warrant

The session opens the morning after [Jaredith's ballad ended the Hellrider protest](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/witness-jaredith-end-the-protest.md). The party lingers outside the [Weary Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). A four-man Flaming Fist patrol marches purposefully toward the door: clearly serving the warrant for [insubordination over the Mad Meggan deal](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md). The party speaks with **Lena** the proprietor; she points them at a back-alley exit. They slip out as the Fist enters; Lena sells the misdirection convincingly. The party walks away publicly fugitive but unbloodied.

## The Circus of the Last Days

The party makes for [the Circus encampment east of Rivington](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_3/sowing-sweet-sorrows.md) to fulfill [Vex's assignment](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/take-vex-circus-assignment.md). Kaelan's previous magically-altered ticket lets them in. Bror approaches the Guild lookout (the same dwarf as last week); the man tells him "Go in" and leaves a Guild token in a wall crevice that Bror discreetly retrieves.

Inside, the senses cut sideways: a beast's roar, a calliope laced with fear, popcorn and rust. Kaelan reads the grounds as suffused with magic; *See Invisibility* reveals ethereal ghosts in the bleachers. Elia's obsidian shard hums in resonance with Bror's Whispering Stone for the first time.

### The mirror tent

A small violet silk tent under the placard *"A glimpse of what you deserve"* draws Elia and Bror in. [The grey-skinned elven proprietor](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/mirror-tent-proprietor.md) is surrounded by hundreds of floating mirrors. They show fragments of the past and possible futures.

- **Elia** picks up an ornate copper hand mirror and sees a perfected version of herself — master broker of the Undercellar, commanding the respect of the Guildmaster herself. The proprietor offers the path to that future for one true secret never told another soul. Elia accepts.
- **Bror** is drawn to a square of polished obsidian and sees himself alone in a silent, dead dwarven hold commanding shadows. The proprietor names him "Shadow Stone" without being told and offers the path for one secret about the oath he swore in the dark. Bror accepts.

Both bargains are **owed but not yet paid**.

### The Big Top

The crowd is captivated by [Lucretius](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/lucretius.md) — tall, pale, in shadow-black velvet — who gives a speech about the circus as a "beautiful lie" to help people forget their sorrows. His gaze finds Bror; Bror feels a flicker of genuine curiosity from him.

The closing act is [Zara the Sorrow-Eater](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/zara.md). Her mournful dance pulls faint gray wisps of grief out of the audience and into her. As the act ends, [Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) — Alena's quartermaster — is among a small group led backstage. Kaelan watches: Lyra looks **diminished**, Zara more vibrant.

## Forcing the portal

The party follows. Finn moves under disguise; the rest stealth. The backstage area is a Feywild interzone: goblins, blink dogs, pixies. Elia warns the party not to give true names or eat fey food. They find Zara leading the patrons through a shimmering bone-white wood portal — guarded by a hulking silent clown ("Grinmaw") and two displacer beasts. Lucretius arrives, recognizes Bror and Elia by their bargains, and steps through, daring them to follow.

Combat. Bror dashes through the portal. Kaelan spends Edge to act first and *Silences* Grinmaw's voice. Elia summons illusory "displacer kittens" (the real beasts find them charming). One real displacer mauls Kaelan with claws and tentacles. Spark rage-charges Grinmaw and shoves him to the ground; Finn disengages and slips through the portal. Grinmaw rises and wounds Spark; Kaelan caps the round with *Burning Hands* on Grinmaw and a beast. Elia, Spark, and Kaelan escape through the gate. The Material Plane closes behind them.
