# Session 15 — Player Summary

## A quieter spot

After ale at the [Blushing Mermaid](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md), Vex leads the party out into the [Gray Harbour](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) night and into a **non-descript warehouse** in the Dock Ward. Finn casts *Detect Magic* as a ritual on the threshold — the place is mundane. That should have been comforting.

## The ambush

Vex's two heavyset Guild guards step in behind the party and plant themselves at the only door. Elia and Bror, scanning the dark rafters with darkvision, count **four archers** above and in the deep shadows. Vex's smile thins; the arrangement was always for tonight. She demands [Bror's Whispering Stone](../../20-Factions/guild/people/vex.md) for the Guild. Bror refuses. Vex orders the kill.

## Combat in the dark

Four arrows fly. Finn eats six; the rest miss. **Bror drops *Darkness* on the party** — only he sees through it. Vex rushes into the sphere to stab Bror, missing, then hides. Kaelan paints the door-guards with *Faerie Fire*. The archers fire again and poison Elia. Bror burns an Edge to flick on **Tremorsense**, finds Vex by the floor's pulse, and clubs her for 10. Finn steps clear of the *Darkness*, rips a chair off the wall, and hurls it at her for 13. Vex barks at her men to drag the party into the light; Elia and Finn shake off the grapples.

Round two arrives mean. Finn whips out, takes two readied arrows for 12, and lands the **killing blow on Vex**. Elia ices one archer dead in a freezing blast. Kaelan heals himself and ends the last door-guard with *Sacred Flame*. The two surviving archers throw down their bows and bolt; Elia crits one, but the last one slips deep into the warehouse and out a back hatch.

## The spoils of treachery

They have minutes before the runner reports. They tear the office apart.

**On Vex.** Heavy iron key. 24 gp. A mechanical messenger bird with a sealed scroll tube. A cipher ledger. The bird's unsent message reads *"Target unreliable as suspected retrieving artifact. Now will report. When secure."* — proof Vex was selling them out the entire time.

**The desk, unlocked.** A sewer map of [Brampton](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) marked *"Roah's new rat-nest. Silas's crew has the present. Awaiting confirmation."* Kaelan's Nat 20 History identifies **Roah Moonglow** as the Zhentarim's Baldur's Gate chief. [The smokepowder run is the Guild burying the Velvet Hand](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md) — and the River-Rats with it.

**The desk, locked.** Vex's key opens it. *File 1:* stolen Guild intel on [the Chionthar Consortium's "native's problem"](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md), with a note: *"hard proof of the print job. Or the print blocks"* to expose **Factor Errinthal** to *Baldur's Mouth*. The party already has those blocks. *File 2:* Finn cracks Vex's Thieves' Cant ledger — her Rivington contacts and movements. *File 3:* **kompromat on Farlin**, the Guild's Eastway Ward Boss, covering for Silas and skimming saffron from the Amnian Kontor.

## A precarious position

They have proof Vex was a traitor; proof of the smokepowder plot; the missing leverage for Errinthal; and blackmail on Farlin. They have also killed a Guild Lieutenant, and a witness is loose. Elia suggests burning the warehouse. The party walks out into the dark and starts arguing about what to do before sunrise.
