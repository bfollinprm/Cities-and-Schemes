# Session 15

- **In-game week:** 5
- **Real-world date:** 2025-10-20
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - Vex leads the party from the [Blushing Mermaid](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md) into a "non-descript warehouse" in the [Gray Harbour](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) Dock Ward. Finn rituals *Detect Magic* — building is mundane.
  - **The trap.** Two Guild guards block the only door. Elia and Bror clock four archers in the rafters via darkvision. Vex demands the [Whispering Stone](../../20-Factions/guild/people/vex.md) from Bror. He refuses; she gives the order.
  - Combat in Bror's *Darkness*. Vex slips into the sphere; Bror's **Tremorsense** pinpoints her for 10. *Faerie Fire* lights the door-guards. Finn steps out, hurls a chair at Vex for 13.
  - **Vex dies.** Finn whips the killing blow. Elia ices an archer; Kaelan ends the last guard with *Sacred Flame*. Two archers flee — one Elia crits, one escapes deeper into the warehouse.
  - **Looting Vex's body.** Iron key, 24 gp, mechanical bird with a scroll tube, ciphered ledger. The unsent scroll: *"Target unreliable as suspected retrieving artifact. Now will report. When secure."* Confirmed: she was always going to betray them.
  - **Looting the desk.** Sewer map of [Brampton](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) marked *"Roah's new rat-nest. Silas's crew has the present."* Kaelan's Nat 20 History: Roah Moonglow, Zhentarim chief in Baldur's Gate. The [Guild's smokepowder run](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md) lands in focus.
  - **Locked drawer (Vex's key).** File 1: stolen Guild intel on the [Chionthar Consortium's "native's problem"](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md), needing "print blocks" to expose **Factor Errinthal** to the *Baldur's Mouth*. File 2: Finn deciphers Vex's Rivington appointment book via Thieves' Cant. File 3: **kompromat on Farlin**, the Guild's Eastway Ward Boss — covering for Silas, skimming the Amnian Kontor.
  - The party leaves the warehouse with a Guild Lieutenant dead, one witness loose, and four explosive secrets in their packs. Elia floats burning the building. They step out into the night.
