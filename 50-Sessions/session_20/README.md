# Session 20

- **In-game week:** 5
- **Real-world date:** 2025-11-24
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - **The Rivington Gambit.** Plan formalized: rather than flee, the party will install **Finneas Kaller** as the new Guild boss of [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), with [Simbar](../../20-Factions/guild/people/simbar.md) as nominal puppet, and pitch [Nine-Fingers Keene](../../20-Factions/guild/people/astele-keene.md) on the upgrade. Sales pitch: more profitable, more stable, more loyal — a "public-service" Guild presence delivering actual security and economic stability while still cutting the proper percentages upstream.
  - **The Triple Monopoly.** Trade in the city is a three-way map. The Archduke owns the land routes (currently blockaded). The [Chionthar Consortium](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md) owns the river (currently haunted by a water weird). Foreign Kontors own the ocean, duty-free.
  - **Errinthal's shadow funding.** Reveal: [Factor Errinthal](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) has been *quietly funding* ['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md). He doesn't want her to win. He wants the blockade to grind on indefinitely so land-trade competitors die and his river monopoly becomes permanent.
  - **The Whispering Stone hums.** Bror — now attuned, painfully — picks up alien-language fragments from a wider eavesdropping network. The transmissions match recent *Baldur's Mouth* "scoops." [Ettvard Needle](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md)'s [propaganda surge](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_5/the-githyanki-garrison.md) has a sound. The party now has a passive intercept on the city's most weaponized newsroom.
  - **The Ember's temple.** Reports from [New Elturel](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md): [the Ember](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) is no longer a street-corner preacher. The [Phoenix Gate temple](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_5/the-survivors-sermon.md) is rising from foundation stones in real time; the Followers of the Phoenix are organized, disciplined, building.
  - **Iron Throne in the rumor mill.** The [Iron Throne's pivot](../../20-Factions/iron-throne/activity/week_5/the-rumor-mill.md) — selling mercenary intel to both sides out of the [Blushing Mermaid](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/blushing-mermaid.md) — is on the Rivington-takeover map as both a buyer and a competitor.
