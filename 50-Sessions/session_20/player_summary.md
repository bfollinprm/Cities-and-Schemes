# Session 20 — Player Summary

## The Gambit

You commit. Rather than flee Nine-Fingers's wrath for [killing Vex](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_5/kill-vex-and-loot-her-office.md), you will install [Finneas](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/finn.md) as the new Guild boss of [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). Simbar is the public face; Finneas runs the ward. The pitch to [Nine-Fingers](../../20-Factions/guild/people/astele-keene.md) writes itself: a Rivington that is more profitable, more stable, and more loyal than Vex's was — a Guild presence that delivers real security and real economic uplift to its locals, while still cutting the expected percentages upstream. **A public-service crime franchise.**

The complication: Vex's former inner-circle fixers — [Glass Malakor](../../20-Factions/guild/people/glass-malakor.md) (intelligence), [Runa "The Hook"](../../20-Factions/guild/people/runa-the-hook.md) (enforcement), and [Belvin "Soot" Hamhock](../../20-Factions/guild/people/belvin-soot-hamhock.md) (logistics) — each have their own conditions before they will follow an outsider's lead.

## The Triple Monopoly

Strategic map clarifies. Trade in the city is split three ways — the Archduke owns the land (currently blockaded by the Free Traders), the [Chionthar Consortium](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) owns the river (currently blocked by *an actual water elemental*), and the foreign Kontors own the ocean by duty-free treaty. **All three are currently broken.** The party is, by accident, the only group on the inside of all three.

## Errinthal's shadow funding

Intelligence shake-out. [Factor Errinthal](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) has been *quietly funding* ['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md). Not to help her win. To keep the blockade alive indefinitely. Every day land trade fails is another day the Chionthar's river monopoly compounds. Meggan's victory and the Archduke's victory are both bad for him; *stalemate is his goal.*

## The Whispering Stone

Bror picks up alien-language fragments through the [Whispering Stone](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_5/monitor-the-tithe.md) — and recognizes them. They are the same items the *Baldur's Mouth* has been printing as "scoops." [Ettvard Needle's](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) propaganda surge has a sound, and Bror is now its passive listener.

## The Ember

Reports from [New Elturel](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md): [the Ember](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) is no longer running street sermons. [Phoenix Gate temple construction is underway](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_5/the-survivors-sermon.md). The Followers are disciplined, organized, building.
