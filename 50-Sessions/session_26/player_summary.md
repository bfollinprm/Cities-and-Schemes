# Session 26 — Player Summary

## The Counting House

We charge through the doors. Singa rises from the ledger desk; her quill is still in her hand. The bone-piles in the corners of the chamber begin to assemble — skeletons, a dozen of them, animated by the chamber's standing ritual. Spark catches an opportunity-attack from one as he barrels past.

Singa watches us calmly.

> "We were waiting for you."

## The Soul-Link

Singa teleports across the room and slashes Kaelan with the scythe-quill. The wound bleeds wrong — black silt at the edges. She teleports again, strikes Jaredith. Every strike Singa lands buys her another instant.

Then the trap. Spark hits Singa hard with the greatsword. Singa does not bleed. **Alfira screams.**

Singa has bound a soul-link onto Alfira above the pit. *Damage dealt to Singa is mirrored onto Alfira.* If we kill Singa the wrong way, Alfira goes first.

We adjust.

## Burn the books

Bror, voiceless, scrawls on his palm and shows it to Elia: **BURN THE BOOKS.** The bloody-vellum ledgers stacked at Singa's desk are *part of* the soul-link working. Elia *Eldritch-Blasts* the largest stack into the brazier and the chamber's standing ritual flickers. Singa hisses for the first time.

Bror shadow-steps to the chamber ceiling, opens his pack, and lets a satchel of smokepowder drop.

## Bror's gambol

Then Bror shadow-steps a second time, behind Singa, **grapples her** in a bear-hold, and drives both of them sideways off the platform and **into the obsidian pit** with the smokepowder satchel still falling. The smokepowder catches at the bottom. The pit becomes a furnace.

We are nearly out of resources. Spark is at half hit-points. Kaelan has burned every spell slot of fourth and fifth level. Elia has *Fractured ID* and cannot Counterspell. The Soul-Link is fluctuating but still active.

## The Beetle's bargain

A small voice at Elia's elbow.

> "Surrender the ledger. The physical book. Hand it to me. I will take it as paid debt. Your friends walk out alive. The curses lift. The Soul-Link drops."

Elia weighs it for half a heartbeat and **agrees**. The Tithe Ledger lifts itself off her belt and *unmakes itself* into the air. The party-wide *Malaise* lifts. Elia's *Fractured ID* lifts. Spark's exhaustion lifts. Bror, climbing out of the pit, can speak again on the next attack.

Singa, dazed in the pit, freezes for a beat as her ledger goes.

## The finish

Kaelan unleashes a fifth-level **Fireball** into the pit. Jaredith follows with a **Chromatic Orb** of fire. Singa burns in the obsidian. The shards shatter on the ritual side and **the trapped souls release** in a long low silver wail that pours up out of the pit and out of the chamber roof toward the New Elturel sky.

The chamber goes silent.

## Aftermath

Alfira drops on her chains. The chains release; she falls toward the obsidian; Bror catches her with one arm at the rim. She is not breathing. Kaelan has *Revivify* left, but no diamond.

Bror, voice raw, points at the pit's bottom. There is a hand-sized **diamond** half-buried in the silt under where Singa burned. Spark fishes it out. Kaelan casts. Alfira gasps awake.

We have rescued her. Singa is dead. The cell on Dusthawk Hill is broken. The Counting House is rubble and ash, with a faint smell of pie still hanging in the air from yesterday morning's alley fight.
