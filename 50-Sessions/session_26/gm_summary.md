# Session 26 — GM Summary

## Setting

The session spanned the transition from the verticality and isolation of [Dusthawk Hill](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) back to the crowded, paranoid atmosphere of the High Rider's Stand in [New Elturel](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md).

## Primary factions

- **[Takers of the Tithe / Bhaalists](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/summary.md).** Specifically [Singa](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/people/singa.md)'s cell. Though Singa is dead, the cult successfully reclaimed their "holy" ledger.
- **[Hellriders of New Elturel](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md).** Currently dealing with the psychological fallout of Alfira's abduction and the growing realization of internal betrayal.
- **[Zhentarim](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/summary.md).** Operating through Roah Moonglow. Their influence is felt through the supply chain and the professional nature of the cleanup poison.
- **[Council of Four](../../20-Factions/council-of-four/summary.md).** Represented indirectly by the legal and political weight of the missing Tithe Ledger.

## Party actions

- **The revivification.** Kaelan brought Alfira back to life, **preventing the cult from claiming her as a martyr** — a central pillar of their strategy to incite civil war.
- **The sacrifice.** Elia surrendered the **physical Tithe Ledger** to [the Skittering Beetle](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/people/the-skittering-beetle.md). Major narrative pivot — the party prioritized immediate survival over long-term legal evidence.
- **Intelligence gathering.** The party extracted crucial information from the Spotter, confirming the mole in the Hellrider camp and the link between [Zhentarim records](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_6/the-ledger-logic.md) and Bhaalist "audits."
- **Communications.** Bror, despite being silenced, maintained party cohesion through written notes, emphasizing the need for haste.

## Antagonist objectives

- **The Broker (Beetle).** Successfully re-acquired the Tithe Ledger. For the cult, the ledger is not just evidence — it is a ritual tool. Reclaiming it "settles the books" and protects the identities of high-level Patriars feeding the cult names.
- **Roah Moonglow.** Damage control. Now that the Counting House has been compromised, the Zhentarim's priority is to eliminate the mole at the High Rider's Stand before the party can identify them.
- **High Watcher Alena.** Maintain the fragile peace of the refugee camp while rooting out the traitor without causing mass panic or a witch-hunt.

## Key motivations & strategy

- **The cult's win-state.** Even with Singa dead, the cult counts this a victory. They have *suppressed the evidence* of their conspiracy and demonstrated they can touch the Ember of the refugee movement.
- **The mole's strategy.** Use of poison in rations suggests a low-visibility but high-access position (cooks, quartermasters, logistics officers). Motivation: fear of the cult, or Zhentarim gold.
- **The Broker's fairness.** The Beetle operates on a "fair trade" philosophy. It didn't kill the party because it got what it wanted — the Ledger. **No personal animosity toward the heroes**, only a professional interest in souls and debts. *Expect to see the Beetle again.*

## Other considerations

- **The evidence gap.** Loss of the ledger means the party cannot currently prove the tax-murder conspiracy to the Council of Four. May force them into more "extralegal" actions to surface the truth.
- **Bror's silence.** Lost his voice to the Beetle's bargain. Significant roleplay hurdle highlighting the cost of dealing with the Bhaalists. Forces the party to rely on written communication, which can be intercepted. *(Note: voice restored at end of fight when the broader Beetle-trade resolved.)*
- **Alfira's connection.** As someone who has been "audited" and returned, Alfira may now be **spiritually marked** — possibly a beacon for other Bhaalist cells, or granting her strange insights into their next moves.
