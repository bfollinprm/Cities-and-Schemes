# Session 26

- **In-game week:** 6
- **Real-world date:** 2026-01-05
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark, Jaredith Heartsong (NPC ally)
- **Notable beats:**
  - **The Counting House.** The party charges in. Singa is gaunt and grey-robed at her ledger; Alfira is suspended over the obsidian pit; bones rise as **skeletons** from piles in the corners.
  - **Singa's Soul-Link.** Singa transfers damage dealt to her *directly to Alfira* via a soul-link. The party fights uphill — every hit on Singa risks killing the Ember.
  - **Bror's gambol.** Unable to speak, Bror writes a note to Elia: *"BURN THE BOOKS."* Elia begins torching the bloody-vellum stacks. Bror shadow-steps to the ceiling, drops a satchel of smokepowder, then shadow-steps behind Singa, **grapples her**, and drives them both into the obsidian pit to trigger the explosion.
  - **The Beetle's bargain.** With the party near defeat from cumulative damage and the Soul-Link's mirrored harm, [the Skittering Beetle](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/people/the-skittering-beetle.md) offers Elia a deal: **surrender the physical Tithe Ledger**, save the party. Elia agrees. The ledger vanishes. Singa freezes. Every party member's debilitating curse / debt lifts at once.
  - **The finish.** Kaelan unleashes *Fireball* into the pit. Jaredith follows with a *Chromatic Orb* of fire. Singa burns. Obsidian shards shatter and the trapped souls release in a low silver howl.
  - **Aftermath.** Alfira is initially lifeless. Kaelan revives her with *Revivify*, fueled by a 300-gp diamond Bror finds in the pit's sand. Singa is dead. The Bhaalist cell on Dusthawk Hill is broken.
