# Session 17 — Player Summary

## The morning briefing

[Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) brings you to the command wagon at first light. ['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) and [Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) have split your week into two operations.

- **Meggan's job.** Take Vex's stolen Guild intel — proof of the [Chionthar Consortium's anti-refugee print campaign](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md) — and ultimatum [Factor Errinthal](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) into withdrawing his backing of the Archduke. Plan B: leak it to the Amnian Kontor and crack the patriar coalition in print.
- **Alena's job.** Use the kompromat on Farlin to force the Eastway Ward Boss to plant the captured smokepowder as defensive mines on the routes the Sembian mercenaries will use to attack the blockade.

Lyra Sunstone reminds you: none of this saves you from Nine-Fingers when she finds out you killed Vex. Meggan agrees, then offers the way through — **take over Rivington.** Double-rate protection, if you do it well enough, makes you too useful to kill.

## The smokepowder split

Finn wants the explosives first. You drop into the sewers and find [Silas](../../20-Factions/river-rats/people/silas-the-eel.md)'s [River Rats](../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_5/the-smokepowder-run.md) hauling enough powder to level a city block — *far* more than the Velvet Hand demolition warrants. The intent is now legible: Nine-Fingers meant the blast to take out **both** the Zhentarim base *and* Silas's incompetents in a single morning. You take the powder off their hands.

You split it three ways. A backpack apiece for personal use, with the remainder rendered inert. Elia summons spectral oxen and hauls one third through the sewers back to Eastway for Alena's mining plan. The last third stays under the Velvet Hand to execute the original mission and maintain your Guild cover. **Finn lights the fuse and cartwheels away.** [The Velvet Hand collapses on Roah Moonglow's safehouse.](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_5/the-financial-front.md)

## Confronting Farlin

You find Farlin in [Eastway](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md). Bror rattles him; Kaelan appeals to his bottom line; the kompromat finishes it. Farlin agrees to plant the mines under the Sembian advance routes. **Eastway is now a defensive minefield.** The first joint operation of the [Hellrider–Free Trader alliance](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) is in the ground before sundown.
