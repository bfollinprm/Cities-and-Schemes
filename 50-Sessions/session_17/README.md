# Session 17

- **In-game week:** 5
- **Real-world date:** 2025-11-03
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - **Morning briefing.** [Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) wakes the party at the Basilisk Gate camp. Two parallel objectives from ['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) and [Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md): break the [Chionthar Consortium](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) with the stolen intel; mine the Eastway approach with the captured smokepowder.
  - **Lyra's warning.** Kompromat and political leverage do not save the party from Nine-Fingers. Meggan offers a path: take over Rivington, double protection payments, make yourselves too useful to kill.
  - **Sewers.** Party tracks the smokepowder shipment. They find Silas's [River Rats](../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_5/the-smokepowder-run.md) — terrified teenagers hauling enough explosive to **level a city block.** Way more than a Velvet Hand demolition needs. Realization: Nine-Fingers planned to blow the Zhentarim *and* Silas's crew at once.
  - **The split.** Three thirds. (1) Personal stash for the party, balance dumped to inert. (2) Spectral oxen haul one third back to [Eastway](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) for Alena's mine plan. (3) Final third stays at the Velvet Hand to execute the cover-mission. **Finn lights the fuse, cartwheels away.** [The Velvet Hand goes up](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_5/the-financial-front.md).
  - **Confronting Farlin.** Party locates the Eastway Ward Boss. Bror intimidates; Kaelan reasons (a war ruins his district); the kompromat seals it. Farlin agrees to plant the smokepowder mines on the routes the Sembian mercenaries would use. **Eastway is now a minefield.**
