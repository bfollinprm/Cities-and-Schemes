# Session 1 — Player Summary

## Coast Way

Four miles south of [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), a [Zhentarim](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/summary.md) crew has set up a "traveler's tithe" across the Coast Way. Each PC arrived with a separate caravan; this is where the table converges. Finneas opened with mercy-pleas; Spark followed with a half-veiled count of how many travelers stood behind them. While the negotiation ran, Bror scouted the dunes and clocked a handful of [Guild](../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) operatives watching from cover. The standoff resolved at 18 gp paid; grateful travelers later reimbursed Finneas 38 gp.

## Rivington

The party reaches Rivington in turmoil. Two crises run in parallel:

- The [Wyrm's Crossing protest](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_1/organize-a-protest-at-wyrms-crossing.md) is blocking the bridge — [High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) at the head of [Hellriders](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) and [Followers of the Phoenix](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md), held by a [Flaming Fist](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/summary.md) line.
- A Sewer Plague is rolling through the poorer districts. The Open Hand Temple is functioning as a hospital and turning patients away because their medicine line is stuck behind the protest — courtesy of the [Worshipful Company of Alchemists & Apothecaries'](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_1/deny-antitoxin-to-a-clinic.md) refusal to release antitoxin.

Kaelan healed a sick child for 10 gp. Elia investigated the Circus of the Last Days and recoiled from [Mister Smiles'](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/mister-smiles.md) signature — too dangerous to poke alone.

## The medicine corridor

Spark approached Alena. She granted the party passage through the protest; a nervous Fist crossbow grazed Spark on the way through. Finneas and Spark talked the Fist sergeant down and brokered a deal: a single medicine wagon could pass through the cordon.

The party split. Spark stayed at the protest line. The rest, escorted by two Fist guards, went to a [Chionthar Consortium](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) warehouse in [Little Calimshan](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/little-calimshan.md). Kaelan bribed the warehouse crew off-shift with drinks; the rest climbed in a window, found four carts, identified the medicine, and walked it out. **Elia, on impulse, also stole a second cart** — later found to contain printed-and-carved anti-Amnian propaganda the Consortium was about to deploy publicly.

Spark's speech turned the protest from anger to opening; the medicine cart rolled through. The Open Hand Temple gifted Spark a Potion of Healing in thanks. Alena sent word: come to the High Rider's Stand tomorrow.
