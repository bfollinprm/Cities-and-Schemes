# Session 11

- **In-game week:** 3 (returning into week 5 on the Material Plane after Feywild dilation)
- **Real-world date:** 2025-09-22
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - Feywild glade. **Severe time dilation:** Finn waits a week before Bror arrives; Bror waits hours for Elia/Spark/Kaelan. Eventually reassembled.
  - Bror reconnoiters: [Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) and the other harvested patrons are inside a marvelous wagon marked *The Ephemera Collection*, with [Lucretius](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/lucretius.md).
  - Inside the wagon: shelves of jars containing captured memories. A ledger shows Lyra traded "Memories of Elturel's Fall" for "The Strength to Prevent Another." Lyra is performing chores with superhuman strength but devoid of her former purpose.
  - **Lucretius's bargain.** Three riddles. Win all three, reclaim Lyra's memories. Win or lose, he activates Elia's shard; if she fails, he listens in.
    - Riddle 1: "A mirror." Correct.
    - Riddle 2: "A promise." (Finn.) Correct.
    - Riddle 3: "An answer." (Finn.) Correct.
  - Lyra's memories returned. She collapses in anguish at Elturel's fall, then rises with purpose intact and thanks the party profusely.
  - Lucretius activates Elia's shard. Elia and Bror access a **cosmic vision**: a psychic ocean of mortal souls being caged and channeled by divine wills (the act of "naming"); a vast consciousness severs a piece of itself rather than be named. The shard is the rejected fragment — **a scream frozen into silence** of a forgotten God of Naming. Elia's shard becomes the **Shard of the First Scribe**, a new spellcasting focus.
  - Party returns to [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). **Two weeks have passed on the Material Plane.** The Flaming Fist warrant has cooled. Lena charges 50 gp for kept rooms; Spark pays. Bror's third Whispering Stone attunement attempt fails on a Wisdom save: psychic damage and deafness again.
