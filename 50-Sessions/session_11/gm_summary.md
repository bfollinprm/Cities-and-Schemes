# Session 11 — GM Summary

Lifted from `.context/converted/dm-notes.md` — Week 3, Session 11.

## Incident 1 — A Game of Wits

- **Location.** *The Ephemera Collection* (Lucretius's wagon), Feywild glade.
- **Primary faction.** [The Carnival of Whispers](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/summary.md).
- **Lucretius's objective.** Acquire **the cosmic memory fragment Elia carries** — the [Shard of the First Scribe](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_3/unlock-the-shard-of-the-first-scribe.md). The riddle game is a diagnostic and seduction tool, not a real obstacle. Revealing Lyra's situation is part of the lure.
- **Strategy.** Classic fey bargain: a "fair" game with a desirable prize ([Lyra's freedom](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md)) and a compelling consolation prize (unlocking the shard). The hope is the party fails, allowing him to make a follow-on offer to fully unlock the shard's knowledge for Elia in exchange for the shard itself afterward.
- **Outcome.** Party success, largely thanks to **Finn's** quick thinking. True to the fey code, Lucretius honors the bargain — but makes a final strategic move: by activating the stone and seeding a cryptic warning, he ensures Elia's obsession with the artifact only deepens. He lost the game; he is still playing the long con.

## Incident 2 — A God's Forgotten Pain

- **Location.** *The Ephemera Collection* and [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md).
- **Party actions.** Returned [Lyra's](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) memory; she is restored, with purpose intact. Elia and Bror access the shard's memory and learn its true origin: a fragment of a forgotten **God of Naming** that rejected its own divinity.
- **Strategy.** This is the **narrative climax of the quest module**. It reframes Elia's personal arc from "warlock of a Great Old One" to "bearer of a forgotten god-fragment that *rejected* divine categorization." Bror's piggy-backing on the vision gains him peripheral knowledge about the relationship between naming and divinity that Mother Nocturne and Shar are interested in.
- **Time dilation.** Standard Feywild consequence, used here to disorient the players and advance the timeline of the outside world. Faction quests and world events have progressed *two weeks* on the Material Plane while the party was occupied. The next Material-Plane session opens in week 5 from the city's perspective.

## Incident 3 — Outstanding consequences

- **New ally.** [Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) now owes the party a significant debt. As Quartermaster for the Hellriders she is a valuable political and logistical contact. Her experience is also a powerful cautionary tale about fey bargains.
- **Outstanding contracts.** Bror and Elia still have **unfulfilled bargains with [the mirror-tent proprietor](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/mirror-tent-proprietor.md)**. The fey will eventually come to collect on the secrets they were promised.
- **Faction timers.** Two weeks have passed in Baldur's Gate. The arrest warrant has cooled, but other faction plans (Zhentarim, Guild, Plague of Remembrance, Archduke's mercenaries) have advanced significantly. The next *Baldur's Mouth* edition will detail much of it.
- **Bror's tether.** Bror's third Whispering Stone attunement attempt fails again with deafness — the stone is consistently rejecting Bror's mortal mind. [Mother Nocturne's standing instructions to Bror](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_3/whispers-from-the-stone.md) (no public attunements without supervision) are now timely.
