# Session 21

- **In-game week:** 5
- **Real-world date:** 2025-12-01
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - **Office hours.** Finneas establishes a "soft campaign" at the [Weary Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) tavern in Rivington — an open office where citizens can bring grievances in confidence. Promise to the locals: under Finneas, the Guild offers *active mercy* and protection, not just extortion. The first ward inhabitants begin to walk in.
  - **Heapside expedition.** Party crosses the wall and goes into [Heapside](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) to find the [Baldur's Mouth](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/summary.md) print shop.
  - **The Press Pass.** Kaelan meets [Ettvard Needle](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md). Successful pitch — Kaelan now holds an investigative-journalist position with the *Baldur's Mouth* and is given the **Credential of Balduran**, a legendary signet ring that reportedly opens doors in restricted parts of the city. Information dropboxes set up at the print shop and at the Plowman.
  - **Heapside landmarks scouted.** While crossing the district the party logs:
    - **Dammon's Forge** — a tiefling smith rumored to have worked legendary infernal engines.
    - **The Singing Lute** — a discreet inn favored for private meetings (the same one [the Harpers' moderate-faction back-channel](../../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_4/the-back-channel-bargain.md) burned on last week).
    - **The Winch and Whistle** — a tavern where mercenaries find work outside the city walls.
    - **[Sorcerous Sundries](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/sorcerous-sundries.md)** — premier hub for magical items and lore; under tightened [Sodality scrying censers](../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_4/the-scrying-censors.md) since week 4.
  - Week 5 ends with the party halfway-installed in Rivington, Kaelan press-credentialed, and the Eastway minefield silently waiting under foot. Nine-Fingers has not yet rendered a verdict on Vex.
