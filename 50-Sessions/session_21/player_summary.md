# Session 21 — Player Summary

## Office hours at the Plowman

Finneas opens up the back room of the [Weary Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) for "office hours." Anyone in [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) with a grievance can walk in, talk in confidence, and leave with the new Guild boss's word that he will look into it. The pitch to the locals is simple — *active mercy, not extortion.* Lena Oakes pours tea. The first inhabitants of the ward filter in. None of them are sure what Finneas's title is yet, but they walk out reassured. The Rivington Gambit's first day is on the books.

## Heapside expedition

Spark and Kaelan cross over the wall into [Heapside](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) and find the *Baldur's Mouth* print shop. Inside is [Ettvard Needle](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) himself, profit-driven and eccentric, fresh off [the Githyanki Garrison front-page coup](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_5/the-githyanki-garrison.md). Kaelan pitches an investigative-journalism position. Needle, after a moment's calculation, agrees and hands him a heavy signet ring — the **Credential of Balduran**. The ring is reputed to open restricted parts of the city to its bearer; Needle calls it "an old company tradition." Kaelan establishes Baldur's Mouth dropboxes for tips at the print shop and back at the Plowman.

## Landmarks logged

While crossing Heapside the party makes mental notes for the next push:

- **Dammon's Forge.** A tiefling smith with rumored hands on legendary infernal engines.
- **The Singing Lute.** A discreet inn, last seen as the venue for [the Harpers' burned back-channel](../../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_4/the-back-channel-bargain.md).
- **The Winch and Whistle.** A mercenary-hire tavern.
- **[Sorcerous Sundries](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/sorcerous-sundries.md).** The city's premier magic-goods hub, currently scryed at the gate by the [Sodality's new censers](../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_4/the-scrying-censors.md).

## End of week

Week 5 closes here. Finneas runs Rivington in fact if not in name. Kaelan has a press pass. The Eastway mines are silent. The Velvet Hand is rubble. The Phoenix Gate is rising. Nine-Fingers has not yet sent a verdict on Vex. The next thing the party hears at the Plowman door will set the tone for week 6.
