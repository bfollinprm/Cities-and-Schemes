# Session 23

- **In-game week:** 6
- **Real-world date:** 2025-12-15
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark, [Jaredith Heartsong](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) (NPC ally)
- **Notable beats:**
  - **Jaredith returns.** Preparing to investigate the captured Reaper, the party encounters their old ally [Jaredith](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md), now in traveler's cloaks playing a lute and committed to "peace and love." He shares news of an assassination attempt against [Alfira](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) by [the Takers of the Tithe](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/summary.md) and agrees to accompany the party to the Hellrider headquarters.
  - **Alley ambush.** En route to the High Rider's Stand, the party is ambushed in a foggy New Elturel alleyway by a Takers collection unit (Spotter, Reaper, Keeper, Thralls). Tactical highlights:
    - Bror shadow-teleports behind the Reaper at the alley's far end and kills him with silvered strikes.
    - Kaelan casts *Detect Magic*, identifies the Keeper's necromantic lantern, and uses *Channel Divinity: Searing Sun* to scatter the Thralls.
    - Spark rages and engages a fallen Spotter; Jaredith *Thunderous Smite*s the Spotter off a balcony, then uses *Tasha's Bubbling Cauldron* to pacify the rest with apple-pie scent and flower petals.
  - **Aftermath & the Ledger.** The Reaper is dead; the Keeper escapes into the fog after smashing his lantern; the Spotter is captured. Loot: a **Soul-Trapping Polearm** and the **Tithe Ledger**. Elia identifies the ledger and confirms **[Alfira](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md), [High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md), and the entire party** are listed as fresh targets. The party gives the dead Reaper a salt-burial to prevent necromantic reanimation and continues toward the Hellrider headquarters.
