# Session 23 — GM Summary

## Setting

The transition from the frantic construction site of the new temple to the claustrophobic, fog-heavy alleys of New Elturel was deliberate — isolating the party from their allies and making them vulnerable to a surgical strike by Bhaalist remnants.

## Primary factions

- **The party.** Successfully integrated [Jaredith](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) as a high-level support NPC.
- **[Takers of the Tithe](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/summary.md).** A specialized cell ("debt collection" / assassination). They use necromancy to sustain thralls and soul-trapping weaponry to ensure targets cannot be easily resurrected.

## Party actions

High tactical efficiency. Kaelan's immediate use of *Detect Magic* neutralized the Keeper's mechanical advantage (the lantern's sustainment of thralls). Bror's high-mobility strikes eliminated the primary damage dealer (the Reaper) before he could stabilize his "collection."

## Antagonist objectives

- **Immediate:** "Collect the debt" (assassinate) Kaelan or Spark as a warning to the Ember's coalition.
- **Secondary:** Reclaim the "resonance" (souls) of those interfering with the Archduke's blockade.

## Key motivations & strategy

The Takers operate on a pseudo-legalistic religious framework where life is a "debt" owed to Bhaal. The cell's standard triad:

1. **Spotter:** identifies and marks targets with psychic/enchantment runes to focus the Reaper's attacks.
2. **Keeper:** uses a necromantic lantern to provide a frontline of disposable Thralls while staying behind cover.
3. **Reaper:** the heavy hitter, finishing marked targets with soul-trapping strikes.

## Other considerations

The **Tithe Ledger** is a major intelligence boon. It confirms the party is now considered a Tier-1 threat by the Bhaalist elements within the city. The **Soul-Trapping Polearm** (+1 glaive with necrotic damage) provides Bror or Spark with a powerful but potentially corrupting tool — the weapon hungers for souls and will require restraint to wield safely.
