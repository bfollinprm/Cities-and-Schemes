# Session 23 — Player Summary

## Reunion with Jaredith

The party leaves the temple construction site to head for the New Elturel stockade. On the way, an old friend steps out of the morning fog. **Jaredith Heartsong** — last seen radicalized at the Phoenix Pits — is in traveler's cloaks playing a lute and looking ten years lighter. He calls himself a pacifist now. He tells the party that the Takers of the Tithe attempted to kill Alfira; he is here to help. The party invites him along.

## Ambush in the alleys

A foggy New Elturel alley between the temple site and the High Rider's Stand. Four assailants:

- **The Reaper.** Hunched, pale, holding a soul-trapping polearm. At the far end of the alley.
- **The Keeper.** Behind crates, cradling a necromantic lantern that hums with green light. Two zombie-like **Thralls** stagger at his shoulder.
- **The Spotter.** Above us, on a sagging wooden balcony.

What we did:

- **Bror.** Shadow-stepped to the alley's end, slipped behind the Reaper, and put two silvered fists into his ribs. The Reaper drops on the third hit.
- **Kaelan.** *Detect Magic* picks up the lantern's necrotic-binding glyph immediately. *Channel Divinity: Searing Sun* turns the Thralls and they break, scattering through doorways.
- **Spark.** Rage. Engages the Spotter on the cobbles after Jaredith knocks him down.
- **Jaredith.** *Thunderous Smite* on the Spotter from the balcony, dropping him into Spark's reach. Then *Tasha's Bubbling Cauldron* (the Mischief variant) — apple-pie scent fills the alley, flower petals carpet the cobbles, the remaining enemy stands down.

The Keeper smashes the lantern as he flees and disappears into the fog. The Spotter is unconscious, alive, gagged, and Jaredith's prisoner.

## The Tithe Ledger

We pat down the Reaper. Two finds: **a Soul-Trapping Polearm** (+1 glaive, hungry-feeling, almost certainly worth the price tag of *not* attuning), and **a Tithe Ledger**. Elia recognizes the binding — names written in the ledger become *debt-bound* to Bhaal upon their death. She reads the open page.

> **Alfira (The Ember). High Watcher Elena. The Party.**

We are on the list. Fresh targets, every one of us.

## Cleanup

We bury the dead Reaper in a salt circle to prevent necromantic reanimation, drag the gagged Spotter along, and walk on toward the High Rider's Stand. Jaredith hums.
