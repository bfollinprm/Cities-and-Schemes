# Session 28 — Player Summary

## The morning paper has changed

Kaelan reads the new edition of [*Baldur's Mouth*](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_7/the-puppet-press.md) over breakfast at [the Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). The byline is not Ettvard's. The tone is unmistakable:

- **Page 1:** Sembian mercenaries are "securing" the city; the Archduke's leadership is "steady in a difficult tenday."
- **Page 2:** A breadline massacre at the Sow & Piglet is blamed on the [Plague of Remembrance cult](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_7/the-refugee-riot.md), claimed to have *disguised themselves as Flaming Fist soldiers* to frame the city's defenders. (We cannot tell from the text whether this is true. We suspect Vhage's hand.)
- **Page 3:** [Commander K'laar of the Unchained Githyanki](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_7/the-astral-quarantine.md) has placed an **astral quarantine** around Wyrm's Rock; psionics will physically repel anyone attempting to cross [Wyrm's Crossing](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) without authorization.
- **Page 4:** A warning against the **[Unveiled](../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_7/the-prison-break-propaganda.md)**, who are passing pamphlets calling the Tragus Ballus murders a false flag.
- **Page 5:** [Master Phineas Droog](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/people/phineas-droog.md) is restricting antidotes exclusively to the Archduke's mercenaries following Zhentarim poison in unregulated ale; a minor riot of Eastway mercenaries broke out under the [Vintners' embargo](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_7/the-dry-riot.md).

We are reading the Archduke's voice in our morning paper for the first time. **Ettvard is gone**. Whoever silenced him is winning the press war.

## Recap

- Bror still owes [the Night Mother](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md) the *sentient source* of the secret of the stone. She believes the source is a Bhaalist cultist. The Whispering Stone hums when Bror goes near a Heapside sewer-grate; we take note.
- The hidden note in [Ettvard Needle's empty office](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) says he fled to the **crypts under [Tumbledown](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md)**, west bank of Dusthawk Hill. Tumbledown is the city's graveyard district — heavy with natural necromantic energy and zealously patrolled by **[the Gravekeepers](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/summary.md)** under Old Man Hemlock.

## Dammon's forge

We swing through New Elturel to see [Dammon](../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/people/dammon.md). He has been working day and night supplying weapons for [High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) and [Mad Meggan's](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) joint Basilisk Gate detail. He hands Bror a **new pair of dwarven unarmed-strike gauntlets** — *+1 to hit on unarmed strikes*. He then tests Spark's recently-attuned cloak by pressing a **glowing forge prong against their chest**. The fire feels *welcoming* and *comforting*, not painful.

He tells Spark something the Followers of the Phoenix did not: the cloak's clasp and metal tendrils were forged from **Karlach's discarded original-heart infernal iron**, salvaged by the Foundry Smiths after she returned from Avernus. He suggests Spark talk to Alfira about Karlach when she wakes.

## Crimson Draughts and the Guild coin

We visit [Master Phineas Droog](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/people/phineas-droog.md) to ask about a route into Tumbledown. He will not relax his antidote monopoly for our Outer City contacts (Kaelan tries; Droog smiles, says no). But he hands us a **Guild coin** and a name: **[Garrett "Grit" Fallow](../../20-Factions/guild/people/garrett-grit-fallow.md)**, a Tumbledown gravedigger on quiet retainer to Nine-Fingers Keene. Show the coin; pass without questions.

## The Eastway crossing

We decide against the sewers — Bror reports the Whispering Stone hums whenever he steps near a Heapside drain, and the [Hands of the Absolute have something leaking up from below](../../20-Factions/hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_7/the-resonance-chamber.md). We take the surface route. We try disguises. They are not good. (Spark's horns are visible from across a market square.) The gate detail is preoccupied with deserter-mercenaries from [the Dry Riot at Eastway barracks](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_7/the-dry-riot.md) and waves us through with a half-glance.

## Tumbledown's edge

We reach the district by midafternoon. It is crypt-still, necromantic-cold; even Spark's fire feels banked-down here. Hemlock's [Cliffside Consecration](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_7/the-cliffside-consecration.md) is on; the Gravekeepers' patrols are heavier than we expected. Perception roll: **a five-strong gravedigger patrol standing in the next yard**, between us and the crypt entrance.

We end the session deciding how to handle them.
