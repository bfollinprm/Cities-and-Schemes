# Session 28

- **In-game week:** 7
- **Real-world date:** 2026-01-19
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - **The Morning News.** Kaelan reads the new (post-Needle) [*Baldur's Mouth*](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_7/the-puppet-press.md) at [the Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). The paper is *visibly* pro-Archduke: Sembian mercenaries are "securing" the city; the Plague-of-Remembrance's [Sow & Piglet false-flag massacre](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_7/the-refugee-riot.md) is reported as the cult disguising themselves as Fist soldiers; [K'laar's astral quarantine on Wyrm's Rock](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_7/the-astral-quarantine.md) is mentioned as a public warning; [the Unveiled's prison-break propaganda](../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_7/the-prison-break-propaganda.md) is denounced; [Master Phineas Droog's antidote restriction](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_7/the-antidote-monopoly.md) is reported as wartime necessity; the Eastway [Vintner Dry Riot](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_7/the-dry-riot.md) is filed as "rogue mercenary outburst."
  - **Recap.** Bror still owes [Mother Nocturne](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md) the *sentient source* of the secret. [Ettvard Needle's note from the ransacked print shop](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) names the Tumbledown crypts as his hiding place.
  - **Tumbledown lore.** Spark recalls Tumbledown as the city's graveyard district, fat with natural necromantic energy and zealously patrolled by [the Gravekeepers](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/summary.md) under [Old Man Hemlock](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/people/hemlock.md), a cleric of Torm.
  - **Dammon's forge.** [Dammon](../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/people/dammon.md) outfits Bror with **dwarven unarmed-strike gauntlets (+1 to hit)**. Tests Spark's cloak with a glowing forge prong; the fire reads as **welcoming** rather than painful. Reveals Spark's cloak was forged from **Karlach's discarded original-heart infernal iron**. Suggests Spark talk to Alfira about Karlach when she wakes.
  - **Crimson Draughts and the Guild coin.** [Master Phineas Droog](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/people/phineas-droog.md) refuses to relax his antidote restriction for Outer City clinics, but hands the party a **Guild coin** and a name: [Garrett "Grit" Fallow](../../20-Factions/guild/people/garrett-grit-fallow.md), Tumbledown gravedigger on Nine-Fingers Keene's quiet retainer.
  - **Eastway crossing.** Disguises are *unconvincing* but the gate detail is preoccupied with the deserter-mercenaries from the [Dry Riot](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_7/the-dry-riot.md). The party reaches Tumbledown by midafternoon.
  - **Tumbledown perimeter.** [The Gravekeepers' Cliffside Consecration patrols](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_7/the-cliffside-consecration.md) are heavy. The party clocks **a five-strong gravedigger patrol** standing in the next yard. Decision deferred to next session.
