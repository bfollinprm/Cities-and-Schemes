# Session 6 — GM Summary

Lifted from `.context/converted/dm-notes.md` — Week 2, Session 6.

## Incident 1 — Confrontation with the Flaming Fist (High Rider's Stand)

[Manip Jerrol's](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/people/manip-jerrol.md) appearance with only five fists was a political probe, not a military assault. He knew he was in hostile territory and could not win a fight against the refugees. His goal was to be **seen** enforcing the Council's will. Deputizing Kaelan was a masterful sidestep: it let him save face, shifted the legal responsibility for the prisoner onto the party, and gave him a legitimate reason to withdraw without bloodshed or appearing weak. He outsourced his problem to the party and turned a potential defeat into a clever stalemate.

## Incident 2 — Audience with High Watcher Alena (High Rider's Stand)

[Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) is continuing to use the party as a **deniable asset**. By revealing the "mage masquerading as a tiefling" and the Jaredith-as-martyr plan, she pointed them at the [Phoenix Pits investigation](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/expose-zora-at-the-phoenix-pits.md) without having to officially sanction an internal probe. Handing over the prisoner was low-risk — she had already extracted what she needed, and giving him to a deputized Kaelan made her look compliant with city law while cementing party trust.

## Incident 3 — Basilisk Gate negotiation (the siege line)

[Mad Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) immediately recognized the prisoner not as a criminal but as a political weapon: his confession, naming "true Balduran patriots" in the upper city, lets her stoke class resentment and delegitimize the ruling elite. The 6,000 gp is a calculated investment in political capital. The stolen [**Medallion of Thoughts**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/4679-medallion-of-thoughts) was her primary tool for reading rivals' intentions; its loss is a major (and currently unknown) blow to her intelligence-gathering. The party are now allied with the disenfranchised but have publicly defied the Fist — consequences pending in [Plan the Unthinkable](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/activity/week_2/plan-the-unthinkable.md).
