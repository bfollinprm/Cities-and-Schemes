# Session 6 — Player Summary

## A precarious morning in Rivington

The session opened a few days after the sewer fight. The latest *[Baldur's Mouth](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_2/fan-the-flames.md)* lays out the city's state: the Hellrider protest at [Wyrm's Crossing](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) is in its second week, the [Free Traders' new blockade has shut the Basilisk Gate completely](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md), and the paper credits the [Chionthar Consortium](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) for "hiring" the party to deliver medicine — Errinthal's PR play.

## Standoff at the High Rider's Stand

At [Alena's compound](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md), [Manip Jerrol](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/people/manip-jerrol.md) intercepts the party with a Fist contingent and demands the prisoner under Council-of-Four authority. The crowd around them grows agitated. Through Elia's intimidation and Kaelan's persuasion, Manip cuts a face-saving deal: he **deputizes Kaelan into the Flaming Fist** and "remands" the prisoner into Kaelan's custody, ordered to deliver him to the [Sea Tower](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/seatower.md).

## Alena's intelligence

With the Fist gone, [High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) shares what the [interrogation](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_2/the-interrogation.md) gave her:

- The [Plague of Remembrance](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) is trying to start a civil war by framing the Elturian refugees.
- A **human mage masquerading as a tiefling** is embedded in the Followers of the Phoenix.
- The cult intends to **martyr [Jaredith](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md)** to incite a riot the Fist will be forced to put down.

She hands over the badly-beaten cultist and presses the party to deal with Jaredith before the cult moves.

## The deal at the Basilisk Gate

The party hires town criers (10 gp) and marches the prisoner publicly across Wyrm's Crossing to the [Free Trader siege at the Basilisk Gate](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md). ['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) is unimpressed by the Fist badge but immediately sees the prisoner as a political weapon — a confession naming patriar families would let her control the narrative.

She makes the offer: a chest of **6,000 gp** (1,000 gp per PC) for custody. Kaelan and Bror enter her command wagon to negotiate; Fen, sneaking under the wagon, lifts a magical pendant from Meggan's neck as she disembarks — a [**Medallion of Thoughts**](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/4679-medallion-of-thoughts).

The party accepts. Kaelan does a cursory deputization of one of Meggan's traders, formally washing his hands of the Fist's order. The party walks back into the [Outer City](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/README.md) significantly wealthier — and now openly defying a Flaming Fist directive.
