# Session 6

- **In-game week:** 2
- **Real-world date:** 2025-08-18
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark; Finn (NPC, played as guest)
- **Notable beats:**
  - At the High Rider's Stand in New Elturel, [Manip Jerrol](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/people/manip-jerrol.md) and five Fist demand the prisoner; Kaelan and Elia de-escalate; Manip deputizes Kaelan, ordering him to deliver the cultist to the Sea Tower.
  - [High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) shares the cultist's interrogation: a "sister in disguise" mage is embedded in the Followers of the Phoenix; the cult plans to martyr [Jaredith](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md).
  - Kaelan hires criers; the party parades the prisoner across Wyrm's Crossing to the Basilisk Gate.
  - ['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) buys the prisoner for **6,000 gp**; Kaelan deputizes one of her traders; **Fen lifts a Medallion of Thoughts** from Meggan's neck during the negotiation.
