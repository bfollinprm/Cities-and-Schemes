# Session 22 — Player Summary

## The Siege Edition at the Plowman

Week 6 opens at [the Weary Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) with the city's worst-printed and best-selling broadsheet flopped open across the breakfast table. Kaelan reads it aloud. **The city is at open siege.** [Archduke Lyra Silvershield's Sembian mercenaries are confiscating personal food stores at the Outer City roads](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_6/the-starvation-siege.md). [The Council fired warning shots at the Basilisk Gate yesterday](../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_6/the-warning-shot.md); [Alena and Mad Meggan held the line in a Hellrider shield wall](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_6/the-shield-wall.md). [The Upper City gates are locked](../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_6/the-gate-lockdown.md). [Githyanki patrols are scanning citizens' minds at Wyrm's Crossing](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_6/the-psionic-sweep.md). [The Lantaners are building "a massive construct"](../../20-Factions/lantaner-concession/activity/week_6/the-prototype-assembly.md) at the Cogwork Quay. The Carnival has gone underground.

Folded inside the broadsheet is **Kaelan's first published piece** — *The Insurance Scam* — naming the Consortiums and the Sembians as arsonists-and-insurers, by source-cited reporting under his press credential. The Lower City is reading it; the Counting House is sweating about it.

## The Reverse Siege

Spark, hand on the table: *"They built a siege. Let's reinforce it the wrong way around."* [The plan is to consolidate the gate](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_6/the-reverse-siege-pitch.md) — bring The Ember and her radicalized refugees to bolster the blockade and **starve the Upper City** of food, fuel, and labor until the patriars sue for terms. The party sketches three supply routes (overland, river, sea) and prioritizes: get the Phoenix on side first, deal with the river through Errinthal and the press, and find the time later to figure out the Amnian sea routes.

## Foundation Stone

The party rides to [New Elturel](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) and finds [The Ember (Alfira)](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) presiding over [a foundation-stone ceremony for the new Temple of the Phoenix](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_6/the-foundation-stone.md). Despite the [starvation siege](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_6/the-starvation-siege.md), volunteers donate stone, labor, and rationed bread; the construction site is heaving with frantic, almost manic energy.

The crowd is **incendiary**. Walking the site, the party speaks to acolytes who say openly they are *"ready to start fires"* and burn the city if that's what it takes. The party realizes Alfira is not leading a refugee community anymore — she is sitting on a powder keg.

## Lunch in the Ember's tent

Spark pitches the Reverse Siege over the Ember's table. Alfira is reluctant. *"My people are not an army equipped for a prolonged military siege."* Spark counters that they are merely bolstering a line that is already being held, and that doing nothing is a slower kind of failure. The Ember invites the party to continue over food. Two revelations come out:

- **The Takers of the Tithe** have placed a target on her. She fears any aggressive move by her flock is the signal for her assassination.
- **The Reaper.** The Hellriders captured a Reaper assassin three weeks ago; he is currently being held at the New Elturel stockade.

The party agrees: address the Tithe threat first, then bring the Phoenix into the Reverse Siege. Saddle up. The High Rider's Stand is the first stop tomorrow morning.
