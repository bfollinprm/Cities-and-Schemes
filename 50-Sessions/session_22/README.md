# Session 22

- **In-game week:** 6
- **Real-world date:** 2025-12-08
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - **The Siege Edition.** The party reads the latest [Baldur's Mouth at the Plowman](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_6/the-siege-edition.md): [the Archduke is starving the Outer City](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_6/the-starvation-siege.md), [Kaelan's *Insurance Scam* exposé runs on page three](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/kaelan.md), [Sembian mercenaries fired warning shots at the Free Traders](../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_6/the-warning-shot.md), [the Watch has locked the Upper City gates](../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_6/the-gate-lockdown.md), [Githyanki are scanning the Outer City for a "psychic miasma"](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_6/the-psionic-sweep.md), the Carnival has retreated underground, and [the Lantaner gnomes are building something at the Cogwork Quay](../../20-Factions/lantaner-concession/activity/week_6/the-prototype-assembly.md).
  - **The Reverse Siege.** Spark proposes [a strategic pivot](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_6/the-reverse-siege-pitch.md): rather than fight *into* the city, *reinforce* the blockade and starve the Upper City. The party maps overland (contested), river (weakened), sea (hardest) supply routes and decides to consolidate allies first.
  - **Foundation Stone in New Elturel.** The party rides to find [The Ember](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) at the [Phoenix Gate temple ceremony](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_6/the-foundation-stone.md). Spark pitches the Reverse Siege; the Ember is reluctant — her followers are radicalizing fast, the crowd is "ready to start fires," and [she is being hunted by the Takers of the Tithe](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_6/the-prison-silence.md).
  - **The Captured Reaper.** Over a private lunch in the Ember's tent, Alfira reveals the Hellriders are holding a Takers of the Tithe **Reaper** at the New Elturel stockade. She fears any aggressive move by her flock will be the signal for her assassination. The party agrees to neutralize the Tithe threat first.
