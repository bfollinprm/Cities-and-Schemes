# Session 18

- **In-game week:** 5
- **Real-world date:** 2025-11-10
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - **Smokepowder handover.** Elia's spectral oxen finish hauling the crates to Farlin's men. The mines are buried on the Sembian advance routes through [Eastway](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md). The first physical fortification of the [Hellrider–Free Trader alliance](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) is in the ground.
  - **New directive.** [Mad Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) at the [Basilisk Gate](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) reframes the war: don't assault the [Western Gate Trading Company](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/summary.md), peel off its money. Deliver an ultimatum to [Factor Errinthal](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) of the [Chionthar Consortium](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md). Withdraw funding for the Archduke's mercenaries, or the print blocks go to the *Baldur's Mouth* and the Amnian Kontor.
  - **Coster Hall.** Party walks into [Brampton](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) and into Coster Hall, the Consortium HQ. Clerks frantic — river trade has stalled because of [a "river monster" downstream](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_5/hunt-the-river-horror.md). Head clerk Veris stonewalls. Party leverages "Heroes of Rivington" reputation; bluffs an "outstanding obligation"; receives a **Black Iron Clearance** token and a private audience.
  - **The audience trap.** Errinthal is hosting [Ettvard Needle](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) of the [Baldur's Mouth](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_5/the-githyanki-garrison.md). Before the party can pull the kompromat, Errinthal "thanks" them publicly for volunteering to escort a Consortium relief caravan to "the starving masses" at the Basilisk Gate. **Refusing in front of the press makes them villains.** Kaelan slips a discreet note re: sensitive information; Errinthal: *"a story is nothing without proof."*
  - Five magic carts of luxury food are loaded for departure. Session ends in the Coster Hall courtyard.
