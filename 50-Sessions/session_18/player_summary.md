# Session 18 — Player Summary

## The smokepowder handover

Elia's spectral oxen finish their last sewer run. Farlin's men take the crates and bury them on the Sembian advance routes through [Eastway](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md). The minefield is in the ground. The [Hellrider–Free Trader alliance](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) has its first physical defense.

## The new directive

You regroup at the camp. ['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) has rethought the war: don't assault the [Western Gate Trading Company](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/summary.md), strangle its money. The party will deliver Vex's stolen Consortium intel to [Factor Errinthal](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) and force him to withdraw his backing of the Archduke. Plan B: leak the print blocks to the *Baldur's Mouth* and the Amnian Kontor and rip the patriar coalition in half.

## Coster Hall

You walk into [Brampton](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) and approach Coster Hall, the Consortium's massive headquarters. Clerks are frantic; river traffic is stopped because [an actual river monster — the water weird the Inner Grove "bound" — has gone downstream into the main Chionthar](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_5/hunt-the-river-horror.md) and is attacking skiffs. The head clerk Veris is colder than the audit she's running. The party leverages their "Heroes of Rivington" rep, bluffs an "outstanding obligation" to the Factor, and is granted a **Black Iron Clearance** token to enter Errinthal's private suite.

## The audience trap

You walk into Errinthal's office. He is not alone. **[Ettvard Needle](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md)** is sitting across from him, taking notes. Before you can produce a single piece of kompromat, Errinthal stands up, smiles, and "thanks" you on the record for volunteering to escort a Consortium relief caravan of food and sundries to "the starving masses" at the Basilisk Gate. **He has trapped you in front of the press.** Refusing makes you villains in tomorrow's *Baldur's Mouth*. Kaelan passes a discreet note. Errinthal reads it, smiles wider, and answers under his breath: *"a story is nothing without proof."*

You are escorted to the courtyard, where five magic-driven luxury carts wait. You are now the Chionthar Consortium's relief escorts. The session ends.
