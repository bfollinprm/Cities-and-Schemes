# Session 4 — Player Summary

## In the shadow of the walls

After the Survivor's Swap, with dark fog rolling in from the Sword Coast, the party tabled the Gareth and Jaredith jobs (waiting on Spark) and chose the Sewer Plague over the Circus of the Last Days. Finneas in particular wanted the sickness gone.

## An unnatural sickness

At the Open Hand Temple in Rivington, the medicine the party had delivered was being rationed — pressure relieved, not solved. Symptoms were *not* a normal sewer-borne plague: paranoia, hallucinations, "worms in their minds," "watchers in the dark." Kaelan's medicine check ruled out anything sewer-natural. Elia's arcane senses caught a sickly poison-aura clinging to the afflicted.

[Brother Billiam](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/people/brother-billiam.md), an Open Hand monk, offered 50 gp from the temple's coffers to find the source. Pooling notes, the party traced the earliest cases to a low shantytown on Rivington's edge called the **Worm Spit**, where a wide drainage canal empties into the Chionthar.

## Into the filth

The canal stank like spoiled medicine — a concentrated version of the smell on the patients. Kaelan's survival check pinpointed the second sewer grate as the source; the party crowbarred it off and descended on a rope. Glowing green-black bioluminescent moss patched the walls.

Twenty minutes in, the tunnel opened on a stagnant cistern with two corpses: men in leather, throats cut, weapons sheathed — Bror identified the [Guild](../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) hand-mark on each. They had been ambushed from a side passage by someone who knew the sewers. A waterproof scrap of paper read:

> *"Nine Fingers was right. Something's down here and it ain't the sense. Stinks like a bad potion. We've seen the glowing moss. Keep an eye out for the mark, they mentioned some kind of weeping skull. We're pushing on to the main junction. — J."*

## The guardian in the garbage

Following the stench, the party crept into a vast junction chamber clogged with a stagnant pool and refuse mounds. An Otyugh erupted from a pile of bones with two ropy tentacles and a stalked eye. Surprise round: Eldritch Blast, Kaelan strike, Finneas flurry, Bror flank. The thing grappled Bror and Kaelan; Bror shook free, but Kaelan was slammed to the ground and went down. The bite poisoned Bror unconscious. Finneas was on the ground. With everyone but her ill or down, **Elia, from cover, dropped a final Eldritch Blast** that detonated the Otyugh into mushroom-and-rot.

Finneas stabilized the fallen. The session ended in the cistern, half-broken and on the verge of pushing deeper.
