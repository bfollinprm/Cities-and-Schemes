# Session 16

- **In-game week:** 5
- **Real-world date:** 2025-10-27
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - **The Saffron crates.** Bror finds the warehouse stocked with saffron in [Amnian Kontor](../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/summary.md) seals — confirmation of the [Farlin kompromat](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md). He pockets a sample and dismembers Vex's body and the dead guards into the crates to delay discovery.
  - **The chase.** The Guild scout who escaped flees through the [Gray Harbour](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) sewers toward the Upper City. Kaelan tracks him; tackle-and-pin in a flooded service tunnel. Scout's name: **Simbar.** He was running to Farlin (Vex's rival), not to Nine-Fingers. Confirms a bounty on the party is now inevitable.
  - **The pivot.** Party debates fleeing or burning the warehouse. Decision: bring the intel and the prisoner to [High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) and ['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md). Sneak back through the sewers to the [Basilisk Gate](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) refugee/trader camp.
  - **The war council.** [Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) ushers them into a command wagon. Full disclosure: the [Errinthal anti-refugee print campaign](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md), the [un-detonated Velvet Hand smokepowder](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md), Vex's appointment book, and Farlin's kompromat.
  - Meggan: ecstatic — political leverage to crack the patriar coalition. Alena: turns the smokepowder into a defensive plan. Both: **Simbar gets installed as puppet "Guildmaster of Rivington"** to keep the Guild operational while pivoted refugee-side. Simbar agrees on the spot.
  - **Sanctuary granted.** Party billeted in the camp. Short rest. **Level 5.** Alena's [formal alliance with the Free Traders](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) now has its first joint operation.
