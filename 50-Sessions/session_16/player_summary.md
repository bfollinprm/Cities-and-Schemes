# Session 16 — Player Summary

## Stripping the warehouse

You ransack Vex's office. Out of the desk: a sewer map of [Brampton](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md) marked *"Roah's new rat-nest. Silas's crew has the present"* — the [Velvet Hand smokepowder plot](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md) in eight words. The locked drawer (Vex's iron key): stolen Guild intel on the [Chionthar Consortium's anti-refugee print campaign](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md); Vex's ciphered Rivington appointment book (Finn cracks it with Thieves' Cant); and **kompromat on Farlin** — the Guild's Eastway Ward Boss, skimming saffron from the [Amnian Kontor](../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/summary.md) and covering for Silas.

Bror investigates the warehouse stock. Crate after crate of saffron, all in Amnian seals. **Confirmation that Farlin is the leak.** Bror pockets a handful, then dismembers Vex and the dead guards into the saffron crates to buy delay.

## The chase

One archer escaped. Kaelan pursues him through the sewers north toward the Upper City. He catches him in a flooded service tunnel and tackles. The scout — **Simbar** — talks fast. He was running to *Farlin*, not Nine-Fingers, because Farlin and Vex were rivals. He confirms: Nine-Fingers will put a massive bounty on you the moment she hears. Kaelan drags Simbar back to the warehouse, prisoner.

## The war council

Decision: don't run. Bring intel and prisoner to [Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) and ['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) at the [Basilisk Gate](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) camp. Sewers, no construct trouble this time. [Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) waves you through.

You confess the killing and lay every secret on the wagon table. Meggan reads the Errinthal print-campaign file and starts cackling — political leverage to break the patriar coalition. Alena reads the smokepowder map and goes still: *"We can take the gate down."* Lyra Sunstone points out the Guild problem. The party offers a solution: **Simbar gets installed as puppet "Guildmaster of Rivington."** Simbar — choosing to thrive — agrees on the spot.

Sanctuary granted. Short rest. **Level 5.**
