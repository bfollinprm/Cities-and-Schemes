# Session 7 — Player Summary

## An offer from the shadows

Heading back across [Wyrm's Crossing](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) toward [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), the party — now significantly wealthier after [the Mad Meggan deal](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md) — was approached by a street urchin near the [Weary Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). Kaelan paid him in gold; Finneas in bread. The note: "**The alley now**." Bror simultaneously spotted [Vex](../../20-Factions/guild/people/vex.md) slipping into the alley.

Kaelan and Bror went to meet her; Elia stayed at the entrance, faking interest in a trinket stall. Vex demanded the full story of the sewer cult — two of her Guild agents had gone missing on the same trail. Bror revealed but refused to surrender the **Whispering Stone**. Vex pivoted: she gave the party a new, higher-priority job — [investigate the Circus of the Last Days and its ringmaster, "who deals in whispers."](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/take-vex-circus-assignment.md) The Gareth job is suspended.

Outside, Elia's obelisk vibrated next to a stone hawk figurine on the trinket stall. She bought it for 25 gp; later *Identify* revealed an alien construct — a magical "rat catcher" that, on a command word, animates and attacks any creature tiny or smaller within 300 feet.

## The serpent in the pits

The party prioritized [Alena's Jaredith mission](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_2/the-interrogation.md) and went to the [Phoenix Pits](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/phoenix-pits.md). A charismatic tiefling preacher named **[Zora](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/people/zora.md)** was inciting the crowd to take up arms; [Jaredith](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) and [Mikkel](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/mikkel.md) looked on adoringly. Bror and Kaelan clocked Zora's eyes — cold, cataloguing — and matched her to Alena's "human mage masquerading as a tiefling."

Spark grabbed a handful of bonfire embers and offered them to Zora. Bror and Kaelan watched her cast something silently; Kaelan's *Detect Magic* confirmed *Absorb Elements*. Bror accused her of cheating. Zora pivoted: insulted her honor, goaded Jaredith into a non-lethal "ember cast" duel.

## A duel of wits and wills

Spark stepped in for Bror. The duel ran with blunted swords; in parallel:

- Elia cast *Detect Thoughts* — Zora was concentrating on *Bless* on Jaredith.
- Bror started a Benny-Hill chase around the bonfire, taunting Zora with the Whispering Stone, drew a third of the crowd, and broke her concentration.
- Spark closed, tackled Jaredith, locked an armbar audibly straining his arm — then **chose to yield** with grace, leaving Jaredith respectfully injured rather than humiliated.

Jaredith accepted the honorable end; the two held burning embers together. The mission succeeded: Jaredith cannot lead a charge for several days and now respects the party. Zora's identity is exposed to the party but not the wider crowd; her plan to martyr Jaredith is derailed.
