# Session 7

- **In-game week:** 2
- **Real-world date:** 2025-08-25
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - Alley meeting near the Weary Plowman: [Vex](../../20-Factions/guild/people/vex.md) confronts Kaelan and Bror over the missing Guild agents, refuses to take the Whispering Stone, reassigns the party to investigate the [Circus of the Last Days](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_2/a-feast-of-frustration.md).
  - Elia buys a magical "rat catcher" stone hawk figurine for 25 gp from a trinket stall; *Identify* reveals it is an alien construct.
  - At the [Phoenix Pits](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/phoenix-pits.md), the party identifies preacher [Zora](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/people/zora.md) as the Plague of Remembrance "sister in disguise."
  - Spark wins a non-lethal "ember cast" duel against Jaredith via armbar, then graciously yields. Jaredith is injured enough that he cannot lead a riot for several days.
  - Zora's identity is exposed to the party; the cult invites the party to a midnight meeting at a ruined watchtower.
