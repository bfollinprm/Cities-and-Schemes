# Session 7 — GM Summary

Lifted from `.context/converted/dm-notes.md` — Week 2, Session 7.

## Incident 1 — A Test from the Guild (Weary Plowman alley)

[Vex's](../../20-Factions/guild/people/vex.md) primary goal was a full sewer-cult intelligence report — both to assess the threat to [the Guild's](../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) territory and to learn the fate of her two missing agents. Secondarily, vetting the party as reliable assets. The party's coyness and Bror's refusal to hand over the [Whispering Stone](../../20-Factions/party/people/bror.md) were minor defiance; Vex pivoted instead of escalating. The new [Circus of the Last Days assignment](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_2/a-feast-of-frustration.md) serves two ends: it solves a real Guild problem (the carnival's chaos hurts business) and tests whether the party can do delicate work. Vex explicitly told them to "prove you can be discreet."

## Incident 2 — The Serpent in the Pits (Phoenix Pits)

[Zora](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/people/zora.md) is a top-tier [Plague of Remembrance](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) operative. Her objective at the Pits was to incite a premature, suicidal riot against the city; she was carefully building crowd fervor toward a tipping point. The party arriving was an unforeseen complication.

- **Countering the test.** Spark's ember test was a direct threat to her human cover. Her in-the-moment *Absorb Elements* preserved it.
- **Controlling the narrative.** When Bror accused her, she did not defend herself — she reframed the accusation as an insult to her and the faith and pushed [Jaredith](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) forward as her champion. Personal threat became public trial by combat; focus shifted away from her.
- **Outcome.** Spark's honorable fighting, Elia's magical reconnaissance, and Bror's chaotic distraction collectively derailed her plan. Jaredith is physically injured exactly as the adventure outline predicted. Identity not blown to the crowd, but exposed to a hostile and capable group of adventurers; she will report the failure and recalibrate.

Bror's antics with the Whispering Stone have branded him as an eccentric public figure — operating in shadow gets harder.
