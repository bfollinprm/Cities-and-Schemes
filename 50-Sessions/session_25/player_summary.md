# Session 25 — Player Summary

## The abduction of the Ember

Mid-interrogation we hear shouting from the camp. Alfira is gone. Elia opens the Tithe Ledger. Alfira's name is *glowing* — softer than candle-flame, brighter than ink. She has been taken for a ritual.

We move. Eric the patriarch waves us a goodbye from his tent. The Spotter rides between Spark and Bror, gagged.

## The ascent

The Spotter leads us up the north face of [Dusthawk Hill](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md). Slate underfoot, scrub-thorn at the elbows, gravel that whispers under boots. The path is barred by **Four Cairns**, each a small black-stone pile humming faintly.

A voice without a body addresses us at the first stone. *Polite. Professional. Four-legged.* The party agrees to call it the **Skittering Beetle**. It is here to broker.

## The Four Cairns

**1. Kaelan — the Debt of Respite.** The cairn accuses Kaelan of stealing respite from Bhaal by using *Revivify* to bring back a soul Bhaal had claimed. The Beetle offers a trade: surrender a portion of his Channel Divinity uses, take the punishment off the table. Kaelan refuses. *24 necrotic damage*; the party-wide curse **Malaise** clamps down — every healing roll, every hit-die roll, rolls *lowest* until a long rest.

**2. Elia — the Debt of Authorship.** The cairn accuses Elia of *playing God with ink* — forging the Tithe Ledger entry was a theological crime. The Beetle offers a spell slot, a memory, "a piece of her story." Elia refuses. She fails a DC 50 INT save. *21 psychic damage*; the curse **Fractured ID** — Elia cannot take Reactions or the Help action until cleared.

**3. Spark — the Debt of Entropy.** The cairn calls Spark a *"blasphemy of logic"* for defying the natural cycle of death/entropy. The Beetle offers Spark's remaining Rage uses. Spark accepts the accusation outright (no negotiation; no Beetle deal needed) and takes *1 level of Exhaustion*.

**4. Bror — the Debt of Silence.** The cairn accuses Bror of *hoarding silence* — offering nothing to the Lord of Murder, no screams, no prayers. The Beetle offers Bror's voice in exchange for keeping his stillness. Bror **accepts**. He cannot speak verbally now. He nods at the Beetle. The Beetle nods back.

**Jaredith** is not on the ledger. He touches a stone out of curiosity; nothing happens. He stays out of the broker's way for the rest of the climb.

## Arrival at the summit

We crest the hill. A massive circular **counting house** built of black stone, ringed by burning pitch-torches. Singa sits at a long desk inside, scratching "The Bard" into a ledger with a scythe-quill made of articulated bone. Bloody vellum is stacked to her shoulders.

**Alfira hangs from silver chains over a forty-foot pit of obsidian shards.**

We rush in. End of session.
