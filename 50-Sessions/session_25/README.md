# Session 25

- **In-game week:** 6
- **Real-world date:** 2025-12-29
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark, Jaredith Heartsong (NPC ally)
- **Notable beats:**
  - **Alfira goes missing.** During the interrogation, [Alfira](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) disappears from the Hellrider camp. Elia checks the Tithe Ledger; Alfira's name is **glowing**. She has been taken for a ritual.
  - **The ascent.** The party climbs the north face of [Dusthawk Hill](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md) under the Spotter's guidance. The path is gated by **Four Cairns**, each demanding a "debt" be paid by named members of the Tithe Ledger. **[The Skittering Beetle](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/people/the-skittering-beetle.md)** — invisible, four-legged, professionally polite — offers brokered settlements.
  - **The Four Cairns.**
    1. **Kaelan (Debt of Respite).** Punished for using *Revivify* — "stealing respite from Bhaal." Refused. Took 24 necrotic damage and triggered party-wide *Malaise* (all healing/hit-die rolls roll lowest until a long rest).
    2. **Elia (Debt of Authorship).** Accused of "playing God with ink" for forging the ledger entry. Refused. Failed a DC-50 INT save. Took 21 psychic damage and was cursed with *Fractured ID* (no Reactions, no Help action).
    3. **Spark (Debt of Entropy).** Accused of "blasphemy of logic" for defying the natural cycle. Accepted the accusation. Took 1 level of Exhaustion.
    4. **Bror (Debt of Silence).** Accused of "hoarding silence." Accepted the Beetle's deal: lost the ability to speak verbally in exchange for keeping his stillness.
  - **Jaredith spared.** He attempted to touch a stone, but his name was not on the ledger; the cairns ignored him.
  - **Arrival at the summit.** The party reaches the top and enters a massive circular **Counting House** styled as an auditor's office. **Singa** sits at a desk covered in bloody vellum, scratching "The Bard" into the ledger with a scythe-like quill. **Alfira hangs from silver chains over a 40-foot pit of obsidian shards.**
