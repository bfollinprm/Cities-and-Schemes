# Session 9 — Player Summary

## Five days downtime

The session opened after five days of recovery from [the watchtower fight](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/survive-the-watchtower-ambush.md). Each PC pursued their own thread:

- **Bror "Shadow Stone."** Still deafened from his first attempt at the Whispering Stone, Bror traveled to the Twin Songs district and the **Penumbra Playhouse**, the local Sharran temple. He met its leader, [Mother Nocturne](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md), and gave her a full debrief of the sewer cult, Zora, and the party's political entanglements. She agreed to guide him through a Sharran attunement ritual: embrace the stone's chaotic whispers. He failed the Wisdom saving throw, took psychic damage, and suffered a brief bout of madness — screaming and laughing for several minutes. Mother Nocturne told him to try again later, with his companions' help. ([Attune to the Whispering Stone](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/attune-to-the-whispering-stone.md).)
- **Elia Thorne.** Tended the grieving [Jaredith](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) at the Weary Plowman. Learned from Alena that Jaredith was refusing to return to Hellrider duty. Observed him meeting an older charismatic tiefling bard, [the Ember](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) — prominent in the Followers of the Phoenix — who was helping him channel grief into a song.
- **Kaelan of Beregost.** Began [Vex's Circus assignment](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/take-vex-circus-assignment.md). The encampment is just east of Rivington; he clocked a dwarf leaning on a wall as a Guild lookout. Kaelan bought a magically-altered ticket from the halfling barker, valid for the next two weeks. He decided to return with the party to investigate further.
- **Spark and Phineas Kaller.** Rest, healing, meditation.

## The end of the protest

When the five days were up, a composed Jaredith descended from his room with his lute. The party intercepted; Kaelan offered a Lathanderian blessing for the road. Jaredith walked to the heart of the [Hellrider protest at Wyrm's Crossing](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md), climbed an overturned cart, and began to play.

The ballad recounted his life: born in Elturel as it was dragged into the Hells, the prejudice he faced in Baldur's Gate, his service as a Hellrider, and the betrayal by his "love" Zora. The Ember appeared and magically amplified the song across the entire protest.

The effect was profound. The raw, authentic grief in the music defused the tense atmosphere completely. Protesters, guards, and merchants alike fell silent. A young tiefling on the verge of violence quietly dropped his brick. Angry merchants began to open their carts and share food — apples, oranges, bread, water — with the Elturian refugees.

The protest dissolved not in violence but in communal empathy. Jaredith, emotionally spent, returned to the Weary Plowman and collapsed into the party's arms, sobbing.

The way into [the main city of Baldur's Gate](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/README.md) is now clear. The party reached **Level 4**.
