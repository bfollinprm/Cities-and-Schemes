# Session 9

- **In-game week:** 2
- **Real-world date:** 2025-09-08
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Phineas Kaller (NPC ally), Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - Five days downtime in Rivington at the Weary Plowman.
  - Bror travels to the **Penumbra Playhouse** in Twin Songs, debriefs [Mother Nocturne](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md), runs a Sharran attunement ritual on the Whispering Stone — fails Wisdom save, brief madness.
  - Elia tends grieving Jaredith; learns he is meeting [the Ember](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) to channel grief into song; Jaredith refuses to return to Hellrider duty.
  - Kaelan scouts the [Circus of the Last Days](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_2/a-feast-of-frustration.md) east of Rivington; spots a Guild lookout dwarf; buys a magically-altered ticket valid for two weeks.
  - Spark and Phineas rest, heal, meditate.
  - Jaredith descends from the Weary Plowman with his lute. The party intercepts; Kaelan blesses him.
  - At Wyrm's Crossing, Jaredith plays a ballad of his life; the Ember magically amplifies it across the crowd. The protest dissolves into communal empathy. Refugees and Baldurians share food.
  - The way into the main city of Baldur's Gate is now clear. Party reaches **Level 4**.
