# Session 24

- **In-game week:** 6
- **Real-world date:** 2025-12-22
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark, Jaredith Heartsong (NPC ally)
- **Notable beats:**
  - **The High Rider's Stand.** The party arrives at [the Hellrider militia headquarters](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md) with the captured Spotter and the previously held Reaper to interrogate. [High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) is at the Basilisk Gate; her command tent is run by deputies.
  - **The "Revivify" ruse.** To break the Reaper's fanatical resolve, the party stages a theological con:
    1. Elia forges the Reaper's name into the Tithe Ledger (calligraphy roll: 20).
    2. Kaelan performs "cleric mumbo-jumbo," pretending to cast a spell that makes the Reaper "unkillable" — cursed with eternal life so he can never reach Bhaal.
    3. Jaredith runs the Reaper through; Kaelan immediately *Revivify*s him with a 300-gp diamond Jaredith procured via a page.
  - **The Reaper breaks.** Believing himself trapped in an endless loop, the Reaper confesses: cell leader is **[Singa](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/people/singa.md)**; the cell is methodologically targeting high-profile political figures to incite civil war; their hideout is on **[Dusthawk Hill](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md)**.
  - **The poisoning.** Just as the Reaper begins to reveal who *stands to win* from the chaos, [he is poisoned mid-sentence by his prison food](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_6/the-prison-silence.md), foaming at the mouth and dying within seconds. The cook — corrupt Hellrider supply staff on Singa's payroll — has no traceable ID. **There is a mole at the High Rider's Stand.**
  - **The Spotter's offer.** Terrified, the Spotter begs for protection and offers to lead the party to Singa's hideout at Dusthawk Hill in exchange for a new identity.
  - **Other prisoners.** [Eric](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/eric.md), a minor patriarch claiming his brothers framed him for embezzlement; he reads books, has independently reconstructed Bhaalist soul-contract theology and the Zhentarim sewer-tax-records operation, and offers future Upper City / Candlekeep access in exchange for help. **The Mercenary**, a large braided-bearded man captured at Wyrm's Crossing during the Hellrider–Free Trader conflict.
