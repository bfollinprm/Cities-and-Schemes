# Session 24 — Player Summary

## The High Rider's Stand

The pavilion-built Hellrider headquarters in [New Elturel](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md). Tactical maps showing the shantytown sectored by [Flaming Fist](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/summary.md) and [Guild](../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) activity. A small altar to Torm. Alena is at the Basilisk Gate; her deputies wave us through to the prison tent.

We are bringing two prisoners. The captured Spotter from yesterday's alley ambush, and the Reaper Alfira's people captured three weeks ago who has been here ever since.

## The unkillable ruse

The Reaper is fanatical and quiet. We need him to talk before his masters reach him. Plan:

1. **Elia forges his name into the Tithe Ledger.** A 20 on the calligraphy check; the Reaper's name is the freshest entry, written in his own captor's hand. He sees this happen.
2. **Kaelan stages the curse.** Long, theatrical mumbo-jumbo: a "spell" that makes the Reaper unkillable, an *eternity-of-life* curse, a body that will not let his soul return to Bhaal even if he dies. He sees Kaelan's holy symbol blaze; he believes.
3. **The execution.** Jaredith runs him through with his sword. The Reaper bleeds out in the chair.
4. **The "rebirth."** Kaelan immediately casts *Revivify* on the corpse, fueled by a 300-gp diamond Jaredith's page slips in. The Reaper wakes up screaming.

He breaks. Words tumbling, he confesses:

- The cell leader is **[Singa](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/people/singa.md)**.
- The cell is methodically targeting political figures to **incite civil war**.
- The hideout is on the north face of **[Dusthawk Hill](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/heapside.md)**.

He is starting to say *who stands to win from the chaos* when his food bowl arrives. He takes one mouthful out of habit and starts foaming at the mouth. Dead in seconds.

The guard tells us food is cooked in bulk. Source of the poison is untraceable. **There is a mole at the High Rider's Stand.**

## The Spotter

The Spotter watches all of this. He stops being a fanatic somewhere between Elia's calligraphy and the dead Reaper foaming on the floor. He begs for protection. He will lead us to **Singa's hideout** in exchange for a new identity and Hellrider-witnessed asylum. We agree.

## The other prisoners

Two more in the side tent.

- **Eric.** A minor patriarch of one of the lesser Upper City houses. Quiet, well-spoken, reading. He says his brothers framed him for embezzlement and theft from the High Watcher's coffers to cut him out of the family inheritance. From books refugees have brought him, he has independently figured out two things: **the Bhaal cult is binding souls via infernal-style contracts**, and **the [Zhentarim](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/summary.md) are stealing tax records through the sewers**. He offers future Upper City or Candlekeep access if we can prove his brothers framed him. He is filed for later.
- **The Mercenary.** A big braided-bearded man, captured at Wyrm's Crossing during the recent Hellrider–Free Trader skirmish. Watching us. We do not engage.

We commit to riding for Dusthawk Hill at dusk.
