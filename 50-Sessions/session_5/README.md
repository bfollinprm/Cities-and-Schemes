# Session 5

- **In-game week:** 1
- **Real-world date:** 2025-08-11
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark; Finn (NPC, played as guest)
- **Notable beats:**
  - Spark catches up to the party in the cistern.
  - [Finn](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/finn.md) — orphaned tiefling girl in the sewers chasing her rust-weasel "Scrap" — joins the party.
  - "Whispering stone" recovered (purple comm device used by the cultists).
  - The Plague-Father's lab in a circular cistern — three copper vats of glowing green plague-fluid, two cult fanatics, the masked Plague-Father.
  - Combat: Plague-Father teleports, drops a nausea cloud, opens a drainage valve to feed a pump. Finn natural-20s a death save and stabs him out of his retreat. Spark KOs him; Elia bursts the last fanatic; Kaelan intimidates the survivor.
  - Loot: two wooden coins (cracked-vial sigil), the Plague-Father's iron neck-key, two doses of Nightmare Root and other reagents pointing at psionic concentration potions.
  - Prisoner and Finn handed off to High Watcher Alena; party long-rests, levels up to 3.
