# Session 5 — Player Summary

## Return to the sewers

The session opened in the aftermath of the Otyugh fight. Spark caught up to the party. Before they could move, a faint scratching and a child's sob came from behind a rusted grate.

## A new companion

A young tiefling girl — [Finn](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/finn.md) — was on the other side, chasing her rust-weasel "Scrap." A New Elturel street-orphan, hardened, magically gifted (Thaumaturgy boom-voiced "Back off!" when Elia reached for her), and not willing to be left in a sewer alone. She agreed to come with the party. She also reported that "unnaturally quiet" people had passed through the chamber about thirty minutes earlier, going the same way the party was.

## The whispering stone

Down a narrower, older tunnel, the sewer stench gave way to ozone and a low subsonic hum. Kaelan spotted scuff marks at a puddle — the quiet group had stopped, confused, and dropped something. He fished out a strange purple, rock-like object. Finn recognized it: a "whispering stone," a magical communication device the quiet group had been using. The party pocketed it.

## The Plague-Father's laboratory

Past a heavy iron door: a converted cistern lit by three massive copper vats bubbling with sickly green liquid. A tall figure in plague-doctor's garb and bird-mask — **the Plague-Father** — and two cult fanatics, robed and tending the vats.

Combat went badly fast. Bror charged the Plague-Father, knocked him down with a flurry of blows; Finn followed with a daggers sneak attack. Wounded, the Plague-Father misted to a corner and filled the chamber with nauseating gas — Kaelan, Finneas, and Spark were benched. A fanatic dropped Bror. The Plague-Father cranked a valve to drain the vats into a pump.

Finn went down, **rolled a natural 20 on her death save**, popped up at 1 hp, and stabbed the Plague-Father out of his retreat. Spark landed the killing blow; the gas dissipated. Elia exploded the last fanatic. Kaelan loomed; the surviving cultist surrendered.

## Aftermath and allegiances

Loot:

- Two wooden coins carved with a **cracked-vial** sigil — a [Plague of Remembrance](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) cell-token, by Bror's read.
- An iron key from the Plague-Father's neck.
- Sacks of alchemical reagents, including two doses of **Nightmare Root**, used in potions that concentrate psionic energy.

Kaelan flagged psychic residue on the powder — a possible mind-flayer-crisis link. Bror's amateur "is there a parasite?" autopsy confirmed only that he was bad at brain surgery.

Rather than dump the prisoner on the Open Hand Temple, the party walked both the cultist and Finn to [High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md), who agreed to interrogate the prisoner and keep Finn. The party returned to a Rivington inn for a long rest. Level 3.
