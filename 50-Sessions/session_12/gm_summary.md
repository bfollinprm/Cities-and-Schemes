# Session 12 — GM Summary

Lifted from `.context/converted/dm-notes.md` — Week 5, Session 12.

## Incident 1 — Confrontation at the Weary Plowman

- **Location.** [Weary Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md), Rivington.
- **Primary factions.** Archduke Silvershield's Symbian mercenaries; **Absolute Remnants** (Intellect Devourer).
- **Symbian objectives.** Enforce the "Emergency Commerce Protection Act" through extortion. Post bounty posters on known agitators (the party). Maintain authority but prioritize self-preservation and profit. Their bad-copy poster (wrong Archduke name) signals a hastily-stood-up mercenary force operating off outdated dossiers.
- **Intellect Devourer objective.** Infiltrate and gather intelligence. The sergeant is a target for his position and access. The devourer used **tailored psychic lures** based on Bror's and Elia's perceived natures — secrets and shadows for one, "lock boxes of useful secrets" for the other. Possession produced fragmented host memory: **"tannery"** and **"smoke."** Possible link to the Butchers & Tanners Guild, Hamhock's slaughterhouse, or the smokepowder trade.
- **Outstanding consequences.** The party is now confirmed as wanted criminals by the Archduke's faction; travel risk is elevated. **Bror's acceptance of the devourer's help has forged a tether** — future Insight may surface a strengthening psychic connection. His deafness was no obstacle to telepathic contact. Lena Oakes saw the whole thing; she now knows the party attracts serious violence.

## Incident 2 — Aid at the Open Hand Temple

- **Location.** [Open Hand Temple](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/open-hand-temple.md), Rivington.
- **Strategy.** The temple is balancing charitable duty with resource scarcity. Kaelan's speech was strategic uplift for the sheltering refugees. Accepting the child's symbolic 10 gp let them offer healing without depleting reserves meant for the desperate. Kaelan's pragmatic-cynicism-warring-with-compassion arc continues; he keeps the moral ledger by returning the coin.

## Incident 3 — The Pilgrim's Parchment

- **Location.** Pilgrim's Parchment bookstore, Rivington.
- **Shopkeeper.** Likely more than a bookseller — a possible sage or info broker, with possible Harper sympathies. Uses the 600-gp price tag and the Shelf of Ascension as a filter for serious seekers. The chaotic shop is part deliberate obfuscation.
- **Book connections.** Several Shelf-of-Ascension titles tie to active plots: *The Githyanki Conspiracy* (Unveiled adjacent — Finneas's Insight finds a kernel of truth), *The Wyrmway Warden's Weekly Ledger* (coded Guild directory via a defunct museum front), *No Title [Scaly Leather]* (Underdark Illithid/astronomy text in Undercommon — Absolute lore adjacent), *The Art of Planar Navigation* (pressed Feywild flowers — Lucretius/Circus link), *The Stonemason's Secret* (coded Clan Iron-Eye political manifesto), *Grimoire of the Unseen Hand* (thieves' cant disguised as a spellbook).
- **The devourer query.** Bror naming the creature to the shopkeeper marks the party as having tangled with Absolute-related entities. The shopkeeper may relay this upward.
