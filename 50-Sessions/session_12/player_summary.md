# Session 12 — Player Summary

## A bad breakfast at the Plowman

The morning after the Feywild, the [Weary Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) is tense and quiet. Lena Oakes serves a meager breakfast and looks strained. Three Symbian mercenaries in fresh Archduke livery shoulder in, accost a family on its way out, and extort **10 gp under the "Archduke's Emergency Commerce Protection Act."** The room studiously refuses to see it.

The sergeant pins a parchment to the bulletin board. It declares a state of emergency near the [Basilisk Gate](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md) blockade and offers **250 gp per head** for "foreign operatives aiding the seditious blockade." The descriptions are unmistakable — Kaelan's sun sigil, Elia's eldritch command, Bror's silent stride, "the Alterian refugee child." **Finneas is not on the poster.** Bror also clocks that the parchment names "Older Silver Shield" instead of the current Archduke Lyra Silvershield. The mercenaries are working off bad copy.

## A beetle in the mind

A skittering, beetle-shaped *presence* speaks into Bror's deaf mind and into Elia's. To Bror it offers to "create a shadow in the soul" of the sergeant. To Elia it offers to pry open his "lock box of useful secrets." Bror shrugs at Elia, then snaps his staff around the sergeant's neck and kicks his legs out. The unseen creature climbs the sergeant; the man's smirk drains, his eyes go wide, and he screams about a **"tannery"** and **"smoke"** before collapsing.

The other two Symbians cut Bror down — one critical hit reduced by Deflect Missiles, then a second hit that drops him. Kaelan stabilizes with Healing Word. Elia talks — *Bror needs medicine for an episode* — and **Spark bribes the survivors 125 gp** to drag their babbling sergeant out the door.

## The Open Hand and the child's coin

Kaelan walks Bror to the [Open Hand Temple](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/open-hand-temple.md). The acolytes are reluctant to spend powerful magic on rest-able wounds. Kaelan offers his voice instead: a pragmatic sermon to the refugees sheltering there about tested faith and the power of community action. A child the party once saved offers her family's 10 gp; the head priest, moved, casts Healing Word on Bror. Kaelan returns the coin to the child — *a good deed is always rewarded in the end* — and Bror wakes.

## Pilgrim's Parchment

Elia, Finneas, and Spark slip away to **The Pilgrim's Parchment**, a chaotic floor-to-ceiling bookshop in [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). The halfling proprietor parts a curtain to reveal **"The Shelf of Ascension"** — rare tomes priced at 600 gp each, suitable as Candlekeep entry-gifts. The party paws through *Personal Diaries of the Archmage Ulbrek*, *The Githyanki Conspiracy: They Never Left!*, *The Wyrmway Warden's Weekly Ledger*, *The Stonemason's Secret*, *A Lexicon of the Extinct Uluik Language*, *Meditations on the Weave* (filled with crayon cats), *The Art of Planar Navigation* (pressed Feywild flowers), and the untitled book bound in dark, scaly leather. Bror asks the shopkeeper about the beetle creature; the halfling demands a sale first.

The session ends with three covers staring up off the counter and Spark's purse hanging heavy.
