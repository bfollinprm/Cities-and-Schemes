# Session 12

- **In-game week:** 5
- **Real-world date:** 2025-09-29
- **Attendance:** Bror "Shadow Stone", Elia Thorne, Kaelan of Beregost, Finneas Kaller, Spark
- **Notable beats:**
  - Morning at the [Weary Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). Lena Oakes is strained; breakfast is meager. Three Symbian mercenaries in Archduke livery enter and shake down a family for 10 gp under the "Emergency Commerce Protection Act."
  - The sergeant tacks up a 250-gp bounty for "foreign operatives aiding the seditious blockade." The descriptions are unmistakably the party — **except Finneas, who is missing from the poster.** Bror catches that the poster names the wrong Archduke ("Older Silver Shield").
  - **Intellect Devourer.** A skittering presence speaks into Bror's deaf mind and into Elia's; it offers to crack the sergeant open. Bror nods and lays the man out with his staff. The devourer scuttles up the sergeant's leg; he babbles "tannery — smoke" before going limp.
  - Combat. The other Symbians drop Bror unconscious. Elia talks; **Spark bribes them 125 gp** to take their leader and leave.
  - Kaelan hauls Bror to the [Open Hand Temple](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/open-hand-temple.md). He gives a pragmatic sermon to the refugees sheltering there; a child the party once helped offers her 10 gp; Kaelan returns it. The head priest casts Healing Word.
  - Elia, Finneas, and Spark visit **The Pilgrim's Parchment** in [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md). The halfling proprietor reveals "The Shelf of Ascension" — Candlekeep-grade tomes at 600 gp each. The party browses *Diaries of Archmage Ulbrek*, *The Githyanki Conspiracy*, *Wyrmway Warden's Weekly Ledger*, *The Stonemason's Secret*, and the untitled scaly-leather book.
  - Session ends mid-shop, the party staring at three books they cannot quite afford.
