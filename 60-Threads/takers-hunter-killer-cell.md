# The Takers' Hunter-Killer cell shadowing the Plowman

After Singa's death and the loss of the Tithe Ledger, [The Collector](../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/people/the-collector.md) dispatched a **Hunter-Killer cell** of three Bhaalist assassins — a Reaper, a Spotter, and a high-rank Keeper — into Rivington with explicit orders to find and kill the party. The cell is operating from an abandoned tannery shed on the outer Rivington road; their information comes from the [Spotter the party left alive](../50-Sessions/session_25/player_summary.md), now a debriefing source under the Collector's knife. They have **identified Alfira convalescing in the Plowman's back room** within two days. They have not moved on her — Singa's failure was *engaging the party near the Ember*; this Reaper has explicit orders to wait until the party splits again. By end of session 28 they have logged Bror, Elia, and Finn's departure for Tumbledown and are watching Kaelan's movements between the Plowman and Sow's Foot. **The party is being hunted and does not know it.**
