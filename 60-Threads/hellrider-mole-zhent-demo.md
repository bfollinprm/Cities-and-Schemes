# Roah's body-in-the-supply-wagon demonstration to Alena

[Roah Moonglow's interrogators reached the High Rider's Stand in time to identify and abduct the actual mole](../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_7/the-mole-hunt.md) — a cook [Alena's Witch Hunt](../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_7/the-witch-hunt.md) had already cleared. The cook died in a Heapside cellar after a night of Zhent interrogation; his body was left in a Hellrider supply wagon as a *demonstration to Alena that the Zhentarim got there first*. **Whether the Hellriders read the gift as friendly or hostile is the open question.** The party does not yet know any of this — they are in Tumbledown. When they return to the Stand the body will already have been recovered, the cook will already have been burned with Hellrider rites, and Alena will have a position. The party stands to gain or lose Alena's trust depending on whether they walk in carrying news of the mole *before* Alena tells them she already has it.
