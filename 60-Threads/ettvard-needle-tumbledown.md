# Ettvard Needle in the Tumbledown crypts

A hidden note in the [ransacked Baldur's Mouth print shop](../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) named the **Tumbledown crypts** as Ettvard's hiding place: *"If they take me, I'm in the crypts under Tumbledown. West bank. Dusthawk Hill. Don't trust the next byline."* The party has [crossed the Eastway blockade and reached Tumbledown](../20-Factions/party/activity/week_7/cross-the-eastway-blockade-to-tumbledown.md) carrying [Phineas Droog's Guild coin](../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/people/phineas-droog.md) and the name of [Garrett "Grit" Fallow](../20-Factions/guild/people/garrett-grit-fallow.md), a gravedigger on Nine-Fingers Keene's quiet retainer. The descent stalls at session-end on a five-strong [Gravekeeper patrol](../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_7/the-cliffside-consecration.md) in the next yard. While the party works the underside, [the Harpers' Nightingale is running a parallel surface search](../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_7/find-the-quill.md) — three Harper cells confirm someone is buying Ettvard's distinctive paper stock in bulk through Western Gate fronts. Neither side knows the other is looking.
