# Quests W-Z

## How To Use This File

The final batch covers late-alphabet factions; these are mostly foreign powers, veterans, and violent syndicates.

### Waterdhavian Kontor (The Splendid Yard) — Acquire 'Lost' Elturian Tomes
- **Quest**: Factor Elara Mornwood uses her agents to 'acquire' a set of rare historical texts, supposedly lost during the fall of Elturel, from a struggling refugee scholar in New Elturel for a pittance. The books are added to the Splendid Yard's library, a source of bitterness for Elturian traditionalists.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 15 / 5
- **Success**: FALSE
- **Public**: FALSE

### Western Gate Trading Company — Clear-Cut the Whisperwood Copse
- **Quest**: Using their newly-ratified charter, Matron Lyra Silvershield's company dispatches logging crews to a section of the Wood of Sharp Teeth known locally as the Whisperwood Copse. They clear-cut the area with brutal efficiency, enraging the Circle of the Inner Grove.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 5 / 5
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: TRUE

### Wharf Rats — Lose' a Crate of Lantaner Goods
- **Quest**: One-Eyed' Jack's dockworkers contrive to 'accidentally' drop a crate from the Lantaner Concession during unloading. The crate, containing valuable mechanical parts, is damaged and its contents are pilfered before being reported as a loss.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 15 / 10
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: FALSE

### Zhentarim (Black Network) — Burn Down a Guild-Protected Tavern
- **Quest**: As part of their move on Brampton, Roah Moonglow's enforcers burn down a tavern that refused to switch its protection payments from The Guild to them. The act is meant as a brutal message to other businesses in the area.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 15 / 10
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: TRUE
