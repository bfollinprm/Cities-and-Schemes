# Quests F-K

## How To Use This File

These factions run from F through K. Each entry highlights the quest, the stakes, and whether the attempt exposed the faction.

### Flaming Fist — Increased Shakedowns on Wyrm's Crossing
- **Quest**: The aggressive shakedowns backfire spectacularly. A confrontation with 'Mad' Meggan of the Free Traders erupts into a public brawl on the bridge. The Free Traders, united and furious, refuse to pay, creating a massive, multi-day blockade of Wyrm's Crossing. Marshal Flint's revenue grab results in a net loss of trade and a public relations disaster for the Fist.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 5 / 10
- **Success**: FALSE
- **Public**: FALSE

### Followers of the Phoenix — Hold a Sermon in New Elturel
- **Quest**: The 'Ember,' the charismatic leader of the Karlach-worshipping cult, holds their first public sermon in an abandoned square in New Elturel. A small crowd of downtrodden tieflings and outcasts is drawn to their message of righteous fury.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 5 / 5
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: TRUE

### Free Traders of the Outskirts — Establish a Mutual Protection Pact
- **Quest**: Mad' Meggan organizes a pact among the independent caravan masters of the Outer City. They agree to travel in larger, combined convoys and share intelligence on the increasingly aggressive Flaming Fist patrols at Wyrm's Crossing.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 5 / 10
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: TRUE

### Hand of the Absolute / Hands of the Absolute — Listen for the 'Echo'
- **Quest**: The ritual is too successful. Instead of a faint echo, they contact a feral psychic entity. The entity lashes out, killing one cultist and driving another mad before vanishing. The 'Voice' is left with a terrified, diminished flock and the knowledge that something dangerous has answered their prayers.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 20 / 15
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: FALSE

### Harpers — Plant an Agent in the Masons' Guild
- **Quest**: The Harper agent is vetted, but his cover story has a minor flaw. Suspicious, Master Borin Stonehand's people put the agent under surveillance. The agent gets the job but is watched at all times and fed misinformation, effectively becoming a double agent without his knowledge. The Harpers' investigation is compromised from the start.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 20 / 15
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: TRUE

### Hellriders of New Elturel — Submit Petition for Citizenship to the High Hall
- **Quest**: An aide to a nativist patriar leaks the petition, along with a falsified cost-analysis report, to the Baldur's Mouth. The resulting article stokes public fear about the 'refugee burden,' turning public opinion against the Hellriders and making citizenship an even more distant goal.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 0 / 15
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: TRUE

### Knights of the Shield — Recruit a Dispossessed Patriar
- **Quest**: Lord Hlath, though bitter, is a traditionalist and finds the Knights' diabolical dealings repugnant. He not only refuses their offer but, in a fit of patriotic pique, anonymously tips off a priest at the Watchful Shield Shrine about the Knights' resurgence. The recruitment attempt fails and exposes their renewed activity.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 20 / 15
- **Success**: FALSE
- **Public**: FALSE
