# Quests Q–V

## How To Use This File

This section documents entities whose names begin with Q through V, covering a wide range of cults, guilds, and watchdogs.

### Remnant Costers (Iron Throne — Sponsor an Adventuring Party
- **Quest**: The High Moon Coster, led by the Oberon family, scrapes together enough coin to sponsor a fledgling adventuring party to explore a rumored ruin in the Cloakwood, hoping to find a rare artifact that can restore a sliver of their family's lost fortune.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 15 / 15
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: TRUE

### River-Rats — Establish a New Sewer Cache
- **Quest**: Silas the Eel's crew identifies and secures a new, defensible sewer nexus beneath the Brampton district to serve as a hidden cache for smuggled goods, bypassing the increased Flaming Fist patrols on Wyrm's Crossing.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 20 / 10
- **Success**: FALSE
- **Public**: FALSE

### Sembian Kontor (The Iron Ledger) — Broker a Mercenary Contract for the Western Gate Trading Company
- **Quest**: Bursar Korgan Ironhand brokers a deal between the Western Gate Trading Company and a tough mercenary band. The mercs are hired to 'protect' the Company's new logging operations from 'bandits' (a clear euphemism for the Circle of the Inner Grove).
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 5 / 5
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: TRUE

### The Bibliophile — Sell Information on a Cheating Merchant
- **Quest**: Nansi Gretta's network sells proof to a Chionthar Consortium member that his partner is secretly making side deals with the Sembian Kontor. The information is sold for a high price, and the resulting fallout benefits Gretta's other clients.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 20 / 10
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: FALSE

### The Carnival of Whispers — Abduct a 'Passionate' Soul
- **Quest**: The abduction attempt turns into a noisy street fight as the poet, a skilled fencer, fights back. The clowns flee after the poet wounds one and raises the alarm. The Bloomridge neighborhood is now on high alert for sinister clowns, making future abductions in the area far more difficult.

### The Elturel Remembrance Society — Declare a Baldurian Merchant 'Infernal'
