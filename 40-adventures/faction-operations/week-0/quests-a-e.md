# Quests A–E

## How To Use This File

This batch contains factions whose names begin with A through E. Each entry captures the quest, essential stats, and whether the move was public or successful.

### Amnian Kontor (The Gold-Gable Hall) — Finalize Foreclosure on the Hlath Estate
- **Quest**: Consul Vorlamin Athar's legal agents finalize the foreclosure on the Hlath patriar estate in Manorborn. The family, ruined by gambling debts owed to Amnian lenders, is quietly and unceremoniously evicted. The Kontor now owns a prime piece of Upper City real estate.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 10 / 5
- **Success**: FALSE
- **Public**: FALSE

### Baldur's Mouth — Print a Pro-Consortium Puff Piece
- **Quest**: For a hefty bag of coin from Matron Lyra Silvershield, editor Ettvard Needle runs a front-page story in the Baldur's Mouth praising the Western Gate Trading Company for 'bravely taming the wilderness' to provide jobs and resources for the city.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 0 / 5
- **Success**: FALSE
- **Public**: TRUE

### Chionthar Consortium — Secure Exclusive Warehousing Contract
- **Quest**: The Sembian Kontor gets wind of Master Joric's plan. Bursar Korgan Ironhand leverages his vast cash reserves to make a last-minute, unbeatable offer to the warehouse owners. The Chionthar Consortium not only loses the exclusive contract but is forced to pay even higher rates for storage, marking a major financial defeat at the hands of their foreign rival.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 10 / 10
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: TRUE

### Circle of the Inner Grove — Perform a Ritual of Warding
- **Quest**: The ritual is corrupted by the region's lingering infernal energy. Instead of strengthening the natural animals, it twists them into fiendish, aggressive versions that attack loggers and druids alike. Elion has inadvertently created a bigger problem, blighting the wood he sought to protect.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 20 / 10
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: TRUE

### Council of Four — Finalize Hinterland Logging Expansion Charter
- **Quest**: Lord Durinbold, leading the Peerage of Blood, finds a procedural loophole in the city charter regarding land use outside the walls. He successfully tables the motion for "further review," delaying the charter indefinitely and dealing a public blow to Archduke Silvershield's authority.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 5 / 5
- **Success**: FALSE
- **Public**: FALSE

### Cult of the Returning Lord (Bhaal) — Consecrate a Sewer Altar with a 'Nobody's' Blood
- **Quest**: Bhaal cultists kidnap a homeless man from a Heapside alley and murder him on a makeshift altar in the Undercity, consecrating the space for future, more important rituals. The body is disposed of and the man is never missed.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 20 / 10
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: FALSE
