# Quests L–P

## How To Use This File

Covering factions L through P, this file collects the political and economic maneuvering that shapes week 0.

### Lantaner Concession (The Cogwork Quay) — Root Out an Industrial Spy
- **Quest**: The apprentice spy, forewarned by an accomplice, manages to escape the Cogwork Quay just before security closes in. They make it out of the enclave with a partial schematic of the smokepowder mechanism, delivering a valuable secret to their handlers in the Worshipful Company of Smiths & Armorers. The Concession now has a known security breach.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 20 / 10
- **Success**: FALSE
- **Public**: FALSE

### Parliament of Peers — Debate the Refugee Relief Act
- **Quest**: The bill is formally shelved after a series of acrimonious debates. The Peerage of Blood, citing fiscal concerns and 'civic purity,' successfully filibusters the final vote, leaving the refugees with no official aid and increasing tensions in the Outer City.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 5 / 10
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: FALSE

### Patriar Heritage Society — Host a 'Baldurian Purity' Gala
- **Quest**: Lord Corin Durinbold hosts an exclusive gala for members of the Peerage of Blood. The theme is 'Baldurian Purity,' and the event is a thinly veiled political rally against the influence of the foreign Kontors and 'new money' consortiums.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 10 / 5
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: FALSE

### Peerage of Blood — Block the 'New Kontor Wayleave' Proposal
- **Quest**: Led by Lord Corin Durinbold, the traditionalist patriars successfully block a proposal from the Amnian Kontor that would grant them a private, toll-free wayleave through a section of the Lower City, arguing it's a gross violation of Baldurian sovereignty. The vote is a rare victory for the old guard.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 10 / 10
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: TRUE

### Peerage of Coin — Push Through the 'Harbor Dredging Levy'
- **Quest**: The major shipping factions, led by the Chionthar Consortium and the Sembian Kontor, successfully lobby to pass a new levy on all non-guild vessels entering Gray Harbour, framing it as necessary to fund the dredging of the Chionthar. The cost is passed on to independent merchants.
- **Discovery DC / DC**: 5 / 5
- **Success**: TRUE
- **Public**: FALSE
