# Calimport

Capital and great port of **Calimshan**, far south of the Sword Coast. The campaign references Calimport almost entirely through its trade relationship with Baldur's Gate.

## Why the campaign cares

- **The Seven Suns coster's lifeline.** [House Jhasso](../20-Factions/seven-suns/people/jhasso-family.md) and the [Seven Suns](../20-Factions/seven-suns/summary.md) trade on a century of Calimport contracts. The campaign-relevant southern trade — luxury goods, single high-value shipments, family heirlooms — moves on the Calimport run.
- **Adventuring hooks.** Cargo recovery, escort of a single high-value Calimport shipment, retrieval of a Jhasso heirloom from an Amnian auction floor. Standard Seven Suns commission shapes.

The city itself is offstage. What matters is the route.
