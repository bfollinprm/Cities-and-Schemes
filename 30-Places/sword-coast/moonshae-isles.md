# Moonshae Isles

A cluster of large, mist-girded islands off the central Sword Coast, divided between the Ffolk human kingdoms (most prominently Callidyrr on Alaron) and a quieter elven realm in the north. Famous for moonwells, druids, and a long, complicated relationship with both the [fey courts](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/README.md) and the great Sword Coast cities.

## Sarifal

On the north of Gwynneth lies **Sarifal**, where a fey queen openly rules in mortal stead — a rare standing arrangement in which the [Feywild](../../10-Setting/cosmology/realms/feywild.md)'s authority and the Material Plane's are not so much "crossed" as *welded*. The arrangement is old, ratified by the moonwells, and has held through more than one age.

The **Moonwell of Sarifal** blackened for one night during the recent [Far Realm](../../10-Setting/cosmology/far-realm.md) portents — taken across Faerûn as a serious omen. The fey queen has not commented; the [Green Masquerade](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/green-masquerade.md) has made repeated, unsuccessful overtures.

## Travel and trade

The Moonshaes are a working sea route — bulk trade between the Sword Coast and the islands, with smaller runs to and from the Korinn Archipelago and the High Forest coast. Storms are constant; pirate trouble is intermittent; the fey queen's "tax" — which falls only on those she dislikes — is unpredictable.
