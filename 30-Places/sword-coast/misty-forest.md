# Misty Forest

A medium, mist-pooled woodland east of Daggerford and south of the High Road. By daylight: ordinary mixed forest, deer trails, a handful of woodcutter camps. By night: one of the active **fey crossings** documented by the [Green Masquerade](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/green-masquerade.md), and lately a far less friendly one than it used to be.

## The glade

A circle of mushrooms and moss-stones near the wood's centre once drifted with golden fireflies and answered to a courteous unseen host. As of 1501 DR it has begun to exude a viscous purple fog at midnight; a hunter who stepped into the ring lost a day, returned with hair gone white, and has since refused to speak the host's name.

The Green Masquerade reads the change as [Far Realm](../../10-Setting/cosmology/far-realm.md) intrusion bleeding through the [Feywild](../../10-Setting/cosmology/realms/feywild.md)'s thin places, and has put two Revelers on permanent watch from a half-day's distance.

## Etiquette and access

Camp at the edge in daylight; do not enter at night. Do not step inside the mushroom ring — even by accident, even briefly. If you must speak the host's name aloud, do not do it twice.
