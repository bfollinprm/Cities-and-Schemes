# Laughing Hollow

A fey-touched glade near [Daggerford](daggerford/README.md). Pixies, centaurs, satyrs, and wild elves live alongside one another under the rule of an ancient fey prince. The Hollow is the public-facing example of a stable mortal-fey neighbourhood on the Sword Coast — one most travellers can actually point to on a map.

## Pact-brokered envoys

[Lord Fox of Secomber](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/seelie.md), via [the Green Masquerade](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/green-masquerade.md), brokered the pact that lets the Hollow's pixies send envoys to [Waterdeep](waterdeep/README.md). The arrangement holds because both sides keep their end small: the pixies do not bring more than a handful at a time, and Waterdeep gives them a quiet welcome through the Masquerade's seasonal balls rather than at the city gates.

## Far Realm pressure

The Hollow has registered the same Far Realm portents as the rest of the Sword Coast's fey neighbourhoods — a pixie was seen with tentacles where its wings should be, a fact that travelled fast through the [Court of Stars](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/court-of-stars.md). Will-o'-wisps and Unseelie sprites lead travellers astray in the Hollow more often than they used to; the [Unseelie](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/unseelie.md) press is real, even here.
