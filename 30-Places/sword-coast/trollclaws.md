# The Trollclaws

A line of hills and broken ground north of Baldur's Gate, traversed by the **Trade Way**. The dangerous middle stretch of the caravan road between Waterdeep and Baldur's Gate — bandit country, monster country, no place to lose a wagon.

## What the campaign references

- **Waterdhavian intelligence frontier.** The senior Waterdhavian intelligence officer south of the Trollclaws is **Elara Mornwood**, factor of the [Waterdhavian Kontor](../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/summary.md) and a high-ranking half-elf member of the Lords' Alliance. The Trollclaws is the boundary that defines her territory.
- **The Vancroft scam.** Elias Vancroft of the [Worshipful Company of Cartographers & Surveyors](../../20-Factions/wc-cartographers-surveyors/summary.md) sold "exclusive" Trollclaws passage maps to two rival caravan masters at once, on a route the Company knows to be dangerous, pocketing both fees.
