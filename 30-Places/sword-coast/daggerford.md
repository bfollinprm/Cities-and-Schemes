# Daggerford

Small fortified town on the Sword Coast road between Waterdeep and Baldur's Gate. In the present campaign, Daggerford appears mostly as a *location for fey weather* rather than a political actor.

## What the campaign references

- **The Laughing Hollow.** A pocket of the [Feywild](../../10-Setting/cosmology/realms/feywild.md) bleeding into the Misty Forest near Daggerford; pixies, centaurs, satyrs, wild elves, and an ancient fey prince. Pact-brokered envoys travel from here to Waterdeep through the [Green Masquerade](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/green-masquerade.md).
- **Bolder fey-touched wilds.** Hags, redcaps, will-o'-wisps, and Unseelie sprites have grown more aggressive in the woods around Daggerford post-Sundering, when the crossings *stabilized rather than closed*.
