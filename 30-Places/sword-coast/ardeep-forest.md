# Ardeep Forest

A close, old forest south of Waterdeep — modest in extent compared to the [Wood of Sharp Teeth](wood-of-sharp-teeth.md), but venerable, and sacred to the local elves long before the human cities rose. Travellers on the High Road skirt its eastern edge; few enter and fewer linger.

At the heart of the wood is a **weirwood circle** — a ring of pale-barked, slow-growing trees considered an active crossing into the [Feywild](../../10-Setting/cosmology/realms/feywild.md). The circle is attended by the **Lady of Twilight**, a Wild Archfey in the [Court of Stars](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/court-of-stars.md)' loose register but bound to no court.

## Recent disturbances

The Far Realm's pressure on the Feywild has begun to make itself felt here as elsewhere. A tentacled dryad was reported in the wood — screaming in Sylvan and Deep Speech — by a Helmite hunting party in 1501 DR. The Lady of Twilight has not commented; the [Green Masquerade](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/green-masquerade.md) has dispatched a Reveler to investigate.

## Etiquette and access

Travel openly, in daylight, and stay on the deer-paths. Do not break weirwood. Do not name yourself aloud at the circle — names spoken there are remembered.
