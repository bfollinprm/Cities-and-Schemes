# Undercity — Events

## Week 0

- **Bhaalist sewer altar consecrated.** [Cult of the Returning Lord](../../../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/summary.md) cultists murdered a kidnapped homeless man on a makeshift altar, consecrating the space for future, more important rituals. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_0/consecrate-a-sewer-altar.md).
- **Undercellar counterfeiter shut down.** [Master Theronius Goldhand's](../../../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/people/theronius-goldhand.md) agents and [The Guild](../../../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) jointly identified and closed a small-time counterfeiter operating out of [the Undercellar](the-undercellar.md). See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_0/expose-a-counterfeit-operation.md).

## Week 3

- **River-Rats Undercellar run wiped out by ghouls.** [Silas the Eel's](../../../../20-Factions/river-rats/people/silas-the-eel.md) crew runs a high-margin patriar wine consignment too close to [the necromantic seepage the Gravekeepers are starting to map](../../../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_3/a-pattern-in-the-rot.md); half the crew killed, shipment lost. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_3/the-undercellar-run.md).
- **Butchers' spoiled-meat run seized at Hamhock's Slaughterhouse.** [Goodman Borlu's](../../../../20-Factions/wc-butchers-tanners/people/borlu.md) attempt to sell questionable rations to Sow's Foot refugees out of the [slaughterhouse](hamhocks-vault.md) is caught by a Hellrider patrol on heightened spoilage watch and burned publicly. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-butchers-tanners/activity/week_3/salvage-the-loss.md).

## Week 4

- **Hidden Bhaalist temple discovered beneath Tumbledown.** [Old Man Hemlock's](../../../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/people/hemlock.md) keepers descend the sewers tracking [last week's necromantic-seepage map](../../../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_3/a-pattern-in-the-rot.md) and find the temple at the bottom of a half-flooded shaft: four sewer altars, congealed offerings, the [Cult of the Returning Lord's](../../../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/summary.md) sigil on the keystone. The unholy pressure of the rituals above is what is making Tumbledown's dead stir. The location is logged with the Watch, the Hellriders, and a Harper contact; no engagement. See [Gravekeepers activity](../../../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_4/the-source-of-the-sickness.md).
- **Silas the Eel loses Harper documents to a Fist patrol close-call.** A Harper-paid courier run through the Undercity tunnels collides with [Fist sewer surveillance pivoted toward the Bhaalist temple](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/activity/week_4/an-alliance-of-steel-and-silver.md); [Silas](../../../../20-Factions/river-rats/people/silas-the-eel.md) ditches the packet into a drainage sluice and barely escapes. The Harpers' sensitive intelligence is lost. See [River-Rats activity](../../../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_4/a-favor-for-a-friend.md).

## Week 6

- **The Bhaalists' Gutter Prayer goes wrong.** Scattered survivors of the [Cult of the Returning Lord](../../../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/summary.md), now without idols or a senior officiant, abduct seven of the city's forgotten — beggars, sewer jacks — for a Slayer-summoning ritual deep in the sewers. The chant misfires on the seventh victim; instead of a Slayer, it calls a chittering four-legged shadow that scatters the cell into the deeper tunnels. **Three cultists go missing by morning.** Something has been called and lost loose down here. See [Cult activity](../../../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_6/the-gutter-prayer.md).
- **The Unveiled run a frantic safehouse-shuffle.** [Xylia](../../../../20-Factions/unveiled/people/xylia.md) keeps the rescued comrades from [last week's Heapside prison break](../../../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_5/the-great-escape.md) one step ahead of the Fist dragnet, rotating between three Undercity safehouses on a hand-walked schedule. The Unveiled survive the week without growing. See [Unveiled activity](../../../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_6/the-safehouse-shuffle.md).
