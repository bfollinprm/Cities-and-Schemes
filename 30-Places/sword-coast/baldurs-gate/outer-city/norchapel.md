# Norchapel

A district of Baldur's Gate's [Outer City](README.md). Mostly notable in the present campaign as the site of a [Worshipful Company of Chandlers & Lamplighters](../../../../20-Factions/wc-chandlers-lamplighters/summary.md) expansion contract.

## What is happening there

- **The Norchapel expansion contract.** The Chandlers won a recent contract to extend lamp service into Norchapel.
- **Lamplighters as informants.** [Anya Brightwood](../../../../20-Factions/wc-chandlers-lamplighters/people/anya-brightwood.md) quietly tasked the Norchapel crews to act as a low-level intelligence network — discreetly reporting unusual activity on their nightly rounds. The expansion is functioning as a covert listening post in the Outer City.
