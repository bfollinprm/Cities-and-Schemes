# Wyrm's Rock

The squat granite island at the centre of [Wyrm's Crossing](wyrms-crossing.md), straddled by the two great spans of the bridge. The fortress on its back is the [Flaming Fist](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/summary.md)'s headquarters: drawbridge-control, gaol, parade ground, and the seat from which Marshal **Valerius Flint** runs whatever piece of the city the Council has not yet stripped from him.

A symbol of strength that no longer reflects reality. The walls are sound, the garrison is not.

## Notable in this house

- **The Marshal's tower.** Flint's offices and quarters; the room from which the [Wyrm's Crossing](wyrms-crossing.md) crisis is being managed. Petitioners — including [WC of Smiths & Armorers](../../../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/summary.md) Master Tor-keg Silverhand — have begun appealing to him soldier-to-soldier, over the Council's head.
- **The drawbridge keep.** Tolls collected, traffic regulated, periodic lockdowns enforced. When the Rock closes its bridges, the south bank suffocates.
- **The gaol.** A short, brutal stone keep in the lower courses; the city's holding cells before transfer to Heapside Prison or the gallows at the Basilisk Gate.
- **The parade ground.** A cobbled court where the Fist's diminished companies still drum out morning roll. Tactical maps of the [Wyrm's Crossing](wyrms-crossing.md) and Basilisk Gate dispositions are kept under lock here — and have already been quietly copied by the [Bibliophile](../../../../20-Factions/bibliophile/summary.md).

## Who you find here

Off-duty Fist, watch officers, gaolers, the Marshal's adjutants, the [Unchained](../../../../20-Factions/unchained/summary.md) embeds who travel under Flint's protection, and the line of petitioners and lawyers who turn up at every audience-day.

## Etiquette and access

Permits or business — nothing else gets you past the outer gate. Peace-tied weapons. Do not disrupt a bridge lockdown; do not approach the Marshal without an appointment. The gaol is entered at the rear, and not voluntarily.

## Pressures

The Heritage Society's silver-bonus loyalty fund is corroding the rank-and-file's allegiance to Flint himself; the Bibliophile's leaked tactical maps are already in patriar hands; and Flint's [Githyanki](../../../../20-Factions/unchained/summary.md)-aligned officers and his Heritage-bribed officers are now openly working at cross-purposes inside his own walls.
