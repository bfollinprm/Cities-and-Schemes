# Wyrm's Bend Market

A wide, dusty square where the [Trade Way](../hinterlands/trade-way/) elbows into Rivington and the south bank's caravan circuits unload their produce, hides, and unlicensed goods. Stalls cluster around the central well, an inn shoulders the south side, and a hundred small mercantile bargains are struck here every day before the chapel bell sounds noon.

Wyrm's Bend is the operating base of the [Free Traders of the Outskirts](../../../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) — a loose cooperative of independent caravan masters and stallholders who work the routes the Foreign Kontors don't bother to undercut.

## Notable in this house

- **The Free Traders' table.** A long oak slab in the inn's common room where the cooperative's council meets; Mad Meggan presides from a chair scarred with knife-marks of every deal sworn over it.
- **The well.** Where caravan masters water their oxen and where Meggan keeps an open ear for hire-and-help work.
- **The "back stalls."** A row of canvas-roofed pitches behind the well where unlicensed produce — and the occasional unlicensed everything else — moves quickly and quietly.

## Who you find here

Free Trader caravan masters, drovers, day-labourers, refugee farmers from the burned Hinterlands hamlets, hedge-priests, fence-runners, and the inn-rooms above hold Meggan's small court of lieutenants.

## Etiquette and access

Buy something before you ask questions. Speak Common, plus whatever scraps of Calant you can muster — the Free Traders take it as respect. Do not flash a Worshipful Company sigil; you will not enjoy the price you are quoted.

## Pressures

The [Chionthar Consortium](../../../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md)'s patrols pressing on the river-side caravans; the [Flaming Fist](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/summary.md)'s on-again, off-again toll enforcement; and a known Mad Meggan bribe from the Council that the patriar press would happily expose if it weren't already in their interest to keep quiet.
