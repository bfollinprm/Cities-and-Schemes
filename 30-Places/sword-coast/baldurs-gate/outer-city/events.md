# Outer City — Events

## Week 0

### Wyrm's Crossing

- **Flaming Fist shakedowns backfire.** [Manip Jerrol's](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/people/manip-jerrol.md) escalation triggered a public brawl with ['Mad' Meggan](../../../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) and a multi-day Free Trader blockade of the bridge. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/activity/week_0/increased-shakedowns-on-wyrms-crossing.md).
- **Mutual-protection pact signed.** Free Trader caravan masters formalize convoys and intelligence-sharing on Fist patrols. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_0/establish-a-mutual-protection-pact.md).

### New Elturel

- **First Phoenix sermon.** [The Ember](../../../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) preached publicly in an abandoned square; a small crowd of tieflings and outcasts answered. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_0/hold-a-sermon-in-new-elturel.md).
- **Hellrider citizenship petition leaked.** A patriar aide leaked the High Hall petition with a falsified cost-analysis to the [Baldur's Mouth](../../../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/summary.md), souring public opinion. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_0/submit-petition-for-citizenship.md).
- **Refugee Relief Act shelved.** [Peerage of Blood](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/summary.md) filibuster killed the bill in [Parliament](../../../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/summary.md). See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_0/debate-the-refugee-relief-act.md).
- **Guild winter relief distributed.** [Nine-Fingers Keene's](../../../../20-Factions/guild/people/astele-keene.md) Ward Bosses ran blankets and food into the camps. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_0/organize-winter-relief-for-new-elturel.md).
- **Refugee strikebreakers hired.** The [Chionthar Consortium](../../../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) recruited refugee labor to break the Wharf Rats' strike, poisoning relations between Baldurian dockers and Elturians. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-fishmongers/activity/week_0/organize-a-dockworker-strike.md).
- **Waterdhavian agents pry "lost" Elturian tomes.** Factor Elara Mornwood acquired rare historical texts from a refugee scholar at a humiliating price. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_0/acquire-lost-elturian-tomes.md).

### Tumbledown and Floodway

- **Gravekeepers cleansing interrupted.** A wraith broke a key warding stone before the Gravekeepers put it down; the graveyard is more vulnerable than before. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_0/perform-a-cleansing-ritual-in-tumbledown.md).

## Week 1

- **Hellrider protest at the southern bridgehead.** [High Watcher Alena](../../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) and the [Followers of the Phoenix](../../../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) hold the line for several days. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_1/organize-a-protest-at-wyrms-crossing.md).
- **Free Trader blockade at the Basilisk Gate, broken.** [Blaze Liara Portyr](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/people/liara-portyr.md) personally storms the line; caravan masters arrested. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_1/blockade-the-basilisk-gate.md).
- **Manip Jerrol beats a protesting merchant.** Public, witnessed, and the spark for the protest. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/activity/week_1/brutalize-a-protesting-merchant.md).
- **Mad Meggan's vessel impounded.** Chionthar/Consortium-Fist enforcement of the Harbor Dredging Levy. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_1/impound-an-independent-vessel.md).
- **Sembian Kontor seats an asset inside the Fist barracks at Wyrm's Rock.** See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/activity/week_1/recruit-a-new-information-asset.md).
- **Medicine corridor brokered by the party.** Spark talks Alena into a corridor; Finneas talks the Fist sergeant into letting it through. See [party activity](../../../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/broker-the-medicine-corridor-at-wyrms-crossing.md).

### Basilisk Gate

- **Mason wall section collapses under shoddy repair.** Master [Borin Stonehand](../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/people/borin-stonehand.md) tries to launder the failure as Zhentarim sabotage in *Baldur's Mouth*; the story dies. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_1/publicly-blame-sabotage-for-flaw.md). The [Unveiled](../../../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_1/find-proof-in-the-masons-wall.md) repurpose the rubble as "psionic resonance weapon" pamphlets; the [Harpers](../../../../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_1/investigate-the-masons-sabotage.md) cannot reach the site for forensics.

### Rivington

- **Sewer Plague pinned to the Worm Spit.** Earliest cases originate from the drainage canal on Rivington's edge; the [Worshipful Company of Alchemists & Apothecaries'](../../../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_1/deny-antitoxin-to-a-clinic.md) refusal of antitoxin makes the temple-as-hospital break first. The party heists the medicine cart out of a Chionthar warehouse in [Little Calimshan](little-calimshan.md) and delivers it to the Open Hand. See [party activity](../../../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/heist-the-rivington-medicine-cart.md).
- **Plague-Father's laboratory destroyed in the sewers under the Worm Spit.** A [Plague of Remembrance](../../../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) cell — three vats of glowing green plague-fluid, **cracked-vial** sigil, Nightmare-Root reagents — was wiped out by the party in session 5. One cultist captured; the orphan [Finn](../../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/finn.md) was rescued from the same tunnel network. See [party activity](../../../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/destroy-the-plague-fathers-laboratory.md).
- **Coast Way Zhentarim toll, four miles south.** Defused; party paid 18 gp for the caravan and was reimbursed 38 gp by grateful travelers. See [party activity](../../../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/pay-the-zhentarim-toll-on-the-coast-way.md).
- **Mister Smiles flagged at the Circus of the Last Days.** Elia walked away rather than poke alone. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_1/host-a-dream-inducing-performance.md).
- **Vex of the Guild starts intaking the party at the Weary Plowman.** First job: silence the mason [Gareth](../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/people/gareth.md), no killing. See [party activity](../../../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/take-the-vex-job-shadow-gareth.md).
- **Purified hand out vials in Rivington.** No converts; everyone drinks the water and tells the story at supper. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/purified/activity/week_1/distribute-purified-water.md).

### New Elturel

- **Followers of the Phoenix granted official representation in the New Elturel council and land for a temple.** [High Watcher Alena](../../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) concedes after the medicine corridor; Spark closes it. See [party activity](../../../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/accept-the-jaredith-mission-from-alena.md).
- **Senior Hellrider lieutenant [Jaredith Heartsong](../../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) drifts toward the Phoenix Pits.** Alena hires the party to discreetly turn him back. See [party activity](../../../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/accept-the-jaredith-mission-from-alena.md).
- **Survivor Swap prices spike.** Inflation visible: clothing, rations, weapons all gouged.
- **Refugee Question hearing fails to reach a vote.** Council of Four hearing devolves into a shouting match. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_1/public-hearing-on-the-refugee-question.md).
- **Dammon buys an infernal-contract shard from a New Elturel tiefling.** See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_1/purchase-an-infernal-contract-shard.md).

### Little Calimshan

- **Chionthar warehouse heist.** The party walks the Open Hand medicine cart out under Fist escort and a second cart of anti-Amnian propaganda out without one. See [party activity](../../../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/heist-the-rivington-medicine-cart.md). The Consortium does not yet know about the second cart.

### The K'liir

- **Hands-of-the-Absolute echoes near the camp go unfound.** Both [Gravekeepers](../../../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_1/investigate-psychic-disturbances-near-the-kliir.md) and [Unchained](../../../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_1/begin-investigating-psychic-echoes.md) sweeps come up empty.
