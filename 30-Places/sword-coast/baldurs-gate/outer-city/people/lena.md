# Lena

Proprietor of **The Weary Plowman** in [Rivington](../rivington.md). Tall, broad, and unimpressed. The Plowman is the kind of room that fills up with people who hate each other, and Lena is the kind of innkeeper who keeps that room from burning down.

## Role with the party

In [Session 2](../../../../../50-Sessions/session_2/player_summary.md) Lena cold-defused the fight between [Gareth](../../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/people/gareth.md) and [Mikkel](../../../../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/mikkel.md) by reminding both men of their shared community ties. Bror gathered week-1 rumors from her at the bar that night — the protest, the Zhentarim Coast Way shakedowns, the Free Trader [Basilisk Gate blockade](../../../../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_1/blockade-the-basilisk-gate.md).
