# Brother Billiam

A young monk of Ilmater serving at the **Open Hand Temple** in [Rivington](../rivington.md). He worked the floor of the temple-as-hospital during the Sewer Plague's first wave and knew, before most of the city's authorities did, that the sickness was unnatural.

## Role with the party

In [Session 4](../../../../../50-Sessions/session_4/player_summary.md), Brother Billiam confirmed the party's suspicion that the plague was a poison "on body and soul," cross-referenced their map of cases against his own, and pinned the earliest infections to the **Worm Spit** drainage canal. He pulled 50 gp out of the temple's meagre coffers and hired the party to find the source — money Kaelan promptly snatched. He is, plainly, the temple's contact for the party going forward.
