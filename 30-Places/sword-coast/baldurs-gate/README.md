# Baldur's Gate

Walled city on the north bank of the **River Chionthar** where it meets the Sea of Swords. Reconstruction has reshaped the city since the Absolute Crisis a decade ago; its hunger for timber, stone, and grain now drives the politics of the surrounding lands.

City-level prose (districts, polities, demographics, internal factions) is owned by the Baldur's Gate dossier ingest (issue #19). Until that lands, this folder is a stub so the surrounding lands have a parent.
