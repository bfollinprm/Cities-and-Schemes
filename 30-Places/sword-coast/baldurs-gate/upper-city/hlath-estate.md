# Hlath Estate

A handsome four-storey manor on the [Manorborn](manorborn.md) ridge, foreclosed when the Hlath family fell out of solvency in the wake of the Bhaalspawn crisis and the Iron Throne collapse. The Hlaths themselves are dispossessed but very much extant, and the ownership of their old house is currently the most-contested patriar property fight in the city.

The [Amnian Kontor](../../../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/summary.md) holds the foreclosure paper and is preparing to sell the estate to one of [Vorlamin Athar](../../../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/people/vorlamin-athar.md)'s allies. [Corin Durinbold](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) of the [Peerage of Blood](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/summary.md) is preparing an injunction to freeze the sale, citing an obscure ancient property law.

Meanwhile the [Patriar Heritage Society](../../../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/summary.md) has publicly attempted to take in the dispossessed Hlaths as a propaganda triumph — and was outmaneuvered when the Amnians intercepted the family first. Lord Hlath, recently approached by the [Knights of the Shield](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/summary.md) to swear an infernal oath, balked, and quietly tipped off a priest at the [Watchful Shield Shrine](watchful-shield-shrine.md) about the Knights' resurgence.

## Notable in this house

- **The shuttered great hall.** Hlath family arms still hang above the cold hearth; the Amnian foreclosure inventory tags are pinned to every piece of furniture.
- **The library.** Stripped of most of its valuables but the family papers are intact — and the Heritage Society would very much like a look.
- **The kitchens.** Operated by a single Amnian-paid steward who lets in the Coin-Sword guards on rotation.

## Who you find here

Two or three Coin-Swords (the Amnian Kontor's private guards) on permanent watch; the steward; periodic Peerage of Blood inspectors with notarised survey paperwork; the occasional Heritage Society sympathiser leaving flowers at the gate.

## Etiquette and access

The Amnians admit visitors by appointment only. The Coin-Swords will turn you away politely the first time and forcefully the second. There is, of course, a back lane.

## Pressures

A live legal injunction in front of the [Parliament of Peers](../../../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/summary.md); a rumoured Heritage Society plan to "restore" the family by force on the night of the auction; and the Watchful Shield priests now actively listening for any rumour of patriar votes for sale that would tie back to the same Knights' Ledger that scared Hlath off in the first place.
