# Watchful Shield Shrine

A small, austere shrine to Helm the Vigilant, set back from the boulevard in the [Temples District](temples-district.md) where the [Lower City](../lower-city/README.md) processions don't quite reach. The Helmite priesthood here is famously incorruptible and famously stubborn, and have lately become an unwelcome conscience for the city's quieter scandals.

The Shrine was tipped off — anonymously, by a balking Lord Hlath — about the resurgent [Knights of the Shield](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/summary.md), and the priesthood has been actively listening ever since. The Knights have flagged the priests as enemies; the priests have begun lighting a votive a night for "patriar votes honestly cast."

## Notable in this house

- **The vigil-room.** A bare chamber with one shielded lantern; senior priests take turns sitting through the night, listening to whatever the city happens to whisper through the open shutters. Confessions overheard here do not stay overheard.
- **The shrine proper.** A plain altar, a polished steel buckler hanging above it, and a stone basin for the rite of accepted oaths.
- **The vestry safe.** Where the priests keep what they have been told and what they have promised to keep secret. The Knights would like very much to know its contents.

## Who you find here

The shrine's three resident priests, an honour-guard of two retired Watch sergeants, lay-helmite scribes copying a dossier of overheard rumour, and the occasional weary patriar who has come to confess what he should not have agreed to.

## Etiquette and access

Lower your hood. Surrender your blade at the door. Pay nothing — the Helmites refuse cash. If you have come to inform, the priest in the vigil-room will hear you alone and will not write your name down.

## Pressures

A live target on the priests' backs from the Knights of the Shield; the rumoured **Infernal Ledger** that the priests are now hunting and which the Knights will kill to recover; and an internal debate about whether to take their dossier of overheard rumour to the [Flaming Fist](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/summary.md) or to the [Harpers](../../../../20-Factions/harpers/summary.md).
