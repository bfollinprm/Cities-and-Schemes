# Upper City — Events

## Week 0

### Manorborn

- **Hlath estate foreclosure finalized.** [Consul Vorlamin Athar's](../../../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/people/vorlamin-athar.md) Amnian agents evicted [Lord Hlath](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/lord-hlath.md) over gambling debts; the Kontor now owns a prime piece of Manorborn. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/activity/week_0/finalize-foreclosure-on-the-hlath-estate.md).
- **Knights of the Shield exposed at the [Watchful Shield Shrine](watchful-shield-shrine.md).** The Knights tried to recruit Hlath; he refused and anonymously tipped off a priest at the shrine, alerting Helm's faithful to the order's resurgence. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_0/recruit-a-dispossessed-patriar.md).
- **"Baldurian Purity" gala.** [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) hosted the Peerage of Blood for a thinly veiled rally against the foreign Kontors. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_0/host-a-baldurian-purity-gala.md).
- **Jannath Estate vandalism written off.** Red paint splashed on the estate's "foreign-style" Sembian statues was dismissed as youthful hijinks by the Watch — actually a [Society of Baldurian Integrity](../../../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/summary.md) statement. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_0/investigate-vandalism-at-the-jannath-estate.md).

## Week 1

### Manorborn

- **Hlath estate sale blocked.** [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) finds a pre-Crisis property statute and files an injunction tying the [Amnian Kontor](../../../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/activity/week_1/sell-the-hlath-estate-to-an-ally.md) sale up in city bureaucracy.
- **Patriar Heritage Society sponsorship of the Hlaths fails.** The Hlaths refuse the aid under Amnian pressure. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_1/sponsor-the-dispossessed-hlath-family.md).
- **Lord Hlath flees a Knights of the Shield oath.** See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_1/swear-in-lord-hlath.md).
- **Goldsmiths deliver a canary automaton to Lord Durinbold.** Quiet alliance gift between the patriar bloc and the [Worshipful Company of Goldsmiths & Jewelers](../../../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_1/create-a-masterwork-for-a-patriar.md).
- **Master goldsmith Elara Varrin survives a Takers-of-the-Tithe Reaper attempt** under hired bodyguard cover. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_1/the-reaper-strikes.md).

### The Wide

- **Hands-of-the-Absolute kidnapping foiled** by an unexpectedly resilient fortune-teller. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_1/kidnap-a-psychic.md).

### Citadel and Watch beat

- **Mocking Watch caricatures appear near the Citadel.** The Watch's [unsanctioned-art crackdown](../../../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_1/crack-down-on-unsanctioned-art.md) is quietly abandoned.
- **Sorcerous Sodality scries Lord Durinbold.** No public footprint; the result is held as leverage. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_1/scry-on-a-peerage-of-blood-member.md).

### Parliament of Peers

- **Heritage Preservation Act killed on the floor.** A generational shift signal: patriar political ceiling is now lower than it was a year ago. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_1/lobby-for-heritage-preservation-laws.md).
