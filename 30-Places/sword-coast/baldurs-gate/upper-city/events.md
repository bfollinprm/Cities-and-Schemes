# Upper City — Events

## Week 0

### Manorborn

- **Hlath estate foreclosure finalized.** [Consul Vorlamin Athar's](../../../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/people/vorlamin-athar.md) Amnian agents evicted [Lord Hlath](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/lord-hlath.md) over gambling debts; the Kontor now owns a prime piece of Manorborn. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/activity/week_0/finalize-foreclosure-on-the-hlath-estate.md).
- **Knights of the Shield exposed at the [Watchful Shield Shrine](watchful-shield-shrine.md).** The Knights tried to recruit Hlath; he refused and anonymously tipped off a priest at the shrine, alerting Helm's faithful to the order's resurgence. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_0/recruit-a-dispossessed-patriar.md).
- **"Baldurian Purity" gala.** [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) hosted the Peerage of Blood for a thinly veiled rally against the foreign Kontors. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_0/host-a-baldurian-purity-gala.md).
- **Jannath Estate vandalism written off.** Red paint splashed on the estate's "foreign-style" Sembian statues was dismissed as youthful hijinks by the Watch — actually a [Society of Baldurian Integrity](../../../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/summary.md) statement. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_0/investigate-vandalism-at-the-jannath-estate.md).

## Week 1

### Manorborn

- **Hlath estate sale blocked.** [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) finds a pre-Crisis property statute and files an injunction tying the [Amnian Kontor](../../../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/activity/week_1/sell-the-hlath-estate-to-an-ally.md) sale up in city bureaucracy.
- **Patriar Heritage Society sponsorship of the Hlaths fails.** The Hlaths refuse the aid under Amnian pressure. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_1/sponsor-the-dispossessed-hlath-family.md).
- **Lord Hlath flees a Knights of the Shield oath.** See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_1/swear-in-lord-hlath.md).
- **Goldsmiths deliver a canary automaton to Lord Durinbold.** Quiet alliance gift between the patriar bloc and the [Worshipful Company of Goldsmiths & Jewelers](../../../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_1/create-a-masterwork-for-a-patriar.md).
- **Master goldsmith Elara Varrin survives a Takers-of-the-Tithe Reaper attempt** under hired bodyguard cover. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_1/the-reaper-strikes.md).

### The Wide

- **Hands-of-the-Absolute kidnapping foiled** by an unexpectedly resilient fortune-teller. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_1/kidnap-a-psychic.md).

### Citadel and Watch beat

- **Mocking Watch caricatures appear near the Citadel.** The Watch's [unsanctioned-art crackdown](../../../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_1/crack-down-on-unsanctioned-art.md) is quietly abandoned.
- **Sorcerous Sodality scries Lord Durinbold.** No public footprint; the result is held as leverage. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_1/scry-on-a-peerage-of-blood-member.md).

### Parliament of Peers

- **Heritage Preservation Act killed on the floor.** A generational shift signal: patriar political ceiling is now lower than it was a year ago. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_1/lobby-for-heritage-preservation-laws.md).

## Week 2

### High Hall

- **Council formally censures Marshal Flint** for refusing the Archduke's order to break the Free Trader siege. The first open break between the city's political and military leadership. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_2/contain-the-crises.md), [Western Gate — Archduke's Mandate](../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_2/archdukes-mandate.md).
- **Peerage of Coin's mercenary motion fails.** [Durinbold's Peerage of Blood](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_2/a-charity-of-spite.md) frames the bill as anti-sovereignty; Parliament gridlocks. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_2/legislate-aggression.md).
- **Patriar Heritage Society "defense of tradition" gala.** [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) further isolates the Archduke. Cover for [the Sodality scrying ritual](../../../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_2/trace-the-arcane-signature.md) running on him at the same time. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_2/empower-the-old-guard.md).

### Manorborn

- **Seven Suns Calimshan silk expedition lost at sea.** Manorborn creditors begin circling the [Jhasso family](../../../../20-Factions/seven-suns/people/jhasso-family.md). See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/seven-suns/activity/week_2/the-captive-market.md).

### Norchapel

- **Chandlers expand lamplighter coverage.** Sanctioned reason to be on Norchapel streets all night, surveillance reach included. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-chandlers-lamplighters/activity/week_2/sell-the-solution-to-fear.md).

## Week 3

### High Hall

- **No-confidence motion against Marshal Flint fails.** [Archduke Lyra Silvershield's](../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) motion ([Council](../../../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_3/the-vote-of-no-confidence.md), [Parliament](../../../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_3/vote-of-no-confidence.md)) is filibustered by [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) on sovereignty grounds. The Peerage of Coin's whip operation holds its bloc but never gets to vote. Flint stays in command. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_3/filibuster-the-mercenary-act.md), [Peerage of Coin](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_3/whip-the-votes.md).
- **Mercenary Act dies on the floor.** Same filibuster kills the proposal to publicly fund foreign mercenaries; the Archduke pivots immediately to [the private option through the Sembian Kontor](../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_3/the-private-option.md).
- **Patriar Heritage Society publicly defends the Marshal.** [Lord Durinbold](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) reframes the win as a defense of Baldurian institutions. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_3/defend-the-marshal.md).
- **Society of Baldurian Integrity chants outside.** [Calliope](../../../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/people/calliope.md) gives the patriar narrative a public soundtrack as the chamber adjourns. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/activity/week_3/preserve-the-true-voice.md).
- **Hellrider citizenship negotiations collapse.** [High Watcher Alena](../../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) refuses the Council's terms. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_3/the-negotiations-price.md).

### Manorborn

- **Vintners' "gift of gratitude" returned with a scathing note.** [Lord Durinbold](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) publicly returns the Worshipful Company's brown-ale gift; the public note circulates faster than the gift did. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_3/a-taste-of-gratitude.md).
- **Elturel Remembrance Society humiliated by a diabolist patriar.** [Lord Perrine Hhune's](../../../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/people/perrine-hhune.md) bid for infernal patronage is dismissed as fanatic amateur work; the patriar in question is independently being courted by [the Knights of the Shield](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_3/a-friend-in-the-coin.md). See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_3/seek-a-new-patron.md).

### The Wide and back lanes

- **Watch coverage thins.** With [Marshal Flint recalling Fist loyalists to Wyrm's Rock](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/activity/week_3/circle-the-wagons.md), the Watch concentrates on Manorborn and the Temples District; the Wide and the back lanes off the Citadel are dangerously underpoliced. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_3/maintain-appearances.md).

### Bloomridge Park Manor

- **Harper infiltration burned at the Archduke's household.** [Randal Evenwood](../../../../20-Factions/harpers/summary.md) attempted to turn a clerk who turned out to be a counter-intelligence trap; he barely escaped a security detail. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_3/find-a-new-inlet.md).

## Week 4

### High Hall

- **Emergency Commerce Protection Act passed.** [Consul Vorlamin Athar's](../../../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/people/vorlamin-athar.md) [Peerage of Coin](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/summary.md) majority pushes through a permanent gate-toll levy that uses the city's own commerce to fund [the Archduke's private army in Eastway](../../../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md). See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_4/the-emergency-levy.md).
- **Letter of Censure passed.** [Lord Corin Durinbold's](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) Peerage of Blood passes a formal letter demanding the Archduke's resignation; carries no legal force, every other kind. Reprinted on the front page of the *Baldur's Mouth*. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_4/the-letter-of-censure.md).
- **Joint emergency session devolves into floor brawl.** Patriars exchange blows on the High Hall floor; the Watch is called *into* the chamber for the first time in living memory. Parliament is suspended indefinitely. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_4/a-debate-of-the-deaf.md).
- **Masons' barracks-discount offer publicly returned.** [Master Borin Stonehand's](../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/people/borin-stonehand.md) discount-for-exclusivity proposal to the Archduke is read aloud and returned with a scathing note from the floor. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_4/a-political-hedge.md).

### Manorborn

- **Bhaalist ambush on a Watch patrol fails.** Bhaalist assassins attempt a sacrificial ambush; the patrol's veteran reacts faster than expected; the cell flees, leaving a distinctive ritual dagger behind — the second such dagger recovered in two weeks. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_4/an-offering-in-the-dark.md).
- **String of high-profile burglaries.** Targeting Peerage-of-Coin patriars: stolen valuables, but more notably *sensitive correspondence* (Coin-bench whip lists, [Levy](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_4/the-emergency-levy.md) ledger drafts, debt schedules). The Watch is too thin to investigate. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_4/the-manorborn-burglaries.md).
- **Jannath Estate "infernal heist" wiped out.** [Lord Perrine Hhune's](../../../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/people/perrine-hhune.md) amateur followers are caught in the wine cellar by professional Jannath guards; bound, sworn statements taken, turned over to the Watch. Perrine flees. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_4/the-infernal-heist.md).

### Bloomridge

- **Mercer mourning-silk prices triple.** [Mistress Elethra Vance](../../../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/people/elethra-vance.md) reads the rising body-count and political grief and triples the price of black silk and velvet overnight; patriars pay; her atelier banks its largest week of the campaign. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_4/the-business-of-grief.md).

### The Helm and Cloak

- **Sharran first-tithe instruction left for Bror.** [Mother Nocturne](../../../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md) instructs Bror — through a junior Sharran cell — to take the Whispering Stone to the Helm and Cloak and listen for political weakness. Bror is in the Feywild. The instruction sits unread at the Plowman. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_4/the-first-tithe.md).

## Week 5

### High Hall

- **The Archduke's three-day ultimatum.** [Lyra Silvershield](../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) [issues a formal, public ultimatum](../../../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md) from the High Hall steps: dismantle the Basilisk Gate blockade in three days, or have it dismantled by force. **The first formal Archducal declaration of force against a Baldurian assembly in living memory.**
- **Parliament fails to reach quorum.** Moderate peers' attempt to call an emergency session collapses for lack of attendance; Parliament remains paralyzed. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_5/the-failed-session.md).

### Counting House

- See [Counting House week-5 entries in `lower-city/events.md`](../lower-city/events.md): Peerage of Coin Emergency Tax dies in the workshops; Goldsmiths abandon the party-bounty search; Zhentarim infiltrate the ledgers.

### Manorborn

- **Knights of the Shield approach a recently-burgled Peerage-of-Coin patriar.** [The Hidden Lord](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/people/the-recruiter.md) reads the Manorborn burglary wave for vulnerability and pitches infernal protection in exchange for loyalty and secrets. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_5/a-desperate-bargain.md).
- **Watch patrols collapse in the outer Upper City.** [Marshal Flint mocks the Watch Captain's plea for reinforcements](../../../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_5/the-plea-for-reinforcements.md); the Watch is forced to abandon patrols in outer districts; several patriar estates are now exposed.
- **Smiths petition Lord Durinbold from Durinbold Manor.** [Master Tor-keg Silverhand](../../../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/people/tor-keg-silverhand.md) begs the patriar for a special exemption to bypass the Basilisk Gate blockade for raw-material caravans. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_5/an-appeal-to-the-old-guard.md).
- **Masons' alliance overture publicly rebuffed.** [Master Borin Stonehand's](../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/people/borin-stonehand.md) offer is dismissed by Lord Durinbold as "new money"; humiliation drives a wedge between the powerful guild and the Peerage of Blood. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_5/an-alliance-of-stone-and-blood.md).

### The Helm and Cloak

- **Sharran sight-line on Bror is lost.** [Mother Nocturne's](../../../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md) cell is blinded by a sudden Helmite-patrol surge; unable to corroborate Bror's movements, the Sharrans cannot verify the Whispering Stone's data; paranoia rises in the cell. See [activity](../../../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_5/monitor-the-tithe.md).

## Week 6

### Upper City Gates

- **The Watch locks the interior gates.** No entry without a resident's pass; trade dries up overnight; Bloomridge runs out of fresh bread; the Upper City becomes a **gilded cage**. See [Watch activity](../../../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_6/the-gate-lockdown.md).

### High Hall

- **Petition of Grievances filed at the steps.** [Lord Corin Durinbold's](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) Peerage of Blood formally demands expulsion of the Sembian "foreign mercenary army" under pre-Crisis statutes; the petition is theatre but creates a paper trail for legal grounds for civic resistance later. See [Peerage of Blood activity](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_6/the-petition-of-grievances.md).
- **Masons' labor strike.** [Master Borin Stonehand](../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/people/borin-stonehand.md) halts all guild reinforcement work on Council buildings citing "unsafe conditions" — actually retaliation for Durinbold's "new-money trowels" insult. Durinbold's emissary is at the guildhall offering a deal inside four days. See [Masons activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_6/the-labor-strike.md).

### Manorborn

- **The Officers' Mess.** [Lord Durinbold](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) hosts the Fist sergeants who took his Silver Fist coin at his manor; explicitly buys their willingness to look the other way. **The Fist's chain of command has a private branch.** See [Heritage Society activity](../../../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_6/the-officers-mess.md).
- **The Infernal Ledger called due.** [The Hidden Lord](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/people/the-recruiter.md) collects on [last week's pact](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_5/a-desperate-bargain.md) — the manorborn noble surrenders the family's private journals on the Peerage of Blood voting history; the Knights swing a tariff vote against Durinbold. See [Knights activity](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_6/the-infernal-ledger.md).

### The Wide

- **The Society's mud-bombs on the Splendid Yard statues.** [Calliope's](../../../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/people/calliope.md) nightlings reach the Upper City through Heapside tunnels (the Watch lockdown does not work) and coat the Waterdhavian Kontor allegorical statues in distinctively Baldurian sluiceway mud. Pamphlets scattered: *"Erasure of Local Culture — A Reply in Mud."* See [Society activity](../../../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/activity/week_6/the-art-vandal.md).
- **Diplomatic Immunity invoked on a Harper agent.** [Factor Mornwood](../../../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/people/elara-mornwood.md) extracts a cornered Harper from a Fist patrol by claiming Lords' Alliance "cultural attaché" status; the High Hall files a formal complaint. **Waterdeep has chosen.** See [Waterdhavian activity](../../../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_6/the-diplomatic-immunity.md).

### Upper City Commercial District

- **The Marked Doors.** [Lord Hhune's](../../../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/people/perrine-hhune.md) Elturel Remembrance Society agents paint invisible Zariel sigils in alchemical ink on Upper City "Hell-gold" businesses — visible only to *detect magic*, a target list for later sabotage. See [Elturel Remembrance activity](../../../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_6/the-marked-doors.md).

### Cartographers' Guildhall

- **The Memory Loss.** [Master Vancroft's](../../../../20-Factions/wc-cartographers-surveyors/people/elias-vancroft.md) hired *Greater Restoration* on the K'liir-mind-wiped surveyor fails — the wipe is psionic, not magical; the 1,500-gp diamond crumbles to dust on the altar; the surveyor remembers nothing. See [Cartographers activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_6/the-memory-loss.md).

### Hellscream Bathhouse

- **The Private Caucus collapses.** Eight moderate cross-aisle peers attempt a secret de-escalation meeting; trust is so low the meeting devolves into a drawn-knife brawl by midnight. **The moderates cannot drink together.** See [Parliament activity](../../../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_6/the-private-caucus.md). *(Note: meeting held in Lower City; the political fallout lands here.)*

## Week 7

### Upper City Gates

- **The Gilded Cage Riots.** Trapped patriars riot against the Watch itself, demanding to be let out before Lower City mobs (which have not yet materialized) break in. The Watch loses teeth at the Wide. The Watch's request for emergency Council authorization to lift the lockdown goes unanswered. See [Watch activity](../../../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_7/the-gilded-cage-riots.md).

### Manorborn

- **The Gilded Extortion.** [The Hidden Lord](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/people/the-hidden-lord.md) bills House Defense Subscriptions to twelve patriar bloodlines under forged Watch contracts; settlement in soul-coins. The Knights of the Shield are paid by Manorborn houses to provide riot suppression — the Watch's last monopoly inside the gates collapses. See [Knights activity](../../../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_7/the-gilded-extortion.md).
- **The Sabotaged Foundations.** [Master Borin Stonehand](../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/people/borin-stonehand.md) escalates last week's strike into active sabotage: his crews quietly remove key structural supports from three Upper City guardhouses on Manorborn's perimeter and leave the structures *visibly fine*. Only the Masons know which three buildings will fall first. See [Masons activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_7/the-sabotaged-foundations.md).

### High Hall

- **The Ultimatum to the Duke.** The Peerage of Blood files a formal ultimatum demanding [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) step down. The vote to forward to the Council fails by four — would have passed had [the Bathhouse Brawl](../../../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_7/the-bathhouse-brawl.md) not poisoned the cross-aisle Coin moderates. **First formal succession demand since the post-Crisis charter.** See [Peerage of Blood activity](../../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_7/the-ultimatum-to-the-duke.md).

### The Wide

- **The Diplomatic Immunity.** [Factor Elara Mornwood](../../../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/people/elara-mornwood.md) opens formal Lords' Alliance negotiations with [High Watcher Alena](../../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md): grain shipments, antitoxins, and a weapons-import exemption in exchange for public Hellrider opposition to Sembian influence. Sembian protest goes unanswered. **Waterdeep is bidding for soft-power dominance of New Elturel.** See [Waterdhavian activity](../../../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_7/the-diplomatic-immunity.md).

### Upper City Commercial District

- **The Alchemical Arson.** [Lord Hhune's](../../../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/people/perrine-hhune.md) Remembrance Society agents ignite alchemical sigils on six storefronts believed funded with Knights-of-the-Shield infernal gold. Two go up — a Mercer milliner and a Goldsmith subsidiary's loan office. See [Elturel Remembrance activity](../../../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_7/the-alchemical-arson.md).

### Helm and Cloak

- **The Sharran Infiltration.** [Mother Nocturne](../../../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/people/mother-nocturne.md) finishes her Purge of the Blind and deploys elite Sharran spies into the Helm and Cloak. By week's end she holds blackmail material on five mid-tier patriars, two of them signatories on the Knights of the Shield's contracts. *She is waiting for [Bror to deliver the sentient source](../../../../50-Sessions/session_27/player_summary.md) before she plays the cards.* See [Silent Shroud activity](../../../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_7/the-helm-and-cloak-infiltration.md).

### Cartographers' Guildhall

- **The Memory Recovery.** [Master Vancroft](../../../../20-Factions/wc-cartographers-surveyors/people/elias-vancroft.md) hires a Sodality psion who successfully recovers the Githyanki map data wiped by the K'liir psionic sweep — a partial astral-bridge map under Wyrm's Rock and a Sembian smuggling-route fragment Vancroft sells to Korgan Ironhand within hours. The Cartographers are back in the espionage game. See [Cartographers activity](../../../../20-Factions/wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_7/the-memory-recovery.md).

### Hellscream Bathhouse

- **The Bathhouse Brawl.** A discreet meeting between moderate peers of Coin and Blood collapses into a physical brawl after Durinbold calls a Coin moderate a "ledger-clerk in patriar collars." Two Knights-aligned junior peers wade in; the Watch detail extracts a half-dressed Coin lord through the steam-room. **Parliament has no functioning back-channel between the houses left.** See [Parliament activity](../../../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_7/the-bathhouse-brawl.md).
