# The Elfsong Tavern

A tall, narrow common-house just up from the docks, named for the wordless elven lament that drifts through its rafters at unpredictable hours — never the same key, never quite the same melody. Drinkers fall silent when it starts; veterans set down their cups.

The Elfsong is one of the few rooms in the [Lower City](README.md) where Flaming Fist sergeants, Worshipful Company wardens, and Guild fixers will share a table without overt incident. That makes it the city's premier back-channel — and the obvious place to pass an envelope or a name.

## Notable in this house

- **The Fist corner.** Off-duty [Flaming Fist](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/summary.md) officers and manips drink here by long tradition. The [Patriar Heritage Society](../../../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/summary.md)'s **Silver Fist Fund** is distributed at this very table — silver "bonuses" passed under the cover of after-hours drinking.
- **The third-party fence.** A regular who keeps a permanent slate behind the bar handles anonymous bounties for parties who would rather not be named — most recently a [Lantaner Concession](../../../../20-Factions/lantaner-concession/summary.md) bounty for "lost clockwork mechanisms."
- **The till-book.** A plain leather ledger kept by the proprietor; the names written into it under the Fund's column are the proof every reformer in the city would like to see.

## Who you find here

Off-duty Fist, dock-front ward bosses, [Wharf Rats](../../../../20-Factions/wharf-rats/summary.md) lieutenants pretending not to know each other, harbour-side mercantile factors, occasional [Harper](../../../../20-Factions/harpers/summary.md) couriers, and the [Society of Baldurian Integrity](../../../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/summary.md), who consider the singing ghost a foreign infestation and have been planning a "tuning fork" exorcism for some time.

## Etiquette and access

Stand when the lament starts; do not attempt to sing along. No knives drawn at table — the bouncers carry coshes and remember every face. Pay your tab in coin, not promises.

## Pressures

A live conduit for the Silver Fist bribery scheme; a rallying point for the Heritage Society's planned exorcism stunt; and a known fence-corner that whichever Worshipful Company has had cargo "go missing" most recently is watching with quiet, patient interest.
