# The Elfsong

A Lower City tavern. The campaign-relevant role of the Elfsong is as a **payment node** in the Patriar Heritage Society's covert program of buying the Flaming Fist's loyalty.

## What goes through it

- **The Silver Fist Fund payout.** A massive purse collected from Heritage Society families is *quietly distributed as silver "bonuses" to Fist sergeants and manips at the Elfsong*, keeping rank-and-file loyalty with the patriar order rather than the chain of command.
- **The paymaster paper trail.** Runs through Durinbold's house steward; the Elfsong's till-book is the secondary record. Any party that pulls the steward, or the Elfsong's till-book, gets the names.

## Other appearances

- One-Eyed Jack of the Wharf Rats has worked an angle here; the Lantaner crate "accidentally dropped" during unloading was the cover.
