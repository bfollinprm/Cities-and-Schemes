# Character

Stand on the lower Chionthar's bank near the city and the river is brown-green, opaque, and *iridescent* in the wrong places — slicks of industrial oil from leaking barges. The smell carries up the bank for a hundred yards: tannery effluent, blood from the slaughterhouses, the sweet rot of dead fish. The water is warm even in spring.

## On the river

Consortium barges run constantly, low in the water, badly maintained, leaving streaks of fuel behind them. Crews are surly; they work for shares of cargo and keep stop-times short. Smaller independent boats hug the banks to avoid being shouldered aside.

After dark, traffic continues — but the night barges are mostly Consortium dumps and the Guild's River-Rat skiffs. The dossier reports the Consortium has been accused of dumping industrial waste directly into the river under cover of darkness. It does not require accusation; the morning film tells the story.

## On the banks

The fishing villages along the lower banks are mostly gone. A row of shuttered houses, abandoned weirs, rotted boats hauled up. The villagers are in the city now — in slums, factories, dockwork, or the New Elturel refugee camps.

What remains on the banks: Consortium warehouses, transfer-docks, and Guild-leased "shipping" sheds that everyone knows are smuggling hubs. A few diehard fishers still try, but the catch is poor and what they pull up is increasingly inedible.

## Upstream

The further from the city, the cleaner the river — but the dossier is firm that the damage is visible all the way up. The salmon that once spawned in the upper reaches are nearly gone. The riverbank vegetation runs sickly brown. Birds are sparse. The ecosystem is rebalancing toward whatever can tolerate the load.

## Useful to whom

- **River-Rats** know the hidden backwaters; for the right price, they will move people or cargo around tolls and checkpoints.
- **Druidic emissaries** are easy to find on the lower banks — they're the ones holding small protest gatherings outside Consortium warehouses, and being ignored.
- **Industrial agents** — mill investors, foundry buyers — are everywhere, walking the bank with notebooks.

## Avoid

- Drinking the water within a day's pull of the city.
- Falling in.
- Standing between a Consortium barge crew and a casting-off post.
