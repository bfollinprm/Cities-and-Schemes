# Friendly Arm Inn

A heavily fortified inn on the [Coast Way](../coast-way/README.md), the major safe stop on the local stretch of road north out of Baldur's Gate. Long-known as a [Harper](../../../../../20-Factions/harpers/summary.md) safehouse — the dossier notes it explicitly as one of the few remaining.

## What's here

- [character.md](character.md) — what visitors see, hear, and (don't) get told.

## Why it matters

In the Coast Way's chain of Zhentarim-leaning toll-forts and shaky independent inns, the Friendly Arm is one of the few places a traveler can sleep behind real walls without paying tribute to a local proxy. The Harpers don't run the inn openly, but the inn's existence and continued resistance to Zhentarim pressure depend on Harper protection and (per the dossier) Harper exfiltration work conducted through it.

The dossier is clear that the Harpers are *outmatched* on the Coast Way and have shifted from confrontation to intelligence and refugee aid. The Friendly Arm is the operational hub for that.

## Players to expect here

- [Harpers](../../../../../20-Factions/harpers/summary.md) — one or two on station at any time, in any role. Most never identify themselves.
- [Western Gate](../../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) and [Chionthar Consortium](../../../../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) caravan crews — overnighting, paying premium rates for the fortifications.
- [Zhentarim](../../../../../20-Factions/zhentarim/summary.md) — they do not run the inn but they *watch* it. Expect a "merchant" or two whose paperwork is too clean.
- Refugees in transit — quietly being moved by Harper handlers.
