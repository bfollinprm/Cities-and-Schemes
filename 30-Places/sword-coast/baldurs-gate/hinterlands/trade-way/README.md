# Trade Way (local stretch)

The road that runs south from Baldur's Gate to the kingdom of Amn. This page is the stretch within the hinterland — from the city to roughly the approach to [Beregost](../../../beregost/README.md).

## What's here

- [character.md](character.md) — feel of the road.

## Why it matters

The Trade Way is **contested** rather than controlled. The dossier breaks it into three zones; only the northernmost is fully in the hinterland:

- **North (BG → Beregost):** [Chionthar Consortium](../../../../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) territory. Their fortified river trading posts undercut the road by capturing trade onto the river; their agents are common in roadside inns.
- **Middle (Beregost → Elturel):** [Hellrider](../../../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) checkpoints. Tenuous control; bandits and refugees both numerous.
- **South (Elturel → Nashkel/Amn):** [Guild](../../../../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) River-Rats and smugglers; haven for outlaws.

In the local stretch, the dominant pressure is **Consortium economic squeeze, not Zhentarim toll**. Where the Coast Way is extorted, the Trade Way is *outcompeted*.

## Players on the road

- [Chionthar Consortium](../../../../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) — sharp-dealing, ruthless, well-coined; agents in every roadside inn.
- [Western Gate Trading Company](../../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) — runs caravans south with logging-camp returns and city goods.
- Bandits and refugees — increasingly indistinguishable, especially closer to Elturel.
