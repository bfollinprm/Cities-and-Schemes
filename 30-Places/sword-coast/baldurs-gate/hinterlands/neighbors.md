# Neighbors

Hinterlands proper is the ring of land within roughly a few days' travel of Baldur's Gate. Beyond that ring sit the settlements and regions below.

## North (Coast Way, toward Waterdeep)

- [Friendly Arm Inn](friendly-arm-inn/README.md) — the first major fortified stop out of the city; on the [Coast Way](coast-way/README.md). [Harper](../../../../20-Factions/harpers/summary.md) safehouse.
- Beyond: the wider Sword Coast north, eventually Waterdeep. Not yet ingested.

## South (Trade Way, toward Amn)

- [Beregost](../../beregost/README.md) — declining Trade Way town a few days south.
- [Elturel](../../elturel/README.md) — city returned from the Hells; Hellrider checkpoints on the road in.
- [Fort Morninglord](../../fort-morninglord/README.md) — abandoned Elturgardian fort, ghost stories.
- [Nashkel](../../nashkel/README.md) — border town with Amn; Trade Way southernmost stop in the campaign's scope.

## East (Trade Way + Wood)

- [Wood of Sharp Teeth](wood-of-sharp-teeth/README.md) — the local edge; macro-region view at [../../wood-of-sharp-teeth.md](../../wood-of-sharp-teeth.md).
- [Boareskyr Bridge](../../boareskyr-bridge/README.md) — far north on the Trade Way at the River Winding Water; Elturgardian paladin remnant.
- [Berdusk](../../berdusk/README.md) — upper Chionthar city in the Western Heartlands; Harpers vs. Zhentarim.

## South-southeast (along Cloakwood)

- [Cloakwood](cloakwood/README.md) — local face; macro-region view at [../../cloakwood.md](../../cloakwood.md).

## West (Sea of Swords)

- [Candlekeep](../../candlekeep/README.md) — fortress-library on a coastal promontory south of the city.
- The [Sea of Swords](../../../../10-Setting/regions/sea-of-swords.md) itself.

## Through the middle

- [River Chionthar (lower)](river-chionthar-lower/README.md) — the polluted local stretch from the city upstream into the hinterland.
- [Coast Way](coast-way/README.md) — local stretch.
- [Trade Way](trade-way/README.md) — local stretch.

## In the city (parent)

- [Baldur's Gate](../README.md) — city proper; districts and internal factions are owned by issue #19's ingest.
