# Coast Way (local stretch)

The road that runs north from Baldur's Gate along the coast toward Waterdeep. This page is the stretch within the hinterland — roughly from the city walls to the [Friendly Arm Inn](../friendly-arm-inn/README.md) and a little beyond.

## What's here

- [character.md](character.md) — what travel on the road is like now.

## Why it matters

The Coast Way is the dossier's headline example of the **fraying edge of law**. The [Flaming Fist](../../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/summary.md) cannot reach it; the [Zhentarim](../../../../../20-Factions/zhentarim/summary.md), through a network of local proxies, effectively control it via a chain of toll-forts. Tolls are arbitrary, "protection" is theatre, and merchants who refuse find their cargo "inspected" or their passage delayed by sudden "road repairs."

The largest consortiums adapt: heavily armed convoys, mercenaries, costs passed to the consumer. The smaller merchants are crushed, and many slide into smuggling or banditry themselves.

## Players on the road

- [Zhentarim](../../../../../20-Factions/zhentarim/summary.md) — patrons of the toll-forts; usually invisible.
- Local proxies — the actual toll-masters; armed by the Zhentarim, kept on a share-of-the-take.
- [Western Gate Trading Company](../../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) — runs convoys with mercenary escorts; pays tolls without enthusiasm.
- [Free Traders of the Outskirts](../../../../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) — the small operators most squeezed; the dossier identifies them as the chief casualties.
- [Harpers](../../../../../20-Factions/harpers/summary.md) — operating from the [Friendly Arm Inn](../friendly-arm-inn/README.md) and a few other safehouses; gather intelligence, exfiltrate refugees and dissidents, decline open confrontation.
