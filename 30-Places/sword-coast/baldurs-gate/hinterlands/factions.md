# Factions in the Hinterlands

Hinterlands prose is owned by the dossier's local features and by `20-Factions/` summaries. This page is a cross-link table.

## Consortiums and merchants

| Faction | Role in the hinterlands |
| --- | --- |
| [Western Gate Trading Company](../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) | Logging in the [Cloakwood](cloakwood/README.md) and clear-cuts in the [Whisperwood Copse](wood-of-sharp-teeth/README.md); convoyed caravans on both roads. |
| [Chionthar Consortium](../../../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) | Fortified trading posts on the river; squeezes the Trade Way from Baldur's Gate to Beregost. |
| [Free Traders of the Outskirts](../../../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) | Independent caravan masters — the small operators most exposed to road tolls and pollution. |

## Order, law, and force

| Faction | Role in the hinterlands |
| --- | --- |
| [Flaming Fist](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/summary.md) | Authority effectively ends at [Wyrm's Crossing](../outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md); tolls there are the last point of city writ. |
| [Hellriders of New Elturel](../../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) | Thin checkpoints on the Trade Way near [Elturel](../../elturel/README.md); refugee militia rather than open-road force. |
| [Watch](../../../../20-Factions/watch/summary.md) | Recently extended onto Western Gate logging routes — controversial overreach, mostly an [Upper City](../upper-city/README.md) body. |

## Underworld and pressure

| Faction | Role in the hinterlands |
| --- | --- |
| [Zhentarim](../../../../20-Factions/zhentarim/summary.md) | Coast Way toll-forts via local proxies; "protection" extortion; light retaliatory patrols. |
| [Guild](../../../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) (River-Rats) | Smuggling on Chionthar backwaters; bypasses Consortium tolls and Hellrider checkpoints. |
| [Harpers](../../../../20-Factions/harpers/summary.md) | Scattered safehouses (notably [Friendly Arm Inn](friendly-arm-inn/README.md)); refugee exfiltration; intelligence over force. |

## New movements (informal, no faction folder yet)

These the dossier names but they are too inchoate to model as `20-Factions/`. Capture as color in the relevant settlement file unless they pick up activity:

- **Peasant Cooperatives** — small rural anti-monopoly mutuals.
- **Confederations of the Wilds** — humanoid tribes in loose mutual-defense pacts against encroachment.
- **Cults of the Damned** — despair-cults rising in the wake of the Absolute Crisis (e.g. the alleged "cult of Zariel" in [Elturel](../../elturel/README.md)).
