# Factions in the Cloakwood

| Faction | Role |
| --- | --- |
| [Western Gate Trading Company](../../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) | Logging-camp operator, mercenary employer, bounty-poster on druids and fey. The aggressor. |
| [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../../../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md) | The ancient guardian-circle. Open war: sabotage, ambush, fey alliance. |
| Emerald Enclave | Moderate druidic body trying to broker peace — without success. Not a `20-Factions/` entry; capture in setting if agency emerges. |
| [Sembian Kontor](../../../../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/summary.md) | Brokers the mercenaries the Western Gate hires for the saw-roads. Indirect but profitable. |
| [Watch](../../../../../20-Factions/watch/summary.md) | Recently authorized to patrol the new Western Gate logging routes — controversial extension of city writ. |
| [Free Traders of the Outskirts](../../../../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/summary.md) | Some independent timber haulers run the saw-roads at lower margin and higher risk than the Western Gate convoys. |
| Fey of the Cloakwood | Now combatants on the Circle's side, per the dossier; not a `20-Factions/` entry. |
