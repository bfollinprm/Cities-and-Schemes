# Cloakwood (local face)

The vast forest south of Baldur's Gate. The macro-region view (extent, history, fey ecology) lives at [../../../cloakwood.md](../../../cloakwood.md). This page is what the forest is now, from inside the hinterland.

## What's here

- [character.md](character.md) — felt experience: a logging frontier turned shooting war.
- [factions.md](factions.md) — who acts in the woods today.

## Why it matters now

Reconstruction's appetite for timber drove the [Western Gate Trading Company](../../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) to push logging operations far deeper into the Cloakwood than the old quiet agreements ever allowed. The [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../../../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md) responded with sabotage and ambush. The Western Gate countered with mercenaries, armed work crews, and bounties on druids and fey heads. The Emerald Enclave's emissaries have so far failed to broker peace.

The result on the ground is not a logging camp with occasional incidents. It is a continuous ambush corridor along the Western Gate's saw-roads, with reprisal raids in both directions and a steady drip of company patrols who don't come back.
