# Whisperwood Copse

A small, ancient stand of weirwood and ash a half-day's ride east of the city, sacred to the local druids long before the Trade Way was paved. Until very recently it was a quiet grove with a weeping spring at its centre. Then the [Western Gate Trading Company](../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/summary.md)'s loggers arrived with axes and a Sembian-mercenary cordon.

The clear-cut of the Whisperwood Copse is now the flashpoint of an open shooting war between the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md) and the WGTC. Elder [Elion](../../../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/people/elder-elion.md) personally led the night-raid that responded to the felling; the circle was ambushed and driven off. Both sides are now waiting for the next move.

## Notable on the ground

- **The cleared centre.** A muddy half-acre of stumps and slash; the spring at the heart of the grove has gone silent. The druids hold this is meaningful.
- **The Sembian cordon.** A ring of canvas-tents and watch-fires garrisoned by mercenaries hired through a Sembian intermediary; rotation every nine days.
- **The ambush ground.** A wedge of standing trees on the north side where the Circle's first counter-raid was broken; the bodies were carried out, but the bloodied earth is still visible.

## Who you find here

WGTC foremen, Sembian mercenaries on rotation, the occasional foolish woodcutter trying to claim deadfall, druids of the Circle observing from a distance, and (once) a Harper courier sent to confirm what the Circle has been claiming.

## Etiquette and access

Approach openly along the road or be shot. The mercenaries treat anyone in robes as a hostile by default. The Circle treats anyone in WGTC livery as a corpse to be made.

## Pressures

A vendetta the Circle is committed to even after one defeat; a WGTC clear-cut schedule that has at least three more groves on the list; and a rumour, circulating in [Berdusk](../../berdusk/), that the Sembian intermediary is in fact the [Sembian Kontor](../../../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/summary.md) operating under a thin cut-out.
