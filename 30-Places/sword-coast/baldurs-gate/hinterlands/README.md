# Hinterlands of Baldur's Gate

The lands beyond the city walls — close enough that Baldurian commerce and conflict shape them daily, far enough that the [Flaming Fist](../../../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/summary.md)'s writ does not run. Source: in-world dossier by Ser Arman de Vries (1502 DR) commissioned by the [Western Gate Trading Company](../../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/summary.md); see `.context/converted/the-hinterlands-of-baldurs-gate-a-dossier.md`.

The dossier's framing is a single in-world voice — useful for color and faction motivation, not infallible.

## Three forces

The dossier calls out three forces shaping every part of the region. They are also the lens for everything in this folder:

1. **Post-boom frontier** — the city's appetite for timber, stone, and grain pulls capital, labor, and violence outward.
2. **Scarred land** — the psychic and magical residue of the Absolute Crisis still warps the wilderness, most visibly in the Wood of Sharp Teeth.
3. **Fraying edge of law** — the Flaming Fist barely reaches Wyrm's Crossing; outside it, jurisdiction is whoever shows up with steel.
