# Elturel

City on the Trade Way south of Baldur's Gate. Returned to the Material Plane after Zariel's archdevil pact dragged it briefly into the Nine Hells. The Companion (the city's artificial sun) still shines but is "a pale and sickly imitation of its former glory."

For the in-Baldur's-Gate refugee district built by survivors, see `30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/.../new-elturel/` (owned by the Baldur's Gate dossier ingest, issue #19, when it lands). For the original city itself — what remains and what's being rebuilt — this folder.

## What's here

- [character.md](character.md) — physical state, mood, and the cult problem.

## Why it matters

Elturel is the source of:

- The refugee crisis straining Baldur's Gate (the [Hellriders of New Elturel](../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md), the [Followers of the Phoenix](../../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md), the [Elturel Remembrance Society](../../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/summary.md), and the [Plague of Remembrance](../../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) all originate or recruit here).
- The psychic shockwave that warped the Wood of Sharp Teeth.
- The dossier's named "cult of Zariel" — survivors who believe Zariel was the city's true savior, recruiting from the dispossessed and actively undermining reconstruction. Note: this cult is the dossier author's framing and may or may not map cleanly to the campaign's existing Followers/Society/Plague factions; treat as in-world claim rather than canonical faction.

## Players in Elturel

- [Hellriders of New Elturel](../../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/summary.md) — the city's surviving paladin force; depleted, demoralized, struggling to maintain order.
- "Cult of Zariel" — per dossier; spreads despair and nihilism, recruits from the dispossessed.
- Refugee returnees — mostly Elturians who fled to Baldur's Gate and have come back to attempt rebuilding.
- Reconstruction labor — scarce, expensive, and politically contested.
