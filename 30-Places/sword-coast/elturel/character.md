# Character

Approach Elturel and you see the Companion first — the city's artificial sun, still hanging, but thin and washed-out where it used to be brilliant. The light it casts is enough to read by but not enough to feel like daylight. People who lived here before describe the difference as *like watching a fire go out from underneath a kettle*.

## The ruins

The city itself is rubble and dust. Great buildings shattered, streets choked with debris, towers reduced to stumps. The dossier describes the air as "thick with the dust of a thousand fallen towers." Reconstruction has cleared a few central streets and patched together a working quarter around the main temple compound; everything else is partially habitable at best.

## The people

Survivors who have stayed are *scarred*. Most lost family in the descent or the fight in the Hells; many have direct memory of infernal abuse. The pattern the dossier observes: a struggle to find new purpose in a world that has been "turned upside down." Some channel it into reconstruction work. Some channel it into faith — Lathander, Ilmater, the Hellriders' fading tradition. Some are recruited into the cult.

## The Hellriders

Decimated, demoralized, faith shaken. The dossier is direct: the order is "a shadow of its former selves." The Hellriders left in Elturel proper struggle to maintain order in a city teetering on chaos and to manage the slow trickle of refugees coming back from Baldur's Gate. Their counterparts in New Elturel (in the Baldur's Gate Outer City) are organized politically but militarily thin.

## The cult problem

The dossier names the cult of Zariel as the most insidious local threat. They believe Zariel was Elturel's true savior; they preach despair and nihilism; they recruit from the dispossessed and the desperate. They actively sabotage reconstruction efforts.

The campaign's existing 20-Factions roster has no folder explicitly titled "cult of Zariel." The closest matches are the [Plague of Remembrance](../../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/summary.md) (death-cult, Lady Vhage), the [Elturel Remembrance Society](../../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/summary.md) (radicalized survivors marking "infernal" merchants), and the [Followers of the Phoenix](../../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) (Karlach-worshippers, *opposed* to nihilism). Ser Arman is writing from outside; his "cult of Zariel" likely conflates several of these.

## Useful to whom

- **Recruiters** for any of the Elturian-derived factions — most leadership chains pass through the city eventually.
- **Salvagers** with the right contacts — there is wealth in the rubble, mostly tied up in disputed clerical claims.
- **Investigators** of the cult — leads almost always start in Elturel proper, not in New Elturel.

## Avoid

- Any "Companion-restoration" pitch from a stranger. They are usually cult recruiters.
- Open Hellrider-livery in cult-suspected districts.
