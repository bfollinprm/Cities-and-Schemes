# Luskan

Northern Sword Coast port. Notorious for piracy and lawlessness; not a member of the Lords' Alliance.

## Why the campaign cares

**Jarlaxle Baenre's** ambition. Bregan D'aerthe, fronting in Waterdeep as the **Sea Maidens Faire**, is engaged in a long con to recover the Vault of Dragons gold and use it to *buy Luskan a Lords' Alliance seat*. A legitimate Luskan would change the political balance of the entire Sword Coast — and Jarlaxle alone would have engineered it.

The dossier treats Luskan as Waterdeep's most dangerous near-neighbor: too close, too lawless, and now in play.
