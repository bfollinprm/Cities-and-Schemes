# Character

The hill carrying Fort Morninglord is bald and exposed; the wind off the surrounding plain is constant and loud. The fort itself is a stone keep with a curtain wall, both intact but weathered. Birds have nested in the towers. The gate hangs slightly off its hinges.

## Inside

The barracks are empty. The mess is empty. The chapel is *swept* — not maintained, but kept clear of debris in a way that suggests someone, recently, did some basic upkeep. The armory is stripped. The keep's upper rooms are untouched and dust-thick.

The parade ground at the center of the courtyard is bare beaten earth. Nothing grows on it. Locals will tell you that nothing has grown there since the abandonment.

## What people have seen

- An eerie light from the tower-tops on moonless nights — described variously as torchlight that doesn't flicker, a pale sun-like glow, or a single small star at the height of a battlement.
- A sound of marching feet on the parade ground at dusk and dawn — heard from outside the walls, almost never from inside, never with any visible source.
- A figure on the battlements: human-sized, in plate, walking the wall as on regular patrol. The figure does not speak and does not engage. People who have called out report no response and no acknowledgment.

The dossier collects these without endorsing any specific explanation. Possibilities range from "a Hellrider ghost still standing watch" to "a survivor in residence who plays up the haunting to keep visitors away" to "residual divine resonance from the Companion's binding, distorted by distance."

## What adventurers have found

A handful of treasure-hunting parties have come back. Most report:

- The keep is genuinely empty of moveable wealth.
- The lower levels (basements, possible cellars) are *sealed* — masonry-blocked, not lockable doors. Whether this is original or post-abandonment is unclear.
- A few have reported *being asked, calmly, by a voice they can't locate, to leave* — and have done so.
- One party did not come back; the others have no interest in going looking.

## Useful to whom

- **Anyone with an Elturgardian thread** — the fort is plausibly the site of the order's last unfinished obligation.
- **Mystery-hunters** willing to spend time without immediate payoff.
- **Rumor-traders** — the fort generates fresh stories every season; some are useful intelligence.

## Avoid

- Camping inside the walls without invitation from whatever-it-is.
- Trying to break into the sealed lower levels with explosives. Multiple parties have. None have benefited.
