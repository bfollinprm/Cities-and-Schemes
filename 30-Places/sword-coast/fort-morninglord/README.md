# Fort Morninglord

Abandoned fort on a hill overlooking the Trade Way. Once a powerful Elturgardian outpost, the fort was emptied of its garrison when the soldiers were recalled to defend Elturel during the city's descent. They did not come back.

## What's here

- [character.md](character.md) — what locals say, what's been seen, what adventurers have found.

## Why it matters

The fort is a *ghost on the frontier* — both literally (in rumor) and figuratively (as a symbol of the old Elturgard). It is a hook waiting for someone to pull on it:

- For Elturgardian survivors, the fort is sacred ground or unfinished business.
- For adventurers, the fort is rumored to hold a hidden treasure somewhere within its walls.
- For locals, the fort is a place of bad luck — they avoid it.

Ser Arman is firm that the fort is *not entirely empty*. Reports recur of:

- A strange eerie light in the towers on moonless nights.
- A ghostly army marching the parade ground at audible-but-invisible volume.
- A mysterious figure walking the battlements — said to be the fort's last and only guardian.

Whether any of this is real, residual magic, cult activity using the fort's reputation as cover, or something else, the dossier doesn't claim to know.

## Players around the fort

- Local farmers — keep their distance, will not guide travelers in.
- Adventurers and treasure hunters — the only people who *do* approach.
- Possibly: Hellrider survivors — the dossier doesn't say so, but a remnant guardian fits the order's psychology.
- Possibly: cultists using the haunted reputation as cover — the dossier doesn't accuse, but the geography (Trade Way, Elturel-adjacent) makes it plausible.
