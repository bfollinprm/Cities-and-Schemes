# Nashkel

Town on the border between the Sword Coast and the kingdom of Amn — the southernmost stop on the Trade Way within the campaign's scope. Long a town of divided loyalties, now living on the knife's edge.

## What's here

- [character.md](character.md) — feel of the town and the mayor's balancing act.

## Why it matters

Two larger powers are pressing on Nashkel from opposite directions:

- **From the north:** the [Chionthar Consortium](../../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md)'s monopolistic practices have squeezed Nashkel's merchants out of the regional trade their livelihoods depended on.
- **From the south:** the kingdom of Amn has become more assertive, increasing military presence and cultivating local political support. Amn sees Nashkel as a strategic buffer against rising Baldurian power.

The mayor — described in the dossier as shrewd and pragmatic — is trying to play both sides, accepting aid from both and maintaining a semblance of independence. The dossier judges this game *unwinnable*: Nashkel's future will be decided by external forces, not by Nashkelans.

## Players around Nashkel

- Mayor and town council — playing the balance.
- [Chionthar Consortium](../../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) — the squeezing northern force.
- Amnian crown agents — the recruiting southern force; military and political both.
- [Amnian Kontor](../../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/summary.md) — Baldurian-based but Amn-aligned; their interests in the corridor pass through Nashkel.
- Local merchants — increasingly fragmented; some siding with Amn, some still trying to hold the Baldurian line.
