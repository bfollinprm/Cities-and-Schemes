# Whisperwood

A forest near the Chionthar, used as a logging concession by Sembian-backed industrial operations. The concession was brokered by the [Sembian Kontor](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/summary.md); the protection contract was placed with Sembian mercenaries.

## What is happening there

- **Logging camps.** Industrial-scale timber operations under Sembian capital.
- **Druid raids.** Night-attacks on the camps from the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md). A recent raid was ambushed and forced to retreat after a sharp skirmish with the Sembian mercenaries.
- **A standing flashpoint.** The contract is the kind that produces a war between the Circle and the Kontor on a slow timer.
