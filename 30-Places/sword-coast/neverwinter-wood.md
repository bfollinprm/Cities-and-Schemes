# Neverwinter Wood

A vast, dark forest hugging the slopes of Mount Hotenow east of Neverwinter. The canopy is so heavy that local rangers measure travel by lantern-shifts rather than miles. Logging is sparse — Neverwinter's lumber comes from gentler woods — and what penetrates the wood, by long habit, takes the deer-paths the eladrin keep open.

The wood holds an active **fey crossing** that opens onto the eladrin city of **New Sharandar** in the [Feywild](../../10-Setting/cosmology/realms/feywild.md). Eladrin guards on that side have lately begun reporting unfamiliar constellations through the gap — confirmation, the [Green Masquerade](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/green-masquerade.md) believes, of [Far Realm](../../10-Setting/cosmology/far-realm.md) pressure on the crossings.

## Etiquette and access

Travel with a guide if you do not know the deer-paths. Do not loose an arrow at anything that looks too tall to be a deer. If a voice greets you in formal Sylvan, answer in formal Sylvan, and do not promise anything.
