# Cloud Peaks

The mountain range that closes the Sword Coast at its southern end. Everything from [Waterdeep](waterdeep/README.md) south to the Cloud Peaks is the [Lords' Alliance](../../10-Setting/polities/lords-alliance.md) heartland; everything past them — [Amn](athkatla/README.md), [Tethyr](../../10-Setting/polities/calimshan-amn.md), [Calimshan](calimport/README.md) — is somebody else's politics.

## Why they matter

- **Geographic boundary.** The southern terminus of the Sword Coast as the campaign uses the term. Caravans crossing the Cloud Peaks pay Amnian tariffs on the far side; ships round them at sea.
- **Mineral wealth.** The peaks are rich in iron and silver — much of the Smiths' raw material runs north from passes here, brokered through the [Amnian Kontor](../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/summary.md)'s Coast Way wagons.
- **Banditry on the passes.** A standing problem the [Lords' Alliance](../../10-Setting/polities/lords-alliance.md) and the Amnian Council jointly fail to address; another reason caravans prefer the sea route between [Athkatla](athkatla/README.md) and Baldur's Gate.
