# Vault of Dragons

The half-mythic Waterdhavian gold cache, sealed away in the city's deeps for centuries, partially recovered in the famous dragon-heist of 1492 DR and never fully accounted for since. What remained — by all accounts a great deal — was relocated under the Lords' personal warding, and the city's official position is that the matter is closed.

It is not closed. [Jarlaxle Baenre's](../../../20-Factions/bregan-daerthe/people/jarlaxle-baenre.md) ambition is to recover the remaining Vault of Dragons gold and use it to buy [Luskan](../luskan/README.md) a legitimate seat in the [Lords' Alliance](../../../10-Setting/polities/lords-alliance.md) — which would change the political balance of the Sword Coast more than any single act in living memory.

## What is publicly known

- The Vault was relocated after the heist; its current location is not on any city map.
- The Open Lord's office has, on every public occasion, declined to discuss the matter.
- The [Harpers](../../../20-Factions/harpers/summary.md) have a standing watch on every known route [Jarlaxle's](../../../20-Factions/bregan-daerthe/people/jarlaxle-baenre.md) people have ever used in the city.
- The [Bregan D'aerthe](../../../20-Factions/bregan-daerthe/summary.md) connection to the [Sea Maidens Faire](../../../20-Factions/bregan-daerthe/associations/sea-maidens-faire.md) provides a cover for moving very large quantities of anything off Waterdhavian wharves.
