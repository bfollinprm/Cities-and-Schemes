# Virgin's Square

A wide flagstoned plaza in the [Trades Ward](README.md), ringed by guild-hall steps and the awnings of the heaviest hiring houses. By long custom the square is Waterdeep's open hiring ground for sell-swords: a noble's factor or a caravan master can stand at the central plinth at first bell, name a contract and a wage, and have a line of takers by the second.

Standard private bodyguard rate is **2–5 gp/day**, with elite work commanding far more from noble clients.

## Notable in this house

- **The plinth.** A flat-topped block at the centre of the square; whoever stands on it speaks the contract. The square's etiquette holds that the plinth-speaker has the floor until they accept a candidate or step down.
- **The hiring houses.** A rough crescent of three brokerage halls along the south side that take a cut for vetting, references, and (notionally) bonded guarantees.
- **The "Doom Raiders' bench."** A long stone seat on the east side; the Doom Raiders work this corner of the Trades Ward as an economic operation — money-lending, mercenary placements, smuggling — and treat the bench as their unofficial office during business hours.

## Who you find here

Sell-swords looking for the day's work, caravan masters and noble factors looking for sell-swords, the brokerage clerks of the hiring houses, retired sergeants on the brokerage payroll, and the Doom Raiders' line-runners.

## Etiquette and access

Stand off the plinth unless you are speaking. Do not undercut another speaker's wage in the same minute. If you accept a contract, you accept its terms — the brokerage clerks remember a runner.

## Pressures

The city's noble houses leaning on the brokerage halls to weed out "untrusted" mercenaries (read: anyone the Watch has ever flagged); the Doom Raiders' bench operating in plain sight of a [Lords' Alliance](../../../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/summary.md) factor's window; and a slow drift of the highest-paid contracts off the open square and into private hiring at the [Splendid Yard](../../../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/summary.md).
