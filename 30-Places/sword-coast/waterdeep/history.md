# Waterdeep — History

Discrete dated events are listed in [40-Timeline/history.md](../../../40-Timeline/history.md). This file is the **city's own memory** — the events that get cited in noble salons, taught in temple schools, or referenced by Magisters in court.

## The founding myths

- **936 DR — Founding.** The town grew from **Nimoar's Hold**, a military settlement on the cliffs.
- **1032 DR — Ahghairon and the Lords' Rule.** The warlord **Raurlor** declared himself emperor; the mage **Ahghairon** killed him and established the **Masked Lords**. Ahghairon is the city's foundational figure; "Ahghaironism" — anonymous council rule — remains the city's official political philosophy.

## The bargains struck

- **1248 DR — Nobility formalized.** The Lords granted the eighty-five noble houses the right to bear arms and keep private armies up to **seventy** soldiers (commoners are capped at sixteen). This is the legal basis of the modern aristocracy and of the **Great Game**.
- **1314 DR — The Lords' Alliance.** Waterdeep founded the Alliance, cementing the city as the political center of the Sword Coast North.

## The cautionary tale

- **1256 DR — Ahghairon dies; the Guildmasters' Folly.** With Ahghairon gone, the **Guildmasters** seized power. Their rule was corrupt and disastrous; they were violently overthrown. This is the institutional reason the Lords distrust the Guilds and the Guilds expect to be purged.
- **1273 DR — The Lords' Rule revived.** Lords **Baeron and Shilarn Silmaeril** restored the Masked Lords system, which has held continuously since.

## The modern wounds

- **c. 1482–1487 DR — Second Sundering.** Mystra's withdrawal cost Laeral Silverhand the bulk of her Chosen-of-Mystra power. She has concealed it for two decades.
- **c. 1489 DR — Neverember ousted; Laeral ascends.** The Masked Lords forced Open Lord **Dagult Neverember** out of office for corruption (he had been selling noble titles to wealthy merchants, fueling the *new money / old money* schism), and reluctantly elevated Laeral.
- **c. 1491 DR — The Masked Lord Murders.** A faction inside the Masked Lords assassinated nearly half the council in an attempted coup. The plot was foiled, but the council has not recovered: paranoia about any new concentration of power, including Laeral's, is institutional now.
- **c. 1491 DR — The Zhentarim Schism.** A clone of **Manshoon** arrived in Waterdeep, fractured the Black Network, and ignited the **Shadow War** with the Xanathar Guild. The war still burns through the Dock and Trades Wards.

## The present (1502 DR)

A city of contradictions: outwardly the prosperous Crown of the North, inwardly fractured by political schisms, a brutal gang war, and social unrest. See [character.md](character.md) for the four standing tensions any scene runs into.
