# Skullport

A subterranean port deep below Waterdeep, reached through the [Dock Ward](dock-ward/character.md) cellars and the **Undercity** tunnels. Lawless, slaver-thick, and effectively the [Xanathar Guild's](../../../20-Factions/xanathar-guild/summary.md) chief access route between the surface city and the Underdark.

## What it is

A port that does not see the sun. The Xanathar's people work it as their own; the Watch does not enter. Goods that cannot move through the Dock Ward openly — slaves, **Black Lilly**, contraband from the deep races — move through Skullport and surface in cellars all over the city.

## Why it matters to the surface

- **The Xanathar's leverage on the Dock Ward.** Anyone who controls Skullport controls how cargo enters and leaves Waterdeep without paying the Watch.
- **The intellect-devourer pipeline.** [Nihiloor's](../../../20-Factions/xanathar-guild/people/nihiloor.md) puppets are seeded from down here.
- **The slave trade.** Open and acknowledged in the right rooms.
