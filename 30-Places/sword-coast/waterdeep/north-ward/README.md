# North Ward

Inward of the Sea Ward. The preferred address for **"new money"** — lesser nobles, fabulously wealthy merchants, and retired adventurers. Its architecture is more ostentatious than the Sea Ward's, and its salons and private clubs are hotbeds of intrigue.
