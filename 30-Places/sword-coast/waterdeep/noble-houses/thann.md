# House Thann

**Tier:** Court (Great Game).
**Business:** Vintners (the more famous of the city's two noble wine houses, alongside Amcathra).
**Compromised:** **Corylus Thann** is one of the two Masked Lords currently blackmailed by Manshoon's Zhentarim — the lever is financial ruin.

A Court house with deep cellars and a deeper political problem. Corylus Thann sits on the Masked Lords council under a magical helm, and Manshoon already owns his vote. Manshoon has, in effect, two of the council's votes through the Thann-Urmbrusk pair, and no one in the council can move on him without exposing whose helms are compromised.
