# House Adarbrent

**Tier:** Court (Great Game).
**Business:** Shipping and exploration.
**Ward:** Sea Ward seat — a *rising* Court family pushing for the Throne.

A Court house climbing the social ladder. Their fortune is built on long-haul shipping out of the Dock Ward and a reputation for backing exploratory ventures. Sea Ward villas signal ambition: Adarbrent wants the conversation around the Throne to include their name.

No public scandal at present, no documented compromise. They occupy the rare position of a Court house with momentum rather than baggage.
