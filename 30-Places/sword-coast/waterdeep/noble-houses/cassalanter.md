# House Cassalanter

**Tier:** Throne (Great Game).
**Business:** Banking, public philanthropy.
**Ward:** Sea Ward.
**Hidden:** Devil-bound. The family secretly worships **Asmodeus**.

The campaign's central archetype of Sea Ward respectability. Cassalanter banking funds half the noble-house balances in the city; Cassalanter charity galas are the Sea Ward's most coveted invitations. Behind the velvet, the family has bound itself to Asmodeus — a contract whose terms eat the souls of their own children unless the household keeps paying in.

Their philanthropy is part of the cover. So is the public face of the family at salons and balls. The dossier reads House Cassalanter as the textbook example of *splendor as theater, paranoia as weather*.
