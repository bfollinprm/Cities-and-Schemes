# Castle Waterdeep

The fortress home of the [City Guard](../../../../../20-Factions/city-guard/summary.md), the city's standing military. The Open Lord is the Guard's supreme commander; in practice, garrison administration runs through career officers headquartered here. The Guard mans the walls, watches the gates, and (by tradition) does not police inside the city — that is the [City Watch's](../../../../../20-Factions/city-watch/summary.md) role.

Castle Waterdeep is paired with the **Palace of Waterdeep** next door, separating civilian and martial authority by design.
