# Palace of Waterdeep

The Open Lord's residence and the city's seat of government. Currently home to **Laeral Silverhand**. The Masked Lords convene in the Palace — entering, sitting, and leaving in concealing regalia — and foreign ambassadors present their credentials in its audience chambers.

The Palace is paired with **Castle Waterdeep** next door: civilian authority and martial authority in adjacent buildings, deliberately separate.
