# The High Forest

A vast and ancient woodland east of the Sword Coast — older than the Lords' Alliance, older than Waterdeep, older than the names mortals call it. The forest is a [Seelie](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/seelie.md) stronghold and one of the [Emerald Enclave](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md)'s most jealously held redoubts.

## Why it matters

- **Fey crossing.** The Seelie maintain an uneasy truce with druidic agents in the wood; some [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md) druids deal with them. Petrified unicorns and tentacled dryads have appeared during the recent Far Realm portents — the wood reads incursion before the cities do.
- **The Prince of Frost's signal.** The [Unseelie](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/unseelie.md) noble's coded warning to the Emerald Enclave manifested here as an unseasonable Midsummer frost. The druids treated it as a coded instruction, not an attack.
- **Cosmic canary.** When the High Forest "shakes," powers like [Oberon](../../10-Setting/cosmology/fey-courts/wild-archfey.md) stir; the wood is one of the standing barometers for whether the Far Realm pressure has crossed a threshold.
