# Week 1 — Events

Early spring, 1502 DR. The week the Outer City stops moving.

## Wyrm's Crossing locks down

The bridge becomes the city's main political fact. The [Chionthar Consortium](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/summary.md) leans on the Fist to [impound 'Mad' Meggan's vessel](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_1/impound-an-independent-vessel.md); the [Worshipful Company of Shipwrights & Calkers](../../20-Factions/wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_1/enforce-guild-only-repairs.md) closes its drydocks to non-guild hulls; the [Free Traders of the Outskirts](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_1/blockade-the-basilisk-gate.md) blockade the Basilisk Gate in retaliation; [Manip Jerrol](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/people/manip-jerrol.md) [beats a protesting merchant](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/activity/week_1/brutalize-a-protesting-merchant.md) in front of the caravan crews; and [High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) puts a [mass peaceful protest](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_1/organize-a-protest-at-wyrms-crossing.md) on the southern end of the bridge, joined by [the Ember's](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) [Followers of the Phoenix](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_1/join-the-hellrider-protest.md). **Blaze Liara Portyr** ends the Free Trader blockade with a Fist storming raid; the protest holds. Lower City supply chokes for several days. The [Council of Four's](../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_1/public-hearing-on-the-refugee-question.md) public hearing on the Refugee Question collapses into shouting and adjourns without a vote.

## The Whisperwood goes kinetic

The week-0 logging dispute turns into an open frontier fight. The [Western Gate Trading Company](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/summary.md) seats Sembian mercenaries who [draw first blood](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_1/first-blood-in-the-whisperwood.md) on the [Circle of the Inner Grove](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/summary.md) at the [Whisperwood Copse](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/hinterlands/wood-of-sharp-teeth/README.md); [Elder Elion's](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/people/elder-elion.md) druids' [retaliatory raid](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_1/ruin-a-log-shipment.md) eats traps and pulls back. Archduke [Lyra Silvershield](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) [deploys Upper City Watch patrols](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_1/deploy-watch-to-secure-logging-routes.md) along the new logging routes — visible patriar overreach onto Fist ground that bruises the [Watch](../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_1/crack-down-on-unsanctioned-art.md) for the rest of the week.

## Elite/property warfare orbits the Hlaths

[Consul Vorlamin Athar's](../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/people/vorlamin-athar.md) [fast sale](../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/activity/week_1/sell-the-hlath-estate-to-an-ally.md) of the foreclosed Hlath estate is blocked by [Lord Corin Durinbold's](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) injunction; the [Patriar Heritage Society's](../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_1/sponsor-the-dispossessed-hlath-family.md) sponsorship play is intercepted and humiliated when the Hlaths publicly refuse the aid; the [Knights of the Shield](../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_1/swear-in-lord-hlath.md) try to swear in Lord Hlath and watch him flee at the literal infernal phrasing. Durinbold's own [Heritage Preservation Act dies in Parliament](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_1/lobby-for-heritage-preservation-laws.md), and the [Sorcerous Sodality](../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_1/scry-on-a-peerage-of-blood-member.md) quietly scries him for next session.

## Criminal and intelligence maps shift

The [Zhentarim](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_1/establish-the-velvet-hand-social-club.md) seat the **Velvet Hand** parlor across from the ashes of the tavern they burned in week 0; the [Guild](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_1/reinforce-bramptons-loyalty.md) answers with [Tusgront's](../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) door-to-door tour of [Brampton](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md). The [River-Rats'](../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_1/smuggle-a-person-past-the-gates.md) people-smuggling run is busted on a tip. The [Wharf Rats'](../../20-Factions/wharf-rats/activity/week_1/misdirect-a-shipment-of-weapons.md) Fist-crossbow misdirection collapses on heightened security. The [Bibliophile](../../20-Factions/bibliophile/activity/week_1/acquire-and-sell-shipping-manifests.md) cannot acquire the Chionthar manifests. The [Sembian Kontor](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/activity/week_1/recruit-a-new-information-asset.md) seats a new asset inside the Fist barracks at Wyrm's Rock; the [Harpers'](../../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_1/investigate-the-masons-sabotage.md) Basilisk-Gate forensics are blocked by Mason Wardens; the [Watchful Shield Shrine](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/lord-hlath.md) is tipped about Hlath after the failed oath.

## Guilds weaponize shortages, patents, and inspections

The [Worshipful Company of Alchemists & Apothecaries](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_1/deny-antitoxin-to-a-clinic.md) refuse antitoxin to the Open Hand Temple in [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md); the [Worshipful Company of Fishmongers](../../20-Factions/wc-fishmongers/activity/week_1/negotiate-a-levy-exemption.md) cut a backroom Levy exemption with the Chionthar; the [Worshipful Company of Vintners & Brewers](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_1/bribe-a-peerage-of-coin-member.md) kill a luxury-goods tax with wine; the [Goldsmiths](../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_1/create-a-masterwork-for-a-patriar.md) deliver a canary automaton to seal the Durinbold alliance; the [Mercers](../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_1/identify-a-fashion-spy.md) blacklist a fashion spy; the [Smiths](../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_1/purchase-an-infernal-contract-shard.md) buy an infernal-contract shard from a New Elturel tiefling. Two guilds eat reputational losses: the [Masons](../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_1/publicly-blame-sabotage-for-flaw.md) lose the Basilisk-Gate sabotage frame, and the [Cartographers](../../20-Factions/wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_1/lose-an-inconvenient-deed.md) lose the deed-suppression hit, throwing a key Western Gate property claim into open dispute. The [Butchers' & Tanners'](../../20-Factions/wc-butchers-tanners/activity/week_1/intimidate-a-health-inspector.md) attempt to intimidate a health inspector also fails. The [Chandlers](../../20-Factions/wc-chandlers-lamplighters/activity/week_1/identify-dark-zones-for-the-guild.md) trade dark-zone maps to the Guild for protection.

## Hidden cult fronts mostly bungle

Most cells fail or stall: the [Cult of the Returning Lord](../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_1/murder-a-flaming-fist-informant.md) flunks a Bhaalist initiation murder; the [Silent Shroud](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_1/cleanse-the-sanctum-entrance.md) cannot crack the sanctum entrance; the [Hands of the Absolute](../../20-Factions/hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_1/kidnap-a-psychic.md) lose the Wide kidnapping; the [Gilded Vein's](../../20-Factions/gilded-vein/activity/week_1/manipulate-grain-futures.md) grain-futures forgery is caught and stabilized by the Sembians; the [Carnival of Whispers'](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_1/host-a-dream-inducing-performance.md) dream-show goes flat; the [Takers of the Tithe's](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_1/the-reaper-strikes.md) Reaper is wounded by a master goldsmith's hired bodyguard. The [Plague of Remembrance's](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_1/plant-evidence-of-a-refugee-murder-plot.md) "refugee murder plot" frame falls apart on a Watch patrol's timing; [Ettvard Needle](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_1/publish-a-story-blaming-refugees-for-merchants-murder.md) takes Vhage's gold and shelves the unprintable splash. The [Gravekeepers](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_1/investigate-psychic-disturbances-near-the-kliir.md) and [Unchained](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_1/begin-investigating-psychic-echoes.md) both miss the Hands of the Absolute echoes near the K'liir, and the cell keeps working.

## The conspiracy press flares

The [Unveiled](../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_1/find-proof-in-the-masons-wall.md) declare a "psionic resonance weapon" behind the Basilisk Gate collapse; the [Purified](../../20-Factions/purified/activity/week_1/distribute-purified-water.md) hand out vials in Rivington and Heapside. The [Elturel Remembrance Society's](../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_1/ritually-condemn-the-waterdhavian-kontor.md) ritual condemnation of the Waterdhavian *[Tome of the Companion](../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_1/translate-the-tome-of-the-companion.md)* translation falls flat; the [Society of Baldurian Integrity's](../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/activity/week_1/deface-a-kontor-funded-mural.md) Heapside mural defacement is foiled by Amnian Coin-Swords on a paid tip.

## From PC actions

The party arrives in [Rivington](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) this week and immediately becomes load-bearing on the Wyrm's Crossing crisis.

- **Sessions 1–2.** They [defuse a Zhentarim toll](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/pay-the-zhentarim-toll-on-the-coast-way.md) on the Coast Way (and clock Guild operatives watching from cover), then [heist the Open Hand medicine cart](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/heist-the-rivington-medicine-cart.md) out of a Chionthar warehouse in [Little Calimshan](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/little-calimshan.md), with Elia stealing a second cart of anti-Amnian propaganda. Spark [brokers a corridor through the Hellrider protest](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/broker-the-medicine-corridor-at-wyrms-crossing.md) and the medicine reaches the temple.
- **Session 2.** [Vex](../../20-Factions/guild/people/vex.md) of the Guild [hires the party](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/take-the-vex-job-shadow-gareth.md) for a "trust-building" silencing of the loud-mouth mason [Gareth](../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/people/gareth.md); Bror tails Gareth home.
- **Session 3.** [Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) [tasks the party](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/accept-the-jaredith-mission-from-alena.md) with turning her radicalized lieutenant [Jaredith Heartsong](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) off the Phoenix Pits' violent line; Spark talks her into granting the [Followers of the Phoenix](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) **official representation in New Elturel's council and land for a temple**. [Factor Errinthal](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) [co-opts the party](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/be-co-opted-by-the-chionthar-consortium.md) over the medicine cart and never thinks to ask after the second cart.
- **Sessions 4–5.** The party [tracks the Sewer Plague](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/track-the-sewer-plague-to-the-worm-spit.md) to the Worm Spit, survives an Otyugh, and [destroys the Plague-Father's laboratory](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/destroy-the-plague-fathers-laboratory.md) — a Plague of Remembrance alchemical cell with a **cracked-vial** sigil and Nightmare-Root reagents implicating a Hands-of-the-Absolute supply chain. They hand the captured cultist and the orphan [Finn](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/finn.md) to Alena, long-rest, and reach **level 3**.

The party leaves week 1 publicly in Consortium employ, privately in Hellrider trust, on retainer with the Guild, and in possession of three pieces of leverage no faction yet knows they hold: the propaganda cart, the cracked-vial sigil, and a captive Plague-of-Remembrance cultist breathing inside Alena's stockade.
