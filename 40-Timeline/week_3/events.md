# Week 3 — Events

Early spring, 1502 DR. The week the political crisis becomes a political war.

## The Council fractures, the Archduke goes private

[Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) brings the formal motion of no confidence in [Marshal Flint](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) to the [Council](../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_3/the-vote-of-no-confidence.md) and to the [Parliament floor](../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_3/vote-of-no-confidence.md). [The Peerage of Coin's whip operation](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_3/whip-the-votes.md) holds its bloc; [the Peerage of Blood](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_3/filibuster-the-mercenary-act.md) under [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) burns the floor with a sustained sovereignty filibuster against rented foreign steel. The motion fails. The mercenary act fails with it. The [Patriar Heritage Society](../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_3/defend-the-marshal.md) reframes the win as a defense of Baldurian institutions against new-money tyranny; [the Society of Baldurian Integrity](../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/activity/week_3/preserve-the-true-voice.md) chants old folk ballads outside the Hall doors as it happens. The chamber adjourns gridlocked.

The Archduke, locked out of every Council and Parliament route, opens her personal coffers and contracts a small elite mercenary company [through the Sembian Kontor](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_3/the-private-option.md). [Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/people/korgan-ironhand.md) signs the paper at the Iron Ledger; the soldiers answer to her, not the Fist, not the Council. A privately-funded army inside the city, accountable to no chamber, is the week's load-bearing fact even if it is not yet visible in the streets.

Marshal Flint, censured the previous week and now publicly defended by the patriar bloc, [recalls his loyalists](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/activity/week_3/circle-the-wagons.md) into [Wyrm's Rock Fortress](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-rock.md), ceding parts of the city to the [Watch](../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_3/maintain-appearances.md), the [Hellriders](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_3/the-negotiations-price.md), and the criminal landlords. He opens a back-channel to Durinbold acknowledging the political siege is real. He receives — and turns away — [a Smiths' Company petition](../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_3/an-appeal-to-the-marshal.md) for relief, [a Knights of the Shield approach](../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_3/a-friend-in-the-coin.md) for patronage (the Knights pivot to a [Chionthar consortium target](../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_3/a-friend-in-the-coin.md) who reports them), and [a clumsy Unveiled infiltration](../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_3/the-puppet-marshal.md) that ends with two cultists in the cells. He quietly entertains [a peacekeeping contract from Commander K'laar](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_3/an-honorable-contract.md) — neutral steel from the Unchained, no fealty to either side. The [Mercers](../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_3/the-color-of-authority.md) deliver new Fist dress uniforms in Peerage-of-Blood red.

## The Basilisk Gate hardens; the bombshell fizzles

['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) tries to convert [the cultist's public confession](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md) into formal patriar arrests. [Editor Ettvard Needle](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md), under direct patriar pressure and busy [chasing Marshal Flint himself](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_3/the-price-of-insubordination.md), refuses to print the names. [Her bombshell fizzles](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_3/press-the-advantage.md). The Sembian buyout offer she refused last week starts to look less generous, and [the Sembian Kontor's parallel scouting mission for an overland bypass east of the Coast Way](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/activity/week_3/the-alternate-route.md) is destroyed (one team to owlbears, one to [the Circle of the Inner Grove](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_3/reclaim-the-tainted-spring.md), whose own purification ritual backfires into a hostile water weird the same week).

Underneath the siege, the cult fronts move in parallel. The [Cult of the Returning Lord](../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_3/consecrate-the-blockade-with-blood.md) tries to ritualize a Fist-sergeant murder near the gate to frame the Free Traders; a [Guild patrol](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_3/recover-the-asset.md) interrupts it, the Bhaal cell flees, and a marked ritual dagger is recovered on the cobblestones. [The Carnival of Whispers](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_3/sowing-sweet-sorrows.md) seeds amplifiers in the protest crowd to harvest its despair. [The Lantaner Cogwork Courier prototype](../../20-Factions/lantaner-concession/activity/week_3/build-the-prototype.md) explodes in its [Cogwork Quay](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md) workshop, which [the Purified take as evidence](../../20-Factions/purified/activity/week_3/analyze-the-cure.md) of mind-control nano-cogs and [the Smiths take as evidence](../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_3/an-appeal-to-the-marshal.md) of Lantaner recklessness.

[High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) walks out of the Council's [citizenship negotiations](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_3/the-negotiations-price.md) when the terms required Hellrider abandonment of the siege. The Council's offer collapses. Alena now publicly stands with Meggan against the Council; the formal Hellrider–Free Trader military alliance becomes inevitable once the Archduke's private mercenaries arrive. [The Plague of Remembrance](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_3/a-new-vector.md) loses a contaminated grain shipment at the Dawnmarket to a Hellrider inspector trained on the Sewer Plague signature. [The Hands of the Absolute](../../20-Factions/hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_3/scour-the-slums-for-a-spark.md) sweep the Sow's Foot slums for a "Voice" candidate and find none, drawing Guild attention.

## New Elturel pivots to faith and to the next target

[The Ember (Alfira)](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) [breaks ground on the Phoenix Gate temple](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_3/the-first-sermon-at-the-phoenix-gate.md) and delivers her first formal sermon there with [Jaredith Heartsong](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) at her side. New converts pour in. The Outer City has an unambiguous spiritual leader the Council cannot pre-empt — and exactly one week later [the Takers of the Tithe](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_3/learn-the-muses-song.md) put a Spotter on the Ember for the entire week, mapping her routine for an eventual abduction. [The Sodality](../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_3/follow-the-resonance.md) loses [Zora's](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/people/zora.md) teleport signature in the chaotic Outer City magic and shifts the case from "investigation" to "standing alert." [Lord Perrine Hhune](../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/people/perrine-hhune.md) [is humiliated by a diabolist patriar](../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_3/seek-a-new-patron.md) the Knights are independently courting.

## Information warfare goes professional

[Editor Needle](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_3/the-price-of-insubordination.md) puts the *Baldur's Mouth* on Marshal Flint and prices the dirt by who will pay to bury it. [The Bibliophile](../../20-Factions/bibliophile/activity/week_3/the-price-of-treason.md) compiles a leverage dossier on the Marshal's *officers* — names, debts, families — for a multi-faction auction. [The Cartographers](../../20-Factions/wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_3/chart-the-chaos.md) draw assault and defense maps of both fortifications and offer them simultaneously to the Archduke's mercenaries and to Mad Meggan. [The Harpers](../../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_3/find-a-new-inlet.md) try to seat a clerk in the Archduke's Bloomridge Park Manor household, walk into a counter-intelligence trap, and lose [Randal Evenwood](../../20-Factions/harpers/summary.md) as a usable agent. [The Mercers](../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_3/the-color-of-authority.md) put Peerage-of-Blood colors on Fist officer parade uniforms before the Fist's officers will admit they have shifted political alignment. [The Chandlers](../../20-Factions/wc-chandlers-lamplighters/activity/week_3/a-light-in-the-storm.md) light Norchapel and quietly run the lamplighter intelligence subscription.

[The Waterdhavian Kontor](../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_3/an-offer-of-patronage.md), reading [last week's Bibliophile dossier on the party](../../20-Factions/bibliophile/activity/week_2/intelligence-for-sale.md), dispatches an agent to make them an offer of patronage — Lords' Alliance influence to clear the Fist warrants in exchange for a long-term retainer. The agent does not find them. The party is currently inside a Feywild pocket dimension.

## The underworld rebalances

[The Wharf Rats overplay](../../20-Factions/wharf-rats/activity/week_3/the-solidarity-tax.md) [last week's labor win](../../20-Factions/wharf-rats/activity/week_2/the-sympathy-squeeze.md) and try to levy a "solidarity tax" on Chionthar Consortium docks; [Factor Errinthal](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_3/secure-the-front-lines.md) hires Roah Moonglow's Zhent muscle to break it. The pier-side brawl breaks the tax and the Guild's hold over the Consortium docks for the first time since the strike of week 0; **Zhentarim sellswords are now visibly working Consortium pay** even as [Roah's parallel Brampton recruitment](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_3/an-offer-you-cant-refuse.md) is reported up to the Guild and the agent is eliminated. [Silas the Eel](../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_3/the-undercellar-run.md) overplays his own week-2 contraband win and loses half a crew and a wine consignment to ghouls in the Undercellar — exactly the necromantic seepage [the Gravekeepers are starting to map](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_3/a-pattern-in-the-rot.md). [The Iron Throne](../../20-Factions/iron-throne/activity/week_3/scavenge-the-scraps.md) quietly buys up the debt of small Outer City businesses dying in the siege. [The Goldsmiths' audit](../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_3/follow-the-money.md) of the party's [6,000 gp](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md) trail dies at the Counting House.

[The Gilded Vein](../../20-Factions/gilded-vein/activity/week_3/twist-the-knife.md) tries to forge a second framing letter to push Chionthar–Amn into open trade war; the targeted factor catches it and only makes the Consortium harder to deceive. [The Amnian Kontor](../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/activity/week_3/identify-the-assassins-patron.md), reading the original frame as Sembian work, opens a quiet financial trade war against [Sembian flagged hulls and Iron Ledger letters of credit](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/activity/week_3/the-alternate-route.md). The Lower City finance war that follows is no one's intended outcome. [The Silent Shroud](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_3/whispers-from-the-stone.md) reaches for the Whispering Stone through Bror; the stone resists; a third party of significant power notices the failed Sharran ritual and Mother Nocturne's intelligence brokerage on Bror is no longer single-buyer.

## Worshipful Companies maneuver around the war

[Marga Saltwind's Fishmongers](../../20-Factions/wc-fishmongers/activity/week_3/feed-the-blockade.md) feed the blockade at cost and become heroes to the people who used to scab on her piers. [Helma Tallowshand's Shipwrights](../../20-Factions/wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_3/a-port-in-the-storm.md) overreach on premium moorings and lose a price war the consortia coordinate against her. [Master Stonehand](../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_3/a-question-of-loyalty.md) brutally consolidates the Masons after a leak attempt; [Belba Quickfoot's Vintners](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_3/a-taste-of-gratitude.md) try to bribe Lord Durinbold with brown ale and have it returned with a scathing public note; [Master Droog's Alchemists](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_3/find-a-scapegoat.md) post a forged bounty on a fired apprentice to bury an internal reagent-theft inquisition. [The Butchers' Sow's Foot rations](../../20-Factions/wc-butchers-tanners/activity/week_3/salvage-the-loss.md) are seized and burned by a Hellrider patrol on the same heightened spoilage watch that caught the Plague of Remembrance grain.

## From PC actions

The party walks into week 3 publicly outlawed by the Fist, broken with the Guild, courted by the Zhentarim, and quietly recruited by the Silent Shroud — and walks out two Material-Plane weeks ahead of themselves, in possession of a forgotten god-fragment, with two outstanding fey debts.

- **Session 10.** Confronted with [a Fist patrol arriving at the Weary Plowman to serve the Mad Meggan warrant](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_3/evade-the-fist-warrant.md), [Lena](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/people/lena.md) misdirects the soldiers and the party slips out the back alley. They go to ground at the [Circus of the Last Days](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_3/infiltrate-the-circus-of-the-last-days.md) on the Guild's investigation assignment. Bror confirms a Guild lookout. Inside, the entire grounds read as planar bleed; Elia's shard hums in resonance with Bror's Whispering Stone for the first time. **Elia and Bror each accept fey bargains with [the mirror-tent proprietor](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/mirror-tent-proprietor.md)** — one true secret each, owed at a later date. They watch [Lucretius](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/lucretius.md) speak the Big Top, [Zara the Sorrow-Eater](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/zara.md) harvest grief from the crowd, and Hellrider Quartermaster [Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md) led backstage diminished. They follow. At the Feywild gate they fight [Grinmaw and a pair of displacer beasts](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_3/force-the-feywild-portal.md) and force the portal — Bror through first, then Finn, then the rest under Kaelan's *Burning Hands* cap.
- **Session 11.** A Feywild glade with severe time dilation: Finn waits a week, Bror hours, the others minutes. Reunited, they enter [*The Ephemera Collection*](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md) and confront Lucretius. Lyra's memories are jarred on the shelf — *"Memories of Elturel's Fall" → "The Strength to Prevent Another."* Lucretius offers a three-riddle bargain: win, get Lyra's memory back; either way, he will activate Elia's shard. The party answers all three correctly (a mirror, a promise, an answer — Finn nailing the last two). They restore Lyra's memory; she rises with purpose intact. Lucretius honors the bargain. He activates Elia's shard. Elia and Bror are flooded with [a cosmic vision](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_3/unlock-the-shard-of-the-first-scribe.md) of mortal souls being caged by divine wills and a forgotten God of Naming severing a piece of itself in rejection — that fragment is Elia's stone, now reshaped as the **Shard of the First Scribe**. The party returns to Rivington. **Two weeks have passed on the Material Plane.** The Fist warrant has cooled. Spark pays Lena 50 gp for held rooms; Bror's third Whispering Stone attunement attempt fails with deafness.

The party leaves week 3 with a forgotten god-fragment that has rejected divinity, an explicit fey-bargain debt to the mirror-tent proprietor, a restored Hellrider quartermaster who owes them their identity, and a city that has aged two weeks without them. The next time they walk through the Plowman door it is week 5; **the world has not waited**.
