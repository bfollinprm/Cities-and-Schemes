# Baldur's Mouth — Week 6 — Siege Edition

*Early spring, 1502 DR. The siege bites.*

## Front page

**THE COUNCIL FIRES ON ITS OWN**
*by Ettvard Needle, Editor*

[Western Gate mercenaries discharged warning volleys at the Basilisk Gate this Threeday](../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_6/the-warning-shot.md), the first time in living memory armed men in the Council's pay have fired on Baldurian civilians. The Free Trader [shield wall](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_6/the-shield-wall.md) did not break. ['Mad' Meggan instituted fair-share rationing inside the line](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_6/rationing-the-hope.md) the same afternoon. The line is hungry. The line holds.

[Sembian patrols are now confiscating personal stores from any traveller crossing the Outer City roads.](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_6/the-starvation-siege.md) The soup at [Open Hand Temple](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/open-hand-temple.md) has halved twice in a Sevenday. *Inside this issue, page four:* **The Insurance Scam.**

## Above the fold

- [The Watch has locked the interior gates of the Upper City.](../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_6/the-gate-lockdown.md) No entry without a resident's pass. Bloomridge is out of fresh bread.
- [Counting Houses and banks now carry liveried Sembian guard at every door.](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_6/the-private-security.md) The Fist is no longer welcome on the coin row.
- [Druids of the Inner Grove called vines from beneath the Tannery vats.](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_6/the-source-of-the-rot.md) Three split. The trade is dead until further notice.

## Page four — *The Insurance Scam*

*Filed under the Credential of Balduran by a correspondent at this paper.*

[A document trail traced through Counting House escrow records](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_6/the-siege-edition.md) names the Chionthar Consortium and the Sembian Kontor as the underwriters of vessels and warehouses they then arranged to lose: the *River's Pride*; two condemned River Quarter warehouses written down a Tenday before they burned. Smokepowder out of the Sembian [Iron Ledger](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/summary.md) moves on the same manifests as the claim numbers. Dates and contracts are reproduced under this issue's masthead.

## City pages

- **Trade Way.** [Two-thirds of a Sembian steel shipment lost to fire.](../../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_6/the-supply-sabotage.md) [Factor Mornwood of the Splendid Yard intervened](../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_6/the-diplomatic-immunity.md) when Fist arrests followed, citing Lords' Alliance immunity. Waterdeep, in this paper's reading, has chosen.
- **[Gray Harbour](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md).** [Matron Saltwind has formed an armed Fishmongers' harbor militia.](../../20-Factions/wc-fishmongers/activity/week_6/the-harbor-patrol.md) Petty thieves reported being thrown into the bay nightly.
- **Phoenix Gate.** [Foundation Stone laid](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_6/the-foundation-stone.md) at the new temple. Crowds attending; this paper notes the mood is no longer scared.
- **High Hall.** [The Peerage of Blood has filed a public Petition of Grievances](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_6/the-petition-of-grievances.md) demanding the Sembian "foreign army" be expelled.
- **Brampton.** [Lamplighters have ordered a "maintenance blackout" on the Zhentarim corridors.](../../20-Factions/wc-chandlers-lamplighters/activity/week_6/the-dark-streets.md) Walk in pairs.

## Notices and obituaries

- **Officers' Mess.** Lord Durinbold receives Fist sergeants at his manor by candlelight this Sevenday; [the wine is generous](../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_6/the-officers-mess.md).
- **Goldsmiths.** [Master Goldhand has admitted outside auditors to the vaults.](../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_6/the-gold-standard.md) The vaults were as he said. The political bill is his to pay.
- **Mercers.** ["Siege Chic" — fur-lined coats, silvered canes](../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_6/the-winter-collection.md) — sold out in two days.
- **Masons.** [Strike continues](../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_6/the-labor-strike.md) on every Council and patriar reinforcement site.
