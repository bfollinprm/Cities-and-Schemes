# Week 5 — Events

Early spring, 1502 DR. The week of the ultimatum.

## The Council's three days

[Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md), with [last week's barracks operational in Eastway](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_4/the-barracks-requisition.md) and [the Sembian "City-Sanctioned Peacekeepers" contract on file at the High Hall](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/activity/week_4/legitimizing-the-contract.md), [issues a formal three-day ultimatum from the High Hall steps](../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md): dismantle the [Basilisk Gate blockade](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md), or have it dismantled. By midday criers are reading it at every gate. **It is the first time in living memory an Archduke has formally declared force against a Baldurian assembly.** [Parliament cannot reach quorum to respond](../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_5/the-failed-session.md); the moderate emergency session collapses for lack of attendees and the chambers stay paralyzed. [The Peerage of Coin's Emergency Commerce Protection Act dies in the streets](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_5/the-emergency-tax.md) when [Guild](../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) wardens physically block the tax collectors out of the Lower City workshops; the levy fails, the chamber is humiliated, and the city now knows the Peerage of Coin cannot enforce its own laws.

['Mad' Meggan answers the ultimatum the same afternoon](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_5/heed-the-ultimatum.md): wagons rolled into a second inner barricade, makeshift weapons distributed, Free Traders organized into a defensive militia, dig-in for siege. [High Watcher Alena formalizes the Elturgard Alliance](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md) by sundown — Hellrider discipline behind the Free Trader line — and the political war is now formally a martial one. [Western Gate's mercenaries answer with rural roadblock checkpoints](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_5/choke-the-supply-lines.md) seizing food and water shipments to the Outer City; the Archduke means to starve the blockade rather than storm it. [The Peerage of Blood pivots from chamber-floor letters to street-level pamphlets](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_5/the-propaganda-campaign.md) — *"Silvershield's Foreign Thugs"* — flooding Lower and Outer Cities with mercenary slander; [Lord Corin Durinbold extends the Loyalty Fund into a "Silver Fist" ribbon-and-purse program](../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_5/the-silver-fist.md) at the [Elfsong Tavern](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/elfsong-tavern.md), buying Fist enlisted ranks back to the patriar coalition.

## Wyrm's Rock garrisons; the Phoenix rises; Eastway is mined

[Marshal Flint orders integration drills between his Fist regulars and Commander K'laar's githyanki phalanx](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/activity/week_5/the-integration-drills.md). The skill gap is enormous; Fist resentment quietly hardens as the K'liir warriors outclass them at every drill. [K'laar establishes a silent psionic perimeter around Wyrm's Rock and Wyrm's Crossing](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_5/establish-the-perimeter.md) that unnerves the city even as it secures the fortress. [The Cartographers' Guild's mapping detail is detected, stripped, mind-wiped, and dumped naked at the bridge end](../../20-Factions/wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_5/mapping-the-battlefield.md) — a public terror lesson. [The Plague of Remembrance's seduction ploy on a disillusioned Hellrider officer fails when he bashes the sleeping draught against the wall](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_5/the-serpents-kiss.md); the agent flees with Hellriders pursuing, exposing the cult's infiltration and putting Alena on high alert.

[The Ember turns the failed Reaper attack into a Survivor's Sermon at the Phoenix Gate](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_5/the-survivors-sermon.md) — *"the city's elite fear our light"* — and crowds triple by sundown. **Phoenix Gate temple construction begins in earnest.** [The Takers of the Tithe send a follow-up assassin to the New Elturel stockade to silence the captured Reaper](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_5/silence-the-loose-end.md); a Hellrider guard dog catches the scent and bloodies the assassin off; the Reaper survives, alive and potentially willing to trade.

[The Hellrider–Free Trader alliance puts its first physical fortification in the ground](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md): Eastway is mined. The mines come from the [Guild's smokepowder shipment that the party intercepted](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md), routed through coerced Eastway Ward Boss [Farlin](../../20-Factions/guild/people/farlin.md). The same smokepowder also goes the other way: [the Velvet Hand collapses on Roah Moonglow's safehouse](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_5/the-financial-front.md). The Zhentarim's smokepowder plot dies with its target. [Roah pivots immediately to the Counting House](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_5/the-financial-front.md) — agents infiltrate, ledgers manipulate, Guild legitimate-business fronts disrupted.

## The trade war fragments

[The Amnian Kontor calls in a debt and seizes two Consortium river barges at Gray Harbour](../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/activity/week_5/retaliatory-asset-seizure.md), publicly humiliating [Factor Errinthal](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/people/factor-errinthal.md) at the [Counting House](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/counting-house.md). [Errinthal's attempt to secure the riverward approaches collapses](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_5/secure-the-riverward-approaches.md) when the hired River Rat smugglers reveal his Undercity gold cache to Amnian spies — the **Consortium's reserve gold is stolen, and the warehouses left critically exposed**. The river-trade chokehold compounds: [the Circle of the Inner Grove's water weird, escaped from binding last week, now hunts skiffs in the main Chionthar](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_5/hunt-the-river-horror.md), pushing Elder Elion's circle into a dangerous downstream pursuit. River traffic stalls. Coster Hall clerks frantically liquidate.

[The Sembian Kontor shifts from politics to logistics](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/activity/week_5/arming-the-aggressors.md) — wagon trains run from the Iron Ledger to the Eastway barracks, supplying weapons, armor, and provisions for the coming attack. [The Vintners' shipment to the camp is intercepted and poisoned by Zhentarim agents](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_5/securing-the-supply-line.md); mercenaries fall ill, the Archduke's commander cancels the contract, the Vintners lose a fortune. [The Bibliophile's mercenary-camp infiltrators are caught and fed disinformation](../../20-Factions/bibliophile/activity/week_5/the-warlords-dossier.md), turning Nansi Gretta's intelligence product worse than worthless.

[The Worshipful Companies fragment](../../20-Factions/wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_5/profiting-from-chaos.md): Shipwright intimidation backfires when independent crews unite under [Wharf Rat](../../20-Factions/wharf-rats/summary.md) protection — [the Wardens are thrown into the harbor in a public brawl](../../20-Factions/wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_5/profiting-from-chaos.md); Master Tallowshand's monopoly cracks. [The Goldsmiths' party-bounty search ends in disgust](../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_5/chasing-ghosts.md), Goldhand's outburst alienating banking contacts. [The Fishmongers' poisoner-bounty draws hundreds of liars and no truth](../../20-Factions/wc-fishmongers/activity/week_5/find-the-poisoner.md), enforcer-led brawls shred Marga's reputation. [The Mercers profit on grief](../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_5/mourning-becomes-profit.md) with a "somber couture" line at the Facemaker's Boutique. [The Masons' overture to Lord Durinbold is publicly rebuffed as "new money"](../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_5/an-alliance-of-stone-and-blood.md), driving a wedge between guilds and the patriar coalition. [The Smiths petition Durinbold for a blockade-bypass exemption](../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_5/an-appeal-to-the-old-guard.md). [The Chandlers' attempt to pull lamplighters from dangerous routes is ignored](../../20-Factions/wc-chandlers-lamplighters/activity/week_5/severing-ties.md) — Anya's crews are now Guild-controlled directly. [The Alchemists initiate a vault lockdown](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_5/the-vault-lockdown.md) hiring Sembian mercenaries to reinforce. [The Butchers stage a Sow's Foot donation drive](../../20-Factions/wc-butchers-tanners/activity/week_5/the-donation-drive.md) the Outer City does not believe.

[The Iron Throne pivots from front-company to information brokerage](../../20-Factions/iron-throne/activity/week_5/the-rumor-mill.md) — selling mercenary numbers, leadership, and supply lines from the Blushing Mermaid to both Free Traders and the Guild. [The Wharf Rats are hit by a Zhentarim hit squad guided by a traitor in their own ranks](../../20-Factions/wharf-rats/activity/week_5/licking-their-wounds.md); One-Eyed Jack loses more crew. [The Knights of the Shield approach a recently-burgled Peerage of Coin patriar in Manorborn](../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_5/a-desperate-bargain.md) with infernal-protection terms. [The Watch's plea for reinforcements is publicly mocked by Marshal Flint](../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_5/the-plea-for-reinforcements.md); patrols are abandoned in outer Upper City districts; Manorborn estates are exposed.

## The hidden plots adapt and break

[The Bhaalist sanctum-relocation to Hamhock's Slaughterhouse goes wrong](../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_5/relocate-the-sanctum.md) when an acolyte spills ritual blood in Sow's Foot; local butchers form a curse-fearing mob and chase the cell back into the sewers without their idols, scattered. [The Gravekeepers' cleansing prep is stalled by tainted holy water and obsolete intel](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_5/plan-the-cleansing.md); Hemlock loses weeks of work and his target. [The Hands of the Absolute's ritual to harvest the city's fear backfires catastrophically](../../20-Factions/hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_5/the-psychic-miasma.md) — the psychic feedback loop knocks The Voice in the Choir unconscious, burns out the resonance chamber, and leaves the cult **psychically blinded** and unable to hear the Echo. [The Gilded Vein's go-to-ground order fails when a former member sells their safehouses to the Chionthar Consortium](../../20-Factions/gilded-vein/activity/week_5/go-to-ground.md); three hideouts raided, leadership scattered, accumulated funds lost. [The Elturel Remembrance Society regroups in a hidden New Elturel cellar](../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_5/regroup-in-the-shadows.md) — Lord Hhune now a fugitive, hatred sharpened. [The Society of Baldurian Integrity's prison visit](../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/activity/week_5/the-prison-visit.md) gets a coded "drunken-merchant vandalism" cover story to the captured nativists at Heapside Prison; the Society survives.

[The Silent Shroud loses track of Bror](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_5/monitor-the-tithe.md) in a Helmite-patrolled Upper City; Mother Nocturne's cell can no longer corroborate his movements, and paranoia rises in the cell. [The Sorcerous Sodality's analysts are overwhelmed](../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_5/analyze-the-arcane-traffic.md) by the magical noise of githyanki arrivals and unshielded refugee artifacts — the [scrying censers stood up last week](../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_4/the-scrying-censors.md) crash under load, the database corrupts, and **fugitive Zora slips through the checkpoints undetected.** [The Carnival of Whispers' fear-harvest in the Rivington markets backfires](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_5/a-feast-of-fear.md); the crowd is too hardened by years of crisis to be afraid of clowns; off-duty Hellriders chase the troupe out; Mister Smiles starves. [The Unveiled break their captured comrades out of Heapside Prison](../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_5/the-great-escape.md) using forgotten Undercity tunnels — a desperate survival operation that buys them another week. [The Lantaner foundry scavenge into the defunct Steel Watch Foundry](../../20-Factions/lantaner-concession/activity/week_5/the-foundry-scavenge.md) goes for rare alloys to rebuild the prototype. [The Purified complete their Cogwork Detector](../../20-Factions/purified/activity/week_5/the-cogwork-detector.md) and begin testing on city water samples.

[The Harpers shift from peace-brokering to war-profiteer exposure](../../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_5/expose-the-war-profiteer.md): the Nightingale tasks [Randal Evenwood](../../20-Factions/harpers/people/randal-evenwood.md) with finding proof that [Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/people/korgan-ironhand.md) is fanning the war for profit. [The Baldur's Mouth runs the Githyanki Garrison cover](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_5/the-githyanki-garrison.md) — a sensational woodcut of K'liir warriors atop Wyrm's Rock — and sells record copies while questioning Marshal Flint's judgment. [The Waterdhavian Kontor begins cultivating a new asset group out of the Elfsong](../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_5/cultivate-a-new-asset.md) after [closing the party's file last week](../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_4/a-closed-file.md).

## From PC actions

The party returns from the Feywild into a Plowman that has stopped being safe. [Sembian mercenaries shake down a family in front of them under the Emergency Commerce Protection Act, then post a 250-gp bounty for "foreign operatives" matching every PC except Finn](../../50-Sessions/session_12/player_summary.md). An **Intellect Devourer** speaks into Bror's deaf mind, helps drop the sergeant, and disappears. Bror is knocked unconscious by a Sembian psychic blade; Spark bribes the rest 125 gp to leave; Kaelan hauls Bror to the [Open Hand Temple](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/open-hand-temple.md) and turns the bedside scene into a pragmatic-faith sermon to the refugees sheltering there. [Lyra Sunstone catches the party on the doorstep of the Pilgrim's Parchment and presents Hellrider surplus](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_5/the-pilgrims-parchment-buy.md): full plate to Kaelan, leather and a perfectly-balanced greatsword to Spark. The Hellriders, she says, have moved to the Basilisk Gate to throw in with Mad Meggan.

The party buys **three Candlekeep-key books for 900 gp** at the Pilgrim's Parchment and decodes them: the **Wyrmway Wardens Weekly Ledger** is the [Guild's Rosetta Stone](../../20-Factions/guild/summary.md) — a coded directory of Guild contacts, safehouses, and assets across the wards. The **Stonemason's Secret** is a Clan Iron Eye relic with two solved ciphers — *"THE FORGE THINKS STILL"* and *"FOLLOW SOLSTICE SUN FROM KARAK-DUR"* — pointing to a lost dwarven forge that creates sentient constructs. The **untitled scaly-leather book** is an Underdark scientific text linking [illithid](../../20-Factions/hands-of-the-absolute/summary.md) psionics to unknown star charts not visible from Faerûn — clearly missing a foundational key.

Then the violent week. [Vex lures the party from the Blushing Mermaid into a Gray Harbour warehouse and springs a pre-planned ambush](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_5/kill-vex-and-loot-her-office.md) — two door-guards, four hidden archers, a demand for the Whispering Stone. **Vex dies** (Finn lands the killing blow); the party walks out with her ciphered Rivington appointment book, the [Velvet Hand smokepowder map](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_5/prepare-the-retaliation.md), stolen Consortium intel on Errinthal's print campaign, and **kompromat on Eastway Ward Boss [Farlin](../../20-Factions/guild/people/farlin.md)**. One archer escapes — the party tracks him down and turns him; his name is [Simbar](../../20-Factions/guild/people/simbar.md). They [bring him and the intel cache to Alena and Mad Meggan at the Basilisk Gate camp](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_5/the-rivington-war-council.md), make full disclosure, and **reach Level 5.** Simbar is installed as the puppet "Guildmaster of Rivington."

The party then [intercepts Silas's smokepowder shipment in the sewers](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_5/mine-eastway-and-detonate-the-velvet-hand.md), realizes [Nine-Fingers](../../20-Factions/guild/people/astele-keene.md) intended the explosion to kill **both** the Zhentarim *and* Silas's crew at once, and splits the cargo three ways. **The Velvet Hand goes up.** Eastway is mined. Farlin plants the third under coercion. [Errinthal's Coster Hall ambush traps the party as the Consortium's relief-caravan escort](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_5/errinthals-relief-caravan-pr-coup.md) in front of Ettvard Needle; the party signals Meggan's scouts under duress, and at the Basilisk Gate Finneas turns the standoff into a free feast at Eastway — Errinthal banks the *Baldur's Mouth* PR; the party banks the goodwill where it matters.

The week closes with the [Rivington Gambit running](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_5/install-finneas-as-guild-boss-of-rivington.md): Finneas opens "office hours" at the Plowman, [Lena](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/people/lena.md) pours tea, the first locals walk in with grievances. Kaelan crosses to Heapside, secures an investigative-journalism position from [Ettvard Needle](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/people/ettvard-needle.md) at the Baldur's Mouth print shop, and walks out with the **Credential of Balduran**. The Whispering Stone, now passively listening through Bror, picks up alien-language fragments from a wider eavesdropping network — recognizable as the same items Needle has been printing. **The Baldur's Mouth's "scoops" have a sound, and the party is now its passive listener.** Intelligence revealed late in the week: Errinthal has been *quietly funding Mad Meggan* the whole time, hoping stalemate kills land-trade competitors and locks in his river monopoly. The party knows. Meggan does not yet know they know.

The week's load-bearing facts: the chambers are paralyzed; the mercenaries garrison Eastway; Eastway is now a minefield under their feet; the Velvet Hand is rubble; the Phoenix Gate temple is rising; Vex is dead and the Guild's Rivington ward is quietly inverting under the party; Nine-Fingers has not yet rendered a verdict. Week 6 begins on the next page of the *Baldur's Mouth*.
