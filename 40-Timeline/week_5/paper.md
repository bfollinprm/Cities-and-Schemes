# Baldur's Mouth — Week 5

*Early spring, 1502 DR. The Ultimatum issue.*

## Front page

**ARCHDUKE DECLARES FORCE ON THE BLOCKADE**
*by Ettvard Needle, Editor*

[Archduke Lyra Silvershield issued a three-day ultimatum from the steps of the High Hall](../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_5/issue-the-ultimatum.md) to the Free Traders camped at the Basilisk Gate: dismantle the blockade, or have it dismantled. Criers carried the order through every quarter by midday. **It is the first time in living memory an Archduke has formally declared force against a Baldurian assembly.**

By sundown the answer had been given. ['Mad' Meggan rolled wagons into a second inner barricade](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_5/heed-the-ultimatum.md) and distributed makeshift arms; [the Hellriders of New Elturel formally took the line beside her](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md). Western Gate mercenaries answered with [rural roadblock checkpoints](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_5/choke-the-supply-lines.md), seizing food and water from inbound caravans.

## Above the fold

- [Parliament fails to seat a quorum](../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_5/the-failed-session.md) for a third consecutive emergency session. The chamber stays dark.
- [Guild wardens block the Peerage of Coin's tax collectors out of the Lower City workshops](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_5/the-emergency-tax.md). The Emergency Commerce Protection Act dies before it is enforced.
- **Brampton, Threeday night.** [A blast levelled the Velvet Hand](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_5/the-financial-front.md). Watch declines comment. *Not a fire,* a neighbour told this paper, *a thunderclap.*

## City pages

- **Eastway.** [Sapper work is observed nightly along the Basilisk Gate line](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_5/the-elturgard-alliance.md). Free Trader pickets caution travellers to keep to the marked road.
- **Phoenix Gate.** [The Ember preached to a tripled crowd](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_5/the-survivors-sermon.md) at the gate altar this Sevenday. Foundation work for a new temple has begun.
- **Wyrm's Rock.** [The K'liir githyanki phalanx walks the parapets](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_5/establish-the-perimeter.md) under Marshal Flint's hand; see this paper's separate cover plate.
- **[Gray Harbour](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md).** [Shipwrights' Wardens were thrown into the bay](../../20-Factions/wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_5/profiting-from-chaos.md) by independent crews under Wharf Rat protection. Master Tallowshand offers no comment.
- **Eastway, again.** [Free Traders set tables at the Basilisk Gate](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_5/errinthals-relief-caravan-pr-coup.md) for a relief caravan supplied by Factor Errinthal of the Chionthar Consortium. The line ate. The Consortium was thanked.
- **Sembian camp, Eastway.** [Mercenaries fall ill on tainted ale](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_5/securing-the-supply-line.md); the Vintners' supply contract has been cancelled.

## Notices and obituaries

- **Silver Fist subscriptions.** Lord Corin Durinbold receives ribbon-and-purse callers at the [Elfsong Tavern](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/elfsong-tavern.md) every afternoon this Tenday. Fist enlisted welcome.
- **Mercers.** Mistress Vance's [somber-couture line](../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_5/mourning-becomes-profit.md) is now stocked in Bloomridge. *"Wear it for the city."*
- **Goldsmiths.** [Master Goldhand has withdrawn the standing party-bounty](../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_5/chasing-ghosts.md). No further questions taken.
- **Masons.** The Worshipful Company of Masons declines all Council and patriar reinforcement contracts pending [satisfactory address](../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_5/an-alliance-of-stone-and-blood.md) from Lord Durinbold's office.
