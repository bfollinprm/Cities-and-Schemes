# Week 2 — Events

Early spring, 1502 DR. The week the political crisis stops being a crisis and becomes the new normal.

## The Basilisk Gate becomes a siege

['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) [digs in](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_2/dig-in-for-the-siege.md) and turns the week-1 picket into a sustained, multi-week blockade. The party walks the captured cultist into her wagon and sells him for [**6,000 gp**](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md); his publicly-broadcast confession naming patriar families ignites a class-warfare firestorm and locks the Council out of any clean negotiation. [Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md) [orders the Fist to break the line](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_2/archdukes-mandate.md); [Marshal Flint](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) refuses; the [Council formally censures him](../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_2/contain-the-crises.md) and the political/military rift becomes load-bearing. The [Peerage of Coin's mercenary motion fails](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_2/legislate-aggression.md) when [Durinbold's Peerage of Blood frames the bill as anti-sovereignty](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_2/a-charity-of-spite.md). Parliament gridlocks.

Sembian envoys ([with K'laar brokering](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/activity/week_2/test-the-barricade.md)) try to buy Meggan off and are refused. The [Knights of the Shield](../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_2/a-bargain-in-the-barracks.md) try to buy Marshal Flint and are refused. Goods rot on the road; [the Butchers' livestock shipment dies](../../20-Factions/wc-butchers-tanners/activity/week_2/secure-the-herd.md), [the Seven Suns lose a Calimshan silk run at sea](../../20-Factions/seven-suns/activity/week_2/the-captive-market.md), and the [Smiths file a multi-guild emergency petition](../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_2/arming-the-inevitable.md). Underneath, [the Cartographers sell the Western Gate "alternate" overland routes](../../20-Factions/wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_2/monetize-the-maze.md) that quietly omit the dangerous fauna; [the Iron Throne lose the resulting caravan-guard bid](../../20-Factions/iron-throne/activity/week_2/the-high-risk-wager.md) to the party.

## The Hellrider protest ends in a song

[High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) breaks the captured cultist and [opens formal Hellrider citizenship negotiations with Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_2/the-interrogation.md). She hands the prisoner to the party; he names a "sister in disguise" mage embedded with [the Followers of the Phoenix](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/summary.md) and a plan to martyr [Jaredith Heartsong](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md). The party [exposes Zora as the mage](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/expose-zora-at-the-phoenix-pits.md) at the [Phoenix Pits](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/phoenix-pits.md), Spark wins a non-lethal duel with Jaredith, and the [watchtower ambush](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/survive-the-watchtower-ambush.md) forces Zora to teleport away — [Vhage extracts her from the city](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_2/silence-and-slander.md).

The downstream political effect is the Outer City's biggest beat of the week: [the Ember](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) magically amplifies a [Jaredith ballad](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/witness-jaredith-end-the-protest.md) at Wyrm's Crossing, the Hellrider standoff dissolves into communal empathy, and the [Followers of the Phoenix break ground on a new temple](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_2/the-call-for-martyrs.md). Alena's disciplined moderates have been upstaged by the Ember's charismatic faith movement; the Council now has a less predictable broker to deal with. The [Sodality opens a case file on Zora](../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_2/trace-the-arcane-signature.md) and seeds permanent scrying censers at Wyrm's Crossing and the Basilisk Gate.

## Underworld and intelligence both quietly profit

With the Fist tied up in the Archduke standoff, [Silas the Eel's River-Rats run a record contraband haul](../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_2/the-blockade-boom.md) up the Chionthar; the [Wharf Rats lever a slowdown into a real wage win](../../20-Factions/wharf-rats/activity/week_2/the-sympathy-squeeze.md); [Vex breaks with the party and pivots to One-Eyed Jack](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_2/retaliation-on-the-river.md) at Gray Harbour. Across the river, [Roah Moonglow makes the party an alternative offer](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_2/consolidate-the-east-end.md) — erase the Fist warrant for Black Network service — and [the Velvet Hand seats a second floor of high-stakes loan-sharking](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_1/establish-the-velvet-hand-social-club.md) into Brampton. [The Bibliophile sells two distinct dossiers on the party](../../20-Factions/bibliophile/activity/week_2/intelligence-for-sale.md) — one to the [Waterdhavian Kontor](../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_2/profile-the-agitator.md), one to Roah; [the Goldsmiths begin tracking the unbanked 6,000 gp](../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_2/corner-the-silver.md).

[The Gilded Vein assassinates a Chionthar factor and frames the Amnian Kontor](../../20-Factions/gilded-vein/activity/week_2/accelerate-the-crash.md) for it, exploiting the still-circulating [anti-Amnian propaganda the Kontor cannot trace](../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/activity/week_2/the-propaganda-war.md) — the second cart the [party stole in week 1](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/heist-the-rivington-medicine-cart.md). Insurance premiums on river trade rise. [The Lantaner Concession's clockwork-sparrow prototype audits the Sembian warehouses](../../20-Factions/lantaner-concession/activity/week_2/acquire-arcane-effluent.md) without being detected.

## Cult and psychic fronts escalate in parallel

The [Cult of the Returning Lord turns a Bhaalist murder spree into a frame-up of the Free Traders](../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_2/a-murder-in-the-crowd.md), and [the Plague of Remembrance amplifies the frame through Heapside whisper networks](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_2/silence-and-slander.md). [The Hands of the Absolute hunt a "Voice" candidate through Brother Billiam at the Open Hand Temple](../../20-Factions/hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_2/harvest-the-echo.md) and lose the trail when the candidate slips out of Rivington under the protest chaos. [The Unchained's training week](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_2/a-profitable-exercise.md) marks a periphery psi-sensitive who the Hands now plan to take next sweep. [The Purified disable the Rivington water pump](../../20-Factions/purified/activity/week_2/purge-the-pump-station.md) and trigger a localized shortage; the Watch dismisses it as vandalism. [The Silent Shroud opens magical surveillance on Bror through Mother Nocturne's intelligence brokerage](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_2/amplify-the-despair.md). [The Sodality formalizes the scrying ritual on Lord Corin Durinbold](../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_2/trace-the-arcane-signature.md). [The Takers of the Tithe mark the Ember as their next target after her amplified ballad](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_2/the-grieving-genius.md). The [Gravekeepers quietly put down a Tumbledown manifestation](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_2/secure-the-sepulchers.md) traced back to the [Plague-Father's destroyed lab](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_1/destroy-the-plague-fathers-laboratory.md). The [Carnival of Whispers harvests another disappearance near the siege line](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_2/a-feast-of-frustration.md). The [Circle of the Inner Grove quietly reinforces the blockade](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_2/purify-the-blight.md) for ecological reasons no one outside the Grove sees.

## Conspiracy press misfires; the Mouth gets paid

[The Elturel Remembrance Society's warehouse-break attempt is publicly arrested](../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_2/infernal-residue.md) and the Society loses what credibility it had; [the Society of Baldurian Integrity's "counter-memetic" ballad becomes the joke of the week](../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/activity/week_2/sigil-of-repulsion.md); [the Unveiled fail to find Harper mind-control evidence in the protest](../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_2/chart-the-chaos.md) and pivot to studying the Sodality's new censers. [Ettvard Needle prints both sides of the Archduke/Marshal split for cash](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_2/fan-the-flames.md) and refuses [a Harper leak on the Archduke](../../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_2/follow-the-paper-trail.md) for lack of provable proof. The [Alchemists' Company forges its own ledger to bury the Plague-Father's reagent supplier](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_2/internal-inquisition.md). The [Vintners crack down on a Rivington unlicensed distillery](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_2/stem-the-illicit-tide.md). The [Fishmongers throw a Gray Harbour festival](../../20-Factions/wc-fishmongers/activity/week_2/the-herring-gouge.md) and the [Shipwrights launch a public dock-repair project](../../20-Factions/wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_2/exploit-the-idle.md) in the same news cycle. The [Mercers quietly redesign the Fist officers' dress uniforms](../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_2/weave-a-web-of-whispers.md). The [Chandlers expand lamplighter coverage in Norchapel](../../20-Factions/wc-chandlers-lamplighters/activity/week_2/sell-the-solution-to-fear.md) on the strength of the Bhaalist murder spree. The [Masons split internally over the Consortium's scab labor](../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_2/enforce-the-standard.md). The [High Moon Coster's adventurer-financing pivot is rebuffed by the party](../../20-Factions/high-moon-coster/activity/week_2/finance-the-smugglers.md) as insultingly small.

## From PC actions

The party walks into week 2 with the captured Plague-of-Remembrance cultist in tow and walks out at **Level 4**, six thousand gold pieces richer, on the Flaming Fist's warrant book, broken with the Guild, courted by the Zhentarim, and quietly recruited by the Silent Shroud.

- **Session 6.** Confronted by [Manip Jerrol](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/people/manip-jerrol.md) at the High Rider's Stand, [Kaelan is deputized into the Fist](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md) under orders to deliver the prisoner to the Sea Tower. [Alena debriefs them](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_2/the-interrogation.md) on the Plague's "sister in disguise" and Jaredith plot. The party parades the cultist publicly to ['Mad' Meggan](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/people/mad-meggan.md) at the Basilisk Gate siege and **sells him for 6,000 gp**; Fen lifts a [Medallion of Thoughts](https://www.dndbeyond.com/magic-items/4679-medallion-of-thoughts) off Meggan's neck during the negotiation.
- **Session 7.** [Vex pivots them to the Circus of the Last Days](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/take-vex-circus-assignment.md) and refuses to take the Whispering Stone. Elia buys an alien clockwork "rat catcher" stone hawk for 25 gp. At the Phoenix Pits, the party [exposes Zora as the Plague mage](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/expose-zora-at-the-phoenix-pits.md); **Spark wins a non-lethal armbar duel with Jaredith** and yields gracefully — the martyrdom plot collapses.
- **Session 8.** Bror's first attempt to attune the Whispering Stone deafens him. [Finn burglarizes the Wondrous Trinkets and Arcane Emporium](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/burglarize-the-arcane-emporium.md) and lifts a *Pass Without Trace* scroll with a hidden waterproof map of the Brampton smuggling tunnels under the Velvet Hand. Vex demands 6,000 gp for an illusion-breaking spell scroll and the party refuses. At the [watchtower ambush](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/survive-the-watchtower-ambush.md), Zora drops *Cone of Cold*, Bror floods the floor with Darkness, Spark goes off the cliff in a rage tackle and survives, and Elia's *Dissonant Whispers* finally compels Zora to teleport away. Loot: cracked-vial cult tokens. Five days of downtime begin.
- **Session 9.** [Bror gives Mother Nocturne a full intelligence debrief](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/attune-to-the-whispering-stone.md) at the Penumbra Playhouse in exchange for ritual aid; the second attunement also fails. Kaelan scouts the Circus and clocks the Guild lookout. Elia tends Jaredith and watches him meet [the Ember](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md). The party then [witnesses the Ember and Jaredith end the Hellrider protest in song](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/witness-jaredith-end-the-protest.md); the way into the main city of Baldur's Gate opens. The party reaches **Level 4**.

The party leaves week 2 publicly outlawed by the Fist, privately on the Zhentarim's recruitment shortlist, broken with the Guild, in possession of Mad Meggan's Medallion of Thoughts, the Velvet Hand smuggling map, an alien rat-catching hawk, and a Sharran handler they did not mean to acquire — alongside an injured but loyal [Jaredith](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) and a New Elturel suddenly tilted toward [the Ember's](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/people/the-ember.md) faith movement.
