# Week 7 — Events

Early spring, 1502 DR. The siege has become a *system*.

## The Press Blackout — and the war for the public's mind

[Archduke Lyra Silvershield's Press Blackout](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_7/the-press-blackout.md) is the load-bearing operation of the week. With [Ettvard Needle missing in the Tumbledown crypts](../../50-Sessions/session_27/player_summary.md), [an Interim Editor on the Western Gate's payroll takes the *Baldur's Mouth*](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_7/the-puppet-press.md) and converts it into a Consortium-aligned mouthpiece in a single tenday. [Factor Errinthal pushes a sweeping retraction of Kaelan's *Insurance Scam*](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_7/the-great-retraction.md) through the puppeted paper. [Nansi Gretta corners every remaining copy of the original Siege Edition](../../20-Factions/bibliophile/activity/week_7/the-missing-ink.md) and offers the surviving stock to the Consortium as a sealed bid. [The Harpers' Nightingale dispatches three cells to find Ettvard alive in the Western Gate warehouses](../../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_7/find-the-quill.md) — they confirm someone is buying his distinctive paper stock in bulk, but no body, no Ettvard. **The party is in the Tumbledown crypts looking for the same man from the other end and does not yet know they have allies.**

The Council also fires on its own information ecosystem from a second angle: [the Council orders a night-raid against the Eastway buffer](../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_7/break-the-buffer.md), and the puppet *Mouth* frames the burned Free-Trader food caches as Guild contraband. By midweek [the Plague of Remembrance dresses cultists in counterfeit Fist armor and massacres a Sow & Piglet breadline](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_7/the-refugee-riot.md); the next morning the *Mouth* runs the riot as a refugee outburst. [The Unveiled answer with pamphlets calling the Bhaalist murders an Archduke false flag](../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_7/the-prison-break-propaganda.md) — internally consistent, almost entirely wrong, *believed* by a Lower City that has watched the press flip overnight.

## The Outer City eats its own pantries

The siege bites deeper. [Sembian patrols now confiscate personal food stores](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_7/the-press-blackout.md) off any traveller crossing the Outer City roads. [The Druids of the Inner Grove sing thorny river-cane up through the Free-Trader wagon-barricades](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_7/vine-barricades.md), making the Basilisk Gate effectively unassailable; [Mad Meggan answers the cordon with strict rationing inside the line and a halfling food-recovery network through the storm-aqueducts under Eastway](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_7/smuggling-the-crumbs.md), surfacing inside Lower City pantries at dawn. [Bursar Korgan Ironhand triggers a 300% starvation premium on hoarded grain](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/activity/week_7/the-starvation-premium.md), selling exclusively to bidding patriar households; [Master Phineas Droog withholds antidotes from any non-Sembian buyer](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_7/the-antidote-monopoly.md); [Matron Saltwind extorts a violent toll on every independent fishing skiff in Gray Harbour](../../20-Factions/wc-fishmongers/activity/week_7/the-harbor-toll.md). The siege is a *food-and-medicine pricing operation* now, and the Outer City has no honest market open to it.

## The institutions harden — then crack

The Upper City's gilded cage cracks first. [Trapped patriars riot against the Watch itself, demanding to be let out](../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_7/the-gilded-cage-riots.md) before the Lower City mob breaks in; the Watch does not have the bodies; [the Knights of the Shield are paid by Manorborn houses to provide riot suppression](../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_7/the-gilded-extortion.md) and convert Upper City fear into infernal soul-coin lien on twelve patriar bloodlines. [The Hidden Lord](../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/people/the-hidden-lord.md) is the silent winner of the lockdown. [Lord Hhune's Remembrance Society agents ignite alchemical sigils on six "Hell-gold" storefronts](../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_7/the-alchemical-arson.md); two go up. [The Peerage of Blood files a formal Ultimatum to the Duke](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_7/the-ultimatum-to-the-duke.md), demanding Silvershield's resignation; the vote fails by four because [the Bathhouse Brawl the night before](../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_7/the-bathhouse-brawl.md) poisoned the cross-aisle Coin moderates. [Lord Durinbold's slander-pamphlets blanket the Lower City](../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_7/the-slander-campaign.md), giving his bribed Fist sergeants written cover to refuse Marshal Flint's orders; [Flint's planned raid on the Free Trader blockade dies in the barracks](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/activity/week_7/the-paralyzed-patrols.md). **The Fist is now a parade-ground unit.**

The Counting House becomes literal infrastructure. [The Peerage of Coin doubles their Sembian-livery private army](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_7/private-armies.md) inside the Counting House walls, turning it into a fortress the Fist cannot enter. [Master Goldhand stages a public magical assay of the city's reserves](../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_7/the-public-assay.md) in the Counting House reading room and saves Lower City confidence in the Goldsmiths' assay authority — even as [the Gilded Vein floods the Lower City retail markets with high-quality forged copper](../../20-Factions/gilded-vein/activity/week_7/the-copper-crash.md) and crashes the small-trade copper-silver ratio 18% in five days. [Master Stonehand quietly removes structural supports from three Upper City guardhouses](../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_7/the-sabotaged-foundations.md) — the strike has become sabotage.

## The water and river fronts

[The Amnian Kontor crews the captured Brindlestone fleet entirely with Coin-Sword retainers and runs the Chionthar like a tollroad](../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/activity/week_7/consolidate-river-control.md) — a foreign-kontor river blockade with no Baldurian charter. [The Shipwrights' drydock lockout fully beaches the city's independent fleet on the Gray Harbour mudflats](../../20-Factions/wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_7/the-beached-fleet.md). [Goodman Borlu arms the Tannery with cleavers and dumps daily waste into the Chionthar](../../20-Factions/wc-butchers-tanners/activity/week_7/the-tannery-expansion.md) in defiance of every standing law; the river runs blood-color along the Lower City bank. [The Purified poison the Rivington cisterns with caustic-detection acid](../../20-Factions/purified/activity/week_7/the-cistern-sabotage.md), trying to "burn out" the Lantaner plague they believe contaminates the city's drinking water; southern Rivington loses fresh water for at least a tenday. [Silas the Eel gambles the cell on a suicidal armed raid against a Zhentarim safehouse](../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_7/the-desperate-dive.md); three River-Rats die; Silas takes a shocking-grasp through the shoulder and ends the week with a Zhent contract on his head and a useless right arm. [One-Eyed Jack preempts the Zhent retaliation with a sewer counter-raid](../../20-Factions/wharf-rats/activity/week_7/the-sewer-skirmish.md) and ends the week controlling four key drainage junctions, including a cistern overflow that opens directly into the Counting House subbasement.

## The covert and magical crackdown peaks

[Roah Moonglow runs the Mole Hunt in parallel with a feint](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_7/the-mole-hunt.md): another round of altered Guild tax records distracts Nine-Fingers, while Roah's two best interrogators reach the High Rider's Stand in time to identify and abduct the cook [Alena's Witch Hunt](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_7/the-witch-hunt.md) had already cleared. The mole dies in a Heapside cellar; his body is left in a Hellrider supply wagon as a *demonstration*. [Nine-Fingers' Digital Purge](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_7/the-digital-purge.md) kills three Zhent ledger-clerks in alleys and one at his desk with no visible wound. [Mistress Brightwood is forced into a planned total streetlamp blackout around the Velvet Hand](../../20-Factions/wc-chandlers-lamplighters/activity/week_7/the-guided-darkness.md) — the *night the Digital Purge happens*. **The Chandlers' Guild is now functionally a Guild auxiliary unit operating as a public utility.**

[The Hands of the Absolute scale up to a living-battery resonance chamber](../../20-Factions/hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_7/the-resonance-chamber.md) and leak waking nightmares across three blocks of Heapside. [Mother Nocturne reads the bleed as a Sharran sign](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_7/the-helm-and-cloak-infiltration.md), finishes her Purge of the Blind, and deploys elite Sharran spies into the Helm and Cloak — by week's end she holds blackmail material on five mid-tier patriars, two of them signatories on the Knights of the Shield's contracts. [The Sodality runs an aggressive magical dragnet across Rivington](../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_7/the-magical-dragnet.md) and fines six honest hedge-casters for the Hands' lattice harmonics. [The Cartographers hire a psion through a Sodality intermediary](../../20-Factions/wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_7/the-memory-recovery.md) and recover the K'liir-wiped Githyanki map data — including a partial astral-bridge map under Wyrm's Rock and a Sembian smuggling-route fragment Vancroft sells to Korgan Ironhand within hours. The recovered memory also picked up the Hands' lattice signature; nobody has noticed yet.

[Commander K'laar orders an absolute astral quarantine around Wyrm's Rock](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_7/the-astral-quarantine.md). The Council's first written inquiry goes unanswered. [The Bhaalist remnant from Dusthawk Hill re-coalesces in the cellar beneath Hamhock's Slaughterhouse in Sow's Foot](../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_7/blood-in-the-slaughterhouse.md) and begins mass ritual murder of refugees folded into the slaughterhouse's evening kill-stream. [Old Man Hemlock walls Cliffside in publicly-blessed undead](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_7/the-cliffside-consecration.md), aimed at a Bhaalist threat that has *already moved*. [The Carnival's Mister Smiles drops a Memory Mite into refugee dreams to track Bror and Elia](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_7/the-memory-mites-message.md) and feeds the patron the city's ambient anxiety. [The Takers of the Tithe dispatch a Hunter-Killer cell to find and kill the party in revenge for Singa](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_7/the-auditors-revenge.md); the cell shadows the Plowman from a tannery shed by midweek. **The party is being hunted and does not know it.**

## The fragments and the survivors

[The Glim-Nimblewhiz finalizes the Clockwork Key — a silent override on every Lantaner-built lock and gate in the city](../../20-Factions/lantaner-concession/activity/week_7/the-clockwork-key.md). [Master Tor-keg Silverhand starts the forges burning day and night to produce mass-produced low-quality blades sold to anyone who pays first](../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_7/the-forge-fires.md); 800 cheap blades go into the city's grey market in four days; [Dammon's bespoke forge refuses to participate](../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/people/dammon.md). [Master Quickfoot's Vintner ale embargo cracks Sembian discipline at Eastway](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_7/the-dry-riot.md); a deserter mercenary unit storms an unlicensed grog-shop and the Sembian command negotiates a triple-rate Vintner truce by sundown of the fifth day. [Mistress Vance closes the Mercer uniform contract for the Archduke's private army](../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_7/the-mercenary-contract.md), publicly picking a side. [Factor Mornwood opens formal Lords' Alliance negotiations with the Hellriders](../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_7/the-diplomatic-immunity.md): grain shipments, antitoxins, and a weapons-import exemption in exchange for public Hellrider opposition to Sembian influence — *Waterdeep is making its bid for soft-power dominance of the New Elturel district*.

[The Iron Throne sells Free-Trader barricade schematics to the Sembian commanders and Sembian patrol intelligence to the Free Traders](../../20-Factions/iron-throne/activity/week_7/the-sabotage-brokerage.md), engineering the violence the coster profits from. [The Followers of the Phoenix accelerate the Phoenix Temple Foundation Stone construction in Alfira's absence with Jaredith presiding](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_7/the-saints-respite.md); the temple's stone footprint is fully laid by the seventh day; the faction is consolidating from a street-level cult into a physical institution. [Calliope's tuning-fork exorcism of the Elfsong ghost ends in a beautiful cultural-fusion duet](../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/activity/week_7/the-sonic-cleansing.md) that publicly refutes the Society of Baldurian Integrity's purity doctrine.

## From PC actions

The party opens week 7 at [the Plowman](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/rivington.md) reading the new (post-Needle) [*Baldur's Mouth*](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_7/the-puppet-press.md) over breakfast — Sembians "securing" the city, the Sow & Piglet massacre blamed on the Plague-of-Remembrance disguising as Fist, K'laar's astral quarantine on Wyrm's Rock as warning, the Unveiled denounced, Droog's antidote restriction reported as wartime necessity, the Vintner Dry Riot filed as "rogue mercenary outburst." Two open threads from session 27 remain: **Bror still owes Mother Nocturne the *sentient source* of the secret of the stone**, and **Ettvard Needle is in the Tumbledown crypts** west of Dusthawk Hill.

[The party visits Dammon's forge in New Elturel](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_7/visit-dammons-forge.md). Dammon — busy supplying weapons for the Hellrider–Free Trader joint detail at Basilisk Gate — fits Bror with **dwarven unarmed-strike gauntlets** (+1 to hit), then tests Spark's cloak with a glowing forge prong: the fire reads as *welcoming, comforting, not painful*. He reveals the cloak's clasp and tendrils are forged from **Karlach's discarded original-heart infernal iron** and suggests Spark talk to Alfira about Karlach when she wakes.

[The party crosses to Brampton to ask Master Phineas Droog for a route into Tumbledown](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_7/cross-the-eastway-blockade-to-tumbledown.md). Droog refuses Kaelan's appeal to relax [the Antidote Monopoly](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_7/the-antidote-monopoly.md) for Outer City clinics, but hands the party a **Guild coin** and the name of one [Garrett "Grit" Fallow](../../20-Factions/guild/people/garrett-grit-fallow.md), a Tumbledown gravedigger on quiet Guild retainer. The party debates the route. Bror's Whispering Stone hums whenever he steps near a Heapside sewer-grate — the [Hands of the Absolute's resonance lattice is leaking up from below](../../20-Factions/hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_7/the-resonance-chamber.md). They commit to the surface route through the Eastway blockade. Disguises are *unconvincing*; the Sembian gate detail is preoccupied with deserter-mercenaries from [the Vintner Dry Riot](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_7/the-dry-riot.md) and waves the party through with a glance. They reach Tumbledown by midafternoon — crypt-still, necromantic-cold; Hemlock's [Cliffside Consecration patrols](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_7/the-cliffside-consecration.md) are heavier than the party expected. Perception roll: a **five-strong gravedigger patrol standing in the next yard**. The session ends on the patrol decision deferred to next tenday.

The week's load-bearing facts: the *Baldur's Mouth* is a Consortium mouthpiece; the Outer City has no food and no public voice; the Upper City has Knights-of-the-Shield infernal-pact rioters patrolling its own gates from the inside; the Counting House is a private fortress; Saltwind owns Gray Harbour; the Bhaalist remnant is killing in a Sow's Foot cellar inside Belvin Hamhock's slaughterhouse; the Hellrider supply-line mole is dead in a Hellrider wagon; the party is being hunted by the Takers' Hunter-Killer cell; and the party has crossed into Tumbledown carrying a Guild coin and Grit Fallow's name. Week 8 begins with the gravedigger patrol decision and the descent into the Tumbledown crypts to find Ettvard Needle alive.
