# Baldur's Mouth — Week 7 — The Restoration Issue

*Early spring, 1502 DR. The first issue under the Interim Editor.*

> *A note from the Interim Management Board: due to the unexplained absence of our editor Mr. Needle, this paper's daily operations have temporarily passed to a board of senior staff. Submissions previously held for verification have been returned to source.*

## Front page

**SEMBIAN PROFESSIONALS SECURE THE CITY**
*by an Interim Correspondent*

[Archduke Lyra Silvershield's contracted Sembian companies](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_7/the-press-blackout.md) continued their orderly patrols of the Outer City roads this Sevenday, providing visible reassurance to lawful travellers and confiscating only such caches of provisions as exceeded household ordinary use. *The Council's leadership remains steady in a difficult tenday.*

## Above the fold

- [The Plague of Remembrance cult, disguised as Flaming Fist soldiers, attacked a refugee bread-line at the Sow & Piglet](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_7/the-refugee-riot.md). Eleven dead. *The cult's intent appears to have been the framing of the city's defenders.*
- [Commander K'laar of the Unchained githyanki has placed an astral quarantine around Wyrm's Rock](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_7/the-astral-quarantine.md). Citizens crossing [Wyrm's Crossing](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) without authorization may experience nausea and confusion. *This paper recommends the Coast Way alternate.*
- **A warning concerning the [Unveiled](../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_7/the-prison-break-propaganda.md).** A pamphlet circulating in Lower City alleys claims the recent Bhaalist murders are an Archduke false-flag operation. *This paper finds no credible evidence for any such claim.*

## Page two — *A Necessary Correction*

[A note of correction is due](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_7/the-great-retraction.md) regarding the Siege Edition of one Sevenday past. The "Insurance Scam" exposé naming the Chionthar Consortium and Sembian Kontor was, this paper has determined, *based on a document trail falsified by Zhentarim ledger-clerks*. **Mr. Needle, prior to his absence, was misled.** We extend our regret to Factor Errinthal and Bursar Korgan Ironhand, and to readers.

## City pages

- **[Counting House](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/counting-house.md).** [Master Theronius Goldhand walked outside auditors through the city's emergency gold reserves](../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_7/the-public-assay.md) in the public reading room. *The vaults were as the Master attested.* Bankruptcy rumors are without foundation.
- **[Counting House](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/counting-house.md).** [The Peerage of Coin voted unanimously to expand liveried Sembian guard at every patriar bank and Counting House door](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_7/private-armies.md). *The Fist's resources are needed elsewhere.*
- **[Crimson Draughts, Brampton](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/brampton.md).** [Master Phineas Droog has restricted his antidote inventory to the Archduke's quartermasters](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_7/the-antidote-monopoly.md) following Zhentarim adulterants in unregulated ale.
- **Eastway barracks.** [A small disturbance among contracted Sembian field mercenaries](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_7/the-dry-riot.md) — a *rogue mercenary outburst* — was attributed to a Vintners' supply gap and resolved within a single evening.
- **[Basilisk Gate](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-bend-market.md).** Free Trader pickets [continue to obstruct the lawful flow of supply](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_7/vine-barricades.md); a recent reinforcement of the barricade with druidic flora has rendered the position theatrically picturesque and operationally unhelpful. *The blockade is now a self-imposed siege.*
- **[Gray Harbour](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/gray-harbour.md).** [Matron Saltwind's Fishmongers' militia](../../20-Factions/wc-fishmongers/activity/week_7/the-harbor-toll.md) assesses a small fee on independent skiffs in support of waterway security. Consul Vorlamin Athar of the Amnian Kontor [has published a new river-traffic schedule](../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/activity/week_7/consolidate-river-control.md) for shipping above the harbor.
- **[Phoenix Gate, New Elturel](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/new-elturel.md).** [Construction at the new temple proceeds](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_7/the-saints-respite.md) under one Jaredith Heartsong, formerly of the Hellriders. The folk-saint Alfira is reported convalescing privately.
- **[High Hall](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/upper-city/the-wide.md).** [The Peerage of Blood filed a motion this Twoday](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_7/the-ultimatum-to-the-duke.md) requesting the Archduke's resignation; the motion failed by four votes. *The chamber's calmer hours prevailed.*
- **Manorborn.** [Several minor noble households have filed disorderly-conduct complaints](../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_7/the-gilded-cage-riots.md) against the Watch over the [interior gate lockdown](../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_6/the-gate-lockdown.md). Negotiations continue.

## Notices and obituaries

- **Hellscream Bathhouse.** [A private gathering of peers Coin and Blood](../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_7/the-bathhouse-brawl.md) was concluded prematurely owing to a misunderstanding between Lord Corin Durinbold and an unnamed senior Coin lord. No charges filed.
- **[Bloomridge](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/bloomridge.md).** [Mistress Vance has secured the dress-uniform contract](../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_7/the-mercenary-contract.md) for the Archduke's Sembian companies.
- **[Elfsong Tavern](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/elfsong-tavern.md).** [A musical event combining the resident ghost's lament with a traditional Baldurian sea shanty](../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/activity/week_7/the-sonic-cleansing.md) attracted favourable notices and one impromptu marriage proposal.
- **[Cliffside](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md).** [Old Man Hemlock](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_7/the-cliffside-consecration.md) is conducting an extended consecration of the cemetery perimeter. Funeral processions are rerouted via the eastern approach.
