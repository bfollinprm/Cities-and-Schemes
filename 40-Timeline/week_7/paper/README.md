# Baldur's Mouth — Week 7 — The Restoration Issue

*Early spring, 1502 DR. The first issue under the Interim Editor.*

> *A note from the Interim Management Board: due to the unexplained absence of our editor Mr. Needle, this paper's daily operations have temporarily passed to a board of senior staff. Service to our readers will continue without interruption. Submissions previously held for verification have been returned to source.*

## Front page

**SEMBIAN PROFESSIONALS SECURE THE CITY**
*by an Interim Correspondent*

[Archduke Lyra Silvershield's contracted Sembian companies](../../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_7/the-press-blackout.md) continued their orderly patrols of the Outer City roads this Sevenday, providing visible reassurance to lawful travellers and confiscating only such caches of provisions as exceeded household ordinary use. *The Council's leadership remains steady in a difficult tenday.* This paper extends its respectful condolences to the Peerage of Blood for the [tone of recent floor remarks](../../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_7/the-ultimatum-to-the-duke.md), which we trust the calmer councils of cooler hours will overtake.
