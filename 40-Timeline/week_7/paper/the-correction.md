# Page two — *A Necessary Correction*

*Filed under the masthead of the Interim Editor.*

[A note of correction is due](../../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_7/the-great-retraction.md) regarding the Siege Edition of one Sevenday past. The "Insurance Scam" exposé — which named the Chionthar Consortium and Sembian Kontor as underwriters of arsoned vessels and warehouses — was based on a document trail this paper has since determined to have been *falsified by Zhentarim ledger-clerks* in service of the Black Network's wider war on the city's lawful merchants. **Mr. Needle, prior to his absence, was misled.** We extend our regret to Factor Errinthal of the Consortium and to Bursar Korgan Ironhand of the Iron Ledger. We extend our regret to readers. We will do better.
