# Notices and obituaries — Restoration Issue

- **Hellscream Bathhouse.** [A private gathering of peers Coin and Blood](../../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_7/the-bathhouse-brawl.md) was concluded prematurely owing to a misunderstanding between Lord Corin Durinbold and an unnamed senior Coin lord. No charges filed.
- **[Bloomridge](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/bloomridge.md).** [Mistress Elethra Vance of the Mercers has secured the dress-uniform contract](../../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_7/the-mercenary-contract.md) for the Archduke's Sembian companies. *Bloomridge's manufacturing district is hiring.*
- **[Counting House](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/counting-house.md).** Several Lower City retailers report a sudden surplus of high-quality copper coinage of [questionable provenance](../../../20-Factions/gilded-vein/activity/week_7/the-copper-crash.md). The Goldsmiths' assay office is at readers' service.
- **[Elfsong Tavern](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/elfsong-tavern.md).** A musical event [combining the resident ghost's lament with a traditional Baldurian sea shanty](../../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/activity/week_7/the-sonic-cleansing.md) attracted favourable notices and one impromptu marriage proposal. The Society of Baldurian Integrity's role in the event is the subject of conflicting accounts.
- **[Cliffside](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/tumbledown-and-floodway.md).** [Old Man Hemlock of the Gravekeepers](../../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_7/the-cliffside-consecration.md) is conducting an extended consecration of the cemetery perimeter. Funeral processions are rerouted via the eastern approach.
