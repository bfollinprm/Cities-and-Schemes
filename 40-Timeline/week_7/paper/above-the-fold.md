# Above the fold — Restoration Issue

- [The Plague of Remembrance cult, disguised as Flaming Fist soldiers, attacked a refugee bread-line at the Sow & Piglet](../../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_7/the-refugee-riot.md) at sundown of the fifth day. Eleven dead. *The cult's intent appears to have been the framing of the city's defenders.* Investigation continues; readers are urged to support the Fist in this difficult time.
- [Commander K'laar of the Unchained githyanki has placed an astral quarantine around Wyrm's Rock](../../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_7/the-astral-quarantine.md). Citizens crossing [Wyrm's Crossing](../../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/outer-city/wyrms-crossing.md) without authorization may experience nausea and confusion. *This paper recommends the Coast Way alternate.*
- **A warning to readers concerning the [Unveiled](../../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_7/the-prison-break-propaganda.md).** A pamphlet circulating in Lower City alleys claims the recent Bhaalist murders are an Archduke false-flag operation. *This paper finds no credible evidence for any such claim* and urges readers to surrender Unveiled materials to the Watch.
