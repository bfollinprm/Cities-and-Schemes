# Week 4 — Events

Early spring, 1502 DR. The week the political war stops being political.

## Private war turns public — the Eastway barracks

[Archduke Lyra Silvershield](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/people/lyra-silvershield.md), locked out of Council and Parliament after [last week's filibustered no-confidence vote](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_3/filibuster-the-mercenary-act.md), converts [the private mercenary contract she signed at the Iron Ledger](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/activity/week_4/legitimizing-the-contract.md) into [boots on the ground](../../20-Factions/council-of-four/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-deployment.md). [A Western Gate Trading Company warehouse is requisitioned](../../20-Factions/western-gate-trading-company/activity/week_4/the-barracks-requisition.md) and converted in days into a fortified barracks, mess, drill yard, and command tent in [Eastway](../../30-Places/sword-coast/baldurs-gate/lower-city/eastway.md), inside the Basilisk Gate, in shield-wall distance of the Free Trader blockade. [Bursar Korgan Ironhand](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/people/korgan-ironhand.md) pushes a "City-Sanctioned Peacekeepers" contract through the High Hall bureaucracy that gives the company arrest authority on city soil. ['Mad' Meggan's order to fortify the blockade](../../20-Factions/free-traders-of-the-outskirts/activity/week_4/digging-in.md) is broken in front of the city by a single mercenary shield-wall march; Meggan's crews have to drop their tools.

The chambers respond by ceasing to function. The Peerage of Coin pushes through [the Emergency Commerce Protection Act](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-coin/activity/week_4/the-emergency-levy.md) — a permanent gate-toll levy that uses the city's own commerce to fund the Archduke's private army. [The Peerage of Blood passes a Letter of Censure](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/activity/week_4/the-letter-of-censure.md) demanding her resignation. [The emergency joint session called to address all of it descends into a physical brawl on the floor](../../20-Factions/parliament-of-peers/activity/week_4/a-debate-of-the-deaf.md); [the Watch is called into the High Hall itself for the first time in living memory](../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_4/the-manorborn-burglaries.md). Parliament is suspended indefinitely. [Lord Corin Durinbold](../../20-Factions/peerage-of-blood/people/corin-durinbold.md) opens [the Loyalty Fund](../../20-Factions/patriar-heritage-society/activity/week_4/the-loyalty-fund.md) and starts buying Fist enlisted ranks one Heritage-red ribbon at a time. [The Harpers' last back-channel compromise meeting](../../20-Factions/harpers/activity/week_4/the-back-channel-bargain.md) is leaked and the moderates are publicly burned. **The peaceful unwind is gone.**

## Wyrm's Rock changes meaning

[Marshal Flint](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/people/valerius-flint.md) takes [the Unchained's discreet peacekeeping offer from last week](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_3/an-honorable-contract.md) and converts it into a public ceremony: [Commander K'laar marches a disciplined githyanki phalanx in full astral battle gear from the K'liir camp, across Wyrm's Crossing, and into Wyrm's Rock](../../20-Factions/unchained/activity/week_4/an-honorable-march.md), where [Flint formally welcomes them onto the fortress walls](../../20-Factions/flaming-fist/activity/week_4/an-alliance-of-steel-and-silver.md). The march itself is the message. The crowd does not cheer. **Wyrm's Rock is no longer the Fist's fortress — it is the fear-symbol of the city's new political war.** [The Smiths' Company offers githyanki forge-services and is dismissed with contempt](../../20-Factions/wc-smiths-armorers/activity/week_4/an-astral-bargain.md) — K'laar reads the offer as condescension. [The Masons' Company offers a discount on barracks expansion to the Archduke and has it returned with a public scathing note](../../20-Factions/wc-masons-sculptors/activity/week_4/a-political-hedge.md). [The Mercers' atelier triples the price of mourning silks](../../20-Factions/wc-mercers-clothiers/activity/week_4/the-business-of-grief.md). [The Vintners shift allegiance and become the informal house brewer of the Eastway barracks](../../20-Factions/wc-vintners-brewers/activity/week_4/a-new-patron.md). The political crisis is being dressed and supplied in real time.

[The Cartographers double-sell tactical maps to both the Archduke's mercenaries and Mad Meggan's lieutenants](../../20-Factions/wc-cartographers-surveyors/activity/week_4/playing-both-sides.md). [The Bibliophile sells the mercenary commander a map of Wyrm's Rock's interior](../../20-Factions/bibliophile/activity/week_4/the-price-of-strategy.md). [The Baldur's Mouth's lurid mercenary-menace front page is debunked within forty-eight hours](../../20-Factions/baldurs-mouth/activity/week_4/the-mercenary-menace.md), confirming Editor Needle as patriar mouthpiece to the working public. [The Society of Baldurian Integrity tries a slogan-paint raid on the Eastway barracks and has its activists captured](../../20-Factions/society-of-baldurian-integrity/activity/week_4/the-writing-on-the-wall.md), giving the mercenaries their first sworn statement on a nativist movement.

## New Elturel becomes the city's last legitimate watch

[High Watcher Alena](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/high-watcher-alena.md) reads the Eastway barracks as the inevitable attack she warned the Council was coming. She [issues a formal call to arms](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/activity/week_4/the-call-to-arms.md), organizes refugees into militia brigades, and runs public dawn-and-dusk drills with [Jaredith Heartsong](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/jaredith-heartsong.md) at her side. [Quartermaster Lyra Sunstone](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md), her purpose restored after [the Feywild Ephemera Collection episode](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md), is back signing requisitions for steel.

[The Takers of the Tithe send their Reaper after the Ember at the Phoenix Gate](../../20-Factions/takers-of-the-tithe/activity/week_4/the-reapers-gambit.md). The attack fails spectacularly — Alfira's followers mob the assassin and the Hellriders take custody of a *live* Reaper for interrogation. [The Ember turns the failed attack into a martyr's sermon](../../20-Factions/followers-of-the-phoenix/activity/week_4/a-martyrs-sermon.md) at the newly-consecrated Phoenix Gate the next morning, attendance triples, and the militia muster and the Phoenix congregation are visibly the same crowd by sundown. [The Plague of Remembrance changes vector](../../20-Factions/plague-of-remembrance/activity/week_4/the-rot-from-within.md) and turns a disillusioned Hellrider officer into a paid informant inside the command tent.

## The cult fronts leave concrete evidence

The hidden wars start producing physical artifacts the city's authorities can hold in their hands. [A Bhaalist ambush on a Manorborn Watch patrol fails](../../20-Factions/cult-of-the-returning-lord/activity/week_4/an-offering-in-the-dark.md) and the cell drops its second ritual dagger in two weeks; the Watch now has unambiguous evidence of an organized Bhaalist cell active in the Upper City. The same week, [the Gravekeepers descend the Tumbledown sewers and find the cell's *hidden temple*](../../20-Factions/gravekeepers/activity/week_4/the-source-of-the-sickness.md) — congealed altars, the sigil on the keystone, the source of the cemetery's necromantic seepage. The intelligence is filed up to the Watch, the Hellriders, and a Harper contact.

[The Hands of the Absolute try Sorcerous Sundries' "low-security" psionic vault](../../20-Factions/hands-of-the-absolute/activity/week_4/the-sundered-vault.md) and run into glyphs of warding, alarms, and arcane constructs. The break-in *fails*; [Lorroakan signs off on permanent scrying censers at Wyrm's Crossing and the Basilisk Gate](../../20-Factions/wc-sorcerous-sodality/activity/week_4/the-scrying-censors.md) the same afternoon, escalating the magical surveillance regime against everyone. [Lord Perrine Hhune's amateur Jannath Estate "infernal heist" is wiped out by the estate's own wards](../../20-Factions/elturel-remembrance-society/activity/week_4/the-infernal-heist.md); his followers are bound and turned over to the Watch. [The Unveiled's prison break to recover their captured operatives is ambushed at the cellar grate](../../20-Factions/unveiled/activity/week_4/the-prison-break.md) and Xylia loses every operative she sent. [The Watch's understaffing is the gap a Manorborn cat-burglar uses](../../20-Factions/watch/activity/week_4/the-manorborn-burglaries.md) to lift Peerage-of-Coin correspondence and feed an unsigned political pamphlet.

[The Carnival of Whispers' Memory Mite courier is intercepted in transit](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_4/the-debt-comes-due.md) by an unknown rival fey creature drawn to the chaotic magical residue around the city — Mister Smiles is now hunting an unfamiliar planar predator on his own turf. [The Silent Shroud sends Bror his first instructed mission](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_4/the-first-tithe.md): take the Whispering Stone to the Helm and Cloak and listen. The instruction sits unread at the Plowman; Bror is in the Feywild.

## The trade war consolidates and the docks rebalance

[The Amnian Kontor's hostile takeover of a Sembian-aligned supplier backfires](../../20-Factions/amnian-kontor/activity/week_4/hostile-takeover.md) when the Sembians counter-bid; Korgan Ironhand uses the goodwill to grease [the mercenary contract](../../20-Factions/sembian-kontor/activity/week_4/legitimizing-the-contract.md) through faster. [The Chionthar Consortium's sloppy "warning" to Amn](../../20-Factions/chionthar-consortium/activity/week_4/a-warning-in-the-warehouse.md) is witnessed by a Watch patrol and escalates into a *criminal matter*. [The Gilded Vein's grain-warehouse sabotage leaves an alchemical fingerprint that traces back to Valerius Thorne](../../20-Factions/gilded-vein/activity/week_4/poisoning-the-well.md): both the Amnians and the Consortium learn they have been *played*. The Lower City finance war now has three visible combatants. [The Shipwrights' rigged crane "malfunction"](../../20-Factions/wc-shipwrights-calkers/activity/week_4/a-crane-malfunction.md) at a Chionthar dock cracks the warehouse front and hospitalizes two longshoremen.

[The Guild's public retaliation raid on the Velvet Hand](../../20-Factions/guild/activity/week_4/retaliation.md) breaks Roah Moonglow's Brampton gambling den with steel and spectacle. [Roah's smokepowder counter-strike is leaked by an informant inside the smuggling chain and the entire shipment is seized](../../20-Factions/zhentarim/activity/week_4/the-smokepowder-plot.md); the Guild now has both the casus belli *and* the explosives. [One-Eyed Jack tries a guerilla war against the Zhent muscle on Consortium docks](../../20-Factions/wharf-rats/activity/week_4/the-pier-side-war.md) and his crew are made a gruesome public example on the pier; the Wharf Rats' rebellion is crushed. [Silas the Eel loses Harper documents in a Fist-patrol close-call in the Undercity](../../20-Factions/river-rats/activity/week_4/a-favor-for-a-friend.md) and his courier reputation is finished. [The Iron Throne's Rivington Recovery Group front is leaked](../../20-Factions/iron-throne/activity/week_4/the-rivington-recovery-group.md) and outed in the Baldur's Mouth.

[The Knights of the Shield pivot to the Outer City with a Brampton Ward Boss meeting](../../20-Factions/knights-of-the-shield/activity/week_4/a-deal-in-the-gutters.md); the Boss is more cautious than corrupt and reports it up to Vex. [The Chandlers' lamplighter intelligence pipeline in Eastway is compromised](../../20-Factions/wc-chandlers-lamplighters/activity/week_4/eyes-in-the-dark.md) when a Zhent agent turns one of Anya's informants. [The Goldsmiths' audit, dead at the Counting House last week, becomes an open underworld bounty for any information on the party's 6,000 gp](../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_4/a-quiet-bounty.md). [The Fishmongers' solidarity-stew goodwill kitchen is sabotaged into a public food-poisoning incident](../../20-Factions/wc-fishmongers/activity/week_4/the-solidarity-stew.md) — most plausibly Plague-of-Remembrance work — and Marga's budding Free Trader alliance shatters. [The Alchemists' framed apprentice trades three of the Worshipful Company's most valuable potion formulas to the Guild for safe passage](../../20-Factions/wc-alchemists-apothecaries/activity/week_4/a-desperate-bargain.md); [the Purified arrive too late to "cleanse" him](../../20-Factions/purified/activity/week_4/the-scapegoats-secret.md). [The Butchers bribe Editor Needle into running a "generous donations" puff piece](../../20-Factions/wc-butchers-tanners/activity/week_4/killing-the-story.md) the Outer City does not believe.

## The peripheral wins and losses

[The Circle of the Inner Grove's binding ritual on the water weird *supercharges* it instead](../../20-Factions/circle-of-the-inner-grove/activity/week_4/binding-the-blight.md): the elemental escapes the tributary and is now loose in the main Chionthar, hostile to river traffic. [The Waterdhavian Kontor formally closes its file on the party as potential Lords' Alliance assets](../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_4/a-closed-file.md) — exactly the week the party returns from the Feywild with a forgotten god-fragment and Mornwood does not yet know it.

## From PC actions

**The party is not in the city for week 4.** [Sessions 10–11 played out in week 3 of campaign-time and ended with the Feywild's two-Material-Plane-week dilation](../../50-Sessions/session_11/README.md): when Bror, Elia, Kaelan, Spark, and Finn step out of [Lucretius's Ephemera Collection wagon](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_3/win-lyras-memory-from-lucretius.md) and back into Rivington, **two weeks have passed without them**. Week 4 is the first of those two weeks. The campaign-notes (`player-companion.docx`) reflect this — the player journal jumps from `# Week 3` directly to `# Week 5` and skips week 4 entirely.

The party leaves week 4 the way it entered: in the Feywild glade, time-dilated. The mechanical consequences of that absence are the consequences of the timeline above:

- The Fist warrant from [the Mad Meggan deal](../../20-Factions/party/activity/week_2/sell-the-cultist-to-mad-meggan.md) cools on its own from neglect; [Spark will pay 50 gp on return for the held Plowman rooms](../../50-Sessions/session_11/README.md).
- [The Goldsmiths' bounty for the 6,000 gp source](../../20-Factions/wc-goldsmiths-jewelers/activity/week_4/a-quiet-bounty.md) is now live in the Lower City underworld; the party's faces will land on a fence's wall the moment they walk back into Brampton or the Undercellar.
- [The Waterdhavian Kontor closes the party's recruitment file](../../20-Factions/waterdhavian-kontor/activity/week_4/a-closed-file.md). Mornwood will need to be re-approached if the party wants Lords' Alliance leverage to clear the Fist warrants.
- [The Silent Shroud's first-tithe instruction for Bror](../../20-Factions/silent-shroud/activity/week_4/the-first-tithe.md) is sitting at the Plowman as a quietly-delivered Sharran note Bror has not seen.
- [The Carnival of Whispers' fey-debts on Elia and Bror](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/people/mirror-tent-proprietor.md) remain outstanding, and the Carnival is now also distracted by [an unknown fey predator hunting Mister Smiles' couriers](../../20-Factions/carnival-of-whispers/activity/week_4/the-debt-comes-due.md).
- [Lyra Sunstone, restored](../../20-Factions/hellriders-of-new-elturel/people/lyra-sunstone.md), is signing requisitions in the muster to which the party will return — she owes them her identity and is now the Quartermaster of a real militia.

The week's load-bearing fact for the party is what the city has done *without* them: the chambers stop functioning, mercenaries garrison Eastway, Wyrm's Rock houses githyanki, the Phoenix faith is the spiritual fact of New Elturel, the Bhaalist temple is logged in three faction registries, and the Goldsmiths are quietly hunting the source of the party's seed money. The next time they walk through the Plowman door it is week 5. The world has not waited.
